# Jasper Verschuur

**Jasper Verschuur** is a researcher on maritime chokepoint and transport-system risk. In this vault he matters as co-author of the 2025 [[Nature Communications]] paper estimating systemic impacts of disruptions at maritime chokepoints.

## May 2026 FT use

The FT visual story uses Verschuur's framework to separate chokepoints with feasible rerouting from those where disruption strands trade. The most important investing distinction is that high-cost rerouting is still a mitigation, while no-reroute chokepoints such as [[Strait of Hormuz]] create direct physical-shortage risk.
