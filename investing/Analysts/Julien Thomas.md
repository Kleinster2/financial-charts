# Julien Thomas

Equity analyst at [[TP ICAP]] covering European defense and maritime autonomy. Maintains a Buy recommendation on [[Exail Technologies]] (as of Mar 2026), highlighting the Strait of Hormuz mine threat as a significant catalyst for Exail's mine countermeasure and autonomous underwater vehicle business. His coverage of Exail is notable because TP ICAP is one of a small number of brokerages actively covering this French mid-cap defense name, making his price targets and catalysts influential for the stock's relatively thin float.
