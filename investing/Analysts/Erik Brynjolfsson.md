**Erik Brynjolfsson** — director of the Stanford Digital Economy Lab and one of the field's reference economists on technology and productivity. Co-author (with [[Bharat Chandar]] and [[Ruyu Chen]]) of "Canaries in the Coal Mine?" — the ADP-payroll study documenting a 16% relative employment decline for 22-25-year-olds in AI-exposed occupations — and author of the FT op-ed "The AI productivity take-off is finally visible" (2026), the take-off claim tracked in [[AI productivity]].
