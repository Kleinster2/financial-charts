# Guy Ward-Jackson

Economic security analyst at the [[Tony Blair Institute]] (TBI), focused on US-China economic statecraft, supply-chain resilience, and industrial policy. Published the "Economic Security Latency Fund" framework in [[ChinaTalk]] on May 15, 2026 — the canonical articulation of [[Economic latency]] as a calibrated middle path between [[CHIPS Act]]-style permanent reshoring and the negative-only toolkit of [[Export controls]] and sanctions.
