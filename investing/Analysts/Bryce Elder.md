## Notable coverage

### AI-enabled quantitative investment (Jun 9 2026)

Elder's FT Alphaville piece "What Aristotle can teach us about AI-enabled quantitative investment" unpacked a [[Jefferies]] chemicals-team note by [[Laurence Alexander]] et al. on [[Narrative lifecycle investing]]. Elder treated the note's Aristotle/Poetics conceit as decorative and surfaced the substantive content: the six-phase narrative lifecycle, the three vectors by which AI alters it, the [[Thesis lock-in]] pathology, and Jefferies' current-state map (Datacenter AI Capex placed in euphoria, "at risk of cracks").

*Source: [[Financial Times|FT]] Alphaville, Jun 9 2026: https://www.ft.com/content/d9420326-c0ea-43f9-8ffc-a6d4a461e64e.*
