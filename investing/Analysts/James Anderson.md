## Public commentary themes

### Long-duration growth

Anderson's recurring edge is patience around extreme winners. Lingotto's official biography says he was a Baillie Gifford partner for more than 30 years and had roughly twenty years leading flagship portfolios including [[Scottish Mortgage]] Investment Trust and [[Vanguard]] International Growth Fund, with aggregate portfolio responsibilities above $100B.

### AI platform-to-hardware shift

In the May 21, 2026 FT article, Anderson argued that the prior platform era's low-capex, high-free-cash-flow economics are no longer the default assumption for Big Tech. The AI buildout forces [[Microsoft]], [[Alphabet]], [[Amazon]], and [[Meta]] to keep funding data centers, chips, power, and deployment capacity, while bottleneck suppliers such as [[NVIDIA]], [[TSMC]], and [[ASML]] retain stronger pricing power.

The filing decision is concept-first: Anderson is the source, but the framework lives in [[AI capex arms race]], [[AI hyperscalers]], [[AI capex chain basket]], and [[Software AI bifurcation]].
