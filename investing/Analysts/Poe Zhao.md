## Relevant analysis

### Tencent agent flood (May 2026)

Zhao's May 27 2026 public preview frames [[Tencent]]'s AI agent wave as a distribution bet under infrastructure pressure. Tencent is placing agents across office productivity, coding, desktop control, data analytics, design, OS-level assistance, browser, input, meetings, and eventually WeChat Mini Programs as callable skills. But the public preview's numbers show the weak foundation: Yuanbao at 57.35 million monthly active users versus [[Doubao]] at 345 million and [[Qwen]] at 166 million; [[Tencent Cloud]] at 8% public-cloud [[IaaS]] share and ranked fifth; and the leading Chinese MaaS players excluding Tencent from the named top-three group.

The investment frame is clean: distribution may still win if agents become embedded in existing user surfaces, but agent proliferation also raises the cost of being late in model quality and cloud scale.
