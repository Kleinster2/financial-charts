**Mike Kuiken** — US national-security and US-China policy analyst. Distinguished visiting fellow at the [[Hoover Institution]] and vice-chair of the [[US-China Economic and Security Review Commission]]. Argues that the [[United States]] should follow [[China]] in formally recognizing [[Data as an asset|data as a balance-sheet asset]], calling for [[FASB]] recognition standards, [[SEC]] disclosure requirements, and federal data valuation (FT op-ed, 9 Feb 2026).
