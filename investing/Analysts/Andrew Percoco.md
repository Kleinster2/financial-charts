## May 12, 2026 — [[Ford Energy]] note

Tuesday evening research note framing [[Ford Energy]] as the overlooked catalyst on the Model e profitability path:

- Run-rate EBIT potential at 20 GWh production capacity: $500M – $600M
- Probability framing: "fairly high likelihood that Ford signs an energy storage system supply agreement with large commercial customers, and potentially hyperscalers, over the next few months"
- Rating maintained at Hold, price target $14
- Catalyst: [[Ford]] stock +13.2% on May 13, 2026 — biggest single-day gain since March 2020

The note was the narrative bridge that closed the gap between the May 3 FT commodity-squeeze framing of Ford and an AI-infrastructure-adjacent re-rating, with [[BlueOval City]] manufacturing leverage and the [[CATL]] LFP technology-licensing partnership cited as the structural enablers.
