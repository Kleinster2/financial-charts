## May 27, 2026 - China's robotics lineage

Zhang's ChinaTalk piece on Jiang Xinsong reframed the 2026 Chinese robotics boom as the visible end of a much longer line: 1970s automation work at Shanghai Jiao Tong and the [[Shenyang Institute of Automation]], 1980s state-backed industrial-robot prototypes, 863 Program intelligent-robot funding, underwater/mobile/welding robot breakthroughs, and the eventual commercial spinout of Siasun from the [[Chinese Academy of Sciences]] system.

The investing relevance is that current names like [[Unitree]] are not just a startup-cycle story. They sit on top of decades of state-lab capability building, industrial-policy persistence, and manufacturing-system learning.

*Source: Irene Zhang, "China's Father of Robotics," [[ChinaTalk]], May 27 2026.*
