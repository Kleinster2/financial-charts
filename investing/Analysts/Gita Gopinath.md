# Gita Gopinath

Gita Gopinath is the economist who preceded [[Pierre-Olivier Gourinchas]] as the [[IMF]]'s chief economist (Economic Counsellor, 2019-2022) and then served as the Fund's First Deputy Managing Director before returning to [[Harvard]] in 2025. She is a co-architect, with Gourinchas, of the dominant currency paradigm — the framework showing that most global trade is invoiced in dollars, so trade and exchange-rate pass-through respond to the dollar specifically rather than to bilateral rates. That work underpins the vault's treatment of dollar centrality in [[Dollar reserve status erosion]] and the financial leg of [[Geoeconomic fragmentation]].
