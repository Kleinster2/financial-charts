## May 2026 chokepoint read

The FT article attributes to Jensen a rule of thumb that each week of [[Strait of Hormuz]] closure can create roughly a month of disruption. The market point is that reopening is not the same as normalization: schedules, cargo positioning, insurance, and port queues lag the headline.
