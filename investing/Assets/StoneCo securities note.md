#securities

StoneCo securities — Nasdaq: STNE. Equity-market note for [[StoneCo]], the Brazilian payments / banking / credit fintech for MSMBs. Founded by André Street and Eduardo Pontes 2012, IPO'd 2018 with [[Berkshire Hathaway]] as anchor investor. Stock peaked during the 2021 fintech-multiple-blowoff and traded structurally lower since on take-rate compression and competitive pressure from [[PagSeguro]] / [[Mercado Pago]] / [[Nubank]].
