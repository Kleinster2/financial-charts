#securities

PagSeguro securities — NYSE: PAGS. Equity-market note for [[PagSeguro]], the Brazilian payments fintech (PagBank parent). Stock has traded structurally lower since the 2021 fintech peak as Pix disintermediated card-acquiring economics and competitive pressure from [[StoneCo]] / [[Nubank]] / [[Mercado Libre]] / [[PicPay]] compressed take-rates. Highly rate-path sensitive on both funding cost (wholesale DI) and consumer-credit book health.
