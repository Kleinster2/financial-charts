## Capital structure considerations

- **PIPE dilution**: The January 29, 2026 PIPE issued 69.8M common shares at $21.50 — a substantial share-count expansion that reset the basis for any equity-linked valuation.
- **Warrants (USARW)**: Public warrants from the de-SPAC remain outstanding; warrant-holders benefit from the re-rating without sharing in the PIPE dilution.
- **Federal equity stake**: The 10% stake tied to the Commerce Department LOI introduces a government counterparty on the capitalization table; governance and exit mechanics remain under [[Zoe Lofgren|Lofgren]]'s House Science Committee inquiry.
- **Serra Verde stock consideration**: The April 2026 acquisition of [[Serra Verde]] is paid ~$2.5B in USAR stock + $300M cash, implying a further ~100M+ shares issued at closing (depending on reference price).
