## Capital structure and returns

The balance sheet turned shareholder-return-heavy as growth matured: $25B of investment-grade bonds issued March 2026 (funding buybacks, [[Moody's]] downgrade A1 → A2 — "financial engineering rather than productive investment"), against ~$14.4B FY2026 FCF and ~$10B pre-deal net debt. The dilution question is mild relative to [[Snowflake]] — SBC is ~8.5% of revenue and the company is GAAP-profitable, so buybacks reduce float rather than merely sterilize SBC.
