# WDAF

[[WisdomTree]] Asia Defense Fund. Passively tracks the WisdomTree Asia Defense Index, selecting Asia-Pacific companies (ex-[[China]]) deriving at least 10% of revenue from defense-related activities. US-listed September 2025; UCITS version launched March 31, 2026. South Korea dominates the top holdings, reflecting its defense export boom ([[K9 howitzer]], [[K2 tank]], [[KF-21]] fighter).

## Index methodology

The WisdomTree Asia Defense Index selects from developed and emerging Asia-Pacific markets: [[Australia]], [[Japan]], [[Singapore]], [[India]], [[Indonesia]], [[South Korea]], [[Malaysia]], [[Philippines]], [[Taiwan]], [[Thailand]]. China is explicitly excluded.

- Revenue threshold: 10%+ from defense-related activities
- Exposure scoring: Score 3 (>50% defense revenue), Score 2 (25-50%), Score 1 (10-25%)
- Weighting: free-float market cap adjusted by exposure score
- Caps: 7.5% for Score 3 companies, 4% for Score 1-2
- Minimum $200M free-float market cap, $1M 3-month daily volume
- Controversial weapons exclusion
- 20-100 constituents
