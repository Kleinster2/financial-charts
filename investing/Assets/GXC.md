# GXC

**GXC** is the [[State Street|SPDR]] S&P China ETF — broad China equity exposure tracking the S&P China BMI Index. Includes A-shares, H-shares, and US-listed ADRs. [[ALLW]] holds GXC directly as a dedicated China equity allocation separate from the broader EM sleeve ([[SPEM]]).
