#securities #energy

**Chevron securities** — CVX (NYSE). The second-largest US oil major and a core energy-beta name (XLE r ≈ 0.92). The stock round-tripped a 2026 war premium — a record high in March on the [[2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis|Iran-war]] oil spike (+36% in Q1, energy the top S&P sector), then much of it given back on the ceasefire/reopening unwind. CVX is now pivoting from pure oil beta toward [[Chevron#Power production — the AI-electricity pivot (Jun 2026)|merchant power]] (a 20-year [[Microsoft]] electricity PPA). Companion to the [[Chevron]] actor note.
