#securities

Disney securities — NYSE: DIS, the single listed class of [[Disney]] common stock. A ~$200B-market-cap entertainment conglomerate whose tape tracks the [[Bob Iger]]/[[Bob Chapek]]/Iger-return succession arc, the streaming-margin transition, and — since 2025 — political pressure on its [[ABC]] broadcast licences. Trades as a core Industrials name (XLI r = 0.74) despite the media franchise.
