# BNO

BNO (United States Brent Oil Fund LP) is a NYSE Arca exchange-traded commodity pool designed to track daily percentage changes in Brent crude oil using near-month ICE Brent futures, collateral income, and fund expenses.

## Role in BWET comparison

Bloomberg's April 2026 BWET story used BNO as the familiar oil-fund comparator: BNO attracted roughly $720M of net inflows over the same window that [[BWET]] drew only about $25M, even though BWET captured the tanker-rate dislocation much more directly.
