## Investment lens

VOYG is a single-asset equity tied to the success of [[Star Lab]] — Voyager's 67% JV commercial space station targeting 2029 launch on [[SpaceX]] [[Starship]]. The existing services business (~$166M FY2025 revenue, 13% gross margin, -$105M net income) does not throw off cash to fund Star Lab development; the IPO proceeds and follow-on equity issuance are doing the heavy lifting through the development cycle.

Single biggest equity catalyst: [[Commercial LEO Destinations]] Phase 2 selection (NASA, mid-2026). A Star Lab Phase 2 win materially de-risks the development financing case; a loss makes the development cost ($2.8-3.3B through 2029) significantly harder to close.
