## Apr 24 2026 Q1 reaction

SAP's Apr 23 2026 Q1 release showed current cloud backlog of EUR21.93B (+25% cc), cloud revenue of EUR5.96B (+27% cc), Cloud ERP Suite revenue of EUR5.21B (+30% cc), and non-IFRS operating profit of EUR2.87B (+24% cc). The stock's +7.4% Apr 24 reaction was the market repricing the Jan-Feb cloud-growth scare back toward execution.

The risk is that SAP's own filing says Q1 cloud growth benefited from quarter-specific effects and that Q2 cloud growth should decelerate. The 2026 guide also assumes near-term Middle East de-escalation, so this is not a clean macro-isolation story.
