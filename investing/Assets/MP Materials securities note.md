#securities #rareearth

**MP Materials securities** — MP (NYSE). The only US rare-earth miner at scale and the market's primary expression of US rare-earth independence — a high-beta, strategic-premium stock that tracks US-China decoupling headlines more than current earnings. It round-tripped much of its 2026 strategic-premium spike (−12% on Mar 30 2026 to $45.59) and was added to China's entities list in June 2026. Companion to the [[MP Materials]] actor note.
