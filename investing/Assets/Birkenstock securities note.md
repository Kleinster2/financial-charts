#securities

Birkenstock securities note — NYSE: BIRK. Equity-market companion for [[Birkenstock]], the 250-year-old German heritage footwear brand turned global luxury player. The stock trades as a blend of consumer-discretionary cyclical exposure and brand-elevation thesis under [[L Catterton]] / Financière Agache stewardship. ~81% float is locked up at the L Catterton sponsor level — the public float is thin and prone to outsized moves on earnings surprises.
