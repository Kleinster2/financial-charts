#securities #china

**Lingyi iTech securities** — [[Lingyi iTech]] trades as an A+H pair: Shenzhen A-shares (002600, CNY) since 2018 and Hong Kong H-shares (1688.HK, HKD) since 26 June 2026. The HK line was created by a ~US$1.06B primary offering at HK$10.18, priced at the top of the range and at roughly a 44% discount to the A-shares — the cheap pool by construction. Companion to the [[Lingyi iTech]] actor note.
