#securities

CSN securities — NYSE ADR: SID; [[B3]]: CSNA3. Equity-market note for [[CSN]] (Companhia Siderúrgica Nacional), Brazil's largest integrated steel producer and major iron-ore miner. Stock trades as a deep cyclical with double exposure to global steel/iron-ore demand and Brazilian domestic rate / industrial activity cycle.
