#securities #taiwan

**Foxconn securities** — Hon Hai Precision Industry (2317.TW, Taiwan; US OTC ADRs HNHPF / FXCOF). The world's largest electronics manufacturer and ~40% of the global AI-server assembly market — the listed picks-and-shovels play on hyperscaler capex. The stock re-rated through 2024-26 as AI-server (cloud/networking) revenue overtook iPhone assembly. Companion to the [[Foxconn]] actor note.
