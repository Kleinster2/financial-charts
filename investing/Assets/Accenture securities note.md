#securities #it-services

**Accenture securities** — ACN (NYSE). The largest listed [[IT services]] pure-play and the marquee public expression of the [[AI disintermediation]] trade. The stock has derated ~68% from a ~$394 Feb-2025 peak to ~$128 (Jun 18 2026, lowest since 2017) as the market repriced the labor-arbitrage services model for AI — even as revenue kept compounding to $69.7B (FY2025). Anchors the validated [[IT services]] cluster.

The ~68% drawdown from the Feb-2025 peak is the [[AI disintermediation]] rerating: the multiple compressed as investors priced AI eating the implementation/consulting wallet, while the fundamentals (see [[Accenture#Financials]]) kept growing. The Jun 18 print was the inflection — a booked guidance cut, not just sentiment.
