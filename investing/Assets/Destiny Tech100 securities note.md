[[Destiny Tech100]] common stock — publicly traded closed-end fund on [[NYSE]] under ticker DXYZ. Provides retail access to top venture-backed private tech companies including [[SpaceX]] (largest position at 16.2% of fund AUM), [[OpenAI]], [[Anthropic]], [[Stripe]], [[Revolut]], [[Discord]].

Listed Mar 26, 2024 at $9, spiked to $105 within two weeks on retail frenzy, then crashed. Has remained one of the most volatile listed securities in the retail-private-equity proxy space. The May 8 to May 12 2026 arc ($54.60 → $71.24 → $53.40) captured both the SpaceX-IPO-fever rally peak and the Anthropic/OpenAI void-declaration drawdown — see [[Destiny Tech100#May 8-11, 2026 — SpaceX IPO buzz|May 8-11 SpaceX IPO buzz]] and [[Destiny Tech100#May 11-12 epilogue|May 11-12 epilogue]] for the full trade structure.
