#securities

BlackSky securities — NYSE: BKSY. Equity-market note for [[BlackSky]], whose common stock is a small-cap, high-volatility [[Space pure-plays]] member levered to government geospatial-intelligence demand — the [[NGA]] [[NGA EOCL contract|EOCL]] commercial-imagery cycle and the Gen-3 constellation. ## Trading character

BKSY is among the smaller, more volatile cohort members — idiosyncratic vol ~100%, PC1 loading 0.378. Its tape is dominated by single-name earnings binaries (the -25% May 7 Q1 miss), government-contract-award catalysts ([[NGA EOCL contract|EOCL]], [[Golden Dome]] adjacency), the space-cohort factor (it co-moves with [[Rocket Lab]], [[Intuitive Machines]], [[Spire Global]], and [[Planet Labs]]), and SpaceX-IPO proxy flow ([[SpaceX debut proxy unwind]]). The market increasingly differentiates the optical-imagery operators (BKSY, [[Planet Labs]]) on annual-cycle commercial-imagery weakness from the RF-SIGINT names ([[HawkEye 360]], [[Spire Global]]) on multi-year government IDIQ tailwinds. The business and strategy live in [[BlackSky]]; this note carries the instrument and price record.
