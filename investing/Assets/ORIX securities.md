## Price history

ORIX is best tracked against Japanese megabank ADRs because the U.S. line gives a clean, same-currency comparison. The local chart shows IX lagging [[SMBC|SMFG]], [[Mizuho|MFG]], and [[MUFG]] from 2024 through April 2026 despite ORIX's more diversified business mix.
