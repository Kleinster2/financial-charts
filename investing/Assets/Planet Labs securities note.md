## Trading character

PL is the cohort's lowest-PC1-loading member (0.298) — the most idiosyncratic, least factor-driven name, because its constellation-data revenue cycle ([[NGA]] commercial-imagery cadence, agricultural and insurance subscriptions) decouples from the launch/lunar/[[Golden Dome]] catalyst chain that drives the basket. It still participates in space-cohort re-ratings and SpaceX-IPO proxy flow ([[SpaceX debut proxy unwind]]), but with more of its variance explained by single-name fundamentals than its peers. The optical-imagery cyclicality it shares with [[BlackSky]] contrasts with the RF-SIGINT tailwind at [[Spire Global]] / [[HawkEye 360]]. Business and strategy live in [[Planet Labs]]; this note carries the instrument and price record.
