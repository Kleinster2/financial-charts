## Securities read

CORZ is no longer a clean bitcoin-mining equity. The security now prices a three-part stack: legacy digital-asset mining volatility, the [[CoreWeave]] anchor contract, and the option value of turning 4.5 GW of gross power pipeline into billable high-density colocation capacity.

The May 2026 $3.3B senior-secured note raise shifts the equity question from survival to conversion speed. If the company signs hyperscale demand fast enough, leverage amplifies the AI-infrastructure upside. If contracted MW lags, the equity sits behind a large secured-debt claim before the colocation platform has fully seasoned.
