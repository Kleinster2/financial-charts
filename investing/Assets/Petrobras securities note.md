#securities

Petrobras securities — NYSE ADR: PBR / PBR.A; [[B3]]: PETR3 / PETR4. Equity-market note for [[Petrobras]], whose shares trade as a blend of world-class pre-salt deepwater asset value, Brazilian sovereign / political-cycle risk discount, and one of the highest dividend yields among global supermajors.
