#securities

Powerlaw Corp securities — Nasdaq: PWRL, the listed shares of the [[Powerlaw Corp]] closed-end fund (direct listing May 27, 2026). The share is a wrapper on a book of private AI/defense/space holdings ([[SpaceX]], [[OpenAI]], [[Anthropic]], [[Anduril]]); it trades on the premium/discount to a monthly-struck NAV and on the lock-up release schedule, not on operating fundamentals.

## Premium/discount dynamics

Closed-end funds typically trade at a discount to NAV; the retail private-access vehicles ([[Destiny Tech100|DXYZ]], PWRL) have been the exception, trading at premiums on scarcity demand for [[OpenAI]]/[[SpaceX]] exposure. PWRL's premium has compressed faster than DXYZ's (which has held nearer ~40%), consistent with its larger free float from the staged lock-up releases and its heavier 3.63% fee load. The premium is the key risk in the security: it can mean-revert toward NAV — or to a discount — independent of how the underlying holdings are marked.
