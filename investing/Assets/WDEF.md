# WDEF

[[WisdomTree]] Europe Defense Fund. Passively tracks the WisdomTree Europe Defence Index, selecting developed European companies deriving at least 10% of revenue from defense-related activities. Launched March 2025 (UCITS) and July 2025 (US-listed). The UCITS version became the fastest equity ETF in European history to reach $3B AUM (~3.5 months), hitting $5.3B by March 2026. The US-listed version has ~$85M AUM.

## Index methodology

The WisdomTree Europe Defence Index applies:
- Universe: companies domiciled and listed in developed European markets with 10%+ defense revenue (military hardware, subsystems, electronics, propulsion, space-defense)
- NPT requirement: primary business in a Non-Proliferation Treaty signatory country
- Exposure-score weighting: 3x multiplier for >50% defense revenue, 2x for 25-50%, 1x for 10-25%
- Minimum $300M free-float market cap, $2M 3-month median daily volume
- Controversial weapons exclusion (cluster munitions, chemical/biological, depleted uranium, white phosphorus)
- 20+ minimum constituents
