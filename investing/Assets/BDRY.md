# BDRY

**BDRY** is the Breakwave Dry Bulk Shipping ETF, issued by [[Breakwave Advisors]]. Sibling product to [[BWET]]. Futures-based ETF providing exposure to dry bulk freight rates via Baltic Dry Index futures (Capesize, Panamax, Supramax weightings). K-1 tax structure.
