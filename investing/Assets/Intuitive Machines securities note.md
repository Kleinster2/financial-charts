#securities

Intuitive Machines securities — Nasdaq: LUNR. Equity-market note for [[Intuitive Machines]], whose Class A common trades as a high-beta, high-idiosyncratic-vol [[Space pure-plays]] member: blended exposure to [[NASA]] [[CLPS]] lunar-lander missions, lunar data-relay and communications backlog, and — since the Jan 2026 [[Lanteris Space Holdings]] acquisition — space hardware and components. It is one of the public proxies that re-rate on the broader space-cohort and [[SpaceX IPO 2026|SpaceX-IPO]] flow, as the [[SpaceX debut proxy unwind]] showed.

## Trading character

LUNR is among the most volatile names in the [[Space pure-plays]] cohort — beta ~3.7, idiosyncratic vol ~115% annualized. Its tape runs on three layers: mission-event binaries (lunar-landing successes and failures), the space-cohort factor (it co-moves with [[Rocket Lab]], [[Redwire]], and [[AST SpaceMobile]] on sector re-ratings), and the SpaceX-IPO scarcity premium (it rallied +15.7% on the May 27 proxy bid and fell -13.12% / -2.16σ on the Jun. 12 [[SpaceX debut proxy unwind]]). The business and strategy live in [[Intuitive Machines]]; this note carries the instrument and price record.
