## Price context

The main 2026 price inflections so far are tied to the collapsed [[Warner Bros Discovery]] transaction and the Q1 2026 earnings reset.

- Feb 27, 2026: NFLX rallied +13.8% after Netflix walked away from the WBD bid and collected a $2.8B breakup fee.
- Apr 2026: after Q1 results, the market focused less on the headline beat and more on unchanged full-year guidance, first-half-weighted content amortization, and [[Reed Hastings]]' planned board exit.

The central market question is no longer whether Netflix is the streaming leader. It is whether pricing, ads, live programming, and tighter cost cadence can keep revenue-per-member growth high as mature-market subscriber growth slows.
