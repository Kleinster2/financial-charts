# Roblox securities

RBLX is [[Roblox]]'s NYSE-listed common equity. The stock prices a high-growth user-generated gaming platform whose valuation depends on DAU durability, bookings growth, creator-economy economics, and whether safety changes protect the platform without compressing engagement.
