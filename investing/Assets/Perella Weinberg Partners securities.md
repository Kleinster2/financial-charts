# Perella Weinberg Partners securities

Securities note for [[Perella Weinberg Partners]]. Listed on [[NASDAQ]] under ticker PWP since June 2021 via merger with FinTech Acquisition Corp IV, a Betsy Cohen-sponsored [[SPAC]]. Class A common stock is the primary traded security; dual-class structure with Class B shares held by partners that convert on sale.
