## Price context

![[psky-price-chart.png]]
*PSKY/PARA price history since Jan 2024. The stock has traded less like a normal media equity and more like a financing vehicle for the [[Warner Bros Discovery]] acquisition since the [[Skydance Media]] merger closed.*

The live market question is whether the $31/share WBD acquisition can close without the combined debt load overwhelming the promised $6B+ synergy plan. The Apr 2026 almost-$24B Gulf sovereign financing package, led by [[Saudi PIF]], reduces Ellison/RedBird equity burden but adds political and regulatory optics to the merger-arb spread.
