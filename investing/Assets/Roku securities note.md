#securities

Roku securities — Nasdaq: ROKU (Class A common; founder [[Anthony Wood]] holds super-voting Class B). Equity-market note for [[Roku]]. As of June 2026 the shares trade as a [[Merger Arbitrage|merger-arb]] instrument: pending acquisition by [[Fox Corporation]] at $160/share ($96 cash + 0.9693 FOXA), the price tracks the deal spread and the floating value of the Fox stock leg rather than Roku's standalone fundamentals.
