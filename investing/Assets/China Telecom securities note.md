---

## ADR delisting (2021)

China Telecom's ADR (CHA) was removed from the NYSE in 2021 under [[Executive Order 13959]] (the November 2020 order barring US persons from holding designated "Communist Chinese military companies"), alongside [[China Mobile]] (CHL) and [[China Unicom]] (CHU). NSCC clearing was withdrawn as of January 11, 2021. See [[Executive Order 13959]] for the framework.
