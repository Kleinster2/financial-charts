# ARC Resources securities

Tradeable instruments of [[ARC Resources]] — Calgary-based Canadian oil and gas producer; the largest pure-play [[Montney]] operator. Issuer profile lives in [[ARC Resources]] (Actors); this note tracks securities-side market data.
