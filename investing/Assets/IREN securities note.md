## Securities read

IREN is no longer a clean Bitcoin-miner equity. The stock prices a three-layer conversion: legacy mining infrastructure, contracted AI cloud revenue, and the balance-sheet risk of financing Blackwell-class GPU deployments before revenue fully ramps.

The May 2026 sequence sharpened the security. The upside case is that IREN has moved beyond powered-land optionality: Microsoft and NVIDIA have become demand anchors, Dell is supplying the hardware, and NVIDIA has a potential $2.1B investment right. The downside case is that equity holders sit behind a fast-growing procurement and financing stack. The May 26 Dell agreement is payable after shipment tranches, while the ARR target depends on early-2027 commissioning and utilization.

May 26 tape: IREN screened +5.2% on the Dell/Blackwell headline, but the broader crypto-to-AI cohort was also bid. Treat the move as confirmation of IREN's idiosyncratic capacity factor, not as proof that the whole funding and commissioning gap has been solved.
