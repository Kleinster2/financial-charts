#securities

Lionsgate securities — NYSE: LION. Equity-market note for [[Lionsgate]], whose single-class shares trade as a leveraged pure-play studio exposure: a ~$1B/yr library annuity plus a volatile theatrical/TV slate, against ~$1.6B net debt and a standing M&A-takeout option.
