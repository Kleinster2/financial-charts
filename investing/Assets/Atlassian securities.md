# Atlassian securities

TEAM is [[Atlassian]]'s Nasdaq-listed common equity. The stock prices the tension between Atlassian's entrenched developer-workflow distribution and the risk that AI-native tools compress traditional seat-based SaaS economics.

## Earnings reactions

| Event | Date | Key numbers | Stock reaction |
|-------|------|-------------|----------------|
| Q3 FY2026 | Apr 30 / May 1 2026 | Revenue $1.79B (+32%), Cloud +29%, Data Center +44%, non-GAAP EPS $1.75 vs $1.05 consensus, FY2026 revenue guide raised to ~24% growth | TEAM closed $88.88 on May 1, +29.6% from Apr 30 |

The May 2026 move matters because it turned the March layoff story inside out. In March, "self-funding AI" looked like cost cutting under an AI banner. After the Q3 print, the same framing became operating leverage plus distribution: Rovo rides inside Jira and Confluence, while [[Gemini]] supplies the model layer. The question for the stock is whether that converts to durable ARR expansion or just a one-quarter relief rally from a deeply oversold base.
