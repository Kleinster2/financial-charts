#securities

Fox Corporation securities — Nasdaq: FOXA (Class A) and FOX (Class B, super-voting), the two listed classes of [[Fox Corporation]]. The Murdoch Family Trust holds ~39% of the Class B vote, giving the family control (see [[Lachlan Murdoch]]). Class A (FOXA) is the more liquid line and the currency of the [[Roku]] acquisition (0.9693 FOXA per Roku share). The shares trade on news/sports advertising and affiliate economics, the durability of [[Fox News]], and — since June 2026 — the market's read on the Roku deal's debt and dilution.
