## Price framing

Quectel is a classic case where the stock cannot be read purely through earnings momentum. The company recovered sharply in 2024 at the operating level, but the market now also has to price U.S. procurement and regulatory risk around embedded modules. That makes the equity a mix of China hardware cyclicality, IoT demand, and geopolitical discounting.
