# WDGF

[[WisdomTree]] Global Defense Fund. Passively tracks the WisdomTree Global Defense Index, selecting companies globally with at least 25% of revenue from defense-related activities -- a stricter threshold than [[WDEF]] (10%) and [[WDAF]] (10%). The index uses a macro+momentum scoring system to allocate across US, European, and other regional defense markets. 93 holdings spanning 15+ countries.

## Index methodology

The WisdomTree Global Defense Index differs from the regional products:

- Revenue threshold: 25%+ from defense (vs 10% for WDEF/WDAF) -- filters out commercial aerospace dilution
- Exposure scoring: Score 2 (>50% defense revenue, capped at 5%), Score 1 (25-50%, capped at 2.5%)
- 80%+ of index weight in companies with >50% defense revenue, vs ~51% for MSCI ACWI Aerospace & Defense
- Regional allocation via macro score (defense budgets, geopolitical threats, R&D intensity) + momentum score
- Regional caps at 70%, floors at 10%
- Minimum $200M free-float market cap, $1M daily volume
- Controversial weapons exclusion
- 20-100 constituents, quarterly rebalance
