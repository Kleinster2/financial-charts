# Lazard securities

LAZ is [[Lazard]]'s [[NYSE]]-listed common equity. The stock is the public-market expression of the [[Peter Orszag]]-era pivot from cyclical M&A advisory toward private capital advisory ([[Campbell Lutyens]] / Lazard CL) and active asset management. Q1 2026 was the cleanest quarter to date showing the segment mix shift in real time — Asset Management inflected to its best quarter in nearly two decades while Financial Advisory drag matched the sector-wide deal-cycle slippage.
