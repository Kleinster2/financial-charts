# UPAR ETF

**UPAR** is the ARIS Ultra Risk Parity ETF, the leveraged (~1.8x target) version of [[RPAR ETF|RPAR]]. Sub-advised by ARIS. Launched 2022. Same systematic risk parity methodology as RPAR but with leverage applied via futures and swaps to amplify returns. Like RPAR, carries 0% TIPS and ~27% commodity allocation.
