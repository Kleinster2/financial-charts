## Securities read

MRVL is the public equity expression of Marvell's move from generic networking semiconductor exposure into the custom-silicon and optical-interconnect layer of AI infrastructure. The May 27, 2026 Q1 FY2027 print tightened that story: data center revenue reached $1.833B, roughly 76% of total revenue, and management raised the forward revenue path on AI-related bookings.

The security is still not a clean [[AI capex chain basket]] constituent. The latest cluster math keeps Marvell in [[Connectivity]], not the validated supplier-chain basket. That distinction matters: Marvell may be fundamentally converging with [[Broadcom]] as a custom-silicon and networking supplier, but the stock has not yet proven it trades as the same factor as [[NVIDIA]], [[TSMC]], [[Micron]], and the WFE quartet.

---

## Watch Items

- FY2027 data-center revenue growth against management's roughly 50% target.
- FY2028 revenue path toward the new ~$16.5B outlook.
- Custom XPU revenue growth: more than 20% expected in FY2027 and more than double in FY2028.
- Reuters-reported FY2029 custom-chip revenue target above $10B.
- Whether Marvell's returns start clustering with the AI supplier chain rather than the looser [[Connectivity]] cohort.
- Customer concentration across U.S. hyperscaler custom engagements.
