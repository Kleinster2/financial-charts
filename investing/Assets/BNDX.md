# BNDX

**BNDX** is the [[Vanguard]] Total International Bond ETF — USD-hedged exposure to investment-grade bonds outside the US. Tracks the Bloomberg Global Aggregate ex-USD Float Adjusted RIC Capped Index. Used in [[ALLW]] replication as a proxy for international bond futures (Euro-Bund, Gilt, Australian government bonds).
