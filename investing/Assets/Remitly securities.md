# Remitly securities

RELY is [[Remitly]]'s NASDAQ-listed common equity. The stock prices the tension between Remitly's first-time-profitable cross-border remit business (FY 2025 $1.635B revenue, +29% YoY, $67.9M net income) and the structural take-rate compression inherent to the corridor — the same [[stablecoin sandwich]] economics that let Remitly route flows more cheaply also let competitors and stablecoin-native challengers do the same.

## Investment frame

The bull case: first profitable year at scale, ~20-21% revenue growth guided for FY26, and a measured stablecoin routing posture that captures rail savings without putting customers into crypto UX they don't want. The bear case: 2.6x sales multiple on a business whose take-rate is structurally compressing along the same path Wei Zhou described for the corridor (8% → 2-4% → 50 bps → 20-30 bps target), and incumbent challenger [[Wise]] plus stablecoin-native competitors building toward end-to-end corridor offerings.
