# Illumina securities

ILMN is [[Illumina]]'s NASDAQ-listed common equity. The stock is the public-market expression of the [[NovaSeq X]] transition thesis: whether the new-platform consumables ramp compounds into a durable margin reset (2027+ FCF visibility), or whether [[China]] tariff exposure and long-read competition compress the multiple toward biotech-tools peers.
