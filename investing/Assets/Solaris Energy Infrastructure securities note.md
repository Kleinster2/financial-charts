**Solaris Energy Infrastructure securities** — SEI (NYSE). A ~$4.7B market cap riding a 9-bagger off the September 2024 power pivot, with 72% annualized volatility, two in-the-money converts totaling $902.5M, a ~28% registration-rights overhang, and a tape that trades on contracts and short reports rather than any peer factor. The market is paying for the contracted-capacity ramp (910 MW → ~3,100 MW guided) while pricing event risk around concentration in [[xAI]].
