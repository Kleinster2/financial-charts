# KARS

The [[KraneShares]] Electric Vehicles & Future Mobility ETF — the most-referenced US-listed pure-play EV/mobility ETF. Tracks the Bloomberg Electric Vehicles Index: a global basket spanning EV makers, battery and raw-material producers, charging/hydrogen, and autonomous/mobility-tech names. Inception Jan 2018; expense ratio 0.70%; a relatively small fund (low-hundreds-of-millions AUM).

Because it is a global supply-chain basket, KARS is led by battery, semiconductor, and materials names — Panasonic, [[CATL]], [[Samsung SDI]], STMicroelectronics — with [[Tesla]] only its #3 position (~4%) and the Chinese EV pure-plays ([[NIO]]/[[Xpeng|XPEV]]/[[Li Auto|LI]]) a minor sleeve ([[BYD]], ~4%, is the main China name). That composition is why, in the [[Chinese EV startups#Cluster validation|China-EV cluster test]], KARS clusters with the broad EV/battery complex and the market rather than with the Chinese-EV cohort: it holds the China names but is ruled by the global supply-chain factor, not the China factor (the [[Vault cluster taxonomy|index-rule law]]).
