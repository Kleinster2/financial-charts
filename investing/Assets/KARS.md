# KARS

The [[KraneShares]] Electric Vehicles & Future Mobility ETF — the most-referenced US-listed pure-play EV/mobility ETF. Tracks the Bloomberg Electric Vehicles Index: a global basket spanning EV makers, battery and raw-material producers, charging/hydrogen, and autonomous/mobility-tech names. Inception Jan 2018; expense ratio 0.70%; a relatively small fund (low-hundreds-of-millions AUM).

Because it is global and market-cap-influenced, KARS is led by [[Tesla]] and the large battery/materials names, with the [[Chinese EV startups|Chinese EV pure-plays]] ([[NIO]]/[[Xpeng|XPEV]]/[[Li Auto|LI]]) and [[BYD]] a substantial but non-dominant China sleeve. That composition is why, in the [[Chinese EV startups#Cluster validation|China-EV cluster test]], KARS clusters with Tesla and the broad market rather than with the Chinese-EV cohort: it holds the China names but is ruled by the global-EV/Tesla factor (the [[Vault cluster taxonomy|index-rule law]]).
