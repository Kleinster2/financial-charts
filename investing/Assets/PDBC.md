# PDBC

**PDBC** is the Invesco Optimum Yield Diversified Commodity Strategy No K-1 ETF, a broad commodity futures ETF designed to provide diversified commodity exposure without issuing a K-1. In this vault it is a liquid, K-1-free commodity proxy rather than a direct [[Bloomberg Commodity Index|BCOM]] tracker.

## Use Case

PDBC is useful when the desired expression is broad commodity beta with a no-K-1 wrapper and roll optimization. It is not official BCOMTR history, but it is one of the better local comparisons for checking whether a BCOM-style allocation is lagging or leading the broader commodity ETF complex.
