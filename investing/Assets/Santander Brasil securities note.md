#securities

Santander Brasil securities — NYSE ADR: BSBR; [[B3]]: SANB11 (units), SANB3 / SANB4 (common / preferred). Equity-market note for [[Santander Brasil]], the Brazilian subsidiary of [[Santander]] group and the country's #5 bank / largest foreign bank. ~30% of [[Santander]]'s global profit comes from the Brazil franchise — making the Brazil-cluster moves a non-trivial input to the parent's consolidated equity story.
