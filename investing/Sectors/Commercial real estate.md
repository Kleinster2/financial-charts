Office buildings, retail, hotels, industrial properties. Facing structural shifts from remote work and e-commerce.

## Key dynamics

- Rising rates crushed valuations 2022-2024
- Office sector distressed — remote work permanent for many
- Industrial/logistics benefiting from e-commerce, reshoring
- Massive debt maturities creating forced sales, refinancing stress
- Regional banks heavily exposed
