## US wireless (Big Three)

| Carrier | Subs | Market cap | Strategy |
|---------|------|------------|----------|
| [[Verizon]] | 90M+ | ~$170B | Premium network, Fios fiber |
| [[AT&T]] | 70M+ | ~$150B | Fiber buildout, debt paydown |
| [[T-Mobile]] | 120M+ | ~$240B | Value/growth, network expansion |

**Market dynamics:**
- Saturated (340M+ total subs vs 330M population)
- ARPU pressure from competition
- 5G investment cycle winding down
- Fixed wireless access (FWA) growth

## 5G evolution

| Phase | Status | Impact |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Sub-6 GHz | Deployed | Broad coverage |
| C-band | Deploying | Capacity boost |
| mmWave | Limited | Dense urban/venues |
| Fixed wireless | Growing | Broadband alternative |

**Investment thesis shift:** 5G capex peak passed, now about monetization.
