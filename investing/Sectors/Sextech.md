# Sextech

#industry #private #consumer

> **Industry, not sector.** No publicly traded pure-plays. All major players are private. This note maps the landscape for context when these companies appear in other investments (PE, creator economy, payments).

## Why no public companies

**1. Payment processing barriers**
- Visa/Mastercard restrict "high-risk" merchants
- Higher processing fees (3-5% vs 1.5-2%)
- Chargeback exposure
- Sudden account termination risk (OnlyFans almost lost Mastercard in 2021)

**2. Banking challenges**
- Many banks won't service adult businesses
- Limits growth capital access
- Forces offshore banking relationships

**3. Advertising restrictions**
- Can't advertise on Google, Meta, TikTok
- Organic/word-of-mouth only
- Limits customer acquisition scaling

**4. Public market stigma**
- ESG screens exclude adult
- Institutional investors avoid
- LELO explored London IPO (2021) — didn't proceed
- OnlyFans exploring private sale instead of IPO

**5. Regulatory complexity**
- Age verification requirements (UK, EU)
- Varying obscenity laws by jurisdiction
- Section 230 liability concerns (US)

## Investment exposure

**Direct investment:** Not possible via public markets. PE firms (CDH, Trustar Capital) own stakes in major players.

**Adjacent plays:**
- **[[Visa]]/[[Mastercard]]** — process payments (small %)
- **Payment processors** — Epoch, CCBill specialize in adult
- **Creator economy platforms** — Patreon (SFW alternative)

**If OnlyFans IPOs:** Would be first major public sextech company. $7-8B valuation discussed. Watch for 2026-2027.

## Key dynamics

**Hardware commoditization:** Chinese manufacturing makes copying easy. Satisfyer disrupted LELO's premium pricing. Only [[Lovense]] has defensible moat (software integrations).

**Platform power:** OnlyFans and Chaturbate control creator access to fans. If they build competing hardware or revoke integrations, hardware companies suffer.

**Creator economy overlap:** OnlyFans competes with Patreon, YouTube, Twitch for creator attention. Adult is just one vertical — fitness, music, cooking creators also use OnlyFans.

**AI disruption:** AI-generated content (deepfakes, AI companions) may disrupt human creator model. Regulatory response unclear.
