Umbrella sector covering residential, commercial, and specialty property. Highly rate-sensitive, cyclical, and fragmented.

| Subsector | Notes | Key dynamics |
|-----------|-------|--------------|
| **Commercial** | [[Commercial real estate]], [[Commercial Mortgage]] | Office distress, industrial strength |
| **Residential** | [[Housing]], [[Residential Mortgage]], [[Multifamily housing]] | Affordability crisis, institutional buyers |
| **REITs** | [[REITs]], [[DC REITs]], [[Mortgage REITs]] | Public market access to RE |
| **Private** | [[Private Real Estate]] | PE/institutional ownership |
| **Specialty** | [[Senior Living]], [[Hotel industry]] | Demographic and travel driven |

## Market structure

| Segment | Public vehicles | Private |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| Residential | Homebuilders (DHI, LEN), apartment REITs | PE SFR portfolios |
| Office | Office REITs (BXP, VNO) | Distressed debt funds |
| Industrial | Prologis (PLD), Duke (DRE) | Blackstone logistics |
| Retail | Simon (SPG), Realty Income (O) | Mall redevelopment |
| Data centers | [[DC REITs]] | Hyperscaler owned |

## Current themes (2025-2026)

- **Office distress** — remote work permanent, conversions accelerating
- **Industrial strength** — e-commerce, reshoring tailwinds
- **Multifamily pressure** — new supply hitting, rent growth slowing
- **Rate sensitivity** — RE trades inversely to rates; Fed pivot key
- **Institutional SFR** — [[Build-to-rent]], institutional homebuying debate
