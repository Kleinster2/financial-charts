# Sensors

**Sensors** — $200B+ market. Image sensors, LiDAR, MEMS, industrial. Sony dominates image; fragmented elsewhere.

---

## Market overview

| Segment | 2025 market | Growth | Leader |
|---------|-------------|--------|--------|
| Image sensors | ~$25B | 7% CAGR | Sony (44%) |
| MEMS | ~$18B | 8% CAGR | Bosch, STMicro |
| Industrial sensors | ~$30B | 6% CAGR | Honeywell, TE Connectivity |
| Automotive sensors | ~$40B | 10% CAGR | Infineon, NXP, ON Semi |
| LiDAR | ~$3B | 20%+ CAGR | Luminar, Hesai, Velodyne |
| IoT/environmental | ~$15B | 12% CAGR | Sensirion, Bosch |

**Total sensor market:** $200B+ (2025), growing to $300B+ by 2030.

---

## Image sensors (CIS)

CMOS Image Sensors — smartphone cameras, automotive, security.

### Market share

| Company | Share | Strength |
|---------|-------|----------|
| **Sony** | ~44% | Premium smartphones, mirrorless cameras |
| Samsung | ~18% | Own devices, mid-tier |
| OmniVision | ~11% | Chinese smartphones, low-cost |
| SK Hynix | ~5% | Emerging |
| Others | ~22% | Fragmented |

### Sony dominance

| Factor | Sony advantage |
|--------|----------------|
| Technology | Stacked sensors, low-light performance |
| Apple relationship | Primary iPhone supplier |
| Automotive | Growing segment |
| R&D | Outspends competitors |

**Sony image sensor revenue:** ~$10B/year (~15% of Sony total).

## Automotive sensors

Fastest growing segment. ADAS, EVs driving demand.

| Type | Use | Key players |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Radar | Adaptive cruise, blind spot | [[Infineon]], NXP, TI |
| LiDAR | Autonomous driving | Luminar, Hesai, Mobileye |
| Image | Cameras, surround view | Sony, ON Semi, OmniVision |
| Ultrasonic | Parking assist | Bosch, Valeo |
| Pressure | Tire, engine | Sensata, TE Connectivity |
| Temperature | Battery, powertrain | Amphenol, Sensata |

**EV multiplier:** EVs use 2-3x more sensors than ICE vehicles.

## LiDAR

Light Detection and Ranging — 3D mapping for autonomous vehicles.

| Company | Status | Focus |
|---------|--------|-------|
| **Luminar** | Public (LAZR) | Automotive OEM partnerships |
| **Hesai** | Public (HSAI) | China leader, robotaxis |
| **Mobileye** | Intel subsidiary | Camera + radar + LiDAR |
| Velodyne/Ouster | Merged | Industrial, mapping |
| Innoviz | Public (INVZ) | Solid-state |

**Challenge:** Cost reduction needed for mass adoption. Camera-only (Tesla) vs sensor fusion debate ongoing.

## Geopolitical relevance

### China-Japan ban (Jan 2026)

China banned exports of dual-use equipment to Japan including **sensors**. Part of Takaichi retaliation.

See [[China-Japan relations]], [[Critical minerals]].

### Supply chain

| Risk | Impact |
|------|--------|
| China MEMS capacity | Growing domestic supply |
| Japan materials | Sensor substrates, specialty chemicals |
| Taiwan packaging | OSATs critical for sensor modules |
