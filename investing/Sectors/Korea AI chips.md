## National chip drive (Jun 2026)

On Jun 29, 2026, President [[Lee Jae Myung]] unveiled a >$576 billion national AI-chip strategy committing [[Samsung]] and [[SK Hynix]] to 800 trillion won ($518.3 billion) for new southwest fabs, with a goal of doubling [[DRAM]] output within five years and AI data-centre investment targets of 550 trillion won by 2029. The plan also earmarks a robotics cluster at Saemangeum ([[Hyundai]]) and reframes Korea as an AI-infrastructure sovereign at [[CHIPS Act]] scale. The market read it as oversupply risk ([[Samsung]] −4.86%, [[SK Hynix]] −1.68% on Jun 29). Full detail in [[South Korea AI chip investment drive June 2026]].
