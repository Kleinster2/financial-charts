## Why asset managers invest in private tech

| Reason | Explanation |
|--------|-------------|
| **Returns** | Private markets outperforming |
| **Access** | Retail wants private exposure |
| **Holding period** | Companies staying private longer |
| **Relationships** | Crossover to public holdings |
