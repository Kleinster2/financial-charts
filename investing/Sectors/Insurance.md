## Business models

### P&C economics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Combined ratio** | Losses + expenses / premiums (<100% = underwriting profit) |
| **Investment income** | Float invested in bonds/equities |
| **Float** | Premiums held before claims paid |

**Berkshire model:** Underwriting profit + float invested = double benefit.

### Health insurance economics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Medical loss ratio (MLR)** | Claims / premiums (regulated ~85%) |
| **Star ratings** | Medicare Advantage quality (affects reimbursement) |
| **PBM spread** | Drug pricing arbitrage |

## P&C cycle

Insurance is cyclical:

| Phase | Pricing | Profitability |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| **Hard market** | Rising rates | Improving |
| **Soft market** | Competitive pricing | Compressing |

Currently in **hard market** (auto, property) due to inflation + cat losses.
