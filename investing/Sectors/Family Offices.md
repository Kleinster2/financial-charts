## Why hedge funds convert

| Reason | Explanation |
|--------|-------------|
| Size constraints | Hard to deploy at scale |
| Fee pressure | No longer need LPs |
| Flexibility | No investor reporting |
| Tax efficiency | No carried interest issues |
| Privacy | Less disclosure |
