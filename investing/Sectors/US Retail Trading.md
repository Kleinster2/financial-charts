## Why this cluster exists

**Shared characteristics:**
- Retail investor base (not institutional)
- Crypto exposure (direct or adjacent)
- Sentiment-driven trading
- High beta to risk-on/risk-off
- Similar investor base (growth, fintech)

**What makes this different from Payments Networks:**
- V-MA are toll booths (take rate on all transactions)
- This cluster competes for trading activity
- Much higher volatility
- Crypto correlation
