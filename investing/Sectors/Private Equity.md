## PE vs other strategies

| Aspect | PE | VC | Growth |
|--------|-----|-----|--------|
| Control | Majority/control | Minority | Minority |
| Leverage | Yes (debt) | No | No |
| Stage | Mature | Early | Late |
| Hold period | 3-7 years | 7-10 years | 3-5 years |
| Value creation | Operational | Growth | Growth |
