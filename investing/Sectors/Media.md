## Key themes

### [[Cord-cutting]]
Linear TV subscribers declining 5-7% annually. Accelerating shift to streaming.

### [[Streaming wars]]
Profitability becoming focus over subscriber growth. Consolidation likely.

### [[Sports rights]]
Only "must-have" live content. Rights inflation 10-15% annually. Key battleground.

### [[CTV advertising]]
Programmatic TV ads growing 15-20% annually. [[Trade Desk]] (buy), [[Magnite]] (sell) benefit from streaming ad tiers.

### AI applications
- Recommendation engines (Netflix, Spotify)
- Content creation tools (Adobe, studios)
- Personalization (thumbnails, playlists)
- Operations (Disney parks)

## Investment considerations

**Bull:**
- Streaming reaching profitability
- Sports rights = defensible moat
- Gaming = secular growth
- Consolidation = M&A premiums

**Bear:**
- Linear decline accelerating
- Content cost inflation
- Subscriber saturation (developed markets)
- Competition from tech giants (Apple, Amazon, Google)
