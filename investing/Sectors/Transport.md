# Transport

Overview of transportation sector: railroads, airlines, logistics, and shipping.

---

## Market segments

| Segment | Key players | Dynamics |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Railroads** | [[Union Pacific]], [[BNSF]], CSX, Norfolk Southern | Duopolies, moats |
| **Airlines** | Delta, United, American, Southwest | Cyclical, consolidation |

## Railroads

### US Class I railroads

| Railroad | Region | Owner |
|----------|--------|-------|
| [[Union Pacific]] | Western | Public (UNP) |
| [[BNSF]] | Western | [[Berkshire Hathaway]] |
| CSX | Eastern | Public (CSX) |
| Norfolk Southern | Eastern | Public (NSC) |
| Canadian National | Canada + US | Public (CNI) |
| Canadian Pacific Kansas City | Canada + US + Mexico | Public (CP) |

**Dynamics:**
- Regional duopolies = pricing power
- Infrastructure moats (can't replicate)
- Intermodal growth (e-commerce)
- Coal secular decline

### Investment case

| Bull | Bear |
|------|------|
| Infrastructure moat | Economic sensitivity |
| Intermodal tailwind | Coal decline |
| Pricing power | Labor issues |
| Dividend growth | Trucking competition |
