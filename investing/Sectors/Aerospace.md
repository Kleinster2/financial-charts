## Aftermarket vs. OEM

| Segment | Margin | Characteristics |
|---------|--------|-----------------|
| OEM (new aircraft) | Low | Competitive, capex heavy |
| Aftermarket (parts/service) | High | Recurring, sticky |

Engine makers often lose on equipment, win on services.
