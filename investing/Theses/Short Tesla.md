## Kalshi operating milestone overlay (May 20, 2026)

[[Kalshi]]'s Tesla company stack gives a cleaner read on what traders think can still rescue the multiple. The Q2 2026 operating markets price a rebound, not an escape-speed inflection: Q2 deliveries above 440K last traded at 49c and above 450K at 33c, while Q2 production above 420K was 54c and above 440K was 41c. That is a meaningful recovery from the worst bear case, but it is not a return to the 2021-2023 hypergrowth narrative.

The optionality markets are mostly "credible but capped." [[Tesla Energy]] remains the strongest non-auto support: more than 15 GWh deployed in a single quarter before 2027 was 61c, more than 20 GWh was 32c, and more than 50 GWh was only 3c. [[Tesla Semi]] is priced for some launch activity, not scale: more than 0 Semis before 2027 was 98c, more than 1,000 was 54c, and more than 5,000 was 16c. [[Tesla Optimus]] on sale before 2027 was 16c, Roadster deliveries before 2027 were 9c, and a Musk-out CEO-change contract for 2026 was 12c.

Read-through for the short: the market is not dismissing every Tesla bull option, but the prices cluster around partial execution rather than a clean multi-leg acceleration. The useful risk control is energy and Semi scale; the useful bear confirmation would be deliveries/production rolling over while the Optimus / Roadster / cheap-car tails stay below 20c.
