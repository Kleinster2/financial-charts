### Cisco era (19 years)

Rose through engineering and business leadership:
- Led systems and product development (VoIP, mobility, video)
- EVP & GM of Networking and Security Business
- Oversaw **$34B** revenue, **25,000+ engineers**
- Defined Cisco's threat-centric security strategy
- Transitioned security to software/recurring revenue model

**Recognition:** Frost & Sullivan Excellence Award in Engineering Operations (2012)

### Western Digital (2020-2025)

Recruited as CEO in March 2020 — started the week WHO declared COVID a pandemic.

Key accomplishments:
- Led WDC through pandemic supply chain chaos
- Drove strategic separation into WDC (HDD) and SanDisk (flash)
- **Chose to lead SanDisk** over WDC in the split — signal about which business he saw as more valuable

### SanDisk (2025-present)

Leading the spinoff through explosive AI-driven growth:
- Stock **+1700%** since Feb 2025 spinoff
