## Investment thesis

**Bull:**
- Helicopter market leader (civil + military)
- Tempest 6th gen opportunity
- Electronics/radar strength
- European rearmament tailwind
- Italian government support

**Bear:**
- Conglomerate complexity
- Lower margins than peers
- Italian governance concerns
- Competition from larger primes
- Civil aerospace cycle exposure
