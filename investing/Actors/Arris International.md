**Arris International** -- telecom equipment maker specializing in broadband infrastructure, set-top boxes, and network equipment for cable operators. Formed through the 2013 merger of Arris Group and [[Motorola]] Home. Merged with [[CommScope]] in April 2019 for ~$7.4B.
