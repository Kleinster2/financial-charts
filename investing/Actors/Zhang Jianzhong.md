Former Global VP of [[NVIDIA]]'s China operations for 15 years — not just a regional head. Founded [[Moore Threads]] in 2020 to build domestic GPU alternatives after US [[export controls]] restricted access to high-end NVIDIA chips. Named the company to imply "Do More" than NVIDIA allowed. [[Moore Threads]] is now a ~$45B company. Moore Threads produces the MTT S-series GPUs targeting AI training and inference workloads. Added to the US [[Entity List]] in October 2023.

State benefit: Chinese military required to source 100% domestic chips — creating captive demand for Moore Threads GPUs.
