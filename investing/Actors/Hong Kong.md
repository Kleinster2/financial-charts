## Market profile

Hong Kong is the world's 4th largest stock exchange by market cap. Serves as the primary offshore listing venue for Chinese companies. Reclaimed top global IPO hub status in 2025 ($24B raised).

Key sectors: financials, real estate, insurance, exchange services. The market skews toward old economy — [[AIA Group]], [[HKEX]], property developers — rather than tech.

Valuations remain cheap relative to US markets — Hang Seng P/E ~11.5x vs [[S&P 500]] ~22x forward.
