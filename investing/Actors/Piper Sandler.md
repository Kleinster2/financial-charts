**Piper Sandler** (formerly Piper Jaffray) — US middle-market investment bank and institutional securities firm. Minneapolis-based. Notable as the incubator for [[Gene Munster]], [[Doug Clinton]], [[Andrew Murphy]], and Mike Olson, who all left to co-found what became [[Deepwater Asset Management]].
