# Blackstone Mortgage

Blackstone Mortgage Trust (NYSE: BXMT) is a commercial mortgage REIT originating senior floating-rate loans on commercial real estate, externally managed by [[Blackstone]]. Office-heavy and credit-sensitive — a levered CRE-credit vehicle. A member of the [[Mortgage REITs]] cohort (commercial/credit wing). Distinct from its manager [[Blackstone]] (BX, the alt-asset manager).
