## Professional evolution

### Early Career (1994-2001)

Foreign policy adviser to Democratic members of House Foreign Affairs Committee. Traveled to [[North Korea]] (1997) to report on economic and military status.

### Private Sector (2001-2011)

Executive VP International Operations at Cassidy & Associates. Lobbied for oil and gas companies and renewable energy firms. Controversial account with Equatorial Guinea's president Teodoro Obiang.

### Obama Energy Diplomacy (2011-2017)

Joined State Department Bureau of Energy Resources. Deputy Assistant Secretary of State (2011), then Special Envoy and Coordinator for International Energy Affairs (2014-2017). Worked closely with VP Biden on [[Ukraine]] energy security after 2014 Russian occupation of Crimea. Obama nominated him for Assistant Secretary of State for Energy Resources (2015) but Senate did not act.

### Private Energy Executive (2017-2020)

Senior VP Marketing at Tellurian, private Houston-based [[LNG]] company. Resigned September 2020.

### Biden Administration (2021-2025)

Senior Advisor for Energy Security (Aug 2021), then Deputy Assistant to the President and Senior Advisor for Energy and Investment (2022). Led Partnership for Global Infrastructure and Investment. Negotiated [[Israel]]-Lebanon maritime border deal (October 2022). Also served as Special Presidential Coordinator for Global Infrastructure and Energy Security.

### Current Role (March 2025)

Managing Partner at [[TWG Global]], focusing on energy and infrastructure investments to support AI growth and partnerships.
