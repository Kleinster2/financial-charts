#actor #datasource #edtech

**Edutechnica** — independent research and tracking service that publishes annual and semi-annual data on US and Canadian higher-education learning management system market share, by both institution count and student enrollment. Operated by George Kroner. The Edutechnica data set is the most widely cited primary source for North American LMS market share figures in academic, journalistic, and investor research.
