# Cirium

Cirium is the aviation industry's standard analytics platform, tracking global flight schedules, on-time performance, fleet data, and airline operational statistics. A subsidiary of [[RELX]] (the Anglo-Dutch information analytics group that also owns LexisNexis and Elsevier), Cirium was formed in 2019 by merging several RELX aviation data assets including FlightStats, Innovata, and Diio. Its data feeds are embedded in airline operations, airport planning, and travel industry analytics worldwide.

## Business profile

Cirium's value lies in its comprehensive, real-time aviation dataset. It tracks schedules for 900+ airlines, fleet status for 220,000+ aircraft, and publishes the industry-standard on-time performance rankings used by airlines in their marketing. The business sits within [[RELX]]'s Risk division and benefits from the same data-moat economics that characterize RELX's other businesses — once embedded in customer workflows, switching costs are high. Competitors include [[Flightradar24]] (real-time tracking), OAG (schedules), and ch-aviation (fleet data), but none match Cirium's breadth.
