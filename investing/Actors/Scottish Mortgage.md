Scottish Mortgage Investment Trust — [[Baillie Gifford]]-managed investment trust on the [[LSE]] (SMT). FTSE 100 constituent. UK's largest investment trust. GBP 15.2B in total assets. Founded 1909. Mix of public growth stocks and private companies (~35% unlisted after Dec 2025 SpaceX revaluation). AIC Dividend Hero — 43 consecutive annual dividend increases.

## Share buybacks

Massive programme — at least GBP 1B announced Mar 2024:
- Apr 2024–Mar 2025: bought back 210M shares (15.2% of share capital), GBP 1.9B
- Total since programme started: GBP 2.6B+
- Oct 2025 alone: GBP 230M (led all UK investment trusts)

Credited with keeping activists at bay. [[Elliott Management]] took a 5% stake, pressed for shareholder value, made an estimated 30% return, and exited.
