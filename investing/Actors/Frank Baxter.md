# Frank Baxter

CEO of [[Jefferies]] from 1987 to 2000. Took over after [[Boyd Jefferies]]'s resignation following the insider trading guilty plea. Stabilized the firm through 15% cost cuts and aggressive hiring from the collapsed [[Drexel Burnham Lambert]] (60 bankers/traders in 1990). Transformed Jefferies from a pure equity trading shop into a diversified investment bank. Later served as US Ambassador to [[Uruguay]] (2006-2009).
