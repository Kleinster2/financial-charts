#actor #company #public #finance #broker-dealer

Dominari Holdings (Nasdaq: DOMH) — Trump Tower-headquartered financial group whose broker-dealer subsidiary [[Dominari Securities]] underwrites small-cap IPOs and acts as financial adviser on reverse mergers, and whose 90%-owned [[American Ventures]] subsidiary operates the SPV factory that recapitalizes those listings. [[Donald Trump Jr.]] and [[Eric Trump]] each own ~6.28% of Dominari (~12.56% combined) and joined the firm's advisory board in late 2024. Dominari's name is Latin for *"to dominate."*

## Synopsis

Dominari is the operator of the canonical [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]] — a sequence in which the same firm underwrites a small-cap shell IPO, recapitalizes it through American Ventures, and merges a chosen private operating company into the listed shell. Five marquee deals run through this pipeline as of May 2026: [[Tron]] via [[SRM Entertainment]] (closed July 2025), [[XTEND]] via [[JFB Construction Holdings]] ($1.5bn, pending), [[Powerus]] via [[Aureus Greenway Holdings]] (PUSA, pending), [[Cove Kaz Capital]] via [[Skyline Builders]] (Kazakh tungsten, $1.6bn EXIM/DFC support, announced Apr 30, 2026), and the [[Dogehash Technologies]] / [[Thumzup Media]] data-center pivot. The firm's connection to the Trump family — both as ~12% shareholder and as advisory-board members — is the marketing wedge; the structural fee capture across the pipeline is the business model.

The firm faces two concurrent narratives. On the upside, [[Donald Trump]]-administration sectors (drones, AI, mining, crypto, nuclear) populate American Ventures' SPV book at the same moment the administration directs procurement and federal financing toward those sectors. On the downside, the [[House Select Committee on China]] sent CEO [[Kyle Wool]] a March 8, 2026 letter requesting information on Chinese small-cap IPOs Dominari underwrote — 11 of 29 such issuers since 2020 fit the committee's "ramp-and-dump" profile, exceeding 30% of the firm's Chinese-IPO book. Dominari has said it intends to cooperate, noting that IPO revenue has been less than 10% of total revenue since the firm's 2023 reorganization.

## The reverse-merger pipeline

Dominari's principal product as of 2025–2026 is the [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]]. The five-stage mechanic is documented in detail there. Dominari's specific role:

| Stage | Dominari role |
|-------|---------------|
| Shell IPO | [[Dominari Securities]] is lead underwriter |
| Recap | [[American Ventures]] raises capital from family offices and HNW investors |
| Reverse merger | [[Dominari Securities]] advises on the combination |
| PIPE at close | [[Dominari Securities]] places the PIPE |
| Aftermarket | Ongoing investment-banking relationship with the merged entity |

Per the firm's own framing to the FT: *"We collaborate with the public entity to clean up the ownership table and eliminate toxic debt or other similar issues that might impact shareholder value. We then fund the vehicle and initiate the process of identifying compelling private American companies to merge into the public entity."*

A person familiar with American Ventures: *"American Ventures pivots towards where the puck is going... We have great vehicles that can go in any direction."*

The brothers have separately contributed to most of American Ventures' SPV vehicles as anchor investors. Per the FT, an investor who worked closely with the firm on a recent merger: *"Eric Trump and Don Jr are anchor investors in most of [American Ventures'] deals. The brothers also befriended [[Kyle Wool]] in 2024; Wool has framed Dominari's success as owing to *"superior products in a superior structure created by me"* (FT, 2025).

## House China Committee inquiry

On March 8, 2026, Reps. [[John Moolenaar]] (R-MI, chair) and [[Ro Khanna]] (D-CA, ranking) of the [[House Select Committee on China]] sent a letter to [[Kyle Wool]], CEO of Dominari Securities, requesting information about the firm's underwriting of Chinese small-cap IPOs implicated in suspected "ramp-and-dump" stock manipulation. Letters were also sent to [[Revere Securities]] and [[D Boral Capital]] (formerly EF Hutton; the firm that took [[Trump Media]] public via the Digital World Acquisition Corp [[SPAC]]).

| Disclosure | Figure |
|------------|--------|
| Chinese-issuer IPOs underwritten by Dominari since 2020 | 29 |
| Of those that fit committee's "ramp-and-dump" profile | 11 (>30%) |
| [[Skyline Builders]] single-session decline (July 2024) | ~90% |
| Cross-broker pattern: U.S. retail wealth extracted via ramp-and-dump (since 2023) | ~$16bn (Bloomberg estimate) |
| DOJ criminal indictments | 10 individuals linked to the PRC |
| DOJ proceeds seized | >$214M |
| DOJ forfeiture | $37.9M |

Dominari's response (March 2026 letter to shareholders and clients): the firm intends to cooperate; since the 2023 reorganization, IPO revenue has been less than 10% of total revenue and Chinese-IPO revenue is a fraction of that share. The firm separately won approval in March 2026 to act as lead or principal underwriter on the [[NYSE]] — an upgrade from its prior Nasdaq-only mandate.

## Risks

- [[SEC]] reclassification of underwritten-shell-then-merger as de facto SPAC (would impose January 2024 SPAC liability rules and forward-looking-projection limits)
- House China Committee escalation; broker-dealer license at risk if the ramp-and-dump profile expands beyond the China book
- Trump premium reversal if administration changes or family political profile recedes
- Counterparty crossover risk: e.g., [[Justin Sun]] simultaneously a [[World Liberty Financial]] investor and the operating principal on Tron/SRM, with a public dispute against the WLF camp ongoing as of May 2026
