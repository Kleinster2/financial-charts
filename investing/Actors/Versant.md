## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Standalone focus on cable turnaround
- Digital assets (Fandango, Rotten Tomatoes)
- MS NOW DTC launch potential
- Undervalued at spin (typical spinoff discount)
- Potential M&A target

**Bear:**
- Structural decline (cord-cutting)
- Revenue falling annually
- No moat vs streaming
- Day 1 stock drop (-13%)
- No obvious path to growth
