# Infinera

Former coherent optical networking company that was acquired by [[Nokia]] in 2025. Prior to the acquisition, Infinera was a public company focused on optical transport solutions for telecommunications and data center applications.

## Strategic significance

The Nokia acquisition of Infinera represents a consolidation play in the optical networking market. Nokia gained both silicon photonics technology capabilities and a global telecommunications infrastructure sales network, positioning it to compete with [[Cisco]] for next-generation optical network market leadership.

## Post-acquisition

As part of Nokia, Infinera's technology and customer relationships strengthen Nokia's optical transport portfolio beyond traditional RAN (Radio Access Network) equipment. The combined entity leverages Infinera's coherent optical expertise within Nokia's broader telecommunications infrastructure platform.
