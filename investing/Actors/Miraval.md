# Miraval

Luxury all-inclusive wellness resort brand founded 1995 in Tucson, Arizona. Acquired by [[Hyatt]] in Jan 2017 for $375M (from [[KSL Capital Partners]]). Three US resorts: Arizona (original, Tucson), Austin (2018), Berkshires (Lenox MA, 2020). Fourth property opening 2026 at The Red Sea, Saudi Arabia. Adults-only, digital device-free. Part of World of Hyatt loyalty program. Previous owners include [[Revolution LLC]] (Steve Case's firm, 2005) and KSL Capital (2014). Emphasis on mindfulness, "life in balance" philosophy. Est. $25–100M revenue, ~250–500 employees.
