## Q1 FY2027 earnings and CEO transition (May 28, 2026)

Q1 FY2027 was the first clean positive-comps print after the post-pandemic consumer-electronics normalization. Enterprise revenue was $8.936B (+1.9% YoY), enterprise comparable sales rose 2.0%, domestic comps rose 1.8%, international comps rose 4.7%, adjusted operating margin expanded to 4.1% from 3.8%, and adjusted EPS was $1.28 (+11% YoY). Management reiterated FY2027 guidance of $41.2-42.1B revenue, -1% to +1% comps, and $6.30-6.60 adjusted EPS.

The qualitative surprise was leadership timing: Corie Barry said she will step down as CEO later in 2026, with Jason Bonfig becoming CEO effective November 1, 2026. The market treated the print as evidence that the AI-PC / gaming / mobile refresh and Best Buy Ads + Marketplace initiatives are finally offsetting the stale electronics cycle.

Market read: BBY closed +15.8% on May 28; using the local beta/volatility profile through May 27, the move screens around a +7 sigma beta-adjusted event.
