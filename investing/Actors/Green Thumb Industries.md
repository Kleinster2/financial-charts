# Green Thumb Industries

The largest US cannabis multistate operator by revenue — a Chicago-based, vertically integrated operator with cultivation, manufacturing, and the RISE dispensary retail chain across more than a dozen states. Among the US MSOs it is the consistent profitability leader (positive operating cash flow, comparatively low leverage), which makes it the lowest-vol, highest-quality name in the [[Cannabis]] cohort and the cohort's primary. Like all US MSOs it is federally illegal under [[Cannabis rescheduling|Schedule I]], taxed under [[280E]], and listed OTC/on the Canadian Securities Exchange rather than a US exchange. The US MSO core ([[Green Thumb Industries|GTBIF]] + [[Cresco Labs|CRLBF]] + [[Curaleaf|CURLF]]) trades at a 0.84 sub-intra correlation and collapses into [[MSOS]].
