# Huntsman

**Huntsman** Corporation (NYSE: HUN) is a US-based global manufacturer of differentiated chemical products. Major producer of MDI (methylene diphenyl diisocyanate) and TDI (toluene diisocyanate) for polyurethane applications -- insulation, automotive, footwear. Significant chlorine consumer through isocyanate production.
