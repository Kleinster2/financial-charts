## Why DFO matters

Michael Dell's personal investment machine:

- **Scale**: $17B+ profits over 20+ years
- **Dell deals**: Financed take-private ($24B) and EMC merger ($67B)
- **Diversification**: Real estate, private equity, credit beyond Dell stock
- **BDT merger**: MSD Partners merged with Byron Trott's BDT & Company (2023)
