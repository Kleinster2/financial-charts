# Tata Chemicals

**Tata Chemicals** is India's largest chlor-alkali producer with 875 kt NaOH capacity at its Mithapur, Gujarat complex, representing ~34% of India's total capacity. Part of [[Tata Group]]. Also produces soda ash, sodium bicarbonate, and specialty chemicals. Growing into new energy materials and agri-sciences.
