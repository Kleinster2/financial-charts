**Kirk Kerkorian** (d. June 15, 2015, age 98) — Armenian-American billionaire investor. Founded [[MGM Resorts]] (via Tracinda Corporation). Owned Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer film studio. Three-time builder of world's largest hotel in Las Vegas. Net worth ~$4B at death.
