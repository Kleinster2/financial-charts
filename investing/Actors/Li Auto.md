## Apr 2026 automotive AI context

[[Reuters]] named Li Auto among the Chinese EV makers designing their own chips to reduce reliance on [[NVIDIA]]. In the [[Automotive AI]] race, that places Li Auto in the same verticalization pattern as [[Xpeng]], [[BYD]], [[Geely]], [[Leapmotor]], and [[NIO]]: cars increasingly require company-specific silicon, operating-system integration, and smart-driving AI rather than off-the-shelf supplier stacks alone.
