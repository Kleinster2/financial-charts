Federal Farm Credit Banks Funding Corporation -- the fiscal and disclosure agent for the [[Farm Credit System]]. Issues and markets debt securities on behalf of the four FCS banks ([[CoBank]], AgriBank, [[AgFirst Farm Credit Bank]], [[Farm Credit Bank of Texas]]).

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Location | New York metro area |

| Owned by | The four FCS banks |
| Role | Issues Systemwide Debt Securities to fund FCS lending |
| Debt outstanding (2024) | ~$448B |
| Ratings | AA+ (S&P), Aa1 (Moody's), AA+ (Fitch) |

All four FCS banks are jointly and severally liable for securities issued by the Funding Corporation.
