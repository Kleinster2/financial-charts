**Anthony Levandowski** — Co-founder of Google's self-driving car program (later [[Waymo]]). Founded Otto (autonomous trucking, acquired by [[Uber]] 2016), then [[Pronto]] (autonomous mining, being acquired by [[Atoms]] 2026). Convicted of trade secret theft (downloaded 14,000+ files from Google), sentenced to 18 months, pardoned by [[Donald Trump]] on his last day in office (Jan 20, 2021). Also founded Way of the Future, a religious organization for AI worship.

## Career timeline

- **2004–2005:** UC Berkeley team built Ghostrider, an autonomous motorcycle for DARPA Grand Challenges.
- **2007:** Co-founded 510 Systems (with Pierre-Yves Droz and Andrew Schultz). Created mobile mapping equipment used for Google Street View.
- **2009–2016:** Co-founded Google's self-driving car program (later [[Waymo]]). Technical lead. Google acquired 510 Systems and Anthony's Robots for ~$20M (2011).
- **Jan 2016:** Founded Otto (autonomous trucking) with Lior Ron. Before leaving Google, allegedly downloaded 9.7 GB of [[Waymo]] confidential files.
- **Aug 2016:** [[Uber]] acquired Otto at $680M reported valuation (effective payout possibly as low as $220M). Granted 5.31M [[Uber]] shares (~$250M), none vested before firing.
- **Apr 2017:** Removed as head of [[Uber]] Advanced Technologies Group amid [[Waymo]] lawsuit.
- **Feb 2018:** [[Waymo]] v. [[Uber]] settled — [[Uber]] paid 0.34% equity (~$245M), agreed not to use the technology.
- **2019:** Ordered to pay $179M to Google in separate arbitration. Filed Chapter 11 bankruptcy.
- **Aug 2019:** Indicted on 33 federal counts of trade secret theft.
- **Mar 2020:** Pled guilty to one count.
- **Aug 2020:** Sentenced to 18 months, $756,499 restitution, $95,000 fine. Judge Alsup: "This is the biggest trade secret crime I have ever seen."
- **Jan 20, 2021:** Full presidential pardon from [[Donald Trump]] (first term, last day). Supported by Peter Thiel and Palmer Luckey.
- **2018–present:** Co-founded [[Pronto]] with Ognen Stojanovski. Pivoted from on-road trucking to off-road mining autonomy (2019). Completed coast-to-coast autonomous drive (2018). Now being acquired by [[Atoms]].

## Way of the Future

Founded 2017 — a religious organization dedicated to worship of AI/superintelligence. Shut down Feb 2021, donated last $172K to NAACP Legal Defense Fund. Rebooted Nov 2023 with "a couple thousand people."
