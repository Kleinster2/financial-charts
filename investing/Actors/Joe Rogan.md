# Joe Rogan

Joe Rogan is the host of The Joe Rogan Experience (JRE), the world's most popular podcast. With 17M+ YouTube subscribers and episodes routinely drawing 10-50M YouTube views, JRE has replaced the long-form interview format (Charlie Rose, Larry King) at 100x the scale. His Trump interview in October 2024 drew 50M+ YouTube views — more than any cable news interview in history, demonstrating the power shift from broadcast to creator-owned distribution.

Rogan signed a $250M multi-year deal with [[Spotify]] in February 2024, but crucially it is non-exclusive — JRE now distributes across [[YouTube]], [[Apple]] Podcasts, and [[Spotify]] simultaneously. This was an upgrade from the original $200M exclusive deal (2020) that pulled JRE off other platforms. The shift back to multi-platform distribution reflects the lesson that exclusivity caps audience growth: Rogan's YouTube viewership surged after the exclusivity window ended. His business model — long-form conversations (2-4 hours), no editing, no advertiser control — is essentially uncopiable at his scale because it depends entirely on personal trust and audience loyalty built over 15+ years and 2,000+ episodes.
