# Rich Handler

CEO of [[Jefferies]] since January 1, 2001 — the longest-tenured CEO on [[Wall Street]]. Born May 23, 1961. Joined Jefferies in April 1990 from [[Drexel Burnham Lambert]], where he worked in [[Michael Milken]]'s high-yield bond department. Owns ~8% of JEF shares (~$837M). Total compensation ~$28M annually (96.5% performance-based). Also became CEO of [[Leucadia National]] upon its 2013 merger with Jefferies. Known for partnership-style compensation philosophy and aggressive talent hiring from distressed competitors.
