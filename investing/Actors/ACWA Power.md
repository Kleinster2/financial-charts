# ACWA Power

[[Saudi Arabia]]-based developer, investor, and operator of power generation and desalinated water plants. 1/3 owner of the $8.4B NEOM Green Hydrogen Complex (with [[Air Products]] and NEOM) — the world's largest green hydrogen/ammonia project, targeting 1.2 Mt/year of green [[Ammonia]] from 2027. Portfolio of 88 projects across the Middle East, Africa, Central Asia, and Southeast Asia.
