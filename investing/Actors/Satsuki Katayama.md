# Satsuki Katayama

Satsuki Katayama is Japan's Finance Minister and the public face of the May 1, 2026 yen FX intervention. Appointed in [[Sanae Takaichi]]'s post-Feb 2026 election cabinet. Her Apr 28 "ready to take bold action within 24 hours" warning, followed by the May 1 ~5.48 trillion yen ($35B) intervention as [[USDJPY]] breached 160.72, established the most explicit BOJ/MOF defense of the yen since the July 2024 episode.

## May 2026 yen intervention sequence

| Date | Event |
|---|---|
| Apr 28 | Public "bold action within 24 hours" warning; instructed reporters to keep smartphones nearby through Golden Week |
| Apr 30 (Japan) | BOJ held rates at 0.75%; FX became the only available defense |
| May 1 (US trading) | [[USDJPY]] breached 160.72; intervention executed; cross fell to ~155.5 |
| May 1 close | USDJPY ~157; Katayama warned of "more action" depending on conditions |
| May 3 | In Samarkand, declined to officially confirm operation per protocol |

The intervention size (~$35B) is just shy of the $36.8B July 2024 reference. See [[BOJ]] for the structural-pressure context.
