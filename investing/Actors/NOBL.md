## What It Tracks

The S&P 500 Dividend Aristocrats Index — at least 25 straight years of dividend increases, equal-weighted and sector-capped. The screen skews toward quality, defensive, cash-generative names (consumer staples, industrials, healthcare) and away from non-payers like big tech, so it lags in momentum markets and cushions in drawdowns.

## Use Case

A quality / dividend-growth tilt and a defensive complement to a cap-weighted core. This is the instrument behind the advice to replace Brazilian FII income with US dividend-aristocrat exposure — durable, growing payouts from stable companies, far more liquid than a property fund.
