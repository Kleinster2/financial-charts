#actor #chef #restaurateur #hospitality #nyc

**Daniel Boulud** — French-born chef and restaurateur, one of [[New York City]]'s most influential figures in fine dining. Opened Restaurant Daniel in 1993, where [[Riad Nasr]] and [[Lee Hanson]] first met and trained. Operates the Dinex Group with restaurants across NYC, Miami, Toronto, Dubai, and other cities. Multiple James Beard Awards including Outstanding Chef (1994) and Outstanding Restaurateur (2006).
