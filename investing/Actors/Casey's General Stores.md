# Casey's General Stores

Casey's General Stores is a large US convenience-store and prepared-food retailer. It operates about 2,900 convenience stores, making it the third-largest US convenience-store retailer and the fifth-largest US pizza chain. For the AI-infrastructure map, Casey's matters as a real-world, high-volume deployment site for [[SoundHound]] voice ordering agents.

## Apr 2026 SoundHound rollout

On Apr 23, 2026, [[SoundHound]] announced that Casey's renewed and expanded its AI ordering-agent partnership. SoundHound agents had handled more than 21M Casey's guest interactions and millions of food orders, with deployment across more than 2,600 Casey's locations. The operational use case is phone-based food ordering during peak periods: reducing missed calls and hold times, routing orders to kitchens, answering menu/store questions, and freeing in-store staff for food preparation and guest service.

This is one of the cleaner public examples of voice AI moving into transactional retail operations rather than remaining a call-center pilot.
