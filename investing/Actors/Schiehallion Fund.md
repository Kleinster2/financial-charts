The Schiehallion Fund — [[Baillie Gifford]]-managed closed-end fund listed on the [[LSE]] (MNTN). Pure-play private companies vehicle, ~95%+ private allocation. Holds stakes in 5 of the world's 10 largest private companies ([[SpaceX]], [[ByteDance]], [[Stripe]], [[Databricks]], Chime). Up 54% share price return over past year (to Feb 2026). Moving from Specialist Fund Segment to LSE Main Market (approved Dec 2025, 98% backing).
