**David E. Shaw** — Founder of [[D.E. Shaw]]. Computer scientist turned quant pioneer turned computational biochemist. **Net worth $7.5-8.8B** (2025). Called "King Quant" by Fortune (1996). Now spends most time on D.E. Shaw Research (protein simulations). Hired Jeff Bezos; spawned Two Sigma founders.

## Investment relevance

**Why track David E. Shaw**:

1. **Quant pioneer** — One of first to apply computational methods to finance at scale.

2. **Alumni factory** — Bezos, Two Sigma founders came from D.E. Shaw. Track new spinouts.

3. **Science pivot** — Rare example of finance billionaire returning to fundamental research.

4. **Hybrid model** — D.E. Shaw combines quant and discretionary — unusual and successful.

5. **Presidential advisor** — PCAST appointments under Clinton and Obama signal policy influence.
