## May 2026 Tau Scaling presentation

At the 2026 IEEE International Symposium on Circuits and Systems in Shanghai, He introduced Tau Scaling Law as Huawei's proposed alternative guiding principle for semiconductor evolution. Huawei's official release says the framework spans devices, circuits, chips, and systems; [[Reuters]] framed it as Huawei's attempt to keep improving chip performance despite US restrictions on advanced semiconductor equipment.

He also acknowledged implementation hurdles, including the need for new design tools and the challenge of controlling heat from mobile chips through large AI data centers. That caveat matters: the announcement is a roadmap and design philosophy, not independent proof of parity with [[TSMC]] or [[NVIDIA]].
