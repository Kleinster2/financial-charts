#actor #software #edtech #k12 #private #pe-owned

**PowerSchool** — leading K-12 student information system and education software vendor in the United States. Owns [[Schoology]] (K-12 LMS, acquired 2019). Taken private by [[Bain Capital]] in October 2024 in an all-cash deal valued at approximately $5.6 billion, ending its NYSE listing as PWSC. Adjacent to but mostly distinct from the higher-ed LMS market dominated by [[Canvas LMS]] — PowerSchool is K-12 SIS first, with Schoology as the LMS layer for that customer base.
