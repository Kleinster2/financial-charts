## Apr 2026: portfolio reset under new leadership

Nestle entered April with investors already focused on a reset story, not a steady-state staples story.

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Chairman message (Apr. 15) | Volume growth has to resume consistently |
| Balance-sheet target | Leverage back to the midpoint of the 2x-3x operating-profit range |
| Asset action | Partial sale / partnership process for the water business |
| New CEO plan (Apr. 16 AGM) | Focus on coffee, pet care, nutrition, and food & snacks |
| Additional portfolio review | Vitamins, minerals, and supplements business could be divested |
| Restructuring scale | ~16,000 jobs, about 6% of workforce |

The broad direction is clear: simplify the portfolio, push for real internal growth rather than just pricing, and shrink the lower-return parts of the estate. That makes the stock more sensitive to whether the new team can actually turn volume growth back on, not just cut costs or sell assets.
