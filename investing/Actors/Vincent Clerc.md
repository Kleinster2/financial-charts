# Vincent Clerc

CEO of [[Maersk]] (AP Moller-Maersk A/S). Appointed January 2023, succeeding Søren Skou. Previously headed Maersk's Ocean & Logistics division. During the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|2026 Iran conflict]], publicly warned of fuel oil shortages at Asian bunkering ports and led industry efforts to shuttle fuel stocks from Europe/Americas to Asia. Also fronted [[Maersk]]'s defense of the Balboa and Cristóbal port concessions in person at [[NDRC]] in Beijing on March 20, 2026, after Beijing demanded European shippers withdraw from the [[Panama Canal]] terminals they took over from [[CK Hutchison]] in [[Panama]] (see [[Panama Canal Ports Crisis 2025-2026]]).
