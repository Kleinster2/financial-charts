# Vincent Clerc

CEO of [[Maersk]] (AP Moller-Maersk A/S). Appointed January 2023, succeeding Søren Skou. Previously headed Maersk's Ocean & Logistics division. During the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|2026 Iran conflict]], publicly warned of fuel oil shortages at Asian bunkering ports and led industry efforts to shuttle fuel stocks from Europe/Americas to Asia.
