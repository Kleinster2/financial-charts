#actor #nonprofit #australia

Minderoo Foundation — [[Australia]]'s largest philanthropic organization, founded by [[Andrew Forrest]] and Nicola Forrest in 2001. Named after Minderoo Station, the family cattle property in Western [[Australia]]. Received A$5B in [[Fortescue]] shares in June 2023 — one of [[Australia]]'s largest philanthropic gifts. Also holds a direct [[Fortescue]] shareholding as part of the Forrest family's 36.7% combined stake.

## Key initiatives

| Initiative | Focus |
|------------|-------|
| Walk Free | Anti-slavery (publishes the Global Slavery Index) |
| Flourishing Oceans | Marine conservation, plastic pollution |
| Thrive by Five | Early childhood development in [[Australia]] |
| Collaborate Against Cancer | Oncology research funding |
| Minderoo-UWA Data Institute | Data science, AI research |

Minderoo's funding model is unusual — the A$5B [[Fortescue]] share endowment means the foundation's value fluctuates with [[Iron ore|iron ore]] prices and [[China]] demand. The June 2023 donation was structured as a transfer of FMG shares, not a sale, so Minderoo retains exposure.
