**Tyson Foods** is the largest US meat processor by revenue — chicken, beef, pork, and prepared foods under brands including Tyson, Jimmy Dean, Hillshire Farm, and Ball Park. It trades on the NYSE as `TSN` and is [[JBS]]'s principal US competitor.

It is a protein-cycle business: margins swing with cattle and chicken economics, feed costs, and consumer demand. Tyson has leaned into branded prepared foods to dampen the commodity swings, but the chicken and beef segments still drive the cycle — and a multi-year US cattle-herd rebuild has squeezed the beef segment's margins.
