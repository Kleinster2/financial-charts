**IHC** — Abu Dhabi's largest listed company. $126B assets. [[Sheikh Tahnoon bin Zayed Al Nahyan]]'s private investment vehicle. Sprawling conglomerate.

---

## Why IHC matters

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Ticker | IHC (Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange) |
| Assets | **$126 billion** |
| Market cap | ~$250B (one of world's largest) |
| Chairman | [[Sheikh Tahnoon bin Zayed Al Nahyan]] |

UAE's most valuable listed company. Owned by Sheikh Tahnoon — sits alongside his sovereign fund roles (ADIA, ADQ) as a private investment vehicle.

## Network position

![[abu-dhabi-swf-network.png]]
*IHC ($126B) connects to Sheikh Tahnoon's broader network including ADIA, ADQ, and various investment vehicles. (Bloomberg, Dec 2025)*

IHC is distinct from the sovereign funds — it's a publicly traded conglomerate, not government-owned. But Sheikh Tahnoon's chairmanship creates coordination.
