# Fumio Kishida

100th Prime Minister of [[Japan]] (Oct 2021 - Oct 2024). Appointed [[Kazuo Ueda]] as [[BOJ]] governor in April 2023, replacing [[Haruhiko Kuroda]]. Oversaw [[Japan]]'s return to inflation and the beginning of monetary policy normalization. Resigned as LDP leader in August 2024 amid a party fundraising scandal, succeeded by [[Sanae Takaichi]].
