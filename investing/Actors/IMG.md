# IMG

IMG is a global sports marketing, media-rights, and licensing agency, founded by Mark McCormack in 1960 and long the spine of [[Endeavor]]'s sports business. It represents and services rightsholders across media rights, events, and brand partnerships — including properties such as Wimbledon and Major League Soccer. In February 2025 IMG was sold by [[Endeavor]] to [[TKO Group Holdings]] as part of the ~$3.25B all-equity sports-assets transaction.
