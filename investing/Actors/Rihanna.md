Rihanna (Robyn Rihanna Fenty) is a Barbadian singer and entrepreneur whose business empire made her the youngest female self-made billionaire (Forbes, 2022) with an estimated net worth of $1.4B. The wealth comes primarily from [[Fenty Beauty]], a 50/50 joint venture with [[LVMH]]'s Kendo division launched in 2017 that generated $600M+ in revenue within its first year by leading with an inclusive 40-shade foundation range — a move that forced the entire prestige beauty industry to expand shade offerings. Also founded Savage X Fenty (lingerie), which went through a failed IPO attempt in 2022-2023 and raised at a ~$1B valuation via a 2021 Series B led by [[SoftBank]]. The Fenty Beauty model — celebrity founder with genuine creative control, backed by a luxury conglomerate's distribution — set the template that [[Rare Beauty]], [[Rhode]], and others followed.
