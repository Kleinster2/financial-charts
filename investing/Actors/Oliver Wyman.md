Oliver Wyman is a global management consulting firm and subsidiary of [[Marsh McLennan]] ($24B+ revenue, 90,000+ employees). Operates across financial services, energy and natural resources, healthcare, transportation, and other sectors. 60+ offices globally with 5,000+ professionals. Grew at ~7% annually from 2013-2019, outperforming the global management consulting market.

In March 2026, hired [[Paul Sankey]] as Senior Advisor to its Energy and Natural Resources practice, led by Mark Pellerin (Global Head). Pellerin: "Paul's deep understanding of oil and gas economics will be invaluable as the sector navigates geopolitical volatility and transition pressures." [[Sankey Research]] continues to operate independently.

Named 40 new partners and 4 executive directors for the 2026 class (announced November 2025).
