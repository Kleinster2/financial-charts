## Context

Acquired by [[Johnson & Johnson]] as a neuroscience play centered on Caplyta (lumateperone), with a major-depressive-disorder adjunct label expansion filed and Phase 2 ITI-1284 for anxiety and Alzheimer's agitation. The deal is part of J&J's cash-deployment response to the STELARA biosimilar cliff — see [[Patent cliffs]] and [[M&A tracker]].
