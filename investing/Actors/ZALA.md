# ZALA

Russian unmanned aerial systems manufacturer. Founded as a private dual-use drone company; majority acquired (51%) by Kalashnikov Concern in February 2015. Now produces military-grade ISR and loitering munition systems for the Russian armed forces, most notably the Lancet loitering munition that has been widely used in [[Ukraine]]. Paradigmatic case in [[Kateryna Bondar]]'s framework of the Russian civil-military pipeline — civilian innovation absorbed by the state-owned military-industrial base. See [[Russian sovereign drone ecosystem]] for the structural argument.
