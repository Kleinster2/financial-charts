**American Century Investments** --- Kansas City-based asset manager (est. 1958). Parent of [[Avantis]] Investors, the fast-growing systematic ETF brand. One of the six firms that captured roughly half of all active ETF inflows in 2025. Unique ownership: the Stowers Institute for Medical Research (cancer research) is the largest shareholder.
