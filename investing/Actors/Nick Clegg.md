## Why Clegg matters for the vault

Clegg sits at the intersection of UK tech policy and AI infrastructure. His appointment to [[Nscale]]'s board (alongside [[Sheryl Sandberg]] and Susan Decker) was an IPO-readiness signal — political connectivity across the US and UK for a company positioning itself as Britain's AI infrastructure champion. [[NVIDIA]]'s Jensen Huang endorsed Nscale as a "national champion for AI infrastructure in the UK"; Clegg's presence reinforces that sovereign AI narrative.

The [[Project Stargate|UK Stargate]] pause (Apr 9, 2026) — citing energy costs and specifically the UK's decision to delay copyright changes easing AI training on copyrighted content (FT) — tests whether Clegg's political capital can reopen the path. His background in UK government and EU regulation (he led Meta's lobbying during the EU AI Act negotiations) is directly relevant to the regulatory barriers OpenAI cited.
