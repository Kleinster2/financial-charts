# TP ICAP

World's largest interdealer broker by revenue, providing liquidity and price discovery across fixed income, FX, commodities, equities, and derivatives. LSE-listed. Formed from the 2016 merger of Tullett Prebon and ICAP's voice broking business (ICAP's electronic platform became [[NEX Group]], later acquired by [[CME Group]]).

TP ICAP operates three divisions: Global Broking (voice and hybrid), Parameta Solutions (data and analytics), and Liquidnet (agency execution for institutional investors, acquired 2021). The firm sits at the center of OTC markets, where large institutional trades are still negotiated by voice -- a business model that has proven more durable than many predicted, as complex products resist full automation.
