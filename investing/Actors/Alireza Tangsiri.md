# Alireza Tangsiri

Commodore Alireza Tangsiri — commander of [[Iran]]'s [[IRGC|Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps]] Navy. Key figure behind the [[Strait of Hormuz]] blockade and the [[Hormuz Transit Regime|toll booth regime]] established during the 2026 Iran war. Killed in an Israeli strike on March 26, 2026, along with other senior IRGC naval commanders, according to Israeli Defense Minister Israel Katz. Iran did not immediately acknowledge the killing.
