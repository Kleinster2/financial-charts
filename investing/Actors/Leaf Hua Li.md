# Leaf Hua Li

Founder, Chairman, and CEO of [[Futu Holdings]].

## Quick stats

| | |
|---|---|
| Role | Founder, Chairman, CEO |
| Company | [[Futu Holdings]] (NASDAQ: FUTU) |
| Previous | Head of Multimedia Business Unit, [[Tencent]] (joined 2000, early employee) |
| Founded Futu | 2012 |
| Nationality | Chinese |

Li joined [[Tencent]] in 2000 as one of its earliest employees, rising to lead the Multimedia Business Unit. Left in 2012 to found Futu, applying Tencent's mobile-first, social-driven product philosophy to online brokerage. [[Tencent]] became Futu's largest investor.
