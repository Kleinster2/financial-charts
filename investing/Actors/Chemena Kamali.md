# Chemena Kamali

**Fashion designer** — creative director of [[Chloé]] since October 2023. German-born, trained under Phoebe Philo at Chloé, later at Saint Laurent. One of the few women currently leading a major luxury house.

## Chloé return (2023–)

**Context:** Replaced Gabriela Hearst, who focused on sustainability. [[Riccardo Bellini]] (then CEO) hired Kamali to revive the boho-chic heritage.

**First collections:**
- Pre-collection (Jan 2024)
- Fall-Winter 2024 (Feb 2024, Paris Fashion Week)
- Winter 2025

**Vision:** Liberation and feminine ease — returning to founder Gaby Aghion's original mission of freeing women from couture stiffness while maintaining youthful spirit.

**Significance:** One of the only women appointed as creative head of a major luxury house in recent years.

---

## Phoebe Philo connection

Started career under [[Phoebe Philo]] at Chloé (2007–2011). Philo's tenure defined modern Chloé — romantic, effortless femininity. Kamali carries that lineage.
