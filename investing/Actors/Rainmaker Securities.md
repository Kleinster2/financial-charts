# Rainmaker Securities

Rainmaker Securities is a US-registered broker-dealer specializing in secondary market trading of private company shares, particularly late-stage venture-backed and pre-IPO names. Co-founded by Greg Martin, the firm provides accredited and institutional investors access to shares in companies including [[SpaceX]], [[OpenAI]], [[Anthropic]], [[Stripe]], and [[Databricks]] through bilateral transactions and structured liquidity programs.

Rainmaker is one of several secondary platforms (alongside [[Nasdaq Private Market]], Forge, EquityZen) that constitute the unofficial secondary market for the largest private companies. Greg Martin commented to Reuters in April 2026 on the SpaceX run-up: *"SpaceX is consistently one of the most actively traded names on our platform because there's nothing else like it in the private markets today. Demand has also almost always outpaced supply, and that's been true even during periods where broader secondary market activity has been more muted."*
