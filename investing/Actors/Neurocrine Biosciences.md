## Soleno acquisition (Apr 6, 2026)

Neurocrine entered a definitive agreement to acquire [[Soleno Therapeutics]] for $53/share ($2.9B). The deal adds Vykat XR, the first and only treatment for hyperphagia in [[Prader-Willi syndrome]], giving Neurocrine a third commercial franchise.

Strategic rationale: PWS is a neurodevelopmental disorder sitting at the intersection of neuroscience and endocrinology — core Neurocrine territory. Vykat XR IP extends into mid-2040s. [[Cantor Fitzgerald|Cantor]] projects >$1B peak sales by 2029.

Funded with cash on hand plus modest prepayable debt. No financing condition. Expected close within 90 days.
