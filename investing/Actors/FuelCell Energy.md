# FuelCell Energy

US stationary fuel-cell developer (Danbury, CT) — designs and operates carbonate and solid-oxide fuel-cell power platforms for distributed generation, plus carbon capture and high-temperature electrolysis (hydrogen production). A member of the listed [[Hydrogen economy|hydrogen / fuel-cell]] cohort alongside [[Plug Power]], [[Bloom Energy]], and [[Ballard]]. Like its peers it rode the 2020–21 clean-energy bubble and gave it back — a serial-dilution, going-concern-pressured small-cap whose share price trades on financing and project headlines more than on a shared "hydrogen" factor.
