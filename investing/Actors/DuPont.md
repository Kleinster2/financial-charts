## Electronics spin-off

DuPont's semiconductor materials business — including photoresists, CMP slurries, and interconnect solutions — was spun off as **[[Qnity]]** (NYSE: Q) on November 1, 2025.

**For semiconductor materials exposure, see [[Qnity]].**

- Industrial Solutions
- Shelter & Safety materials
