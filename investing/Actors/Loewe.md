# Loewe

Spanish luxury fashion house — oldest brand in [[LVMH]] portfolio. Part of LVMH since 1996.

## Business

- **Revenue:** >$1.5B annually (as of 2024)
- **Categories:** Leather goods, ready-to-wear, perfumes, accessories
- **Heritage:** Founded 1846 in Madrid — nearly 180 years of leather craftsmanship
- **Position:** Key growth driver in LVMH's Fashion & Leather Goods division

## Creative Direction

| Director | Tenure | Signature |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Jack McCollough & Lazaro Hernandez | Apr 2025– | Casual, sporty, vivid color (Proenza Schouler founders) |
| Jonathan Anderson | 2013–2025 | Craft-focused, cultural curation, viral moments |

Anderson transformed Loewe from sleepy heritage brand to cultural phenomenon — Puzzle bag, craft collaborations, celebrity dressing.

## LOEWE Foundation

Craft Prize (est. 2016) — annual award celebrating excellence in modern craft. Reinforces brand positioning around artisanship.
