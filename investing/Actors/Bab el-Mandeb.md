# Bab el-Mandeb

**Bab el-Mandeb** is the 18-mile-wide strait between [[Yemen]], [[Djibouti]], and [[Eritrea]] connecting the Red Sea to the Gulf of Aden — the southern gateway to the [[Suez Canal]]. Handles ~4.2M bbl/day of oil and LNG. Houthi attacks diverted ~50% of traffic in 2024.
