# ChinaTalk

Hybrid think tank and media outlet focused on [[China]], emerging technology, and US-China strategic competition. Founded circa 2018 by [[Jordan Schneider]], runs a [[Substack]] newsletter and podcast with 70,000+ free subscribers. Describes itself as supplying non-partisan analysis to decision-makers in Silicon Valley and Washington.
