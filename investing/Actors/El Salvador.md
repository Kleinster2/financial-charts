**El Salvador** — Central American nation. First country to adopt [[Bitcoin]] as legal tender (Sep 2021) under President Nayib Bukele. Serves as domicile for [[Tether]] Holdings since January 2025 (relocated from BVI), granting a Digital Asset Service Provider license. Population ~6.5M. Economy heavily reliant on remittances (~24% of GDP).
