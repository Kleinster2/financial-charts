**Arctos** — Sports and entertainment private equity firm. ~$15B AUM. Being acquired by [[KKR]] for $1.4B.

## Why it matters

Arctos is the largest institutional investor in professional sports franchises — first platform approved to invest in multiple franchises across MLB, NBA, NHL, MLS, and global leagues.
