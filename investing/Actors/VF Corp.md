# VF Corp

VF Corporation (NYSE: VFC) is a US apparel and footwear holding company whose brands include Vans, The North Face, Timberland, and Dickies (Supreme was sold to EssilorLuxottica in 2024). It is in a multi-year turnaround under CEO Bracken Darrell — debt reduction, a dividend cut, and a Vans recovery effort. In the [[Athletic footwear and apparel]] cluster validation it serves as a broad-apparel control.
