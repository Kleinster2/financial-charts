# ARIS

**ARIS** (Advanced Research Investment Solutions) is the sub-adviser to [[RPAR ETF]] and [[UPAR ETF]], the risk parity ETFs issued by [[Tidal Financial Group]]. Designs the portfolio allocation and rebalancing methodology for both products. Not publicly traded.
