**ASEAN** — Association of Southeast Asian Nations. 10 members, 680M people. Economic community, not customs union. Key manufacturing hub and China+1 destination.

---

## Overview

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Members | 10 |
| Population | 680M |
| GDP | ~$3.6T |
| Founded | 1967 |
| HQ | Jakarta |
| Chair | Rotates annually |

## ASEAN Economic Community (AEC)

| Goal | Status |
|------|--------|
| Free flow of goods | Mostly achieved |
| Free flow of services | Partial |
| Free flow of investment | Partial |
| Free flow of capital | Partial |
| Free flow of skilled labor | Limited |

**Not a customs union:** Each member has own external tariffs.
