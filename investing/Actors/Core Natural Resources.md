# Core Natural Resources

A diversified US coal major formed by the January 2025 merger of Arch Resources and CONSOL Energy — spanning high-calorific thermal coal (the Pennsylvania Mining Complex / Powder River Basin) and seaborne metallurgical coal, plus the CONSOL Marine Terminal export gateway. The combination gives it the broadest US coal footprint after [[Peabody Energy]], straddling power-generation thermal and steel-market met. A member of the [[Coal miners]] cohort, and (with [[Peabody Energy]]) the diversified anchor of its core. The former tickers ARCH (Arch Resources) and CEIX (CONSOL Energy) were retired into CNR at the merger.
