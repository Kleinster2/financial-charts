# Devon Park

Devon Park Advisors is a specialist GP-led secondaries advisory firm, acquired by [[Perella Weinberg Partners]] in Q2 2025. The acquisition extended PWP's capital advisory platform into GP-led secondaries — a fast-growing segment of the private-equity aftermarket where existing PE funds restructure to extend hold periods on trophy assets or generate LP liquidity.

Post-close, Devon Park operates as the secondaries advisory practice within [[Perella Weinberg Partners]]. Consideration was not publicly disclosed.
