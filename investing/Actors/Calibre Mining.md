# Calibre Mining

Calibre Mining was an Americas-focused gold producer (TSX: CXB) with operations in Nicaragua (El Limón and La Libertad) and Nevada (the Pan mine), plus the Valentine gold project in Newfoundland. In June 2025 it completed an all-share business combination with [[Equinox Gold]], contributing Valentine and its Nicaragua/Nevada operations; it now trades as part of [[Equinox Gold]].
