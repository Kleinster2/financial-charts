**Standard Chartered** is a global bank headquartered in London, focused on Asia/Africa/Middle East markets. Trades as STAN on LSE.

---

## AI productivity and role cuts (May 2026)

On May 19, 2026, [[Reuters]] reported that Standard Chartered plans to cut about 15% of corporate-function roles by 2030 as it steps up AI and automation adoption. The FT framed the same move as almost 8,000 jobs; Reuters' headline version was more than 7,000. Management tied the plan to higher returns and productivity rather than a one-off restructuring event.

This is a useful non-US data point for [[AI in banking]]: large banks are moving from AI pilot programs into explicit headcount and cost-base targets. Standard Chartered's geography also matters - Asia/Africa/Middle East exposure gives the AI-efficiency story a different labor and regulator mix than US money-center banks or coordinated retraining models.

*Sources: [Reuters via Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/stanchart-to-cut-more-than-7000-jobs-as-bank-steps-up-ai-adoption-4697509), [Reuters via MarketScreener](https://www.marketscreener.com/news/stanchart-targets-higher-return-in-2028-plans-15-cut-in-corporate-roles-by-2030-ce7f5adbd98ef126), May 19 2026.*
