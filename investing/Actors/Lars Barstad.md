# Lars Barstad

Lars Barstad is Chief Executive Officer of [[Frontline]] (FRO), the world's largest listed pure-play crude tanker operator. A long-tenured shipping executive, Barstad is a regular commentator on VLCC rate dynamics and shipping market structure.

During the [[2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis]], Barstad drew attention to a structural point about freight index methodology on Frontline's March 4, 2026 conference call: *"The TD3C index, and indexes in general, have much wider market implications than they used to."* The comment framed the risk that [[Baltic Exchange]] assessments published during periods when physical fixtures are absent — what Lloyd's List later termed [[Imaginary freight rate|imaginary freight rates]] — still drive real money through FFAs, [[BWET]], and charter contracts. Barstad's point was that benchmark integrity during chokepoint closures is no longer a reporting issue but a financial-market issue.
