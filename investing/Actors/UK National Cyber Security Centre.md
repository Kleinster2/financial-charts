**UK National Cyber Security Centre** — the UK's cyber defense agency, responsible for national cyber resilience, incident response, and guidance for critical infrastructure. It sits inside the British state as the technical counterparty when frontier cyber threats spill beyond normal enterprise security.

## Anthropic Mythos response (Apr 2026)

Reuters reported on April 12 that the NCSC was in talks with the [[Bank of England]] and the [[Financial Conduct Authority]] about the risks posed by [[Anthropic]]'s [[Claude Mythos|Claude Mythos Preview]]. The concern was that Mythos-class models could expose vulnerabilities in critical IT systems used by banks, insurers, and exchanges.

That puts the NCSC at the point where AI capability turns into national operational-risk management.
