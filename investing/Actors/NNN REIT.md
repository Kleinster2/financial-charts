# NNN REIT

NNN REIT (NYSE: NNN), formerly National Retail Properties, is a retail-focused net-lease REIT owning single-tenant retail properties (convenience stores, restaurants, auto service) on long-term triple-net leases, with a long dividend-growth record. A member of the [[Net-lease REITs]] cohort.
