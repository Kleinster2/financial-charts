## History

- **1966:** Founded in Boston by Michio & Aveline Kushi (macrobiotic pioneers)
- **2011:** Tony & Josephine Antoci bought the Fairfax location, transformed into luxury format
- **2022:** Hailey Bieber smoothie goes viral on TikTok (450M #Erewhon views)
- **2025:** "Erewhon 2.0" — national expansion begins

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Cult brand with pricing power ($20 smoothies)
- Genius influencer/brand partnership model
- High revenue per location
- National expansion runway
- [[Stripes LLC|Stripes]] backing

**Bear:**
- LA-centric, unproven elsewhere
- Premium grocery historically hard to scale
- Trend-dependent (wellness fatigue risk)
- Execution risk on 20-city expansion
