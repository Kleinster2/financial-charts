**Dean & DeLuca** — iconic NYC gourmet grocery chain. Founded 1977 in Soho. Closed 2020 after financial troubles under Thai owner Pace Development. Its closure left a void in the NYC premium grocery market now being filled by [[Meadow Lane]], [[Erewhon]], Happier Grocery, and others.
