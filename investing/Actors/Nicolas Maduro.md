## Market relevance

Maduro's capture is the hinge event in the Venezuela note graph. Before January 2026, Venezuela was primarily a sanctions-isolated oil reserve with degraded infrastructure and limited [[Chevron]] access. After the operation, the market question shifted to whether a US-backed transition could reopen oil production, restructure [[PDVSA]], resolve creditor claims, and bring Western capital back into the heavy-sour crude complex.
