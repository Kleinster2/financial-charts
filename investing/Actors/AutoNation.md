# AutoNation

AutoNation (NYSE: AN) is one of the largest US franchised auto retailers — new and used vehicles, finance & insurance, parts and service, plus AutoNation USA used-only stores and a captive-finance arm. A member of the [[Franchised auto dealers]] cohort and a long-standing buyback-driven compounder.
