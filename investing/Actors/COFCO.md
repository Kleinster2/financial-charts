**COFCO** — China's largest state-owned food and agriculture conglomerate. One of the "ABCD+C" global grain traders (alongside [[ADM]], [[Bunge]], [[Cargill]], Louis Dreyfus). Operates soybean crushing and biodiesel production in [[Brazil]]. COFCO International (overseas arm) handles grain trading, oilseed processing, and sugar across 35+ countries. Top-8 biodiesel producer in Brazil.
