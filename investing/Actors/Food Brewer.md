#actor #company #foodtech #switzerland #private

Food Brewer — Swiss foodtech start-up developing cell-based cocoa via bioreactor cultivation, positioning as an alternative supply path for chocolate manufacturers exposed to West [[Africa]] cocoa price and climate risk. Backed by [[Lindt Spruengli]]. Sits alongside [[Celleste Bio]] ([[Israel]], [[Mondelez]]-backed) and [[Kokomodo]] ([[Israel]], [[Pluri]]/[[Cargill]]) as one of three confectionery-major-aligned cell-based cocoa start-ups in April 2026.

## Context

The Lindt investment is the Swiss chocolate industry's equivalent of the [[Mondelez]]-[[Celleste Bio]] and [[Cargill]]-[[Kokomodo]] partnerships: each major chocolate manufacturer backing a domestic-or-adjacent cell-based cocoa start-up as a supply-diversification hedge against the 2024-25 cocoa spike. [[Lindt Spruengli]]'s brand proposition rests on premium chocolate, making cocoa butter supply security strategically central.
