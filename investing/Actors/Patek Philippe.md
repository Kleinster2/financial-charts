**Patek Philippe** — Swiss luxury watchmaker, one of the "Holy Trinity" alongside [[Audemars Piguet]] and [[Vacheron Constantin]]. Family-owned by the Stern family since 1932. Produces ~70,000 watches per year. Revenue estimated at CHF 2.2B+ (2023). Known for Nautilus, Calatrava, and Grand Complications collections.
