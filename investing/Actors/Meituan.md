## Robotics thesis

Meituan's food delivery business requires massive last-mile logistics. Investing in robotics to:
- Automate delivery (autonomous vehicles, drones, robots)
- Reduce labor costs
- Improve delivery density in urban areas

**Strategic rationale:** Unlike pure financial investors, Meituan has operational deployment path for robotics investments.
