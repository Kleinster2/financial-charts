# Shelly Ibach

Former Chair, President, and CEO of [[Sleep Number]] (2012-2025). Transformed the company from commodity air-mattress retailer (Select Comfort) into a technology-enabled smart-bed platform. Launched [[SleepIQ]], the 360 Smart Bed, and completed the 2017 rebrand from Select Comfort to Sleep Number. Announced retirement October 2024; succeeded by [[Linda Findley]] in April 2025. Serving as strategic advisor to the new CEO and board through end of 2025.
