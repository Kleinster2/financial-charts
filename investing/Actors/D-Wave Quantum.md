# D-Wave Quantum

D-Wave Quantum (NYSE: QBTS) is a listed pure-play quantum-computing company and the longest-commercial of the pure-plays, built on quantum annealing (a narrower optimization-focused approach than gate-model quantum computing) plus an emerging gate-model program. It went public via a 2022 de-SPAC — a high-beta member of the listed [[Quantum computing]] momentum cohort.
