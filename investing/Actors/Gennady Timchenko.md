# Gennady Timchenko

Russian businessman; co-founder of [[Gunvor]] (2000) with [[Torbjörn Törnqvist]]. Held ~44% of Gunvor until March 2014, when the US Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control added him to the SDN list in response to [[Russia]]'s annexation of Crimea, citing his close ties to Vladimir Putin and his role in Gunvor's handling of significant Russian crude flows. Timchenko divested his Gunvor stake to Törnqvist the day before the sanctions announcement. Remains one of [[Russia]]'s wealthiest individuals through holdings in Volga Group (transport, petrochemicals) and stakes in Novatek and Sibur.
