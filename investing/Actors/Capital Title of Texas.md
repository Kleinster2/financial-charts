## Insights

**Largest independent agency** — Not an underwriter (doesn't bear insurance risk), but the biggest independent agency in the US. Proves scale is achievable outside Big Four direct operations.

**Texas dominance** — 100+ branches, 85% population coverage in the nation's largest housing market by volume. Texas model: high transaction volume, no state income tax attracting migration, strong commercial real estate.

**Multi-underwriter model** — Works with all major national underwriters ([[Fidelity National Financial]], [[First American Financial]], [[Old Republic International]], [[Stewart Information Services]]) to serve client needs.
