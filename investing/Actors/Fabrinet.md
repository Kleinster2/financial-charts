## Business model

**Contract manufacturing:**
- Customers design products
- Fabrinet manufactures
- Precision assembly
- High-mix, low-to-medium volume

**Vertically integrated:**
- Optical assembly
- Test and packaging
- PCB assembly
- Complete modules

**800G/1.6T ramp:**
- Transceivers need precision assembly
- Volume growing rapidly
- Fabrinet = preferred manufacturer
- Capacity constrained
