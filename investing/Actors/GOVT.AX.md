## Composition

Predominantly AAA-rated Australian Commonwealth government bonds plus state government bonds. Duration ~5.2 years, average maturity ~6.2 years. Quarterly distributions. Relatively small AUM — niche product compared to Vanguard's VGB.

## Use Case

Australian sovereign rates proxy. In the global bonds chart, GOVT.AX represents Australia alongside [[TLT]], [[2510.T]], [[IGLT.L]], [[IBGL.L]], [[XLB.TO]], and [[CBON]]. Note: ticker "GOVT" on ASX is unrelated to the US GOVT ETF (iShares US Treasury Bond).
