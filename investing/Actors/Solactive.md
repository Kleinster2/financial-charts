## Business model

**Index licensing:**
- Creates and maintains indices
- ETF issuers pay licensing fees
- Lower cost than incumbents (S&P, MSCI)
- Custom index capability

---

| Provider | Market share | Strength |

**Disruptor:** Offers 50-70% lower fees than big 3.

---

## Why ETF issuers use Solactive

**Cost savings:**
- Index fees = drag on ETF returns
- Solactive = lower expense ratios
- Attracts cost-conscious issuers
- Enables competitive pricing

**Flexibility:**
- Custom index creation
- Fast turnaround
- Thematic indices
- Smart-beta strategies

## Investment case (as trend)

**Bull (index disruption):**
- Fee compression favors low-cost providers
- ETF growth = index demand growth
- Custom indices increasingly popular
- Thematic ETF boom benefits Solactive

**Bear:**
- Private company (not investable directly)
- Big 3 have brand moats
- [[Benchmark]] indices hard to displace
- Scale disadvantage
