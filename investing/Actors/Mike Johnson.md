# Mike Johnson

Speaker of the US House of Representatives since October 2023, elected after the unprecedented removal of [[Kevin McCarthy]]. A Republican from Louisiana's 4th congressional district, Johnson was a relatively low-profile backbench conservative and constitutional lawyer before his surprise elevation to the speakership. He spoke at the [[Hill and Valley Forum]] in May 2024 and oversaw passage of significant legislation including the TikTok forced-sale bill and various defense/tech appropriations. His speakership has been marked by narrow Republican majorities requiring coalition management with both MAGA hardliners and moderate members, making every major vote (debt ceiling, government funding, defense spending) a negotiation exercise that creates policy uncertainty for markets.
