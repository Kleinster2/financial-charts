# Mike Johnson

Role: Speaker of the US House of Representatives
State: Louisiana
Party: Republican
Speaker since: Oct 2023 (elected after [[Kevin McCarthy]] ousted)

Became Speaker in October 2023 after the unprecedented removal of Kevin McCarthy. Spoke at the [[Hill and Valley Forum]] in May 2024. Oversaw passage of the TikTok forced-sale bill and various defense/tech legislation. Relatively low-profile before becoming Speaker — previously a backbench conservative.
