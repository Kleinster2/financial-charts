**Farallon Computing** -- Mac networking company founded in 1986 by [[Reese Jones]], best known for inventing PhoneNET (LocalTalk-over-phone-wire adapter). Rebranded to [[Netopia]] in 1999 to reflect its shift toward broadband equipment.
