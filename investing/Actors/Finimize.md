**Finimize** is a financial news and analysis platform targeting retail investors, acquired by [[Aberdeen]] (now [[abrdn]]) in 2021 and placed under strategic review in February 2026.

## Synopsis

Finimize launched in 2015 with a simple premise: distill financial markets into a daily email that takes three minutes to read. The format resonated — the subscriber base grew to 1M+ across email, app, and community events — and [[Aberdeen]] acquired the company in 2021 for a reported ~GBP 150M as part of then-CEO Stephen Bird's strategy to reach retail investors directly rather than through adviser intermediaries. The logic was sound on paper: [[abrdn]]'s brand was invisible to retail, and Finimize had exactly the young, engaged audience that asset managers covet. In practice, the acquisition suffered from the same disease that kills most media-inside-asset-manager deals: the content team wanted editorial independence, the parent wanted distribution for its own products, and the user base could smell the difference.

The February 2026 strategic review signals that [[abrdn]] is likely to divest or shut down Finimize as part of CEO Jason Windsor's simplification strategy (which already jettisoned the interactive investor platform). The write-down risk is real but contained — GBP 150M against [[abrdn]]'s ~GBP 500B AUM is a rounding error. The broader lesson is that asset managers consistently overpay for retail distribution assets ([[Invesco]] with Jemstep, [[Franklin Templeton]] with AdvisorEngine) because they mistake content engagement for investment intent. Finimize readers want market literacy, not necessarily another fund provider.
