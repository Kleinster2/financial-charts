Aker ASA (Oslo Bors: AKER) is a [[Norway|Norwegian]] industrial investment company controlled by billionaire [[Kjell Inge Rokke]], who holds ~68% of voting rights through TRG Holding. The company traces its roots to the Aker mechanical workshop founded in 1841, making it one of Norway's oldest industrial names. Today it functions as a holding company with concentrated positions in energy, maritime, and marine biotech.

Core holdings include [[Aker BP]] (oil and gas E&P, ~30% stake), [[Aker Solutions]] (energy services and engineering), [[Aker BioMarine]] (krill harvesting and omega-3 supplements), and [[Aker Horizons]] (renewables, carbon capture, green hydrogen). The portfolio reflects Rokke's strategy of leveraging deep Norwegian maritime and offshore expertise across both fossil fuel extraction and the energy transition. Aker BP is the crown jewel — one of Europe's largest independent E&P companies, operating primarily on the Norwegian Continental Shelf.
