# Aker ASA

Norwegian industrial investment company (Oslo Børs: AKER). Controlled by [[Kjell Inge Røkke]]. Holdings span oil & gas, maritime, renewable energy, and seafood. Major subsidiaries include Aker BP (oil), Aker Solutions (energy services), and Aker BioMarine (krill).
