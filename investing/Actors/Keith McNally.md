**Keith McNally** — British-born [[New York City]] restaurateur behind Balthazar, Pastis, Minetta Tavern, Schiller's, Morandi, and other iconic downtown Manhattan restaurants. Opened Balthazar in SoHo in 1997 with [[Riad Nasr]] and [[Lee Hanson]] as co-executive chefs; the pair ran his kitchens for 15+ years before launching [[Frenchette Group]].
