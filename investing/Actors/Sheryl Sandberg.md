**Sheryl Sandberg** — former COO of [[Meta]] (2008-2022), now board member at [[Nscale]]. One of the most prominent executives in Silicon Valley history, she scaled Meta's advertising business from ~$300M to $100B+ in annual revenue.

## Why Sandberg matters for the vault

Sandberg's appointment to [[Nscale]]'s board (alongside [[Nick Clegg]] and Susan Decker) is an IPO-readiness signal. Her track record scaling Meta into a public company and her relationships across Wall Street and Silicon Valley lend credibility to Nscale's H2 2026 IPO plans.
