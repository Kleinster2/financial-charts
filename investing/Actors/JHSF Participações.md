# JHSF Participações

**JHSF Participações** — Brazilian publicly-traded luxury real estate, hospitality, and aviation conglomerate. Ticker JHSF3 on [[B3]]. Chairman José Auriemo Neto, recognized as Brazilian Person of the Year at the [[Brazil Week NYC 2026]] gala on May 13. Owns Cidade Jardim mall and Catarina Fashion Outlet (São Paulo), the Fasano hotel and restaurant brand, Boa Vista golf community, and Catarina Aeroporto Executivo — Brazil's only private executive airport. Building out the JHSF Capital arm as a US-facing asset management vehicle for affluent allocators.

---

## Synopsis

JHSF is the listed expression of high-end Brazilian consumer demand — luxury malls, Fasano hotels, executive aviation, golf and country clubs — engineered to be defensive against [[Selic]] swings because its affluent customer base is the least rate-sensitive segment of Brazilian retail. The Q3 2025 results (gross revenue +38%, net profit more than doubled) confirm the operational profile. The 2026 strategic question is whether JHSF Capital, the asset management arm, can recruit US institutional capital — Auriemo Neto's [[BrazilCham]] recognition reads as the chamber's bet that the answer is yes. The cyclical risk: a populist 2026 election outcome could compress luxury spend more than the rate cycle has, since high-end consumption is correlated with both BRL strength and political confidence.

## Why it matters

JHSF is the listed expression of Brazilian affluent consumer demand, with a defensive earnings profile relative to broad Brazilian cyclicals (high-end customers less sensitive to [[Selic]] swings). The JHSF Capital push is the structural development worth tracking — it is the company's bet on becoming a US-facing private-markets sponsor rather than a pure Brazilian operator. Auriemo Neto's recognition at the [[Brazil Week NYC 2026]] BrazilCham gala signals that the chamber views JHSF Capital as a credible bilateral capital channel.
