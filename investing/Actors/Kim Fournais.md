**Kim Fournais** is the co-founder of [[Saxo Bank]] (1992) and served as CEO from founding until March 2026, when he transitioned to chairman while retaining a ~28% ownership stake. A Danish fintech pioneer, he built Saxo from a small Copenhagen-based online brokerage into a global multi-asset trading platform offering equities, FX, options, futures, bonds, and mutual funds across 60+ exchanges. The CEO transition came as Saxo prepared for a potential IPO or strategic sale — [[Geely]] (via Sampo) acquired a 51.5% stake in 2018, later complicated by [[US]] sanctions concerns over Geely's Chinese ownership. Fournais's large retained stake means he remains the single most influential voice on Saxo's strategic direction.
