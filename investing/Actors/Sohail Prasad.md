## Controversy

Co-founder [[Samvit Ramadurgam]] sued Prasad and [[Destiny Tech100]] in Delaware Chancery (Jan 2024, Case No. 2024-0057), alleging an improper stock-split scheme that squeezed him out of his 32% stake. Prasad fired Ramadurgam in Nov 2023. Case ongoing as of late 2025. Note: Destiny Tech100 Inc. and Destiny Advisors LLC were not named as defendants.
