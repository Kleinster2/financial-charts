## Brexit / EU clearing wars

**Timeline:**
- 2020: Brexit transition ends
- March 2022: ESMA recognized LCH Ltd as Tier 2 CCP under EMIR 2.2
- December 2024: Equivalence extended to **June 30, 2028**

**EMIR 3.0 Active Account Requirement (June 2025):**
- EU firms must maintain "active accounts" at EU CCPs for EUR/PLN OTC IRD
- Minimum 5 trades per subcategory annually must clear through EU CCP
- Attempt to break UK CCP dominance, though full forced relocation deferred

**Market share shift:**
- Eurex grew from 14% (2019) to ~20% (2021)
- Stabilized at ~19% Eurex vs ~81% LCH
