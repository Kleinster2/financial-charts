# GKN

GKN was a UK industrial engineering conglomerate with origins in 1759 (as Guest, Keen & Nettlefolds), latterly organised into an aerospace components business and an automotive driveline business. Listed on the [[FTSE 100]] for decades.

In March 2018, [[Melrose Industries]] successfully completed a £10.4B hostile takeover of GKN — the largest UK contested M&A battle since Kraft / Cadbury in 2009. GKN's defence was advised by [[Gleacher Shacklock]], JPMorgan Cazenove, and UBS. The board opposed the takeover and argued for an alternative plan involving a joint venture with Dana Incorporated, but shareholders narrowly approved Melrose's offer.

Post-acquisition, GKN was split under Melrose: GKN Aerospace remained inside Melrose, and GKN Automotive was spun off in 2023 as [[Dowlais Group]]. GKN as a standalone listed entity no longer exists.
