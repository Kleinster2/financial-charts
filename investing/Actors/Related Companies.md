## Why Related matters

**Hudson Yards.** The largest private development in US history — $25B, larger than Rockefeller Center (22 acres) or World Trade Center (16 acres). Mixed-use megaproject on Manhattan's West Side with office, residential, retail, and public space. Almost $1B per acre.

**Scale and ambition.** Related develops at a scale few private firms can match. From affordable housing origins to trophy developments, the firm has shaped American cities for 50+ years.

## Equinox ownership

Related Companies is majority owner of [[Equinox]] (since 2006). Stephen Ross led investment in luxury fitness. [[L Catterton]] is minority investor.

**Blink Fitness:** Budget gym brand owned by Equinox filed Chapter 11 bankruptcy in August 2024 — €280M+ debt, 100+ locations. Equinox parent not affected.
