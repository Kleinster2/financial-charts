# Fortune Brands

Fortune Brands Innovations (NYSE: FBIN) is a US building-products maker — Moen plumbing, cabinets, and security/connected products (Master Lock, Yale, SentrySafe). Renamed from Fortune Brands Home & Security (FBHS) in 2022 after spinning off its cabinets distribution. A member of the [[Building products]] cohort, in the housing-products pole.
