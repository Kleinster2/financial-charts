# Energy Transfer

Energy Transfer (NYSE: ET) is one of the largest US midstream MLPs — a diversified network of crude, natural gas, NGL, and refined-products pipelines, storage, and terminals, built up by Kelcy Warren through serial acquisition. A member of the [[Midstream and pipelines]] cohort.
