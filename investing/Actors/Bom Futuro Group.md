**Bom Futuro Group** — Largest soybean farm in [[Brazil]], with 35 production units totaling 700,000+ hectares of cultivation in Mato Grosso. Produces 1.9M tonnes of grains, 360,000 tonnes of cotton lint, and 440,000 tonnes of cotton seed. Founded 1982 in Campo Verde, Mato Grosso.
