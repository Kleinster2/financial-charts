# Ladurée

**French luxury pâtisserie** — iconic macarons, Parisian tea salons. Founded 1862, popularized the modern macaron. Groupe Holder owned 1993-2022. Stéphane Courbit's Lov Group acquired 80% in 2022. 130+ locations globally. Symbol of French art de vivre.
