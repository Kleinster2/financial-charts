# Stryker

Stryker (NYSE: SYK) is one of the largest US medical-device makers — orthopaedics (hips, knees, trauma), medical-surgical equipment (hospital beds, power tools, the Mako surgical robot), and neurotechnology. A diversified device generalist, it is the most-connected name in the [[Medtech]] cohort rather than a single-franchise pure-play.
