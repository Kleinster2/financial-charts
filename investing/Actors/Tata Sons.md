#actor #holding-company #india

**Tata Sons** — Principal holding company of [[Tata Group]]. ~66% owned by Tata Trusts (philanthropic foundations). Holds controlling stakes in listed Tata companies including [[TCS]], [[Tata Motors]], [[Tata Steel]], [[Tata Consumer Products]], and others. Chairman: [[N. Chandrasekaran]]. Backed EUR 3.875B bridge loan for [[Tata Motors]]' acquisition of [[Iveco Group]].
