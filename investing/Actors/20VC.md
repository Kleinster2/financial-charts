**20VC** is a VC-focused interview podcast hosted by Harry Stebbings, launched ~2015 when Stebbings was 18. Over 100M total downloads across 190 countries. Stebbings parlayed the podcast into an $800M+ fund empire, making 20VC the clearest example of the podcast-to-fund pipeline.

## Synopsis

The 20VC model inverted the traditional venture capital path. Instead of building a track record at a firm and then raising a fund, Stebbings built an audience of founders and LPs through the podcast, then converted that attention into capital. The progression was rapid: $8.3M micro-seed fund (2020), $140M across two funds (2021), and a $400M fund (2024). With 13+ unicorn investments, the returns have validated the strategy -- though the early vintage benefited from the 2020-2021 ZIRP-era markup cycle, and the later funds face a more hostile fundraising and exit environment.

The investment relevance extends beyond Stebbings himself. 20VC demonstrated that media platforms can function as venture deal flow engines -- the podcast creates asymmetric access to founders (who want the exposure) and LPs (who listen for pattern recognition). This model has been replicated across the venture ecosystem ([[All-In Podcast]]'s hosts all run funds, a16z built a media arm, etc.) but 20VC was among the first to prove it at scale. The risk is concentration: the entire franchise depends on Stebbings personally, and a $400M fund requires a different skill set than a $8M micro-seed. The jump from scout-sized checks to growth-stage deployment is where podcast-turned-funds historically struggle.
