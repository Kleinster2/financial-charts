20VC — VC-focused interview podcast hosted by Harry Stebbings. Launched ~2015 when Stebbings was 18. 100M+ total downloads across 190 countries. Stebbings parlayed the podcast into an $800M+ fund empire: $8.3M micro-seed (2020), $140M across two funds (2021), $400M fund (2024). 13+ unicorn investments. The clearest example of the podcast-to-fund pipeline.
