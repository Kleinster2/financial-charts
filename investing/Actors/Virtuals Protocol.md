#actor #crypto #ai #agents

**Virtuals Protocol** — [[AI agents|AI agent]] launchpad on [[Base]] ([[Coinbase]] L2). Lets anyone create an autonomous AI agent and issue a token representing its ownership, governance, and revenue share. VIRTUAL token peaked above $5B market cap in the 2024-2025 AI-agent bubble — ranked among top 40 cryptocurrencies at peak. Represented the first fully-tokenized agent primitive.

## How it works

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Creator designs an autonomous agent (chatbot, trader, content producer, etc.) |
| 2 | Virtuals launches a bonding-curve token representing the agent |
| 3 | Token holders earn share of agent's economic activity + governance |
| 4 | Agent uses its token treasury to pay for inference, storage, promotion |

Analogous to Pump.fun for meme tokens, but tailored to AI agents with ongoing economic activity rather than pure speculation assets.

---

## Relevance

Virtuals crystallized the "AI agent as economic entity" pattern. Most of the resulting tokens have crashed 55-85% from peaks as 2024 hype deflated, but the category template persists: agent launchpads are a distinct sub-layer in the [[Decentralized AI landscape]].
