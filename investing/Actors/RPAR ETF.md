# RPAR ETF

**RPAR** is the ARIS Risk Parity ETF, a systematic risk parity fund sub-advised by ARIS (Advanced Research Investment Solutions). Launched 2020. Targets equal risk contribution across asset classes without leverage (0.87x as of March 2026). Notable for 27% commodity allocation but 0% TIPS — the inverse of [[ALLW]]'s positioning.
