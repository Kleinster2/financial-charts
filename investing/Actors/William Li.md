## Apr 2026 chip strategy

In an Apr 24, 2026 [[Reuters]] interview at the Beijing Auto Show, Li said [[NIO]] developed its own automotive silicon because [[NVIDIA]]'s auto chips carry very high gross margins and because in-house chips can be matched more tightly to NIO's algorithms and sensor layout. That strategy is now housed in [[Shenji]], NIO's spun-off chip unit.
