**Neuralink** — Brain-computer interface. $9B valuation. 12 human implants. FDA breakthrough designation. Thought-to-text trials.

Neuralink is the high-bandwidth invasive BCI bet in the vault: the bull case is FDA momentum, growing human-implant data, and Musk-scale capital access; the bear case is surgical risk, thread durability, ethics, and slower reimbursement/regulatory timelines. The signal to watch is whether the implant count and use-hours keep rising without safety setbacks that push commercial approval into a much longer cycle.

- Private (no public access)
- Early technical challenges (thread retraction)
- Regulatory uncertainty
- Ethical concerns
