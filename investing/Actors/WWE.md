# WWE

WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment) is the sports-entertainment company behind Raw, SmackDown, and WrestleMania, built by the McMahon family. It is now a core asset of [[TKO Group Holdings]], which was formed in September 2023 when [[Endeavor]] merged [[UFC]] into the public WWE company and renamed the combined entity TKO. WWE's [[NYSE]] listing is the lineage behind the [[TKO securities note|TKO ticker]] — the stock traded as WWF from its October 1999 IPO, became WWE in 2002, and was renamed TKO at the merger.
