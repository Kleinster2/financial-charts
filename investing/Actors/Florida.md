# Florida

Florida is the third-largest US state by population (~23M) and fourth-largest by GDP (~$1.7T, 2024). A key hub for compounding pharmacies, financial services, tourism, and defense contractors. The state's regulatory and legislative posture on pharmaceutical compounding has direct investment implications for the [[GLP-1 receptor agonists]] market — SB 860 targets weight-loss-specific compounding restrictions and API documentation requirements. Florida also operates a Canadian drug import program, creating potential semaglutide leakage channels that complicate [[Novo Nordisk]]'s pricing strategy.

## Investment relevance

Florida's no-income-tax status and business-friendly posture have driven significant population and corporate migration from high-tax states, particularly since 2020. [[Citadel]], [[Elliott Management]], and numerous hedge funds relocated headquarters to Miami. The state is also home to major defense installations (MacDill AFB, Cape Canaveral, Jacksonville Naval Station) and a growing space launch industry. Hurricane exposure makes it the most consequential state for property insurance markets.
