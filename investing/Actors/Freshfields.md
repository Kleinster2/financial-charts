## Anthropic AI co-build (April 2026)

April 2026: Freshfields announced an alliance with [[Anthropic]] under which the firm helps build specialist legal AI tools that could later be sold to rival firms, in exchange for early access to future Anthropic models and tools. It is the clearest example of the co-build sourcing path in [[AI in legal services]] — between buying a finished third-party platform ([[Harvey]], [[Legora]]) and building a fully proprietary one ([[Kirkland & Ellis]], $500M, May 2026).
