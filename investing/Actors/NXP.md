## Key events

### Qualcomm acquisition blocked (2018)

[[Qualcomm]]'s $44B bid for NXP was blocked when China withheld antitrust approval. See [[Qualcomm NXP blocked deal]].

**Impact on NXP:**
- Remained independent (stock initially dropped 8%)
- Proved resilient as standalone company
- Avoided integration risk and culture clash
- Benefited from automotive semiconductor boom post-2020
