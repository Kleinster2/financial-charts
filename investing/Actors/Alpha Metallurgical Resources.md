# Alpha Metallurgical Resources

A leading US pure-play metallurgical (coking) coal producer, mining Central Appalachia for the seaborne and domestic steel markets — the successor to the restructured Alpha Natural Resources. Its driver is the met-coal price and global steel demand (China/India blast-furnace consumption), not thermal/power coal. A member of the [[Coal miners]] cohort and part of the met-coal core with [[Warrior Met Coal]], [[Ramaco]], [[Peabody Energy]], and [[Core Natural Resources]].
