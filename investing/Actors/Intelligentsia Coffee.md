# Intelligentsia Coffee

Chicago-founded specialty coffee roaster, a pioneer of direct-trade sourcing. Acquired by Peet's Coffee ([[JDE Peet's]]) in 2015, part of the [[JAB Holding]] coffee portfolio alongside [[Stumptown Coffee]].
