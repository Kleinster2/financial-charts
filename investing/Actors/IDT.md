IDT (Integrated Device Technology) — former US semiconductor company specializing in memory interface chips, PCIe switches, and timing solutions. Acquired by [[Renesas]] in 2019 for $6.7B. IDT's memory interface product line remains a key competitor to [[Montage]] and [[Rambus]] under the Renesas brand.
