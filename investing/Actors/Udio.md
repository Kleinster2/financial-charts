## Streaming fraud vector

AI music generation tools including Udio are being used for large-scale streaming fraud. [[Deezer]] reported (Mar 2026) that 80%+ of AI-generated music streams on its platform are fraudulent — bots playing thousands of AI-generated songs to extract royalties. 60,000+ AI tracks are uploaded daily to Deezer alone (39% of daily intake), with 13mn+ tagged as AI-created in 2025. Industry estimate: 5-10% of content across all streaming platforms is fraudulent AI. [[IFPI]] CEO Victoria Oakley: *"This is theft."*
