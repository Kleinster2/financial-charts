**Trojena** -- [[NEOM]]'s planned mountain tourism and winter-sports destination in northwest [[Saudi Arabia]]. It was originally the venue for the 2029 Asian Winter Games, but the event was postponed in January 2026 and Trojena is now a clear marker of how [[Saudi PIF]] is sequencing NEOM toward must-have industrial/logistics infrastructure before good-to-have prestige tourism.

## Strategy reset relevance

Trojena is not dead, but its loss of the 2029 Asian Winter Games deadline changes the investment case. It moves from a hard-date global event deliverable to a phased tourism asset competing for capital against [[Oxagon]], data centers, logistics, and other domestic infrastructure with clearer near-term strategic value.
