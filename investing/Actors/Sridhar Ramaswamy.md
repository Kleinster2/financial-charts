#actor #executive #person

**Sridhar Ramaswamy** — CEO of [[Snowflake]] since February 2024, succeeding Frank Slootman. Came to Snowflake via its 2023 acquisition of Neeva, the ad-free search startup he co-founded; before that ran [[Google]]'s advertising business (SVP, ads & commerce) during its growth to a $100B+ revenue line. Has steered Snowflake's pivot from warehouse-growth story toward the enterprise-AI control-plane positioning (Cortex AI, Snowflake Intelligence, the $6B [[Amazon]] Graviton/AI commitment).
