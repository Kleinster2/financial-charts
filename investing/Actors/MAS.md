**MAS** — [[Singapore]]'s central bank, financial regulator, and national financial development agency, all in one institution. Founded 1971. Uniquely uses the exchange rate (SGD nominal effective exchange rate band) rather than interest rates as its primary monetary policy tool. Regulates banking, insurance, securities, and digital payment tokens under the Financial Services and Markets Act (FSMA). Manages a portion of Singapore's foreign reserves alongside [[GIC]].
