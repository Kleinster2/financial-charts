#actor

Shenji is [[NIO]]'s spun-off in-house automotive chip unit, built to match NIO's smart-driving algorithms and sensor layout while reducing dependence on [[NVIDIA]].

---

## Apr 2026 Reuters disclosure

[[NIO]] CEO William Li told [[Reuters]] on Apr 24, 2026 that NIO developed its own silicon because Nvidia automotive chips carry very high gross margins and because in-house chips can be matched more tightly to NIO's algorithms and sensor layout. Shenji is open to supplying chips to outside customers.

In the [[Automotive AI]] race, Shenji is the clearest example of a Chinese EV maker treating silicon as a strategic control point rather than just another sourced component.
