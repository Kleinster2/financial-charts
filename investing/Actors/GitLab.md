| **Dmytro Zaporozhets** | Co-founder (departed 2021) | Ukrainian developer, created original project |

### Origin story

- 2011: Zaporozhets created GitLab as open-source project in Ukraine
- Working from house without running water
- Wanted better collaboration tool for programmers
- 2012: Sijbrandij emailed Zaporozhets: "I started GitLab.com, hope you don't mind"
- Zaporozhets agreed to commercialize together
- 2013: Incorporated as GitLab B.V. in Netherlands
- 2014: Reincorporated as GitLab Inc. in Delaware
- 2015: Entire 9-person team flew to Silicon Valley for YC

### Ukrainian tech success

- First partly-Ukrainian unicorn (2018)
- Zaporozhets became richest Ukrainian techie
- Forbes 2021: estimated his fortune at $450M (\#23 richest Ukrainian)
