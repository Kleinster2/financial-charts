# Maybe Mars

Beijing-based independent record label founded in 2007 by [[Michael Pettis]] (Carnegie Endowment senior fellow + Peking University finance professor). Maybe Mars consolidated the Chinese indie-music scene through the late 2000s and 2010s, releasing bands launched at Pettis's earlier D-22 rock club (2002-2012): Carsick Cars, Snapline, Hedgehog, The Gar, plus subsequent additions. Caixin Global profiled Pettis as "the godfather of Beijing's indie music scene" in 2017 on the strength of the Maybe Mars roster + the D-22 → XP venue lineage.
