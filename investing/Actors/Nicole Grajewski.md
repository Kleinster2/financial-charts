# Nicole Grajewski

[[Iran]] specialist at [[Sciences Po]]. Named in the Financial Times' April 14, 2026 investigation into the [[IRGC]]'s operational use of the [[TEE-01B]] satellite (see [[IRGC Chinese satellite targeting March 2026]]). Grajewski confirmed the satellite's military role on grounds that it is operated by the IRGC Aerospace Force rather than Iran's civilian [[Iranian Space Agency]].

## Notable statement (FT, April 14, 2026)

"This satellite is clearly being used for military purposes, as it is being run by the IRGC's Aerospace Force and not Iran's civilian space programme... Iran really needs this foreign-provided capability during this war, as it allows the IRGC to identify targets ahead of time and check the success of its strikes."
