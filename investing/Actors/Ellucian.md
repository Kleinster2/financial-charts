#actor #software #edtech #private

**Ellucian** — leading higher-education student information system (SIS) and ERP vendor, headquartered in Reston, Virginia. Owned by Blackstone, [[Vista Equity Partners]], and others. Acquired the Enterprise Operations business of [[Anthology]] (formerly Campus Management products) for approximately $70 million in the Anthology Chapter 11 sale, closed December 31, 2025 — consolidating its position in higher-ed administrative software. Ellucian's SIS is complementary to LMS products like [[Canvas LMS]] and [[D2L Brightspace]] (which Ellucian does not compete with directly).
