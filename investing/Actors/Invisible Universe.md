# Invisible Universe

Invisible Universe is an internet-first animation studio creating animated IP for social media platforms. The company has built 7 original animated franchises with a combined 13M+ followers across [[TikTok]], [[Instagram]], and [[YouTube]]. In May 2025, it launched Invisible Studio, an AI-powered engine that enables faster production of animated content, positioning the company at the intersection of [[AI-Native Studios|AI-native content creation]] and the [[Creator economy]].

Founded by Tricia Biggio, Invisible Universe raised a $20M Series A led by [[Seven Seven Six]] ([[Alexis Ohanian]]'s fund), with celebrity backers including Serena Williams, Jennifer Aniston, and Will Smith participating. The company's model — owning original animated characters rather than licensing existing IP — gives it full control over merchandising, content, and platform distribution. Its bet is that social-first animated characters (short-form, episodic, meme-native) can build audiences and monetize faster than traditional animation pipelines, especially with AI reducing production costs.
