## NTO battery program

- Partnered with [[CBMM]] and [[Sojitz]] on [[Niobium]] titanium oxide anodes
- SCiB-Nb cells: 80% charge in 10 minutes, 15,000-cycle lifespan
- Began sample shipments June 2025
- Targeting e-buses, trucks, ships, stationary storage
