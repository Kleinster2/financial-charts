# ICBA

Independent Community Bankers of America. Trade association representing community banks across the [[United States]]. Primary advocacy organization pushing for reform of the [[Farm Credit System]], arguing FCS tax advantages create an unfair competitive playing field for agricultural lending.

## Quick Stats

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Trade association |
| Members | ~5,000 community banks |
| Key campaign | "Reform and Refocus the Farm Credit System" |
| Focus | Eliminating FCS tax exemption advantage |
