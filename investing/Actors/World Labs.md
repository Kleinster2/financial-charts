## Spatial intelligence thesis

**The problem:** LLMs understand language but not the physical world. Robots trained in simulators fail in real environments ("sim-to-real gap").

**The solution:** AI that understands 3D space — can estimate physicality of objects and environments, create digital replicas without extensive data collection.

**Li's vision:** "The world model is the key to achieving spatial intelligence and is the core main line for AI in the next decade after LLMs."
