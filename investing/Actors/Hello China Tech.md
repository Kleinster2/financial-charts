# Hello China Tech

Hello China Tech is an independent [[China]] technology newsletter by [[Poe Zhao]], focused on Chinese AI, cloud, robotics, EVs, semiconductors, policy, and industrial structure.

## Vault relevance

Hello China Tech is useful when [[China]] tech stories need a native-source filter rather than a Western headline filter. The publication's edge is translating Chinese-language source material into investable mechanisms: how policy creates demand, how procurement channels shape adoption, how platform companies subsidize usage, and how market-share claims differ depending on whether the metric is users, tokens, cloud revenue, or profit.
