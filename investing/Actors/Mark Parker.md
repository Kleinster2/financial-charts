Mark Parker — long-time Nike executive (CEO 2006-2020, then executive chairman). His move to executive chairman — stepping back from day-to-day CEO while staying on as chair — was the model [[Bob Iger]] cited for his own 2020 transition to executive chairman alongside [[Bob Chapek]] as Disney CEO (FT, Jun 2026), a structure Iger conceded "quickly went bad."
