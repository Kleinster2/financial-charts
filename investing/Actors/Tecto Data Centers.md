## Why Tecto matters

V.tal spun out its DC business to capture [[Brazil]]'s AI infrastructure boom:

- **Fiber advantage**: Every Tecto DC connects to V.tal's 450,000+ km fiber network
- **Submarine connectivity**: 26,000 km submarine cables to US, LatAm
- **Renewable focus**: São Paulo hyperscale will be 100% renewable
- **Strategic investors**: BTG, GIC, CPP provide patient capital for build-out
