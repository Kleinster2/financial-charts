# Gan AI

Indian AI startup focused on generative video personalization and AI agent tooling. Shortlisted for the [[India AI Impact Summit 2026|IndiaAI Mission]]'s indigenous foundation model development program.

---

## Synopsis

Gan AI's core product enables enterprises to generate personalized video at scale — a single recording can be transformed into thousands of unique versions with customized names, data points, and messaging for each recipient. The technology combines lip-sync generation, voice cloning, and dynamic content insertion, targeting use cases in sales outreach, HR communications, and marketing campaigns. The company has attracted enterprise clients across [[India]] and globally, positioning itself in the generative media layer that sits between foundation model providers and end-user applications.

The IndiaAI Mission shortlisting signals government recognition of Gan AI's technical capabilities and places it in the cohort of Indian startups racing to build indigenous AI models rather than simply wrapping Western APIs. The personalized video market is early-stage but growing rapidly as enterprises seek to scale outbound communication without losing the human touch. The risk for Gan AI is commoditization — as foundation models from [[OpenAI]], [[Google]], and open-source projects improve video generation capabilities, the middleware layer where Gan AI operates could face margin compression. The company's defensibility depends on proprietary training data, enterprise integrations, and execution speed in a market where the technology frontier shifts quarterly.
