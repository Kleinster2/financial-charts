**Kazatomprom** — World's largest uranium producer. State-owned Kazakh company. ~25% of global uranium supply. Critical for nuclear fuel supply chain.

---

## Why Kazatomprom matters

Kazatomprom is the Saudi Arabia of uranium:

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Global market share | ~25% |
| Production (2024) | ~22,000 tonnes U |
| Ranking | \#1 globally |
| Ticker | KAP (LSE, AIX) |

---

## Production

| Metric | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Method | In-situ leaching (ISL) |
| Cost | Among lowest globally |
| Reserves | Massive (decades of supply) |
| Location | Kazakhstan steppes |

ISL mining = lower cost, lower environmental impact than conventional mining.
