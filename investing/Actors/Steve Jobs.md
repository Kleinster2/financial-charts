Steve Jobs — co-founder and CEO of [[Apple]]; majority owner of [[Pixar]] before its 2006 sale to [[Disney]]. [[Bob Iger]] mended the Disney-Pixar relationship Jobs had declared dead, first by putting Disney content on the video iPod, then by acquiring Pixar. At a pivotal Pixar meeting Jobs listed every reason a deal would fail on a whiteboard before conceding the pros outweighed the cons (FT, Jun 2026).
