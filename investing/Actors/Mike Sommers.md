## Career

### American Petroleum Institute

**15th CEO of API** — largest trade association representing US oil & gas industry.

Since 2018: Overseen strategic realignment of API priorities, introduced integrated organizational structure and core API Principles.

### Prior career

| Years | Role |
|-------|------|
| 2016-2018 | President & CEO, American Investment Council (private equity trade group) |
| 2011-2015 | Chief of Staff to Speaker John Boehner |
| Earlier | Deputy CoS, Policy Director, Legislative Director, Press Secretary to Boehner |
| 2005 | Special Assistant to President, National Economic Council (White House) |

**10+ years in House leadership** — at center of every major policy decision.

### Major legislation involvement

- TARP (2008)
- Fiscal cliff resolution (2013)
- Bipartisan Budget Agreement (2015)
- Trade Promotion Authority (2015)

**Recognition:** Roll Call named him one of top 50 staffers in last 50 years (2015).
