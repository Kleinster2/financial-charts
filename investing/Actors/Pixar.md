Pixar — animation studio acquired by [[Disney]] in 2006 for $7B. The acquisition was [[Bob Iger]]'s foundational repair move: the Disney-Pixar relationship had collapsed under [[Michael Eisner]], and Iger mended ties with [[Steve Jobs]] (then Pixar's majority owner) before buying the studio. Pixar restored Disney's animation dominance and became a pillar of Iger's content-and-technology strategy.
