## Insights

- **Correspondent dominance** — ~$100B annual volume, ~20% market share. Buys closed loans from smaller originators, provides liquidity to fragmented market.
- **Dual business model** — Origination (correspondent + broker + consumer direct) plus servicing. Hedged against rate cycles: origination up when rates fall, servicing up when rates rise.
- **Growing broker channel** — $7.2B locked in Q2 2025. Competing with [[UWM]] for broker business.
