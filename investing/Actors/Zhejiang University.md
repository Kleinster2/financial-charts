Zhejiang University (ZJU) — top Chinese research university in [[Hangzhou]], [[Zhejiang]]. Key talent pipeline for the [[Hangzhou Robotics Cluster]]. Produced 102 AI startup executives by Sep 2024 (#3 nationally). Alumni founded 3 of 6 "[[Hangzhou Robotics Cluster|Six Little Dragons]]": [[DeepSeek]], [[Deep Robotics]], [[Manycore Tech]].

Key robotics/AI programs: College of Control Science and Engineering, College of Information Science & Electronic Engineering, Chu Kochen Honors College, Robotics Engineering major (est. 2019), AI undergraduate program (est. 2019, one of first 35 approved). Closely linked to [[Zhejiang Lab]] (之江实验室).
