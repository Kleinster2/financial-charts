**Daniel O'Day** is CEO of [[Gilead Sciences]] (GILD), a position he has held since 2019. Previously spent over 30 years at [[Roche]], where he led the pharmaceuticals division. Led [[Gilead Sciences]]'s dealmaking spree from 2024 to 2026, including the $21B [[Immunomedics]] acquisition (2020) and the $4.3B [[CymaBay Therapeutics]] acquisition (2024).
