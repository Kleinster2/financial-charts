## Background

Born 1998, raised Upper West Side of Manhattan. Attended public schools in Hell's Kitchen. Studied computer science at NYU, dropped out to pursue crypto/prediction markets.

**Early interest in markets:** At age 14, emailed SEC asking how to create new marketplaces. Fascination with information discovery through markets.

**Ethereum:** Participated in 2014 Ethereum ICO — early investor when ETH was pennies.

---

## Polymarket founding

Founded 2020 (age 22). Mission: combat "rampant misinformation" by creating markets where users bet on real-world outcomes. Started with predictions about NYC reopening during COVID.

**Oct 2025:** Became world's youngest self-made billionaire (age 27) after ICE investment valued Polymarket at $9B.
