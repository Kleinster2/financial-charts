**China Unicom** — one of China's three state-owned mobile network operators, with roughly 345mn mobile subscribers. Notable in this vault as the marquee early adopter of China's 2024 data-asset accounting rule: it reported Rmb204mn (~$29mn) in [[Data as an asset|data assets]] in its first filing under the new framework, drawing on subscriber data.
