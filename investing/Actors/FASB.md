#actor #usa #accounting #standard-setter #stub

**FASB (Financial Accounting Standards Board)** — the private body that sets [[GAAP]], the accounting standards US public companies must follow in [[SEC]] filings. Issues the Accounting Standards Codification (ASC). Relevant to this vault as the standard-setter that has no project to recognize internally generated [[Data as an asset|data as a balance-sheet asset]] — the gap [[Mike Kuiken]] argues it should close. International counterpart: [[IASB]] (which sets [[IFRS]]).
