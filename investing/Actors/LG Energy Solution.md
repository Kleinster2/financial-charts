## Why the pause?

**US EV demand collapse:**
- $7,500 tax credit ended Sept 2025
- GM/Ford halting EV investments
- Shifting to hybrids and ICE
- LG adjusting to lower demand

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- GM locked in long-term
- LFP pivot for cost competitiveness
- Arizona ESS capacity
- LMR technology breakthrough
- Korean government support

**Bear:**
- US EV demand collapse
- Plant pauses = cash burn
- CATL scale advantage
- GM pivoting away from EVs
- Overcapacity risk
