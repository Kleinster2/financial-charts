# Mário Frias

**Mário Frias** — Brazilian actor (early-2000s television fame via *Malhação* and similar) turned politician. Special Secretary of Culture in the Bolsonaro federal administration June 2020 – March 2022 (the fifth person to hold the position after Bolsonaro dissolved the Ministry of Culture in 2019). Federal Deputy for São Paulo for the [[PL]] (Partido Liberal) since the 2022 elections. Load-bearing in the May 2026 [[Brazil Week NYC 2026|Dark Horse]] scandal at *two* points simultaneously: executive producer and credited screenplay source of the *Dark Horse* Bolsonaro biopic, AND the PL deputy whose R$2M parliamentary emenda allegedly funded Instituto Conhecer Brasil, the non-profit subsequently tied to film producer Go Up Entertainment in the STF probe opened by Justice [[Flávio Dino]] on May 15, 2026 *(per Rio Times STF coverage)*.
