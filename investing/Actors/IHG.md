## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Strong international footprint (100+ countries)
- Holiday Inn brand recognition
- IHG One Rewards scale (145M members)
- European expansion (Novum deal)

**Bear:**
- Smaller than [[Marriott]]/[[Hilton]] in US
- British HQ = currency exposure
- Holiday Inn brand aging in some markets
