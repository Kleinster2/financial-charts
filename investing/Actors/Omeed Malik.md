# Omeed Malik

Managing partner of [[1789 Capital]], a right-leaning investment firm he founded in 2022 that positions itself as an alternative to "woke capital." Previously a managing director at [[Bank of America]] and a senior banker at [[Merrill Lynch]], where he focused on hedge fund and asset management clients. Malik has become a vocal critic of AI companies' governance alignment policies — he publicly mocked [[Anthropic]] at a defense summit in February 2026 over the company's cautious stance on Pentagon contracts, framing it as a national security liability. 1789 Capital invests in companies that the firm believes are undervalued because of ESG-driven divestment or political controversy, making it a contrarian vehicle for "anti-ESG" capital allocation. The firm raised ~$125M for its inaugural fund.
