## The bottleneck

**Gas turbines can't be built fast enough:**

> "There just aren't enough gas turbines to go around and the problem is probably going to get worse." — Paul Browning (ex-GE Power CEO)

| Constraint | Details |
|------------|---------|
| Current capacity | ~55 heavy-duty turbines/year |
| Expanding to | 70-80/year by 2026 |
| Demand | Far exceeds supply |
| Projects at risk | $400B+ through end of decade |
