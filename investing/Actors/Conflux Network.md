# Conflux Network

Chinese-origin public blockchain, notable as one of the few regulatory-compliant crypto networks with operational roots in mainland China. Uses a Tree-Graph consensus designed to balance scalability with security. Positioned as the host for [[AnchorX]]'s AxCNH offshore yuan stablecoin — a strategic placement if [[Belt and Road]] trade settlement scales on CNH-denominated rails.

## Strategic context

Conflux is the only major public blockchain with an operational presence in China tolerated by the Beijing regulatory environment, reflecting its early coordination with Chinese academic and government stakeholders. This gives it a structural edge as the potential default rail for any licensed CNH stablecoin or BRI-corridor settlement product. Whether that edge translates to volume depends on whether [[HKMA]] and [[PBOC]] bless the Conflux route or route licensed issuers onto permissioned infrastructure instead.
