Jimmy Kimmel — late-night host; Jimmy Kimmel Live! airs on [[ABC]], the Disney-owned network. Central figure in Disney's 2025-26 political-pressure saga: jokes targeting Charlie Kirk and "Lady Trump" drew escalating pressure from the Trump administration and the FCC (FT, Jun 2026). After a Kirk-shooting joke, Disney took Kimmel off the air and asked him to apologize; after a later "Lady Trump" joke, Trump demanded ABC fire him and the FCC ordered Disney into an early ABC licence-renewal process, but Disney stood firm.
