WuXi AppTec — Chinese contract research, development, and manufacturing organization (CRO/CDMO) for small-molecule drugs. Sibling company to [[WuXi Biologics]] within the broader WuXi ecosystem. Listed on Hong Kong Stock Exchange (2359.HK) and Shanghai Stock Exchange (603259.SS). Provides drug-discovery services, preclinical and clinical CRO, and small-molecule manufacturing. Named in the [[BIOSECURE Act]] (2024) alongside [[WuXi Biologics]] and [[BGI]] as a biotech entity of US national security concern.

## Apr 28, 2026 — Q1 beat: revenue +29%

WuXi AppTec posted Q1 2026 sales and net income above analyst estimates, with revenue growing 29% year-over-year. Per Bloomberg "The China Show" 4/28/2026 broadcast, results were supported by strong growth in discovery and management services for small-molecule drugs. The stock surged in Hong Kong on the print.

The Q1 beat is consistent with the broader [[China biotech R&D leadership]] structural arc — outlicensing trajectory ($30B 2024 → $100B 2025 → $60B YTD 2026) requires CDMO capacity, and WuXi AppTec is the largest Chinese small-molecule CDMO supplying the originator-licensee chain. The BIOSECURE Act's friction on US contract manufacturing partnerships had been the primary headwind through 2024-2025; the Q1 beat suggests CDMO clients have continued or expanded WuXi AppTec relationships despite the legislative overhang, or that non-US client growth has more than offset BIOSECURE-driven attrition.

*Sources: Bloomberg "The China Show" 4/28/2026 broadcast; WuXi AppTec Q1 2026 disclosure.*
