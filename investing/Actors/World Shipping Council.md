## AI cargo screening tool

Launched Sep 2025: real-time AI scanning of bookings to flag risky containers, targeting misdeclared hazardous cargo — a primary cause of the 250 container ship fires in 2024 ([[Allianz]] data).

Kramek (FT, Mar 15, 2026): *"We have seen too many tragic incidents where misdeclared cargo has led to catastrophic fires, including the loss of life."*
