# Endeavor

Endeavor Group Holdings is the Hollywood entertainment and sports conglomerate built around the [[WME]] talent agency, led by CEO [[Ariel Emanuel]]. It is the controlling shareholder of [[TKO Group Holdings]] (~61.7%), the listed UFC + WWE company it created by merging [[UFC]] into [[WWE]] in September 2023. [[Silver Lake]] — Endeavor's largest investor since 2012 — took the company private in March 2025, so Endeavor now sits between Silver Lake and TKO in the control chain. The take-private rebranded the parent WME Group.

## Ownership

Post take-private, Endeavor (WME Group) is controlled by [[Silver Lake]] together with a co-investor group; management (CEO [[Ariel Emanuel]] and Executive Chairman Patrick Whitesell) holds rollover equity. Co-investors in the 2025 transaction included sovereign and institutional capital alongside Silver Lake. Precise post-close percentages are not publicly disclosed; the economically significant public exposure to Endeavor's assets now runs through its ~61.7% stake in the listed [[TKO Group Holdings]].
