## DFO Management (family office)

**Formerly MSD Capital** (1998-2022):
- Founded 1998 with $400M
- Co-managing partners: John Phelan, Glenn Fuhrman
- Generated $17B+ profits through 2019
- 2021: Gregg Lemkau (ex-Goldman) joined as CEO
- Dec 2022: Restructured as DFO Management

**MSD Partners** (2009): Outside investor vehicle. Merged with Byron Trott's BDT & Company (2023) → [[BDT & MSD Partners]].
