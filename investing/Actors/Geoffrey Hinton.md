# Geoffrey Hinton

**Geoffrey Hinton** is a foundational neural-network researcher, University of Toronto professor emeritus, and 2024 Nobel Prize in Physics co-laureate whose work on artificial neural networks helped create the technical lineage that later produced deep learning and frontier AI.

## Why he matters

Hinton matters to the investing vault because the current AI capital cycle rests on the neural-network research tradition he helped establish. His work predates the transformer era, but it created the intellectual and institutional ecosystem that trained later researchers, including the Toronto and [[Google Brain]] circles that fed frontier-lab talent. For [[Chris Olah]], Hinton is relevant less as a company operator than as part of the lineage from neural-network science to interpretability research.
