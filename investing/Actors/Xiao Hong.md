# Xiao Hong

Co-founder and chief executive of [[Manus AI]] (parent: Butterfly Effect). Together with [[Ji Yichao]] (chief scientist), built the Chinese-founded [[AI agents|AI agent]] platform that closed a $2B [[Meta]] acquisition in December 2025 — the showcase deal for the [[Singapore washing]] offshore-restructuring playbook. Summoned to Beijing by [[NDRC]] on March 22, 2026 and informed he could not leave mainland China during the regulatory review (cited as 3-12 months). With Xiao held in mainland China and ~100 Manus engineers already moved to Meta's [[Singapore]] office, the deal could not close operationally even before [[NDRC]] formally ordered the unwind on April 27, 2026.
