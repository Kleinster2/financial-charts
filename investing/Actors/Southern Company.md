# Southern Company

**Southern Company (SO)** — one of the largest US regulated electric utilities, headquartered in Atlanta; NYSE-listed. Operates regulated electric utilities across the Southeast (Georgia Power, Alabama Power, Mississippi Power) plus Southern Company Gas. Notable as the builder of Vogtle Units 3 and 4 — the only new US nuclear reactors completed in a generation (2023-24). A pure-regulated, high-dividend, low-volatility (~17%) name, it forms the tightest pair in the [[Regulated utilities]] cohort with [[Duke Energy]] (0.853).
