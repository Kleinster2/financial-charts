# Jeff Yan

Jeff Yan is the co-founder of [[Hyperliquid]] and its namesake L1 blockchain. Former trader at [[Hudson River Trading]]. Built Hyperliquid in response to the [[FTX]] collapse (2022), deliberately taking no venture capital — a point of pride in an industry still scarred by the blowups of the last cycle.
