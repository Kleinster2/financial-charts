## What makes Crypto.com, Crypto.com

**Full-stack crypto platform:** Exchange, wallet, debit card, DeFi, NFTs — everything under one brand. Aggressive marketing: Staples Center naming rights, Super Bowl ads, Matt Damon "Fortune Favors the Brave."

**Prediction markets pivot (2024-2026):** Launched sports event contracts via CFTC-regulated CDNA (Crypto.com Derivatives North America). Partnered with [[Truth Social]] and Fanatics. Sports now fastest-growing segment.

## Prediction markets (CDNA)

**Crypto.com Derivatives North America** — CFTC-registered exchange/clearinghouse

| Date | Development |
|------|-------------|
| Dec 2024 | Launched sports event contracts (NFL Super Bowl) |
| Oct 2025 | [[Truth Social]] partnership — Truth Predict |
| Dec 2025 | Fanatics partnership — Fanatics Markets |
| Jan 2026 | Travis McGhee promoted to Global Head of Predictions |

**Product:** Yes/no contracts on sports, elections, interest rates, commodities.

**Marszalek quote:** "We saw a significant opportunity when we first launched our initial sports event contracts in 2024, and their popularity and our platform have grown exponentially since."
