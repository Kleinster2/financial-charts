**Phillips 66** — Major US refiner and midstream operator. Gulf Coast heavy crude capacity. Venezuela beneficiary.

---

## Why Phillips 66 matters for Venezuela

**Gulf Coast refineries configured for heavy crude:**

- Alliance refinery (Louisiana) — heavy crude capable
- Sweeny refinery (Texas) — complex, flexible
- Venezuela return = cheaper feedstock option

## Diversification advantage

**More diversified than pure refiners:**

- Midstream provides stable cash flow
- Chemicals less cyclical
- Marketing adds margin

**[[Trade]]-off:** Less pure-play refining leverage.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
