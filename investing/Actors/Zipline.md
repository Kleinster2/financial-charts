## Technology

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Aircraft | Fixed-wing, catapult launch |
| Payload | ~2 kg |
| Range | ~80 km round trip |
| Delivery | Parachute drop (no landing) |
| Speed | ~100 km/h |

**Advantage:** Fixed-wing more efficient than multirotor for distance. Parachute drop avoids landing infrastructure.
