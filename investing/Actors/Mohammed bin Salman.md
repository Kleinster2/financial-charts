**Mohammed bin Salman** — Crown Prince and de facto ruler of [[Saudi Arabia]]. Architect of Vision 2030 economic diversification. Key figure in the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|Iran conflict]] — reportedly the "principal incentivador" of Trump's strikes on Iran. Saudi oil exits via Red Sea (not [[Strait of Hormuz]]), making MBS a beneficiary of Hormuz closure through elevated prices on continued production.
