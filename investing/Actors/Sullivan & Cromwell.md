# Sullivan & Cromwell

Major NY-based US law firm, one of the original "white shoe" firms whose institutional culture the four Jewish [[NYU Law School]] founders of [[Wachtell Lipton]] reacted against in 1965. Long-running M&A, banking, and securities practice; peer of [[Wachtell Lipton]], [[Cravath, Swaine & Moore]], [[Davis Polk]], [[Skadden]], and [[Paul, Weiss]] in elite NY corporate work.

## AI hallucination filing (April 2026)

Sullivan & Cromwell told a US federal bankruptcy court that a major filing it made in a high-profile case contained multiple AI-generated hallucinations — one of a run of 2026 elite-firm incidents (alongside [[Pinsent Masons]]) that extended the professional-sanction risk first crystallised by the June 2025 London High Court judgment.
