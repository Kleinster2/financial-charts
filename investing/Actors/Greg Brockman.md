# Greg Brockman

[[OpenAI]] co-founder and president. Previously CTO of [[Stripe]] (2010-2015). Co-founded OpenAI in 2015 alongside [[Sam Altman]], [[Ilya Sutskever]], and others. Went on extended sabbatical in late 2024; returned by March 2026 to lead computing efforts and help [[Fidji Simo]] oversee the "superapp" product revamp and organizational restructuring.
