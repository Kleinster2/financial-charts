## Business model

**Membership economics:**
- Low product margins (~11%)
- Membership fees = profit
- 93% renewal = predictable revenue
- Limited SKUs = efficiency

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Membership moat (93% renewal)
- Value proposition in inflation
- International expansion
- Kirkland brand strength
- E-commerce growth

**Bear:**
- Valuation premium (~50x P/E)
- Warehouse saturation risk
- Amazon/Walmart competition
- Labor costs
