## Business model

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Membership fees | ~$4.5B/year (nearly all profit) |
| Product margin | ~11% (breakeven intentional) |
| SKU count | ~4,000 (vs 30K+ at competitors) |
| Renewal rate | 93% (US/[[Canada]]) |

**Key insight**: Costco doesn't make money on products—membership fees are the profit.

## Investment thesis

**Bull:**
- Membership moat unbreakable
- Value proposition in any economy
- International expansion runway
- Kirkland brand equity
- E-commerce opportunity
- Pricing power (membership fees)

**Bear:**
- Valuation premium (50x+ P/E)
- Warehouse saturation in US
- Amazon/Walmart competition
- Labor costs rising
- Limited margin expansion
