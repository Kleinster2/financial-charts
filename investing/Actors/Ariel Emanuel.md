**Ariel Emanuel** — Executive Chairman & CEO of [[TKO]] Group Holdings (formed from [[WWE]]-UFC merger, 2023). Also Executive Chairman of [[WME Group]] (formerly Endeavor). Brother of former Chicago mayor Rahm Emanuel. Led Endeavor from talent agency to global sports/entertainment conglomerate before taking it private (~$13B, 2024) with [[Silver Lake]].
