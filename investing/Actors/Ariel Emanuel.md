Ariel Emanuel is the Executive Chairman and CEO of [[TKO]] Group Holdings, the publicly traded entity formed from the September 2023 merger of [[WWE]] and UFC. He is also Executive Chairman of [[WME Group]] (formerly Endeavor Group Holdings), which he took private in a ~$13B deal with [[Silver Lake]] in April 2024 after concluding that public markets undervalued the conglomerate's asset mix.

Emanuel co-led WME (later Endeavor) from 2001, transforming it from a Hollywood talent agency into a global sports, entertainment, and live events conglomerate through acquisitions including UFC (2016, $4B), Professional Bull Riders, and the Miss Universe Organization. The WWE-UFC merger under TKO created the dominant combat sports/entertainment platform, with a combined enterprise value exceeding $20B. His brother is Rahm Emanuel, former Chicago mayor and former US ambassador to [[Japan]]. The personality model for [[Ari Gold]] in the HBO series *Entourage*.
