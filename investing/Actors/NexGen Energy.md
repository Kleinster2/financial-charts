# NexGen Energy

**NexGen Energy (NXE)** — Canadian uranium developer headquartered in Vancouver, listed on the NYSE and TSX (NXE) and ASX (NXG). Its flagship is the Rook I project (Arrow deposit) in the Athabasca Basin, Saskatchewan — one of the largest and highest-grade undeveloped uranium deposits in the world. Pre-production, targeting first output late this decade, with offtake contracts already signed with US utilities. A development-stage member of the [[Uranium equity beta]] cohort, levered to the uranium supply-deficit thesis rather than current spot — it tightly partners [[Denison Mines]] (the cohort's tightest pair, 0.89).
