# Natoma

Natoma is an enterprise AI-agent infrastructure company that [[Snowflake]] agreed to acquire in May 2026. Snowflake described the deal as a way to add Model Context Protocol governance and secure agent access controls to its AI Data Cloud, making Natoma part of the Snowflake Cortex AI and agentic-AI stack rather than a standalone public-market actor.
