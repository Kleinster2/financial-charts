**IDG Capital** — one of [[China]]'s oldest and largest venture/growth equity firms, founded 1992. Pioneer of VC in China. ~$20B+ AUM. Backed [[Baidu]], [[Tencent]] (early), [[Xiaomi]], [[SenseTime]], and hundreds of others. US-origin (International Data Group) but China operations became the dominant franchise.
