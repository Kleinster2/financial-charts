# Public Storage

Public Storage (NYSE: PSA) is the largest US self-storage REIT, owning and operating thousands of storage facilities under the Public Storage brand. A pure single-asset REIT levered to self-storage demand (the "4 Ds" — death, divorce, dislocation, downsizing — plus moves and small-business inventory) and local supply. The bellwether of the [[Self-storage REITs]] cohort.
