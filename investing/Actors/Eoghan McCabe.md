# Eoghan McCabe
Co-founder and CEO of [[Intercom]]. Stepped down in 2020, returned as CEO October 2022, restructured the company around AI. Previously co-founded Contrast (software design consultancy) and Exceptional (bug-tracking tool, sold to Rackspace 2011).
