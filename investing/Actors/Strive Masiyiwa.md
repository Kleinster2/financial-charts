**Strive Masiyiwa** — Zimbabwean billionaire, founder and chairman of [[Econet Group]]. Built Econet Wireless into Africa's largest independent telecom group after a 5-year legal battle for a license (1993-98). Also founded Liquid Intelligent Technologies (pan-African fiber/cloud). Forbes estimated net worth ~$2.7B. Sits on boards of [[Netflix]], Unilever, [[National Geographic]], and the Rockefeller Foundation.
