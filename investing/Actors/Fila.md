# Fila

**Italian-Korean sportswear brand.** Founded in Italy (1911), now owned by [[Fila Holdings]] (South Korea). [[Anta Sports]] holds China license — Fila China is ~40% of Anta's revenue and its premium growth engine. Heritage in tennis and basketball. Global brand with fragmented regional ownership structure.
