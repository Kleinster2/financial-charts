# Fila

Italian-origin sportswear brand (founded 1911), now globally owned by [[Fila Holdings]] / Misto Holdings Corp (South Korea). [[Anta Sports]] holds the [[China]] license — Fila China generated RMB 26.6B (~$3.7B) in 2024, representing ~38% of Anta's total revenue and its premium growth engine. The Korean parent (consolidated revenue ₩4,269B / ~$3.1B in 2024) includes [[Acushnet]] (Titleist, FootJoy) via a 51% stake. US business shuttered in 2024 with a $78.6M operating loss; re-entry model TBD.

## Ownership structure

| Entity | Role |
|--------|------|
| [[Fila Holdings]] / Misto Holdings (Korea) | Global brand owner, public (KRX) |
| [[Anta Sports]] | China license holder (since 2009) |
| [[Acushnet]] (51% owned by Fila Holdings) | Titleist, FootJoy — majority of consolidated revenue |

Fila handed China business rights to Anta in 2009 for a low single-digit % of annual sales as a design fee. Design fees expected to rise from ₩80B (2024) to ₩90B (2025) as China business grows.

## Anta's Fila segment (China)

| Period | Revenue | Growth | Operating margin |
|--------|---------|--------|-----------------|
| FY2024 | RMB 26.6B | +6.1% | ~27-28% |
| H1 2025 | RMB 14.2B | +8.6% | 27.7% |

Fila China is Anta's premium lifestyle brand, positioned between the mass-market Anta brand and the ultra-premium [[Descente]]. Heritage in tennis and basketball, now riding retro fashion trends.

---

## US business collapse

| Year | Fila USA operating loss |
|------|------------------------|
| 2023 | -$108.8M |
| 2024 | -$78.6M |

Fila Holdings shuttered US operations and is selling off inventory through end of 2025. Re-entry options under evaluation: direct operations, licensing, or distribution model. 2025 overall guidance: flat to +5% revenue, driven by [[Acushnet]] and China design fees, excluding Fila North America.

---

## 2025 outlook

[[Fila Holdings]] guided 2025 operating profit of ₩507B (consensus), +28% YoY, driven by:
- Rising China design fees (Anta's Fila segment growing)
- Acushnet stability (Titleist/FootJoy)
- US loss elimination
