# Jacob Kling

40-year-old M&A partner at [[Wachtell Lipton]] — protégé of [[Edward Herlihy]] and recently named co-head of the M&A practice with David Lam (50). Also added to the firm's executive committee. Per the FT's May 2026 account, Kling's promotion is read as Wachtell's explicit succession plan to keep its bank-sector M&A franchise intact after Herlihy's eventual departure — and to give the firm a young, high-revenue partner enough institutional standing to dissuade a Kirkland- or Latham-style outside offer.
