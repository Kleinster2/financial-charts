# ICE

ICE (Intercontinental Exchange, NYSE: ICE) is a US-based exchange operator and clearing house that runs commodity and financial derivatives markets globally, including ICE Futures Europe (home of the [[Brent]] crude oil contract and European natural gas benchmarks) and ICE Futures US. ICE is the primary clearing venue for [[Baltic Exchange]] freight futures (FFAs), including the TD3C (Middle East Gulf → China [[VLCC]]) contract that underlies the [[BWET]] ETF.

| Capesize FFA | BCI | [[BDRY]] |
| Panamax FFA | BPI | [[BDRY]] |

## Other relevance

ICE also hosts the [[Brent]] crude futures market, European TTF natural gas, and a wide range of financial and credit derivatives. Outside of shipping, ICE owns the [[NYSE]].
