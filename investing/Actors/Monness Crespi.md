# Monness Crespi

**Monness Crespi** Hardt is a New York-based boutique equity research and brokerage firm known for contrarian sell-side calls and independent analysis.

## Synopsis

Monness Crespi Hardt occupies a distinctive niche in the sell-side landscape — a small, independent shop that regularly takes positions against consensus, giving it outsized visibility when those calls hit. The firm covers technology, internet, fintech, and consumer sectors with a lean analyst roster that punches above its weight in media attention. Their February 2026 downgrade of [[Coinbase]] to Sell with a $120 price target (against near-universal Street bullishness) exemplifies the contrarian approach that defines the franchise.

In the investment research ecosystem, boutique shops like Monness Crespi serve a specific function: they provide institutional clients with variant perspectives that the large banks — constrained by investment banking relationships — cannot or will not publish. The firm's staying power since its founding in 1979 reflects a viable if small business model: agency trading commissions and soft-dollar arrangements from institutional clients who value independent, unconflicted research. For the vault, Monness Crespi calls are worth tracking as a contrarian signal — when they diverge sharply from bulge-bracket consensus, the underlying analysis often surfaces risks the market is underpricing.
