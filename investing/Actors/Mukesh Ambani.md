# Mukesh Ambani

Chair and managing director of [[Reliance Industries]], India's largest private-sector conglomerate (petrochemicals, refining, retail, media, and telecoms/digital via [[Jio Platforms]]). A second-generation tycoon (his father Dhirubhai founded Reliance); aged 69 and grooming his three children for succession. India's second-richest person as of June 2026, having been overtaken by [[Adani Group|Gautam Adani]] after the Iran-war crude move hit Reliance's refining business.

## Notable

On June 19 2026 Ambani filed the draft prospectus for the [[Jio Platforms]] IPO — set to be India's largest ever (~$4bn for a ~2.5% float, ~$160bn implied) — calling it "the most important value creation milestone this year" and naming his three children to lead the process.
