# Todd Young

Role: US Senator
State: Indiana
Party: Republican
In office since: 2017

Co-author of the CHIPS and Science Act (2022) alongside [[Chuck Schumer]] — the landmark $52B+ semiconductor subsidy bill designed to reshore chip manufacturing to the US. One of the most tech-fluent Republican senators. Spoke at the [[Hill and Valley Forum]] in May 2024. Active on AI policy and US competitiveness vis-à-vis China.
