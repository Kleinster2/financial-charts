# Alation

Data-catalog and "data intelligence" vendor — popularized the machine-learning-assisted catalog (behavioral analysis of query logs to surface trusted data). Founded 2012 in Redwood City (Satyen Sangani and co-founders). Sits in the same legacy-pure-play tier as [[Collibra]], facing the same question as catalogs shift from a human-search surface to an agent-consumption surface.
