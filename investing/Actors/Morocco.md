#stub

Morocco is a North African kingdom and the world's dominant phosphate power, controlling ~70% of global phosphate rock reserves via state-owned [[OCP Group]]. The Jorf Lasfar complex is the world's largest integrated phosphate fertilizer facility. Morocco is also a growing hub for automotive and aerospace manufacturing with proximity to European markets.
