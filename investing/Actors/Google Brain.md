#actor #ai #research

**Google Brain** — [[Google]]'s deep learning research division (2011-2023). Merged with [[DeepMind]] in April 2023 to form [[Google DeepMind]]. Founded by [[Jeff Dean]], Andrew Ng, and Greg Corrado. Produced TensorFlow, the Transformer paper ("Attention Is All You Need," 2017), and trained researchers who seeded most major AI labs.
