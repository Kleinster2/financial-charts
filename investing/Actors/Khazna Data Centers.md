# Khazna Data Centers

> [!info] One-line read
> The dominant data-center operator of the [[UAE]] and the wider Middle East (70%+ regional colocation share), a [[G42]] company now part-owned by [[MGX]] and [[Silver Lake]] — and the builder of the first 200 MW of [[Project Stargate|Stargate UAE]]. The physical-asset spine of Abu Dhabi's sovereign-AI build.

Khazna Data Centers is the largest data-center platform in the [[UAE]] and the Middle East, controlling 70%+ of regional colocation capacity. Headquartered in Abu Dhabi, it is the operating arm behind the Emirates' hyperscale and sovereign-AI buildout — including the first 200 MW cluster of [[Project Stargate|Stargate UAE]] and a separate 200 MW [[Microsoft]]/[[G42]] expansion landing by end-2026.
