# Blink Fitness

**Budget gym chain** — [[Equinox]] Group's affordable brand. Founded 2011, $15-45/month memberships. Filed Chapter 11 bankruptcy August 2024. 100+ locations, 2,000 employees, $280M+ debt. Seeking sale while keeping gyms open. Parent Equinox not affected.

## Why bankruptcy?

- **COVID aftermath:** Slow membership recovery
- **Rising costs:** Rent, labor, utilities
- **Competition:** Planet Fitness, Crunch, others
- **Debt burden:** $280M+ unsustainable

Budget fitness is a brutal business — high fixed costs, low margins, commodity service.
