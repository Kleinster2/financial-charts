# Conor Gleeson

Co-founder of [[Sacra]], the private-company research platform that provides independent financial analysis and revenue estimates for venture-backed startups.

## Synopsis

Conor Gleeson co-founded [[Sacra]] alongside [[Walter Chen]] and [[Jan-Erik Asplund]] to fill a gap in the private markets: credible, independent financial research on companies that don't file public disclosures. Sacra's model involves bottom-up revenue estimation using alternative data sources, employee counts, and industry benchmarking, then packaging the analysis into subscriber-facing reports. The platform covers companies from Series A through pre-IPO, serving VCs, LPs, and corporate development teams who need diligence beyond the pitch deck.

Gleeson's contribution sits on the research and analytical side of Sacra's operations. The company occupies an interesting niche as private markets grow — the same information asymmetry that makes private investing lucrative also makes independent research valuable. Sacra competes loosely with [[PitchBook]], Forge, and Carta's data products, but differentiates on depth of individual company analysis rather than database breadth.
