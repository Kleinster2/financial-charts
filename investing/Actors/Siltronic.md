## GlobalWafers bid (failed)

**2021-2022:**
- GlobalWafers ([[Taiwan]]) bid €4.35B
- German government blocked on security grounds
- Siltronic remained independent
- Reflects strategic importance of wafer supply

## Wafer demand data

**Siltronic publishes useful market data:**

From 2025-12-29 daily notes:
- Datacenter = 15% of wafer consumption but 50% of incremental 2025 demand
- Servers +23% y/y
- Smartphones = 22% share (still biggest)
- PC +8%, Auto +7%
- Total wafer consumption +8% in 2025

**AI is THE demand driver.**
