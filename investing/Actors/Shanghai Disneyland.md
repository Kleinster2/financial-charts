Shanghai Disneyland — Disney theme park in [[China]], a joint venture with a Shanghai state-owned enterprise. [[Bob Iger]] considers its opening among his greatest achievements and planned to attend its 10th anniversary in June 2026 (FT, Jun 2026). One of Disney's most successful international parks.
