# WME

WME (William Morris Endeavor) is the premier Hollywood talent agency at the core of [[Endeavor]], representing talent across film, television, music, sports, and digital. It was formed in 2009 by the merger of the William Morris Agency and the Endeavor Talent Agency. After [[Silver Lake]] took [[Endeavor]] private in March 2025, the parent group was rebranded WME Group, with the agency remaining its anchor business.
