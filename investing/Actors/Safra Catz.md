#actor #tech #ai

**Safra Catz** — Executive Vice Chair of [[Oracle]]'s board of directors. Served as Oracle CEO from 2014 to 2025, succeeding founder [[Larry Ellison]]. One of the highest-paid women in tech ($170M+ in peak years). Israeli-born American. Net worth ~$3.1B. Also joined the [[Paramount]]-[[Skydance]] board after the 2025 merger.

## Significance

Catz transformed Oracle from a legacy database company into a cloud and AI infrastructure player. Under her leadership, Oracle landed major cloud deals (Pentagon JEDI bid, TikTok hosting deal) and became a key [[Stargate]] partner. Her closeness to the Trump administration and appearances at the [[Hill and Valley Forum]] position Oracle at the center of government tech spending.
