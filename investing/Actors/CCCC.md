# CCCC

**China Communications Construction Company** — Chinese state-owned infrastructure giant. One of world's largest construction/engineering firms. Owns 32% of [[Mota-Engil]] (Portugal). Active in LatAm via [[Mota-Engil]] partnership.

## Why CCCC matters

**Belt and Road infrastructure arm:**

- **Scale**: One of world's largest construction companies
- **Global reach**: Ports, bridges, tunnels, roads across 150+ countries
- **LatAm expansion**: Via [[Mota-Engil]] partnership (32% stake acquired 2021)
- **Sanctions risk**: US-sanctioned for South China Sea activities

---

## [[Mota-Engil]] partnership

CCCC acquired 32.4% of Portuguese construction group [[Mota-Engil]] in 2021. This gives CCCC access to:

- LatAm markets (Brazil, Mexico, Peru, Colombia)
- European markets (Portugal, Poland)
- African markets (Angola, Mozambique)

**Key project:** [[Santos-Guarujá Tunnel]] — R$6.8B Brazil PPP won by [[Mota-Engil]]/CCCC (Sep 2025).
