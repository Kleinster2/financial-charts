**Aloizio Mercadante** — President of [[BNDES]] (since Jan 2023), Brazil's development bank. Senior PT figure, former senator, education minister, and Lula's chief of staff. Attended [[Lula]]'s Brasília meeting on the [[Raizen]] crisis (~Feb 2026). His economic team demanded a concrete capitalization plan from [[Cosan]] before considering any BNDES support for Raízen.
