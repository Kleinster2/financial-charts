# CS Disco

US public legal-tech company; cloud-native AI-driven ediscovery, legal hold, and document-review platform. NYSE: LAW. Micro-cap (~$295M, May 2026) — the only listed US legal-tech pure-play. Directly exposed to the [[Big Law transformation]] thesis as both beneficiary (firms shedding in-house ediscovery to vendors) and casualty (vulnerable to [[Harvey]] / [[Legora]] subsuming workflow above ediscovery).

## Vault read

CS Disco sits at the riskiest point in the [[Big Law transformation]] equity surface. It is a pure-play legal-tech name (no diversification cushion like [[RELX]] or [[Thomson Reuters]]), too small to reach scale-defensive moats, and structurally exposed to AI-platform competition that compresses the document-review value layer. The Big Law transformation thesis says junior-associate hours are the most disrupted; ediscovery sits adjacent to that work.
