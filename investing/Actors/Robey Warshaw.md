# Robey Warshaw

Robey Warshaw is a London-based independent M&A advisory firm founded in 2013 by [[Simon Robey]] (ex-[[Morgan Stanley]] UK) and [[Simon Warshaw]] (ex-[[UBS]] UK), with Philip Apostolides joining shortly after. Small by staff count (roughly a dozen bankers pre-acquisition) but outsized by mandate profile — the firm advised on some of the largest UK-listed deals of the 2010s and 2020s, including the Sky/Fox transaction, Unilever, AstraZeneca, and BHP.

On 2025-07-30, [[Evercore]] announced the acquisition of Robey Warshaw for £146M / $196M, payable in two tranches — first tranche in Evercore stock at closing, second tranche in stock or cash at the one-year anniversary, with multi-year performance contingency on top. The deal closed Q4 2025. Post-close, [[Evercore]] has more than 400 bankers across nine EMEA countries. See [[Boutique advisory consolidation]].
