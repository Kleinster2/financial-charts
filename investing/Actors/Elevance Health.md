## Blue Cross Blue Shield relationship

Elevance is the largest *for-profit* licensee of the Blue Cross Blue Shield brand, operating in 14 states. The BCBS brand provides:
- Local market recognition and trust
- Access to BCBS nationwide provider networks
- Regulatory relationships in each state

---

## Carelon growth strategy

Carelon is Elevance's services platform (analogous to UnitedHealth's Optum):
- **CarelonRx** — pharmacy benefits management
- **Carelon Behavioral Health** — mental health services
- **Carelon Health** — primary/specialty care delivery

Growing at 36% YoY (Q2 2025) — represents Elevance's diversification beyond traditional health insurance into healthcare services and delivery.

---

## Industry context

Health insurers face different dynamics than life/P&C:
- **Medical loss ratio (MLR)** regulated at ~85% (ACA requirement)
- **Medicare Advantage** growth from aging demographics
- **Healthcare cost inflation** pressuring margins
- **PBM scrutiny** — regulatory risk for pharmacy operations
- **UnitedHealth CEO tragedy** (Dec 2024) — increased industry scrutiny
