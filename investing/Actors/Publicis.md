## Strategic differentiation

**Epsilon acquisition (2019, $4.4B):** Publicis bet big on first-party data. Epsilon has data on 250M+ US consumers.

**Thesis:** Data + creativity = defensible moat. Clients need Publicis for audience insights, not just creative.

---

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Data advantage via Epsilon
- Integrated offering (creative + data + tech)
- Better positioned vs. peers for AI era
- European market leadership

**Bear:**
- AI content generation threatens creative
- In-housing trend
- Epsilon data assets face privacy headwinds
- Margin pressure
