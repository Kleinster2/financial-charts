# [[Shenzhen]] Stock Exchange

China's second-largest exchange. Tech and growth focus — hosts ChiNext board (China's NASDAQ equivalent). Founded 1990.

---

## Key facts

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Founded | 1990 |
| Listed companies | ~2,800 |
| Market cap | ~$5T |
| Focus | Tech, growth, SMEs |
| Boards | Main board, ChiNext (growth), SME board |

---

## ChiNext

Growth enterprise board launched 2009. China's answer to NASDAQ:
- Lower listing requirements than main board
- Tech, biotech, clean energy focus
- Higher volatility, higher growth

---

## Role in A-share market

Primary venue for:
- Tech companies
- Growth stocks
- Private enterprises (vs SOE-heavy Shanghai)

Works in coordination with [[Shanghai Stock Exchange]] and [[Beijing Stock Exchange]] on regulatory policy. See [[China A-share exchanges]] for coordinated actions.
