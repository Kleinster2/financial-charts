**BMG** — German music rights management company owned by [[Bertelsmann]]. Fourth-largest music publisher globally. Former CEO Hartwig Masuch advocated a more "rational" model focused on maximizing catalogue value, arguing the major labels' business model is "based on an old paradigm."
