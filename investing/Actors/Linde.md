#actor #industrial-gas #helium

**Linde** — world's largest industrial gas company by market cap. Produces and distributes atmospheric gases (oxygen, nitrogen, argon), process gases (hydrogen, CO2), and specialty gases including helium. Formed from 2018 merger of Germany's Linde AG and US-based [[Praxair]]. HQ in Dublin, listed on NYSE.
