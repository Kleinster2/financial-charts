# Jordan Schneider

Founder and editor-in-chief of [[ChinaTalk]], a hybrid think tank and media outlet covering [[China]], [[US-China relations]], AI, and semiconductors. Supplies non-partisan analysis to audiences in Silicon Valley and Washington, with 70,000+ free subscribers on [[Substack]]. Previously worked at [[Rhodium Group]] and [[Bridgewater]]. Holds an MA in economics from Peking University's Yenching Academy and a BA in history from Yale.

Publishes original analysis alongside podcast interviews with policymakers, researchers, and operators on Chinese industrial policy, export controls, AI chip supply chain, and the technology dimension of great-power competition. Research has appeared in Foreign Affairs, Foreign Policy, Wired, and Lawfare.
