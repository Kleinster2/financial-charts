## What It Tracks

The Ibovespa — the main Brazilian equity index — in reais. It is the largest and most liquid Brazilian equity ETF and the local-currency expression of Brazil-equity beta. [[EWZ]] is the USD-listed version a foreign investor buys; the two differ by currency (BRL vs USD) and, secondarily, by index construction (Ibovespa vs MSCI Brazil). For a Brazilian investor (the Carteira Aberta guests), BOVA11 is the home-currency Brazil-equity sleeve; the rate-sensitivity runs through the [[Brazilian rate cycle]] (Selic) like the rest of the domestic complex.
