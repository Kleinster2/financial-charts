# Simpson Thacher

Simpson Thacher & Bartlett — major NY-based US law firm with deep [[Private equity]] practice. One of the firms that selectively adopted [[Big Law transformation|scale-plus-pay]] compensation structures in the 2010s, particularly for top originators in PE-backed M&A and finance.
