# PubMatic

PubMatic (Nasdaq: PUBM) is a listed independent supply-side platform (SSP) — sell-side ad-tech that helps publishers monetize their advertising inventory programmatically, with growing exposure to connected-TV (CTV) and the open internet outside the [[Google]]/[[Meta]] walled gardens. A pure-play SSP and one of the [[Ad-tech]] cohort members; it pairs tightest with [[Magnite]] (the other listed SSP).
