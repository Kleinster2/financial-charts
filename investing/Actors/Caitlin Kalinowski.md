## Resignation

Posted on X (Mar 8, 2026): *"This wasn't an easy call. AI has an important role in national security. But surveillance of Americans without judicial oversight and lethal autonomy without human authorization are lines that deserved more deliberation than they got."*

[[OpenAI]] confirmed the departure and said it believes the Pentagon agreement "creates a workable path for responsible national security uses of AI while making clear our red lines, no domestic surveillance and no autonomous weapons."
