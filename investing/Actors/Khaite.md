## The Katie Holmes moment

**August 2019:** Katie Holmes photographed hailing a cab in Manhattan wearing Khaite cashmere cardigan + matching bra ($520).

- Sold out within 1 hour
- Brand searches up 217%
- Still Khaite's most-viewed item ever
- Holstein: "It was mania. It changed our business."

Organic celebrity moment that put Khaite on the map.

## Founder background

**Catherine Holstein:**
- Raised between Southern California and London
- Parsons — Barneys ordered her junior thesis collection
- Dropped out, launched eponymous line (sold at Saks, 40 stores)
- Closed ~2008 ("I was a kid out of college")
- 2009-2014: Design director at [[Gap]], Vera Wang, Maiyet, The Elder Statesman
- Gap experience gave her denim and knitwear expertise
- Relaunched as Khaite in 2016

---

## Investment case

**Strengths:**
- Proven product-market fit (cashmere, bags)
- Organic celebrity adoption
- Strong PE backing (Stripes $150M)
- Professional management (Kleine from Tory Burch)
- American luxury positioning — less crowded than European

**Weaknesses:**
- Still sub-scale vs major luxury houses
- Retail buildout execution risk
- Founder-dependent creative vision
- Luxury cycle exposure

Potential IPO or strategic acquisition candidate if hits $250M+ revenue.
