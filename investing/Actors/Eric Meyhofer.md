## At Lab37

Leads kitchen robotics R&D from a Pittsburgh warehouse facility (commercial R&D kitchen, fabrication shop, electrical lab, assembly lab, testing lab). Developed the Bowl Builder robot: 19-foot wide, 9-foot deep automated bowl assembly line, 18 ingredient dispensers + 10 sauce dispensers, ~80 bowls/hour (hot and cold), <60 sq ft footprint. Automates ~40% of manual food prep. Tested through Hungry Group (virtual restaurant R&D division). Now expanding scope under [[Atoms]]' broader robotics mandate.
