**Miriam Adelson** — Israeli-born physician, philanthropist, and controlling shareholder of [[Las Vegas Sands]] (~54% stake). Widow of [[Sheldon Adelson]]. Republican mega-donor ($100-120M to Trump 2024). Purchased majority stake in [[Dallas Mavericks]] (funded partly by ~$2B LVS stock sale).
