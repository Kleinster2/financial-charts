#actor #vc #accelerator #uk #private

**Entrepreneur First** — Talent-first accelerator that pays individuals to find a cofounder, then invests once teams form. Founded in London. Operates in 19 countries (London, Paris, Berlin, San Francisco, Bangalore, Singapore among them). 637 startups supported, $16B+ portfolio value, 1 unicorn (Tractable), 56 portfolio exits as of April 2026. Notable alumni: Deliveroo, Cleo AI, Aztec. Structurally distinct from [[Y Combinator]] in that it inverts the order: capital-then-team, not team-then-capital. See [[Startup accelerators]] for landscape context.

## The talent-first model

EF's structural innovation: pay technical individuals a stipend during a 12-week cohort to meet potential cofounders and develop a thesis. Then make a check decision once a team forms. This inverts the YC model (which requires an existing team and product) and addresses the pre-team gap that no generalist accelerator fills. The model is most replicated by [[Antler]], with variations at South Park Commons (community-led, pre-idea).
