**Dominari Securities** — registered broker-dealer subsidiary of [[Dominari Holdings]] (Nasdaq: DOMH), headquartered in the Trump Tower at 725 Fifth Avenue, New York. Operates as the underwriting and investment banking arm of the Dominari complex. CEO is [[Kyle Wool]]. Architects and book-runs the small-cap IPOs and reverse-merger sequences that constitute the [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]]. Co-book-running the New America Acquisition I Corp. SPAC IPO alongside D. Boral Capital. Named recipient (alongside [[Kyle Wool]]) of the March 8, 2026 [[House Select Committee on China]] letter citing the small-cap Chinese-issuer ramp-and-dump pattern.
