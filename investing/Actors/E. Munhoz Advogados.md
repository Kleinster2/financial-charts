**E. Munhoz Advogados** — São Paulo boutique law firm specializing in restructuring, insolvency, and distressed M&A. Founded by Eduardo Secchi Munhoz, full professor of commercial law at USP and widely regarded as [[Brazil]]'s top restructuring attorney (Chambers, "the best restructuring and insolvency attorney in Brazil"). Deliberately specialized — not a full-service firm. Co-led by Munhoz and Carolina Machado Letizio Vieira.
