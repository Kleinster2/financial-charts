Ren Zhengfei — Founder and CEO of [[Huawei]] (since 1987). Former PLA engineer turned telecom equipment magnate. Central figure in the [[US-China tech race]] — [[Huawei]]'s entity-list sanctions (2019) were the opening salvo of the semiconductor decoupling. Attended [[Xi Jinping]]'s February 2025 private sector symposium.
