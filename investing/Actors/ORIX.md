**ORIX Corporation** (欧力士株式会社, TYO: 8591 / NYSE: IX) — Japanese diversified financial services conglomerate. One of Asia's largest non-bank financial institutions. Specializes in leasing, corporate finance, real estate, private equity, and insurance. Acquired majority stake in [[Hilco Real Estate]] in September 2025.

## Hilco Global acquisition (Sept 2025)

**Strategic move:** ORIX USA acquired majority stake in [[Hilco Global]], adding distressed asset expertise.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Acquired | September 2, 2025 |
| Stake | Majority (exact % undisclosed) |
| Seller | Hilco Global (founded 1987) |
| Founder retained | Jeffrey B. Hecktman remains CEO |
| Rationale | Build asset-based lending arm |

**What Hilco adds:**
- 770+ employees worldwide
- Deep asset valuation expertise (tangible + intangible)
- [[Hilco Real Estate]] — distressed/commercial real estate advisory
- Bankruptcy disposition capabilities
- Proprietary deal flow from advisory relationships

**Combined play:** Hilco's asset knowledge + ORIX's capital = differentiated private credit platform for special situations.

## Investment thesis

**Bull case:**
- Diversified model reduces cyclical risk
- Hilco acquisition creates unique asset-based lending capability
- Strong balance sheet (investment grade) for opportunistic deployment
- Middle-market focus in US avoids competition with mega-funds

**Risks:**
- Interest rate sensitivity in leasing/finance segments
- Japanese regulatory environment
- Integration risk with Hilco (different cultures: Japanese conglomerate vs Chicago distressed shop)
- FX exposure (JPY/USD) on US earnings

**Peers:** [[Mitsubishi UFJ Financial]], [[Sumitomo Mitsui Financial]], [[Mizuho]], US comparables: [[Ares Management]], [[KKR]] (more PE-heavy)
