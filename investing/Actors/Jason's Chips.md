## Frameworks

- In optics, the true bottleneck is qualified, high-yield supply of the physical component that customers cannot substitute quickly.
- Opportunity pipelines, conversations, and development programs need to be separated from backlog and volume purchase orders.
- In CPO, architecture matters: WDM laser arrays and ultra-high-power external laser sources solve different problems, and the production winner may not be the company with the loudest social-media narrative.
