**D2L Brightspace** — Canadian learning management system, publicly listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange as DTOL, founded 1999 by John Baker and originally branded Desire2Learn. The Brightspace product is the primary surviving credible challenger to [[Canvas LMS]] in North American higher education, holding approximately 20% of NA higher-ed enrollment by Spring 2025 [[Edutechnica]] data and growing — picking up institutions defecting from [[Blackboard]] who reject dominant-vendor lock-in to Canvas. Strong international and Canadian presence; competes well at the system-wide level.
