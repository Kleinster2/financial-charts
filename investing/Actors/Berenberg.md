## Ownership / funding history

Berenberg is a **private partnership** (not a stock company) — has remained unlisted and in partnership form since 1590. Ownership has passed through the Berenberg-Gossler-Seyler family lines for 400+ years. There is no public cap table, no VC rounds, and no IPO path; ownership structure is equity held by a small group of managing partners. This is the standard structure for German private banks of this vintage (see also M.M.Warburg & Co.).

No funding rounds — capital retained from operating profits since 1590.
