DFC — U.S. International Development Finance Corporation. Federal development-finance agency created by the BUILD Act of 2018 (consolidating OPIC and USAID's Development Credit Authority). Provides debt, equity, political-risk insurance, and feasibility-study grants to support U.S. private investment in developing countries. Under the second Trump administration, has emerged alongside [[EXIM Bank]] as a primary federal-financing vehicle for non-China critical-minerals supply-chain projects.
