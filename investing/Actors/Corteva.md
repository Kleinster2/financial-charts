# Corteva

Corteva (NYSE: CTVA) is a pure-play agriculture-inputs company — seeds (genetics/traits) and crop protection (herbicides, insecticides, fungicides) — spun out of DowDuPont in 2019. Unlike the fertilizer nutrient producers, its model is seeds + crop-chem (R&D/IP-driven), not commodity nutrients. The seeds/crop-chem control for the [[Fertilizer producers]] cluster validation.
