### Capacity crisis (2025)

Aguirre Unit 1 catastrophic failure created generation shortfall. Genera requested emergency procurement of 800 MW of temporary mobile generators. [[PREB]] authorized 700-850 MW emergency procurement plus 2,500-3,000 MW long-term baseload solicitation.

LUMA projected 90 load-shed events May-Oct 2025 vs 20 in same period 2024.

### New infrastructure

- 52 MW BESS at Costa Sur: $109.5M (federal funds), 68 [[Tesla Energy|Tesla Megapacks]], operational Spring 2026
- Tranche 1 solar/storage: 579 MW solar + 350 MW BESS (2025-2026 in-service)
