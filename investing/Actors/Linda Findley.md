# Linda Findley

President and CEO of [[Sleep Number]] since April 2025. Previously CEO of [[Blue Apron]] (2019-2024), where she led a turnaround that ended in a sale to a strategic buyer (Sept 2023). Earlier, COO of [[Etsy]] (global operations, product, marketing) and COO of Evernote (2012-2015). Held senior roles at [[Alibaba]].com (2009-2012, based in Hong Kong).
