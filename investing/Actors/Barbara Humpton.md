Barbara Humpton — CEO of [[USA Rare Earth]] since October 1, 2025. Formerly president and CEO of [[Siemens|Siemens USA]] for approximately seven years (2017–2024), where she led electrification, automation, and digitalization strategy across Siemens' US industrial businesses. Her appointment at USAR aligned the company's leadership with the industrial-policy scale the 2026 Commerce Department LOI and Serra Verde acquisition would demand.
