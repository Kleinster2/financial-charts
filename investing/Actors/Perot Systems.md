## Founding

[[Ross Perot]] and eight associates founded Perot Systems in June 1988 — after selling [[EDS]] to GM (1984) and being bought out amid culture clash (1986).

**Second act**: Perot couldn't stay retired. Built another IT services company from scratch.

---

## Why Perot Systems mattered

- **Healthcare IT strength**: Digitizing/automating medical records
- **Government contracts**: Federal IT services
- **Ross Perot brand**: Founder's reputation opened doors
- **EDS playbook**: Same facilities management model
