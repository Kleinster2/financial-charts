## Apr 2026 Beijing Auto Show

Just before the Beijing Auto Show, Horizon Robotics launched the Starry 6 processor, which [[Reuters]] described as integrating cockpit and driving functions while supporting up to 12 in-vehicle displays. In the [[Automotive AI]] race, Horizon is the domestic chip-supplier layer: it gives Chinese EV makers a path to reduce reliance on [[Qualcomm]] and [[NVIDIA]] for cockpit / ADAS compute.
