# Aixtron

German MOCVD (metal-organic chemical vapor deposition) equipment maker. Makes the deposition tools that grow compound-semiconductor epitaxial layers for optoelectronics (datacom/telecom lasers, VCSELs, micro-LED), power electronics ([[Silicon carbide|SiC]] and [[Gallium nitride|GaN]]), and emerging applications. Trades on XETRA as AIXA; US OTC ADR as AIXG. Became a structural beneficiary of the AI-networking buildout — every 800G/1.6T optical interconnect requires deposition tools Aixtron sells.

## Q1 2026 earnings and guide raise (Apr 2026)

Aixtron raised FY2026 revenue guidance by ~8% at the midpoint to €560m ±€30m (from €520m ±€30m). Driver: [[Optoelectronics]] demand — specifically the deposition tools required for AI datacenter optical interconnects (VCSEL-based transceivers, 800G/1.6T, future co-packaged optics). Management expects opto revenue to more than double YoY from 2025 to 2026, implying >€200m in 2026 opto revenue alone. Opto is now approaching half of total company revenue and only started ramping materially last quarter.

What is *not* yet priced: [[Gallium nitride|GaN]] power semi ramp, which management frames as "hasn't even started yet." GaN is the next leg — datacenter power supplies, EV inverters, RF — and Aixtron has incumbent MOCVD tool position for GaN-on-Si.

## Why Aixtron matters to the vault

- Only dedicated public MOCVD equipment vendor at scale; incumbent for compound-semi epitaxy
- AI optical networking thesis requires compound semiconductors (Si can't lase), so every 1.6T transceiver buildout flows partly to Aixtron's order book
- Opto ramp is just starting — Jason's Chips call that Aixtron could reach $1B/yr in opto alone by 2030 (versus 2026 guide of ~€200m) implies a multi-year tailwind the sell side is late to
