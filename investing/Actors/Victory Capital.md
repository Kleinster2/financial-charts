**Victory Capital** — Texas-based asset manager (NYSE: VCTR). Competing bidder for [[Janus Henderson]] against [[Trian Partners]]/[[General Catalyst]]. Made two bids: first ($30 cash + 0.25 shares, $8.6B total) rejected by JHG board; revised ($40 cash + 0.25 shares, same $8.6B total) pending. Claims 16% premium to Trian's $7.4B all-cash offer. Targeting $500M/year in cost synergies from a merger.
