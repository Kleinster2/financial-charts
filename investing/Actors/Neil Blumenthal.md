## Why Neil Blumenthal matters

Blumenthal is one of the consumer-brand operators behind Warby Parker's digitally native retail model. His appearance on Blank Street's cap table matters because it adds a founder-operator consumer lens to the financing, not just institutional venture backing.

## Blank Street link

Blank Street's Oct. 13, 2021 Series A announcement named Neil Blumenthal as one of the strategic investors joining the round led by [[General Catalyst]] and [[Tiger Global]].
