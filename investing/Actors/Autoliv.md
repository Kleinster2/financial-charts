# Autoliv

Autoliv (NYSE: ALV) is the global leader in passive automotive safety — airbags, seatbelts, and steering wheels — with roughly a third of the world market. Swedish-American, it is the lowest-volatility Tier-1 supplier (content-driven, oligopolistic safety market) and a member of the [[Tier-1 auto suppliers]] cohort, forming its tightest internal pair with [[Lear]].
