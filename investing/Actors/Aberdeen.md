## Name change (Feb 2026)

Rebranding from "abrdn" to "Aberdeen Group plc." The vowel-less rebrand under prior CEO Stephen Bird (2021) was widely ridiculed. New CEO [[Jason Windsor]] called it "a pragmatic decision marking a new phase." LSE ticker remains ABDN. No changes to subsidiary legal entity names or fund names.

## AI disintermediation exposure
