## Use Case

Short-term rate proxy. SHY tracks near the fed funds rate with a slight lag. In the dashboard yield curve, SHY sits between [[BIL]] (cash) and [[IEI]] (intermediate). SHY vs [[TLT]] spread = yield curve slope.
