## Model

Hybrid venture studio + fund. Invests both cash and in-kind operational support (product dev, commercial strategy, fundraising, governance, go-to-market, exit prep). Also co-creates companies from scratch with entrepreneurs-in-residence.

Commitment: 51% of capital to companies led by female founders and C-level leaders. All ventures must have African or female founders.
