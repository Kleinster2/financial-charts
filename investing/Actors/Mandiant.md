**Mandiant** — incident-response and threat-intelligence firm acquired by [[Google]] for $5.4B in cash in September 2022; now operates as part of Google Cloud Security. Founded 2004 by Kevin Mandia (former US Air Force computer-security officer); previously trade-listed on Nasdaq as MNDT. Mandiant's distinguishing capability is the maintenance of named threat-actor tracking taxonomies — the UNC ("Uncategorized") naming convention covers groups of activity not yet attributed, with promotion to APT (state-sponsored) or FIN (financially motivated) designations as attribution matures. The firm tracks [[Scattered Spider]] as UNC3944, [[ShinyHunters]] as UNC6040 in some campaigns, and supplies incident-response engagements during major breaches across the SaaS / cloud-platform attack surface ([[Salesforce]] 2025 vishing, [[Snowflake]] 2024 customer credential theft, [[Canvas Instructure security incident May 2026|Canvas / Instructure 2026]]).
