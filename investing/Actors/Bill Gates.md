## Investment philosophy

Gates' climate thesis:
1. **Electrify everything** possible
2. **Clean the grid** (nuclear, renewables)
3. **Innovate** for hard-to-abate sectors
4. **Scale** solutions that work

Nuclear is central — not optional.
