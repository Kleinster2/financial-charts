# Politan Management

**Politan Management** is an activist hedge fund founded by [[Quentin Koffey]], an [[Elliott Management]] alumnus. Best known for back-to-back proxy fights at [[Masimo]] (2023-2024) that won four board seats, ousted founder-CEO [[Joe Kiani]], forced divestiture of the $1.03B Sound United consumer audio acquisition (sold for $350M), and ultimately catalyzed the $9.9B sale to [[Danaher]] (February 2026). Politan holds ~9% of Masimo, worth ~$900M at the $180 deal price.
