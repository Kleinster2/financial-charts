# On Location

On Location is a premium live-experiences and hospitality provider, packaging tickets, travel, and on-site hospitality for major sporting and entertainment events — including premier NFL events and the Olympic Games. Formerly an [[Endeavor]] business, it was sold to [[TKO Group Holdings]] in February 2025 as part of the ~$3.25B all-equity sports-assets transaction.
