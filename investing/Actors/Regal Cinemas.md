# Regal Cinemas (Cineworld Group)

Second-largest US cinema chain (~500 theaters, ~7,000 screens). Subsidiary of UK-based **Cineworld Group**, which emerged from Chapter 11 bankruptcy in August 2023.

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Parent | Cineworld Group |
| US theaters | ~500 |
| US screens | ~7,000 |
| Global screens | ~9,000 (US + UK + Europe) |
| HQ | Knoxville, TN (Regal) / London (Cineworld) |
| Status | Private (post-bankruptcy, creditor-owned) |

## Bankruptcy and restructuring

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| Sep 2022 | Cineworld files Chapter 11 (US) — $5B debt load, pandemic devastation |
| Apr 2023 | Restructuring agreement with creditors (83% of term loans) |
| Aug 2023 | Emerged from bankruptcy — slashed billions in debt, creditors took equity |
| 2024 | Closed 6 UK cinemas, ongoing optimization |
| 2024-25 | Post-bankruptcy growth mode — premium formats (IMAX, 4DX, ScreenX) |

Emerged just in time for Barbenheimer summer 2023. Debt-to-equity conversion gave creditors control.
