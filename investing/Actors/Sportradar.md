# Sportradar

Sportradar (NASDAQ: SRAD) is a listed B2B sports-data supplier — data feeds, odds services, and integrity monitoring — that supplies most US sportsbooks. Like [[Genius Sports]], it is a picks-and-shovels provider to the [[Sports betting]] industry rather than an operator.
