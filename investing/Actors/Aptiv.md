# Aptiv

Aptiv (NYSE: APTV) is a Tier-1 automotive supplier focused on the vehicle's electrical architecture — high-voltage wiring, connectors, and the advanced safety/ADAS and software ("Smart Vehicle") systems that ride on it. Spun out of the old Delphi, it is the most software/EV-content-levered of the major suppliers, which is why it co-moves least with the volume-driven seating/safety names. A member of the [[Tier-1 auto suppliers]] cohort.
