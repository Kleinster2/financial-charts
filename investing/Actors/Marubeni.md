# Marubeni

One of [[Japan]]'s five major *sogo shosha* (general trading houses), founded 1858. Diversified across agriculture, energy, metals, power, transport, food, finance, and consumer products. One of the five trading houses [[Warren Buffett]] / [[Berkshire Hathaway]] accumulated stakes in starting 2019 (see [[Buffett Japan sogo shosha trade]]).

## Why Marubeni matters

Together with [[Mitsubishi]], [[Mitsui]], [[Itochu]], and [[Sumitomo Corp]], Marubeni is one of the five Japanese trading houses that operate at the intersection of physical commodity logistics, energy, and emerging-market industrial finance. Their business model — bridging price and information gaps across fragmented goods markets — has come back into market focus in 2024-2026 as the [[Law of one price]] retreats. See [[Buffett Japan sogo shosha trade]] for the full investment frame.
