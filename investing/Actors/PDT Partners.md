## History

Peter Muller joined [[Morgan Stanley]] and eventually ran the Process Driven Trading Group, a direct descendant of the Advanced Proprietary Trading (APT) desk where Gerry Bamberger pioneered [[Statistical arbitrage|pairs trading]] in the early 1980s. The same desk produced [[David E. Shaw]], who left to found [[D.E. Shaw]] in 1988.

When Morgan Stanley spun off PDT as an independent hedge fund in 2012, it preserved one of the longest continuous stat-arb trading operations on Wall Street.
