## HPE acquisition (2015)

HP (pre-split) acquired Aruba for $3B to strengthen enterprise networking. Now part of HPE's Intelligent Edge segment.

---

## Instant On divestiture (2025)

HPE divested Aruba's **Instant On** (SMB wireless) product line as condition for [[Juniper Networks]] acquisition DOJ approval.
