## Composition

Sterling-denominated UK government bonds (gilts) across all maturities, excluding index-linked bonds. Uses sampling replication. Semi-annual distributions.

## Use Case

UK sovereign rates proxy. The ~4.3% yield reflects [[Bank of England]] policy rates. In the global bonds chart, IGLT.L represents UK gilts alongside [[TLT]], [[2510.T]], [[IBGL.L]], [[XLB.TO]], [[GOVT.AX]], and [[CBON]].
