# Phillips

Third-largest Western auction house ($927M in 2025, +10%). Focused on 20th/21st-century art, design, watches, and jewelry — younger-skewing than Christie's or [[Sotheby's]]. Owned by Mercury Group, a private investment vehicle of Russian-origin businessmen Leonid Friedland and Leonid Strunin. Founded 1796 in London.

## Ownership

Owned by **Mercury Group**, controlled by **Leonid Friedland** and **Leonid Strunin** — Russian-born, London-based businessmen who acquired Phillips de Pury in 2008. Mercury also holds luxury retail and real estate assets.

Russian-origin ownership creates potential sanctions/reputational risk depending on geopolitical environment, though Friedland and Strunin have not been sanctioned.
