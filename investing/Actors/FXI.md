# FXI

iShares China Large-Cap ETF — tracks the FTSE China 50 Index, providing exposure to the 50 largest Chinese stocks trading on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (H-shares and Red Chips). Includes mainland tech giants like [[Alibaba]], [[Tencent]], and [[Meituan]].
