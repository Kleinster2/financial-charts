# IHF

IHF (NYSE Arca: IHF) is the iShares US Healthcare Providers ETF — despite the "providers" label, it is dominated by the managed-care payers ([[UnitedHealth|UNH]], [[Elevance Health|ELV]], and peers at top weight), with hospital operators a small slice. That weighting is the key to the [[Hospital operators]] cluster result: IHF clusters with the payers, NOT with the hospitals.

## Use case

The "providers ETF" control for the [[Hospital operators#Cluster validation|hospital-operators cluster validation]]. The finding: IHF is payer-dominated, so at the 0.50 cut it clusters with [[UnitedHealth|UNH]]/[[Elevance Health|ELV]] — not with the hospital operators ([[HCA Healthcare|HCA]]/[[Tenet Healthcare|THC]]/[[Universal Health Services|UHS]]). The hospital core carries a +0.30 intra-advantage over IHF, which is why no liquid ETF cleanly expresses the hospital-operator factor. See [[Hospital operators]].
