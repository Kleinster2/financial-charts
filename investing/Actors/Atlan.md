# Atlan

Active-metadata and data/AI governance platform — a modern challenger to the legacy data-catalog vendors. Founded 2018 by Prukalpa Sankar and Varun Banka (India-origin, dual Delhi / San Francisco base). Positions itself as the control plane for data and AI agents, and claims it wins roughly three of four deals when competing head-to-head against [[Informatica]], [[Collibra]], and [[Alation]]. Named a Leader in the 2026 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Data and Analytics Governance Platforms.
