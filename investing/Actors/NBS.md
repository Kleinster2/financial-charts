## Industrial profits

NBS publishes monthly profit data for industrial enterprises above the designated size — industrial legal entities with annual main-business revenue of at least 20 million yuan. The series is a useful bridge between [[China PPI]], industrial production, and corporate margins because it reports profit totals, revenue, costs, margins, receivables, inventories, and industry splits.

May 2026 live read: profits for January-April 2026 rose 18.2% year on year to 2.43584 trillion yuan, accelerating from 15.5% in Q1; April alone rose 24.7%. The NBS interpretation tied the improvement to faster industrial production, recovering industrial-product prices, equipment/high-tech manufacturing, raw materials, and lower unit costs, while still warning that strong supply and weak domestic demand remained a prominent imbalance.
