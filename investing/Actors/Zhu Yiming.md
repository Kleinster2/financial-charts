Founded [[GigaDevice]] in 2005. Resigned from GigaDevice in 2018 to lead [[ChangXin Memory]] — [[China]]'s national DRAM memory project. CXMT is now tied to a reported Shanghai [[HBM]] packaging facility and a broader state-backed memory-financing ecosystem. The specific older direct-funding amount claim for the [[China semiconductor strategy|Big Fund]] was not confirmed in the Jun 2026 deepdive; the sourced public financing datapoint is the related Changxin Xinqiao 39B yuan / $5.4B government-backed financing.
