Lectrosonics — US manufacturer of professional UHF wireless audio systems for film, broadcast, and theatre. Founded 1971, headquartered in Rio Rancho, New Mexico. Won an Academy Award in 2017 for Digital Hybrid Wireless Technology. Acquired by [[Rode Microphones|The Freedman Group]] in May 2025. Continues to manufacture all products in New Mexico.
