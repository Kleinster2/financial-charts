## Controversies

**Summitville mine (1991):**
- Galactic Resources (Friedland as CEO) operated gold mine in Colorado
- Heap leaching operation caused cyanide contamination of Alamosa River
- One of worst environmental disasters in US mining history
- EPA cleanup cost $100M+; company went bankrupt 1992
- Friedland resigned day EPA issued notice; denied responsibility
- Relocated to Singapore shortly before collapse

**Early life:**
- Arrested by FBI (1970) for LSD trafficking while at Bowdoin College
- Served prison time; conviction expunged 1986
- Later at Reed College, ran apple farm commune where Steve Jobs worked weekends
- The orchard inspired the name "Apple" for Jobs's company
