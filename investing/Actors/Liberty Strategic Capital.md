**Liberty Strategic Capital** — PE/growth equity fund founded by [[Steven Mnuchin]] (2021, post-Treasury). Focus on technology, telecom, and financial services. Raised ~$2.5B Fund I. Backed by [[Saudi PIF]], [[SoftBank]], and Gulf sovereign wealth. Investments include Satellogic, Cybereason, AT&T fiber stake, and Panasonic Avionics.
