#actor #person #founder

**Aron D'Souza** — Australian businessman and attorney, co-founder of [[Enhanced Group]] (NYSE: ENHA) and the original architect of the [[Enhanced Games]] concept. Best known publicly for his role advising [[Peter Thiel]] in the Thiel-funded [[Hulk Hogan]] / [[Gawker]] litigation that bankrupted Gawker Media in 2016. Holds a doctorate in law from Oxford. Founded the Enhanced Games concept in 2023 around the thesis that medically supervised performance-enhancing substance use should be permitted in elite competition.
