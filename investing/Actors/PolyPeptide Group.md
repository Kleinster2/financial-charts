# PolyPeptide Group

Swiss-listed peptide CDMO specializing in GMP manufacturing of peptide APIs for pharma and biotech clients. Trades on SIX Swiss Exchange (PPGN). Spun off from [[Bachem]] in 1996, IPO in 2021.

## Capacity expansion

EUR 100M expansion to double SPPS capacity at Malmo manufacturing site. New large-scale SPPS capacity at Braine-l'Alleud, Belgium started production December 2024 with ramp-up through 2025. Mainly serving a large commercial agreement in the metabolic space.
