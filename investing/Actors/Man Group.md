## Anthropic partnership (Feb 2026)

Man Group partnered with [[Anthropic]] to embed [[Claude]] across investment and operational workflows. Announced Feb 11, 2026.

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Scope | Alpha generation, data synthesis, risk modeling, ops |
| Tools | Claude, [[Claude Code]], Claude Skills |
| Internal tool | AlphaGPT (proprietary alpha generation, LLM-powered) |

CTO Gary Collier: LLMs accelerated proprietary alpha generation tools like AlphaGPT. Anthropic's enterprise focus allows direct integration into investment processes.

Beyond trading — partnership extends to distribution, HR, and application development. Anthropic engineers working side-by-side with Man Group teams.

Anthropic's Chris Ciauri positioned Man Group's 35-year systematic investing heritage as a natural fit. Financial services regulatory/compliance requirements favor Anthropic's enterprise approach.

*Sources: man.com press release, Hedgeweek, P&I (Feb 11 2026)*
