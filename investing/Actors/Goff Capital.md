# Goff Capital

Family office of John C. Goff, based in Fort Worth, Texas. Founded ~2009 after Goff sold [[Crescent Real Estate]] to [[Morgan Stanley]] for $6.5B (2007). Invests across real estate, energy, wellness, aerospace, gaming, and entertainment. Key holdings: [[Canyon Ranch]] (principal owner since 2017, bought out [[Barclays]] interest), [[Crescent Energy]] (NYSE: CRGY, chairman), [[Crescent Real Estate]] (reacquired 2009, now $10B+ AUM/development capacity). ~$2B+ in active development projects. Goff began career in 1987 with Richard Rainwater.
