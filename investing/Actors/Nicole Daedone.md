Nicole Daedone — founder and former CEO of [[OneTaste]], a San Francisco company that marketed "orgasmic meditation" as wellness education. Convicted of forced labor conspiracy (EDNY, Jun 9, 2025). Sentenced to 9 years (Mar 30, 2026). Court ordered forfeiture of $12M from her 2017 sale of the company.
