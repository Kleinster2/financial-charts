# POET Technologies

Small-cap Canadian company developing an optical interposer platform aimed at revolutionizing silicon photonics packaging costs. The company's Optical Interposer technology addresses one of the key bottlenecks in [[Silicon Photonics]] commercialization: the high cost and complexity of packaging optical components.

## Investment case

POET Technologies aims to reduce silicon photonics packaging costs through its proprietary optical interposer approach. This could address a key economic barrier to widespread silicon photonics adoption across the optical networking and AI infrastructure markets.

## Apr 27, 2026 — Marvell cancels all Celestial AI purchase orders; -46% intraday

POET disclosed that all purchase orders received from [[Celestial AI]] — including the initial production-unit orders first announced in April 2023 — have been cancelled. Notice came from [[Marvell Technology]], which completed its $3.25B acquisition of [[Celestial AI]] on Feb 2, 2026 and is now the post-close counterparty on all [[Celestial AI]] commercial agreements. Cancellation notice was dated Apr 23, 2026; POET disclosed Apr 27.

The cited basis is significant: [[Marvell]] alleged POET had made disclosures of information related to the purchase order and shipping information in contravention of confidentiality obligations. The cancellation is therefore framed as a breach-driven termination, not a redesign or capacity decision — meaning the [[Co-Packaged Optics]] / photonic-interposer demand thesis itself is not the public reason for the cancellation, but the direct customer relationship is severed.

Stock reaction: ~-46% on the day to ~$8 (the largest single-day decline on record for POET). Shareholder-loss law firms (Johnson Fistel) initiated outreach by Apr 27 close.

POET partial offset: a separately announced $5M purchase order from another (unnamed) technology company was disclosed alongside the cancellation. The order is materially smaller than the [[Celestial AI]] pipeline it replaces but signals POET is attempting customer diversification.

Read-through for the [[Co-Packaged Optics]] thesis: the Marvell-POET dispute is the first public visible commercial fallout from the Marvell-Celestial integration. Three competing readings of the cancellation: (1) Marvell genuinely judged POET's confidentiality conduct as material and severed the relationship as enforcement; (2) Marvell post-acquisition reviewed the Celestial supply chain and chose to internalize POET's interposer scope, using the confidentiality issue as legally clean grounds; (3) the photonic-interposer roadmap itself is being deferred or redesigned post-acquisition, and the cancellation is a downstream consequence. Without the Marvell-side commentary, all three are consistent with the disclosure. Watch [[Marvell Technology]] earnings and any [[Celestial AI]] integration updates for which reading is operative.
