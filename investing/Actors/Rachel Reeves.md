# Rachel Reeves

Rachel Reeves — UK Chancellor of the Exchequer (Labour, from July 2024). First woman to hold the role. Met [[Sir Charles Roxburgh]] ([[Lloyd's of London]] chair) on March 11 during the [[Iran conflict economic disruption|2026 Iran conflict]] insurance crisis; agreed the issue is crew safety, not insurance availability, and that the priority is de-escalation.
