## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Focused strategy (transport)
- Technology leadership
- Partnership model (asset-light)
- Heavy transport decarbonization mandate
- Better financial position than peers

**Bear:**
- Hydrogen infrastructure slow to build
- Battery trucks improving (Tesla Semi)
- China revenue risk
- Still unprofitable
- Long timeline to scale
