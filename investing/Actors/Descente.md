# Descente

**Japanese premium sports apparel company.** Founded 1935. Known for skiing and golf apparel. [[Anta Sports]] holds significant stake through JV — Descente China is part of Anta's premium brand portfolio. Public company (Tokyo: 8114). Also owns [[Umbro]] (Japan/Korea rights), [[Le Coq Sportif]] (Japan rights), [[Arena]] swimwear.
