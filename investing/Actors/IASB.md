#actor #reference #accounting

**IASB (International Accounting Standards Board)** — sets IFRS (International Financial Reporting Standards), used by ~140 countries. The international counterpart to FASB ([[GAAP]]).

---

## Overview

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Full name | International Accounting Standards Board |
| Parent | IFRS Foundation |
| HQ | London |
| Standards | IFRS (International Financial Reporting Standards) |
| Adoption | ~140 countries |
| US status | Not accepted for domestic filers (SEC requires [[GAAP]]) |

---

## IFRS vs GAAP

| Feature | IFRS (IASB) | [[GAAP]] (FASB) |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| Approach | Principles-based | Rules-based |
| Inventory | LIFO prohibited | LIFO allowed |
| R&D | Capitalize development phase | Expense as incurred |
| Revaluation | Permitted for PP&E | Not permitted |
| Adoption | ~140 countries | US only |
