# Social Capital
Venture capital firm co-founded by [[Chamath Palihapitiya]] and [[Mamoon Hamid]] in 2011. Early investor in [[Slack]], [[Intercom]], and Netskope. Hamid departed to [[Kleiner Perkins]] in 2017. Firm has evolved from traditional VC to a more technology-driven investment approach under Palihapitiya.
