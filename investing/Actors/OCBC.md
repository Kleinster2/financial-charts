**OCBC** — Second-largest bank in [[Singapore]] by market cap (~S$72B, 2025). Founded in 1932 through the merger of three Chinese banks. Full-service banking, wealth management (Bank of Singapore private banking arm), and insurance (Great Eastern Holdings). Listed on SGX (O39.SI).
