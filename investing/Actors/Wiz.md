## The 2024 rejection

In July 2024, Wiz rejected Google's $23B offer, citing:
- Strong growth momentum
- Antitrust concerns
- Investor preference for IPO

Nine months later, accepted $32B — 39% premium for waiting.

## Product

Cloud-native application protection platform (CNAPP):
- Agentless cloud security
- Vulnerability management
- Cloud misconfiguration detection
- Identity & access analysis
- Works across AWS, Azure, GCP, Oracle

Key differentiator: agentless architecture, fast time-to-value.

---

## Post-acquisition

Joining [[Google]] Cloud. Will continue supporting multi-cloud (AWS, Azure, Oracle) — critical for enterprise adoption.

Rappaport: Goal was to build "the best cybersecurity company in the world."
