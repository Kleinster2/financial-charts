**Egan-Jones Ratings Co.** — Small credit rating agency. In 2024, rated more than **3,000 private credit investments** with approximately **20 analysts**. The SEC has been probing whether the company and senior executives exerted improper commercial influence on ratings procedures — a "ratings shopping" concern where borrowers seek the most favorable grade.

[[UBS]] Chairman Colm Kelleher (Nov 2025): "What you see now is a massive growth in small rating agencies ticking the box for compliance of investment."
