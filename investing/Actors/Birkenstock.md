# Birkenstock

**German heritage footwear brand** — 250-year-old sandal maker turned global luxury player. IPO October 2023 at $46/share (NYSE: BIRK). €2.1B revenue FY2025 (+18% CC), 32% EBITDA margin. [[L Catterton]] owns ~81%. Closed-toe expansion driving growth beyond sandals.

## Investment thesis

**Bull:**
- 250-year heritage, iconic brand
- 32% EBITDA margin — luxury-level profitability
- Closed-toe expansion (38% of revenue) extends seasonality
- APAC +34% — massive runway in Asia
- L Catterton/LVMH expertise in brand elevation
- $200M buyback authorized for FY2026

**Bear:**
- L Catterton controls 81% — limited float
- IPO stumble (-12.6% day one) still remembered
- Fashion risk — sandals could go out of style
- Premium valuation (~5x revenue)
- Currency headwinds (EUR reporting, global sales)
