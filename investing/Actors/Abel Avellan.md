# Abel Avellan

Founder, Chairman, and CEO of [[AST SpaceMobile]], the direct-to-cell satellite company. Avellan holds super-voting Class C common stock whose "Class C Share Voting Amount" formula targets up to 88.31% of total voting power; he and his permitted transferees control roughly 71.6% of votes, which makes AST SpaceMobile a Nasdaq controlled company that relies on the controlled-company governance exemption. He drove the company public via a 2021 SPAC merger and remains the controlling shareholder and operating leader behind the BlueBird constellation strategy.
