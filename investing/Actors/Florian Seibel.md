**Florian Seibel** — German founder of two defense-tech companies: [[Quantum Systems]] (2015, reconnaissance drones) and [[Stark]] (2024, weaponized drones). Split Stark out as a separate company after Quantum Systems investors opposed military applications. Represents the first wave of European founders building for NATO demand rather than commercial markets.

## Why Seibel matters

The Stark spin-out is a cleaner version of the [[Anduril]]/[[Palmer Luckey]] pattern: when existing investors won't let a commercial company pivot into weapons, found a new company purpose-built for that mandate. The two-company structure lets reconnaissance-only customers keep working with Quantum Systems while giving Stark a clean sheet for strike products. Both have ended up as [[Bundeswehr]] vendors in the 2026 rearmament cycle.
