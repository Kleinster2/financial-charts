# Kylie Jenner

Media personality and entrepreneur who built [[Kylie Cosmetics]] into a $1.2B brand valuation within three years of launch (2015-2018), selling 51% to [[Coty]] for $600M in January 2020. The deal valued the brand at $1.2B, making Jenner the youngest self-made billionaire at 21 (though Forbes later revised the claim, citing inflated revenue figures). Kylie Cosmetics pioneered the influencer-to-DTC beauty pipeline — launch products via social media, sell direct, then exit to a legacy beauty house. With ~400M Instagram followers, she remains one of the highest-reach individual brands globally. Coty has since written down the Kylie Cosmetics acquisition as sales declined post-COVID.
