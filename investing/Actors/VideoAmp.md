# VideoAmp

Cross-platform TV and digital measurement company and the most aggressive challenger to [[Nielsen]]'s dominance. Revenue grew from ~$15M to ~$100M+ by 2024. Secured $3B in upfront advertising deals in 2024 (up 880% YoY). Uses machine learning and LLMs to process viewing data from 40M+ households and 65M+ devices. Certified by the U.S. JIC (Joint Industry Committee) as a transactable currency for the 2025-26 TV season alongside [[Comscore]] and [[iSpot]].
