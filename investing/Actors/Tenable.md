## Why it matters here

Tenable's core vulnerability-scanning franchise is most directly exposed to AI code-scanning tools like [[Claude Code Security]] that reason about vulnerabilities contextually rather than matching rules. The [[AI cybersecurity disruption basket]] holds TENB as a directly-threatened constituent.
