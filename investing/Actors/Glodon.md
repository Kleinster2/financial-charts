## Evolution

- 1998 — Founded in Beijing by Diao Zhizhong
- 2010 — IPO on Shenzhen SME Board (002410) — first listed construction IT company in China
- 2014 — Acquired Progman Oy / MagiCAD Group (Finland) — European expansion hub
- 2016 — Renamed from "Glodon Software" to "Glodon Company Limited" — signaling platform pivot
- 2017-2019 — "Seventh Three-Year Plan" — full cloud transformation
- ~2020 — Acquired Hongye Technology — expanded BIM design business
- 2022 — Peak profitability: ~RMB 800M on ~RMB 4B revenue
- 2024 — Launched AecGPT and AI platform at Digital Building Summit
- 2025 — Southeast Asia expansion push. Gsite platform launch

## Investment case

Bull:
- ~32% domestic share — dominant position in China construction software
- AI integration (AecGPT) could drive efficiency gains and pricing power
- Southeast Asia BIM adoption growing 40% CAGR
- Profitability restored (+116% YoY) after cloud transition squeeze
- 300+ patents, deep domain expertise

Bear:
- China construction/real estate downturn reduces demand
- Cloud subscription mix unclear — transition progress opaque in English disclosures
- Revenue declining (-5% in 2024)
- [[Autodesk]] remains global standard in BIM
- Overseas revenue only ~3% of total — international expansion early stage
