# Ryanair

**Ryanair** — [[Ireland|Irish]] ultra-low-cost carrier and Europe's largest budget airline by passenger volume. ~200M passengers/year. Ticker RYAAY (ADR) / RYA (LSE). 70-80% fuel hedged on rolling 12-month contracts, limiting near-term exposure to oil price spikes.
