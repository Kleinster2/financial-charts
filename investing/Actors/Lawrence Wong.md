**Lawrence Wong** — Prime Minister of [[Singapore]] since May 15, 2024. Fourth PM in Singapore's history and first from outside the Lee family. Economist by training, former [[MAS]] official and Finance Minister. Leading the PAP's fourth-generation leadership team. Won the 2025 general election with 65.6% of the vote. Key priorities: Johor-Singapore Special Economic Zone with [[Malaysia]], AI adoption, and enhanced social safety nets.
