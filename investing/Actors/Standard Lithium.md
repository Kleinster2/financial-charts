## Lafayette project

Located in Lafayette County, southwestern Arkansas, over the Smackover Formation. Brines in the project area contain 400+ mg/L lithium — high enough to make DLE profitable at lower price thresholds than most competitors.

Standard processed ~120M litres of oilfield brines over five years at its pilot plant near El Dorado, co-located at a Lanxess bromine facility. Lanxess pumps brine for bromine production; a portion is then piped to Standard's DLE plant using Koch-licensed filtering technology.
