# Matthew McConaughey

Academy Award-winning actor (Best Actor, *Dallas Buyers Club*, 2014) and partner/investor at [[ElevenLabs]], the leading AI voice synthesis company. McConaughey's relationship with ElevenLabs goes beyond typical celebrity endorsement — he holds an equity stake and serves as a strategic partner, lending both his voice and his name to the platform's marketing. The partnership is one of the highest-profile celebrity-AI investor relationships, reflecting ElevenLabs' strategy of recruiting talent that legitimizes synthetic voice technology in Hollywood. ElevenLabs was valued at $3.3B as of its January 2025 Series C, making McConaughey's stake potentially significant if the company reaches IPO. He has also been a long-time brand ambassador for [[Lincoln]] (Ford's luxury division) and Wild Turkey bourbon.
