#actor #software #edtech #lms #private #restructured

**Anthology** — higher-education software roll-up assembled by [[Veritas Capital]] starting July 2020, combining Campus Management, Campus Labs, and iModules. Acquired [[Blackboard]] in October 2021 for an enterprise value reportedly above $3 billion. Filed Chapter 11 September 29, 2025 with $1.625B of funded debt against EBITDA reportedly collapsed from $33M to $4M. Emerged February 27, 2026 with the surviving Teaching & Learning business as a standalone, debt-free [[Blackboard]] Inc. under [[Oaktree Capital]] and Nexus Capital ownership. The Anthology brand effectively ended with the restructuring; Enterprise Operations sold to [[Ellucian]] for ~$70M, Lifecycle Engagement & Student Success to Encoura LE for ~$50M.
