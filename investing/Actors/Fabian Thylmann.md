#actor #person #media

[[Fabian Thylmann]] — German internet entrepreneur, original consolidator of the modern online pornography industry. Created NATS (Next-Generation Affiliate Tracking Software) in the late 1990s. In 2010, acquired Mansef and Interhub assets, merging them into Manwin — bringing [[Pornhub]], [[Brazzers]], and dozens of properties under one roof. Sold his stake to [[Feras Antoon]] and [[David Tassillo]] in October 2013 for €73M after facing tax evasion charges following extradition from Belgium to Germany. The company rebranded to MindGeek (now [[Aylo]]).
