# Colliers

Global commercial real estate services and investment management firm. ~$4.8B revenue, ~$7B market cap (TSX/NASDAQ: CIGI). Originally part of old FirstService Corporation — renamed to Colliers when [[FirstService]] spun off in June 2015. [[Jay Hennick]] is founder, Chairman, and CEO of both Colliers and [[FirstService]].
