# Ross Beaty

Ross Beaty is a Canadian mining entrepreneur and one of the most prolific company-builders in the resource sector, best known for founding Pan American Silver (1994) and, more recently, [[Equinox Gold]], where he is founder and Chairman. His pattern is consistent: assemble assets into a vehicle, scale it by acquisition, and list it — repeated across silver (Pan American), copper (Lumina Copper), gold ([[Equinox Gold]]), and renewable power (Alterra Power). He is also a noted conservation philanthropist through the Sitka Foundation.
