#actor #crypto #ai #compute #depin

**Akash Network** — Decentralized cloud computing marketplace built on [[Cosmos]]. Positions itself as the "permissionless cloud for AI." Uses a reverse-auction model: workloads are posted, GPU providers bid down for the contract, the lowest competent bidder wins. AKT token used for payments and governance; burns $0.85 in AKT for every $1 spent on the network.

Currently acquiring up to 7,200 [[NVIDIA]] GB200 GPUs via the Starbonds mechanism — a major test of whether decentralized clouds can land enterprise-class hardware at scale.

## Relevance to decentralized AI

Among the three major decentralized GPU networks ([[Render Network]], [[Akash Network]], [[io.net]]). Akash's differentiation is the reverse-auction marketplace design — prices discovered by provider competition rather than set by a pricing algorithm. Cost reduction vs [[AWS]]: 60-80% per network marketing; 70%+ substantiated by internal stats.

Active integrations with decentralized-AI agent platforms ([[Morpheus]], [[ElizaOS]]) position Akash as compute backend for the agent layer, not just a general-purpose cloud.
