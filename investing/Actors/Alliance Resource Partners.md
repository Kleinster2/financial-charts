# Alliance Resource Partners

The largest thermal-coal producer in the eastern US, structured as a master limited partnership (MLP), mining the Illinois Basin and Appalachia for domestic and export power-generation demand; it also holds a growing oil-and-gas mineral-royalty business. In the [[Coal miners]] cluster validation it is the decoupler — the lowest-correlation, lowest-volatility member (a stable, high-distribution MLP whose thermal/power and royalty drivers diverge from the met-coal core). A member of the [[Coal miners]] cohort.
