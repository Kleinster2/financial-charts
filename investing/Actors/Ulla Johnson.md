# Ulla Johnson

**American bohemian womenswear brand** — romantic, artisanal, globally-inspired. Founded 1999 by designer Ulla Johnson with $5,000. Never taken outside investment in 25+ years. ~$38M revenue, 75 countries wholesale distribution. New CEO Thibaut Perrin-Faivre (ex-Burberry) hired 2024 to scale.

## The long game

"Slow but steady growth" — Ulla Johnson waited 18 years before opening first store (2017). No outside investors in 25+ years. Proves brand-building patience in era of blitz-scaling.

**Thibaut Perrin-Faivre hire (2024):** Former Burberry Americas president. Signals readiness to scale while maintaining founder control.
