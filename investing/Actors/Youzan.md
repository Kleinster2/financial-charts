## Listing structure

Listed on HKEX GEM Board via reverse takeover of China Innovationpay in January 2018 (HK$2.1B). Renamed from China Youzan to Youzan Technology in August 2024. Still listed, not delisted. Stock trades at HK$0.15-0.16.

---

## Evolution

- Nov 2012 — Founded in Hangzhou's Beta Cafe by Zhu Ning ("Bai Ya")
- Jan 2018 — Reverse takeover of China Innovationpay on HKEX GEM Board for HK$2.1B
- 2019 — [[Tencent]] invests HK$1B for 6.7%. Revenue doubles to HK$685M
- 2019 — [[Baidu]] partnership for mini-program e-commerce
- 2020 — Launched AllValue (international version). GMV crosses RMB 100B
- Aug 2024 — Renamed to Youzan Technology Limited
- 2024 — Adjusted EBITDA turns positive (+288%). GMV RMB 102.8B
- H1 2025 — First GAAP net profit (RMB 73M). CEO declares "continuous and stable profitability"

---

## Investment case

Bull:
- Profitable at last (H1 2025 net profit RMB 73M)
- RMB 103B GMV — massive platform scale
- CEO: "continuous and stable profitability" beginning 2024, "active growth" in 2025
- Stock up 133% over 12 months — momentum
- AllValue for international expansion

Bear:
- GEM Board listing limits institutional access
- HK$4.5B market cap on HK$0.15 stock — micro-cap territory
- FY2024 GAAP still negative (-RMB 177M)
- Revenue flat (-0.4%) — not growing
- Analyst target HK$0.11 (30% below current price)
- Direct competition with [[Weimob]] in same niche
- Has been loss-making for most of its listed life
- Reverse takeover history adds governance complexity
