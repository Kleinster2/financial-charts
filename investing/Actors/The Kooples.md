# The Kooples

French fashion brand founded 2008 in Paris by brothers Alexandre, Laurent, and Raphaël Elicha. Rock-inspired "street chic" men's and women's ready-to-wear. Revenue peaked at €220M (2019) but declined to ~€106M (2023) with a net loss of €24.3M. Owned by [[MF Brands Group]] (Lacoste parent) from 2019 to 2025 — sold to turnaround specialist [[Verdoso]] after MF Brands threw in the towel. 270 stores (184 in France, 86 elsewhere in Europe). French competition authority approved Verdoso acquisition March 2025.
