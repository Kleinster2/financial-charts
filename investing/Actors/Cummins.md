# Cummins

**Largest independent diesel engine manufacturer globally** — powers ~27% of US commercial vehicles (326,000+ of ~1.2M confirmed vehicles). Ticker CMI. Dominant in Class 8 heavy-duty truck engines; [[PACCAR]] (Kenworth/Peterbilt) is historically the largest customer. Also produces natural gas engines (X15N) and is investing in hydrogen/battery-electric powertrains for the energy transition.
