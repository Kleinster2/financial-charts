## Insights

- **Military niche** — Serves active duty, veterans, DoD civilians, and families. Captive market with strong loyalty.
- **VA loan expertise** — Top 10 VA lender by volume, though [[Veterans United]] originates ~4x more VA loans.
- **Credit union advantages** — Not-for-profit = lower rates, fewer fees. Above-average J.D. Power satisfaction scores.
