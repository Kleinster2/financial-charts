## At Scala

**April 2020**: DigitalBridge (then Digital Colony) acquired UOL Diveo data center assets to form Scala. Peigo, as DigitalBridge Operating Partner, became CEO.

**Growth**: 900% in 3+ years — from startup to LatAm's largest hyperscale DC developer.

**Key projects**:
- Scala AI City (4.75GW potential, Rio Grande do Sul)
- Tamboré Campus expansion (117MW, São Paulo)
- LatAm expansion: Mexico, Chile, Colombia
