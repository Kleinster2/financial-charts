# Kevin McCarthy

Role: Former Speaker of the US House of Representatives
State: California
Party: Republican
Speaker: Jan 2023 — Oct 2023 (removed by motion to vacate)
Left Congress: Dec 2023

Attended the inaugural [[Hill and Valley Forum]] dinner in March 2023 while serving as Speaker. His presence signaled early bipartisan political support for the Silicon Valley-Washington tech-security nexus. Removed as Speaker in October 2023 in an unprecedented motion to vacate led by Republican hardliners.
