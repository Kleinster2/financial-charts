## US restrictions

| Action | Date | Impact |
|--------|------|--------|
| Entity List | Dec 2020 | Export license required |
| Treasury investment ban | Dec 2021 | US persons can't invest |
| Federal agency bans | Ongoing | DOD, DOI restrictions |
| Proposed import ban | Pending | "Countering CCP Drones Act" |

**State/local:** Many US police/fire departments still use DJI despite federal concerns. Cost and capability gap vs alternatives.

**Security concerns:** Data transmission to [[China]], potential backdoors, PLA links alleged.
