**Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette** (DLJ) was a US investment bank founded in 1959, acquired by [[Credit Suisse]] in 2000 for $11.5B. [[Safra Catz]] was a managing director at DLJ before joining [[Oracle]], where she became co-CEO and then sole CEO.
