**Aurubis** (XETRA: NDA) — Europe's largest copper smelter and refiner. Pivoting strategically from primary concentrate processing to secondary (recycled) supply as the [[Copper smelter squeeze|TC/RC crisis]] makes primary smelting uneconomic.

## Strategic pivot

Aurubis is betting on "urban mining" — processing scrap and e-waste rather than fighting for scarce concentrate in a market with negative TC/RCs. The Richmond facility is the flagship of this transition.
