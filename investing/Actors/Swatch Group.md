# Swatch Group

Swiss watch conglomerate — owns Omega, Longines, Tissot, [[Harry Winston]], Breguet, Blancpain, and the Swatch brand. Publicly traded (SIX: UHR). Has been losing market share to private brands like [[Rolex]] in recent years.
