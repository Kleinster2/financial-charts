# Vulcan Materials

Vulcan Materials (NYSE: VMC) is the largest US producer of construction aggregates — crushed stone, sand, and gravel — plus asphalt and ready-mix concrete. Demand is weighted to public infrastructure and non-residential construction. Used as the construction-materials control in the [[Building products]] cluster validation.
