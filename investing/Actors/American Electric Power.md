# American Electric Power

**American Electric Power (AEP)** — a large US regulated electric utility operating across 11 states, headquartered in Columbus, OH; NYSE-listed. Owns the largest electricity transmission network in the US, making it a wires-heavy, transmission-led regulated play. A primary beneficiary of data-center load growth in its Ohio and Texas service territories. A pure-regulated, low-volatility (~19%) defensive and a core member of the [[Regulated utilities]] cohort.
