## Investment case

**Bull:**
- IP moat (Marvel, Star Wars, Pixar)
- Parks pricing power
- Streaming profitable now
- ESPN direct-to-consumer
- Experiences secular growth

**Bear:**
- Cord-cutting (linear TV decline)
- Content misses
- Park capex requirements
- Streaming competition
- Sports rights inflation
