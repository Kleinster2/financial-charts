# Golden Ocean Group

Norwegian-Bermudan dry-bulk shipping company — Nasdaq GOGL and Oslo Stock Exchange listing. One of the world's largest operators of [[Capesize]] and Panamax dry-bulk vessels. Part of the [[John Fredriksen]] shipping cluster, which also includes [[Frontline]] (tankers) and SFL Corporation. Partner with [[Frontline]] and [[Trafigura]] in the TFG Marine bunkering joint venture. Stub note — chart data unavailable pending full expansion.
