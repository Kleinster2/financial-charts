## Apr 2026 embodied-AI alignment

At the Apr 2026 Beijing Auto Show, Dongfeng said it would build cars using embodied AI technology in line with China's long-term sector plans. Chairman Yang Qing framed it explicitly as national-policy alignment: "When the nation calls, Dongfeng answers."

The signal matters because it shows [[Automotive AI]] is not only a private-sector race among [[Xiaomi]], [[Xpeng]], [[BYD]], and [[NIO]]. State-owned automakers are also translating [[China AI Plus]] into product roadmaps, often through partnerships with [[Huawei]] for smart-driving systems.
