# Mongolia

Landlocked Central/East Asian country between [[China]] and [[Russia]]. Economy driven by mining (copper, coal, gold, fluorspar) — mining accounts for ~25% of GDP and ~90% of exports. Key mining assets include Oyu Tolgoi (copper-gold, operated by Rio Tinto) and Tavan Tolgoi (coking coal). Significant fluorspar producer (~0.7 Mt in 2024, ~7% of global supply), with expanding exports — secured 70,000 tonnes/year fluorspar export deal with [[South Korea]] starting 2026.
