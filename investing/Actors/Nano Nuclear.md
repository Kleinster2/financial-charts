# Nano Nuclear

Nano Nuclear (NASDAQ: NNE) is a US microreactor developer, one of the pre-revenue [[Nuclear primer|SMR]]/microreactor pure-plays riding the [[Nuclear renaissance]] / [[Long nuclear]] re-rating. It is the smallest and most speculative of the listed developers — a development-stage company designing portable microreactors rather than utility-scale plants.

## Financials

Development-stage / pre-revenue (fiscal year ending Sept 30).
