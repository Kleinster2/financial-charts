**Staples Center** (renamed Crypto.com Arena in December 2021 under a 20-year, $700M naming rights deal) is a 20,000-seat multi-purpose arena in downtown Los Angeles, home to the Lakers, Clippers (until 2024), Kings, and Sparks.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Opened | October 17, 1999 |
| Location | Downtown LA, near Convention Center |

| Naming rights | [[Crypto.com]] ($700M/20 years, Dec 2021) |
| Conceived by | [[Steve Soboroff]] during Riordan administration |
