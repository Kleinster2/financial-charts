# Rancho La Puerta

North America's original wellness destination spa, founded 1940 by Edmond and Deborah Szekely in Tecate, Baja California, [[Mexico]]. 4,000-acre nature preserve at the foot of Mount Kuchumaa. Max 150 guests/week. Family-owned and operated since founding — president Sarah Livia Brightwood (daughter of founders). Co-founder Deborah Szekely turned 103 in May 2025. Repeatedly named "World's Best Destination Spa" by Travel + Leisure. Lacto-ovo pescatarian dining. 32 acres of gardens, 40 miles of trails, 11 gyms, four pools. Motto: "Siempre Mejor" (Always Better). Early guests paid $17.50/week to pitch tents; now a world-renowned luxury wellness destination.
