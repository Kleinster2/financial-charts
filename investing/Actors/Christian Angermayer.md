#actor #person #investor

**Christian Angermayer** — German entrepreneur and founder of [[Apeiron Investment Group]], a family-office-style investment firm focused on biotechnology, life sciences, psychedelics, longevity, and adjacent frontier-medicine bets. Co-founder and Executive Chairman of [[Enhanced Group]] (NYSE: ENHA) since 2023. Known publicly for his role in the commercial development of psychedelic therapies (psilocybin and MDMA-assisted psychotherapy) through Apeiron-backed companies including atai Life Sciences and Compass Pathways. Holds approximately 96.6% of [[Enhanced Group]] voting power post-merger via Class B shares held at Enhanced Holdings LP.
