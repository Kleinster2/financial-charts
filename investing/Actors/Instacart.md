## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Advertising high-margin growth
- 1,500+ retailer relationships
- Instacart+ subscription stickiness
- Grocery delivery leader
- Path to profitability

**Bear:**
- DoorDash grocery competition
- Amazon/[[Walmart]] scale
- Shopper economics challenged
- Delivery margins thin
- Retailer disintermediation risk
