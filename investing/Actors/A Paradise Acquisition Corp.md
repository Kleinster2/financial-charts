#actor #finance #spac #completed

**A Paradise Acquisition Corp** (formerly NASDAQ: APAD) — special purpose acquisition company that completed its business combination with [[Enhanced Ltd]] on May 1, 2026, with the surviving entity becoming [[Enhanced Group]] Inc. and beginning to trade on NYSE under ticker ENHA on May 8, 2026. Sponsored by A SPAC IV (Holdings) Corp., a BVI entity. IPO'd July 30, 2025 at $10.00 per public share with approximately $200M in trust. As of March 31, 2026 trust balance had grown to $205.1M with accrued interest. At combination, public shareholders redeemed 19,611,370 shares at $10.28 per share for approximately $201.7M — roughly a 98% redemption rate, leaving only ~$3.4M of trust cash to fund the combined Enhanced Group.
