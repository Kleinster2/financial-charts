Baird (Robert W. Baird & Co.) is an employee-owned US investment bank and financial services firm headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Founded in 1919, the firm operates across wealth management, asset management, investment banking, and equity research. With over 4,700 associates and $405B+ in client assets, Baird is one of the largest independent, employee-owned financial firms in the US.

Baird's equity research division covers mid-cap industrials, technology, and healthcare — sectors where it competes with [[Stifel]], [[William Blair]], and [[Piper Sandler]] for mid-market coverage. The investment banking arm is active in M&A advisory and capital markets for middle-market companies. The employee-ownership model (100% since 2004) has fostered low turnover and consistent culture, which differentiates it from publicly traded or PE-owned competitors. Baird analysts are regularly cited in the investing vault on industrial and tech coverage.
