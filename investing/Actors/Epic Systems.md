Epic Systems — American healthcare software company. Founded 1979 by Judith Faulkner in a basement in Madison, Wisconsin. Builds electronic health records (EHR) used by major hospital systems. Largest bootstrapped software company in the world — never took outside investment. Faulkner retains majority ownership. ~$4.6B revenue (2023), ~13,000 employees.
