## Why Piemonte matters

Brazil's DC-focused financial group:

- **Elea founder**: Created Elea Digital in 2019, now building Rio AI City (3.2GW)
- **Goldman partnership**: GSAM invested in Elea (2021), completed Feb 2022
- **Piemonte Capital**: First Brazilian asset manager focused exclusively on data centers
