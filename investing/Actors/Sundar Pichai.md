# Sundar Pichai

CEO of [[Alphabet]] (and [[Google]]). Joined Google 2004; took over as Google CEO in 2015 when Alphabet was created and as Alphabet CEO in 2019. Steered Google through the AI transition that produced [[Google DeepMind]] (via the 2023 merger of Google Brain and DeepMind) and [[Gemini]]. Among the named succession candidates inside Alphabet are [[Thomas Kurian]] ([[Google Cloud]] CEO) and others.
