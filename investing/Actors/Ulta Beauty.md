# Ulta Beauty

**Largest US beauty retailer** — unique mass + prestige model under one roof. ~$12.3B revenue (FY25E), 1,473 stores, 44M+ loyalty members. Lost market share for the first time in FY24, but "Ulta Unleashed" turnaround under CEO Kecia Steelman showing results: Q3 FY25 comps +6.3%, raised guidance twice. Acquired [[Space]] NK ([[UK]], 83 stores). Expanding to [[Mexico]], Middle East.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Turnaround working (Q3 comps +6.3%, guidance raised twice)
- International expansion just starting ([[UK]], [[Mexico]], Middle East)
- Marketplace adds long-tail brands without inventory risk
- 44M loyalty members = data advantage
- $2.2B buyback remaining
- Gen Z's \#2 favorite in-store shopping destination

**Bear:**
- [[Amazon]] taking beauty share aggressively
- Sephora at Kohl's = 1,000+ new points of distribution
- [[TikTok]] Shop disrupting discovery/purchase
- Mass gaining share (dupe economy)
- Cosmetics share of sales declining
- Physical retail model vs digital shift
