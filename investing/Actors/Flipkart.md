**Flipkart** — India's largest e-commerce platform. 500M+ users. ~85% owned by [[Walmart]] ($16B acquisition 2018). Targeting $60-70B IPO valuation in 2026 — would be India's largest new-economy IPO. Completed "reverse flip" (Singapore → India domicile) Dec 2024 to enable domestic listing. Still unprofitable.
