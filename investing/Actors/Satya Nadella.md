#actor #executive #usa

**Satya Nadella** — CEO and Chairman of [[Microsoft]] since 2014/2021. Transformed Microsoft from Windows-centric to cloud-first, AI-forward.

---

## Background

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Born | August 19, 1967, Hyderabad, India |
| Education | BS Electrical Engineering (Mangalore), MS Computer Science (Wisconsin-Milwaukee), MBA ([[University of Chicago]] Booth) |
| At Microsoft | 1992 - present (33 years) |
| CEO since | February 4, 2014 |
| Chairman since | June 16, 2021 |

Son of Indian Administrative Service officer. Wanted to be professional cricketer before pivoting to tech. Flew Seattle→Chicago every weekend for 2.5 years to complete MBA while working at Microsoft.

## Key moves as CEO

**Acquisitions:**
- [[LinkedIn]] — $26B (2016)
- [[GitHub]] — $7.5B (2018)
- [[Nuance]] — $19.7B (2021)
- [[Activision Blizzard]] — $69B (2023)

**Strategic:**
- Azure cloud pivot — now \#2 behind [[AWS]]
- [[OpenAI]] partnership — $13B+ invested, 27% equity stake
- "Mobile-first, cloud-first" → "AI-first" transformation
- In "founder mode" as of Jan 2026 — weekly cross-team AI meetings

**Results:**
- Stock ~10x since becoming CEO (2014-2024)
- 27% annual growth rate vs near-zero under Ballmer's final years
- Market cap: ~$300B (2014) → ~$3.2T (2026)

---

## Leadership style

Known for cultural transformation at Microsoft. Replaced internal competition ("stack ranking") with growth mindset culture. Book: *Hit Refresh* (2017).

Currently 16 direct reports. Elevated young talent: Asha Sharma (37), Charles Lamanna (37) leading AI product categories.

Age 58 (same age Ballmer left) — expected to stay several more years.
