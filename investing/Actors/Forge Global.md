## What Forge does

Private securities marketplace connecting buyers and sellers of pre-IPO shares. Three pillars:

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Marketplace | Matching buyers/sellers of private company stock |
| Custody | Holding and administering private securities |
| Data | Private market intelligence and pricing data |

Competes with Nasdaq Private Market, EquityZen, and secondary market brokers.
