**Forge Global** — Secondary market for private company shares. Trade pre-IPO stock in companies like SpaceX, Stripe, Databricks.

---

## Why Forge matters

Forge is the stock exchange for private companies:

| Function | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Secondary trading | Buy/sell private shares |
| Price discovery | Market data on private valuations |
| Custody | Hold private securities |
| Data | Private market intelligence |

---

## How it works

**Secondary market mechanics:**
1. Employee/early investor wants liquidity
2. Lists shares on Forge
3. Accredited investor buys
4. Forge handles transfer, compliance

**Key difference from tokenization:**
- Actual equity (not derivative)
- Accredited investors only
- Company must approve transfers
- Real shareholder rights

## vs Tokenized shares

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Only public pure-play on private markets
- Companies staying private longer
- Growing demand for pre-IPO access
- Data moat on private valuations
- Tokenization could drive more interest

**Bear:**
- Low trading volumes (illiquid)
- Accredited investor limit
- Company approval bottleneck
- IPO window affects demand
- Tokenization could disintermediate
