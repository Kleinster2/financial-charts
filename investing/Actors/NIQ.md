# NIQ

Consumer intelligence company formed from [[Nielsen]]'s Global Connect business. Sold to [[Advent International]] for $2.7B in March 2021. Acquired German market research firm [[GfK]] in 2023 and rebranded from NielsenIQ to NIQ. Measures consumer purchasing behavior, retail sales, and market trends for CPG companies globally. Completely separate from [[Nielsen]] (media measurement).
