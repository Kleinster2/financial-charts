# Blue Bottle Coffee

Oakland-founded specialty coffee roaster and cafe chain. Acquired by [[Nestle]] in 2017 for ~$500M (68% stake, later increased to full ownership). Operates cafes in the US, Japan, South Korea, and Hong Kong. Known for minimalist aesthetic and single-origin focus.
