21st Century Fox — the media company whose film and TV assets were acquired by [[Disney]] in 2019 in a $71bn deal (~$54bn net after disposals including the Sky stake). The acquisition gave Disney the content volume and executives (including [[Dana Walden]]) that [[Bob Iger]] said enabled Disney+ to scale to "number two to Netflix globally." The rump business became Fox Corporation.
