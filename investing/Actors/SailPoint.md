# SailPoint

Identity governance and administration (IGA) leader. Manages who has access to what, certifies it's appropriate, and automates lifecycle (joiner/mover/leaver). Publicly traded on NASDAQ (SAIL) after Thoma Bravo took it private in 2022 and re-IPO'd.
