# Nirma

[[India]]'s second-largest [[Soda Ash|soda ash]] producer (~27% domestic market share) with ~1.5 Mt/year capacity (4,000 MTPD) at manufacturing units in Kalatalav and Porbandar, Gujarat. Originally known as a detergent and FMCG company, Nirma diversified into soda ash and cement. Privately held.
