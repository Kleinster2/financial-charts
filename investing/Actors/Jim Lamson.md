# Jim Lamson

Former [[CIA]] analyst focused on [[Iran]]; senior research associate at the [[James Martin Center for Nonproliferation Studies]]. Named in the Financial Times' April 14, 2026 investigation into [[Iran]]'s use of the Chinese-built [[TEE-01B]] satellite (see [[IRGC Chinese satellite targeting March 2026]]). Lamson framed the Chinese ground-station arrangement as a "dispersion strategy" — Iran's own satellite ground stations were repeatedly hit by [[Israel]] in 2025-2026, but Chinese ground stations in third countries remain operationally available.
