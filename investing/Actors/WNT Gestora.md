**WNT Gestora** — Brazilian asset management firm controlling ~R$11.5B across 112 funds. Primary investment vehicle of [[Nelson Tanure]]. Also held resources from [[Daniel Vorcaro]] ([[Banco Master]]). The fund was the vehicle through which Tanure built his 21.8% position in [[Light SA]] and other portfolio companies.

## Dissolution

After the [[Banco Master]] collapse (Nov 2025), [[Daniel Vorcaro]] sold his WNT stake to [[BTG Pactual]]. BTG now holds ~15% of [[Light SA]] through this position and leads the judicial recovery process. Multiple fund positions were subsequently unwound by creditors.
