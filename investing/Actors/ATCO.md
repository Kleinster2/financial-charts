**ATCO** — Canadian diversified energy infrastructure corporation. Operates regulated utilities in Alberta, international energy projects, and modular structures. 50% owner of [[LUMA Energy]] (Puerto Rico grid operator). Publicly traded on TSX as ACO.X; subsidiary [[Canadian Utilities]] (CU) also listed.
