#actor #government #usa #regulator

**CFPB** -- Consumer Financial Protection Bureau. US federal regulator for consumer financial products (created by Dodd-Frank, 2010). Oversees credit cards, mortgages, student loans, BNPL.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Established | 2010 (Dodd-Frank Act) |
| Jurisdiction | Consumer financial products and services |
| Key actions (BNPL) | May 2024 interpretive rule classifying pay-in-4 BNPL as credit cards; revoked Mar 2025 |
