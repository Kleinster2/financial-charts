# Boyd Jefferies

Founder of [[Jefferies]] (1962). Pioneer of the "third market" — over-the-counter trading of listed stocks with negotiated commissions. Started the firm with $30,000 in borrowed capital and a seat on the [[Pacific Coast Stock Exchange]]. Pleaded guilty in March 1987 to securities parking for [[Ivan Boesky]] and margin violations; received a five-year ban from the securities industry. Resigned as chairman. Died August 2001.
