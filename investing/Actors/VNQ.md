## What It Tracks

The MSCI US Investable Market Real Estate 25/50 Index — data-center, industrial, retail, residential, healthcare, and tower REITs, cap-weighted. Top names include the large specialized REITs (towers, industrial, data centers) rather than generic office.

## Use Case

Rate-sensitive income exposure and the listed-real-estate alternative to owning property or opaque private funds. In [[Bruno Corano]]-style advice it is the liquid answer to the Brazilian FII bias — diversified, daily-priced, and penalty-free, versus single-building funds. Trades closer to [[XLU]] (rate-sensitive income) than to [[SPY]].
