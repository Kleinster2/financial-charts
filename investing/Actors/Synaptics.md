**Synaptics** — Edge-device semiconductor and software company being acquired by [[onsemi]] in a roughly $7B all-stock deal announced Jun 25 2026. Trading symbol SYNA on [[Nasdaq]] at the acquisition announcement.

Synaptics matters in the vault because the deal converts onsemi's [[Automotive semiconductors|automotive/power-semiconductor]] story into a broader [[Embodied AI|physical-AI]] claim. Its portfolio sits closer to edge compute, human-machine interface, wireless connectivity, and multimodal sensing than onsemi's historical power/Silicon Carbide franchise.
