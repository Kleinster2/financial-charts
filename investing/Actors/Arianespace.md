# Arianespace

European launch services provider, subsidiary of [[ArianeGroup]] (joint venture of Airbus and Safran). Operates Ariane 6 heavy-lift rocket from Guiana Space Centre, French Guiana.

---

## Why it matters

Arianespace is [[Amazon]]'s largest booked launch provider for Leo with 18 total missions contracted. The Feb 2026 LE-01 mission (Ariane 64, four-booster config) deployed the largest Amazon Leo payload to date (32 satellites). Heavy-lift capability enables faster constellation deployment.
