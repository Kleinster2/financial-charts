## AI server expansion

**Supercomputing 2025 lineup:**
- CG480-S5063: 4U AI server (8x NVIDIA H200/RTX PRO 6000)

- NVIDIA DGX Station and DGX Spark platforms
- Support for Blackwell Ultra GPUs

**Strategy:** Leverage gaming expertise for enterprise AI systems.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Strong gaming brand recognition
- AI server diversification
- CES 2025 innovation awards
- India/US manufacturing expansion
- NVIDIA partnership for enterprise

**Bear:**
- PC market cyclicality
- Dependent on NVIDIA/AMD for GPUs
- Competition from ASUS, Gigabyte
- Market cap declined 34% in 2025
- Margin pressure in components
