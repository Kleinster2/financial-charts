## Business model

Traxys works at the center of the global metals and minerals industry:

**Services:**
- Sourcing and procurement
- Logistics and transportation
- Trade financing
- Marketing to end users

**Value proposition:** Move metals and minerals to market faster, more efficiently, with lower working capital and less risk.

---

## Commodity focus

Trades across the full spectrum of industrial metals and minerals:

**Key areas:**
- Ferroalloys
- Battery metals (lithium, cobalt, nickel)
- Minor metals
- Industrial minerals
- Energy materials

**Trade organization memberships:**
- Battery Council International (BCI)
- Cobalt Institute (CI)
- International Lithium Association (ILiA)
- International Manganese Institute (IMnI)
- Minor Metals Trade Association (MMTA)
- Conflict Free Tin Initiative (CFTI)

## [[Project Vault]] role

Selected as one of three **procurement managers** for the $12B strategic critical minerals stockpile alongside [[Hartree Partners]] and [[Mercuria Energy Group]].

Traxys's expertise in:
- Global sourcing networks
- Physical commodity logistics
- Trade financing

Makes it well-suited for building strategic mineral inventories.
