## Why UBS BB matters

**Unique hybrid model:**

- **Global + local**: UBS global network + Banco do Brasil presence in 99% of Brazil municipalities
- **Full service**: M&A, DCM, ECM, institutional brokerage, research
- **LatAm reach**: Not just Brazil — covers 6 South American markets
