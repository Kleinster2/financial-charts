# Cox Communications

US cable and broadband operator owned by Cox Enterprises. In 2025, agreed to merge with [[Charter Communications]] in a $35.2bn transaction — the sixth-largest US M&A deal of the year per LSEG. [[Wachtell Lipton]] advised on the deal.
