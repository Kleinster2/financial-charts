**Brian Whitmer** — co-founder of [[Instructure]] and co-creator of [[Canvas LMS]]. BYU graduate student in human-computer interaction in 2008 when he and [[Devlin Daley]] built the Canvas prototype in a software-business class taught by [[Josh Coates]]. Served as Chief Product Officer and later Chief Learning Officer at Instructure. Operational role ended in 2018, though he remained associated with the company through the public period.
