**IRGC** — [[Iran]]'s Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps. Elite military, intelligence, and economic force operating parallel to Iran's conventional armed forces. Controls the Strait of Hormuz northern shore via its naval arm (IRGCN). Oversees Iran's ballistic missile and drone programs. Extensive economic interests through construction, energy, and telecom conglomerates (Khatam al-Anbiya, etc.). US-designated Foreign Terrorist Organization since 2019.
