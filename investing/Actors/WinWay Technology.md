# WinWay Technology

**WinWay Technology** is a Taiwanese semiconductor test socket and interconnect supplier, listed on the TWSE as 6190.TW. The company designs and manufactures high-performance test sockets used in final test and burn-in stages of semiconductor production. As AI accelerator chips grow larger (with higher pin counts, tighter pitch, and greater thermal output), test sockets have become a critical bottleneck — they must handle higher insertion forces, lower contact resistance, and maintain signal integrity at frequencies above 50 GHz.

WinWay's customer base includes major OSAT firms and IDMs testing advanced logic and HBM memory. The AI buildout has been a direct tailwind: test houses running [[NVIDIA]] GPU and [[AMD]] accelerator validation need more sockets per device, and the sockets wear out faster under AI-grade thermal stress, driving replacement demand. WinWay competes with [[Enplas]] (Japan), [[LEENO]] (Korea), and [[ISC Co]] (Korea) in a market that remains fragmented but is consolidating around suppliers with high-frequency signal expertise.
