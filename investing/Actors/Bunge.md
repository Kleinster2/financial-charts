**Bunge** (NYSE: BG) — one of the world's largest agribusiness and food companies. Major oilseed processor and supplier of vegetable oils to the biodiesel and [[Renewable fuels|renewable fuel]] industries globally. Merged with [[Viterra]] in 2024.
