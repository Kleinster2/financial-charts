# Quentin Koffey

**Quentin Koffey** is the founder of [[Politan Management]], an activist hedge fund. Previously at [[Elliott Management]]. Led back-to-back proxy fights at [[Masimo]] in 2023 and 2024, winning four board seats (including his own in 2023). The activism ousted founder-CEO [[Joe Kiani]], forced divestiture of the Sound United consumer audio business, and catalyzed the $9.9B [[Danaher]] acquisition (February 2026).
