# Stella Chemifa

Japanese specialty chemicals company and the dominant supplier of electronic-grade [[Hydrofluoric Acid]] for semiconductor fabrication. Supplies ~90% of [[Japan]]'s semiconductor-grade HF. Listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange (4109.T). In March 2025, introduced a new HF variant for selective silicon etching with 42% better performance in high-aspect-ratio structures, now being tested in over 18% of Japanese fabs.
