# Clearlake Capital

US private equity firm based in Santa Monica, California, managing ~$80B+ in assets. Focuses on technology-enabled services, industrials, and consumer sectors. Founded in 2006 by Behdad Eghbali and Jose Feliciano. Known for an operationally intensive approach that combines software tools and data analytics with traditional PE playbooks.

Notable portfolio includes [[Cornerstone OnDemand]] (HR/talent software, taken private 2022), a stake in [[Chelsea FC]] alongside Todd Boehly, and various enterprise software companies. The Cornerstone position surfaced in [[Private Credit Software Concentration]] analysis when Glendon Capital flagged it as a concentrated exposure risk in private credit portfolios (March 2026).
