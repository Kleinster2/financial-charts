## How to read it as a source

Contributor articles are individual opinions of varying quality, not house research — cite as "[[Seeking Alpha]] contributor" with date, never as institutional analyst consensus. The Quant Ratings and fair-value estimates are mechanical factor outputs; useful as a screen, not a price target. Transcripts are reliable primary material.
