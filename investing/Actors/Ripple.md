Ripple — San Francisco-based fintech focused on cross-border payments using blockchain. Operates RippleNet and created the [[XRP]] Ledger. Fought a multi-year SEC lawsuit (2020-2025) that became a landmark case for crypto regulation. Raised $0 in VC but holds significant XRP reserves.
