## Economy segment dynamics

**16,000+ independent economy hotels in US** — large addressable market for soft-brand conversions.

**Recession resilience:** Select-service hotels outperformed higher segments by 2,500 bps during 2020 pandemic. Budget travelers trade down in downturns.

**2025 headwind:** Economy RevPAR -1.8% YTD — inflation pressuring lower-income travelers.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Largest by property count (franchise scale)
- Economy segment = recession hedge (historically)
- High franchise fee margins
- Dividend growth (+8%)
- Analyst target $95 (+32% upside)

**Bear:**
- Economy segment struggling in 2025 (-1.8% RevPAR)
- Smaller revenue base ($1.4B vs MAR $26B)
- Lower-income consumer pressure
- Cut full-year outlook (Q3 2025)
