**Fitch Ratings** — One of the "Big Three" credit rating agencies alongside [[Moody's]] and [[S&P Global]]. Owned by Hearst Corporation (80%) and Fimalac (20%). Headquartered in New York and London.

---

## Big Three context

| Agency | Parent | Revenue | HQ |
|--------|--------|---------|-----|
| [[S&P Global]] | S&P Global (public) | ~$3.5B (ratings) | New York |
| [[Moody's]] | Moody's Corp (public) | ~$3.6B (ratings) | New York |
| **Fitch Ratings** | Hearst (private) | ~$2B (est.) | New York / London |

Fitch is the smallest of the three and the only one that is privately held. Dual-headquartered in New York and London.
