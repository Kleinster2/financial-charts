# Aurora Cannabis

A Canadian licensed producer that pivoted from a debt-laden, oversupplied consumer model after the 2018 cannabis collapse toward a higher-margin global medical-cannabis franchise (Canada, Europe, Australia). One of the original large-cap Canadian LPs; among the lower-volatility names in the [[Cannabis]] cohort (57% annualized) after years of restructuring and share consolidation. Federally legal in Canada and NASDAQ-listed. A member of the Canadian LP bloc; collapses into [[MSOS]]/[[MJ]]. Distinct from [[Aurora]] (Aurora Innovation, the self-driving-truck company).
