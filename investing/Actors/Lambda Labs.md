## Why Lambda matters

Lambda is the developer-focused alternative to hyperscaler GPU clouds:

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Focus | GPU cloud for ML/AI |
| Differentiation | Developer experience, simplicity |
| Customers | AI researchers, startups, enterprises |
| Status | Private |

---

## Business model

**What they offer:**
- On-demand GPU instances (H100, A100, etc.)
- GPU clusters for training
- Inference endpoints
- Simpler than AWS/Azure
- Competitive pricing

**Target customers:**
- AI/ML researchers
- Startups (can't negotiate hyperscaler deals)
- Academic institutions
- Enterprise ML teams

---

## Developer focus

**Why developers like Lambda:**
- Simple API and interface
- No cloud certification needed
- Fast spin-up
- ML-native tooling
- Good documentation

**Contrast with hyperscalers:**
- AWS = complex, enterprise-focused
- Lambda = simple, developer-focused

## Competitive position

| Competitor | Strength | Lambda advantage |
|------------|----------|------------------|
| AWS/Azure/GCP | Scale, ecosystem | Simpler UX |
| [[CoreWeave]] | Availability | Developer focus |

| Together AI | Inference | Training + inference |
