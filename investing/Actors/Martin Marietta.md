# Martin Marietta

Martin Marietta (NYSE: MLM) is a US construction-aggregates producer — crushed stone, sand, and gravel, plus cement and ready-mix in select markets — the #2 US aggregates company. Demand is weighted to public infrastructure and non-residential construction. Used as the construction-materials control in the [[Building products]] cluster validation.
