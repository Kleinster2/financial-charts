# Terra Industries

Former nitrogen fertilizer producer. Acquired by [[CF Industries]] in 2010 for $4.7B — the deal that made CF one of the world's largest nitrogen producers, roughly doubling its capacity. Headquartered in Sioux City, Iowa.
