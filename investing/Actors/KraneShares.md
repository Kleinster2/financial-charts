# KraneShares

US thematic-ETF issuer (Krane Funds Advisors LLC, New York), founded 2013 by Jonathan Krane, specializing in China and disruptive-theme funds. Its flagship is [[KWEB]] (China Internet), the largest US-listed China-internet ETF; it also runs [[KARS]] (electric vehicles & future mobility), a China carbon/clean-tech suite, and other thematic products. Majority-owned by China International Capital Corporation ([[CICC]]) since the late 2010s, which gives it distribution and index ties into onshore China.

The China focus is why two of its funds anchor cluster-validation verdicts in this vault: [[Chinese internet]] resolves to [[KWEB]] (its dedicated China-internet ETF), while [[Chinese EV startups]] lands on broad [[China]] beta rather than [[KARS]] (which is Tesla-led/global).
