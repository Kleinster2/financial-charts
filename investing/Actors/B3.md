**B3** (Brasil, Bolsa, Balcão) — Brazilian exchange and clearinghouse. Monopoly on [[Brazil]]'s capital markets. Formed 2017 from BM&FBovespa + CETIP merger. Publishes [[CDI]], registers all OTC derivatives.

## CETIP legacy (OTC infrastructure)

CETIP (Central de Custódia e Liquidação Financeira de Títulos) merged into B3 in 2017. Now handles:

| Function | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **[[CDI]] publication** | Daily interbank rate calculation |
| **OTC derivatives registration** | All swaps, NDFs must register within 2 business days (BCB Resolution 277) |
| **Fixed income custody** | Corporate debentures, CDBs |
| **Clearing** | Central counterparty for registered trades |

For [[Cross-currency swaps]]: both legs registered at B3, providing counterparty risk mitigation and regulatory compliance.
