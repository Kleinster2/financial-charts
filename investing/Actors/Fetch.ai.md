#actor #crypto #ai #agents

**Fetch.ai** — Autonomous-agent framework and network founded 2017 in Cambridge, [[UK]]. Provides infrastructure for developers to build AI agents that can transact, negotiate, and coordinate on-chain. In March 2024 Fetch.ai merged with SingularityNET and Ocean Protocol to form the [[ASI Alliance]]; FET token migrated 1:1 to the combined ASI token in July 2024. Fetch.ai functions remain the agent-layer contribution to the merged alliance.

## Relevance

Fetch.ai is one of the longer-running attempts to merge AI agents with tokenized economic coordination, predating the current agent-framework cycle ([[ElizaOS]], [[Virtuals Protocol]]) by half a decade. The [[ASI Alliance]] merger was an attempt to consolidate this older cohort into a single vehicle. For investors evaluating the decentralized-AI agent layer, Fetch is the precedent case: long operational history, weak recent token performance, meaningful technology base.
