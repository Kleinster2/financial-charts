# iSpot

Real-time TV ad measurement company. Measures ad occurrences and audience across 51M+ TVs and set-top boxes using smart TV ACR (automatic content recognition) integrations. Early alternative measurement partner to [[NBC|NBCU]]. Certified by the U.S. JIC as a transactable currency for the 2025-26 TV season alongside [[Comscore]] and [[VideoAmp]].
