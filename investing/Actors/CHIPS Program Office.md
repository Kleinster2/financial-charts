CHIPS Program Office (CPO) — bureau within the [[Commerce Department]] created to administer funding authorized by the 2022 [[CHIPS Act|CHIPS and Science Act]]. Originally scoped to semiconductor manufacturing grants and loans, its mandate has broadened in 2025–2026 under the second Trump administration to include adjacent critical-minerals and advanced-manufacturing awards structured with federal equity participation alongside grants and loans.
