# Red Cat Holdings

US-based defense and national-security drone holding company (Nasdaq: RCAT). Operates through wholly-owned subsidiaries Teal Drones, FlightWave Aerospace, and (acquired Apr 2026) Apium Swarm Robotics. Flagship product is the Black Widow small unmanned aircraft system (sUAS) — NDAA-compliant tactical-edge ISR drone descended from the US Army's Short-Range Reconnaissance program lineage. Top-ten holding in the [[DRNZ]] drone ETF.

## Key products

- Black Widow — flagship sUAS; tactical-edge ISR; modular mission payloads. Selected by NATO ally on competitive tender Mar 2026; APAC ally Dec 2025/Feb 2026 — both with CY2026 deliveries.
- Teal 2 — small-form reconnaissance drone (Teal Drones lineage).
- FlightWave Edge 130 — VTOL fixed-wing platform for longer-endurance ISR.
- Apium Swarm — undersea/surface autonomous swarm robotics (acquired Apr 2026).

## Investment context

Public-market exposure to the [[Replicator Initiative]] / [[Defense Autonomous Warfare Group]] thesis at the small-cap, NDAA-compliant end. Differentiates from larger drone primes ([[General Atomics]], [[Northrop Grumman]]) by ticker accessibility and from private autonomy-stack leaders ([[Anduril]], [[Shield AI]]) by being investable. NDAA-compliance is the procurement gate for federal/military buyers — a structural moat against PRC-supplied sUAS alternatives.
