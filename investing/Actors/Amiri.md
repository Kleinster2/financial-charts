## Mike Amiri

**Background:** Born 1976, grew up in Hollywood surrounded by graffiti artists, skaters, musicians. Self-taught designer.

**Aesthetic:** Californian nostalgia + rock 'n' roll + streetwear luxury. Distressed denim, bandana prints, leather.

**Celebrity following:** Early adopters included Justin Bieber, Kanye West, A$AP Rocky.

---

## Brand positioning

**Price point:** $500-2,000+ (luxury streetwear)

**Target:** Hip-hop, entertainment industry, aspirational luxury consumers

**Signature:** Distressed jeans, MA logo, bandana motifs, leather jackets

**Competition:** Fear of God, Palm Angels, Off-White (RIP Virgil), Rhude

---

## OTB partnership (2019)

OTB took minority stake to:
- Provide global infrastructure
- Support international expansion
- Strategic guidance from Renzo Rosso

Amiri retains creative control and majority ownership.
