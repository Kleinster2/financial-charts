Dana Walden — [[Disney]] entertainment executive; joined Disney via the 2019 [[21st Century Fox]] acquisition. [[Bob Iger]] credited her arrival, alongside the Fox content library, with giving Disney enough TV and film volume to scale streaming to "number two to Netflix globally" (FT, Jun 2026). Co-chair of Disney Entertainment.
