**Anthony Wood** — Founder, chairman, and CEO of [[Roku]]. Founded Roku in 2002; previously created ReplayTV, an early DVR pioneer, and incubated the streaming-player project inside [[Netflix]] before spinning it out as Roku. Steered Roku's pivot from low-margin streaming hardware to a [[Platform economics|platform-and-advertising]] model. June 2026: agreed to sell Roku to [[Fox Corporation]] for ~$22B, framing the platform as "the leading TV streaming platform, reaching more than 100 million households globally."
