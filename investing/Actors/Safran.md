## Pax Silica supplier-screening risk (May 2026)

The May 28, 2026 [[Journal of Space Commerce]] / Ex Terra Media excerpt names [[Safran]] among commercial-space and aerospace procurement networks exposed to [[Pax Silica]] trusted-vendor screening. Safran's relevance is its aerospace/defense electronics and systems footprint: even when the prime is European and allied-aligned, sub-tier electronics, sensors, precision structures, and rare-earth magnet inputs can become qualification issues if they remain China-anchored.

The investment read-through is procurement friction rather than an immediate revenue shock. US-aligned customers may increasingly expect aerospace suppliers to document [[China plus one]] or [[ASEAN]] diversification plans for sensitive hardware inputs.
