## Business model

**Razor/blade model:**
- Equipment sales (one-time)
- Consumables (blades, wheels) — recurring
- Service and maintenance
- High gross margins (~50%)

---

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Monopoly position
- Every chip needs dicing
- Advanced packaging = more cuts
- Consumables = recurring revenue
- Japanese quality reputation

**Bear:**
- Cyclical with wafer starts
- Concentrated in dicing
- Yen exposure
- Limited growth drivers beyond volume
