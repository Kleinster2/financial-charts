#actor #vc #usa

Draper Fisher Jurvetson — Silicon Valley venture capital firm founded in 1985 by Tim Draper, John Fisher, and Steve Jurvetson. Early investor in [[Tesla]], [[SpaceX]] (Series B, D, F), [[Skype]], [[Baidu]], and [[Hotmail]]. Rebranded to Threshold Ventures in 2019 after Jurvetson's departure. DFJ Growth (growth-stage affiliate) participated in [[SpaceX]] Series G.
