## Scandal recovery

**2016 fake accounts scandal:**
- Unauthorized account openings
- $3B+ in fines
- Fed asset cap (still in place)
- Multi-year remediation

**Charlie Scharf (CEO since 2019):**
- Efficiency improvements
- Risk management overhaul
- Expense reduction
- Working to lift asset cap

---

## Asset cap constraint

**Fed restriction:**
- Cannot grow assets above ~$1.95T
- In place since 2018
- Limits growth potential
- Removal = major catalyst

---

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Asset cap removal catalyst
- Efficiency gains continuing
- Rate sensitivity
- Discount to peers

**Bear:**
- Asset cap timeline uncertain
- Regulatory overhang
- Reputation damage lingering
- Competition in core markets
