### Northvolt contract cancellation

| Fact | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Original deal | $2.3B (signed 2020) |
| Problem | Quality issues, 2-year delays |
| Cancelled | Jun 2024 |
| Replacement | [[Samsung SDI]] |

BMW's exit triggered Northvolt's collapse within 6 months.

### In-house production

BMW is building 5 battery facilities near vehicle plants. Dingolfing (Bavaria) has produced 1.5M motors, 1M high-voltage batteries, 10M modules since 2014.
