## What it does

Context positions itself as an infrastructure layer for enterprise AI: connect internal systems once, make institutional knowledge accessible to agents, and automate workflows across documents, spreadsheets, presentations, data analysis, and coding tasks.

---

## Apr 2026 — compromise tied to [[Vercel]] incident

[[Vercel]] said its April 2026 security incident originated with a compromise of Context.ai, a third-party AI tool used by a Vercel employee. According to Vercel's bulletin, that compromise allowed an attacker to take over the employee's Vercel Google Workspace account and reach some Vercel environments plus environment variables that were not marked as sensitive.

The episode makes Context.ai relevant to the vault as a supply-chain case study. Enterprise AI tools increasingly sit inside identity, document, and workflow graphs with enough privilege to become lateral-movement paths if their OAuth or admin surfaces are compromised.
