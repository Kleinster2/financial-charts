#actor #sovereign-wealth-fund #state-owned #kazakhstan

Samruk-Kazyna — Kazakhstan's sovereign wealth fund. Holds the state's principal industrial assets including the national mining company [[Tau-Ken Samruk]] (which holds 30% of the Severniy Katpar LLP tungsten JV with [[Cove Kaz Capital]]) and major energy holdings. The fund's mining-asset arm is Kazakhstan's principal counterparty for inbound foreign capital into critical-mineral projects.
