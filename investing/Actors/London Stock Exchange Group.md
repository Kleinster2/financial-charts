**London Stock Exchange Group** (LSE: LSEG) — Exchange and financial data conglomerate. Owns London Stock Exchange, [[Refinitiv]], FTSE Russell indices. Data now >70% of revenue after $27B Refinitiv acquisition.

## Refinitiv acquisition

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Announced | August 2019 |
| Closed | January 2021 |
| Deal value | **$27B** (all-stock) |
| Sellers | [[Blackstone]] (55%), [[Thomson Reuters]] (45%) |
| Seller stake in LSEG | ~37% economic, 29% voting |

**Rationale:** Combine exchange infrastructure with Refinitiv data to compete with [[Bloomberg LP]].

**Synergies:** Targeting £350-400M annual run rate by 2025. Achieved £292M by end of 2024.

**Brand retirement:** Refinitiv name discontinued August 2023. Eikon terminal → LSEG Workspace.

## Competitive position

**vs. Bloomberg:**
LSEG is the main challenger to [[Bloomberg LP]] in financial data. But:
- Refinitiv/Eikon losing share (19.6% vs Bloomberg's 33%)
- No equivalent to Bloomberg Message network effects
- Competing on price (~$22K vs Bloomberg ~$27K)

**Differentiation:** Integration with exchange/clearing infrastructure. [[Microsoft]] partnership for cloud/AI.
