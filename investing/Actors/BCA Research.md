# BCA Research

**BCA Research** — independent macro strategy research firm. Positioned as a structural bear in 2025-2026, favoring government bonds over corporates and short equity positions. Known for contrarian geopolitical risk calls.

## Key calls

- Overweight government bonds vs corporates
- Warned concentrated bull markets (Nifty Fifty 1973, dot-com 2000) don't transition peacefully — Doug Peta: *"The most benign outcome would be a peaceful transfer of power to a broad coalition of the other 493. That's not how potent and highly concentrated bull markets typically evolve."*
- [[Marko Papic]] predicted US attack on [[Iran]] as tail risk for 2026 (FT, Jan 11 2026)
- Mid-April 2026 deadline for [[Strait of Hormuz]] reopening or "first post-Covid-19 break in supply chains"
