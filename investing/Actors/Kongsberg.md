# Kongsberg

Kongsberg (Oslo: KOG.OL), formally Kongsberg Gruppen ASA, is the Norwegian defense-and-maritime technology group — Kongsberg Defence & Aerospace (Naval Strike Missile / Joint Strike Missile, the NASAMS air-defense system with [[Raytheon]], Protector remote weapon stations), Kongsberg Maritime, and Kongsberg Discovery. The Norwegian state is the majority owner (~50%). A [[European rearmament]] beneficiary via its missile and air-defense franchises, but with a maritime/offshore leg that gives it a different return profile from the pure defense names.
