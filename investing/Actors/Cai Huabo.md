Co-founded [[Longsys Electronics]] in 1999 with twin sister Cai Lijiang. The Chinese name "Jiang-Bo-Long" (江波龙) combines their given names and shared zodiac sign (Dragon). Acquired the [[Lexar]] brand from [[Micron]] in 2017. State benefit: [[Greater Bay Area]] incentives and [[Big Fund]] (National Integrated Circuit Industry Investment Fund).
