# NEC

Japanese multinational electronics and IT firm founded 1899 as Nippon Electric Company, listed on Tokyo (6701.T). NEC's submarine cable business (NEC Submarine Cable Systems) is one of the "big three" globally in subsea cable manufacturing and installation — alongside [[SubCom]] (US) and [[Alcatel Submarine Networks]] (France/Nokia subsidiary). NEC has built or contributed to a substantial share of trans-Pacific and Asian regional subsea cable systems since the 1960s.

Beyond submarine cables, NEC operates across IT services, defense electronics, biometric authentication systems (a global leader), and 5G network infrastructure. The submarine-cable business is small relative to total revenue but strategically important and unusually exposed to the [[Economic latency]] policy framework if a US or G7 subsea-cable surge-capacity programme materialises.
