**Alphawave Semi** — High-speed wired connectivity and compute IP company. Acquired by [[Qualcomm]] for $2.4B (closed Dec 2025).

---

## Why it matters

Alphawave designs the serializer/deserializer (SerDes) and connectivity IP that moves data between chips at the highest speeds. Critical for data centers, AI networking, and chiplet interconnects.
