# INEOS

**INEOS** is a privately held British multinational chemicals company founded by [[Jim Ratcliffe]] in 1998, headquartered in London. One of the world's largest chemical producers by revenue (~$65B), with major operations in chlor-alkali/vinyls (Inovyn JV), olefins, polymers, and specialty chemicals. Significant European chlorine and PVC producer through its Inovyn subsidiary.
