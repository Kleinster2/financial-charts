# Alexis Bittar

Brooklyn-based jewelry and accessories brand founded by designer Alexis Bittar (b. 1968). Known for hand-carved Lucite pieces blending sculptural art with fashion.

## Business

- **Signature:** Hand-sculpted Lucite jewelry — mixed metals, semi-precious stones, distinctive textures
- **Categories:** Earrings, necklaces, bracelets, rings, handbags, home decor
- **Retail:** Own boutiques (NYC, San Francisco) plus wholesale via [[Saks Fifth Avenue]], Neiman Marcus
- **Private company** — not publicly traded

## History

- Founded 1990s in Brooklyn
- CFDA Accessory Designer of the Year (2010)
- Brand of the Year, Accessories Council (2014)
- Sold to [[Brooks Brothers]] (pre-2015)
- Bittar stepped away from fashion (2015)
- Reacquired brand after Brooks Brothers bankruptcy (2020-2021)
- Relaunched with new boutiques and expanded into handbags/home decor (2021)
