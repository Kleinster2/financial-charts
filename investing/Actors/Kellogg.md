**Kellogg** is a cereal and snack company that split into two entities in October 2023: Kellanova (global snacking, ticker K) and WK Kellogg (North American cereals). [[Mars]] acquired Kellanova for $36B in 2024. Previously acquired [[RXBAR]] for $600M in 2017.
