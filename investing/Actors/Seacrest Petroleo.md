# Seacrest Petroleo

Bermuda-domiciled upstream oil and gas operator focused on Brazilian onshore production. Holds assets in the Potiguar Basin (northeastern Brazil) through the divestiture programmes that [[Petrobras]] ran in the late 2010s and early 2020s. [[Felipe Germini]] (ENERGY Pipeline Substack) was appointed Senior Vice President of Well Construction (Drilling) at Seacrest in March 2024 — the operating role that grounds the Germini cargo-flow analysis in actual drilling-side operational experience.

## Why it matters in the vault

Seacrest is the operating-side anchor for [[Felipe Germini]]'s ENERGY Pipeline analytical voice. Germini's commentary on Brazilian refining slate dynamics, cargo flows, and OPEC structural decay is grounded partly in his SVP role at Seacrest — he sees the upstream drilling and operational side of Brazilian energy in addition to the trading and analytical sides through Germini Energy and A|F Consulting Partners. The triangulation gives his physical-market analysis a different texture from pure analyst commentary.
