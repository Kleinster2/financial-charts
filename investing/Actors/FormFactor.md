# FormFactor

US-based semiconductor probe card manufacturer. Dominates the memory probe card market — primary supplier to [[SK Hynix]]. Also serves logic customers. Each chip design requires a unique probe card (non-transferrable between designs), creating recurring demand with each new product generation.
