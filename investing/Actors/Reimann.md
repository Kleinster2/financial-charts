**Reimann** -- German billionaire family controlling [[JAB Holding]] (~95% via Agnaten SE). Four remaining heirs of Albert Reimann Jr. (d. 1984): Matthias Reimann-Andersen, Renate Reimann-Haas, Stefan Reimann-Andersen, Wolfgang Reimann. Family wealth ~$12B (Forbes 2025). Barred from managing the business by founding charter -- rely on professional managers.
