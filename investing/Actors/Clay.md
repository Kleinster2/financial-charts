#actor #ai #saas #gtm #private #usa

Clay is a go-to-market (GTM) software company whose platform automates sales prospecting, data enrichment, and AI-driven outbound by orchestrating dozens of data sources and AI agents. It is a named customer of [[Parallel Web Systems]], using web-research APIs to power the data-enrichment and research steps inside its workflows. Its own customer base — over 10,000 companies including [[OpenAI]], [[Anthropic]], Canva, [[Intercom]], and Rippling — makes it a useful read on AI-GTM adoption.
