**Zoox** — Amazon's robotaxi. First purpose-built AV in public service. SF/Vegas live. Paid service 2026.

---

## Why Zoox matters

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Parent | [[Amazon]] |
| Acquired | 2020 for ~$1.3B |
| Fleet | **50 robotaxis** (SF + Vegas) |
| Status | **Public service** (free, paid 2026) |
| Milestone | **1M+ autonomous miles** |

**First in US:** Purpose-built autonomous vehicle in public service.

## Public service launches

| City | Date | Model |
|------|------|-------|
| **Las Vegas** | Sep 10, 2025 | Fixed destinations (5 stops) |
| **San Francisco** | Nov 18, 2025 | Point-to-point ([[Uber]]-like) |

**Las Vegas stops:** Resorts World, AREA15, Topgolf, NY-NY, Luxor.

**SF neighborhoods:** SoMa, Mission District.

Currently **free rides** — commercial exemption pending.

## Purpose-built vehicle

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| Passengers | 4 |
| Max speed | 75 mph |
| Battery | **130 kWh** (larger than competitors) |
| Direction | Bidirectional |
| Design | Symmetrical, no steering wheel |

**Advantage:** Bigger battery = fewer daily charges = more uptime vs retrofitted competitors.
