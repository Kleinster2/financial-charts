**Red Sea Global** -- [[Saudi PIF]]-owned luxury tourism developer behind The Red Sea and AMAALA destinations on [[Saudi Arabia]]'s western coast. It is one of the tourism pillars of [[Saudi Vision 2030]] and sits inside PIF's 2026-2030 tourism, travel, and entertainment ecosystem.

## Strategy reset relevance

The Apr 2026 [[Saudi PIF]] strategy reset protects Red Sea Global because tourism is one of the six domestic ecosystems that can convert sovereign capex into repeat visitor spend, hotel operations, aviation demand, and non-oil GDP. The project remains more defensible than prestige-only foreign cultural deals because it is domestic infrastructure with operating revenue, though resort phasing remains exposed to the same PIF capital-efficiency discipline affecting other giga-projects.
