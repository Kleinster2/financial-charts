Kevin Mayer — former [[Disney]] senior executive; chaired the direct-to-consumer and international business and oversaw the Disney+ launch. Departed in 2020 as a potential-successor casualty of [[Bob Iger]]'s extended tenure (FT, Jun 2026). Briefly served as TikTok CEO. Later co-founded Candle Media with [[Tom Staggs]].
