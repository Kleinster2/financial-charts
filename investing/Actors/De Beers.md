# De Beers

World's largest diamond company by value — mining, trading, and retail. Owned by [[Anglo American]] (85%) and [[Botswana]] government (15%). Operates mines in [[Botswana]], [[South Africa]], Namibia, and Canada. Also runs De Beers Jewellers retail chain and Lightbox (lab-grown diamonds).
