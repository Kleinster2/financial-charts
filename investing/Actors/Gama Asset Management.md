# Gama Asset Management

**Gama Asset Management** is a global macro fund with [[Rajeev de Mello]] as portfolio manager. The firm runs macro strategies that trade across fixed income, currencies, and commodities based on top-down macroeconomic and geopolitical analysis.

Gama gained visibility in the vault context for its hedging strategies during the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|2026 Iran conflict]], where de Mello was cited discussing portfolio positioning around oil supply disruption, [[Strait of Hormuz]] risk, and broader Middle East escalation scenarios. The fund represents the type of macro-specialist shop that actively trades geopolitical event risk rather than treating it as a background factor.

De Mello previously held senior roles at major asset management firms before establishing Gama. The fund is based in Geneva, Switzerland, consistent with the concentration of macro and commodity-focused funds in the Swiss financial center.
