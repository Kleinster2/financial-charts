# The Wire China

US-based digital publication focused on the China-US business relationship and Chinese business / economic / regulatory developments. Founded by David Barboza (former [[New York Times]] Shanghai bureau chief, Pulitzer Prize winner for the 2012 investigation into Wen Jiabao family wealth). Publishes long-form investigative journalism + weekly newsletter coverage of Chinese companies, US-China commercial dynamics, and the political economy of the bilateral relationship. [[Noah Barkin]] (Watching China in Europe) is among the analysts who contribute commentary to The Wire China alongside his Rhodium Group + GMF + DGAP affiliations.

## Why it matters in the vault

The Wire China is one of the small set of US-based publications producing substantial original reporting on Chinese business + regulatory dynamics from a journalism (rather than think-tank) base. Barboza's reporting credibility — including the Pulitzer-winning Wen Jiabao family-wealth investigation — anchors the publication's standing. For vault threads on Chinese corporate dynamics, the US-China commercial relationship, or specific company-level investigations, The Wire China articles are citable as primary-source reporting rather than aggregator commentary.
