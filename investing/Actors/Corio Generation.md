#actor #energy #wind #private

[[Corio Generation]] — offshore wind developer backed by [[Macquarie Group]]'s Green Investment Group. Global portfolio across [[Europe]], [[Asia]], and the Americas. Former JV partner in the Attentive Energy project (New York offshore wind) with [[TotalEnergies]] and [[Rise Light and Power]] — dissolved after Total exited US offshore wind entirely in March 2026.
