## Why IFC matters

The bridge between development capital and private infrastructure:

- **Scale**: $71.7B committed in FY25 — record year
- **Mandate**: Private sector development in emerging markets
- **Infrastructure**: $62B+ invested in energy/infra over last decade
- **Leverage**: Every $1M in public infra generates 10-17 jobs in emerging markets (vs 3-7 in advanced)
