# Sun Country

Sun Country Airlines Holdings (NASDAQ: SNCY) is a Minneapolis, Minnesota-based small-cap US ultra-low-cost carrier with a hybrid passenger / cargo / charter operating model. Sun Country differs from peers ([[Frontier Airlines]] / [[Allegiant Travel]]) in operating a meaningful Boeing 737-cargo franchise (largely Amazon Air feedstock) alongside its passenger ULCC business — diversifying revenue away from pure passenger-fuel-cost exposure.

## Post-Spirit shutdown context (May 2026)

Sun Country sits as the smallest US ULCC after the May 2 2026 [[Spirit Airlines]] shutdown ([[2026 Spirit Airlines shutdown]]) and is the most exposed to the [[2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis|Iran-war]] jet-fuel shock among remaining US ULCC names by virtue of scale (less hedge-coverage capacity than [[Frontier Airlines]] / [[Allegiant Travel]]). The cargo-and-charter revenue mix is partially offsetting — those revenues pass through fuel surcharges more directly than pure passenger fares — but the passenger-side exposure remains.
