# Emerson Electric

US industrial-automation company (St. Louis), reshaped since 2023 into a pure-play automation business — it sold a majority of its Climate Technologies arm to [[Blackstone]] (Copeland) and exited its commercial/residential and InSinkErator units, then built out process and discrete automation through AspenTech (industrial software, majority-acquired) and National Instruments (test & measurement). The flagship Emerson Automation Solutions serves process industries (oil & gas, chemicals, power, life sciences). A member of the [[Factory automation]] cohort with [[Rockwell Automation]], [[Fanuc]], and [[Siemens]].
