# Neiman Marcus

**Neiman Marcus** — Luxury department store chain. Founded 1907. Acquired by [[Saks Global]] in 2024, contributing to [[Saks bankruptcy]] in 2026. Survived the June 2026 emergence as a brand under renamed parent [[Exemplar Luxury Group]] — name and logo retained; store footprint reduced from 36 to 33.

Neiman is now a brand-level survival case rather than an independent retail equity story: the store banner survived two restructurings, but control shifted from LBO sponsors to Saks/HBC and then to creditor-owned Exemplar Luxury Group.

## The debt legacy

2013 LBO by [[Ares Management]] and [[Canada]] Pension Plan loaded company with ~$5B debt.

2020 bankruptcy reduced this to ~$2.8B but didn't eliminate it.

2024 merger passed this legacy burden to Saks Global, contributing to combined $5B debt load that proved fatal.
