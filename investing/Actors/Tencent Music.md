## Investment case

**Bull:**
- China streaming underpenetrated vs. West
- SVIP driving ARPU (still <$2/month)
- Social exit = margin expansion
- Ximalaya = audio super-app
- Gross margin expanding (43.5%)

**Bear:**
- MAU declining (551M, -4% YoY)
- China regulatory risk
- NetEase Cloud Music competition
- Limited global expansion
- Parent Tencent overhang
