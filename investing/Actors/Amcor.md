# Amcor

Global flexible- and rigid-packaging maker (films, pouches, and rigid plastic containers for food, beverage, healthcare, and home/personal care). Dual-listed (NYSE: AMCR / ASX), with origins in Australia; it acquired Berry Global in 2025 to become the dominant Western flexible-packaging group. Its substrate (plastics/films) and end-markets (consumer staples + healthcare) are different from the paper/containerboard names ([[International Paper]], [[Packaging Corp]]) and the metal-can name ([[Ball]]) — defensive, volume-driven, resin-cost-exposed. A member of the [[Packaging]] cohort.
