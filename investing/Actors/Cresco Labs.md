# Cresco Labs

A US cannabis multistate operator known for the Sunnyside dispensary chain and a wholesale-branded products strategy (Cresco, High Supply) that makes it one of the largest branded cannabis wholesalers in the country. Chicago-based like [[Green Thumb Industries]]; its planned acquisition of Columbia Care (the largest-ever US cannabis deal) was terminated in 2023, leaving it focused on core states. The highest-volatility name in the [[Cannabis]] cohort's US MSO core (140% annualized) and the tightest-correlated to [[Green Thumb Industries|GTBIF]] (0.89). Federally illegal under [[Cannabis rescheduling|Schedule I]], 280E-taxed, OTC/CSE-listed; collapses into [[MSOS]].
