#actor #private #media

[[GirlsDoPorn]] — defunct American pornographic production company whose operators were convicted of sex trafficking for coercing women into appearing in videos under false pretenses. Content was hosted on [[Pornhub]] ([[Aylo]]). December 2023: [[Aylo]] agreed to $1.8M fine plus deferred prosecution agreement and three years of independent content moderation assessment over knowingly profiting from GirlsDoPorn material.
