#actor #asset-manager #crypto #usa

**Grayscale** — U.S. digital asset manager, best known for operating the Grayscale Bitcoin Trust (GBTC) — the vehicle whose SEC approval for spot [[Bitcoin ETF]] conversion in January 2024 opened the spot crypto ETF category. Extends the same trust-to-ETP playbook across other tokens. Subsidiary of Digital Currency Group.

## 2026 relevance

- Dec 30, 2025: filed S-1 to convert Grayscale Bittensor Trust into a spot ETP under ticker GTAO on NYSE Arca.
- March 14, 2026: filed formal spot TAO ETF application.
- If approved, GTAO would be the first U.S.-listed decentralized-AI-native vehicle — a new category distinct from [[Bitcoin]] / Ethereum ETPs.
