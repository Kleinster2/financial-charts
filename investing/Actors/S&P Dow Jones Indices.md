# S&P Dow Jones Indices

S&P Dow Jones Indices is the index division of [[S&P Global]], maintaining the [[S&P 500]], Dow Jones Industrial Average, and hundreds of other benchmarks. Highest-margin segment (~68% operating margin) of [[S&P Global]]'s business.
