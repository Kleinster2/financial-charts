# Fractal Analytics

Indian enterprise AI and analytics company providing decision intelligence solutions to Fortune 500 clients. Shortlisted for the [[India AI Impact Summit 2026|IndiaAI Mission]]'s indigenous foundation model development program.

---

## Synopsis

Fractal Analytics occupies the enterprise AI services layer — building custom AI/ML solutions for large corporations in CPG, insurance, healthcare, and financial services. Founded in 2000 in Mumbai, the company has grown to 4,000+ employees (dubbed "Fractalites") with offices across [[India]], the [[United States]], and [[United Kingdom]]. Clients include blue-chip names across industries, with engagements typically spanning data engineering, predictive analytics, and increasingly generative AI integration. The company raised $360M from [[TPG]] in 2022 at a reported $1B+ valuation, placing it in the Indian AI unicorn category.

The IndiaAI Mission shortlisting for indigenous foundation model development positions Fractal at the intersection of two themes: India's push for AI sovereignty and the growing enterprise demand for domain-specific LLMs. Most Indian AI companies have been services firms applying Western-built models; building an Indian foundation model would be a step-change in capability and strategic value. The question is whether Fractal can make the transition from analytics services (project-based, linear scaling) to platform/model company (product-based, exponential scaling). [[TPG]]'s backing provides the capital runway, and the IndiaAI Mission provides government support and compute access, but execution in frontier AI research is a different discipline than enterprise consulting.
