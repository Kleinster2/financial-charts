Latka — SaaS founder interview platform and revenue database. Tracks ARR, headcount, and growth metrics for thousands of private SaaS companies via founder-reported data and follow-up interviews. Used in the vault as third-party read on private-company revenue trajectories where companies do not file or publish numbers.

---

## Why Latka matters

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Founder | Nathan Latka |
| Coverage | Private SaaS companies — ARR, growth rate, headcount |
| Methodology | Founder-reported data, verified via podcast / video interviews |
| Vault use cases | [[Fathom AI]] ARR trajectory ($100K → $30M, 2022–2025); headcount data |
| Status | Private |

Latka data is founder-reported and therefore self-selected and self-aggregated; the platform's value is breadth (thousands of companies, multi-year revenue histories) rather than audit-grade verification. Useful for trajectory and inflection signals, less so for point-in-time precision.
