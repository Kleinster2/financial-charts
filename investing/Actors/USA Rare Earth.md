**USA Rare Earth** (USARE) — American company building a vertically integrated rare earth supply chain, from mining to magnet manufacturing. Focused on reshoring critical materials for defense and clean energy.

## Business model

USA Rare Earth is building a fully domestic supply chain:
1. **Mining** — rare earth ore extraction
2. **Processing** — refining and separation
3. **Magnets** — sintered neodymium magnet manufacturing

Key markets:
- Defense & aerospace (propulsion, guidance, sensors)
- Electric vehicles (motors, batteries)
- Robotics and automation
- Industrial applications

---

## Strategic significance

The US currently has:
- **16%** of global rare earth mining production
- **0%** of global rare earth refining (historically)
- **0%** of global rare earth magnet manufacturing (historically)

USARE aims to change this by establishing domestic capability at each stage.
