## VMware

Co-founded [[VMware]] with husband Mendel Rosenblum and three others (1998). As CEO, created mainstream x86 virtualization market.

**Ousted (2008)**: Board fired Greene after disappointing financial performance. Replaced by Paul Maritz.

---

## Google Cloud

Joined [[Google]] 2015 to lead Google Cloud. Built enterprise sales org. Left 2019, replaced by Thomas Kurian.
