# Lear

Lear (NYSE: LEA) is a Tier-1 automotive supplier with two segments — Seating (the global #2) and E-Systems (electrical distribution, connectors, electronics). Its revenue tracks global vehicle production volume and content-per-vehicle. A member of the [[Tier-1 auto suppliers]] cohort, and its correlation hub — Lear is the most central name in the cluster.
