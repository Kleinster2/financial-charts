Marc Majzner — founder and portfolio manager of [[Clearline Capital]], a New York catalyst-driven long/short equity hedge fund focused on mid-cap stocks (~$1.19B AUM). Before launching Clearline he was a senior portfolio manager at [[Henry Swieca]]'s [[Talpion Fund Management]], which seeded Clearline with $70M at inception in 2012. The fund returned 26%+ net in its first year.

## Background

Studied computer science at Brown University, then worked at [[North Run Capital]] before joining [[Talpion Fund Management]], where he spent roughly two years as a senior portfolio manager. In 2012, [[Henry Swieca]] seeded his new fund, [[Clearline Capital]], with $50M plus $20M from the Swieca family — adopting the family-office seeding model Swieca learned from [[Stanley Druckenmiller]]. Clearline operates from Talpion's offices on Talpion infrastructure, with Swieca taking a profit share.
