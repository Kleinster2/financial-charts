Built on M3, the open-source metrics platform created at [[Uber]] to handle billions of time-series data points. Raised ~$343M in venture funding (General Atlantic, Greylock, Lux Capital, a16z) before acquisition. Last private valuation ~$1.6B (2022).

Extends Palo Alto's security operations platform with cloud-native monitoring and observability --- part of the strategy to own the full SecOps stack alongside Cortex XDR and XSIAM.
