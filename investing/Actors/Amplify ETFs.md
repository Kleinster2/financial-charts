# Amplify ETFs

Amplify ETFs (Amplify Investments LLC) is a US ETF sponsor and issuer based in Illinois, managing a suite of thematic and commodity-focused exchange-traded products. On December 28, 2023, Amplify assumed the sponsor role for the ETF Managers Group Commodity Trust I from ETF Managers Capital LLC, taking over two shipping freight futures ETFs sub-advised by [[Breakwave Advisors]]: [[BDRY]] (Breakwave Dry Bulk Shipping ETF) and [[BWET]] (Breakwave Tanker Shipping ETF).

Amplify's most prominent vehicle outside shipping is the Amplify Transformational Data Sharing ETF (BLOK), one of the largest US blockchain-themed ETFs. The firm's role in the Breakwave funds is administrative and distributional — Breakwave Advisors retains the sub-advisor and trading manager role.
