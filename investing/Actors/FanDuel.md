FanDuel -- #1 US online sportsbook by GGR (~40-43% market share). Subsidiary of [[Flutter Entertainment]] (NYSE: FLUT). Originally a daily fantasy sports platform (founded 2009), pivoted to sports betting post-[[PASPA]] repeal. Live in 23 states.
