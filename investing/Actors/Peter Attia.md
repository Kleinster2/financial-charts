Peter Attia is a physician specializing in longevity medicine and host of The Drive, one of the most popular health podcasts (~100M+ lifetime downloads). Trained at Stanford Medical School and Johns Hopkins Hospital, then spent time at the NIH and McKinsey before pivoting to private practice focused on applied longevity science. His 2023 book "Outlive: The Science and Art of Longevity" became a NYT bestseller. Was chief science officer and seed investor of [[David]] protein bars until departing in February 2026 after his name appeared in Epstein files. The Epstein fallout also affected his podcast listenership and brand partnerships.
