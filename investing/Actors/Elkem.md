Elkem ASA — Norwegian silicon-based advanced materials company. Founded 1904. Listed on Oslo Stock Exchange (ELK). Majority owned by Bluestar Elkem International (52.9%, a [[ChemChina]]/[[Sinochem Holdings]] subsidiary), though a 2025 deal to sell the Silicones division in exchange for Bluestar's stake would make Elkem fully public.
