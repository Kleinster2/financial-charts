## Key positions

Chair Steffen Meister warned (Mar 12, 2026) that [[Private Credit Software Concentration|private credit default rates]] could double from the ~2.6% decade average to above 5% in coming years, driven by AI-disruption bifurcation and the asymmetric payoff structure of credit (capped upside, full downside). Argued that the leverage-on-diversified-portfolio strategy that worked at low default rates will break down.
