# Melrose Industries

Melrose Industries (LSE: MRO) is a UK-listed industrial conglomerate that pursues a buy-improve-sell model, historically acquiring underperforming engineering businesses and restructuring them for disposal or spin-off. The firm is best known for the contested £10.4B hostile takeover of [[GKN]] in 2018 — the largest UK hostile M&A battle since Kraft/Cadbury (2009) — which it won despite GKN's defence advised by [[Gleacher Shacklock]], JPMorgan Cazenove, and UBS.

In 2023 Melrose spun off its automotive businesses as Dowlais Group (LSE: DWL), concentrating Melrose itself on aerospace engine components (primarily GKN Aerospace). The post-spin Melrose is effectively a pure-play aerospace supplier with exposure to Rolls-Royce, Pratt & Whitney, and other engine OEMs.
