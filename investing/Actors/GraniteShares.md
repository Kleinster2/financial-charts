# GraniteShares

**GraniteShares** is an ETF issuer known for [[Commodities|commodity]], leveraged single-stock, income, and alternative strategy ETFs. In this vault it matters mainly as the issuer of [[COMB]], the [[Bloomberg Commodity Index]]-linked broad commodity ETF used as the local charting proxy for [[Bloomberg Commodity Index|BCOM]].
