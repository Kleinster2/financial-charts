# Tony Volpon

Former Deputy Governor for International Affairs at [[Banco Central do Brasil|Brazil's Central Bank]] (2015-2016), where he sat on COPOM. 30+ year career in financial markets — held managing director positions at [[UBS]] and [[Nomura]]. Co-founded [[CF Inovação]] in 2023 with José Carneiro to build tokenized financial products. Now launching [[BRD]], a yield-bearing [[Brazilian real]]-pegged stablecoin.
