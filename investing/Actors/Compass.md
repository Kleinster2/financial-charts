## Business model

Compass recruits top-producing agents with proprietary technology platform ($1.6B invested), then takes commission split. Revenue from transaction commissions.

| Platform feature | Value prop |
|-----------------|------------|
| Compass Platform | Cloud CRM, marketing, client services |
| Three-phase marketing | Private Exclusive → Coming Soon → Open Market |
| Compass One | Client dashboard (Feb 2025) |
| Compass AI 2.0 | Voice-activated assistant (June 2025) |

**Results:** Pre-marketed homes close 20% faster with 2.9% price premium.

## Investment implications

**Bull case:**
- Post-merger scale creates dominant brokerage
- Technology platform differentiates from legacy brokers
- Housing recovery = transaction growth
- Path to profitability via synergies

**Bear case:**
- Commission compression (NAR settlement impact)
- Integration risk with Anywhere
- Still losing money
- SoftBank overhang as they sell
