#actor #executive #person

**Frank Slootman** — CEO of [[Snowflake]] 2019-2024 and chairman through the era: the operator who took it public in September 2020 in the then-largest software IPO and rode it to the $401.89 peak. Previously CEO of ServiceNow (2011-2017, took it public 2012) and Data Domain (2003-2009, sold to EMC) — the rare three-time IPO/exit operator. Handed Snowflake to [[Sridhar Ramaswamy]] in February 2024. Net worth ~$3.1B (Bloomberg Billionaires, Nov 2025).
