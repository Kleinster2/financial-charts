# BlueHalo

US defense technology company specializing in space superiority, counter-UAS, cyber, and directed energy systems. Acquired by [[AeroVironment]] in May 2025 for ~$4.1B, creating a combined defense platform spanning small UAS, loitering munitions, and space ground infrastructure.

Core product is the BADGER advanced ground station, which uses electronically steerable phased-array antennas for satellite tracking and communication. BlueHalo holds the SCAR (Satellite Communications Augmentation Resource) contract with the [[US Space Force]], valued at ~$1.4B, which came under recompetition as of March 2026 -- a catalyst for [[AeroVironment]]'s stock volatility during the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact]].
