# GoodRx

GoodRx (Nasdaq: GDRX) is a prescription-discount marketplace — it aggregates pharmacy-benefit-manager pricing into consumer coupons and earns fees on filled scripts, plus a pharma-manufacturer-solutions advertising business. Adjacent to, not part of, telehealth: it monetizes drug-price dispersion rather than virtual care, which is why it is the loosest member of any "digital health" cohort.
