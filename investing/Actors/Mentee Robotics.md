**Mentee Robotics** — Israeli humanoid robotics startup. Acquired by [[Mobileye]] for $900M (Jan 2026). Co-founded by [[Amnon Shashua]] (Mobileye co-founder).

---

## Why Mentee matters

Mobileye's $900M bet on "Physical AI" — applying autonomous driving perception to humanoid robots. Warehouses by 2028, potentially home by 2030.

## Acquisition details

Announced Jan 6, 2026 at [[CES 2026]]. Approved by Mobileye board and [[Intel]] (88% shareholder). Shashua recused from board vote due to dual role.

Expected to close Q1 2026. Mobileye expects low-single-digit OpEx increase in 2026.
