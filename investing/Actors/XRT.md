# XRT

XRT (NYSE Arca: XRT) is the [[State Street]] SPDR S&P Retail ETF — an equal-weighted basket of US retail industry stocks (apparel, specialty, internet, department stores, auto and food retail). The equal weighting gives it a small/mid-cap specialty-retail tilt, distinct from the cap-weighted [[XLY]] (which is dominated by [[Amazon]] and [[Tesla]]).
