# Peter Atwater

Economist and author, adjunct professor at William & Mary. Credited with popularizing the concept of the **[[K-shaped economy]]** during the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] recovery (~2020). Focuses on confidence-driven economics and the intersection of mood, decision-making, and economic outcomes.

Author of *The Confidence Map* (2023).
