## Why Sycamore matters

As enterprises deploy AI agents at scale, the governance layer — who authorized what, what did the agent do, was it compliant — becomes critical infrastructure. Sycamore is betting that the "operating system" for enterprise AI agents is a distinct, large market, separate from the models themselves or the cloud platforms hosting them. The [[Agentic AI]] thesis requires exactly this kind of trust infrastructure.

## The thesis

Enterprise AI agents create a new governance problem:
- **Autonomy at scale:** Agents making decisions, executing tasks, spending money without human approval at every step
- **Compliance:** Regulated industries (finance, healthcare, legal) need audit trails for autonomous actions
- **Security:** Agents with access to enterprise systems are a new attack surface
- **Trust:** Enterprises won't deploy agents they can't monitor and control

Sycamore's bet: this governance layer is a platform-level opportunity, not a feature of existing cloud providers.
