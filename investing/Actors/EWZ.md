## What It Tracks

The MSCI Brazil 25/50 Index — large- and mid-cap Brazilian equities, cap-weighted and concentrated in financials ([[Itaú Unibanco]], [[Nubank]]), commodities ([[Petrobras]], [[Vale]]), and consumer names. Because it is quoted in USD, EWZ bundles Brazilian equity beta with the BRL/USD rate — so a normalized EWZ against a BRL-quoted local stock shows the currency drag explicitly.

## Use Case

The benchmark for any Brazilian single name in the vault ([[Petrobras]], [[Marfrig]], [[Equatorial]], etc.) and the cleanest one-line read on "Brazil risk-on/off" for a dollar investor. Pairs against [[SPY]] for the Brazil-vs-US relative-strength call.
