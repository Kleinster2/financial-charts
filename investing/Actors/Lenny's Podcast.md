# Lenny's Podcast

Product management, growth, and career podcast hosted by Lenny Rachitsky (ex-[[Airbnb]] growth PM). Combined with Lenny's Newsletter (1.1M subscribers), it forms the dominant media franchise in the product/growth operator space — the product manager's equivalent of what [[All-In Podcast]] is for investors.

## Synopsis

Lenny Rachitsky spent seven years at [[Airbnb]] as a growth product manager before launching his newsletter in 2019 and podcast in June 2022. The newsletter grew to 1.1M subscribers by 2025, making it the largest product-focused publication on Substack by a wide margin. The podcast has surpassed 2M downloads with ~325 episodes, featuring guests like [[Brian Chesky]] (Airbnb CEO), Shishir Mehrotra (Coda), and leaders from [[Stripe]], [[Figma]], [[Notion]], and other high-profile product companies. Combined annual revenue exceeds $1M from sponsorships and paid subscriptions, making Lenny's operation a single-person media business generating venture-like economics with zero dilution.

The franchise matters to the investing vault in two ways. First, as a signal amplifier: when Lenny features a company or framework, it reaches the exact audience (product leaders, growth operators, VPs of product at scale-ups) that determines which tools, platforms, and strategies gain adoption in tech. A Lenny endorsement or deep-dive episode functions as distribution to decision-makers. Second, as a case study in creator-economy economics: Rachitsky demonstrates that a single domain expert with strong distribution can build a multi-million-dollar media business that competes with institutional publications, without venture funding or a team beyond a few contractors. The model scales through audience trust rather than content volume.
