# Celine

French luxury fashion house. Part of [[LVMH]] since 1996 (~$540M acquisition).

## Business

- **Revenue:** ~€2.5B annually (as of 2024)
- **Categories:** Ready-to-wear, leather goods, menswear, perfume, beauty
- **Position:** Core brand in LVMH's Fashion & Leather Goods division

## History

- Founded 1945 by Céline Vipiana in Paris — originally children's shoes
- Acquired by [[LVMH]] (1996)
- Phoebe Philo era (2008–2018) — defined minimalist aesthetic, cult following
- Hedi Slimane era (2018–Oct 2024) — added menswear, perfume/beauty; more than doubled sales
- Michael Rider appointed creative director (2025) — previously design director under Philo

## Creative Direction

| Director | Tenure | Signature |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Michael Rider | 2025– | Return to quiet luxury, tailored minimalism |
| Hedi Slimane | 2018–2024 | Rock-influenced, skinny silhouettes, menswear launch |
| Phoebe Philo | 2008–2018 | Minimalism, oversized tailoring, iconic handbags |
