**SK Group** — South Korea's second-largest conglomerate. Parent holding company for [[SK Telecom]], [[SK Hynix]], and other subsidiaries.

---

## Memory shortage may persist through 2030 (Mar 2026)

Chair Tae-won Chey, speaking on the sidelines of [[NVIDIA]]'s [[GTC]] event in San Jose (Mar 2026): a global memory chip shortage could persist through 2030, as suppliers struggle to keep up with surging AI-driven demand. The industry will need at least 4-5 years to expand wafer capacity.

Rising demand for AI chips has driven up memory prices, with [[SK Hynix]] prioritising higher-value products such as [[HBM]] for customers including [[NVIDIA]].

When asked about expanding overseas manufacturing, Chey said [[SK Hynix]] will focus on new plants in [[South Korea]] for now — finding enough electricity and water resources remains the main hurdle for overseas expansion.

*Source: Nikkei Asia (Yifan Yu at GTC), Mar 19, 2026*
