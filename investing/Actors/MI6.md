**MI6** (Secret Intelligence Service, SIS) is the [[United Kingdom]]'s foreign intelligence agency, responsible for gathering intelligence overseas in support of British national security and foreign policy. Headquartered at Vauxhall Cross in London, MI6 operates under the authority of the Foreign Secretary. The agency is relevant to the investing vault primarily through its alumni pipeline into finance — former chief [[Alex Younger]] (served 2014-2020) joined [[Goldman Sachs]] as a senior adviser, reflecting a broader pattern of intelligence-to-finance career transitions that brings geopolitical expertise to institutional investors. MI6's assessments of threats to energy infrastructure, trade routes, and geopolitical stability feed indirectly into the risk models that drive sovereign and commodity pricing.
