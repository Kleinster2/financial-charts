# iXblue

French navigation and maritime autonomy company, now absorbed into [[Exail Technologies]]. Founded in 2000 by Hervé Arditty (a former CNRS researcher), iXblue specialized in fiber-optic gyroscope (FOG) inertial navigation systems, photonics, and maritime autonomy products. The company built a vertically integrated technology stack from photonic components through inertial measurement units to autonomous surface and underwater platforms. [[Groupe Gorgé]] (led by [[Raphaël Gorgé]]) acquired iXblue for approximately EUR 410M in 2022, merging it with [[ECA Group]] to form [[Exail Technologies]]. The combination was strategically significant: ECA Group brought autonomous underwater vehicles and mine countermeasure robotics, while iXblue contributed the precision navigation systems those vehicles depend on — creating a rare vertically integrated defense autonomy platform from sensors to complete systems.
