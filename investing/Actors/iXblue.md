# iXblue

French navigation and maritime autonomy company. Founded 2000 by Hervé Arditty. Specialized in fiber-optic gyroscope inertial navigation systems, photonics, and maritime autonomy products. Acquired by [[Groupe Gorgé]] for ~€410M in 2022, merged with [[ECA Group]] to form [[Exail Technologies]].
