# Air Products

Global industrial gas company. 1/3 owner of the $8.4B NEOM Green Hydrogen Complex (with [[ACWA Power]] and NEOM) — targeting 1.2 Mt/year of green [[Ammonia]] by 2027. Also in advanced negotiations with [[Yara]] on low-emission ammonia projects (announced December 2025). Core business: hydrogen, oxygen, nitrogen, argon, and specialty gases.
