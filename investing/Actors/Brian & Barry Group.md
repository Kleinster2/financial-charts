Italian retail holding owned by the Zaccardi family (Carlo, Claudio, Roberto). Parent of [[Boggi Milano]]. Originally a childrenswear tailor shop in Monza founded by the Zaccardi grandfather; converted to luxury multibrand retail in 1985. Acquired Boggi in 2003 for €13.2M.
