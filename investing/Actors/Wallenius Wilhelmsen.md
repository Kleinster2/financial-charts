## Lithium-ion fire mitigation

CEO Lasse Kristoffersen (FT, Mar 15, 2026) noted that fire risk on car carriers is associated with used vehicles, not necessarily EVs — all cars have small lithium-ion batteries for radios. Since Wallenius Wilhelmsen began disconnecting the radio battery before loading, "we have had no fire incidents on our vessels."

Practical fix that works for RoRo operations but not for containerized battery cargo.
