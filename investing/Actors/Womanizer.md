Womanizer is a brand within [[Lovehoney Group]], not a standalone company.

## Brand history

- **Invented:** 2014 by [[Michael Lenke]] (Bavaria, Germany)
- **Innovation:** First clitoral suction ("air pulse") vibrator
- **Sold to:** Johannes Plettenberg (2017)
- **Merged:** 2018 with We-Vibe → WOW Tech Group
- **Current owner:** [[Lovehoney Group]] (via 2021 merger)

## Technology

**Air pulse technology** — uses pressure waves instead of vibration for clitoral stimulation. Novel mechanism that created a new product category. Now widely copied by [[Satisfyer]] and others.

## Origin story

[[Michael Lenke]] was inspired by a study showing ~50% of women have difficulty orgasming. Developed prototype over 2 years; wife Brigitte helped test.
