## Nuclear energy investments

Altman is betting big on nuclear for AI power — both fission and fusion:

| Company | Technology | Altman's role | Investment |
|---------|------------|---------------|------------|
| [[Helion Energy]] | Fusion | Chairman (since 2015) | $375M (largest ever) |
| [[Oklo]] | Fission (SMR) | Chairman | Significant stake |

**Why both?**
- Fission (Oklo): Works, but slow to deploy
- Fusion (Helion): Doesn't work yet, but unlimited upside
- Hedging, or conviction that AI needs nuclear-scale power

**Helion-Microsoft deal:** Altman's Helion has PPA with Altman's partner Microsoft for 2028 fusion power.

> "Decreasing the cost of energy is one of the best ways to improve people's quality of lives." — Altman

## April 2026 attacks on Altman's home

Two attacks against Altman's San Francisco residence in three days, in the context of a broader rise in anti-AI sentiment that [[Dylan Patel]] (Apr 23, 2026) cites as a leading edge of a predicted protest cycle against frontier-AI companies.

| Date | Incident | Charges / outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Apr 10, 2026 ~4 a.m. | 20-year-old Daniel Moreno-Gama threw a Molotov cocktail at Altman's residence, igniting an exterior gate. Suspect had traveled from Texas to San Francisco "intending to kill Altman," carried a document with anti-AI views and a list of AI-company executives and investors as targets | Charged with two counts of attempted murder, attempted arson; federal charges include possession of an unregistered firearm and damage by means of explosives |
| Apr 12, 2026 | Altman's home struck by gunfire. SF Police said no immediate evidence the two incidents were directly linked at time of report | Two suspects subsequently arrested per SF Standard |

The motive document and target list elevate this from a one-off security incident to a pattern signal. Patel's read on [[Invest Like the Best]] (Apr 23, 2026): "People hate AI ... AI is less popular than ICE, less popular than politicians ... You look at the comments of news articles where Sam Altman had a Molotov cocktail thrown in his house twice in like two weeks. They're like, people are cheering it on. And this is just the beginning. So, I think we'll see large scale protests against AI in three months." The Patel three-month protest forecast is testable against [[Pew]] and [[Fortune]] sentiment polling and street-level events through July 2026.

[[Anthropic]] / [[OpenAI]] communications response (Patel's prescription): "Sam Altman and [[Dario Amodei|Dario]] have to stop getting on interviews. They're so uncharismatic ... Two, they need to start showing uplifting things that can be done with AI. Three, they need to stop talking about how the capabilities are going to change the whole world constantly because then people are going to get fear of that capability because they have no connection." Specific Patel example: "Sam being on [[Tucker Carlson]] probably made all Republicans hate OpenAI."

*Sources: NPR, CNN, ABC News, CNBC, Fox News, SF Standard, Al Jazeera (Apr 10-13, 2026); [[Dylan Patel]] commentary on [[Invest Like the Best]] (Apr 23, 2026)*
