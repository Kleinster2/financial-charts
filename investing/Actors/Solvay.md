# Solvay

Belgian chemical company founded in 1861 by Ernest Solvay, inventor of the Solvay process for synthetic [[Soda Ash|soda ash]] production. Following the Dec 2023 spin-off of specialty chemicals into Syensqo, Solvay retained its soda ash and essential chemicals business (~$6B annual sales, 9,000 employees). Currently ~4-5 Mt/year soda ash capacity across EU and US. Cutting synthetic capacity at Torrelavega, Spain (600 kt to 420 kt, Q3 2026) while building a carbon-neutral natural plant in Saudi Arabia (500 kt by 2030, 1.5 Mt by 2035).
