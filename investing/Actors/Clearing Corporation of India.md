**Clearing Corporation of India** (CCIL) — Central counterparty for government securities, forex, and money market transactions in India. Operates under [[Reserve Bank of India]] oversight.

---

## Quick stats

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Type | Central counterparty / clearing house |
| Oversight | [[Reserve Bank of India]] |
| HQ | Mumbai, India |
| Key markets | Government securities, forex, money markets |

In April 2026, imposed additional margin requirements on dollar-rupee forwards following heightened volatility from RBI's [[Indian rupee#April 2026 crackdown — "toughest measures in a decade"|FX crackdown]].
