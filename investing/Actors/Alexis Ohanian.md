Alexis Ohanian is the co-founder of [[Reddit]] and founding partner of Seven Seven Six (776), a venture fund launched in 2020. After departing Reddit's board in 2020, Ohanian pivoted fully to venture investing, focusing on early-stage bets across consumer tech, AI, gaming, and creator economy startups. The fund's name references the year 776 BC, when the first Olympic Games were held — a nod to competition and his wife [[Serena Williams]]'s athletic legacy.

776 has deployed across multiple funds, with notable investments in AI-native consumer companies. Ohanian led the $20M Series A for [[Invisible Universe]], an AI animation studio, and has invested in gaming, web3, and health-adjacent startups. He maintains high public visibility through social media and tech conference appearances, positioning 776 as a founder-friendly, operator-led firm.
