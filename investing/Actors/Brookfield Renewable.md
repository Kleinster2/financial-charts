## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Largest pure-play renewables
- Hydro baseload (stable)
- Hyperscaler demand tailwind
- Brookfield sponsor support
- Dividend growth (5-9% target)
- Global diversification

**Bear:**
- Interest rate sensitivity
- Development execution risk
- Hydrology variability
- Complex structure (LP vs Corp)
- Sponsor conflicts possible
