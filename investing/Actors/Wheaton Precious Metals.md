# Wheaton Precious Metals

Wheaton Precious Metals (NYSE/TSX: WPM) is one of the largest precious-metals streaming companies — it pays mine operators upfront for the right to buy a fixed share of future gold and silver production at a low fixed price. Capital-light, with no mining operating or capital costs. A larger silver weight than its peers. A member of the [[Precious metals royalties]] cohort.
