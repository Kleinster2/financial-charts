**CBMM** (Companhia Brasileira de Metalurgia e Mineracao) — World's dominant [[Niobium]] producer with 77% global market share. Operates the Araxa mine in Minas Gerais, [[Brazil]], which contains ~70% of known global niobium reserves. Private, owned 70% by the Moreira Salles family.

## Battery pivot

Expanding beyond steel alloys into niobium titanium oxide (NTO) battery anodes. Partnership with [[Toshiba]] and [[Sojitz]]. Targeting 25-30% of revenue from non-steel products by 2030. Launched world's first NTO anode production facility (Nov 2024).
