**Khalaf Ahmad Al Habtoor** — Emirati billionaire ($2.3B, Forbes), founder and chairman of [[Al Habtoor Group]]. Born 1949 in Dubai's Shindagha district. Built the group from a 100-person construction firm (1970) into a $5B conglomerate. Unusually outspoken: publicly backed Trump in 2015, reversed over Muslim ban, then in March 2026 published open letter (Arabic, on X) criticizing Trump over Iran strikes — extraordinary for a UAE businessman. Built [[Burj Al Arab]] and Dubai Airport infrastructure.
