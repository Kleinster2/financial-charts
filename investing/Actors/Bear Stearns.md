**Bear Stearns** — Former Wall Street investment bank. Collapsed March 2008, sold to [[JPMorgan]] for $10/share in Fed-brokered rescue. Fifth-largest US investment bank at peak.

## Collapse

- Overexposed to subprime mortgages and CDOs
- Two hedge funds failed July 2007
- Liquidity crisis March 2008
- Fed-brokered sale to JPMorgan over a weekend
