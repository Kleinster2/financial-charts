# Spinelli Kilcollin

Los Angeles-based fine jewelry brand. Known for sculptural linked ring designs.

## Business

- **Founded:** 2010 by Yves Spinelli and Dwyer Kilcollin
- **Headquarters:** Downtown Los Angeles
- **Signature:** "Galaxy Rings" — interlinked bands connected by jump rings
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, modernist, gender-neutral
- **Private company**

## Retail Expansion

- Los Angeles flagship (2024)
- New York flagship (2024)
- London Mayfair flagship (Nov 2025) — 15th anniversary milestone

Also sold through Bergdorf Goodman, Net-a-Porter, and independent jewelers.

## Products

Best-sellers include:
- Vega SG ring (grey/white diamonds, silver/yellow gold)
- Sonny ring (rose gold)
- Nexus ring

Positioned as everyday luxury — designed for daily wear, not just special occasions.
