**Oleksandr Kamyshin** — Adviser on strategic industries to [[Volodymyr Zelenskyy]]. Former Minister of Strategic Industries of [[Ukraine]] (2023–2024); before that CEO of Ukrainian Railways during the 2022 [[Russia]]n invasion. One of the most public Ukrainian voices on the country's wartime industrial mobilization, including its drone industry.

## Apr 2026 — response to Papperger

After [[Armin Papperger]] ([[Rheinmetall]] CEO) compared Ukraine's drone industry to playing with "Legos" and said drones were being made by "Ukrainian housewives" with "3D printers in the kitchen," Kamyshin responded that Ukraine's "Lego drones" had already burned more than 11,000 Russian tanks. The exchange framed the competitive tension in [[European defense spending]]: legacy primes with slow delivery cycles vs. a Ukrainian cottage industry producing battlefield-tested attritable munitions at scale.

See [[Armin Papperger]] and [[Drone warfare]] for the full exchange.
