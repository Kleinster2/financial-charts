**Arthur Mensch** — French AI researcher and entrepreneur. Co-founded [[Mistral]] (2023) with two ex-Meta AI researchers (Guillaume Lample and Timothée Lacroix); CEO. Ex-[[Google DeepMind]] research scientist. The most-prominent [[DeepMind diaspora]] founder operating outside the US/UK hubs.

## Significance

Mensch is the standout example of a [[DeepMind diaspora]] founder building in Paris rather than London or San Francisco. [[Mistral]]'s open-weight model strategy and European-sovereignty framing have made it the European AI flagship — the Paris-based parallel to the UK's [[Ineffable Intelligence]] sovereign-AI positioning.
