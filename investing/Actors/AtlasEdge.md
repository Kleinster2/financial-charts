## Strategy

**Edge-first:** Colocation services from single racks to bespoke halls. Focusing on higher-density, scalable facilities in strategic metros.

**2025 shift:** Sold 9 smaller data centers to Templus (Spain). Refining portfolio around larger sites with growth potential. Expanding in Germany, Austria, Portugal.
