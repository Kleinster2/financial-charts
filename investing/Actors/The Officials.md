## Iran conflict appearance (April 15)

Edward Hayden-Briffett, research analyst at The Officials, quoted in the FT ("US crude exports hit record as Iran war disrupts Middle East supplies," Jamie Smyth, April 15): Asian and European buyers are looking to substitute Middle Eastern supply, so US supply is the obvious replacement. Many tankers are booked for loading from the Gulf in coming weeks, so US exports can remain elevated for a while.
