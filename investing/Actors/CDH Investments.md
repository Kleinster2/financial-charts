## History

**CICC spinout.** In 1992, Wu Shangzhi left World Bank to join China International Capital Corporation (CICC) to build their direct investment business. In 2001, China Securities Regulatory Commission prohibited investment banks from PE — the team spun out as CDH in 2002.

**Six founding partners** from CICC's Direct Investment Division, including Wu Shangzhi and Jiao Shuge.
