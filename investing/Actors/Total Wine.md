# Total Wine

**Largest independent alcohol retailer in the US** — ~280 stores across 27+ states. Founded by brothers David and Robert Trone. Known for deep selection (8,000+ wines, 3,000+ beers, 3,000+ spirits per store) and aggressive pricing that undercuts competitors. Privately held, no disclosed revenue. Retail distribution partner for [[Aubi & Ramsa]] (14 stores).
