# OCI

Netherlands-headquartered global nitrogen fertilizer and ammonia producer with ~3.5 Mt/year ammonia capacity. Operations in Netherlands, US (Beaumont, Texas), Egypt, Algeria. Sold methanol unit to [[Methanex]]. Developing a 1.1 Mt blue ammonia project at Beaumont, Texas (sold equity interest). Transitioning toward clean ammonia positioning.
