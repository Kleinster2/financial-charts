# Peter Weinberg

Peter Weinberg is a US investment banker, co-founder of [[Perella Weinberg Partners]] (2006), and former CEO of [[Goldman Sachs]] International (1999–2005). Grandson of Sidney Weinberg, who ran [[Goldman Sachs]] as senior partner from 1930 to 1969 and is credited with building the firm's modern investment banking practice.

Joined [[Goldman Sachs]] in 1983, led the European M&A practice in London, became CEO of Goldman Sachs International in 1999, and retired from Goldman in 2005. Joined [[Joseph Perella]] and [[Terry Meguid]] in 2006 to found [[Perella Weinberg Partners]], bringing the Goldman European client network and the Weinberg-family brand equity.
