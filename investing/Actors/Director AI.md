# Director AI

AI-content studio founded by Ben Chiang. Produces AI-generated cartoons and short dramas using primarily Chinese video models — [[Kling]] as the main tool, with [[Seedance]] 2.0 and [[Hailuo]] as secondary options depending on task and cost. Cited in the FT (Eleanor Olcott, May 17 2026) as a working-creator example of the [[AI Video Generation]] production economy.

## Tool stack and why it matters

Per Ben Chiang in the FT:

> *"Most of the American models that we've tried are not very good at video generation."*

> *"It comes down to quality and how well the model follows the prompt."*

Chiang also said US tools "would constantly run into errors" — denying requests for violating usage terms without further explanation. The combination of (a) higher raw video quality on Chinese models and (b) friction with US-model content filters explains the all-Chinese tool stack.

Director AI is data-point evidence for the [[AI Video Generation]] hub's argument that Chinese video models have moved from "competitive on benchmarks" to "production-default for working creators." The point of the FT framing was not that benchmarks show Chinese leadership (HappyHorse 1.0 had already overtaken Seedance in April 2026) — it was that creators choosing tools for paid commercial work were converging on Chinese models. Director AI is that convergence in one creator's stack.

*Source: [FT](https://www.ft.com/content/9804b1de-653b-40b2-bffb-17c76ebebe34), May 17 2026.*
