# Viktor & Rolf

**Dutch avant-garde fashion house** — founded 1993 by Viktor Horsting and Rolf Snoeren. [[OTB]] owns 70%, founders retain 30%. Couture-only since 2015 (no ready-to-wear). Fragrance (Flowerbomb, Spicebomb via L'Oréal) generates majority of revenue.

## Business model

**Couture:** Artistic statement, brand-building, not profit center. Shows during Paris Haute Couture week.

**Fragrance:** Lion's share of revenue via L'Oréal partnership:
- **Flowerbomb** (2004) — bestseller, women's
- **Spicebomb** (2012) — bestseller, men's
- **Bonbon** (2014)
- **Good Fortune**

**No ready-to-wear:** Deliberately exited RTW in 2015 to focus on art/couture. Fragrance funds the operation.

---

## Founders

**Viktor Horsting & Rolf Snoeren:**
- Met at Arnhem Academy of Art and Design (1989)
- Equal creative partners for 30+ years
- Known for theatrical, conceptual shows
- Each owns 15% of the brand (30% combined)

**Philosophy:** Fashion as art. Blur boundaries between wearable and sculptural.
