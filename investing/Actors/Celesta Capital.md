## Synopsis

Global deep tech VC founded ~2016 in San Francisco by a group of semiconductor and hardware veterans — [[Sriram Viswanathan]] (ex-[[Intel Capital]], ~20 years), [[Lip-Bu Tan]] (now [[Intel]] CEO, ex-[[Cadence]] CEO, founder of Walden International), Nicholas Brathwaite, and Michael Marks (ex-Flextronics CEO). The firm invests at the layers closest to the metal: semiconductors, AI infrastructure, networking, power management, and the US-India tech corridor. Fund IV closed at $375M (per PitchBook). Every GP is a former operator, not a career financier — and that matters when portfolio companies are taping out chips or building custom silicon. Key holdings include [[SambaNova]] (AI inference, Lip-Bu Tan is chairman), [[Tensordyne]] (inference chips, claims 1/8 power per token vs [[Blackwell]]-gen [[NVIDIA]] racks), and [[Upscale AI]] ($300M raised, building scale-up Ethernet to challenge [[NVLink]]). The constraint-flow thesis driving their investments: as bottlenecks shift from GPU → memory → networking → power management → energy, each layer opens a new venture opportunity.

## Investment thesis

Celesta's thesis tracks the **constraint flow** in AI infrastructure — as each layer saturates, the bottleneck migrates downstream and opens new investment targets:

1. GPU / core compute — the first wave (mostly played out at the venture level)
2. [[Memory]] — [[HBM]] shortage, new architectures
3. Networking / interconnects — scale-up Ethernet vs proprietary ([[NVLink]])
4. Power management — data center power delivery and efficiency
5. Energy — raw power generation and grid capacity

Each transition represents a venture window before incumbents consolidate. The thesis tracks a similar framing in [[Supply chain bottlenecks]].

## US-India corridor

Celesta has a strong US-India investment angle — [[Sriram Viswanathan]] previously founded IndusAge Partners, a cross-border US-India VC, which merged with another fund to form Celesta. The firm leverages deep networks in both ecosystems for deal sourcing and portfolio company talent.
