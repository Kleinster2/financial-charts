**DBS** — Largest bank in [[Singapore]] and [[Southeast Asia]] by assets. Market cap ~S$150B (2025). Originally the Development Bank of Singapore (founded 1968), now a full-service banking group with operations across Asia. Majority owned by [[Temasek]] (~29%). Listed on SGX (D05.SI).
