## Why it matters

Speedrun is the high-velocity, low-check-size top of [[a16z]]'s funnel for consumer and AI bets. It writes seed-sized checks across dozens of companies per cohort, with a16z taking a small stake in exchange for capital, mentorship, and access to the firm's portfolio network.

The selection function shows up in cohort composition. SR005 (the late-2025 cohort) included [[Doublespeed]], whose model violates the stated terms of every social-media platform it targets — a useful signal of where Speedrun's screening tolerance sits versus, say, [[Y Combinator]]'s Winter cohorts.
