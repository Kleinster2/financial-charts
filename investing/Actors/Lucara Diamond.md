# Lucara Diamond

Canadian diamond mining company, listed on TSX (LUC.TO). Operates the Karowe mine in [[Botswana]], which produced the 1,109-carat Lesedi La Rona rough — sold to [[Graff]] for $53M in 2017 and cut into the 302-carat Graff Lesedi La Rona, the largest highest-color/clarity diamond ever GIA-certified.
