**Khalid Al-Falih** — Former Minister of Investment of [[Saudi Arabia]], relieved of that post by Royal Decree on Feb 12, 2026, and made Minister of State. Previously served as Minister of Energy, Industry, and Mineral Resources (2016-2019) and Chairman of [[Saudi Aramco]] (2015-2019). A key figure in the early execution phase of [[Saudi Vision 2030]] and [[Saudi PIF]] strategy.
