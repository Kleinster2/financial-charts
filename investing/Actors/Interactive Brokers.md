## Differentiation

**Not competing with Robinhood:**
- Complex platform (learning curve)
- Professional tools
- Global markets access
- Options, futures, forex, bonds
- Hedge fund / RIA services

**Target:** Traders who outgrow Robinhood.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Global market access moat
- Lowest cost provider
- Sophisticated client base (sticky)
- Growing international
- Benefits from volatility

**Bear:**
- Complex platform limits TAM
- Founder risk (Peterffy aging)
- Competition from free brokers
- Regulatory risk (global operations)
