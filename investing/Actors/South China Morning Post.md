# South China Morning Post

The South China Morning Post (SCMP) is [[Hong Kong]]'s leading English-language daily, founded 1903 and owned by [[Alibaba]] since 2016. It is among the most-cited English-language sources on [[China]], Hong Kong, and Asian markets and politics, and is a recurring primary source for vault China coverage.

## Quick stats

- Type: Newspaper / digital news (English-language)
- Founded: 1903
- HQ: [[Hong Kong]]
- Owner: [[Alibaba]] Group (acquired the media business 2016, ~$266m)
- Coverage: [[China]], [[Hong Kong]], [[Asia]] — politics, economy, markets, technology
