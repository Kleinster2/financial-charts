# SKIMS

SKIMS is a shapewear and apparel brand co-founded by [[Kim Kardashian]] and Jens Grede in 2019. Valued at $5B after a $225M [[Goldman Sachs]] Alternatives-led round (Nov 2025), the company is on track to surpass $750M in revenue by end of 2025. SKIMS has expanded from shapewear into loungewear, swimwear, menswear, and activewear, positioning itself as a body-inclusive essentials brand rather than a single-category company. It is widely considered the leading IPO candidate among creator-founded brands.

## Business profile

SKIMS' growth trajectory — from launch to $5B valuation in six years — makes it the highest-profile test case for whether celebrity-founded brands can sustain post-IPO. The key differentiator versus other creator brands is category breadth: while [[Rhode]] concentrated on skincare and [[Kylie Cosmetics]] on makeup, SKIMS spans multiple apparel categories, reducing single-product risk. Grede (who also co-founded Good American with Khloe Kardashian and FRAME denim) provides operational depth that pure celebrity vehicles often lack. The $5B valuation implies ~6.7x forward revenue — aggressive for apparel but defensible if the brand can maintain its 50%+ annual growth rate into a public listing.
