# Stadium Capital

Activist-leaning investment firm. Largest shareholder of [[Sleep Number]] with ~11.5% stake (as of June 2025). In March 2025, entered a cooperation agreement with Sleep Number that resulted in five board members retiring and the board agreeing to declassify. Stadium withdrew its own director nominations in exchange for the governance overhaul.
