Stadium Capital is an activist-leaning investment firm based in Greenwich, Connecticut, specializing in small- and micro-cap companies where it can take concentrated positions and drive governance changes. Founded by Alex Seaver and Bradley Bostic. The firm's highest-profile campaign targeted [[Sleep Number]], where it accumulated an ~11.5% stake (largest shareholder as of June 2025) and forced a March 2025 cooperation agreement: five board members retired, the board agreed to declassify (eliminating staggered terms), and Stadium withdrew its own director nominations in exchange. The playbook — accumulate quietly, then negotiate governance concessions without a proxy fight — mirrors the approach of mid-tier activists like Engaged Capital and Starboard Value at smaller scale.
