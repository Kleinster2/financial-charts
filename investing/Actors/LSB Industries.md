# LSB Industries

US regional ammonia and nitrogen fertilizer producer. ~0.9 Mt/year capacity across El Dorado (Arkansas) and Pryor (Oklahoma) facilities. Operating a green ammonia pilot at Pryor (~30 kt/year). Ticker LXU (NYSE). Products include [[Ammonia]], ammonium nitrate, and UAN solutions.
