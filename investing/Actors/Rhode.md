# Rhode

Rhode is a skincare brand founded by [[Hailey Bieber]] in 2022 that became the fastest creator-to-brand exit in history when [[e.l.f. Beauty]] acquired it for $1B in May 2025 — just three years after launch. The brand generated $212M in net sales in the trailing twelve months ending March 2025, implying a ~4.7x revenue multiple at acquisition. Rhode's cult Gen-Z following was built on a minimalist product line (lip treatments, peptide creams, cleansers) and Bieber's 53M+ Instagram following as a distribution channel.

## Significance

The Rhode acquisition established the high-water mark for the [[Influencer-to-brand playbook]]. Prior creator-brand exits (Kylie Cosmetics to [[Coty]] at $600M in 2020, later written down) had mixed track records. Rhode's cleaner economics — higher revenue base, lower SKU count, genuine product-market fit rather than just celebrity endorsement — may prove whether the model scales post-acquisition or whether the founder's personal brand is inseparable from the business.
