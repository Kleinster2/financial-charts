# Ball Corporation

The world's largest maker of aluminum beverage cans (plus aerosol and specialty containers) — a pure-play metal-packaging company since it sold its aerospace segment to [[BAE Systems]] in 2024 for ~$5.6bn. Its economics turn on beverage-can volumes, aluminum input costs, and the can-vs-plastic substrate-mix shift. Within the [[Packaging]] cohort it is the substrate decoupler: its aluminum/beverage drivers move it apart from the paper/containerboard names ([[International Paper]], [[Packaging Corp]]) and the flexible-plastics name ([[Amcor]]).
