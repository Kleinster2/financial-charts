# Daniela Amodei

**Daniela Amodei** is co-founder and president of [[Anthropic]], the operating counterpart to [[Dario Amodei]]'s research-led CEO role and one of the central people behind the company's attempt to make frontier AI safety a company structure rather than only a research slogan.

## Why she matters

Daniela Amodei matters because Anthropic's safety story is not just a model-training method; it is an operating and governance claim. Stanford's profile describes her as managing Anthropic's senior leadership team, and Anthropic's own board disclosures list her alongside Dario Amodei and other directors. That places her at the junction where research culture, policy posture, enterprise execution, and public-benefit governance have to become a functioning company.

Her durable investing-vault relevance is Anthropic-specific: she helps explain why the company is organized around trust, safety, and governance as product attributes. If Anthropic's valuation rests partly on being the safer enterprise AI lab, Daniela is one of the people responsible for turning that brand into operating discipline. She also appears in Anthropic's 2026 board communications alongside [[Chris Liddell]] and in [[Stanford]]'s entrepreneurship materials as the executive managing the senior leadership team.
