**$8.2B deal:**
- Acquired NYSE Euronext
- Spun off Euronext (2014)
- Kept NYSE
- Transformed from commodities to equities

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- NYSE brand/moat
- Data revenue growth
- Mortgage tech optionality
- Clearing fees = stable
- IPO/listing fees

**Bear:**
- Mortgage market cyclical
- Competition from NASDAQ, CBOE
- Regulatory scrutiny on data fees
- Trading volume volatility
