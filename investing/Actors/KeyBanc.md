**KeyBanc** (KeyBanc Capital Markets) is the equity research and capital markets division of [[KeyCorp]], a US regional bank headquartered in Cleveland, OH. KBCM operates as an institutional brokerage providing equity research, investment banking, and sales/trading services. Its research team covers mid-cap industrials, technology, consumer, and healthcare sectors, with particular depth in industrial distribution and specialty chemicals. As a regional bank-affiliated broker, KeyBanc often provides differentiated coverage of names too small for bulge-bracket attention, making its calls relevant for under-followed mid-caps.
