# LEENO

Korean semiconductor test socket and probe pin manufacturer listed on KOSDAQ. Produces test sockets, pogo pins (spring-loaded contact pins), and test consumables used in wafer probing and final test. LEENO is distinctive in manufacturing both the sockets and the contact pins that go inside them, giving it vertical integration advantages on cost and lead time.

The company benefits from AI-driven demand for higher-performance test interfaces -- as chip I/O counts increase and power delivery requirements grow (especially for [[HBM]] and AI accelerators), test sockets need more pins at tighter pitch, which drives both unit volume and average selling price. LEENO competes primarily with [[WinWay Technology]] (Taiwan), [[Enplas]] (Japan), and [[ISC Co]] (Korea) in the test socket segment.
