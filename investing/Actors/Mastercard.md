**Mastercard** — Global payments network. AI for fraud, data analytics. Duopoly with Visa on card networks.

## Why Mastercard matters

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Market cap | ~$450B |
| Ticker | MA (NYSE) |
| Transaction volume | $9T+ annually |
| Network | 210+ countries |

---

## Business model

**Network economics:**
- Not a lender (unlike banks)
- Takes small fee on every transaction
- Operating margin ~55%
- Capital-light, high [[ROIC]]

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Cash-to-digital secular trend
- Cross-border travel recovery
- B2B payments opportunity
- Data services expansion
- Dividend + buybacks

**Bear:**
- Regulatory pressure (interchange fees)
- [[Fintech]]/RTP disruption
- [[Crypto]] threat (long-term)
- Valuation premium
