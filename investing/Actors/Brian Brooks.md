## Insights

**Regulatory credibility hire** — Meridian brought in Brooks after Freddie Mac scandal specifically for his regulatory relationships and compliance expertise. Former top banking regulator now rebuilding a firm's risk culture.

**"CryptoComptroller"** — At OCC, green-lit banks for crypto custody and created federal charter for digital asset banks. Crypto-friendly stance made him controversial in traditional banking.

**Pattern of short tenures** — Binance.US (3 months), Bitfury (~1 year). Meridian is a test of whether he can execute a multi-year turnaround.
