# California Closets

Leading custom closet and storage solutions brand in North America. Subsidiary of [[FirstService]] (acquired 1998). Hybrid franchise + company-owned model: ~80 franchised territories, ~20 company-owned. FirstService has systematically acquired franchisees since 2014 to bring high-performing markets in-house. Operates Western Manufacturing Center (2015) and Eastern Manufacturing Center (2017) for company-owned operations. Revenue not disclosed separately — reported within FirstService Brands segment.
