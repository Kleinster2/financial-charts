**AMC Entertainment** — World's largest movie theater chain. Became the defining meme stock of 2021 when retail investors on Reddit's r/WallStreetBets drove shares from ~$2 to $72, squeezing short sellers. Majority-owned by [[Dalian Wanda Group]] (China) until dilution. Ticker: AMC.
