Prime Hydration is a beverage brand co-founded by [[Logan Paul]] and KSI in January 2022 that became the highest-profile example of influencer-driven CPG — and its limits. Peak sales hit $1.2B in 2023, fueled by scarcity marketing (limited drops, resale premiums up to $100/bottle) and youth-targeted social media. The collapse was equally dramatic: UK revenue fell 70% YoY to £33M in 2024 with profits down 91% to £312K, while US sales dropped ~40% in H1 2024. The brand is distributed by [[Dr Pepper]] (via Keurig Dr Pepper), holds sponsorships with UFC, FC Barcelona, and Arsenal, and expanded into energy drinks (Prime Energy, 200mg caffeine — criticized for marketing to children). Congo Brands handles manufacturing and operations. The trajectory mirrors classic beverage hype cycles: novelty-driven trial → repeat purchase failure → channel contraction.
