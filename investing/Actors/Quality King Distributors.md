**Quality King Distributors** — Queens-based gray-market distributor of health, beauty, and pharmaceutical products. Founded 1961 by Bernard and Ruth Nussdorf. Family net worth ~$1.6B (Forbes, 2014). Source of [[Meadow Lane]] founder Sammy Nussdorf's family wealth.
