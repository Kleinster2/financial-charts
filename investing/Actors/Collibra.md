# Collibra

Data-governance and catalog pioneer — the enterprise standard for the policy/lineage/stewardship layer above the warehouse. Founded 2008 in Brussels (Felix Van de Maele, Stijn Christiaens), HQ now New York. Backed by CapitalG ([[Alphabet]]'s growth fund). The most governance-heavy of the pure-plays, which is both its moat (compliance, lineage, access policy) and its exposure if an open format commoditizes the catalog interchange.
