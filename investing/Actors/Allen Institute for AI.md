# Allen Institute for AI

Nonprofit AI research institute founded in 2014 by Paul Allen (co-founder of [[Microsoft]]), based in Seattle. Ai2 has become one of the most important voices in open-source AI, producing the OLMo (Open Language Model) family and making all training data, code, and weights publicly available -- a deliberate contrast to the closed approach of [[OpenAI]] and [[Anthropic]].

Led by CEO Ali Farhadi. [[Nathan Lambert]] runs post-training and is the driving force behind the [[ATOM Project]] (American Truly Open Models), an initiative to ensure US leadership in open-weight AI. The institute operates on a ~$80M annual budget funded primarily by the Paul Allen estate.
