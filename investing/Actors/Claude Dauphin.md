# Claude Dauphin

French commodities trader (1951–2015); co-founder and longtime CEO/Chairman of [[Trafigura]]. Founded Trafigura in 1993 with Eric de Turckheim and other former Marc Rich traders after Marc Rich's firm split (the rump became [[Glencore]]). Under Dauphin's leadership Trafigura grew into one of the world's top three independent commodity traders. Died in 2015 at age 64.
