**JD.com** — Chinese e-commerce and logistics company (NASDAQ: JD). Second-largest e-commerce platform in [[China]] after [[Alibaba]]/[[Taobao]]. Known for first-party retail model (vs Alibaba's marketplace) and self-built logistics network. Partnering with [[BYD]] on fast-charging station infrastructure (Mar 2026).
