# Surat Diamond Bourse

The world's largest diamond trading complex, located in Surat, [[India]]. A $350M, nine-tower complex with more floor space than the Pentagon, opened in 2023 to showcase India's dominance in the global diamond trade (9 out of 10 diamonds globally are cut and polished in Surat). As of March 2026, effectively empty — a monument to the [[Diamond market collapse]].
