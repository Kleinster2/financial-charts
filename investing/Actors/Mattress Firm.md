# Mattress Firm

Largest U.S. mattress specialty retailer. ~3,000 stores. Filed Chapter 11 in October 2018, emerged within two months. Acquired by [[Tempur-Sealy International]] in February 2025, creating a vertically integrated mattress manufacturing-to-retail platform.
