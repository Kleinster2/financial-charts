# Mattress Firm

Largest US mattress specialty retailer with approximately 3,000 stores nationwide. Founded in 1986 in Houston, Texas, Mattress Firm grew through aggressive acquisition (including the 2016 merger with Sleepy's) before overexpansion forced a Chapter 11 filing in October 2018 — one of the fastest-ever large retail restructurings, emerging in just two months by December 2018 after closing ~700 unprofitable locations. South African retailer [[Steinhoff International]] had acquired Mattress Firm for $3.8B in 2016 just before Steinhoff's own accounting fraud scandal erupted. [[Tempur-Sealy International]] completed the acquisition in February 2025, creating a vertically integrated mattress manufacturing-to-retail platform that controls both the factory (Tempur-Pedic, Sealy, Stearns & Foster) and the primary distribution channel.
