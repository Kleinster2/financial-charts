# NSG Group

NSG Group (formerly Nippon Sheet Glass) is a Japanese glass manufacturer and the owner of UK-based Pilkington, a pioneer of float glass technology. The company produces architectural, automotive, and technical glass across 30+ countries. The 2006 acquisition of Pilkington — a company three times NSG's size — was a defining bet that gave NSG global scale but burdened it with debt and integration challenges during the 2008 financial crisis.

## Business segments

NSG operates in three segments: Architectural (~40% of revenue), covering flat glass for buildings and solar panels; Automotive (~50%), supplying OEM and replacement glazing globally; and Technical Glass (~10%), producing specialty glass for displays, lenses, and glass fiber. The Pilkington brand remains the commercial identity in most markets outside Japan. NSG is one of four companies (with [[AGC Inc.]], [[Saint-Gobain]], and [[Guardian Industries]]) that dominate global float glass production.
