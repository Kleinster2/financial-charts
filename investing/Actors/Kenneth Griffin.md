**Kenneth Griffin** — Founder, CEO, and Co-Chief Investment Officer of [[Citadel]] (~$65B AUM hedge fund) and [[Citadel Securities]] (largest US market maker). Started trading from his Harvard dorm (1987). Moved Citadel HQ from Chicago to Miami (2022). One of the most successful hedge fund managers in history — 19% net annualized returns since 1990. Forbes estimated net worth ~$43B.
