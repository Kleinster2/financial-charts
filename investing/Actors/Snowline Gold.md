## Why Muddy Waters went long

A short-selling firm buying a small exploration company is the structural opposite of its usual posture. Two read-throughs:

1. [[Muddy Waters]] launched a resource/junior mining fund in 2024 with a partner they had publicly shorted a company alongside in 2017. This is a distinct strategy from the core short book, not a pivot of the firm.
2. [[Muddy Waters|Carson Block]] has a long-term constructive view on metals under a *"QE forever, yield curve control forever"* regime where financial assets reflate after cyclical dislocations. Junior gold is a long-dated call option on that macro tail.

Block on the experiential difference: *"When you're long, the whole world is long back at you. It's the smiles on people's faces as they came up to me... as opposed [to being short when] I get off stage and I'm tense and... how long till the lawsuit hits."*
