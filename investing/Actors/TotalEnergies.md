## Differentiated strategy

Unlike peers, maintained transition commitment:
- 35 GW renewable capacity target (2025)
- Large solar/wind project pipeline
- Battery storage investments
- Kept long-term oil decline targets

But still investing heavily in oil/gas projects.

## Google solar PPA (Feb 2026)

1 GW solar capacity for [[Google]] data centers in [[Texas]] — 28 [[TWh]] over 15 years. Sites: Wichita (805 MWp), Mustang Creek (195 MWp). Construction Q2 2026.

---

## Venezuela exit (Feb 2026)

CEO Pouyanné rejected [[Donald Trump]]'s calls for Big Oil to invest in [[Venezuela]]: "too expensive and too polluting." Fully exited Venezuelan operations in 2022. Heavy crude doesn't fit the strategy.

---

## Namibia expansion (Feb 2026)

Acquired 42.5% operated stake in PEL 104, following December's 40% stake in [[Galp]]'s Mopane discovery. [[Namibia]] is Africa's hottest exploration frontier.
