# Flávio Dino

**Flávio Dino** — Justice of Brazil's Supreme Federal Court ([[Supremo Tribunal Federal|STF]]) since February 22, 2024. Nominated by [[Lula]] in November 2023 to fill the vacancy left by Justice Rosa Weber's retirement; Senate confirmed December 13, 2023 by 47-31-2. Career path: federal judge (12 years) → federal deputy for Maranhão (PCdoB, 2007-2011) → president of Embratur (2011-2014) → Governor of Maranhão (2015-2022, two terms, PCdoB) → Senator (briefly, 2023) → Minister of Justice and Public Security under Lula (January 2023 – February 2024) → STF. Currently STF rapporteur on parliamentary-amendment transparency; opened the sealed preliminary probe into the *Dark Horse* / [[Mário Frias]] emendas pipeline on May 15, 2026 *(per Rio Times)*.

## Why he matters for the vault

Dino is the judicial-channel node for the May 2026 *Dark Horse* scandal cluster. His role as STF rapporteur on parliamentary-amendment transparency gives him the institutional jurisdiction to open the probe into whether three PL deputies — [[Mário Frias]] (R$2M), Marcos Pollon (R$1M, claimed redirected), Bia Kicis — diverted federal budget earmarks through Instituto Conhecer Brasil to film producer Go Up Entertainment *(per Rio Times STF coverage)*. Beyond the *Dark Horse* matter specifically, his career arc — PCdoB politician, Lula's Justice Minister, then Lula's STF appointee — exemplifies the executive-judicial bridge under the current administration that the Bolsonaro field has framed as a target for impeachment in the broader CPI-vs-STF backdrop (see [[2026 Brazil presidential election#STF backdrop (April 2026)|the election note's STF backdrop section]]) *(inference on the political-positioning framing)*.
