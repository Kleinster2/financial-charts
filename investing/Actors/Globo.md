## Why Globo matters

Brazil's media backbone — shapes national conversation:

- **Scale**: Largest commercial TV network in Latin America, 2nd largest globally
- **Content**: World's largest telenovela producer
- **Reach**: 98% population coverage, 80-100M daily viewers
- **Private**: Not publicly traded — Marinho family control since founding

## Marinho family

**Founder**: Irineu Marinho (1925) — died 25 days after O Globo's first edition

**Builder**: Roberto Marinho (1925-2003) — expanded from print → radio (1944) → TV (1965)
