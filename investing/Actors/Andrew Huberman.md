Andrew Huberman is a neuroscientist, tenured professor of neurobiology at [[Stanford]] School of Medicine, and host of the Huberman Lab podcast — one of the most popular science podcasts globally, routinely in the top 10 on Spotify and Apple Podcasts. His content focuses on neuroscience-backed protocols for sleep, focus, exercise, and supplementation, and he has become arguably the most influential figure in the science-meets-wellness media space.

Huberman's influence extends into consumer products and venture capital. He was a seed investor in [[David]] protein bars (August 2024) alongside [[Peter Attia]], lending the brand scientific credibility in a crowded protein bar market. His podcast endorsements carry significant commercial weight — products he discusses often see measurable sales spikes, creating a "Huberman effect" analogous to the Oprah effect in an earlier media era. His reach makes him a key distribution channel for health/wellness and supplement brands looking to reach an educated, high-income male demographic.
