**Phia** — AI shopping agent that recommends products and compares prices across sites for new and used items. Co-founded by **Phoebe Gates** (Bill Gates' daughter) and **Sophia Kianni** (climate activist). Building "the AI alignment layer between brands and consumers."

## Product

AI-powered product recommendation app — users describe what they want, Phia finds and compares options across retailers (new + used/resale). Positioned as making "shopping fun again."

**"AI alignment layer for commerce":** Aligning what consumers actually want with what brands offer — rather than ads/SEO determining what consumers see.
