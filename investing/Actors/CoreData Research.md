**CoreData Research** is an Australian financial services research and consulting firm specializing in proprietary surveys, benchmarking, and competitive intelligence for asset managers and wealth platforms.

## Synopsis

CoreData occupies a niche but influential position in fund distribution intelligence. The firm runs large-scale quantitative surveys of financial advisers, institutional investors, and retail platforms across [[Australia]], [[United Kingdom]], [[United States]], and [[Asia]], producing benchmarking reports that asset managers use to calibrate distribution strategy. Their partnership with [[Aberdeen]] on a US commodities adviser survey (Feb 2026) illustrates the business model: white-label research that gives the commissioning firm both market intelligence and a marketing asset. Founded in Sydney in 2002, CoreData has expanded into the UK and US but remains privately held and small relative to peers like [[Cerulli Associates]] or [[Broadridge]] Financial Solutions' distribution analytics unit.

The relevance to the vault is indirect but real — CoreData surveys surface sentiment shifts in fund flows before they appear in AUM data. When CoreData reports that 40% of US advisers plan to increase commodities allocations, that's a leading indicator for inflows into commodity ETFs and active managers like [[Aberdeen]], [[Invesco]], and [[abrdn]]. The firm itself is not investable, but its output is a useful signal.
