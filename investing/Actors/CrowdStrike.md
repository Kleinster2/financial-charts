## July 2024 outage

**Global IT crisis:**
- Faulty content update crashed 8.5M Windows devices
- Airlines, banks, hospitals affected
- $5.4B+ estimated damages
- Stock dropped ~30%, recovered partially

**Aftermath:**
- CEO George Kurtz testified to Congress
- Enhanced testing procedures
- Customer trust damaged but retention high
