# Terry Meguid

Terry Meguid is a US investment banker, co-founder of [[Perella Weinberg Partners]] (2006), and former head of worldwide investment banking at [[Morgan Stanley]]. Left Morgan Stanley alongside [[Joseph Perella]] in 2005 during the boardroom dispute that forced out CEO [[Philip Purcell]], then joined Perella and [[Peter Weinberg]] to found [[Perella Weinberg Partners]].
