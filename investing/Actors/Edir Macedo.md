**Edir Macedo** — Brazilian bishop, founder of [[Igreja Universal do Reino de Deus]] (Universal Church of the Kingdom of God), owner of TV Record/Record Group, and controller of [[Banco Digimais]]. One of Brazil's most influential evangelical leaders with significant media empire and political arm through [[Republicanos]] party. Controls major segment of evangelical caucus in Congress.
