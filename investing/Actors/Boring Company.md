## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Novel infrastructure approach
- Vegas Loop proof of concept
- Nashville expansion validates model
- Musk ecosystem synergies
- Lower cost than traditional transit

**Bear:**
- Private (no public access)
- Safety/regulatory concerns
- Unproven economics at scale
- Competition from transit systems
- Dependent on Tesla vehicles
