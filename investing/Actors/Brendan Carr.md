# Brendan Carr

Role: Chairman, Federal Communications Commission (FCC)
Appointed FCC Chairman: Jan 2025 (by [[Donald Trump]])
Previously: FCC Commissioner (2017-2025)
Party: Republican

Telecom and tech regulator. Known for aggressive stance on [[TikTok]] (called for its removal from app stores before the congressional ban) and for pushing to rein in Big Tech. Attended the inaugural [[Hill and Valley Forum]] dinner in March 2023.
