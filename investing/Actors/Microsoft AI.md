**Microsoft AI** — [[Microsoft]] division formed Mar 2024 to absorb the [[Inflection AI]] leadership team in a $650M-structured acquihire. Led by [[Mustafa Suleyman]] (CEO of Microsoft AI), reports to [[Satya Nadella]]. Distinct from [[Microsoft]]'s separately-managed [[OpenAI]] partnership.

## Significance

The Microsoft AI division resolved [[Microsoft]]'s post-OpenAI question of in-house consumer-AI capability. With [[Sam Altman]] reporting to no one within Microsoft and the [[OpenAI]] governance crisis of Nov 2023 fresh in mind, [[Satya Nadella]] used the [[Inflection AI]] acquihire to bring a senior AI executive ([[Suleyman]]) directly inside Microsoft's reporting line. The structure also gave Microsoft an internal team capable of building consumer products without dependence on OpenAI's release schedule.

For the [[DeepMind diaspora]], Microsoft AI is the most significant acquihire exit precedent to date — establishing that a Big Tech buyer will absorb a frontier-lab founder team at >$500M.
