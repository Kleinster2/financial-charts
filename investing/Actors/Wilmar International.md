**Wilmar International** (SGX: F34) — Singaporean agribusiness giant and the world's largest palm biodiesel producer. Operations span oil palm cultivation (230,951 hectares planted as of Dec 2024, ~66% Indonesia, ~25% East Malaysia, ~9% Africa), oilseed crushing, edible oils refining, and [[Biodiesel (FAME)|biodiesel]] manufacturing. Biodiesel plants in Malaysia, Indonesia, and Singapore.
