# Bland.ai

Bland.ai (also "Bland AI") is a US-based voice-AI infrastructure company building conversational voice agents for enterprise customer-service, sales, and operational call workflows. The platform abstracts the speech-to-text, LLM-reasoning, and text-to-speech stack into a developer-friendly API that integrates with existing telephony infrastructure ([[Twilio]] APIs, SIP, PBX systems). Notable Y Combinator alum.

## Twilio Q1 2026 customer win

[[Twilio]] (NYSE: TWLO) named Bland.ai as a Q1 2026 customer win in its April 30 earnings release, alongside [[Sierra]] and [[KPN]]. The strategic read for the [[AI SaaS Disruption|"New Industrial Order"]] frame: voice-AI agent companies like Bland.ai are layering on top of [[Twilio]]'s legacy SMS/voice/Verify API base rather than disintermediating it — agent traffic *increases* the underlying API call volume that Twilio bills against. Twilio's Q1 2026 print produced its fastest organic growth quarter since 2022 (+16% organic / +20% reported revenue), with the AI-agent-traffic flywheel cited as the proximate driver.
