**CNPC** — China's largest oil company (state-owned). Major Venezuela creditor and oil buyer. Parent of listed PetroChina.

| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|

| Venezuela exposure | Major creditor, oil-for-loans |

| Metric | Value |
