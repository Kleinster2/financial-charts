# Rotterdam

Netherlands port city and the largest oil-trading and refining hub in Europe. Home to the ARA (Amsterdam-Rotterdam-Antwerp) refined-products complex, which sets Northwest European gasoline and gasoil price discovery. Rotterdam was the founding location of [[Vitol]] (1966) before the firm's HQ shifted to [[Geneva]]. Major terminal and storage operators include Vopak and Koole.
