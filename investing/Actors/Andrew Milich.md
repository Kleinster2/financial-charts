# Andrew Milich

AI engineer and co-founder of [[Nous Research]], poached from [[Cursor]] by [[xAI]] in March 2026 to work on the Grok coding product.

## Synopsis

Andrew Milich co-founded [[Nous Research]], an open-source AI lab known for fine-tuned LLMs (Hermes, Capybara) that consistently outperform their weight class on benchmarks. He also worked at [[Cursor]], the AI-powered code editor that became one of the fastest-growing developer tools in 2024-2025. His move to [[xAI]] in March 2026 reflects Elon Musk's aggressive talent acquisition strategy -- poaching engineers with hands-on experience shipping AI coding products rather than hiring researchers from scratch.

The hire signals that [[xAI]] views coding assistance as a critical competitive frontier. "Grok Code Fast" is xAI's answer to [[GitHub Copilot]], [[Cursor]], and [[Claude Code]], and bringing in someone who built products at both an open-source AI lab and a leading coding IDE suggests xAI is serious about closing the capability gap. For the broader AI talent market, Milich's path (open-source research lab to startup to Musk venture) illustrates how top AI engineering talent circulates through the ecosystem, with compensation and mission pulling people between small labs, well-funded startups, and the mega-labs.
