## Recent developments (2025-2026)

- **Video ads** growing 30% YoY — TikTok-style short-form content
- **Premium subscriptions** hit record $2B+ trailing 12 months
- **AI integration** — LinkedIn AI assistant for job searching, profile optimization
- **1.3B members** as of Q1 FY26

## Strategic value to Microsoft

- **B2B data** — Professional graph for enterprise sales
- **Dynamics 365 integration** — CRM enrichment
- **Recruiting** — Competes with Indeed, ZipRecruiter
- **Recurring revenue** — Subscription-heavy model
