#actor #country

**Belarus** — Eastern European country, world's second-largest potash exporter via state-owned Belaruskali. Under Western sanctions since 2022 following Russia's invasion of Ukraine, which Belarus supported. Sanctioned exports have partially recovered via alternative routing (Russian Baltic ports, non-Western buyers), complicating global potash supply calculus.
