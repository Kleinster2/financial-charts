## ESG Risk Ratings methodology

**Five severity levels** — absolute measure of risk:
1. Negligible (0-10)
2. Low (10-20)
3. Medium (20-30)
4. High (30-40)
5. Severe (40+)

Assesses exposure to material ESG risks AND management of those risks.
