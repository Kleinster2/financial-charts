# Teladoc

Teladoc Health (NYSE: TDOC) is the largest listed B2B / enterprise virtual-care provider — the ex-pandemic telehealth bellwether whose $18.5B [[Livongo]] acquisition (2020) became the sector's defining capital-destruction event. A different business from the DTC cash-pay platforms ([[Hims & Hers]], [[LifeMD]]): Teladoc sells access through employers and health plans, and its revenue has stalled while the DTC names grew.
