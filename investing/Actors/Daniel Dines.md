# Daniel Dines

**Daniel Dines** — founder and CEO of [[UiPath]] (NYSE: PATH). Romanian-born, built UiPath from a Bucharest outsourcing shop into the dominant RPA platform before pivoting toward [[Agentic AI]] orchestration. Returned as CEO in 2024 after brief handoff to Rob Enslin (who lasted ~6 months).
