Subscription ARR reached $1.46B (+34% YoY). Net new subscription ARR: $115M — all-time quarterly record (+22% sequential acceleration from $94M in Q3). Cloud ARR $1.29B (88% of total subscription ARR, +48% YoY).

Security products now >45% of subscription net retention (up from 40% prior quarter, 34% year-ago). Identity Recovery & Resilience customers more than doubled from ~400 to 900+ in one quarter.

Full year FY2026: total revenue $1.32B (+48%), subscription revenue $1.26B (+53%), free cash flow ~$238M (10x prior year). Near non-GAAP breakeven (-$0.01 EPS vs -$1.57 prior year).

CEO Bipul Sinha called Q4 "spectacular" — win rates "crossed 90%" against data protection vendors. Competitive positioning as "system of record of last resort" for data and identity.

### FY2027 guidance

| Metric | Guidance |
|--------|----------|
| Revenue | $1.597B–$1.607B (~21% growth) |
| Subscription ARR | $1.829B–$1.839B (+25-26%) |
| Non-GAAP EPS | $0.07–$0.27 |
| Free cash flow | $265M–$275M |

ARR growth guidance of 25-26% vs 34% in FY2026 represents headline deceleration. Management noted FY2027 guidance does not embed meaningful AI revenue — upside optionality.

Stock fell 7.1% from pre-earnings close ($57.52 on Mar 11) to $53.43 on Mar 13 despite the beat. Trading ~48% below 52-week high of $103.

### Strategic updates

Two-platform architecture: Rubrik Security Cloud (cyber resilience) + Rubrik Agent Cloud (AI agent controls, entered GA with Fortune 500 POCs). Identity security inflection drove the customer doubling. Cloud transition near-complete at 88% of ARR.
