# Michelle Brennan

**Michelle Brennan** is the interim CEO of [[Masimo]] since September 2024, following the departure of founder [[Joe Kiani]]. Previously spent 30+ years at [[Johnson & Johnson]], overseeing medical device businesses globally and consumer pharmaceutical operations. Elected to Masimo's board in June 2023 as a [[Politan Management]] nominee during the first proxy fight.
