# In-N-Out Burger

**In-N-Out Burger** — privately held, family-owned (Snyder family) West Coast premium-burger chain founded 1948 in Baldwin Park, California; known for a minimal menu, fresh never-frozen patties, the "secret menu," and deliberately slow geographic expansion — the highest-AUV, narrowest-footprint member of the US premium-burger cohort with [[Five Guys]] and [[Shake Shack]].
