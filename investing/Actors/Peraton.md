## How Peraton was built

Veritas Capital assembled Peraton through acquisitions:

| Date | Acquisition | Value | What it added |
|------|-------------|-------|---------------|
| 2017 | Harris IT services | — | Foundation, renamed Peraton |
| Dec 2020 | Northrop Grumman IT/Mission Support | **$3.4B** | Scale, classified programs |
| Jan 2021 | Perspecta | **$7.1B** | Enterprise IT, civilian |
| 2025 | ViON (as-a-Service segment) | — | IT infrastructure-as-a-service |

**2021 megamerger:** Revenue jumped from ~$1B to ~$7B, headcount from 3,500 to 24,000.
