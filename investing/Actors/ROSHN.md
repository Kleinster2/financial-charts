#actor #real-estate #saudi #private

**ROSHN** — Saudi national real estate developer wholly owned by [[Saudi PIF]], targeting 400,000 homes by 2030 across [[Saudi Arabia]]. Created as the housing delivery vehicle for [[Saudi Vision 2030]]'s goal of raising Saudi homeownership from ~47% to 70%.
