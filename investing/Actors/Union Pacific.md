## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Duopoly in Western US (with BNSF)
- Pricing power (rail vs truck)
- AI efficiency gains
- Dividend growth
- Nearshoring tailwind

**Bear:**
- Economic sensitivity
- Regulatory pressure
- Service reliability issues
- Labor relations
- Truck competition
