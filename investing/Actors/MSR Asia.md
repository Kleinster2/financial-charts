**MSR Asia (Microsoft Research Asia)** — The "Whampoa Military Academy" of China AI. Founded by Kai-Fu Lee. Alumni lead AI at every major China tech company.

> **Key insight:** One research lab trained a generation of China's AI leaders. MSR Asia alumni are now competitors to Microsoft.

---

## Why MSR Asia matters

The most influential AI research lab in China's history:

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Founded | 1998 |
| Founder | [[Kai-Fu Lee]] (for Microsoft) |
| Location | Beijing |
| Focus | Fundamental AI/ML research |
| Legacy | "Whampoa of China AI" |

---

## The "Whampoa" nickname

Named after the Whampoa Military Academy that trained leaders for both sides of China's civil war. MSR Asia similarly trained leaders who went everywhere:

| Alumni | Role |
|--------|------|
| Zhang Yaqin | Baidu President (2014-2019) |
| Qi Lu | Baidu COO, later Y Combinator China |
| Wang Jian | Alibaba Cloud CTO |
| Tang Xiao'ou | SenseTime founder |
| Yin Qi | Megvii (Face++) founder |
| Harry Shum | Microsoft EVP AI (later) |

**The irony:** Microsoft funded the training of its future competitors.

## Why it worked

| Factor | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Resources | Microsoft budget, no pressure to ship products |
| Talent | Recruited best from Tsinghua, PKU |
| Freedom | Pure research, publish openly |
| Location | Beijing = talent hub |
| Leadership | Kai-Fu Lee built culture |

**The deal:** Microsoft got research prestige. Researchers got training + platform. China got an AI talent factory.

## Alumni network effects

```
MSR Asia (1998-2010s)
    ↓
Alumni leave for:
├── Baidu (search, autonomous driving)
├── Alibaba (cloud, e-commerce AI)
├── Tencent (social, gaming AI)
├── ByteDance (recommendation, content)
├── SenseTime, Megvii (computer vision)
└── Startups (Tigers, etc.)
    ↓
They hire more MSR Asia alumni
    ↓
Network reinforces itself
```

## Current status

MSR Asia still operates but landscape has changed:

| Then | Now |
|------|-----|
| Only major AI lab in China | Dozens of competitors |
| Best talent destination | Competes with Big Tech, startups |
| Pure research | More applied pressure |
| Talent stayed | Alumni founded companies |

**Still valuable:** Continues producing research, but no longer the singular talent source.
