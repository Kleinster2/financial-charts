# TransDigm

TransDigm Group (NYSE: TDG) is a maker of highly-engineered, largely proprietary aerospace components, run as a private-equity-style roll-up: ~90% proprietary products with a high-margin razor-blade aftermarket, aggressive leverage, and periodic large special dividends. A member of the [[Aerospace aftermarket]] cohort, where it is the idiosyncratic singleton.
