# Expana

Commodity-pricing benchmark and research provider, frequently cited as the reference price source for soft commodities (incl. nuts, dairy, proteins). Publishes the Expana Benchmark Prices that are referenced in commodity trade publications and used by [[FT]] in commodity-market reporting.
