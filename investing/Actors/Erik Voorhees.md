**Erik Voorhees** — [[Crypto]]-libertarian entrepreneur. Founded [[ShapeShift]] in 2014 (non-custodial crypto exchange). Early [[Bitcoin]] investor and long-time public voice for censorship-resistant money. Pivoted to decentralized AI in 2024 as founder/CEO of [[Venice AI]], which he positions as the "antidote to AI being captured by large tech companies in bed with the government."

## Public positioning

Voorhees's consistent public argument — across [[Bitcoin]] in the 2010s and AI in the 2020s — is that centralized intermediaries will be captured by governments, and the antidote is open-source decentralized alternatives. Venice is a direct application of that thesis to consumer AI.

Notable podcasts / interviews: Unchained (Laura Shin), CoinDesk Podcast Network, The Block (crypto media, distinct from Jack Dorsey's payments holding company), Decrypt.
