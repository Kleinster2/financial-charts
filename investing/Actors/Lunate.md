**Lunate** — Abu Dhabi alternative asset manager. $115B AUM. Largest alternatives manager in Middle East. Launched 2023.

---

## Why Lunate matters

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Founded | 2023 |
| AUM | **$115 billion** |
| Backers | [[ADQ]], Chimera ([[IHC]] subsidiary) |
| Position | Largest alternatives manager in Middle East |

In just two years, Lunate became the region's largest alternative asset manager — deploying Abu Dhabi sovereign capital into private credit, hedge funds, and infrastructure.
