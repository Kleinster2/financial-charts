**Bloomberg Philanthropies** — [[Michael Bloomberg]]'s philanthropic organization. Focus: climate, public health, education, government innovation. $25B+ lifetime giving.

---

## Overview

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Founded | 2006 (formalized) |
| Founder | [[Michael Bloomberg]] |
| Lifetime giving | $25.4B+ |
| 2024 giving | $3.7B |
| 2025 giving | $4.3B |
| Focus areas | Climate, public health, education, cities |

Bloomberg has topped the list of America's biggest donors two years running.

---

## Focus areas

### Climate

| Initiative | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Beyond Carbon** | Close coal plants, clean energy transition |
| **American Sustainable Cities** | 25 cities, federal funding leverage (launched 2024) |
| **Paris Agreement support** | Pledged to cover US funding gap after withdrawal (Jan 2025) |
| **Climate and Health Coalition** | $300M fund with Gates, Rockefeller, Wellcome |

**COP30 (2025):** Announced Local Leaders Climate Awards with C40.

### Public Health

| Initiative | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Anti-tobacco** | Global campaigns, tax advocacy |
| **Road safety** | Bloomberg Initiative for Global Road Safety |
| **Obesity prevention** | Sugary drink taxes, food policy |
| **Health partnerships** | $150M renewed (July 2024) |

### Education

| Gift | Amount | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Johns Hopkins (2024) | $1B | Free medical school tuition |
| HBCUs (2024) | $600M | Medical school endowments (Howard, Meharry, Morehouse, Drew) |
| Healthcare high schools (2024) | $250M | 10 schools training healthcare workers |

### Government innovation

**Bloomberg Cities:** Technical assistance, data programs for mayors worldwide.
