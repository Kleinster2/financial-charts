# Four Seasons

Canadian luxury hotel and resort company founded 1960 by Isadore Sharp in Toronto. 133+ hotels and resorts across 47 countries, 60+ in development. Asset-light model — manages properties under long-term contracts, owns no real estate. Majority-owned by Bill Gates' [[Cascade Investment]] (71.25%) and Prince Al-Waleed bin Talal's [[Kingdom Holding]] (23.75%); founder Sharp retains 5% via Triples Holdings. Valued at ~$10B (2021 transaction). Gates first invested in 1997; 2007 buyout at $3.8B took company private. Expanding into yachts (Four Seasons I, 95 suites, launching Jan 2026), private jet experiences, and branded residences. Named to Fortune "100 Best Companies to Work For" every year since survey inception (1998).
