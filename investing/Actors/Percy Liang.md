## Percy Liang

Stanford Computer Science associate professor and co-founder of [[Simile]]. Founding director of the Center for Research on Foundation Models (CRFM) at Stanford HAI — his group coined the term "foundation model." PhD from UC Berkeley. Recipient of the Presidential Early Career Award for Scientists and Engineers and the IJCAI Computers and Thought Award.
