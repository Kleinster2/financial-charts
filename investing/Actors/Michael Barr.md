**Michael Barr** — [[Federal Reserve]] Governor. Served as Vice Chair for Supervision (2022-2025) under the Biden administration, where he championed the stricter [[Basel III]] Endgame proposal. Replaced by [[Michelle Bowman]].

## Key actions

Sole dissenting vote (6-1) on the Mar 19, 2026 [[Basel III]] Endgame proposals, warning the weakened US version *"could trigger a race to the bottom on standards, harming the global financial system."*
