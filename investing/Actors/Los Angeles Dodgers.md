The **Los Angeles Dodgers** are an MLB franchise owned by the Guggenheim Baseball Management group (since 2012, purchased for $2.15B from [[Frank McCourt]]). [[Steve Soboroff]] served as vice chairman for 67 days in 2011 during the McCourt ownership crisis.
