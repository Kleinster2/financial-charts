[[CQ Brown|CQ Brown Jr.]] is a retired [[United States Air Force]] four-star general who served as the 21st Chairman of the [[Joint Chiefs of Staff]] from October 2023 until his firing by President [[Donald Trump]] in February 2025. Replaced by Gen. [[Dan Caine]]. Now executive-in-residence and senior adviser at [[Powerus]], a Trump-family-backed drone company.
