Ro Khanna — U.S. Representative for California's 17th congressional district (Silicon Valley). Democrat. Ranking member of the [[House Select Committee on China]] alongside chair [[John Moolenaar]] (R-MI). Co-author of the bipartisan March 8, 2026 letter to [[Dominari Securities]], [[Revere Securities]], and [[D Boral Capital]] requesting information on Chinese small-cap IPO underwriting practices implicated in suspected ramp-and-dump manipulation.
