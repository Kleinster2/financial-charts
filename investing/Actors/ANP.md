**ANP (Agencia Nacional do Petroleo, Gas Natural e Biocombustiveis)** — Brazil's petroleum, natural gas, and biofuels regulatory agency. Authorizes biodiesel production plants, registers supply contracts, publishes weekly biodiesel prices, sets quality specifications (Resolution 920/2023 for B100), and maintains production/capacity data. Formerly ran the bimonthly biodiesel auction system (2007-2021); transitioned to free-market direct negotiation in January 2022.
