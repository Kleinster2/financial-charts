**Morpheus Research** — activist short-selling research shop founded by former [[Hindenburg Research]] analysts; described by [[Bloomberg]] as "Hindenburg-linked" at its March 17, 2025 debut. Its first report targeted [[Solaris Energy Infrastructure]] ("How A Crumbling Texas Oilfield Services Company Gambled It All On A Convicted Felon And The World's Richest Man"), knocking the stock down 16.9% in a session and seeding the Pirello securities class action.
