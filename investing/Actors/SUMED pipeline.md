# SUMED pipeline

**SUMED pipeline** is a 200-mile crude oil pipeline across [[Egypt]] from Ain Sokhna (Red Sea) to Sidi Kerir (Mediterranean) with 2.8M bpd capacity — the bypass route for VLCCs too large to transit the [[Suez Canal]].
