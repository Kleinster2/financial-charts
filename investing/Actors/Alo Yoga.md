# Alo Yoga

**Premium athleisure brand** — celebrity-driven, LA-based, ~$10B valuation (2023). Revenue ~$1B+ (est. 2024), exploded from $200M in 2020. **100% founder-owned** — no outside investment. Danny Harris and Marco DeGeorge (co-founders, ~$4.7B net worth each) bootstrapped via Color Image Apparel (parent company, ~$2B revenue). 100+ retail stores (up from 10 in early 2023). Competing directly with [[Lululemon]] for premium yoga/athleisure market.
