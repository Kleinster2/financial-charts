George Lucas — filmmaker and founder of [[Lucasfilm]], creator of Star Wars and Indiana Jones. Sold Lucasfilm to [[Disney]] in 2012 for $4B as part of [[Bob Iger]]'s franchise-IP acquisition spree, transferring the Star Wars engine of box office, merchandise, and theme-park revenue to Disney.
