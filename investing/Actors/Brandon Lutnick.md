# Brandon Lutnick

Chairman and CEO of [[Cantor Fitzgerald]], succeeding his father [[Howard Lutnick]] after the elder Lutnick became Commerce Secretary (2025). Received a letter from Senators [[Ron Wyden]] and [[Elizabeth Warren]] (Aug 2025) demanding answers about Cantor's [[Tariff refund claims market|tariff refund claims trading]] and potential insider dealing.
