## Key contributions

**Microsoft:**
- Led board search committee that selected [[Satya Nadella]] as CEO (2014)
- First independent chairman (Gates was founder-chairman)
- Oversaw early cloud transformation years
- Succeeded by Nadella as Chairman (2021)

**Symantec:**
- 10x revenue growth ($600M → $6B) over 10 years
- Built into security software leader
- First Black CEO of major tech company
