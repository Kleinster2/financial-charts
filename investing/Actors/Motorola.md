### Razr Fold

**First book-style foldable from Motorola:**
- 6.6" external + 8.1" internal display
- Moto Pen Ultra stylus support (Samsung dropped this)
- 50MP quad camera system
- Dolby Vision recording
- Pantone colors (Blackened Blue, Lily White)
- Competes with Samsung Z Fold, Google Pixel Fold

### Motorola Signature

**New ultra-premium line:**
- 6.99mm thickness ("thinnest quad-curved")
- Aircraft-grade aluminum
- Snapdragon 8 Gen 5
- Quad 50MP cameras
- €999 / £899 (Feb 2026)
- 7 years of OS updates

### Qira integration

Part of [[Lenovo]]'s cross-device AI:
- Syncs with Lenovo PCs and tablets
- Real-time transcription
- Notification summaries
- AI agent coordination
