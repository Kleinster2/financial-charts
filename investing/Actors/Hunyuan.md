#actor #product #ai #china

**Hunyuan** (混元) — [[Tencent]]'s AI model family. Deep WeChat integration — **90% of question queries** on WeChat use AI search. Consumer-facing via Yuanbao app. 30+ models released, fully open source.

---

## Why Hunyuan matters

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| WeChat AI search | **90%** of question queries |
| Yuanbao (consumer app) | Integrated into WeChat |
| Models released | **30+** (past year) |
| Parent | [[Tencent]] |
| Latest model | Hunyuan 2.0 (Dec 2025) |

Tencent's AI strategy: embed into WeChat's 1.3B users rather than compete as standalone app.

---

## WeChat integration

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| AI Search (搜一搜) | 90% of question queries |
| Yuanbao assistant | Article summaries, image/file responses |
| Mini-program generation | Natural language → mini-programs (no code) |
| WeChat Reading | AI Q&A within books |
| Red Packet Cover | AI-generated designs |

**Strategy:** Don't make users download new app — bring AI to where 1.3B users already are.

**DeepSeek integration:** WeChat AI search also integrates [[DeepSeek]] alongside Hunyuan.
