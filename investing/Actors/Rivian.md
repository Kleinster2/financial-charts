- Gross profit positive
- Amazon partnership (fleet + investment)
- Cheap valuation (3x revenue vs Tesla 14x)

**Bear:**
- Years from profitability
- R2 execution risk
- Tesla price cuts pressure margins
- EV market softening
- Competition from legacy OEMs
