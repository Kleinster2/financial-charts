**Hard Rock International** — casino and hospitality company owned by the [[Seminole Tribe of Florida]]. Operates ~260 Hard Rock locations (hotels, casinos, cafes) globally. Building Hard Rock at [[Metropolitan Park]] (NYC, $8.1B with [[Steve Cohen]]). Acquired The Mirage from [[MGM Resorts]] for $1.075B (2022).
