#actor #person #usa #energy

**Toby Neugebauer** — Energy financier and entrepreneur. Co-founded [[Quantum Energy Partners]] and [[Fermi]], where he served as CEO until Apr 17, 2026. Previously led [[GloriFi]], the anti-woke digital bank that collapsed into bankruptcy in 2023. Son of former congressman Randy Neugebauer (TX-19). Forbes estimated his net worth at roughly $5B in late 2025, mostly tied to Fermi stock.

## Apr 2026 exit from Fermi

An Apr 17, 2026 8-K disclosed that Neugebauer had departed the CEO role at [[Fermi]]. The board installed an interim Office of the CEO and began a formal search for a replacement, while Neugebauer stayed on the board.

The market treated the move as part of a broader governance crisis rather than a routine succession. Fermi's shares fell 17.6% on Apr 20, a -2.6σ move, after the company paired Neugebauer's exit with the CFO's departure and a "Fermi 2.0" governance reset.

## Why he matters

Neugebauer embodies both the appeal and the risk in [[Fermi]]. He helped raise huge amounts of capital and market an 11GW nuclear-and-gas AI campus, but his track record also includes the collapse of [[GloriFi]] and litigation around that venture. In practice, the investment question around Fermi has always been inseparable from the question of whether Neugebauer could turn ambition and political access into a real operating company.
