# Raphaël Gorgé

CEO and Chairman of [[Exail Technologies]] since 2011. Son of founder [[Jean-Pierre Gorgé]]. Drove the 2022 acquisition of [[iXblue]] and transformation from [[Groupe Gorgé]] into a pure-play maritime defense autonomy company. Won EY Entrepreneur of the Year 2025. Born 1971.
