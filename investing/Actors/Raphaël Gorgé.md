# Raphaël Gorgé

CEO and Chairman of [[Exail Technologies]] since 2011, and the driving force behind transforming a family industrial conglomerate into a pure-play maritime defense autonomy company. Son of founder [[Jean-Pierre Gorgé]], he joined the family business in 2004 as Deputy CEO before taking full control at age 40. His defining strategic move was the 2022 acquisition of [[iXblue]] for ~EUR 410M, merging its fiber-optic gyroscope inertial navigation systems and photonics with [[ECA Group]]'s autonomous underwater vehicles and robotics to form Exail Technologies. The family controls ~56% of capital and ~61% of voting rights via the Pelican Venture holding company, giving Raphaël effective autonomy over strategy without activist interference. Won EY Entrepreneur of the Year 2025.
