**Mercosur** — Southern Common Market. Brazil, Argentina, Uruguay, Paraguay. Customs union with common external tariff. Historically protectionist, now pursuing EU deal.

## How Mercosur works

### Customs union

| Feature | Status |
|---------|--------|
| Internal tariffs | Zero (mostly) |
| Common External Tariff (CET) | Yes (with exceptions) |
| Trade policy | Common (in theory) |

**Leaky customs union:** Many exceptions, bilateral deals undermine CET.

### Institutions

| Body | Function |
|------|----------|
| CMC | Council (ministers), decisions |
| GMC | Common Market Group, coordination |
| Parlasur | Parliament (advisory) |
| Secretariat | Administration (Montevideo) |

## Protectionist history

| Feature | Reality |
|---------|---------|
| High external tariffs | CET averages 11%+ |
| Import substitution | Industrial policy legacy |
| Auto regime | Managed trade |
| Few FTAs | Bloc negotiates together (slowly) |

**Contrast to [[Pacific Alliance]]:** Mercosur = closed, PA = open.
