## Business model

**Closed-book reinsurance specialist:**
- Acquires in-force blocks of life and annuity liabilities from primary insurers
- Manages the run-off — optimizing assets against known liability profiles
- Carlyle manages the investment portfolio for spread income

**Advisory relationship with Carlyle:**
- ~$22B committed capital allocated across Carlyle funds
- ~$75B advisory mandate (Carlyle directs asset allocation)
- New advisory agreement (2025) expected to add $50B fee-earning AUM to Carlyle

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Closed-book run-off = predictable, low-volatility liabilities
- Carlyle asset management generates spread + fees
- Asia expansion via FCA Re sidecar
- T&D relationship opens Japan market

**Bear:**
- Complex ownership structure
- Regulatory risk (Bermuda AIR scrutiny)
- Legacy AIG liabilities may have tail risk
- Illiquid private credit in investment portfolio
