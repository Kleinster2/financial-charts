# Torbjörn Törnqvist

Swedish commodities trader; co-founder of [[Gunvor]] (2000) with [[Gennady Timchenko]]. After Timchenko's 2014 forced exit under US Treasury sanctions over [[Russia]]'s annexation of Crimea, Törnqvist became Gunvor's sole majority owner and chairman-CEO, steering the firm's diversification away from Russian crude toward a globally-sourced book. On December 1, 2025 he announced a management buy-out in which he sold his remaining stake to an employee-partner group, with Gary Pedersen succeeding as CEO.
