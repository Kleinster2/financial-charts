**Matt Clifford** — British technology entrepreneur, investor, and AI policy adviser. Co-founder of [[Entrepreneurs First]] (talent-investor model). Author of the UK's 2024 AI Opportunities Action Plan commissioned by PM Keir Starmer. Long-time associate of [[Demis Hassabis]]; angel investments overlap.

## Significance

Clifford sits at the intersection of three [[UK]] AI threads: founder-incubation infrastructure ([[Entrepreneurs First]] has produced multiple AI founders), government policy (his Action Plan shaped UK AI sovereignty positioning, including the [[UK Sovereign AI Fund]] that backed [[Ineffable Intelligence]]), and angel-investor proximity to [[Demis Hassabis]] and the [[DeepMind diaspora]].

His public framing of the diaspora (FT, May 2026): "Demis has a huge amount of soft power at Google and beyond ... He was a pioneer before it was obvious what AI was, and the availability of capital for his protégés is pretty striking. That's a big driver of what has happened."
