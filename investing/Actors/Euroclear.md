## Core services

**Settlement:** Delivery-versus-payment for domestic and international securities. Connected to ECB's [[TARGET2-Securities]] (T2S) since September 2023.

**Custody:** Safekeeping, corporate actions, income processing, tax services.

**Collateral Highway:** Open-architecture platform for cross-border collateral mobilization. Triparty repo (+17% YoY in 2024), securities lending, CCP margining.

**Funds:** FundsPlace order routing and settlement (2,500+ clients, EUR 3.6T AUC).

## Competitive position

**Duopoly with [[Clearstream]]:** Two ICSDs dominate international bond settlement. Euroclear is larger — Clearstream generates ~58% of Euroclear's revenue.

**vs [[DTCC]]:** DTCC dominates US ($100T AUC). Euroclear dominates Europe. They collaborate via GlobalCollateral joint venture.

**Moat:**
- Network effects (1,800+ participants)
- Regulatory relationships (decades-deep)
- Collateral Highway infrastructure
- User ownership = aligned incentives
- T2S integration

---

## Russian sanctions exposure

**Critical risk:**
- EUR 183-195B in sanctioned Russian assets on balance sheet
- December 2025: Russia's Central Bank sued for EUR 195.5B in damages
- Russia confiscated ~EUR 33B in Euroclear client assets
- Belgium pressured to use principal for Ukraine aid
- Political controversy: Belgium called "a Russian asset" for demanding EU liability guarantees
