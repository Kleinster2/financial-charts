# Labour Party (Norway)

Norway's centre-left party (Arbeiderpartiet). Led by [[Jonas Gahr Støre]], who served as PM 2021–2025 and won re-election in September 2025. Raised the [[Wealth tax]] in 2022, triggering significant [[Capital flight]].
