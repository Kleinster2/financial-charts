# Tenet Healthcare

Tenet Healthcare (NYSE: THC) is a US acute-care hospital operator that has reshaped itself around higher-margin ambulatory surgery — its USPI (United Surgical Partners International) network of surgery centers — plus a hospital portfolio and the Conifer revenue-cycle-management business. A member of the [[Hospital operators]] cohort.
