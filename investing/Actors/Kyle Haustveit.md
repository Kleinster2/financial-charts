**Kyle Haustveit** is the Assistant Secretary of the Hydrocarbons and Geothermal Energy Office (HGEO) at the [[Department of Energy]]. In this role he oversees federal hydrocarbon and geothermal energy programs, including policy relating to the [[Strategic Petroleum Reserve]], fossil energy R&D, and unconventional resource development. The HGEO position gained relevance during the 2026 energy policy environment as the Trump administration pushed to maximize domestic hydrocarbon production while navigating supply disruptions from the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|Iran-Hormuz crisis]]. Haustveit's office sits at the intersection of SPR management decisions and broader energy security coordination.
