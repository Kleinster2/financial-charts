**M42** — Abu Dhabi-based health and AI technology group formed by merging G42 Healthcare and Mubadala Health (2023). Group CEO Dimitris Moulavasilis. Backed by [[Mubadala]] and [[G42]]. Operates hospitals, diagnostics, and genomics platforms across the UAE and internationally. Combines [[G42]]'s AI capabilities with Mubadala's healthcare infrastructure.
