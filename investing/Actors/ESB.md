**ESB** (Electricity Supply Board) is [[Ireland]]'s semi-state electricity company, operating power generation, distribution networks (ESB Networks), and retail supply. Founded 1927.

| Field | Value |

| Ownership | 95% Irish state, 5% employee trust |

ESB Networks operates the distribution grid. Together with [[EirGrid]] (transmission), expected to detail implementation rules for the [[CRU]]'s December 2025 data centre policy by end of March 2026.
