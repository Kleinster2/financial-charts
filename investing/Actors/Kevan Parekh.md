# Kevan Parekh

Chief Financial Officer of [[Apple]], in the role since January 2025 (succeeded Luca Maestri). Sets the tone on Apple's cost structure and capital return; on recent calls he has characterized Apple as "managing costs well" through the [[Memory shortage 2025-2026|memory cost cycle]], and oversaw the FY2026 $100B buyback authorization and Apple's first bond issuance since 2023.
