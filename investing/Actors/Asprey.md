**Asprey** — British luxury house. Jewelry, silverware, leather goods, timepieces. Founded 1781. Royal warrants since Queen Victoria. Private, owned by [[Sciens Capital]] (John Rigas is Chairman).

## Royal warrants

| Year | Monarch | For |
|------|---------|-----|
| 1862 | Queen Victoria | Dressing cases, travelling bags |
| 1889 | Prince of Wales (Edward VII) | General |
| 1911 | George V | Trunk makers |
| 1920 | Prince Edward (VIII) | Silversmiths |
| 1940 | George VI | Jewellers & Silversmiths |
| ~1952-2022 | Elizabeth II | Jewellers, Goldsmiths, Silversmiths |

Held continuous royal warrants 1862-2022. **Notably absent from Charles III's first warrant grants (May 2023)** — break in 160+ year tradition.

## Investment case

**Strengths:**
- 240+ years heritage
- Royal warrant pedigree
- Bespoke manufacturing capability
- Ultra-high-net-worth clientele

**Weaknesses:**
- Private, limited scale vs [[LVMH]] brands
- Lost Charles III warrant
- Small retail footprint (4 stores)
