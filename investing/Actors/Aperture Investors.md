Aperture Investors is an active equity management firm founded in 2018 by [[Peter Kraus]] (former [[Bernstein|AllianceBernstein]] CEO). Backed by [[Generali]], the Italian insurer. Aperture uses a fee structure tied to outperformance rather than fixed management fees.
