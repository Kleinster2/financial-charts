**Dilma Rousseff** — 36th President of [[Brazil]] (2011-2016). Impeached August 2016. Her economic interventionism — particularly forcing [[Petrobras]] to sell fuel below market prices (R$59B+ in refining losses) and replacing the [[Banco Central do Brasil|BCB]] board to cut rates — destroyed institutional credibility and triggered the worst recession in Brazilian history. The "New Economic Matrix" policy became the canonical [[Time consistency]] violation in Brazilian monetary history, directly causing the 2021 CB independence law.
