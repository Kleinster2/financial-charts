# Jeanine Pirro

US Attorney for the District of Columbia in the second Trump administration. Former NY state judge, district attorney, and Fox News host. The DC US Attorney role gave her direct authority over the criminal investigation into [[Jerome Powell]] over Federal Reserve building cost overruns.

## Powell investigation closure (Apr 24, 2026)

Pirro announced via social media that the DOJ is closing the criminal investigation into [[Jerome Powell|Powell]] over Fed building cost overruns. The closing statement included a reservation: she would not hesitate to reopen if "facts warrant." That last line is the operative one — without it the closure would be a clean dismissal; with it, Powell remains exposed to pretextual recharge under any future IG criminal referral. The reservation also shapes [[Tom Tillis|Sen. Tillis]]'s confirmation calculus on [[Kevin Warsh]] — the question is whether "fully resolved" requires a clean close or accepts a hold-fire.

[[Scott Alvarez]] (former Fed General Counsel) on the language: *"It does smell of transparency and finality up to the last sentence... that last sentence is the critical one."*
