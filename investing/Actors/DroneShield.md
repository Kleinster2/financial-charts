# DroneShield

Australia's most valuable publicly listed defense company by market capitalization (ASX: DRO) and a global counter-drone leader. Develops, commercializes, and sells counter-UAS hardware and software across Australia, the US, Europe, Asia, the UK, and other markets. Top-ten holding in the [[DRNZ]] drone ETF. Primary public-market beneficiary of the [[Joint Interagency Task Force 401]] counter-UAS marketplace.

## Investment context

Pure-play public counter-drone exposure on a global scale — no comparable scale exists in the US public markets. Direct beneficiary of three macro tailwinds: (1) [[Replicator Initiative|Replicator 2]] / JIATF-401 marketplace expansion, (2) Australian A$7B counter-drone budget framework, (3) NATO + EU rearmament with counter-UAS prioritization after [[Russia]]/[[Ukraine]] battlefield lessons.

The asymmetry: most US-listed counter-drone exposure is nested inside diversified primes ([[Lockheed Martin]], [[RTX]], [[Northrop Grumman]]) where C-UAS is a small share of revenue. DroneShield is one of the few liquid pure-plays at scale.
