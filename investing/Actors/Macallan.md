# Macallan

**Highland single malt Scotch whisky distillery** — owned by [[Edrington]] Group (privately held, Glasgow). One of the world's most valuable whisky brands, known for sherry-cask maturation and secondary-market collectibility. Distillery in Craigellachie, Speyside. Used as a premium spirit partner by brands like [[Aubi & Ramsa]] (Highland Truffle flavor).
