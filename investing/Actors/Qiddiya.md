#actor #entertainment #saudi #private

**Qiddiya** — entertainment mega-city under development near Riyadh, wholly owned by [[Saudi PIF]]. Part of [[Saudi Vision 2030]]'s strategy to build domestic entertainment infrastructure and reduce leisure spending outflows. Includes gaming, sports, and entertainment venues across a site larger than [[Walt Disney World]].
