#actor #space #usa #private

**Sierra Space** — Colorado-based private space company spun off from Sierra Nevada Corporation (SNC) in 2021. Builds the Dream Chaser space plane (autonomous reusable winged spacecraft for ISS resupply) and partners with [[Blue Origin]] on the [[Orbital Reef]] commercial space station. [[Commercial LEO Destinations]] Phase 1 awardee (via Orbital Reef partnership).

Sierra Space's 2021 Series A was one of the largest space-startup rounds at the time, valuing the company at ~$4.5B. Subsequent disclosure is limited.
