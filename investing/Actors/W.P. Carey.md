# W.P. Carey

W.P. Carey (NYSE: WPC) is a diversified net-lease REIT weighted toward industrial and warehouse properties (plus retail and self-storage), with significant European exposure. It exited office in 2023 (spinning off Net Lease Office Properties / NLOP) and reset its dividend. A member of the [[Net-lease REITs]] cohort.
