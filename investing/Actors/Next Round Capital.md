# Next Round Capital

Next Round Capital — secondary marketplace for pre-IPO shares, founded by Ken Smythe. Has handled $2.5B in transactions. Became a key source on AI secondary market dynamics in early 2026, when the firm reported ~$600M in [[OpenAI]] shares from ~6 institutional investors (hedge funds, VCs) with no buyers, while simultaneously seeing $2B+ in buyer demand for [[Anthropic]] shares at a ~$600B implied valuation (58% premium to $380B Series G). Smythe (Apr 2026): *"We literally couldn't find anyone in our pool of hundreds of institutional investors to take these shares"* (on OpenAI).
