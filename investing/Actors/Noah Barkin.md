# Noah Barkin

China-Europe analyst, German Marshall Fund senior advisor, and author of the *Watching China in Europe* Substack. Tracks the EU-China relationship through trade, investment, technology, and security lenses — a niche underserved by US-centric China commentary.

## What he covers

- German and EU-level China policy (de-risking, screening regimes, FDI restrictions)
- European industrial response to Chinese subsidies and overcapacity (EVs, solar, steel)
- China's diplomatic posture toward Europe and EU member states
- Spillovers from US-China dynamics into European strategic options
