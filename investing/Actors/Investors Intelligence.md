# Investors Intelligence

The oldest continuously published sentiment survey in existence, launched in 1963 by Abe Cohen of Chartcraft. Classifies over 100 independent investment newsletter writers as bullish, bearish, or expecting a correction. Only four editors have done the classification in 63 years, giving the data unusual consistency. Originally intended to confirm trend direction, it turned out advisors were "almost always wrong at major market turning points" — making it one of the first contrarian indicators.
