## Background

Born 1996 in Belo Horizonte, Brazil. Grew up between Timóteo, Niterói, and Joinville. Parents: math teacher and engineer.

**Ballet career:**
- Trained at Escola do Teatro Bolshoi in Joinville (affiliated with Russia's Bolshoi)
- 13-hour days: 7am academics to 9pm rehearsals
- Danced professionally in Austria (~9 months) including Swan Lake
- Won Gold in Brazilian Astronomy Olympiad, Bronze in Santa Catarina Math Olympiad

**MIT:**
- BS in Computer Science and Mathematics (2018)
- Master of Engineering
- Met [[Tarek Mansour]]

**Career before Kalshi:**
- Bridgewater Associates — quantitative trader
- [[Citadel]] — quantitative trader
- Five Rings Capital — quantitative trader

---

## Kalshi founding

Co-founded Kalshi 2018 with Mansour. Y Combinator Winter 2019. Built regulatory memos, prototype order book. 2+ years of CFTC engagement to get DCM license.

**Dec 2025:** Became world's youngest self-made female billionaire after $1B Series E valued Kalshi at $11B. Her 12% stake = ~$1.3B.
