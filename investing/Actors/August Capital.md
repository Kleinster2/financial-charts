# August Capital

**Enterprise IT venture capital** — founded 1995 by David Marquardt ([[Microsoft]] Series A investor, board member since 1981). **~$2B AUM**. Portfolio includes [[Bill.com]], Splunk (IPO), Atheros. 7 unicorns, 11 IPOs.

## Founder: David Marquardt

| Detail | Value |

**Origin story:** In 1980, Marquardt heard about "Billy Gates" through the grapevine. Called Steve Ballmer ([[Stanford]] classmate) for a meeting. In 1981, invested $1M for 5% of Microsoft through TVI. Still on Microsoft board today.
