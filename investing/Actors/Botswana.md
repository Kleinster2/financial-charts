# Botswana

Southern African diamond-dependent nation. Formerly Africa's largest diamond producer by value, now overtaken by [[Angola]] (2025). Economy heavily dependent on mining (~20% of GDP), undergoing two consecutive years of economic contraction as the [[Diamond market collapse]] erodes its primary revenue source.

## Diamond crisis impact

[[Angola]]'s diamond sales jumped ~70% in 2025, overtaking Botswana as Africa's largest producer. Mineral revenue projected to fall to 10.3B pula ($733M) this fiscal year — less than half the historical average. The government is seeking to diversify away from diamond dependence, but the industry remains structurally central to the economy.

Debswana (the De Beers-Botswana JV) cut output 40% in 2025 as [[De Beers]] reduced supply to stabilize prices. The combination of lab-grown disruption, Chinese demand collapse, and supply management breakdown leaves Botswana's fiscal model structurally impaired.

*Source: Bloomberg, March 18 2026*
