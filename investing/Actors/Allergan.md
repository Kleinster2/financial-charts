**Allergan** — Irish-domiciled specialty pharmaceutical company. Best known for Botox. Subject of a hostile ~$47B bid by [[Valeant Pharmaceuticals]] and [[Bill Ackman]] / [[Pershing Square]] in 2014. Ultimately acquired by [[Actavis]] for ~$66B in November 2014 (the combined company kept the Allergan name). [[AbbVie]] then acquired Allergan for ~$63B in May 2020.
