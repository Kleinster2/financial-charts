**Susquehanna International Group (SIG)** — Global quantitative trading firm and one of the largest market makers in listed options. Notable equity research on semiconductors. Major investor in [[ByteDance]]/[[TikTok]].

## Equity research (Susquehanna Financial Group)

SFG is the institutional broker-dealer arm. Covers ~200 companies with differentiated research.

### Key analysts

| Analyst | Coverage | TipRanks rank | Success rate |
|---------|----------|---------------|--------------|
| **Mehdi Hosseini** | Semi equipment, foundries, OSAT, displays | \#319 / 9,143 | 58.6% |
| **Christopher Rolland** | [[Semiconductors]] (NVIDIA, AMD, Broadcom, etc.) | Top tier | 52.2% |

### Mehdi Hosseini

**Background**: 20+ years experience. Joined SFG August 2010. Previously FBR Capital Markets (6 years, top-ranked), SoundView Technology Group (4 years). Former design engineer at National Semiconductor (5 years).

**Education**: MBA Carnegie Mellon; MS/BS Electrical Engineering, University of Florida.

**Coverage**: [[TSMC]], [[ASML]], [[Applied Materials]], [[Lam Research]], [[KLA]], [[Micron]], Teradyne, [[Western Digital]], and others.

**Style**: Known for conservative guidance expectations, focus on EPS power over revenue.

### Christopher Rolland

**Background**: Columbia Business School. Covers fabless semis and AI/hyperscale.

**Coverage**: [[NVIDIA]], [[AMD]], [[Broadcom]], [[Intel]], [[Marvell]], [[Qualcomm]], ON Semi, [[NXP]], others.

**Notable call (Aug 2025)**: "$250B revenue reasonable for NVIDIA in 2026"

**NVIDIA view (Nov 2025)**: Positive rating, PT raised $230 → $250. "Revenue is bigger story than gross margins."

## Investment relevance

**Why track Susquehanna**:

1. **Equity research** — Hosseini and Rolland are go-to analysts on semiconductors. Their calls move stocks.

2. **13F signals** — $720B AUM means their positioning matters. Options-heavy strategy provides sentiment signals.

3. **[[TikTok]]/ByteDance** — Yass's stake means SIG has direct interest in US-[[China]] tech policy outcomes. Political donations correlate with [[TikTok]] regulatory timeline.

4. **Market making** — As one of largest options market makers, SIG's activity affects liquidity and pricing in derivatives markets.
