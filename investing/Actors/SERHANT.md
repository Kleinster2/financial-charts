#actor #realestate #company #usa

**SERHANT.** — Real estate brokerage founded by [[Ryan Serhant]] in 2020. Grew from zero to $7B+ volume in 5 years. Known for media-driven agent recruitment and AI-powered technology platform. Raised $45M in December 2024.

---

## Why SERHANT. matters

Demonstrates the celebrity-broker model at scale: leverage media presence to attract listings and agents, then differentiate with technology. Fastest-growing brokerage of the 2020s.

| 2025 volume | $7B+ |
| Agents | 1,100+ |

| Agent retention | 99% (vs 92% industry avg) |

## Business model

### Three pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Brokerage** | Traditional real estate sales (residential focus) |
| **Education (Sell It)** | Agent training and coaching programs |
| **Content/Media** | Netflix shows, YouTube, social media |

The media flywheel: TV shows → brand awareness → agent recruitment → listings → more content.

## Funding

**December 2024: $45M seed round**
- Lead: Camber Creek (real estate VC)
- Participant: Left Lane Capital
- Use of funds: AI platform "S.MPLE", expansion

This was first outside capital after 4 years bootstrapped.

**January 2025:** Investment in RLTYco (early-stage VC deal).

## Technology: S.MPLE

AI-powered workflow automation platform for agents. Key differentiator vs traditional brokerages.

Features:
- Lead management
- Transaction coordination
- Marketing automation
- CRM integration

Funded by $45M raise specifically to accelerate development.
