**Zabar's** — iconic Upper West Side gourmet food store. Founded 1934. Known for smoked fish, coffee, cheese, and kitchenware. NYC institution for 90+ years. Part of the legacy premium grocer landscape now joined by newer entrants like [[Meadow Lane]] and Happier Grocery.
