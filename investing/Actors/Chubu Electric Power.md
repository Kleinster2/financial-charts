# Chubu Electric Power

[[Japan]]'s third-largest electric utility, serving the Chubu region (Nagoya). Co-owner (50%) of [[JERA]], Japan's largest power generation company (JV with [[Tokyo Electric Power]]). Through JERA, connected to ammonia co-firing and blue ammonia supply chain development.
