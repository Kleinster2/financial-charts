# Western Sahara

Disputed territory in North Africa occupied by [[Morocco]] since 1975, claimed by the Polisario Front (representing the Sahrawi people) as the Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic. Home to significant phosphate rock deposits mined by [[OCP Group]]'s subsidiary Phosboucraa, making it a flashpoint for resource sovereignty debates.
