Patagonia — American outdoor apparel company. Founded 1973 by Yvon Chouinard in Ventura, California. Bootstrapped, never took outside investment. $1B+ annual revenue. In 2022, Chouinard transferred 100% of ownership to the Patagonia Purpose Trust and Holdfast Collective (a climate-focused nonprofit) — profits (~$100M/year) now go to fighting climate change.
