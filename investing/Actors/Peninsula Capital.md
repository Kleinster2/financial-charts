# Peninsula Capital

Luxembourg-based private equity fund co-founded 2018 by Stefano Marsaglia and the [[Qatar Investment Authority]]. ~€1.6B in capital. Specializes in Southern European and Latin American markets — invests in companies with leading positions in their sectors. Offices in Luxembourg, London, Dubai, Milan, and Madrid. Marsaglia departed in 2021 to found [[Azzurra Capital]]. Notable investment: ~30% minority stake in [[Zadig & Voltaire]] (acquired Jan 2020, replacing [[TA Associates]]).
