**Legendary Entertainment** is a privately held film/TV studio — the producer of *Dune*, the *Monsterverse* (Godzilla/Kong), and *A Minecraft Movie* — now owned by management and [[Apollo Global Management]] funds, and reported in August 2025 to have explored an acquisition of [[Lionsgate]].

## Ownership history

| Year | Event | Amount |
|------|-------|--------|
| 2000 | Founded by Thomas Tull | — |
| 2016 | Acquired by Wanda Group (China) | ~$3.5B |
| Jan 2022 | Apollo minority equity investment | $760M |
| Oct 2024 | Legendary buys out Wanda's remaining stake | undisclosed |

After the 2024 Wanda buyout, Legendary is owned solely by management and funds managed by affiliates of [[Apollo Global Management]]. The Apollo capital and the removal of Chinese ownership repositioned the studio for M&A — including the reported interest in [[Lionsgate]].

Upcoming: a *Street Fighter* adaptation, *Animal Friends* (Ryan Reynolds), an untitled Alejandro G. Iñárritu film starring Tom Cruise, further Denis Villeneuve projects, and additional Monsterverse entries. The studio runs a high-budget, franchise-event model — the inverse of [[Lionsgate]]'s library-and-mid-budget approach — which is part of the strategic logic of a potential combination.

---

## Lionsgate M&A interest

Legendary was reported (August 2025) among parties exploring a bid for [[Lionsgate]] following its separation from [[Starz]] — consistent with the separation's design to make the studio a clean takeout target, and with Legendary's stated post-Wanda appetite for M&A. No deal has been confirmed. See [[Lionsgate]] → M&A / strategic position.
