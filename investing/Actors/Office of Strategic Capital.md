#actor #government #defense-tech

**Office of Strategic Capital** — Pentagon entity planning $200B in capital deployment to address defense supply chain bottlenecks. Led by [[Emil Michael]] (Under Secretary of Defense for R&E). Team nicknamed "Deal Team Six."

| Historical parallel | Air Force titanium supply chain bootstrap (1950s-60s) |

---

## Approach

Strategic injection of capital down the supply chain to unblock structural bottlenecks. Many drone companies will bottleneck on brushless motors. Key parts of the supply chain lack sufficient US production capacity.

[[Trae Stephens]] ([[Anduril]], Apr 6, 2026): "There's a tremendous opportunity with the Office of Strategic Capital to think about what are the structural bottlenecks in production."
