# NAAIM

The National Association of Active Investment Managers, a nonprofit founded in 1989 representing registered investment advisors providing active management. Publishes the NAAIM Exposure Index (since 2006): member firms report actual U.S. equity exposure weekly (Wednesday close), scaled from -200% (leveraged short) to +200% (leveraged long). Unlike survey-based sentiment, this reflects real money allocations.
