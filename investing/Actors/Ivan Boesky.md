# Ivan Boesky

[[Wall Street]] arbitrageur convicted in the 1986 insider trading scandal. Pleaded guilty to securities manipulation, paid a $100M fine, and served 20 months in prison. Cooperated with the [[SEC]], secretly taping [[Michael Milken]] and providing evidence leading to guilty pleas from [[Boyd Jefferies]] and others. His cooperation brought down [[Drexel Burnham Lambert]]. Died May 2024 at age 87.
