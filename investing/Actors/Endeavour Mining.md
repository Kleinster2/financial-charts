# Endeavour Mining

Endeavour Mining (LSE: EDV, TSX: EDV) is a West Africa-focused gold producer with operations primarily in [[Côte d'Ivoire]], [[Burkina Faso]], and [[Senegal]]. Listed in both London and Toronto, with production around 1.1M ounces per year across multiple operating mines.

Advised by [[Gleacher Shacklock]] on past strategic transactions.
