#company #acquired #food-tech

**Epogee** was the sole manufacturer of EPG (esterified propoxylated glycerol), a modified plant fat delivering 0.7 kcal/g vs 9 kcal/g for conventional fat. Acquired by [[David]] in May 2025 using bulk of its $75M Series A proceeds. David accounted for ~90% of Epogee's revenue pre-acquisition.
