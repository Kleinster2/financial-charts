# Orbia

Mexican industrial conglomerate, formerly Mexichem. World's largest fluorspar miner (~18% of global supply) and a top-10 global [[Hydrofluoric Acid]] producer (~10% of global HF). Listed on BMV (ORBIA.MX). Fluor & Energy Materials division operates as Koura. In July 2024, signed a 5-year ultra-pure HF supply agreement with [[Intel]] for Arizona semiconductor fabs. Operations span fluorspar mining, HF production, fluoropolymers, and building/infrastructure products.
