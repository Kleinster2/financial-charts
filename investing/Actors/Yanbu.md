**Yanbu** — Saudi Red Sea port city and western terminus of the East-West (Petroline) crude oil pipeline. Critical bypass route when the [[Strait of Hormuz]] is disrupted.

## Strategic significance

Yanbu is [[Saudi Arabia]]'s insurance policy against Hormuz closure. The Petroline carries crude ~1,200 km from the Eastern Province oil fields to Yanbu for Red Sea export. During the [[Operation Epic Fury|March 2026 Iran conflict]], it became the only meaningful bypass — but **port loading infrastructure caps effective throughput at ~3 mb/d**, far below the pipeline's 5-7 mb/d capacity. Even at maximum, Yanbu can't compensate for 15+ mb/d of Hormuz flows.
