#actor #data #energy

**Vortexa** — London-based cargo and energy analytics firm specializing in real-time tracking of waterborne oil, gas, and refined product flows. Uses satellite AIS data, proprietary algorithms, and market intelligence to map global commodity movements. Competes with [[Kpler]] in the cargo-tracking space.
