**Banco BMG** is a Brazilian mid-tier bank specialized in consignado (payroll-deduction) lending, with a digital-banking and cards arm. Controlled by the Guimarães family, it trades on B3 as `BMGB4`. (Not to be confused with [[BMG]], the Bertelsmann music company.)

The consignado niche — loans repaid by automatic deduction from payroll or social-security benefits, with correspondingly low default — is BMG's core franchise; it has pushed from there into a digital account and credit cards. For retail investors it shows up mainly as a [[CDB]] issuer, where a mid-tier bank like BMG pays above the big banks and is FGC-insured up to the limit.
