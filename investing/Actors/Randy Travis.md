# Randy Travis

Country music legend who regained his singing voice through AI vocal restoration after a devastating stroke in 2013 left him largely unable to speak or sing. One of the most emotionally compelling use cases for AI voice technology — using AI not for commercial exploitation but for medical/personal restoration. His AI-restored performances have become a landmark example of AI's positive potential.
