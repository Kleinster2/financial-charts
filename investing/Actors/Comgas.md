# Comgas

[[Brazil]]'s largest piped natural gas distributor — ~2.3M customers across 95+ municipalities in Sao Paulo state, ~20,400 km network. Founded 1872. Concession extended to 2049. Controlled by [[Compass Gas e Energia]] ([[Cosan]]). Ticker: CGAS3 ([[B3]]).
