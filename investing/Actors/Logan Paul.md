# Logan Paul

YouTuber, WWE wrestler, and entrepreneur. Co-founded [[Prime Hydration]] with KSI in 2022, which peaked at $1.2B in annual revenue before a sharp decline driven by fading novelty and distribution pullbacks. Also co-founded Lunchly (2024) with [[Beast Industries|MrBeast]] and KSI — a school lunch alternative brand that drew criticism for nutritional content. ~23M YouTube subscribers. Paul's business trajectory illustrates both the power and fragility of the [[Influencer-to-brand playbook]]: explosive initial demand driven by audience conversion, followed by a retention problem once the novelty premium fades. His CryptoZoo NFT project (2021) resulted in a class-action lawsuit and FBI investigation, further complicating his brand.
