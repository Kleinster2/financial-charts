# Denison Mines

**Denison Mines (DNN)** — Canadian uranium developer headquartered in Toronto, listed on the NYSE American (DNN) and TSX (DML). Its flagship is the Wheeler River project (Phoenix deposit), a high-grade in-situ-recovery (ISR) development in the Athabasca Basin, Saskatchewan, alongside a stake in the McClean Lake mill and a holding of physical uranium. Pre-production, with Phoenix targeting first output in the late 2020s. A development-stage member of the [[Uranium equity beta]] cohort, tightly paired with [[NexGen Energy]] (0.89) as the two Athabasca developers.
