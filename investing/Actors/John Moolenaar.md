## China Committee actions

**NVIDIA-DeepSeek letter (Jan 28, 2026):**
- Sent letter to Commerce Secretary Lutnick with documents obtained from [[NVIDIA]]
- Alleged technical assistance helped [[DeepSeek]] achieve training efficiency
- Internal docs showed DeepSeek-V3 required "only 2.788M H800 GPU hours — less than US developers typically require"
- Acknowledged NVIDIA treated DeepSeek as "legitimate commercial partner" at the time
- Argued: if even NVIDIA "cannot rule out military use," rigorous export licensing essential

**Committee focus areas:**
- [[Export controls]] on semiconductors to [[China]]
- CCP influence in US institutions
- [[TikTok]] ban advocacy
- Supply chain security
