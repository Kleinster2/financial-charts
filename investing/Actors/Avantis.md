**Avantis Investors** --- Investment division of [[American Century]] Investment Management, offering systematic, factor-tilted ETFs. ~$50B in AUM, 90%+ in ETFs. One of the fastest-growing fund companies in the US, built on the intellectual tradition of [[Dimensional]] (several founders came from DFA).
