**Quantum Systems** — Munich-based reconnaissance-drone maker founded 2015 by [[Florian Seibel]]. Focuses on ISR (intelligence, surveillance, reconnaissance) drones for military and civilian use. Investor resistance to weaponization led Seibel to spin out [[Stark]] in 2024 as a separate vehicle for strike products.

## Role in German drone ecosystem

Quantum Systems is the ISR half of Seibel's two-company strategy. [[Stark]] took the weapons mandate; Quantum keeps the reconnaissance mission. Both supply the [[Bundeswehr]] and both are scaling into Ukraine's operational demand.
