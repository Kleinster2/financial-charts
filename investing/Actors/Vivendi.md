**Vivendi** — French media conglomerate controlled by [[Bolloré Group]]. Split into four companies in Dec 2024: Canal+, Havas, Louis Hachette Group, and Vivendi holdco. Previously owned [[Universal Music Group]].

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Breakup unlocks value

- Havas digital transformation
- Bolloré = aggressive capital allocation

**Bear:**
- Bolloré control (governance)
- Canal+ cord-cutting exposure
- Havas commoditized
- Political/reputational risk
