# DÔEN

California-based women's fashion brand. Romantic aesthetic inspired by coastal California nostalgia. Known for quality knitwear and cashmere.

## Business

- **Founded:** 2016 by sisters Margaret and Katherine Kleveland
- **Headquarters:** Los Angeles
- **Growth:** ~40% YoY
- **Private company**

## Product

Started cottage-core (prairie dresses, feminine blouses), evolved into broader fashion:
- Cashmere knitwear (hand crochet, shell beading details)
- Occasion wear (silk, tulle)
- Swim, lingerie-inspired pieces
- Using Italian and English wools

**Price points:** $200-$800 knitwear, higher for occasion wear.

## Retail

| Channel | Status |
|---------|--------|
| DTC (shopdoen.com) | Primary — majority of sales |
| Own stores | 5 locations (incl. Sag Harbor) |
| Expansion | 15 more planned (Upper East Side next) |
| Wholesale | Selective — Net-a-Porter, Saks |
