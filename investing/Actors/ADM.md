#actor #agriculture #commodities #public

**ADM** (NYSE: ADM) — one of the world's largest agricultural processors and food ingredient companies. Major soybean crusher and biodiesel producer, supplying soybean oil to the [[Biodiesel (FAME)|biodiesel]] and [[Renewable fuels|renewable fuel]] industries. Also produces bioethanol from corn.
