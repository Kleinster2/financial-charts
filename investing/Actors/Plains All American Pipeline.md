#actor #energy #midstream #pipeline

**Plains All American Pipeline** (NASDAQ: PAA) is a publicly traded midstream energy company that owns and operates crude oil and NGL pipeline, gathering, and storage assets across the US and Canada. Responsible for the May 2015 Refugio Beach oil spill — a rupture of Pipeline 324 near Santa Barbara that released ~120,000+ gallons of crude onto 3,700 acres of coastline. Convicted on nine criminal counts; paid $3.3M in criminal fines, $61M in civil penalties, and $230M in lawsuit settlements.
