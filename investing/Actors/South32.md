# South32

**South32** is a diversified mining and metals company spun off from BHP in 2015. Major alumina producer (Worsley refinery in Western Australia) and significant consumer of [[Caustic Soda]] for the Bayer process. Also produces manganese, nickel, silver, lead, and zinc.
