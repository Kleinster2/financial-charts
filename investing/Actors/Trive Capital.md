# Trive Capital

**Trive Capital** is a Dallas-based private equity firm focused on middle-market businesses. Controlling shareholder of [[Karman Holdings]] at IPO (Feb 2025). The firm assembled Karman through a roll-up of defense and space subsystem manufacturers over several years.
