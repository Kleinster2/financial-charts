# Trive Capital

**Trive Capital** is a Dallas-based private equity firm focused on middle-market businesses, primarily in industrials, business services, and defense/aerospace subsystems. The firm manages over $5B in committed capital across multiple funds and specializes in buy-and-build strategies where it assembles fragmented niche players into scaled platforms.

## Synopsis

Trive's marquee achievement is the creation of [[Karman Holdings]], a defense and space subsystem platform assembled through a multi-year roll-up of specialized manufacturers — aerostructures, energetics, composite components, and propulsion systems. Trive took Karman public in February 2025, retaining a controlling stake at IPO. The Karman playbook exemplifies Trive's core strategy: identify a fragmented industrial subsector where small manufacturers serve defense primes, acquire 5-10 companies at reasonable multiples, integrate operations for cross-selling and procurement leverage, then exit via IPO or strategic sale at a platform premium.

Beyond Karman, Trive's portfolio spans industrial distribution, specialty chemicals, and business services — sectors where the same buy-and-build thesis applies. The firm targets companies with $15-75M EBITDA and typically takes controlling positions with active operational involvement. The Dallas base gives Trive proximity to the Texas defense and energy ecosystems, where deal flow for industrial middle-market companies is dense. The risk in Trive's model is integration execution — roll-ups destroy value when acquisitions are poorly integrated or when platform multiples compress before exit.
