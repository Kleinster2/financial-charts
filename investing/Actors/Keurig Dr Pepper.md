**Keurig Dr Pepper** -- US beverage company formed from 2018 merger of Keurig Green Mountain and [[Dr Pepper Snapple]]. Coffee (K-Cups, single-serve) + soft drinks (Dr Pepper, 7UP, Snapple, Canada Dry). Agreed Aug 2025 to acquire [[JDE Peet's]] for EUR15.7B ($18.4B), then separate into Beverage Co. and Global Coffee Co. [[JAB Holding]] stake reduced from 56% to ~4.4%.
