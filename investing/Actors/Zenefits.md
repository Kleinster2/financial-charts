#actor #software #hr-tech #private #usa

Cloud-based HR platform for SMBs. Co-founded by [[Parker Conrad]] and Laks Srini in 2013. Reached ~$4.5B valuation before a 2016 compliance scandal (unlicensed insurance brokers) led to Conrad's resignation and regulatory fines. Restructured under new leadership, later acquired by TriNet in 2022.
