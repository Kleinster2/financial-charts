#actor #private #media #internet

[[Pornhub]] — world's largest pornographic video-sharing website, owned by [[Aylo]] (formerly MindGeek). Founded 2007 by Matt Keezer under Interhub. ~4 billion monthly visits globally (Semrush, Oct 2025). Blocked in 23 US states, France, and the UK over age verification laws as of early 2026. [[Visa]] and [[Mastercard]] suspended payment processing in December 2020 following [[New York Times]] exposé on CSAM and non-consensual content. Subject to FTC consent order (Sep 2025) and DOJ deferred prosecution agreement (Dec 2023).
