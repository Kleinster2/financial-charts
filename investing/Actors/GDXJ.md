# GDXJ

GDXJ (NYSE Arca: GDXJ) is the VanEck Junior Gold Miners ETF — a basket of small- and mid-cap gold (and silver) miners, more volatile and higher-beta to the gold price than the large-cap [[GDX]]. Launched 2009.

## Use case

A second gold-miner control for the [[Precious metals royalties#Cluster validation|royalty cluster validation]] — the junior-miner counterpart to [[GDX]]. The royalty cohort's negative intra-advantage versus the GDX/GDXJ pair confirms the royalties trade as part of the gold-miner complex, not a separable factor. See [[Precious metals royalties]].
