# Genius Sports

Genius Sports (NYSE: GENI) is a listed B2B sports-data and technology supplier — official data rights, live streaming, and integrity services — best known for its exclusive [[NFL]] data distribution deal. A picks-and-shovels supplier to the [[Sports betting]] industry rather than an operator.
