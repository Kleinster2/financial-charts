# Unilever

**Anglo-Dutch consumer goods giant** — €60.8B revenue (2024, +4.2% underlying). Five business groups: Beauty & Wellbeing (€13.2B), Personal Care (€14.4B), [[Home]] Care, Nutrition, Ice Cream. Beauty & Wellbeing grew +6.5%. Key brands: Dove (40% of Personal Care), Axe/Lynx, TRESemmé, Vaseline. Prestige beauty portfolio: Hourglass, Tatcha, Paula's Choice, K18. **Separating Ice Cream division by end of 2025** (Ben & Jerry's, Magnum). ~€120B market cap.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Beauty & Wellbeing +6.5% — above-market growth
- Prestige portfolio (Hourglass, Tatcha) growing double-digits
- Dove mega-brand with pricing power
- Ice Cream separation simplifies story
- ~3% dividend yield, defensive characteristics
- K18 acquisition adds biotech haircare growth

**Bear:**
- Conglomerate discount — too many categories
- Food/Nutrition slower growth (~2%)
- Prestige slowing (US beauty market soft)
- Ben & Jerry's brand controversies
- Competition from [[L'Oréal]], [[Estée Lauder]], [[P&G]]
- Emerging market currency headwinds
