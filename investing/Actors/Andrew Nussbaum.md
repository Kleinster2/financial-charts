# Andrew Nussbaum

M&A specialist at [[Wachtell Lipton]]. Co-chair of the firm's executive committee since 2023, alongside [[William Savitt]]. In his sixties.

In late February 2026, Nussbaum sent a firm-wide email noting Wachtell had advised on transactions worth $230bn announced in the preceding five days *and* won a landmark corporate-governance case at Delaware's Supreme Court — quoted in the FT (May 2026) as "Only our firm can do this." The same week, two partners left.
