#actor #automotive #manufacturing #italy #public

Pirelli — Global premium tire manufacturer. EUR 6.77B revenue (2024). Exclusive [[Formula 1]] tyre supplier through 2027. Listed on Milan Stock Exchange. Complex ownership: [[Sinochem]] holds 37% but Italian government's "Golden Power" decree effectively stripped control; Marco Tronchetti Provera (~26.4%) is de facto leader.
