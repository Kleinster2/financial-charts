# Chang Guang Satellite Technology

[[China|Chinese]] commercial satellite operator behind the Jilin-1 earth-observation constellation. The [[Financial Times]] reported in 2025 that Chang Guang provided satellite imagery to the Iran-backed [[Houthis]] to help them target US warships and commercial shipping in the Red Sea. This was the first publicly documented case of a Chinese commercial-space firm supplying targeting-relevant imagery to a US-designated adversary; [[Earth Eye Co]] / [[TEE-01B]] is the second (see [[IRGC Chinese satellite targeting March 2026]]). Chang Guang has ties to the Chinese military documented in US House China Select Committee reporting. See [[Chinese dual-use commercial satellite exports]] for the framework view.
