**UK Sovereign AI Fund** — UK government fund for direct equity investment in AI companies with UK presence. Established under the 2025 implementation of [[Matt Clifford]]'s AI Opportunities Action Plan. First major position: $1.1B seed of [[Ineffable Intelligence]] (Apr 2026) and $2.1B Series B of [[Isomorphic Labs]] (May 2026).

## Significance

UKSAIF gives the UK a direct equity-investor presence in [[DeepMind diaspora]] rounds — a formal mechanism for the UK to participate in the upside of London-based alumni-founded AI labs. Position-taking alongside [[NVIDIA]], [[Sequoia Capital]], and [[Google]] in the Ineffable seed signals that UK AI sovereignty policy has translated into a working capital vehicle, not just a policy document.
