# Meta Superintelligence Labs

[[Meta]]'s rebuilt AI division, formed June 2025 after the [[Llama]] 4 stumble and the hiring of [[Alexandr Wang]] as Chief AI Officer. Replaces the prior Llama-era research structure under [[Yann LeCun]] (who departed to start [[AMI Labs]]). Mission framed as delivering "personal superintelligence" at consumer scale across Meta's 3B+ user base.

Meta also took a minority stake in NFDG (Friedman/Gross's $1.1B AI fund) as part of the package.

## Mandate

- Ground-up rebuild of Meta's model stack, distinct from [[Llama]] architecture and data pipelines.
- First output: [[Muse Spark]] — proprietary, not open-weights, breaking with Meta's prior open-source playbook.
- Reasoning-focused model line (contemplating mode, multimodal).
- Ship across Meta AI app, [[WhatsApp]], [[Instagram]], Messenger, Ray-Ban Meta glasses.
