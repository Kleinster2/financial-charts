## Why VMware mattered

Created mainstream virtualization:

- **First x86 virtualization**: Made Intel servers run multiple OS simultaneously
- **Server utilization**: Transformed from ~15% to 80%+ utilization
- **Cloud foundation**: VMware tech underlies most enterprise clouds
- **vSphere dominance**: De facto enterprise virtualization standard

## Broadcom acquisition (2023)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Announced | May 26, 2022 |
| Closed | Nov 22, 2023 |
| Price | $69B (cash + stock + $8B debt) |
| Delay | Chinese regulatory approval |
| Layoffs | 2,800+ employees post-close |
| HQ move | Broadcom moved to VMware's Palo Alto campus |

**Post-acquisition restructuring:**
- Four divisions: Cloud Foundation, Tanzu, Software-Defined Edge, App Networking & Security
