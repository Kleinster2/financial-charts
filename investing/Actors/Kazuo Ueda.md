# Kazuo Ueda

Governor of the [[BOJ]] since April 2023. Ended negative interest rates with first hike in 17 years (Mar 2024). Navigating [[Japan]]'s transition from deflation-fighting to inflation management. Policy rate at 0.75% as of Feb 2026.
