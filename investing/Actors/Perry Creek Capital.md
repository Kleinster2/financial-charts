# Perry Creek Capital

**Perry Creek Capital** is a US-based investment firm with a focus on technology and frontier sectors, notable for its investment in [[Bedrock Robotics]].

## Synopsis

Perry Creek Capital operates in the growth-stage technology investment space, with portfolio exposure to robotics and AI — sectors where capital requirements are high and the gap between research-stage and commercial viability defines returns. The firm's investment in [[Bedrock Robotics]] places it in the emerging robotics ecosystem where autonomous construction and infrastructure maintenance are being automated. This is a capital-intensive vertical where the technology risk is significant but the addressable market (global construction spending exceeds $10T annually) justifies patient capital deployment.

The firm's profile fits a pattern common in frontier tech investing: smaller, specialized funds that take early positions in companies building physical-world AI applications, before the sector matures enough to attract megafund attention. Perry Creek's willingness to invest in robotics at the pre-scale stage — when unit economics are unproven and regulatory frameworks barely exist — suggests a thesis aligned with the broader conviction that foundation models will accelerate the path from lab prototype to commercial deployment in physical automation.
