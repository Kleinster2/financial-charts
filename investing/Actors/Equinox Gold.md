# Equinox Gold

Equinox Gold (NYSE American / TSX: EQX) is an Americas-focused diversified gold producer headquartered in Vancouver, with operating mines across the US, Mexico, [[Brazil]], and Canada. Its June 2025 merger with Calibre Mining reshaped it into one of the larger Americas producers, anchored by two long-life Canadian mines — Greenstone (Ontario) and Valentine (Newfoundland). Used here as an Americas mid-tier gold peer benchmark for [[Aura Minerals]].

## Overview

The Calibre merger added the ramping Greenstone mine and the Valentine project, turning Equinox into an Americas producer spanning five countries, with the two Canadian mines expected to average ~590koz/yr combined at capacity. It sits in the same Americas gold cohort as [[Aura Minerals]] at roughly 4x Aura's current production scale, which is why it anchors the peer comparison in [[Aura Minerals securities note]].
