**UBS** — Swiss multinational bank. World's largest wealth manager. $6.9T invested assets (Q3 2025). $1.63T total assets. HQ Zurich/Basel. Acquired [[Credit Suisse]] for CHF 3B in emergency rescue (Mar 2023). CEO Sergio Ermotti returned to lead integration. Target: $5T+ wealth AUM by 2028.

## Credit Suisse acquisition (2023)

| Fact | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Announced | 19 March 2023 (emergency weekend deal) |
| Price | **CHF 3B** ($3.2B) — 60% discount to book |
| Completed | 12 June 2023 |
| Negative goodwill | $35B (accounting gain) |
| Swiss government backstop | Up to CHF 9B loss protection |
| Integration timeline | 4 years (through ~2027) |

### Integration milestones

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| Apr 2023 | Sergio Ermotti returns as CEO |
| Jun 2023 | Acquisition closes |
| May 2024 | Credit Suisse AG merged into UBS AG |
| Jul 2024 | Swiss entities merged |
| 2025 | Client migration to UBS platform (ongoing) |
| Apr 2025 | India service entities merged (24,000 staff) |

### Cost savings

Decommissioned 1,100+ legacy apps in 2025. Targeting billions in cost synergies through 2027.
