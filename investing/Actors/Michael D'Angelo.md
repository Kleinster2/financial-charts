# Michael D'Angelo

**Michael D'Angelo** — CTO & co-founder of [[Promptfoo]], the open-source AI security testing platform acquired by [[OpenAI]] (Mar 2026). Former VP of Engineering & Head of ML at Smile Identity (identity verification APIs serving 160M+ people across Africa). Co-founded Arthena (quantitative art investment platform, acquired by [[Masterworks]]) and Matroid. BS Electrical/Computer Engineering, University at Buffalo; MS Computational & Mathematical Engineering, Stanford.
