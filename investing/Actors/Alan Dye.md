# Alan Dye

Former Apple VP of Human Interface Design — one of the company's most senior software-interface designers, working in the lineage of the [[Jony Ive]] design organization. Departed Apple in December 2025. His exit is cited ([[Financial Times]], May 2026) alongside AI chief [[John Giannandrea]]'s as evidence of a depleted design/AI bench just as [[John Ternus]] takes over and Apple ramps its AI catch-up.
