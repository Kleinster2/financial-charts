#stub

[[BASF]] is the world's largest chemical producer by revenue. German multinational operating across chemicals, materials, industrial solutions, surface technologies, nutrition and care, and agricultural solutions. Integrated sulfuric acid production and consumption across multiple chemical operations.

## Iran conflict impact (March 2026)

Announced 30% price hikes on some products from Ludwigshafen Verbund (Mar 19) — driven by surging feedstock (naphtha) costs from the [[Strait of Hormuz]] closure. One of the first concrete examples of energy cost passthrough from the conflict into European industrial output. Petrochemical feedstock shortages may force curtailment if the disruption persists.

*Source: Bloomberg TV (Will Kennedy, Mar 19, 2026)*
