#actor #crypto #exchange

**Bitstamp** — Luxembourg-based cryptocurrency exchange, one of the longest-running in the industry (founded 2011). Acquired by Robinhood in 2024 for ~$200M. Delisted [[Tether|USDT]] for EEA users in compliance with [[MiCA]] regulations (Jul 2025).
