**Aguassanta** — Family office and investment vehicle of [[Rubens Ometto|Rubens Ometto Silveira Mello]], controlling shareholder of [[Cosan]]. Holds ~53.5% voting capital in Cosan via Aguassanta / Vertiz Holding structure. Committed R$500M to the Mar 2026 [[Raizen]] rescue alongside [[Shell]]'s R$3.5B — the only Ometto/Cosan-side capital in the deal, as Cosan corporate contributed nothing.
