# Joe Steinberg

Chairman of [[Jefferies]]. Co-founded [[Leucadia National]] with [[Ian Cumming]] in 1979, compounding annual returns at ~20% for 34 years. [[Harvard Business School]] 1970. Previously at [[Carl Marks & Company]]. Took control of Talcott National Corporation (1979), renamed it Leucadia (1980), and built it into a $6.8B conglomerate through value investing. Became chairman of the combined entity after the 2013 Jefferies-Leucadia merger.
