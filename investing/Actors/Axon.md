## Financials

![[axon-fundamentals.png]]
*Revenue growing 30%+ annually for three consecutive years. GAAP net income volatile due to heavy stock-based compensation and investment mark-to-market gains.*

### Annual

| Year | Revenue | Gross profit | Operating income | Net income |
|------|---------|-------------|-----------------|------------|
| FY2024 | $2.08B | $1.24B | $59M | $377M |
| FY2023 | $1.56B | $955M | $155M | $176M |
| FY2022 | $1.19B | $726M | $93M | $147M |

GAAP operating income collapsed in FY2024 ($155M → $59M) due to $131M in Q4 stock-based compensation alone. Adjusted EBITDA was $521M (25.0% margin), up from $329M. Net income more than doubled on investment gains (Dedrone mark-to-market).

### Quarterly

| Quarter | Revenue | YoY growth | Net income |
|---------|---------|-----------|------------|
| Q3 2025 | $711M | 31% | -$2M |
| Q2 2025 | $669M | 33% | $36M |
| Q1 2025 | $604M | 31% | $88M |
| Q4 2024 | $575M | 34% | -$16M |
| Q3 2024 | $544M | 32% | $67M |

FY2025 guidance: ~$2.74B revenue (~31% growth), ~25% adjusted EBITDA margin. Q3 2025 was first GAAP net loss in recent quarters — operating expenses +40% YoY and tariff headwinds.

## Business segments

Reorganized from "TASER" and "Software & Sensors" to "Connected Devices" and "Software & Services" effective Q1 2025.

| Segment | Products |
|---------|----------|
| Connected Devices | TASERs (TASER 10), body cameras, in-car video, drones |
| Software & Services | Evidence.com, records management, Axon 911, Draft One (AI) |

Cloud & services revenue: $806M in FY2024, up from $561M in FY2023. ARR trajectory: $697M (end FY2023) → $1.0B (end FY2024) → $1.3B (Q3 2025).
