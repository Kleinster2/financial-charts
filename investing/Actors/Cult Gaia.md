# Cult Gaia

Los Angeles-based lifestyle brand known for sculptural accessories. Famous for the Ark bag.

## Business

- **Founded:** 2012 by Jasmin Larian
- **Headquarters:** Los Angeles
- **Signature:** Ark bag (bamboo half-moon shape) — viral 2017
- **Private company**

## Product

| Category | Signature styles |
|----------|-----------------|
| Bags | Ark, Eos Clutch, Roksana |
| Clothing | Dresses, tops, ready-to-wear |
| Jewelry | Meta Earrings, sculptural pieces |
| Shoes | Ziggy Platform, sandals |

**Materials:** Natural materials — bamboo, wood, raffia, acrylic. Sustainability focus.

**Design philosophy:** Art, architecture, and nature-inspired. Sculptural shapes, heirloom quality.

## Retail

- DTC via cultgaia.com
- Wholesale: Nordstrom, FWRD, specialty boutiques

## Positioning

Fits [[Modern luxury]] — founder-led, Instagram-native, accessible price points ($200-$800). The Ark bag pioneered "Instagram It-bag" phenomenon without traditional fashion marketing.
