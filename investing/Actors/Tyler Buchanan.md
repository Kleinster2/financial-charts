#actor #person #cybercrime

**Tyler Buchanan** — 23-year-old Scottish national identified by US prosecutors as the alleged leader of the [[Scattered Spider]] cybercrime collective. Operated under the alias TylerB. Arrested by Spanish police in June 2024 while attempting to board a flight from Palma de Mallorca to Italy; ~$27M in Bitcoin seized at the time of arrest. Extradited to the United States in April 2025 to face charges of wire fraud, conspiracy, and aggravated identity theft. Pleaded guilty in April 2026 to major crypto-theft and conspiracy charges in US federal court. US prosecutors allege Buchanan and co-conspirators hacked into dozens of US and foreign companies and that he personally controlled more than $26M in stolen victim funds. Buchanan's operational role within Scattered Spider focused on social-engineering tradecraft and identity-provider compromise — the two capabilities the group uses to breach enterprise environments without conventional malware.
