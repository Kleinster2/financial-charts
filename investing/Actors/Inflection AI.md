**Inflection AI** — AI lab founded 2022 by [[Mustafa Suleyman]] (DeepMind co-founder), [[Reid Hoffman]] ([[LinkedIn]] co-founder, AI investor), and Karén Simonyan (ex-DeepMind researcher). Built Pi, a personal-AI assistant. Acquihired by [[Microsoft]] in Mar 2024 in a ~$650M structured deal that moved the leadership team and most staff to [[Microsoft AI]] without acquiring the company directly. The structure was widely read as designed to sidestep antitrust review.

## The Microsoft acquihire (Mar 2024)

[[Microsoft]] paid approximately $650M for a non-exclusive license to Inflection's models and a hiring package that brought [[Mustafa Suleyman]] (as CEO of newly-formed [[Microsoft AI]]), Karén Simonyan (Chief Scientist), and most of the technical team to Microsoft. Inflection the company continued to exist with a pivot to enterprise AI but stripped of its consumer-product leadership.

The structure was novel: rather than buying the company outright, Microsoft hired the team and licensed the IP. Investors received cash distributions from the licensing payment. The deal triggered FTC and CMA scrutiny — both later closed without enforcement action, but the structure became the template for subsequent acquihires (e.g., Google's reported approach to Character.AI later in 2024).

For the [[DeepMind diaspora]], the Inflection acquihire demonstrated that a major Big Tech buyer would absorb a frontier-lab founder team at scale — an exit precedent that informs the pricing of subsequent alumni-led labs.
