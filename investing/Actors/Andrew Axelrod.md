## Career path

| Period | Firm | Role |
|--------|------|------|
| ~2005-07 | [[Goldman Sachs]] | Principal |
| ~2007-09 | [[KKR]] | Analyst |
| ~2009-15 | [[Mount Kellett Capital]] | Joined at inception → Co-Head North America (2011) → Partner (2013) |
| 2015-present | [[Axar Capital]] | Co-Founder, CEO & CIO |

Mount Kellett was a global distressed/special situations firm ($7B+ AUM at peak) founded by Mark McGoldrick and Jason Maynard in 2008-09. Axelrod spent six years there, gaining hands-on restructuring experience across 80+ situations before launching Axar at age ~33 with $200M seed from [[Louis Bacon]] and [[Julian Robertson]].
