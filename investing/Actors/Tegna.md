Tegna Inc — U.S. broadcast television company operating local TV stations across the country. Formerly the broadcasting arm of Gannett (spun off in 2015). Being acquired by [[Nexstar Media Group]] for $3.54B in a deal approved by the [[FCC]] on March 19, 2026.

## Acquisition by Nexstar

The [[FCC]] approved [[Nexstar Media Group]]'s $3.54B acquisition of Tegna on March 19, 2026. The combined entity will operate 265 television stations in 44 states and D.C. — the largest local TV operator in the U.S. The [[FCC]] waived its 39% household cap to permit the deal. [[Nexstar Media Group|Nexstar]] agreed to divest six stations.

Eight Democratic-led states and [[DirecTV]] filed separate lawsuits on March 18, 2026, seeking to block the merger.
