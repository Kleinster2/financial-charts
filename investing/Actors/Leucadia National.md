# Leucadia National

[[Berkshire Hathaway]]-style conglomerate co-founded by [[Ian Cumming]] and [[Joe Steinberg]] in 1979 (as Talcott National Corporation, renamed Leucadia 1980). Compounded returns at ~20% annually for 34 years, reaching a net worth of $6.8B. Value investing playbook: buy distressed assets, fix, hold. Merged with [[Jefferies]] in November 2012 (its largest investment). Renamed Jefferies Financial Group in May 2018. The Leucadia entity no longer exists independently.
