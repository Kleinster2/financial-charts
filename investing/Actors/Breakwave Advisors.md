# Breakwave Advisors

Specialist ETF issuer focused on maritime freight exposure. Best known for [[BDRY]] (Breakwave Dry Bulk Shipping ETF) and [[BWET]] (Breakwave Tanker Shipping ETF) — both futures-based funds that give listed-market investors direct exposure to shipping rates. Founded and managed by [[John Kartsonas]]. Private firm.

## Products

## Kartsonas framing (April 2026)

On BWET's post-crisis thesis transition: "The story right now is war and disruption, but this could transition to a fundamental story" — citing concentrated tanker ownership ([[Sinokor Group]]), [[Russia]] / [[Iran]] sanctions shrinking the compliant fleet, and years of underinvestment. Defends the 3.5% fee: headline drops with scale, and cash collateral backing the futures earns interest that offsets costs.
