## Product

**XOS (XTEND Operating System)** — field-proven platform for remote operation of multiple aerial, ground, and maritime drones executing complex missions. Key selling points:
- Autonomous systems with remote-operation capabilities
- Multi-domain (air, ground, sea)
- Immediate operational readiness
- Marketed as **"low cost-per-kill"** systems
- Combat-proven in [[Israel]]
