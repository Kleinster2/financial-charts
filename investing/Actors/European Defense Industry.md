## Spending dynamics

### By country

| Country | 2024 spend | % GDP | NATO target |
|---------|------------|-------|-------------|
| UK | ~$75B | 2.3% | 2.5% target |
| Germany | ~$80B | 2.1% | Catching up |
| France | ~$60B | 2.1% | Nuclear + conventional |
| Poland | ~$35B | 4%+ | Highest in NATO |
| Italy | ~$30B | 1.5% | Below target |

### Rearmament drivers

- Ukraine war exposed stockpile gaps
- US reliability concerns
- "Strategic autonomy" push
- NATO 2%+ commitments

See [[European defense spending]].

## Munitions ramp

EU artillery production expanding massively:

| Metric | Pre-war | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| 155mm shells/year | 230K | **3M** (by decade end) |

Key beneficiary: **Rheinmetall** (largest European munitions producer).
