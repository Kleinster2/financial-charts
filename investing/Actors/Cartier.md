**Cartier** — French luxury jewelry and watch maison, owned by [[Richemont]]. Richemont's largest and most profitable brand by far, anchoring the Jewellery Maisons segment. Known for Love bracelet, Tank watch, Santos, and Panthère collections. Founded 1847, often called "the jeweler of kings."
