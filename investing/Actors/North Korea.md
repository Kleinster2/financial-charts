**North Korea** -- nuclear-armed state and ballistic missile proliferator. Key to the investing vault as the original technology source for [[Iran]]'s missile program (Scud variants, BM-25/Musudan → [[Khorramshahr]]) and as the geopolitical reference case for nuclear breakout under sanctions. The "North Korea model" -- where nuclear weapons provide regime survival insurance against military attack -- is the explicit argument inside [[Iran]]'s post-war hardliner faction for pursuing a nuclear weapon.
