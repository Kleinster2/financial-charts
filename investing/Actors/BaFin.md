BaFin (Bundesanstalt fur Finanzdienstleistungsaufsicht) -- [[Germany]]'s federal financial supervisory authority. Supervises banks, insurance companies, and securities trading. Oversees the BVR cooperative banking protection scheme. Independent federal agency under the [[Germany|German]] Federal Ministry of Finance. HQ: Bonn and Frankfurt.
