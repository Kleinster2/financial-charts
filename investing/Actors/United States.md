# United States

Largest economy globally. GDP ~$29T (2025E), growth slowing to ~1.7-2.0%. Inflation persistent at ~3% CPI (Sep 2025), above the [[Federal Reserve]]'s 2% target. Fed holding rates steady — Deloitte expects no cuts until Dec 2026. Unemployment rising (4.6% Nov 2025, up from 4.0% in 2024). Tariff policy under [[Trump II]] reshaping trade relationships and adding inflationary pressure.

## Tariffs and trade

[[Trump II]] tariff policy is a defining macro variable. Key impacts:
- ~$100M additional cost estimated for [[Under Armour]] alone in FY26
- Tariffs increasingly passed through to consumer prices
- India secured interim reduction (50% → 18%, Feb 2026)
- [[China]] tariffs remain elevated, driving supply chain restructuring

IEEPA tariffs struck down by Supreme Court (Feb 20, 2026) — see [[SCOTUS IEEPA ruling]]
