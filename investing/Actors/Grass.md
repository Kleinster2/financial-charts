**Grass** — Decentralized network operated by Wynd Labs where users share idle internet bandwidth in exchange for GRASS tokens. The network uses that bandwidth to scrape and structure public web data, then sells the data to AI labs as training corpora. As of March 2026, Grass reported 8.5M monthly active users, ~20 paying AI-lab clients, $247M market cap — one of the more operationally real projects in the decentralized-AI data layer.

## Relevance

Primary example of the data layer of the [[Decentralized AI landscape]]. Unlike pure speculation projects, Grass has two sources of commercial reality: (a) 8.5M users generating consistent bandwidth supply, (b) real AI-lab buyers paying for structured web data. Natural peer to [[Vana]] (user-owned data DAOs) and surviving [[ASI Alliance]] data components.
