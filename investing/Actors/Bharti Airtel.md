## Nxtra in Google Vizag AI hub

[[Nxtra]] by Airtel is a named local infrastructure partner for [[Google Cloud]]'s $15B Visakhapatnam AI hub, alongside [[Adani Group]]'s [[AdaniConneX]]. Google says the project includes three data-center campuses, an expanded fiber-optic network, cable-landing infrastructure, and a long-term clean-energy strategy.

For Bharti Airtel, the read-through is that telecom infrastructure is becoming part of the AI data-center stack: low-latency fiber, cable landing, data-center operations, and enterprise connectivity sit next to power and construction. The upside is local infrastructure capture; the caveat is that the model and software rents remain with Google unless [[India AI]] develops domestic capability.

*Sources: Google Cloud press release, Apr 28 2026; [[Rohit Lamba]] / [[Raghuram Rajan]], May 7 2026.*
