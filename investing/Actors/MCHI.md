# MCHI

iShares MSCI China ETF — tracks the MSCI China Index, providing broad exposure to Chinese equities across all share classes (A-shares, H-shares, B-shares, Red Chips, P Chips, ADRs). Holds 600+ names versus [[FXI]]'s concentrated 50-stock portfolio, making MCHI the more diversified China vehicle. AUM ~$5B. Top holdings typically include [[Tencent]], [[Alibaba]], [[Meituan]], [[PDD Holdings]], and [[JD.com]]. The ETF captures the full spectrum of China's investable equity market, including mainland A-shares via Stock Connect — a key differentiator from older China ETFs that only held Hong Kong-listed shares. Expense ratio of 0.59% is high relative to US equity ETFs but standard for single-country EM exposure.
