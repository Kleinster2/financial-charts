# John Hegeman

Co-founder of [[Ithaca Holdings]]. Former Chief Revenue Officer at [[Meta]] (2007-2025, 17 years). Board member at [[Robinhood]].

## Career at Meta

- **2007-2014:** Engineering leader on Meta's ads system (7 years). Built the technical foundation of what became a $160B+ annual revenue machine
- **2014-2021:** Led Facebook Feed and other core product areas (5 years). Oversaw algorithmic feed ranking during peak growth
- **2021-2024:** Led monetization strategy as VP, expanded into new ad formats
- **Dec 2024:** Named CRO after role was unfilled since David Fischer's 2021 departure
- **Nov 18, 2025:** Announced departure to launch own company. Cited "mix of emotions." Responsibilities transferred to Andrew Bocking (ads product/strategy) and Naomi Gleit (business AI)

Departure came amid Meta's aggressive AI pivot — Zuckerberg reorganizing around Superintelligence Labs, hiring Alexandr Wang from [[Scale AI]]. Clara Shih (business AI) also departed around the same time.
