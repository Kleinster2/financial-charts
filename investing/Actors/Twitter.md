# Twitter

Pre-acquisition name of the social-media platform now operated as part of [[Elon Musk]]'s X / [[xAI]] umbrella. Retained as a vault entity for historical events that occurred under the Twitter name — most consequentially the 2022 hostile-bid saga, in which [[Wachtell Lipton]] represented the Twitter board, including the April 2022 [[Poison pill]] adoption in response to Musk's hostile tender offer, then the subsequent litigation forcing Musk to complete the acquisition at $54.20/share. Wachtell collected a $90M project-based fee against time sheets that suggested under $30M; X subsequently sued Wachtell to recover the gap, then abandoned the case under Musk in 2024.
