## Why Neste matters

Neste is the global benchmark for SAF production at scale. While most refiners are still piloting SAF, Neste has been producing renewable fuels commercially for over a decade. Their HEFA (hydroprocessed esters and fatty acids) pathway uses waste and residue feedstocks — used cooking oil, animal fat waste, and other residues. Refineries in Porvoo (Finland), Rotterdam (Netherlands), and Singapore give global coverage.
