#actor #bank #italy

Banco BPM -- Italy's third-largest bank by assets. Formed in 2017 from merger of Banco Popolare and Banca Popolare di Milano. Subject of competing interest from [[UniCredit]] (unsolicited EUR 10.1B bid, Nov 2024) and [[Crédit Agricole]] (19.8% strategic stake). 39% owner of Agos (consumer finance JV with CA).
