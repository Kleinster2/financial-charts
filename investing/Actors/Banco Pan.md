**Banco Pan** is a Brazilian digital and consumer-credit bank (vehicle financing, consignado, cards and a digital account), historically controlled by [[BTG Pactual]]. In a late-2025 reorganization BTG moved to absorb its remaining minority and delist the shares.

Price chart unavailable and fundamentals chart data unavailable — Banco Pan is being delisted (absorbed into [[BTG Pactual]]), so it is no longer an independently traded instrument.

## What happened

- BTG Pactual was already Pan's controller. In October 2025 BTG proposed a corporate reorganization turning Banco Pan into an indirect BTG subsidiary.
- Each Pan share is exchanged for 0.2128 of a BTG Pactual unit (a ~30% premium to the pre-announcement price); dissenting holders could take book value of R$6.64 per Pan share.
- Extraordinary general meetings were held on 9 December 2025; on completion Banco Pan leaves B3's Level 1 and cancels its registration as a publicly traded company (subject to shareholder and Central Bank approval).
- The practical result: `BPAN4` ceases trading and Pan's economics fold into [[BTG Pactual]] (`BPAC11`). Listed as an instrument only for the record; the live exposure is now BTG.
