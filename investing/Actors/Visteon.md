# Visteon

Visteon (Nasdaq: VC) is a Tier-1 automotive supplier focused on cockpit electronics — digital instrument clusters, infotainment displays, and the SmartCore domain controllers. A pure cockpit-electronics play (it exited other segments), levered to the digitalization-of-the-cabin content trend. A member of the [[Tier-1 auto suppliers]] cohort.
