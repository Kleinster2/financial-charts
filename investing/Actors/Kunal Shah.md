# Kunal Shah

Co-CEO of [[Goldman Sachs]] International and global co-head of fixed income, currencies, and commodities (FICC). Based in London, Shah oversees Goldman's international operations alongside [[Richard Gnodde]]. Rose through the FICC division, which has been Goldman's most consistent revenue engine — generating $14.7B in FY2024 trading revenue. Shah became a public-facing figure in March 2026 when he expressed being "glad" about [[Private Credit Software Concentration]] on a client call, highlighting Goldman's view that private credit's growing scale creates systemic software infrastructure demand that benefits Goldman's markets business.
