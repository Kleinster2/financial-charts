# Frame

American fashion brand blending California ease with Parisian sophistication. Known for premium denim.

## Business

- **Founded:** 2012 by Jens Grede and Erik Torstensson (Swedish entrepreneurs, ex-Saturday Group)
- **Headquarters:** Los Angeles
- **Revenue:** $100M+ (as of recent reporting)
- **Private company**

## Product

Started with denim ("Le Skinny de Jeanne" — worn by Karlie Kloss, Miranda Kerr), expanded to full ready-to-wear:
- Denim (core)
- Cotton, leather, cashmere pieces
- Handbags

## Retail

| Channel | Presence |
|---------|----------|
| Own stores | 14 North America, 1 London |
| E-commerce | frame-store.com |
| Wholesale | Department stores, boutiques worldwide |

## Founders

Jens Grede also co-founded:
- SKIMS (with Kim Kardashian)
- Good American (with Khloé Kardashian)

Pattern of building celebrity-adjacent brands in fashion/denim.

## Positioning

Premium denim positioned between fast fashion and luxury. Sustainable manufacturing commitment.
