## Why Elea matters

Building Latin America's largest DC campus in Rio de Janeiro:

- **Rio AI City**: 3.2GW potential — announced April 2025 at Web Summit Rio
- **Nationwide footprint**: 9 interconnected campuses across Brazil's critical cities
- **Acquisition-driven growth**: Rolled up [[TIM Brasil|TIM]], [[Globo]], [[Oi]], [[DXC Technology|DXC]] assets
- **Goldman-backed**: Institutional capital for hyperscale buildout

## Investment thesis

**Bull case:**
- Rio AI City = largest LatAm DC campus
- Goldman backing provides capital for hyperscale
- Oracle partnership signals hyperscaler demand
- Brazil AI demand explosion
- Acquisition pipeline (telecom divestitures)

**Bear case:**
- Private = no direct investment path
- Execution risk on 3.2GW buildout
- Competing with well-capitalized Scala
- Brazil macro/currency volatility
