Wood Mackenzie is a global energy, chemicals, renewables, metals, and mining research and consultancy firm headquartered in Edinburgh. Founded in 1973 as a division of wood brokers Wood & Co. Acquired by [[Veritas Capital]] from [[Verisk Analytics]] in October 2022 for $3.1B. CEO: Jason Liu. ~2,610 employees (as of December 2025).

Products span data analytics, consulting, and advisory across the full energy value chain: upstream oil & gas, downstream/refining, LNG, power & renewables, chemicals, metals & mining. Clients include IOCs, NOCs, governments, utilities, and financial institutions. Frequently cited alongside [[IHS Markit]] (now part of [[S&P Global]]) and [[Rystad Energy]] as a top-tier energy research provider.

Former employer of [[Paul Sankey]] (1995-2000), where he developed expertise in the emerging global LNG trade before moving to [[Deutsche Bank]].
