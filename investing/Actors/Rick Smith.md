Rick Smith is the founder and CEO of [[Axon|Axon Enterprise]] (AXON), the company behind TASER devices and the dominant law enforcement body camera and digital evidence management platform. Co-founded the company as TASER International in 1993 with brother Thomas P. Smith after a friend was killed in a road rage shooting. The company's transformation from a single-product stun gun maker into a SaaS-driven public safety platform (Axon Evidence, Axon Records, Axon Fleet) is one of the most successful hardware-to-software pivots in defense/law enforcement tech. Smith has been CEO for over 30 years, an unusual tenure for a public company founder. Harvard BA (cum laude), University of Chicago MBA (honors).
