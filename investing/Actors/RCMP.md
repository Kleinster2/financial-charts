**RCMP** — Canada's federal and national police service, providing policing at the federal, provincial, and municipal levels across the country. One of the most recognized law enforcement agencies globally, with approximately 30,000 employees.

---

## Synopsis

The Royal Canadian Mounted Police serves as both Canada's FBI-equivalent for federal investigations and as the contracted police force for eight provinces, three territories, and roughly 150 municipalities. This dual mandate makes it one of the largest and most complex policing organizations in the world, with an annual budget exceeding CAD 5B. For investors in defense and security technology, the RCMP is a significant procurement channel — its scale means technology adoption decisions ripple across Canadian law enforcement.

The RCMP's relevance to the investing vault centers on its role as a customer for companies like [[Axon]], which supplies body-worn cameras and the Axon Evidence digital evidence management platform under a major federal contract. As police forces globally face pressure to adopt transparency technology (body cameras, AI-assisted dispatch, digital evidence chains), the RCMP's procurement patterns serve as a leading indicator of government security spending trends in [[Canada]] and across the Five Eyes alliance.
