# Filorga

**French anti-aging skincare brand** — pioneer in aesthetic medicine. Founded 1978 in Paris by Dr. Michel Tordjman. Acquired by [[Colgate-Palmolive]] in 2019 for $1.68B. Originally [[HLD]] portfolio company (2010-2019). Known for NCEF (cell regeneration) technology and professional-grade formulas.
