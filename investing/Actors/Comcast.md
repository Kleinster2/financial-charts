## Cable business challenges

| Metric | Trend |
|--------|-------|
| Video subs | Declining 5-7% YoY |
| Broadband subs | Flat to slight decline |
| ARPU | Rising (pricing power) |
| Competition | FWA (T-Mobile, Verizon), fiber |

**Response:** Focus on broadband, mobile bundling, Peacock.

## Versant spinoff

| Detail | Status |
|--------|--------|
| Announcement | Nov 2024 |
| New entity | **Versant** |
| Networks | USA, CNBC, Oxygen, E!, Golf, Syfy |
| **MS NOW** | MSNBC rebranded Nov 2025, goes to Versant |
| HQ | 229 West 43rd Street (ex-NY Times building) |
| Timeline | 2026 completion |
| Rationale | Separate declining linear from growth assets |

**Key change:** MSNBC rebranded to **MS NOW** (Nov 15, 2025) ahead of spinoff. NBC News stays with Comcast.

---

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Broadband still essential utility
- Peacock reaching scale
- Theme parks = experience economy
- Sports rights (NFL, Olympics)
- Cable spinoff unlocks value

**Bear:**
- Cord-cutting accelerating
- Broadband competition (fiber, FWA)
- Peacock still unprofitable
- Linear networks declining
- Heavy debt load
