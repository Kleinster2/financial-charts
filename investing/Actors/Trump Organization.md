Trump Organization — privately-held real-estate, hospitality, and licensing conglomerate founded by [[Donald Trump]]. Day-to-day operations during the second Trump term are run by [[Donald Trump Jr.]] and [[Eric Trump]] in the absence of a true blind trust. Headquartered at Trump Tower in New York City; principal post-2020 operating presence at Trump National Golf Club Jupiter and Mar-a-Lago in Florida.
