## What are cobots?

**Collaborative robots** work alongside humans without safety cages.

| vs. Traditional | Cobot advantage |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Safety cages required | Works near humans |
| Complex programming | Easy teach mode |
| High cost | Lower TCO |
| Fixed applications | Flexible redeployment |
