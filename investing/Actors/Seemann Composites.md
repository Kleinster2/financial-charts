# Seemann Composites

**Seemann Composites** and its sister company **Materials Sciences Corporation (MSC)** are manufacturers of advanced composite systems for maritime defense applications — submarines, UUVs/USVs, and strategic naval surface platforms. Acquired by [[Karman Holdings]] in February 2026, expanding Karman into the maritime defense market. Sid Charbonnet, president of Seemann and MSC, joined Karman's leadership team.
