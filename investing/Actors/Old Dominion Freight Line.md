# Old Dominion Freight Line

Old Dominion Freight Line (Nasdaq: ODFL) is the premier US less-than-truckload (LTL) carrier, known for best-in-class operating ratios, service quality, and density. The anchor of the [[Trucking and LTL]] cohort.
