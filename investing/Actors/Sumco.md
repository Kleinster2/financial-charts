## Pure-play wafer exposure

**Unlike [[Shin-Etsu]]:**
- 100% semiconductor silicon
- No PVC or chemicals hedge
- More volatile but more levered to wafer demand
- Higher beta to chip cycle

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Pure-play wafer leverage
- AI demand = wafer demand
- Shortage pricing power
- Duopoly with Shin-Etsu

**Bear:**
- Highly cyclical
- No diversification
- Yen volatility
- Customer concentration
