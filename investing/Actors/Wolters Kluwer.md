## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Sticky subscription revenue
- Compliance = non-discretionary spend
- Healthcare segment is defensive
- High recurring revenue %

**Bear:**
- AI disruption of legal/tax workflows
- Slower growth vs. pure software
- European headquarters (governance discount)
