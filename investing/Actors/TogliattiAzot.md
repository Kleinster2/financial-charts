# TogliattiAzot

[[Russia]]'s largest ammonia producer with ~3 Mt/year capacity, located in Togliatti, Samara Oblast. Operated the 2,417 km Togliatti-Odessa ammonia pipeline — the world's longest — until it was shut on February 24, 2022, following Russia's invasion of Ukraine. The pipeline section in Kharkiv Oblast was physically damaged in June 2023. Four of seven ammonia lines shuttered; remaining three at reduced rates.
