Celestial AI was a Santa Clara-based startup developing "Photonic Fabric" technology — optical interconnects designed to solve the memory bandwidth bottleneck in AI compute by using light instead of electrical signals to move data between compute, memory, and storage. Founded in 2020, the company raised over $250M in venture funding, including a $175M Series C in 2024 led by [[US Innovative Technology Fund]] with participation from [[Samsung]], [[Temasek]], and others.

Acquired by [[Marvell Technology]] as part of Marvell's strategy to build an end-to-end optical interconnect portfolio for AI data center infrastructure. Marvell announced the $3.25B cash-and-stock acquisition (with up to $5.5B total consideration including revenue-milestone earnouts) in December 2025; deal closed Feb 2, 2026. The acquisition paired Celestial's photonic fabric IP with Marvell's existing electro-optics and custom silicon capabilities, alongside Marvell's separate acquisition of [[XConn Technologies]] (chiplet interconnect). The combined portfolio positions Marvell to compete with [[Broadcom]] and [[Intel]] in the high-growth interconnect layer of the AI infrastructure stack — the segment [[Jensen Huang]] identifies as critical in the [[Jensen Five Layer Cake]] framework.

## Apr 27, 2026 — Marvell cancels POET Technologies purchase orders

[[Marvell Technology]], as post-acquisition counterparty on Celestial commercial agreements, sent [[POET Technologies]] notice on Apr 23, 2026 cancelling all purchase orders Celestial had placed with POET — including the initial production-unit orders first disclosed in April 2023. Cited basis: POET's disclosure of information related to the purchase order and shipping in contravention of confidentiality obligations. POET disclosed the cancellation Apr 27, 2026; POET stock fell ~46% on the day to ~$8. See [[POET Technologies]] for full event detail.

The cancellation is the first publicly visible commercial fallout from the Marvell-Celestial integration. Whether the cancellation reflects Marvell enforcement of confidentiality, a post-acquisition supply-chain reorganization (with confidentiality as legally clean grounds), or a roadmap deferral on the photonic-interposer scope is the open question.

*Source: POET Technologies press release Apr 27, 2026; Investing.com; Stocktwits.*
