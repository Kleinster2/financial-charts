**Gary Gensler** — Former [[SEC]] chair (2021-2025) and former [[CFTC]] chair (2009-2014). Defined the Biden-era crypto enforcement posture before the 2025-2026 Atkins/Selig pivot toward joint taxonomy and rulemaking.

For the vault, Gensler is the baseline case for "regulation by enforcement" in crypto. His SEC treated many tokens and intermediaries as securities-law problems before Congress or the agencies had written a clean market-structure framework, making the later [[SEC-CFTC Joint Crypto Taxonomy]] a direct reversal point.
