#actor #private #private-equity #canada

[[Ethical Capital Partners]] — Canadian private equity firm, owner of [[Aylo]] (Pornhub parent) since March 2023. Founded 2022 in Montreal by cannabis entrepreneur Rocco Meliambro (chairman) and criminal defense lawyer [[Fady Mansour]] (managing partner, 75%+ beneficial owner of voting shares). Acquired [[Aylo]] for $400M (seller-financed, down from ~$1.5B pre-scandal valuation). Team includes Solomon Friedman (VP Compliance, adjunct law professor at University of Ottawa) and Derek Ogden (VP Law Enforcement Relations).
