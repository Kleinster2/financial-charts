## Current context

The Bank of Russia's Apr. 24 2026 decision cut the key rate by 50 bp to 14.50% while warning that inflation expectations and external uncertainty remained elevated. In the May 2026 [[Russian ruble]] episode, the central bank's tight policy stance was one of the supports for RUB strength, alongside energy export receipts, weak imports, and capital-flow constraints.
