# Foreign Policy Research Institute

Philadelphia-based foreign-policy think tank founded 1955. Nonpartisan; focused on US foreign policy, national security, and historical context for contemporary geopolitical issues. Hosts the *Telegram* podcast and a substantial publishing footprint across US foreign-policy research, China-focused work, and military history. Notable contributors include [[Chris Miller]] (semiconductor-geopolitics), [[Jordan Schneider]] (China-tech), and a broader stable of regional specialists.
