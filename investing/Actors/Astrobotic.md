#actor #space #usa #private

Astrobotic — Pittsburgh-based lunar logistics company. NASA CLPS (Commercial Lunar Payload Services) provider. Part of [[Blue Origin]]'s [[Artemis]] V Human Landing System team alongside [[Lockheed Martin]], [[Boeing]], Draper, and Honeybee Robotics. Peregrine lander (Mission One, Jan 2024) suffered propulsion failure and did not reach the lunar surface. Griffin lander carries NASA's VIPER rover.
