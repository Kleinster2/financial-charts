Lucasfilm — the Star Wars and Indiana Jones studio acquired by [[Disney]] in 2012 for $4B. Bought from founder [[George Lucas]] as part of [[Bob Iger]]'s franchise-IP acquisition spree; Star Wars became a multibillion-dollar engine of box office, merchandise, and theme-park revenue (including the ~$1bn Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge park expansion).
