**Onsi Sawiris** is co-founder and partner of [[HOF Capital]], focusing on cloud-based technologies and enterprise software while leading portfolio acceleration through the firm's global LP network. In the founder trio, he is the clearest bridge between HOF's venture platform and the Sawiris/Orascom operating-capital network.

## Why he matters

Sawiris is the person who makes the "LP network as product" claim easiest to understand. HOF's official bio says he leads portfolio acceleration and helps founders secure commercial traction by identifying synergies inside the firm's global LP network. That is the core HOF distinction: founders are not only raising money from HOF, they are supposed to get customer and partnership access through the network behind HOF's funds.

His background also explains why HOF is not a conventional Sand Hill Road partnership. He studied mechanical engineering at [[MIT]], worked in TMT-focused M&A at [[Arma Partners]], and holds board roles that include [[Orascom Financial Holding]]. That makes him the network/enterprise-software partner rather than merely a named co-founder.
