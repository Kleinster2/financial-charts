#actor #chef #restaurants #nyc

**Lee Hanson** — [[New York City]] chef, co-founder of [[Frenchette Group]]. Trained at Le Cirque under [[Daniel Boulud]], then cooked at Aureole ([[Charlie Palmer]]) and Vong ([[Jean-Georges Vongerichten]]). Met [[Riad Nasr]] at Daniel in 1993. Co-executive chef at [[Keith McNally]]'s Balthazar (1997–2013). Launched Frenchette in Tribeca in 2018. 2025 James Beard Outstanding Restaurateur (with Nasr).
