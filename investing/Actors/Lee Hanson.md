# Lee Hanson

[[New York City]] chef and co-founder of [[Frenchette Group]]. Trained at Le Cirque under [[Daniel Boulud]], then cooked at Aureole ([[Charlie Palmer]]) and Vong ([[Jean-Georges Vongerichten]]). Met [[Riad Nasr]] at Daniel in 1993, beginning a three-decade cooking partnership. The pair served as co-executive chefs at [[Keith McNally]]'s Balthazar from 1997 to 2013, running one of New York's most iconic French brasseries.

Launched Frenchette in Tribeca in 2018 with Nasr, building it into a multi-venue restaurant group. Won the 2025 James Beard Outstanding Restaurateur award (with Nasr). The Frenchette Group now includes Frenchette, Le Rock, Le Veau d'Or, and Wild Cherry -- a portfolio spanning French brasserie, fine dining, and bar concepts across downtown Manhattan.
