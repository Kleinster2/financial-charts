# Chopard

Swiss luxury watch and high jewelry house, privately held by the Scheufele family since 1963. ~$1B+ estimated revenue. Known for ethical gold pioneering (first major luxury house to use 100% ethical gold) and Cannes Film Festival partnership (Palme d'Or trophy maker).
