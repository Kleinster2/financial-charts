Sinochem Holdings Corporation — Chinese state-owned conglomerate formed May 2021 from merger of [[ChemChina]] and [[Sinochem Group]]. One of the world's largest chemical enterprises with ~$143B revenue and 220,000 employees. Overseen by [[SASAC]]. #31 Fortune Global 500 (2022).

## Formation

[[Sinochem Group]] (petroleum, chemicals, agriculture) and [[ChemChina]] (specialty chemicals, agrochemicals) merged under State Council approval. Ning Gaoning served as first chairman of both pre-merger entities before leading the combined group. [[Li Fanrong]] succeeded him in Aug 2022.
