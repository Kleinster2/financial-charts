# Orla Mining

Orla Mining (TSX and NYSE American: ORLA) is a North-American-focused gold producer whose flagship is the Camino Rojo mine in Mexico, expanded in 2025 by acquiring the Musselwhite mine in Ontario from [[Newmont]] and advancing the South Railroad development project in Nevada. In May 2026 it agreed an all-stock at-market merger with [[Equinox Gold]], under which Orla shareholders would own roughly 33% of the combined ~1.1Moz North American senior producer (close expected Q3 2026, subject to shareholder, court, and Canadian/Mexican competition approvals). The deal is backed by mining financiers including Pierre Lassonde.
