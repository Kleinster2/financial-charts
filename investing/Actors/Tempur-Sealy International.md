# Tempur-Sealy International

Largest mattress company globally. ~20-25% U.S. market share. Manufactures and distributes Tempur-Pedic, Sealy, and Stearns & Foster brands through wholesale and retail channels. Acquired [[Mattress Firm]] in February 2025, creating a vertically integrated entity with ~3,000 retail stores and 30 e-commerce platforms. Revenue ~$5.3B (2024). Operating margins ~29.5%.
