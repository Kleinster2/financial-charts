# Johnson Matthey

**Johnson Matthey** (LSE: JMAT) is a British specialty chemicals and sustainable technologies company, founded in 1817. Global leader in catalysts, precious metals, and fine chemicals. Developed a mercury-free gold-on-carbon catalyst for vinyl chloride monomer (VCM) production, relevant to replacing China's mercury-dependent calcium carbide PVC process.
