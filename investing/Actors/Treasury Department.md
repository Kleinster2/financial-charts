# Treasury Department

Treasury Department is the U.S. cabinet department responsible for federal finance, debt issuance, sanctions implementation through [[OFAC]], and official foreign-exchange-policy reporting. In the vault it is the institutional actor behind TIC data, FX reports, sanctions, stablecoin reserve policy, and Treasury-market plumbing.

## Vault Relevance

Treasury is the official U.S. reporter for foreign-exchange-policy surveillance and cross-border securities holdings. Its FX reports frame whether countries such as [[China]] are being watched for currency-management practices; its TIC data is the core public series for foreign ownership of U.S. securities.
