# La Colombe

Philadelphia-founded specialty coffee roaster and cafe chain. Known for Draft Latte (nitrogenated canned coffee) innovation. Operates ~30 cafes across the US. Backed by private equity — Chobani founder Hamdi Ulukaya invested in 2015.
