## 2026 restructuring

As part of [[Volkswagen 50k job cuts March 2026]], Audi contributes 7,500 positions to VW Group's 50,000-job reduction plan. The cuts reflect:

- [[China]] market share erosion (BYD, local premium EVs)
- High German labor and energy costs
- Slow EV ramp — e-tron line has underperformed versus [[BMW]] iX and [[Mercedes-Benz]] EQ series
