## May 2026 platform reset

On May 7, 2026, [[Google]] used the Fitbit brand to launch:

- [[Fitbit Air]] — $99 screenless wellness band, ships May 26, positioned vs [[WHOOP]].
- [[Google Health Premium]] — replaces Fitbit Premium at $9.99/mo or $99/yr (up from $79.99/yr).
- [[Google Health Coach]] — Gemini-powered coaching service inside Premium, launches globally May 19.
- [[Google Health]] app — replaces the Fitbit app.

This is the first material monetization push under the Google ownership since the 2021 acquisition. The strategic frame: hardware is a customer-acquisition wedge for the [[Google AI Pro]] subscription stack, not a hardware business in its own right. Full breakdown in [[Fitbit Air launch May 2026]].

## Competition

Beat [[Jawbone]] in the fitness tracker wars. Lost smartwatch battle to [[Apple Watch]]. Google acquisition aimed to compete with Apple in health/wearables.
