## Products

- Fitness trackers (Charge, Inspire, Ace)
- Smartwatches (Versa, Sense)
- Aria smart scales

## Competition

Beat [[Jawbone]] in the fitness tracker wars. Lost smartwatch battle to [[Apple Watch]]. Google acquisition aimed to compete with Apple in health/wearables.
