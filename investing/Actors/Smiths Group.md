# Smiths Group

Smiths Group (LSE: SMIN) is a UK-listed industrial conglomerate with operations in detection (security/airport screening), John Crane (mechanical seals for oil and gas), Smiths Interconnect, and Flex-Tek. One of the long-standing [[FTSE 100]] names, periodically the subject of activist pressure to break up its diversified portfolio.

Advised on multiple strategic transactions by [[Gleacher Shacklock]] over the past two decades.
