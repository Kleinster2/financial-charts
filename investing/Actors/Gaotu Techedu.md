# Gaotu Techedu

Gaotu Techedu (NYSE: GOTU), formerly GSX Techedu (高途), is a [[China]] online-focused education company founded 2014 by Chen Xiangdong (Larry Chen). It was the most online-leveraged of the major tutoring names and the hardest hit by the 2021 [[Common Prosperity|"double-reduction" (双减) crackdown]] — its ADR fell from above $100 to low single digits. It pivoted to college-entrance and civil-service exam prep, vocational training, and adult education, and markets an "All with AI" strategy built on [[DeepSeek]] models. The company was also a 2020 short-seller target ([[Muddy Waters]], Citron) over allegations of inflated user and revenue figures.

Member of the [[Chinese ed-tech]] cohort (the three US-listed tutoring ADRs tested for cluster structure); the lowest-priced and most idiosyncratic of the three.
