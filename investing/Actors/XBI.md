# XBI

XBI (NYSE Arca: XBI) is the SPDR S&P Biotech ETF — an equal-weighted basket of US biotech companies, the standard proxy for biotech risk appetite and small/mid-cap biotech funding sentiment. Equal weighting tilts it toward smaller, higher-beta names than cap-weighted biotech ETFs. Launched 2006.
