**Zeng Fangqin** (曾芳勤) is the founder and chair of [[Lingyi iTech]], the [[China]] precision-components and intelligent-manufacturing group she started in 2006 and built into a top [[Apple]] supplier. She is the controlling shareholder and a billionaire on the strength of her Lingyi stake.
