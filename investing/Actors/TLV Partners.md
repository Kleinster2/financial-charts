#actor #vc #israel

**TLV Partners** is a Tel Aviv-based venture capital firm focused on early-stage [[Israel|Israeli]] [[Cybersecurity|cybersecurity]] and enterprise software companies, operating at the seed and Series A stage where Israel's deep bench of military intelligence alumni (Unit 8200, Mossad) generates a disproportionate share of global cyber startups.

## Synopsis

TLV Partners invests at the earliest stage of Israel's cybersecurity pipeline, typically leading seed rounds in the $5-15M range for companies founded by ex-intelligence and military personnel. The firm's edge is local network density — Israel produces roughly 30% of global cybersecurity venture deals despite being a country of 10 million people, and TLV operates in the inner circle where military alumni, serial founders, and prior exits create a self-reinforcing deal flow engine. The team includes partners with operational backgrounds in cybersecurity and enterprise software, giving them credibility with technical founders who might otherwise gravitate toward US-based funds.

The investment that anchors TLV's track record is leading [[Cyata]]'s $8.5M seed round in 2025, a company subsequently acquired by [[Check Point]] — illustrating the typical Israeli cyber exit path where large acquirers (Check Point, [[Palo Alto Networks]], [[CrowdStrike]]) absorb early-stage companies for talent and technology. TLV's returns depend on maintaining access to this pipeline as competition for Israeli cyber deals intensifies from US crossover funds and growth-stage investors moving earlier.
