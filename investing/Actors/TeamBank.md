TeamBank AG -- consumer finance center of excellence in the cooperative financial network. Subsidiary of [[DZ Bank]]. Offers "easyCredit" in [[Germany]] and "der faire Credit" in Austria. 1.07M customers. Loans EUR 9.9B. 94% of German cooperative banks are partners. PBT EUR 23M (2024, down from EUR 81M due to higher loss allowances). HQ: Nuremberg.
