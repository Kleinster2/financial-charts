**Avant Gardner** — Brooklyn's largest nightclub complex (Brooklyn Mirage, Great Hall, Kings Hall). 80,000 sq ft in East Williamsburg. Founded 2016 by Jurgen "Billy" Bildstein and Philipp Wiederkehr. DJ Mag "Best Large Club" in North America (2022, 2023). Filed Chapter 11 August 2025 with ~$155M in secured debt to [[Axar Capital]]. Assets transferred to [[FIVE Holdings]] ([[Pacha]] brand) post-bankruptcy. Reopening as Pacha New York June 2026.
