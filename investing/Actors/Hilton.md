## Business model

**Purer asset-light than [[Marriott]]:**
- Spun off owned hotels (Park Hotels & Resorts) in 2017
- Spun off timeshare (Hilton Grand Vacations) in 2017
- Now nearly 100% fee-based revenue

| Revenue type | % |
|--------------|---|
| Franchise fees | ~70% |
| Management fees | ~25% |
| Other | ~5% |

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Purer asset-light = higher ROIC
- Outperforming MAR (+175% vs +115%)
- Strong brand pipeline (Spark, Outset)
- Hilton Honors loyalty growing
- Goldman upgrade to Buy (Dec 2025, PT $317)

**Bear:**
- Smaller scale than [[Marriott]] (1.25M vs 1.75M rooms)
- Premium valuation (P/E ~41x)
- Economy segment headwinds (2025 RevPAR weak)
- Dependent on travel demand recovery
