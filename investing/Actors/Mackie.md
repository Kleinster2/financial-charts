Mackie — American pro-audio brand known for live sound mixers, powered speakers, and recording interfaces. Founded 1988 in Woodinville, Washington. Acquired by [[Rode Microphones|The Freedman Group]] in 2022. Previously moved manufacturing to Asia; Freedman Group plans to reshore US production via the [[Lectrosonics]] facility in New Mexico.
