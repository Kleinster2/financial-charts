# Beijing Stock Exchange

China's newest exchange, launched 2021. SME and "specialized, refined, differential, innovative" (专精特新) company focus. Converted from New Third Board (NEEQ Select).

---

## Key facts

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Founded | 2021 |
| Listed companies | ~250 |
| Market cap | ~$50B |
| Focus | SMEs, innovation |
| Origin | Upgraded from NEEQ Select tier |

---

## Strategic purpose

Created to:
- Support SME financing (underserved by Shanghai/Shenzhen)
- Incubate innovative smaller companies
- Provide pathway to larger exchanges

Lower listing thresholds than Shanghai/Shenzhen main boards.

---

## Role in A-share market

Smallest of three mainland exchanges but growing. Works in coordination with [[Shanghai Stock Exchange]] and [[Shenzhen Stock Exchange]] on regulatory policy. See [[China A-share exchanges]] for coordinated actions.
