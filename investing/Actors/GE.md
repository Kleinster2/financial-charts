**General Electric (GE)** — Historic American conglomerate. Split into three independent companies in 2023-2024: [[GE HealthCare]], [[GE Vernova]], [[GE Aerospace]].

---

## The Breakup

| Date | Event | Ticker |
|------|-------|--------|
| Jan 2023 | GE HealthCare spun off | GEHC |
| Apr 2024 | GE Vernova spun off | GEV |
| Apr 2024 | Remaining GE renamed GE Aerospace | GE |

**Distribution:** 1 share GEV for every 4 shares GE held.

---

## The Three Companies

### [[GE Aerospace]] (GE)
- **Focus:** Jet engines, aviation services
- **2025 performance:** +85%
- **Guidance:** Revenue double-digit growth 2025-2028, EPS $6.10 → $8.40

### [[GE Vernova]] (GEV)
- **Focus:** Power generation, grid equipment, renewables
- **2025 performance:** +95% (400% since spinoff)
- **Backlog:** $135B → $200B target by 2028
- **AI tailwind:** Data center power demand

### [[GE HealthCare]] (GEHC)
- **Focus:** Medical imaging, diagnostics
- **Spun off:** January 2023

---

## Why the split

- **Conglomerate discount** — Market undervalued sum of parts
- **Focus** — Each business can allocate capital to its own priorities
- **Debt reduction** — Legacy GE carried heavy debt load
- **Management** — Specialized leadership for each industry

---

## Legacy

Founded by Thomas Edison (1892). Was largest company in world by market cap (2000). Fell from ~$600B to ~$60B during 2008-2018 collapse under Jeff Immelt.

---
