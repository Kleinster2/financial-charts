## Synopsis

NetApp is a Sunnyvale-based storage software and systems company best known for its ONTAP storage operating system. The business straddles three modes: traditional on-prem hybrid arrays for enterprise data center customers, native cloud storage services with the three hyperscalers (Azure NetApp Files, Amazon FSx for ONTAP, Google Cloud NetApp Volumes), and AI-oriented offerings (ONTAP AI, AIPod) that pair NetApp storage with [[NVIDIA]] reference architectures. The competitive question is whether NetApp's installed base of enterprise data plus its first-party cloud integrations can keep pace with AI-native challengers like [[VAST Data]] and Weka, who built around GPU-direct architectures rather than evolving from legacy.
