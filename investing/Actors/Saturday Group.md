# Saturday Group

**Fashion marketing and brand-building group** — founded 2003 by Jens Grede and Erik Torstensson. Built creative agency empire, then pivoted to consumer brands. Sold agencies to Omnicom/IPG (2015-2018). Now best known for co-founding Frame, Good American, and [[SKIMS]]. Holding company: Popular Culture.

## Investment thesis

**The Grede playbook:**
1. Identify underserved category (denim, shapewear, men's basics)
2. Partner with celebrity with authentic connection
3. Build DTC-first with social media distribution
4. Premium positioning, inclusive sizing
5. Scale rapidly with capital partners
