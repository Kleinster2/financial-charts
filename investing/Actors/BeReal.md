#actor #consumer #socialmedia #brand #france

**BeReal** — French social app. Daily notification prompts users to share unfiltered photos within 2-minute window. Acquired by [[Voodoo]] for €500M (2024).

## Monetization

Launched ads April 2025:
- In-feed ads
- Full-day brand takeovers
- Early advertisers: Nike, Levi's, Netflix, Amazon

---

## Product

**Core mechanic:** Random daily notification → 2-minute window to post front + back camera photo simultaneously. No filters, no editing.

Anti-Instagram positioning: authenticity over curation.
