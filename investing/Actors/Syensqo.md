# Syensqo

Belgian specialty chemicals company, demerged from [[Solvay]] in December 2023. Listed on Euronext Brussels (SYENS.BR). Inherited Solvay's high-value specialty businesses: fluoropolymers (Solef [[PVDF]]), specialty polymers, composites, aroma performance, and oil & gas solutions. Solef PVDF is a leading binder and separator material for lithium-ion batteries, positioning Syensqo as a key supplier to the EV battery supply chain.
