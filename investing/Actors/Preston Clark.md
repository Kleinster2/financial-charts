# Preston Clark

Executive chef at [[Lure Fishbar]] and [[Bar Mercer]] for [[Mercer Street Hospitality]], the restaurant group founded by [[John McDonald (restaurateur)|John McDonald]]. Has led the Lure Fishbar kitchen for over a decade, making it one of the most consistently regarded seafood restaurants in SoHo. Named one of 16 Black chefs changing food in America by the New York Times in 2019.

Clark's cooking at Lure Fishbar leans on precise seafood preparation -- sashimi, raw bar, grilled fish -- with a French technique foundation. His longevity at a single restaurant in a city where chef turnover is constant speaks to both the kitchen's quality and the stability of the McDonald partnership.
