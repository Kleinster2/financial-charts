**Bolloré Group** — French conglomerate controlled by the Bolloré family. Controlling shareholder of [[Vivendi]], [[Canal+]], and ~28% stake in [[Universal Music Group]] — the single largest holder. Known for aggressive, activist-style capital allocation and tight-lipped investor communication.

## UMG stake and Pershing Square bid

Bolloré is the pivotal shareholder in the [[Pershing Square UMG takeover bid]]. The family's ~28% stake makes them the single largest holder — and the biggest hurdle for [[Bill Ackman]]'s €55bn proposal, which requires two-thirds shareholder approval.

The family's prolonged silence on whether it might sell has weighed on [[Universal Music Group|UMG]] sentiment for years. Combined with AI disruption fears, investors describe it as a "double hit" to the stock (FT, Apr 9 2026).

[[Bill Ackman|Ackman]] spoke to Cyrille Bolloré before making his approach public, but the family has not commented publicly on the proposal.
