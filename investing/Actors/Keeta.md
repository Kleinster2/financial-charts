**Keeta** — [[Meituan]]'s international food delivery app, launched in [[Brazil]] (Santos) in October 2025. Part of a combined R$7.6B ($1.5B) Chinese investment pledge alongside [[Didi]]'s [[99]] to challenge [[iFood]]'s ~80% market share. CEO Tony Qiu.

## Brazil launch

Launched in Santos in October 2025 with subsidized promotional offers. Part of [[Meituan]]'s push to internationalize as domestic [[China]] market saturates (competition with [[Alibaba]]'s Ele.me squeezing margins). Day after Santos launch, Qiu claims 8-10 people impersonated Keeta employees at restaurants, photographing systems and shutting down accounts. Police investigating.
