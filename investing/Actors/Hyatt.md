## Strategic moves (2025)

- **Playa Hotels acquisition** — all-inclusive expansion
- **Record pipeline** — 148,000 rooms (+7% YoY)
- **Luxury pipeline** — 170+ hotels, 141,000 rooms globally
- **Asset-light transition** — selling owned properties, moving to fee-based

---

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Luxury segment outperforming (+5% RevPAR vs economy -2%)
- High-end consumer resilient
- Asset-light transition improving ROIC
- Differentiated from mass-market competitors

**Bear:**
- Smallest major hotelier (~330K rooms vs MAR 1.75M)
- Revenue declining (-8.7% in 2024)
- Premium pricing limits growth
- All-inclusive integration risk (Playa)
