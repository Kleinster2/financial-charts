## Investment process

- Fundamental, bottom-up — deep research on individual businesses, not macro bets
- Concentrated — 12-15 positions; conviction-weighted
- Mid-cap bias — companies in evolving markets poised to take share
- Long/short — hedges downside with selective shorts
- Long hold periods — target 50% return over ~2 years per position
- TMT focus — [[Semiconductors|semiconductors]], software, internet platforms, gaming
