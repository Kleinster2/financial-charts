# Microchip Technology

Microchip Technology (Nasdaq: MCHP) is a US integrated device manufacturer (IDM) of microcontrollers (the PIC and AVR families), analog and mixed-signal chips, FPGAs (via the Microsemi acquisition), and embedded-connectivity products. Its end-markets are autos, industrial, aerospace/defense, and data center — so it runs on the auto/industrial inventory cycle, not the AI-compute cycle. A member of the [[Analog and auto-industrial semiconductors]] cohort.
