# Technoprobe

Italian semiconductor probe card manufacturer and the global leader in advanced logic probe cards for AI/HPC die testing. Listed on the Milan exchange. Founded in 1996 by the Crippa family near Milan, the company has grown from a niche European supplier into the dominant force in logic probe cards, particularly for cutting-edge node testing.

Technoprobe is a key beneficiary of AI-driven test intensity -- each chip design requires a unique probe card that cannot be transferred between designs, and shorter [[NVIDIA]]/[[AMD]] product cycles create structural volume growth. The company's MEMS-based probe technology enables higher pin counts and finer pitch than traditional vertical probe cards, a critical advantage as chip I/O density increases. Competes with [[FormFactor]] (US, memory-focused) and [[MJC]] (Korea) in different segments of the probe card market.
