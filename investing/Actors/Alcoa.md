# Alcoa

**Alcoa** is one of the world's largest bauxite miners, alumina refiners, and aluminum producers. Major consumer of [[Caustic Soda]] for the Bayer process (alumina refining). Spun off from Arconic in 2016. Operations span Australia, Brazil, US, Spain, and Iceland.
