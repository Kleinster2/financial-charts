Richard White — founder and CEO of [[Fathom AI]] (2020–present). Serial B2B-SaaS founder with a [[Y Combinator]] arc that runs back to the platform's first batch.

---

## Why Richard White matters

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Current role | Founder / CEO, [[Fathom AI]] |
| Founded Fathom | 2020 |
| Prior company | [[UserVoice]] — enterprise customer-feedback platform (Fortune 500 customers) |
| Earlier | Kiko (calendar startup), [[Y Combinator]] W05 first batch with [[Justin Kan]] and [[Emmett Shear]] |
| Based | San Francisco |
| YC cohorts | W05 (Kiko), W21 ([[Fathom AI]]) |

White's stated product philosophy at [[Fathom AI]] is to defer heavy AI-feature investment until the underlying models matured — he timed Fathom to product-market fit with [[GPT-4]] and [[Claude]] 2 rather than racing ahead of the model frontier. Shipped speaker diarization with the April 15 2026 "Fathom 3.0" launch, attributing the timing to six months of model improvements.

The Kiko → [[UserVoice]] → [[Fathom AI]] arc gives White two prior YC-era exits/builds before Fathom — a tighter founder profile than typical for a 2020-founded company.
