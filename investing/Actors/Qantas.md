# Qantas

Qantas Airways — [[Australia]]'s flag carrier and the world's third-oldest continuously operating airline (founded 1920). Hub at Sydney Kingsford Smith (SYD); secondary hubs at Melbourne (MEL) and Brisbane (BNE). Operates Qantas mainline plus low-cost subsidiary Jetstar.

## Iran conflict impact (March-April 2026)

Initial response (March): Perth-London rerouted via Singapore for refueling to avoid Middle East airspace closures. See [[Iran conflict airline disruption]].

April 27 update: per [[Financial Times|FT]] reporting, Qantas joined [[Deutsche Lufthansa|Lufthansa]], [[Delta Air Lines|Delta]], and [[Cathay Pacific]] in cutting flights as [[Jet fuel|jet fuel]] doubled. The cuts are first-mover demand-destruction in the slate-rigidity framework — they reduce jet demand without rebalancing crude. See [[Refining slate rigidity]].
