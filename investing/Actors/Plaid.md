## Business

API platform enabling apps to access user bank data (balances, transactions, identity verification). Powers account linking for [[PayPal|Venmo]], [[Robinhood]], [[Coinbase]], and thousands of fintech apps.

Revenue model: per-API-call fees from developers.

## History

- 2020: [[Visa]] acquisition announced at $5.3B
- 2021: DOJ sued to block; deal terminated
- 2021: Raised at $13.4B valuation post-termination
