#actor #hospitality #entertainment #uae #private

**FIVE Holdings** — Dubai-based hospitality and nightlife conglomerate. Founded 2011 (as SKAI Holdings, renamed 2017) by Kabir Mulchandani ($2.2B net worth). Revenue $589M (2024). Acquired The Pacha Group for €302.5M (~$330M) in October 2023 — includes Pacha Ibiza (opened 1973) and global trademarks. 1,700+ hotel keys, 3,000+ employees. Taking over Brooklyn Mirage operations post-[[Avant Gardner bankruptcy 2025|Avant Gardner bankruptcy]], reopening as Pacha New York June 2026.
