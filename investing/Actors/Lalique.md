## René Lalique legacy

**Art Nouveau period (1888-1910):**
- Revolutionary jeweler — mixed precious with semi-precious materials
- Used horn, enamel, glass alongside gold
- Clients: Sarah Bernhardt, Cartier, Boucheron
- Supplied Samuel Bing's "Maison de l'Art Nouveau"

**Art Deco period (1910-1945):**
- Transitioned from jewelry to glass
- Perfume bottles for major houses
- Architectural glass, car mascots
- 1925 Paris Exposition cemented legacy

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Iconic heritage brand (136 years)
- Diversification into whisky/hospitality
- Glenturret upside (ultra-premium positioning)
- Silvio Denz collector credibility
- Small cap, under-followed

**Bear:**
- Tiny scale vs [[LVMH]], [[Richemont]]
- Niche appeal (crystal declining category)
- Whisky JV — only 50% ownership
- Swiss-listed, low liquidity
- Luxury cyclicality
