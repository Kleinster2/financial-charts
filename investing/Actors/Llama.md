## Llama 4 family (Apr 2025)

| Model | Active params | Total params | Context | Experts |
|-------|---------------|--------------|---------|---------|
| **Scout** | 17B | 109B | **10M tokens** | 16 |
| **Maverick** | 17B | 400B | 1M tokens | 128 + shared |
| **Behemoth** | 288B | ~2T | — | 16 (training) |

**Architecture:** Mixture of Experts (MoE) — only 17B active per query despite massive total size.
