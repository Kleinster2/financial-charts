# Anwar Gargash

UAE diplomatic adviser to the President. Senior career Emirati diplomat; former Minister of State for Foreign Affairs (2008-2021). Has served as the UAE's primary public-facing voice on regional and international policy questions in recent years, with particular weight on [[GCC]] dynamics, [[Iran]] policy, and US-UAE relations.

## Key positions

On Gulf cohesion under the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|Iran war]], April 28, 2026: "The Gulf Cooperation Council countries supported each other logistically, but politically and militarily, I think their position has been the weakest historically." Unusual public admission from the Emirati side, published the same day as the [[2026 UAE OPEC exit|UAE OPEC exit announcement]] — read by the press as part of the broader UAE strategic-reorientation narrative.

Earlier framing on the war (April 2026): the UAE "has the right to self-defense" — used in response to Iranian accusations that US HIMARS were firing into Iran from UAE territory. Did not directly deny the underlying claim.
