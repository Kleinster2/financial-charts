# KXI

KXI (NYSE Arca: KXI) is the iShares Global Consumer Staples ETF — a basket of global consumer-staples companies (food, beverages, household, tobacco) spanning the US and international markets. The global-staples counterpart to the US-only [[XLP]]. Launched 2006.

## Use case

A global-staples control for the [[Alcohol and spirits#Cluster validation|alcohol cluster validation]] (the cohort includes global names like [[Diageo|DEO]], so KXI complements the US-only [[XLP]]). In the 1Y test the alcohol names do not even cohere into one cluster, and what cohesion exists is staples-adjacent. See [[Alcohol and spirits]].
