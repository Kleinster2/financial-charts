# Cronos

A Canadian licensed producer notable for the 2019 investment by [[Altria]], which took a large minority stake (~45% fully diluted) and left Cronos with one of the strongest balance sheets in the sector — a deep net-cash position that has let it survive the cannabis downturn without the dilution that hit its peers. That cash cushion makes it the lowest-volatility name in the [[Cannabis]] cohort (42% annualized) and the highest inverse-vol mimic weight. Federally legal in Canada and NASDAQ-listed. A member of the Canadian LP bloc; collapses into [[MSOS]]/[[MJ]].
