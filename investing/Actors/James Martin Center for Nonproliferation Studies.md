# James Martin Center for Nonproliferation Studies

Research centre at the Middlebury Institute of International Studies at Monterey, focused on nonproliferation of weapons of mass destruction, missile programmes, and nuclear policy. Home institution of [[Jim Lamson]], former [[CIA]] Iran analyst who provided the "dispersion strategy" framing in the FT's April 14, 2026 investigation of the [[IRGC]]'s [[TEE-01B]] operations (see [[IRGC Chinese satellite targeting March 2026]]).
