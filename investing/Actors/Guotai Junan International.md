**Guotai Junan International** (1788.HK) is the Hong Kong-listed international arm of one of [[China]]'s largest brokerages, active in equity and debt underwriting, brokerage and asset management for the offshore China market. It was sole sponsor of the [[Lingyi iTech]] Hong Kong IPO (June 2026).
