#actor #person #semiconductor

**Jim Keller** — Legendary chip architect. Led CPU/chip design at [[AMD]] (K8, Zen), Apple (A4/A5), Tesla (FSD chip), Intel, and now [[Tenstorrent]] (CEO since late 2022). One of the most influential semiconductor engineers alive. His arrival at Tenstorrent prompted [[Taalas]] founder Ljubisa Bajic to leave and start his own company.
