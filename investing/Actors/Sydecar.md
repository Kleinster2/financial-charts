# Sydecar

Sydecar — SPV administration platform (not a sponsor or investor itself). Provides back-office infrastructure — entity formation, fund admin, tax/compliance — for sponsors creating SPVs to pool capital into single investments. Named in [[Anthropic]]'s May 11 2026 unauthorized-platform blocklist; Sydecar's public response was that it operates in an *administrative-only* capacity and requires SPV sponsors to attest that proper issuer approvals exist before the platform processes transactions.

## May 11 2026 — Anthropic blocklist inclusion (disputed)

[[Anthropic]]'s May 11 2026 help-center notice listed Sydecar among eight platforms whose transfers of [[Anthropic]] stock are declared void. Sydecar's defense — distinct from pure SPV-sponsor responses — was that it provides administration services only, with sponsors bearing responsibility for issuer-approval attestation. The structural argument is that an SPV administrator that does not control the underlying transaction approval cannot itself be the source of an unauthorized transfer.

The inclusion is consequential operationally: Sydecar's fund-admin clients (sponsors using the platform to set up SPVs) are now on notice that any [[Anthropic]] exposure routed through their Sydecar-administered SPVs is void unless directly board-approved. This effectively shifts compliance enforcement upstream to the sponsor level — the issue Sydecar's response was pointing at.
