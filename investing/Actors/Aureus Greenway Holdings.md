Aureus Greenway Holdings Inc. (Nasdaq: AGH) — Florida-based golf course operator that completed a Feb 2025 IPO (lead underwriter [[Dominari Securities]]) and is the listed shell for the reverse merger with [[Powerus]] announced March 9, 2026. Upon completion (expected summer 2026) the combined entity will rename to "Powerus Corporation" and trade under the ticker PUSA. Pre-merger AGH was lossmaking with stock trading below $1; post-announcement AGH stock rose ~55%.

## Pre-merger profile

AGH was a Florida-based golf course operating company with no profitable operations and a stock trading below $1 per share at the time of the merger announcement. The IPO in Feb 2025 was lead-underwritten by [[Dominari Securities]] and is one of the cleanest examples of the [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]] mechanic in operation: a small-cap shell that exists primarily to serve as a vehicle for a future reverse merger.

## Federal-policy alignment (post-announcement)

In late April 2026 (approximately 7 weeks after the merger announcement), [[Powerus]] disclosed a U.S. Air Force first purchase order for the Guardian-2 Interceptor counter-drone system. The Guardian-2 is purpose-built for Middle East deployment against Shahed-type attack drones. The procurement timing is one of the clearest cases of federal-policy alignment with the Dominari/American Ventures pipeline — see [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]] and [[Trump family investment portfolio]] for the broader pattern.
