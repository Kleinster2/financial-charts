# MJ

The Amplify Alternative Harvest ETF (formerly ETFMG Alternative Harvest, the original cannabis ETF, launched 2017) — a global cannabis fund historically weighted toward NASDAQ-listed Canadian licensed producers ([[Canopy Growth]], [[Tilray]], [[Aurora Cannabis]], [[Cronos]]) plus tobacco and ancillary names, in contrast to [[MSOS]]'s pure US-MSO swap book. It can hold the Canadian LPs directly because they are federally legal and exchange-listed, so unlike [[MSOS]] it does not need a swap structure. A member of the [[Cannabis]] cohort's control set and, with [[MSOS]], the ETF the cohort collapses into.
