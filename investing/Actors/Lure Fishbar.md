#actor #restaurant #seafood #nyc

**Lure Fishbar** — flagship seafood restaurant of [[Mercer Street Hospitality]], opened 2004 in SoHo by [[John McDonald (restaurateur)|John McDonald]]. Serves 350-400 covers on busy nights; one of New York City's largest oyster sellers. Executive chef [[Preston Clark]] has led the kitchen for over a decade. Locations in New York, Chicago, and South Beach (Miami).
