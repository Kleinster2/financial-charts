# GDX

GDX (NYSE Arca: GDX) is the VanEck Gold Miners ETF — the benchmark basket of large-cap gold-mining companies, market-cap-weighted, with top holdings spanning the major producers ([[Newmont]], Agnico Eagle, [[Barrick Gold]]) and the large royalty/streaming names ([[Franco-Nevada]], [[Wheaton Precious Metals]]). A broad gold-equity proxy. Launched 2006.

## Use case

The gold-miner-ETF benchmark for the [[Precious metals royalties#Cluster validation|royalty cluster validation]] and [[Gold equity beta]]. Because GDX holds both the big miners and the royalty names ([[Franco-Nevada|FNV]], [[Wheaton Precious Metals|WPM]]), the royalty cohort cannot separate from it — the −0.034 intra-advantage versus GDX/[[GDXJ]] is the ETF-replicable signature: royalties = the gold complex.
