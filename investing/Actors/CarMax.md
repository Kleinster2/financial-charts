# CarMax

CarMax (NYSE: KMX) is the largest US used-vehicle retailer — a no-haggle, big-box used-car format with an omni-channel (in-store + online) model and a captive-finance arm (CarMax Auto Finance). Used-only, so it is distinct from the new-franchise dealers; it serves as the used-car-retail control for the [[Franchised auto dealers]] cluster validation.
