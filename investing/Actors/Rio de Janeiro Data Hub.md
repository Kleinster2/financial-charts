## Why Rio matters

[[Brazil]]'s emerging AI infrastructure hub:

- **Fastest growth**: 9.69% CAGR to 2030 vs slower São Paulo
- **Rio AI City**: 3.2GW mega-campus by [[Elea Data Centers]] — largest in LatAm
- **Submarine cables**: Trans-Atlantic landings for low-latency connectivity
- **Cost advantage**: Lower than São Paulo with state tax incentives
- **Energy sector**: [[Petrobras]] offshore data needs

## Rio AI City

**[[Elea Data Centers]]'s flagship project** (Olympic Park / Porto Maravilha):

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Developer | [[Elea Data Centers]] |
| Ultimate capacity | 3.2GW |
| Phase 1 | 1.5GW |
| RJO1 | Operating (ex-[[Globo]] facility) |
| RJO2 | Under construction, 80MW by 2026 |
| Partners | [[Oracle]], City of Rio de Janeiro |
| Jobs | 10,000+ expected |

Announced by Mayor Eduardo Paes at Web Summit Rio, April 2025.

**Not to be confused with**: [[Scala Data Centers]]'s Scala AI City (4.75GW) in Rio Grande do Sul — different state, different company.
