# Guillermo Rauch

#actor #individual #founder #developer-tools #argentina

**Guillermo Rauch** — Founder/CEO of [[Vercel]]. Creator of Next.js, Socket.IO, Mongoose. Argentine, self-taught programmer. Dropped out of high school at 16 to work at Swiss startup. Built tools powering Midjourney, Grok, Notion. Angel investor in [[Supabase]].

## Philosophy

**"A hundred million builders"** — Rauch's mission is to democratize product creation, expanding who can build software from ~5M professional developers to 100M+ people through tools like v0.

**Key belief:** The best developer tools remove friction entirely. His "now" command (one-word deployment) exemplified this.
