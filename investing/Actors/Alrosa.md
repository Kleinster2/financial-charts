# Alrosa

[[Russia]]'s state-controlled diamond mining company, the world's largest diamond producer by volume (~25% of global output). Subject to Western sanctions since 2022 ([[Russia-Ukraine war]]), forcing costly workarounds for the global diamond trade. Competes with [[De Beers]] for market share but operates outside the Sightholders system.
