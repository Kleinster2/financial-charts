# Yemen

**Yemen** is a country on the southern Arabian Peninsula. In this vault it mainly matters through [[Houthis]] control of [[Red Sea]]-adjacent territory and the resulting pressure on [[Bab el-Mandeb]] and [[Suez Canal]] shipping.
