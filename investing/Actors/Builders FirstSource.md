# Builders FirstSource

Builders FirstSource (NYSE: BLDR) is the largest U.S. supplier of structural building products, value-added components, and services to professional homebuilders, with a national manufacturing and distribution footprint. Unlike the [[Building products]] makers, BLDR is a distributor and fabricator — its revenue is volume-levered to single-family construction activity, which makes it one of the highest-beta listed ways to express new-home construction.

## Cluster context

Not a member of the [[Building products]] makers cohort (HVAC and housing-products manufacturers) — BLDR is a distributor, so its driver is construction volume rather than product pricing. It sits closest to the [[Homebuilders]] complex as a high-beta read on single-family starts; on Jun 24 2026 it was the single largest mover in the housing complex (+11.31%), above every builder and building-products maker. A formal cluster validation against the homebuilders, building-products, and distribution peers is a follow-up.

## Jun 24 2026 — ROAD to Housing Act rally

BLDR closed +11.31% on Jun 24 (+3.39σ on a flat [[SPY]], -0.04%) — the largest single move in the housing complex — after Congress passed the [[21st Century ROAD to Housing Act]] and the 10-year yield fell ~9bps to 4.40%. As a distributor levered to construction volume, BLDR carries more operating leverage to a supply-side bill (permitting, manufactured homes) than the makers or builders. The bill is unsigned ([[Donald Trump]] withholding signature pending his SAVE America Act). See [[21st Century ROAD to Housing Act]].
