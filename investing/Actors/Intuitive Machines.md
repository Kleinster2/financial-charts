#actor #space #usa #public

Intuitive Machines (LUNR) — Houston-based lunar services company. NASA CLPS provider. Achieved the first US soft landing on the Moon in over 50 years with the Nova-C "Odysseus" lander (Feb 2024), though it tipped on its side upon landing. Publicly traded on Nasdaq. Provides lunar landers, data transmission, and navigation services for NASA and commercial customers.
