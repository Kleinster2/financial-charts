# VICI Properties

S&P 500 experiential REIT. Owns 93 properties (54 gaming, 39 other experiential) across the US and Canada — ~127M sqft, ~60,300 hotel rooms, 500+ restaurants/bars. Marquee assets include Caesars Palace, MGM Grand, and The Venetian on the Las Vegas Strip. ~$3.85B revenue (TTM), ~$30B market cap, 6.4% dividend yield. Triple-net lease model — tenants bear all operating expenses. Expanding beyond gaming into wellness ([[Canyon Ranch]] — $300M investment), entertainment (Chelsea Piers, Great Wolf Resorts), and hospitality. AFFO guidance $2.5–2.52B (2025).
