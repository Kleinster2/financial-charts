Selena Gomez is a singer, actress, and entrepreneur who became a billionaire (Bloomberg, 2024) primarily through her equity stake in [[Rare Beauty]], the cosmetics brand she founded in 2020. With an estimated net worth of $1.3B, her wealth is driven by Rare Beauty's $2.7B valuation ($540M revenue in 2024, doubling YoY) rather than entertainment income. The most-followed woman on Instagram (~430M followers) as of early 2026, a distribution advantage that translates directly into zero-CAC brand awareness. Also co-founded Wondermind, a mental health media platform. Her transition from Disney Channel actress to billion-dollar beauty founder is the clearest success case in the [[Influencer-to-brand playbook]] after [[Rihanna]]'s [[Fenty Beauty]].
