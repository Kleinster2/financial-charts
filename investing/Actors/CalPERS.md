# CalPERS

California Public Employees' Retirement System, the largest public pension fund in the [[United States]] with ~$500B+ in assets under management. Provides retirement and health benefits to ~2 million California state, public school, and local government employees and retirees.

CalPERS is a market-moving allocator whose investment decisions signal trends in institutional capital flows. The fund has been increasing exposure to alternatives, including [[private credit]] and infrastructure, while maintaining large public equity and fixed income books. Its sheer size means it faces liquidity constraints -- any meaningful portfolio shift takes months to execute without moving markets.
