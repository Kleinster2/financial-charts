**LightShed Partners** — Independent TMT equity research firm founded September 2019 in New York by [[Rich Greenfield]], Walter Piecyk, and Brandon Ross, all formerly at BTIG. Covers ~24 stocks across streaming, media, telecom, and tech. Also operates LightShed Ventures, a $75M seed/Series A fund (launched Feb 2021) investing at the intersection of media, communications, and commerce.
