# Dennis Wilder

Former head of [[China]] analysis at the [[CIA]]. Named in the FT investigation (April 14, 2026) placing Chinese support to [[Iran]] within a decades-long pattern of Chinese weapons transfers, including Silkworm anti-ship missiles previously used by Iran to threaten shipping in the [[Strait of Hormuz]]. Framed the transfers as "a pragmatic strategy to influence the Islamic republic on other issues." See [[IRGC Chinese satellite targeting March 2026]] and [[Chinese dual-use commercial satellite exports]].
