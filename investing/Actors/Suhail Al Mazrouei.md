# Suhail Al Mazrouei

UAE Minister of Energy and Infrastructure. Long-serving Emirati energy official, in office since 2013. Lead spokesperson on UAE oil-policy decisions including the [[2026 UAE OPEC exit|April 28, 2026 announcement]] of UAE departure from [[OPEC]] and OPEC+ effective May 1, 2026.

## Key statements

In the Bloomberg "The Asia Trade" 4/28/2026 interview with Jumana Versace announcing the OPEC exit, Mazrouei framed the decision as a "pure policy change" that gives the country flexibility to ramp toward [[ADNOC]]'s 5 mb/d 2027 capacity target, citing the [[Strait of Hormuz]] closure as the reason the timing wouldn't shock markets. He explicitly denied any political dispute with [[Saudi Arabia]] — a denial that the surrounding political evidence (Yemen coalition rupture, Bessent swap-line architecture, Gargash GCC weakness framing) makes difficult to detach from broader Gulf realignment.
