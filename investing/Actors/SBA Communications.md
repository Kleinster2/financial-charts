# SBA Communications

SBA Communications (NASDAQ: SBAC) is the third of the three US cell-tower REITs, owning and leasing communications towers (US plus a large Latin America footprint) to the wireless carriers. A member of the [[Tower REITs]] cohort, where it pairs tightly with [[American Tower]] and [[Crown Castle]].
