# Sandro

French accessible luxury ready-to-wear brand founded 1984 in Paris by Evelyne Chetrite. Part of [[SMCP]] Group (Sandro, [[Maje]], [[Claudie Pierlot]], [[De Fursac]]) — publicly traded on Euronext Paris. SMCP revenue €1.21B (2024), roughly half from Sandro. 1,600+ points of sale in 43 countries. Formerly majority-owned by [[Shandong Ruyi]] (acquired from [[KKR]] in 2016 for ~€1.3B), which defaulted on €250M in bonds in 2021 — stake dropped from 53% to 24%. Board control shifted to institutional shareholders ([[BlackRock]], [[Carlyle Group]]). CEO Isabelle Guichot (since Aug 2021). Expanding into India via [[Reliance Brands]] partnership (2024). Latin America expansion ongoing.
