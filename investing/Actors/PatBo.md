# PatBo

Brazilian fashion brand known for intricate embroidery and swimwear. Founded by Patricia Bonaldi.

## Business

- **Founded:** 2002 by Patricia Bonaldi in Uberlândia, Brazil
- **Signature:** Hand embroidery, beadwork, bold prints, Brazilian artisanal techniques
- **Categories:** Ready-to-wear, swimwear, resort
- **Private company**

## Production

200 women artisans in Uberlândia — Brazil's embroidery capital. All pieces handcrafted.

**Projeto Costurando Sonhos** (2015) — social initiative giving back to local community through craft training.

## Retail

- DTC via patbo.com
- NYFW runway shows (Spring 2026: "Latin Soul" collection)
- Wholesale: Revolve, Net-a-Porter, specialty boutiques

## Positioning

Fits [[Modern luxury]] — founder-led, artisan production, distinctive aesthetic. Brazilian heritage as differentiator in crowded resort/swim market.
