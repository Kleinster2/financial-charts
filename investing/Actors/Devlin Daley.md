**Devlin Daley** — co-founder of [[Instructure]] and co-creator of [[Canvas LMS]]. BYU computer science graduate student in 2008 alongside [[Brian Whitmer]] when they built the Canvas prototype. Departed Instructure in 2014. Subsequently co-founded Derivita, an adaptive math instruction platform, where he serves as CEO.
