ABC — American broadcast television network, owned by [[Disney]]. Home of ABC News and Jimmy Kimmel Live!. Disney's ABC station licences became a pressure point in 2025-26 when the FCC ordered an early renewal process amid the Trump/Kimmel dispute (FT, Jun 2026).
