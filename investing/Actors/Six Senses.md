# Six Senses

Ultra-luxury wellness and sustainability-focused hotel brand. 27 properties in 22 countries, 38 in pipeline. Acquired by [[IHG]] in 2019 for $300M cash from [[Pegasus Capital Advisors]]. Asset-light model — management contracts only, no real estate ownership. Spas are core to brand (37 spas operated). Voted world's top hotel brand pre-acquisition. IHG targeting 60+ properties globally over the next decade. Key locations: Maldives, Seychelles, Thailand, Oman, Portugal (Douro Valley), Courchevel. Newest: Six Senses Myoko (Japan, 2025). Properties typically fewer than 55 rooms — emphasizes intimate, crafted experiences with sustainability DNA.
