# LKQ Corporation

LKQ Corporation (Nasdaq: LKQ) is a distributor of alternative and salvage/recycled auto parts in North America plus a large European mechanical-parts distribution business — a different model from the DIY/DIFM retailers. A member of the [[Auto parts retail]] cohort, in the laggard group.
