## Background

Born in California, spent early years in Lebanon. Moved to US for college. MIT — studied electrical engineering, computer science, and mathematics.

**Career before Kalshi:**
- Goldman Sachs — equity derivatives analyst
- [[Citadel]] — macro trader

At Goldman and Citadel, observed that financial decisions hinge on event predictions but no clean instrument to trade them directly. Met [[Luana Lopes Lara]] at MIT — co-founded Kalshi 2018.
