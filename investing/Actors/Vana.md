**Vana** — Decentralized network for user-owned data DAOs. Individuals contribute personal data to a topic-specific DAO (e.g., social media history, health data), the DAO aggregates and sells access to AI labs, and contributors are compensated proportionally in VANA tokens. In 2026 launched Playground — a platform giving AI developers access to 12.7M+ community-contributed data points.

## Relevance

Parallel to [[Grass]] in the data layer but architecturally different: Grass monetizes bandwidth for web scraping; Vana monetizes consented personal data. Both compete on the "AI training data should compensate humans" thesis. Vana's challenge is market response — token has been weak through 2026 despite operational progress on Playground.
