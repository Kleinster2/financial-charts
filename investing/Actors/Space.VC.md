# Space.VC

**Space.VC** is a US-based venture capital firm focused exclusively on the space industry, participating in [[SpaceX]]'s June 2022 equity round alongside institutional investors like [[Mirae Asset]].

## Synopsis

Space.VC operates as a sector-specialist venture fund in the commercial space economy, a market that has grown from a niche government-contractor ecosystem into a venture-backed infrastructure layer. The firm's participation in [[SpaceX]]'s June 2022 equity round ($1.68B at ~$127B valuation) placed it among the investors accessing the most sought-after private allocation in the space sector. Space-focused VCs like Space.VC, [[Seraphim Capital]], and others have proliferated as the commercial space TAM has expanded — driven by launch cost reductions (SpaceX's reusable rockets cut costs by ~10x), satellite broadband ([[Starlink]], [[OneWeb]]), Earth observation, and in-space servicing.

For the investing vault, Space.VC represents the specialist capital layer in the space venture ecosystem. Unlike generalist funds that participate in SpaceX rounds for portfolio diversification, space-native VCs bring deal flow and sector expertise across the supply chain — from launch and satellite manufacturing to ground systems and space data analytics. The fund's focus aligns with the thesis that commercial space is transitioning from a SpaceX-dominated era to a broader ecosystem play where multiple companies will capture value from the infrastructure SpaceX has built (cheaper access to orbit as a platform for downstream applications).
