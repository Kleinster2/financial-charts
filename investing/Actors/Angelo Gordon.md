# Angelo Gordon

**Credit and real estate investment manager (1988-2023)** — founded by John Angelo and Michael Gordon (ex-L.F. Rothschild arbitrage). Distressed debt, direct lending, real estate. **Acquired by [[TPG]] for $2.7B** (Nov 2023). Now operates as [[TPG Angelo Gordon]].
