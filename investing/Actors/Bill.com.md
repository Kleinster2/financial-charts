# Bill.com

**SMB financial operations platform** — accounts payable, accounts receivable, expense management. Founded 2006 by René Lacerte (PayCycle founder). **NYSE: BILL**. ~$5B market cap. Acquired [[Divvy]] ($2.5B) and Invoice2go ($625M) in 2021. 500K+ business customers.
