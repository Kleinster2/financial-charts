**Olaf Scholz** — German Chancellor 2021-2025 (SPD). Led three-party coalition (SPD/Greens/FDP). Initiated Zeitenwende defense shift after Ukraine invasion (Feb 2022). Replaced by [[Friedrich Merz]] (CDU) after 2025 election.
