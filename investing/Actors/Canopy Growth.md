# Canopy Growth

A Canadian licensed producer, once the highest-valued cannabis company in the world after Constellation Brands took a multibillion-dollar stake in 2017–18. Since the post-2018 cannabis collapse it has restructured repeatedly amid heavy cash burn, and built the Canopy USA holding structure to consolidate US assets (Wana, Jetty, Acreage) the moment US federal law permits — making it a leveraged call option on [[Cannabis rescheduling|US reform]]. Federally legal in Canada and NASDAQ-listed (unlike the US MSOs). A member of the [[Cannabis]] cohort's Canadian LP bloc and the deepest drawdown in the complex (down ~96% over the chart window). Collapses into [[MSOS]]/[[MJ]] like the rest of the cohort.
