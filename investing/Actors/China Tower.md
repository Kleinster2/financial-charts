**China Tower** — the world's largest telecom tower company, jointly owned by [[China Mobile]], [[China Unicom]], and [[China Telecom]], created in 2014 to consolidate the three operators' passive tower infrastructure and end duplicative buildout. Listed in Hong Kong (0788.HK, IPO 2018). Relevant to this vault as the source of the "share of profit from associates" line that lifts the telecoms' net income above operating income — the tower annuity inside the telecom equity story.
