# MOO

MOO (NYSE Arca: MOO) is the VanEck Agribusiness ETF — a broad basket of global agribusiness companies spanning fertilizer/nutrient producers, seeds and crop protection ([[Corteva]], Bayer), farm equipment (Deere), animal health, and ag processors/traders. Because it spreads across the whole ag value chain, it is a broad-agribusiness proxy, not a pure-fertilizer one. Launched 2007.

## Use case

The agribusiness-ETF benchmark for the [[Fertilizer producers#Cluster validation|fertilizer cluster validation]]. Because MOO mixes fertilizer nutrients with seeds, equipment, and processors, the question is whether the pure-fertilizer basket out-coheres it. In the 1Y test, the fertilizer core carries a +0.283 intra-advantage over MOO/[[DBA]], but the cohort is loose and [[Mosaic|MOS]] defects toward MOO. See [[Fertilizer producers]].
