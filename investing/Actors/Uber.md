## Waymo partnership

**Robotaxi on Uber platform:**
- Waymo vehicles bookable via Uber app
- Phoenix deployment (2023)
- Expanding to more cities
- Uber provides demand, Waymo provides AVs

Network effects: largest rider base makes Uber preferred AV partner.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Path to sustained profitability
- AV optionality without capex
- Network effects moat
- Advertising revenue growth
- Freight marketplace potential

**Bear:**
- Regulatory risk (gig worker)
- AV disruption long-term
- Competition from robotaxi
- Delivery margin pressure
