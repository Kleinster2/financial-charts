# NBC

[[Comcast]]-owned broadcast network and media conglomerate operating under the NBCUniversal umbrella. Founded in 1926, NBC is the oldest major broadcast network in the US. The sports division holds some of the most valuable live rights in American media: [[NFL]] Sunday Night Football (the most-watched weekly primetime program), exclusive [[Olympics]] US broadcast rights through 2032 ($7.75B deal), and [[Premier League]] US rights. NBC was the original US broadcaster for [[Formula 1]] (2013-2017) but declined to renew the $4M/year deal — a decision that looks catastrophic in hindsight given F1's subsequent US viewership explosion under [[ESPN]] and [[Liberty Media]]'s promotion. The Peacock streaming service (~36M paid subscribers as of early 2026) has been a drag on NBCUniversal's profitability, with cumulative losses exceeding $5B before approaching break-even.
