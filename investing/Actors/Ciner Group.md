# Ciner Group

Turkish conglomerate with major natural [[Soda Ash|soda ash]] operations. ~7 Mt global trona capacity. The Ciner family also controls [[WE Soda]] (London-headquartered). Sold US Wyoming operations (Ciner Resources, formerly CINR) to [[Sisecam]] in Dec 2021 for a controlling stake, with the partnership eventually delisted from NYSE (Dec 2024). Retains Turkish soda ash operations.
