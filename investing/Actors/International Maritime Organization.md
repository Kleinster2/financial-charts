**International Maritime Organization** — UN specialized agency responsible for regulating international shipping. Headquartered in London. Sets global standards for maritime safety, environmental protection, and emissions.

---

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Founded | 1948 (operational 1959) |
| HQ | London |
| Members | 176 states |
| Type | UN specialized agency |
| Emissions target | Halve shipping emissions (3% of global CO2) by 2050 |
