**Investor AB** — [[Sweden]]'s largest listed holding company and the investment vehicle of the Wallenberg family (5th/6th generation). Controls a portfolio of Nordic and global blue chips worth ~$500B+ combined market cap through dual-class A-shares with 10x voting rights. The only major European holding company to consistently trade at a NAV premium (+6%). Ticker: INVE-B (OMX Stockholm).
