Flagship Pioneering — Cambridge, Massachusetts life-sciences company-creation platform. Relevant to the investing vault as the creator/backer of platform biotech companies such as [[Generate Biomedicines]], where AI-enabled discovery makes the line between venture portfolio and industrial research platform important.

## Why it matters

Flagship is less a conventional venture investor than a venture studio for scientific platforms. In the [[NVIDIA]] [[13F]] context, it matters because a small visible stake in [[Generate Biomedicines]] shows NVIDIA's ecosystem investing reaching beyond AI infrastructure into AI-enabled drug discovery.
