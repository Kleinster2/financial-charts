# Vizio

Smart TV manufacturer and advertising platform. Acquired by [[Walmart]] in December 2024 for $2.3B ($11.50/share). Formerly traded on the NYSE under VZIO before the acquisition. Key data partner for [[Nielsen]]'s Big Data + Panel measurement methodology. Vizio's SmartCast OS and ACR (automatic content recognition) data provide viewing insights across millions of connected TVs.
