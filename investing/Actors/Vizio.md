# Vizio

Smart TV manufacturer and advertising platform. Acquired by [[Walmart]] in December 2024 for $2.3B ($11.50/share). Previously publicly traded (NYSE: VZIO). Key data partner for [[Nielsen]]'s Big Data + Panel measurement methodology. Vizio's SmartCast OS and ACR (automatic content recognition) data provide viewing insights across millions of connected TVs.
