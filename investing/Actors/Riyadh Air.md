**Riyadh Air** — new Saudi national airline wholly owned by [[Saudi PIF]], being built from scratch as part of [[Saudi Vision 2030]]. Headquartered in Riyadh, intended to position [[Saudi Arabia]] as a global aviation hub competing with [[Emirates]] and [[Qatar Airways]].
