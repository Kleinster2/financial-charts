# Hidden Forces

Long-form interview podcast hosted by Demetri Kofinas, focused on macro economics, geopolitics, finance, and the structural forces shaping markets. Guests include [[Brad Setser]] (global geopolitics of finance), [[Helima Croft]] adjacent voices, and a broader rotation of think-tank + academic + finance-industry analysts. Distinguished by long-form interview format (typically 90+ minutes) and a deep-dive analytical register rather than news-cycle commentary.
