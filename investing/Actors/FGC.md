#actor #institution #brazil #finance #deposit-insurance

**FGC (Fundo Garantidor de Créditos)** — Brazil's deposit guarantee fund, private-sector funded. Provides coverage up to R$250,000 per person per institution. Pre-crisis reserves ~R$120B. [[Banco Master]] orbit payout: R$52B (~43% of reserves). Potential [[Banco Digimais]] exposure: R$8.5B+ would push total payouts over R$60B (50%+ of pre-crisis reserves).
