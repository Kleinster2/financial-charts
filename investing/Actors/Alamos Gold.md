# Alamos Gold

**Alamos Gold (AGI)** — Canadian intermediate gold producer headquartered in Toronto, listed on the NYSE and TSX (both AGI). Around 600,000 ounces of annual production, weighted to lower-risk jurisdictions: the Young-Davidson, Island Gold, and Magino mines in Ontario (Island Gold and the adjacent Magino are being run as one combined complex) plus Mulatos in Mexico, with the Lynn Lake project (Manitoba) in development. Positioned as a growth-oriented intermediate — smaller than the [[Newmont]]/[[Barrick Gold]]/[[Agnico Eagle]] seniors but with a Canada-heavy, expanding production profile.
