# Whataburger

**Whataburger** — Texas-founded (1950, Corpus Christi) regional burger chain concentrated in Texas and the US South; majority-owned since 2019 by merchant bank [[BDT & MSD Partners]] (then BDT Capital Partners), with the founding Dobson family retaining a minority — a private-equity-controlled counterpoint to founder-controlled [[Five Guys]] and [[In-N-Out Burger]].
