**Apeiron Investment Group** — Malta-based family-office-style investment firm founded and owned by [[Christian Angermayer]]. Investment focus is concentrated in biotechnology, life sciences, psychedelics, longevity, and adjacent frontier-medicine bets. Notable portfolio companies include atai Life Sciences (founder), Compass Pathways (early backer), and [[Enhanced Group]] (NYSE: ENHA), where Apeiron seeded the company in 2023 and co-led the Series B round. Functions as the primary capital-deployment vehicle for [[Christian Angermayer]]'s personal investment program.
