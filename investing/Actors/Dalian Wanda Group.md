**Dalian Wanda Group** is the Chinese property-and-entertainment conglomerate founded by Wang Jianlin (Dalian, 1988) that in the mid-2010s assembled the largest Chinese-owned footprint in Hollywood — majority control of [[AMC Entertainment]] (2012) and ownership of [[Legendary Entertainment]] (2016, ~$3.5B) — then unwound it as Beijing curbed offshore leverage and the China property downturn forced asset sales.

## The Hollywood build-and-unwind

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 2012 | Acquires majority of [[AMC Entertainment]] |
| 2016 | Acquires [[Legendary Entertainment]] for ~$3.5B |
| 2017+ | Beijing curbs "irrational" offshore M&A; Wanda begins selling hotels, malls, overseas assets |
| 2021 | AMC meme-stock dilution takes Wanda's stake to negligible; exits board |
| Oct 2024 | [[Legendary Entertainment]] buys out Wanda's remaining stake — full Hollywood exit |

The arc is the canonical case of the 2010s Chinese outbound-M&A wave and its forced reversal — relevant context for [[Legendary Entertainment]]'s current Apollo-backed structure and for the China property-deleveraging complex ([[Evergrande]], [[Country Garden]], [[Three Red Lines]]).
