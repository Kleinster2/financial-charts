## January 2026 chokepoint power framing

Chatham House described a Russia-China naval exercise off South Africa's Indian Ocean coast as a chokepoint power play. The key investing read is that naval signaling around [[Suez Canal]], [[Bab el-Mandeb]], [[Strait of Malacca]], [[Strait of Hormuz]], and [[Cape of Good Hope]] can affect perceived route security even before physical trade is blocked.
