**PREB** (Puerto Rico Energy Bureau) — Independent energy regulator for [[Puerto Rico]], part of the Public Service Regulatory Board. Oversees rate cases, generation procurement, and utility performance for [[LUMA Energy]] and [[Genera PR]].
