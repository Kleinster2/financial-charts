Spencer Dale — visiting professor in practice at the [[LSE|London School of Economics]], formerly chief economist at [[BP]]. Provided supply-shock calculations at a [[NIESR]] webinar (early April 2026) framing the [[2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis|Iran war]] oil disruption as roughly half of [[Covid-19]] in scale, and noting that the [[Crack spread|refined product price surge]] better reflects consumer pain than the headline crude oil price.
