# OpenAI

#actor #ai #usa #private

Overview
- US AI lab and platform company (founded 2015; reorganized as capped‑profit structure)
- Flagship GPT family (GPT‑5 current), ChatGPT product line, Codex developer tools
- Distribution: ChatGPT (Plus/Team/Pro/Enterprise/Edu), API, Codex/Assistants

Products (selected)
- [[OpenAI GPT-5.4]] — GPT‑5 minor version (agentic focus; Thinking/Pro variants)
- [[OpenAI GPT-4.5]] — 2025 research preview; later deprecated for most tiers
- GPT‑5 family — supersedes GPT‑4.x; multiple variants including “Thinking” and “Pro”
- ChatGPT — consumer/enterprise interface for GPT family
- Codex — coding/copilot tooling built on GPT models

Model cadence / positioning
- Shift from pure chat LLMs toward agentic capabilities (native computer use, tool/API calling, multi‑step retrieval)
- Tiered variants: core vs “Thinking” (reasoning‑forward) vs “Pro” (max performance)

Go‑to‑market & pricing
- Subscription tiers (Plus/Team/Pro/Enterprise/Edu) + metered API; premium pricing for top models
- Enterprise features: security/compliance, longer context, higher rate limits

Selected partners/coverage
- Data/finance: FactSet, MSCI, Moody’s, Third Bridge (per launch reporting)
- Platform integrations across productivity and developer ecosystems

Timeline (recent)
- 2025‑02‑27 — GPT‑4.5 (research preview)
- 2025‑04‑14 — 4.5 API deprecation announced (effective 2025‑07‑14)
- 2025‑08‑07 — GPT‑5 launch; 4.5 removed for most tiers
- 2026‑03‑05 — GPT‑5.4 released; agentic focus; Thinking/Pro variants
