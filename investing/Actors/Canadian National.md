# Canadian National

Canadian National Railway (NYSE/TSX: CNI) is a North American Class I railroad with a transcontinental Canadian network plus a unique north-south line reaching the US Gulf Coast (Chicago to the Gulf via the former Illinois Central). A member of the [[Railroads]] cohort, pairing with [[Canadian Pacific Kansas City]] in the Canadian sub-group.
