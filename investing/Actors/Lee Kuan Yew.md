**Lee Kuan Yew** — Founding Prime Minister of [[Singapore]] (1959-1990). Architect of Singapore's transformation from a low-income port city to a high-income financial hub in one generation. Established the institutional pillars: English as working language, zero-tolerance corruption, rule of law, compulsory national service, and the sovereign wealth fund model ([[GIC]], [[Temasek]]). Father of [[Lee Hsien Loong]]. Died March 23, 2015.
