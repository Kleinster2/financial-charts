## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Diversified client base
- Healthcare and precision marketing growth
- Dividend yield ~3%
- AI could boost productivity, cut costs

**Bear:**
- AI-generated content threatens creative services
- In-housing by clients
- Secular decline in traditional advertising
- Talent costs

## February 2026: Claude Cowork selloff

**Feb 3-4, 2026:** Stock fell **-11%** on [[Claude Cowork disruption February 2026|Claude Cowork launch]].

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Two-day decline | -11% |
| Catalyst | Claude Cowork marketing automation |

**Disruption thesis:** Claude Cowork Marketing automates:
- Campaign ideation and strategy
- Content creation (copy, creative briefs)
- Media planning optimization
- Reporting and analytics

**What agencies sell:** Human creativity and strategic thinking. If AI can do 80% of the work, agencies can't justify current billing rates.

**Bull case:** Clients pay for relationships and accountability, not just output. Complex brand strategy still needs humans.
