**JDE Peet's** -- Dutch-listed global coffee company. Brands include Jacobs, Douwe Egberts, L'OR, Peet's Coffee, Senseo, Tassimo. Formed 2015 from merger of D.E. Master Blenders 1753 and Mondelez coffee division. [[JAB Holding]] controls ~68%. Being acquired by [[Keurig Dr Pepper]] for EUR15.7B ($18.4B) -- deal announced Aug 2025, expected to close H1 2026.
