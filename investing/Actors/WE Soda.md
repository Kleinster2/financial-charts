# WE Soda

World's largest producer of natural [[Soda Ash|soda ash]], headquartered in London. Operates two production facilities in [[Turkey]] (Kazan Soda and Eti Soda) and acquired Genesis Alkali's Wyoming operations in Feb 2025 for $1.425B. Combined capacity ~9+ Mt/year. Attempted a $7.5B LSE IPO in June 2023 but cancelled citing "extreme investor caution."
