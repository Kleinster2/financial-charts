**Heathrow Airport** — London's primary international airport and the world's busiest by international passenger traffic. [[Saudi PIF]] acquired a 15% stake in December 2024 as part of a combined $4.2B deal with [[Ardian]], valuing the airport at approximately £8.7B. Operated by Heathrow Airport Holdings.
