## The name

"2PointZero" follows Abu Dhabi's pattern of using "42" references — a homage to *The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy*, where 42 is the answer to "the ultimate question of life, the universe, and everything."

See also: [[G42]], [[M42]] (healthcare JV).
