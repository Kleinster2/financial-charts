**The Com** — loose decentralized community of native-English-speaking cybercriminals (predominantly teens and young adults from the United States, United Kingdom, and Canada) coordinating across Discord, Telegram, and gaming-adjacent platforms. The community originated in SIM-swapping subculture circa 2018-2020 and evolved into a recruiting pool for higher-impact cybercrime operations. The "Hacker Com" subset specifically refers to operators focused on enterprise breaches rather than financial-fraud SIM swaps. Core threat-actor brands rooted in The Com include [[Scattered Spider]], [[ShinyHunters]], [[LAPSUS$]], and the August 2025 [[Scattered Lapsus$ Hunters]] alliance. The community's structural property — peer recruitment, fluid membership, brand portability across operators — makes it durable under law-enforcement pressure: arrested operators are replaced from the broader pool faster than disruption can shrink the community.
