# BrazilCham

**BrazilCham** — Brazilian-American Chamber of Commerce. Independent, not-for-profit business membership organization based in New York, founded 1969 (continuing the mission of the American Brazilian Association, established 1927). Mission: strengthen trade and investment between Brazil and the United States. Produces the annual Person of the Year Awards Gala (the anchor event of [[Brazil Week NYC 2026]]) and the Brazil Summit (25th edition in 2026, co-produced with the [[Financial Times|FT]]).

## Why it matters

BrazilCham is the most institutionally durable convening platform for the Brazil-US business axis. Its sponsor stack — [[Banco do Brasil]], [[Itaú Unibanco]], [[Citigroup|Citi]], [[JPMorgan Chase|J.P. Morgan]], [[Qualcomm]] in the Inner Circle; [[Morgan Stanley]], [[MUFG]] at Platinum — functions as the active wholesale-banking syndicate that underwrites Brazilian deal flow into US capital markets. The chamber's Person of the Year selection signals which Brazilian and American executives the syndicate views as the year's most relevant bilateral connectors.
