# Puma Energy

Global midstream and downstream fuels company — terminals, storage, retail fuel stations, aviation fuel, and bitumen. Spun out of [[Trafigura]] as a separate business in the early 2010s, retained majority-Trafigura ownership for over a decade. Operates across Africa, Latin America, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.
