## State connection

CITIC Group is state-owned — makes CITIC Capital indirectly state-linked. This creates interesting dynamics:

- Access to Chinese deal flow and relationships
- Credibility with state-owned counterparties
- But also: potential geopolitical scrutiny on outbound investments
