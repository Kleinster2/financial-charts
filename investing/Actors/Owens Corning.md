# Owens Corning

Owens Corning (NYSE: OC) is a US building-products maker — roofing, insulation, and (after the 2024 Masonite acquisition) doors. A member of the [[Building products]] cohort, in the housing-products pole.

## Jun 24 2026 — ROAD to Housing Act rally

OC closed +8.10% on Jun 24 (+2.92σ on a flat [[SPY]], -0.04%) after Congress passed the [[21st Century ROAD to Housing Act]] and the 10-year yield fell ~9bps to 4.40%. As a housing-products-pole name (roofing and insulation, residential demand), OC rode the residential-supply catalyst with the rest of its pole ([[Fortune Brands|FBIN]] +9.8%, [[Masco|MAS]] +6.6%) while the HVAC pole lagged — live confirmation of the [[Building products]] cluster split.
