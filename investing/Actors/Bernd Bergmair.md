#actor #person #media

[[Bernd Bergmair]] — Austrian entrepreneur, former hidden majority owner of [[Aylo]] (MindGeek). Sold [[RedTube]] to Manwin in 2013, then accumulated majority ownership through offshore structures in Luxembourg and Curaçao. Identity concealed until [[Financial Times]] exposé in December 2020 (used alias "Bernard Bergemar" in court documents). Sold stake to [[Ethical Capital Partners]] as part of $400M deal in March 2023. Also linked to Lukoil-connected assets per FinTelegram reporting.
