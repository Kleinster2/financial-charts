## Varda Space Industries

Space manufacturing startup — builds reentry capsules to manufacture pharmaceuticals and materials in microgravity, then returns them to Earth. Also developing hypersonic testbed capabilities (dual-use: pharma + defense). Backed by [[Founders Fund]], [[Khosla Ventures]], and others.
