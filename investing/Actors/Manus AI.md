# Manus AI

Chinese AI agent platform designed for complex autonomous tasks — research reports, data analysis, multi-step workflows. Founded in China and backed by [[HongShan]] ([[Neil Shen]]'s post-Sequoia China vehicle) at an ~$85M valuation. Gained viral attention in early 2025 with demos showing the agent completing research tasks end-to-end with minimal human supervision. Acquired by [[Meta]] for $2B+ in 2025, making it one of the largest AI acqui-hires and delivering ~24x returns for HongShan — a standout exit that validated Shen's strategy of aggressive early-stage AI investing post-Sequoia split. The acquisition gave Meta a ready-made agent framework to accelerate its push into AI assistants beyond the chatbot paradigm.
