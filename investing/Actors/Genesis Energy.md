# Genesis Energy

Houston-based midstream energy MLP (NYSE: GEL). Formerly had a major [[Soda Ash|soda ash]] segment through Genesis Alkali, which operated Wyoming trona mining and processing (Westvaco + Granger facilities, 4.35 Mt/year combined capacity). Sold Genesis Alkali to [[WE Soda]] for $1.425B (closed Feb 28, 2025). Now a pure-play midstream energy company focused on offshore pipeline transportation, sodium minerals and sulfur services, and marine transportation.
