## Role and significance

The April 2026 promotion to Chief Scientist of [[Google Cloud]] (held alongside the DeepMind VP role) is structurally similar to [[Koray Kavukcuoglu]]'s June 2025 elevation to Google-wide Chief AI Architect: senior DeepMind researchers given expanded Google-wide mandates at a moment when the [[DeepMind diaspora]] is at record output.

Kohli's scope is distinct from Kavukcuoglu's. Where Kavukcuoglu owns model-to-product integration across Google surfaces, Kohli owns the scientific applications of AI — the franchise that produced AlphaFold and continues to produce Alpha-prefixed scientific systems. The Google Cloud title means his team's outputs route into enterprise/biotech customers via cloud rather than into consumer products.

The [[Isomorphic Labs]] spinout (Hassabis as CEO, $2.7B+ raised through May 2026 Series B) emerged from Kohli's unit. The promotion likely reflects both the AlphaFold/Nobel halo and the commercial validation of the AI-for-science approach via Isomorphic's pharma deals (~$3B [[Eli Lilly]] + [[Novartis]]).
