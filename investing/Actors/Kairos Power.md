**Kairos Power** — Advanced nuclear startup. Molten salt-cooled reactor design. Google deal for 500 MW by 2030. One of the most credible SMR developers.

---

## Why Kairos matters

Kairos has the first major hyperscaler SMR commitment:

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Partner | [[Google]] |
| Capacity | 500 MW by 2030 |
| Technology | Molten salt-cooled, pebble fuel |
| Significance | First Big Tech SMR deal |

---

## Technology: Fluoride salt-cooled

| Feature | Advantage |
|---------|-----------|
| Coolant | Molten fluoride salt |
| Fuel | TRISO pebbles |
| Pressure | Low (safer) |
| Temperature | High (efficient) |
| Passive safety | No active cooling needed |

**Hermes reactor**: Demonstration unit under construction in Tennessee. First non-water-cooled reactor approved by NRC in decades.
