# Lindsey Graham

Role: US Senator
State: South Carolina
Party: Republican
In office since: 2003

Senior Republican on Senate Judiciary and Appropriations committees. Defense hawk, China hawk, and key voice on national security policy. Spoke at the [[Hill and Valley Forum]] in May 2024. Has been vocal on TikTok ban, AI regulation, and defense spending.
