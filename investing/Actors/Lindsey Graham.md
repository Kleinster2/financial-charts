# Lindsey Graham

Senior US Senator from [[South Carolina]] (since 2003) and one of the Senate's most vocal defense hawks. Serves on the Senate Judiciary, Appropriations, and Budget committees. Graham has been a consistent advocate for higher defense spending, aggressive China policy, and military intervention — he was among the first senators to call for direct strikes on [[Iran]] during the 2026 Gulf crisis. Spoke at the [[Hill and Valley Forum]] in May 2024, emphasizing the national security dimensions of AI development. Key legislative positions include support for the [[TikTok]] forced-sale bill, expanded military aid to [[Ukraine]] and [[Israel]], and increased semiconductor reshoring incentives.
