# Ursula von der Leyen

**Ursula von der Leyen** is President of the [[European Commission]] and a central [[EU]] policy actor for trade defense, industrial policy, critical raw materials, and European security spending.

## Market-policy relevance

Von der Leyen is the Commission-side operator for the [[EU]]'s China economic-security turn. At the June 18 2026 leaders' summit, FT reporting said she described the EU goods deficit with [[China]] as roughly EUR1bn per day and was instructed to continue dialogue with [[China|Beijing]] while developing stronger trade-defense and industrial-policy tools. See [[EU-China trade]].
