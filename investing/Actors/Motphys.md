#actor #robotics #simulation #private

**Motphys** is a Chinese startup that has built an embodied intelligence simulation platform for robot training. Based in Wuhan. Co-founded by Zhao Xiang.

## Key positions

Uses VR headsets to collect robot training data more efficiently and at lower cost than physical teleoperation. Zhao Xiang: "An intelligence breakthrough may require hundreds of millions or even billions of hours of data." Operates from a state-backed Wuhan lab.
