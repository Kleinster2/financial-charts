**Oman Investment Authority** — Oman's sovereign wealth fund, formed 2020 by merging the State General Reserve Fund and Oman Investment Fund. President: Abdulsalam Al Murshidi. Estimated AUM ~$17-18B. Invests across energy, infrastructure, and diversification projects aligned with Oman Vision 2040.
