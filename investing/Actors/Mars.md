## Novo Nordisk board observer (March 2026)

Mars CEO Poul Weihrauch — a Danish national — joined the [[Novo Nordisk]] board as an observer in March 2026, with the expectation of becoming a non-executive director in 2027. The brief: bring FMCG consumer-engagement expertise to Novo's GLP-1 portfolio as the company shifts from a science-led to a consumer-led commercial model. The hire is one of several Novo board moves under [[Mike Doustdar]] aimed at consumerizing the franchise — see [[Pharma consumerization]] for the broader pattern.

The cross-pollination is notable: a confectionery and petcare CEO advising a weight-loss-drug manufacturer. The implicit framing is that GLP-1s and consumer-packaged-goods compete for the same shelf-of-mind in an obesity-conscious consumer base, and that the marketing skills are transferable.
