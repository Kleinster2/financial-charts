# New Balance

**Private athletic footwear company** — $7.8B revenue (2024, +20%), fourth consecutive year of 20%+ growth. Headquartered in Boston, family-owned (Davis family). Taking share from [[Nike]] as cultural momentum shifts. Known for "dad shoe" aesthetic turned fashion statement (550, 990, 2002R). US-made heritage (25% of US sales manufactured domestically). ~10,000 employees. CEO Joe Preston targeting $10B revenue in coming years.
