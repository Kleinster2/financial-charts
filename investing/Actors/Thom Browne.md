# Thom Browne

**American luxury fashion house** — shrunken suiting, avant-garde tailoring. Founded 2001 by designer Thom Browne. Owned by [[Zegna]] Group (acquired 2018). €129M revenue H1 2025 (-22% YoY). Struggling with wholesale reduction strategy. New CEO Sam Lobban appointed July 2025.

## Investment case

*Note: Thom Browne is part of [[Zegna]] Group (NYSE: ZGN).*

**Bull:**
- Iconic brand with loyal following
- New CEO with retail expertise
- Q3 2025 showed improvement
- DTC shift eventually higher margin
- Zegna support and resources

**Bear:**
- -22% revenue decline
- Wholesale exit painful
- Avant-garde limits mass appeal
- Execution risk under new leadership
- Drag on Zegna Group
