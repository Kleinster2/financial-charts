## Composition

Fixed-rate, investment-grade Canadian bonds with at least 10 years remaining maturity. Mix of federal, provincial, and corporate issuers. Duration ~14 years. Monthly distributions.

## Use Case

Canadian long-duration rates proxy. In the global bonds chart, XLB.TO represents the Canadian leg alongside [[TLT]], [[2510.T]], [[IGLT.L]], [[IBGL.L]], [[GOVT.AX]], and [[CBON]]. Note: ticker "XLB" on TSX is unrelated to [[XLB]] (US materials sector ETF).
