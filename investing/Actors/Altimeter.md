**Altimeter Capital** is a technology-focused investment firm founded by Brad Gerstner in 2008, managing both public equities and private venture capital. Headquartered in Menlo Park, California, the firm is known for a crossover strategy — taking private positions in late-stage tech companies and maintaining them through IPO into public markets.

Altimeter gained wide visibility through its SPAC activity during 2020-2021: Altimeter Growth Corp 1 took [[Grab]] Holdings public in a $40B deal (which subsequently collapsed in value), and a second SPAC targeted [[Aurora Innovation]] for autonomous trucking. The SPAC record was mixed, but the firm's private portfolio has included stronger performers. Gerstner has been a vocal advocate for AI investment, and Altimeter participated in [[Anthropic]]'s massive Series G round ($20B+ valuation, Feb 2026), signaling conviction in foundation model companies.

The firm also held meaningful positions in [[Snowflake]], [[MongoDB]], and other enterprise cloud/data companies during their growth phases. Gerstner is active on social media and at conferences (including an annual Altimeter conference), positioning himself as a thought leader on tech investing and AI.
