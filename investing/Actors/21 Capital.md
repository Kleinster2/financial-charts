# 21 Capital

$3B [[Bitcoin]] investment vehicle launched by [[Brandon Lutnick]] through Cantor Equity Partners, a SPAC founded under [[Cantor Fitzgerald]]. Backed by [[Tether]] ($1.5B in BTC), [[SoftBank]] ($900M), and [[Bitfinex]] ($600M). Aims to replicate [[MicroStrategy]]'s Bitcoin treasury strategy — using equity and debt issuance to accumulate BTC. Announced April 2025.
