# NIOC

National Iranian Oil Company — [[Iran]]'s state oil monopoly, responsible for exploration, production, and export of crude oil and natural gas. Reports to the Ministry of Petroleum. Controls ~3.2M bbl/day of production capacity and manages [[Iran]]'s oil reserves (~209B barrels, 4th largest globally).

NIOC is the seller-of-record for legitimate Iranian crude transactions, including the March 2026 sanctions waiver sales. Under comprehensive [[US sanctions]], most transactions route through intermediaries and [[Shadow fleet|shadow fleet]] networks. The March 20 waiver temporarily permitted direct purchases from NIOC for oil already at sea.
