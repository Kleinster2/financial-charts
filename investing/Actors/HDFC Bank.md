## March 19, 2026 crash

Stock fell ~9% to 52-week low after part-time Chairman Atanu Chakraborty resigned citing "ethical concerns" — no further detail provided. As the heaviest-weighted stock in both Sensex and Nifty, the crash dragged both indices sharply lower (Sensex -1,900pts, Nifty below 23,200). The vague "ethical concerns" framing without elaboration spooked markets. Global headwinds amplified the move: [[Federal Reserve]] held rates, raised inflation forecast, and Gulf energy infrastructure attacks sent oil to $113.54.
