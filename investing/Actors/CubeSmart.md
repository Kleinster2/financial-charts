# CubeSmart

CubeSmart (NYSE: CUBE) is a US self-storage REIT — the third-largest of the public operators — owning, operating, and managing self-storage facilities, with a concentration in dense urban markets. A pure self-storage single-asset REIT. A member of the [[Self-storage REITs]] cohort.
