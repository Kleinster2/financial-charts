# Nextracker

Nextracker (Nasdaq: NXT) is the leading maker of single-axis solar trackers — the motorized mounting systems that tilt utility-scale solar panels to follow the sun and lift energy yield. Spun off from [[Flex]] and IPO'd February 2023, it is a utility-scale, policy/utility-capex-driven name (IRA manufacturing incentives, domestic content, utility solar buildout), structurally distinct from the rate-sensitive residential-solar names. Pairs with [[First Solar]] as the utility-scale leg of the [[Solar]] cohort.
