# Canadian Pacific Kansas City

Canadian Pacific Kansas City (NYSE/TSX: CP) is a North American Class I railroad formed by the 2023 Canadian Pacific–Kansas City Southern merger — the only single-line railroad connecting Canada, the US, and Mexico. A member of the [[Railroads]] cohort, pairing with [[Canadian National]] in the Canadian sub-group.
