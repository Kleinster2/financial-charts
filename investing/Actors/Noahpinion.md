# Noahpinion

Noahpinion is [[Noah Smith]]'s independent economics and technology newsletter, published on [[Substack]]. Smith describes it as mostly economics, with recurring technology, geopolitics, and culture coverage, written from an economics-trained perspective.

## Vault relevance

Noahpinion is most useful when it turns a technology story into a labor, productivity, or political-economy mechanism. The source is less of a company-specific tape source and more of a framework source: how AI adoption changes work organization, what industrial policy is trying to solve, where US-China decoupling changes incentives, and how macro institutions respond to new technology.
