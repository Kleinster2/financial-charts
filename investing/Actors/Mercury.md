## Investment relevance

**Why Mercury matters:**

1. **Startup ecosystem infrastructure** — Default bank for YC companies, tech startups.

2. **SVB beneficiary** — Gained significant deposits after SVB collapse (Mar 2023).

3. **Bank charter pursuit** — If approved, becomes actual bank vs fintech.

4. **IPO candidate** — $3.5B valuation, $650M revenue, profitable unit economics.

5. **YC network effects** — Founder is YC insider; built for YC companies.
