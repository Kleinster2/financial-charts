# Aidan Powers-Riggs

Analyst at the [[CSIS]] with published research on Chinese commercial-space firms and [[Military-civil fusion|MCF]] provenance. Named in the FT investigation (April 14, 2026) characterising [[Emposat]] as "a product of the state and military establishment" despite commercial form. Conducted the primary open-source research tying Emposat's funding to national military-civil fusion vehicles (see [[IRGC Chinese satellite targeting March 2026]], [[Chinese dual-use commercial satellite exports]]).
