**Stanley Ho** (d. May 26, 2020, age 98) — "King of Gambling," [[Macau]] casino patriarch who held a 40-year monopoly on Macau gaming (1962-2002). Founded [[SJM Holdings]]. Father of [[Lawrence Ho]] ([[Melco Resorts]]) and [[Pansy Ho]] ([[MGM China]] partner).
