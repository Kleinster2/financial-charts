# LuxExperience

**Global luxury e-commerce platform** — formed from Mytheresa + YNAP merger (April 2025). NYSE: LUXE. Owns Mytheresa, [[Net-a-Porter]], MR PORTER, YOOX, THE OUTNET. [[Richemont]] holds 33%. Medium-term target: €4B revenue, 7-9% EBITDA margin. Separating luxury from off-price operations.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Scale from combination (€4B target)
- Mytheresa profitable and growing
- Richemont support (credit facility, board)
- Off-price separation clarifies model
- Luxury e-commerce consolidation winner
- Trading at 1.6x P/E (cheap)

**Bear:**
- YNAP integration risk (historically troubled asset)
- Near-term EBITDA negative (-2% to +1%)
- Richemont control limits upside
- Brand DTC channels growing
- Luxury slowdown hurts aspirational buyers
