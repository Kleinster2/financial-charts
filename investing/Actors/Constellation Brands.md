# Constellation Brands

**Major US beverage company** — NYSE: STZ. Dominant in US beer through its exclusive rights to import, market, and distribute [[Grupo Modelo]] brands (Corona, Modelo Especial, Pacifico) in the US. Also holds wine and spirits portfolio (Kim Crawford, Meiomi, High West, Casa Noble). Acquired Tipsy Scoop (alcohol-infused ice cream), validating the category where [[Aubi & Ramsa]] competes.
