Deng Xiaoping — Paramount leader of [[China]] (1978-1992). Architect of "reform and opening up" that transformed China from an agrarian economy into the world's manufacturing base. Established the two-term succession norm that [[Xi Jinping]] broke in 2022. Father of the Special Economic Zones, the township-village enterprise model, and the foundational bargain: economic freedom in exchange for political obedience.
