**CADE** (Conselho Administrativo de Defesa Econômica) — [[Brazil]]'s antitrust authority, responsible for merger review and competition enforcement. Part of the Ministry of Justice. Decisions by its superintendence become final after 15 days if no appeal or change by board members (administrative tribunal).
