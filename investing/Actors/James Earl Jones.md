# James Earl Jones

Legendary actor (voice of Darth Vader, Mufasa) who licensed his iconic voice to live forever through AI. Signed a deal with [[Lucasfilm]]/[[Disney]] to allow AI recreation of his Darth Vader voice in perpetuity, even after his death (Sep 2024). One of the first major actors to formally consent to posthumous AI voice usage — setting a precedent for the estate-level economics of AI voice licensing.
