**Ahmed Al-Khateeb** — Minister of Tourism of [[Saudi Arabia]] since 2020. Former advisor to the Royal Court and former chairman of the Saudi Commission for Tourism and National Heritage. Leads the tourism pillar of [[Saudi Vision 2030]], which targets 150 million annual visits by 2030.
