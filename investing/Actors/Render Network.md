**Render Network** — Decentralized GPU rendering network, originally built by OTOY for 3D content creation, now expanded to AI video synthesis and general GPU compute. Migrated from [[Ethereum]] (RNDR) to [[Solana]] (RENDER) 2023-2024 at a 1:1 swap. Used by visual effects studios, AI video startups, and — increasingly — as one of the "industry standard" backends for decentralized visual AI workloads.

## Relevance to decentralized AI

Render is one of three major decentralized GPU networks (alongside [[Akash Network]] and [[io.net]]) that together challenge [[AWS]] on-demand GPU pricing with 60-80% discounts. Differentiated focus: visual workloads — 3D rendering, AI video gen, VFX pipelines. Integration with major creative suites (including iPad Pro ecosystem) positions it as the default "decentralized visual compute" provider.
