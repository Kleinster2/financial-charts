# OxyChem

**OxyChem** (Occidental Chemical Corporation) is the chemical manufacturing subsidiary of [[Occidental Petroleum]] and one of the largest chlor-alkali producers in North America with ~3.3 Mt NaOH capacity. Produces both diaphragm and membrane-grade caustic soda. Also a major producer of PVC resins and chlorinated solvents.
