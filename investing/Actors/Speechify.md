# Speechify

AI text-to-speech platform founded in 2017 by brothers Cliff and Erix Weitzman. Converts text into natural-sounding audio across 1,000+ voices in 60+ languages. Revenue ~$17.6M (Jul 2025 est.), ~160 employees. Won Apple Design Award at WWDC 2025. 20M+ users across Chrome extension, iOS, and Android. Celebrity voice partnerships with Gwyneth Paltrow and Snoop Dogg. Competes with [[ElevenLabs]] in the AI voice/TTS space, though Speechify focuses more on accessibility and reading while ElevenLabs targets content creation and voice cloning.
