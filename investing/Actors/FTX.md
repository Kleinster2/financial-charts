# FTX

FTX was a cryptocurrency exchange founded by [[Sam Bankman-Fried]] in 2019. Collapsed in November 2022 amid revelations of customer fund misappropriation. The implosion triggered an industry-wide crisis and directly motivated the creation of [[Hyperliquid]] by ex-[[Hudson River Trading]] trader [[Jeff Yan]].
