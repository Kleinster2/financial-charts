# Jim Krane

Diana Tamari Sabbagh Fellow in Middle East Energy Studies at the [[Baker Institute]]'s Center for Energy Studies, Rice University. Specializes in Middle East oil and gas producers and how Gulf states are responding to decarbonization and economic diversification pressures. Lead vault attribution source for Baker Institute estimates of [[UAE]] and [[Saudi Arabia]] production economics, including the $50-70B/yr unconstrained-revenue figure cited in the [[2026 UAE OPEC exit]] note.
