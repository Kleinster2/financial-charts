Sennheiser — German audio company. Founded 1945 in Wedemark. Known for headphones, microphones, and wireless systems. Consumer electronics division sold to [[Sonova]] (2021); professional audio division retained by the Sennheiser family. Competes with [[Shure]], [[Rode Microphones]], [[Audio-Technica]], and [[Yamaha]].
