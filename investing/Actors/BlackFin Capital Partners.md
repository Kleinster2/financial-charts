**BlackFin Capital Partners** — Paris-based private equity firm focused on taking controlling stakes in financial-services companies across Europe. [[Blue Owl]] agreed to acquire a minority interest on Apr 14, 2026 via its GP Strategic Capital franchise (terms undisclosed; Houlihan Lokey advised BlackFin).

## Why it matters

BlackFin gives [[Blue Owl]] direct exposure to European financial-services PE at a moment when both [[PE-insurance convergence]] and alt-manager consolidation are live themes. The stake-sale format (minority, not buyout) reflects Blue Owl's GP Strategic Capital playbook — buying pieces of other managers rather than consolidating them — which traces back to the Dyal Capital franchise absorbed in the 2021 Blue Owl merger.
