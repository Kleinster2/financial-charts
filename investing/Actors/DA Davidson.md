DA Davidson is a mid-market US investment bank and wealth management firm headquartered in Great Falls, Montana, founded in 1935. It operates across four segments: equity capital markets, fixed income capital markets, wealth management, and financial advisory. The firm is employee-owned and has ~1,500 employees across 90+ offices, primarily in the western US. DA Davidson is notable in the research world for covering small- and mid-cap technology names that larger banks ignore, making its analyst calls occasionally market-moving for names in the $500M-$5B range.

The firm's equity research team focuses on technology, financial institutions, consumer, and diversified industrials. Its fixed income division is a significant player in municipal bond underwriting, particularly for western states. Unlike bulge bracket banks, DA Davidson has stayed private and employee-owned since founding, which keeps it aligned with a long-term client-service model rather than quarter-to-quarter revenue pressure.
