# H Partners Management

New York-based activist hedge fund running a concentrated, long-term equity strategy. Founded by Usman Nabi, the fund typically holds 5-10 positions with a bias toward deep-value situations where operational improvements or strategic changes can unlock value over multi-year horizons.

## Synopsis

H Partners operates with a classic concentrated activist playbook — take large positions in undervalued companies, engage management or push for board representation, and wait for the gap between intrinsic value and market price to close. The fund manages an estimated $2-4B in AUM and is known for its patience, holding positions for years rather than the months typical of event-driven funds. Founder Usman Nabi previously worked at Ziff Brothers Investments before launching H Partners in 2002.

The fund's track record includes notable wins in industrials and transportation. H Partners was a significant investor in [[Six Flags]] during its restructuring and held major positions in companies like [[Caesars Entertainment]] and Avis Budget Group. The activist approach means H Partners positions often show up in 13D filings, which makes them trackable signals for other investors. When H Partners takes a 5%+ stake, it typically presages operational pressure on management. The fund's alumni network is also worth noting — Darren Dinneen, formerly at H Partners, went on to found [[Totem Point]], carrying the concentrated value discipline into a new vehicle.
