# Peak XV Partners

#actor #vc #india #southeast-asia #private

## The Sequoia split (2024)

In 2023, Sequoia Capital ($85B AUM) announced split into three independent entities, completed March 2024:

| Entity | Geography | Leadership |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| **Sequoia** | US & [[Europe]] | Roelof Botha |
| **[[HongShan]]** | [[China]] | Neil Shen |
| **Peak XV Partners** | India & SEA | Shailendra Singh |

**Reason:** Regulatory complexity, geopolitical tensions, LP preferences for geographic focus.

**Name origin:** "Peak XV" is K2's original survey designation — the world's second-highest mountain.
