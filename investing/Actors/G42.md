## Microsoft partnership (2024)

**$1.5B investment + strategic alliance:**
- Microsoft took stake in G42
- G42 runs on Azure (not Huawei)
- AI collaboration
- **Condition:** Sever China/Huawei ties

**Geopolitics:** US pressured UAE to choose sides. G42 chose Microsoft/US.

---

## China ties (severed)

**Previous relationships:**
- Huawei cloud infrastructure
- Chinese AI partnerships
- BGI Genomics (controversial)

**US pressure:** Biden administration warned UAE about technology transfer risks.

**Resolution:** G42 divested China ties to secure Microsoft deal.
