## Why he matters

Coleman matters because he gives the cleanest public read on what was desk noise versus structural weakness inside Goldman. On the Q1 2026 earnings call he said lower revenues in interest-rate and mortgage trading came from a tougher market-making backdrop, partly offset by stronger currencies and commodities. That formulation is important: Goldman did not describe the quarter as a franchise problem, but it did confirm that rates and mortgages were the pocket of weakness.
