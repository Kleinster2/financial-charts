## Pix: Instant payments revolution

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Launch | November 2020 |
| Users | **182M+** (87% of adults) |
| Transactions (2024) | 64B (+53% YoY) |
| Mobile banking adoption | 90% (vs 56% in 2020) |
| vs Cards | Surpassed debit + credit combined by 80% |

**Key milestones:**
- **Pix Automático** (Jun 2025) — recurring payments
- **Pix por Aproximação** (Feb 2025) — contactless
- **17M Brazilians** brought into financial system

**Why it matters:** Low-income Brazilians adopted Pix because they lack credit card access. Leapfrogged traditional payments — mobile banking adoption (90%) now exceeds US (75%).

## Investment themes

### Fintech dominance
- Pix killed card growth
- 90% mobile banking penetration
- [[StoneCo]], [[Nubank]] competing with traditional banks
- AI + WhatsApp payments emerging (Itaú, PicPay)

### Rate cycle
- Selic at 15% — one of highest globally
- Easing expected 2026
- Growth stocks compressed; could re-rate on cuts

### China dependency
- #1 iron ore supplier (Vale)
- Soybean exporter
- China slowdown = Brazil commodity risk

### Currency as swing factor
- BRL volatility creates timing opportunities
- USD strength pressures EM broadly
