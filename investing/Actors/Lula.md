**Lula (Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva)** — President of [[Brazil]] (2003-2010, 2023-present). Founded the Workers' Party (PT). Launched the [[Brazil Biodiesel Program|PNPB biodiesel program]] (2004), [[Bolsa Familia]] welfare program, and presided over Brazil's commodity boom era. Third term focused on energy transition (Fuels of the Future Law), reindustrialization, and social spending. Key figure in Brazil's biofuel policy — his governments have consistently advanced biodiesel mandates while conservative administrations (Bolsonaro) have frozen them.
