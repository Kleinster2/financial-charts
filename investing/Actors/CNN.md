# CNN

Cable News Network, a global news organization owned by [[Warner Bros. Discovery]]. CNN Business (formerly CNNMoney) publishes the [[Market Sentiment Indicators|Fear & Greed Index]], a widely referenced composite sentiment indicator launched in 2012.
