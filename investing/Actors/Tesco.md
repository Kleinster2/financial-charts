#actor #retail #grocery

**Tesco** is a British multinational grocery and general merchandise retailer, the largest supermarket chain in the [[United Kingdom]]. LSE: TSCO. ~4,000 stores across the UK, Ireland, and Central Europe, with revenue exceeding GBP 60B. HQ in Welwyn Garden City, Hertfordshire. Notable cautionary tale in international expansion: the Fresh & Easy venture in the US (2007-2013) resulted in a ~GBP 1.8B loss and full market exit.
