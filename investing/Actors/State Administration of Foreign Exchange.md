# State Administration of Foreign Exchange

State Administration of Foreign Exchange (SAFE) is China's foreign-exchange regulator and the official data source for balance-of-payments, FX settlement, and cross-border capital-flow series used to analyze [[China]]'s currency-management regime.

---

## Vault Relevance

SAFE matters because China reserve analysis cannot be read from [[PBOC]] reserves alone. The SAFE settlement series shows when banks buy or sell foreign exchange against renminbi, while deposit and banking-system series help separate visible reserve accumulation from [[China hidden reserve intervention]].
