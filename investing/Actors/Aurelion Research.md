## May 2026 AI mega-IPO stress test

Aurelion's May 26 2026 article framed [[SpaceX]], [[OpenAI]], and [[Anthropic]] as a single public-market event rather than three separate listings. The article's central claim was that the IPO sequence itself can become a valuation catalyst: roadshows, media attention, benchmark inclusion, and direct public access to AI-lab exposure can lift thematic multiples even before fundamentals catch up.

The note is also a good example of how to handle source estimates. [[SpaceX]]'s public S-1 and [[OpenAI]]'s $122B / $852B financing are hard facts; Aurelion's SpaceX sum-of-the-parts and OpenAI downside multiple are valuation opinions. Aurelion's $900B+ [[Anthropic]] target was superseded two days later when Anthropic announced a $65B Series H at a $965B post-money valuation.
