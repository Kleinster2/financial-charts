# Alaïa

French fashion house with couture DNA. Known for sculptural, body-conscious designs. Part of [[Richemont]].

## Business

- **Founded:** 1964 by Azzedine Alaïa
- **Owner:** [[Richemont]] (since 2007; previously Prada Group 2000-2007)
- **Positioning:** Couture sensibility, timeless femininity

## Creative Direction

| Director | Tenure | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Pieter Mulier | 2021–Mar 2026 | Departing after Summer/Fall 2026 show |
| Studio (interim) | 2026– | Successor TBD (Rousteing rumored) |
| Azzedine Alaïa | 1964–2017 | Founder, died 2017 |

Mulier drove product growth, retail expansion, critical acclaim. Rumored moving to Versace ([[Prada]]).

## Positioning

Unique within Richemont — fashion house in jewelry-dominated portfolio. Tests Richemont's ability to compete with [[LVMH]] and [[Kering]] in fashion.

## Legacy

Azzedine Alaïa was the "King of Cling" — sculptural knits, body-conscious silhouettes, refusal to follow fashion calendar. Dressed Grace Jones, Naomi Campbell, Lady Gaga.
