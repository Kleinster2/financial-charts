**GlobeNet** — Submarine cable operator connecting the Americas. 23,500 km network linking Brazil, Argentina, Colombia, Venezuela, Bermuda, and US. Merged into [[V.tal]] (2022). Co-owns [[Malbec Cable]] with [[Edge Network Services]] ([[Meta]]).

## Why GlobeNet matters

Critical Americas connectivity infrastructure:

- **Scale**: 23,500 km subsea network with ring protection
- **Coverage**: 7 landing stations across US, Brazil, Argentina, Colombia, Venezuela, Bermuda
- **Malbec**: Co-ownership with [[Meta]] connects Argentina to US (<80ms)
- **Scala enabler**: Porto Alegre branch provides [[Scala Data Centers]] AI City US connectivity
