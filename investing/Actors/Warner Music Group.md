## Ownership structure

| Owner | Stake | Control |
|-------|-------|---------|
| [[Access Industries]] | 72% economic | 98% voting |
| Public | 28% | 2% voting |

Len Blavatnik's Access Industries took WMG private in 2011, re-IPO'd in 2020. Dual-class structure = total control.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Streaming tailwind continues
- Artist services expanding (higher margin)
- Catalog acquisitions
- AI licensing opportunity
- Stable management (Blavatnik control)

**Bear:**
- Dual-class = no governance rights
- Smaller catalog vs. UMG/Sony
- DSP pricing pressure
- Limited M&A (debt load)
- Subscale in some markets
