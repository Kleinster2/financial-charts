# Trulieve

A US cannabis multistate operator built on a dominant position in Florida medical cannabis, where its dense dispensary network gives it the largest single-state share of any US MSO. Expanded beyond Florida via the 2021 Harvest Health acquisition and bankrolled Florida's 2024 adult-use legalization ballot campaign. Among the largest US MSOs by revenue alongside [[Curaleaf]]. Federally illegal under [[Cannabis rescheduling|Schedule I]], 280E-taxed, OTC/CSE-listed. A member of the [[Cannabis]] cohort's US MSO bloc, but omitted from the cluster computation because its OTC ticker returns only a live quote, no price history, from the data provider.
