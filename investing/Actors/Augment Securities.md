# Augment Securities

Augment Securities — secondary marketplace for pre-IPO shares, co-founded by Adam Crawley. Seeing record demand for [[Anthropic]] shares alongside [[Hiive]] and [[Next Round Capital]]. Bids for Anthropic at ~$600B, 58% above $380B Series G. Crawley (Apr 2026): *"It's just better risk-reward right now. People are betting that Anthropic's valuation will catch up with OpenAI's. But if you buy OpenAI shares, it's less clear what the return will be in the near term."*
