# Penske Automotive

Penske Automotive Group (NYSE: PAG) is a diversified transportation-services retailer — premium-brand franchised auto dealerships (US + UK/Europe), plus commercial truck dealerships (Premier Truck Group) and a stake in Penske Transportation Solutions. The most premium/international and least pure-US of the [[Franchised auto dealers]] cohort.
