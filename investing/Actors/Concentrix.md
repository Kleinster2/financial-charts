**Concentrix** is the world's second-largest business process outsourcing (BPO) company by revenue (~$9B), listed on NASDAQ as CNXC. The company provides customer experience (CX) management, technology services, and digital engineering to enterprise clients across industries. Concentrix merged with Webhelp in September 2023 in a ~$4.8B deal, creating a combined entity with over 440,000 employees across 70+ countries.

Concentrix was spun off from [[SYNNEX Corporation]] (now TD SYNNEX) in 2020 and quickly became the pure-play CX competitor to [[Teleperformance]] (world's #1 BPO). The Webhelp merger was designed to strengthen European and multilingual capabilities, but integration has been rocky — the stock has been under pressure since the deal closed.

The existential question for Concentrix (and the entire BPO sector) is AI-driven disintermediation. When [[Klarna]] announced in February 2024 that its AI chatbot was handling the work of 700 customer service agents, Concentrix stock fell -11% in a single session. The market is pricing in a scenario where LLM-powered customer service reduces demand for human agents at scale. Concentrix has responded by investing in its own AI/automation offerings, but the fundamental tension remains: the company sells labor arbitrage, and AI collapses the labor component.
