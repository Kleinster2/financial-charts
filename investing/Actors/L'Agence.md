# L'Agence

Premium fashion brand blending California lifestyle with French sophistication. Known for elevated denim.

## Business

- **Founded:** 2008 by Margaret Maldonado
- **Headquarters:** Los Angeles
- **Positioning:** California ease + Parisian chic
- **Private company**

## Product

- Premium denim (core) — Mira Cropped Bootcut, Alicent Wide-Leg
- Tailored blazers, suiting
- Silk dresses (Seridie Slip Dress)
- Day-to-night versatility

**Materials:** Italian fabrics, Japanese textiles.

## Retail

| Channel | Presence |
|---------|----------|
| Own stores | Beverly Hills flagship (recent) |
| Wholesale | 300+ locations — Neiman Marcus, Saks |
| E-commerce | lagence.com |

## Positioning

Premium tier — above Frame, below true luxury. Workwear-to-evening versatility. Strong wholesale presence vs. DTC-first peers.
