## Banco Master connection

When Tanure assumed the Dia Brasil operation, the company received a restructuring aporte of nearly R$100M. Per federal police investigation and press reports, a portion of this capital was placed in [[Banco Master]] CDBs. Tanure denies wrongdoing; investigation ongoing.
