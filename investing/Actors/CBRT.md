# CBRT

Central Bank of the Republic of Turkiye. Conducts monetary policy for [[Turkey]]. History of political interference under Erdogan — multiple governor dismissals between 2019-2021. Since mid-2023 pivot, returned to orthodox tightening (policy rate peaked at 50%).

|--------|-------|
| Policy rate | 45% (Feb 2026, after 100bp cut) |
| Peak rate | 50% (2024) |
| Inflation | ~31% (Dec 2025) |
| Stance | Cautious easing cycle |
