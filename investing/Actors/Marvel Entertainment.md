Marvel Entertainment — the comic-book and IP company acquired by [[Disney]] in 2009 for $4B. Chaired at sale time by [[Ike Perlmutter]]. Source of the Marvel Cinematic Universe franchises (Avengers, Black Panther, Spider-Man) that drove Disney's 2010s box-office dominance.
