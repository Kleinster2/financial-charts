**Mubadala** — Abu Dhabi's most active sovereign wealth fund. $330B AUM. 330+ deals since 2020. Most global of three UAE SWFs.

---

## Why Mubadala matters

| Metric          | Value                                  |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Founded         | 2002                                   |
| AUM             | **$330 billion**                       |
| Chairman        | [[Sheikh Mansour bin Zayed Al Nahyan]] |
| CEO             | [[Khaldoon Al Mubarak]]                |
| Deals (2020-25) | **330+** (most active SWF globally)    |

World's most active sovereign wealth fund in 2024 (Global SWF). More than half of deals focus on US and European markets.
