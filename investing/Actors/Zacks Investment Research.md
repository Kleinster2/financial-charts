**Zacks Investment Research** — Equity research and ratings firm. Known for the Zacks Rank system based on earnings estimate revisions. Founded 1978 by Len Zacks.

---

## Why Zacks matters

Earnings estimate revisions are the single strongest predictor of near-term stock performance. Zacks built an entire business around this insight.

## Zacks Rank system

| Rank | Label | Meaning |
|------|-------|---------|
| #1 | Strong Buy | Earnings estimates rising fastest |
| #2 | Buy | Estimates trending up |
| #3 | Hold | Estimates flat |
| #4 | Sell | Estimates declining |
| #5 | Strong Sell | Estimates falling fastest |

Purely quantitative — based on direction and magnitude of analyst earnings estimate revisions. Not fundamental analysis.

## Style Scores

| Score | Measures |
|-------|----------|
| Value | P/E, P/S, P/CF, PEG |
| Growth | Earnings and sales growth rates |
| Momentum | Price and estimate momentum |

Scores range A (best) to F (worst). Used alongside Zacks Rank for filtering.

---

## Earnings ESP (Expected Surprise Prediction)

Proprietary model predicting earnings beats/misses. Positive ESP + Zacks Rank #1-3 = higher probability of earnings beat. Used for pre-earnings positioning.
