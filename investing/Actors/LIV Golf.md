#actor #sports #golf #saudi #private

**LIV Golf** — professional golf league backed by [[Saudi PIF]], launched in 2022 as a rival to the [[PGA Tour]]. Cumulative PIF investment exceeds $5.3B with an estimated burn rate of ~$100M/month. The proposed PGA Tour merger has stalled after PIF's $1.5B investment offer was rejected.
