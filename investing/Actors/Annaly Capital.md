## Rate sensitivity

| Rate environment | Impact |
|------------------|--------|
| Rates falling | Book value rises, MBS appreciate |
| Rates rising | Book value falls, MBS depreciate |
| Stable rates | Carry trade works, steady dividends |

**MSR hedge:** MSR values rise when rates rise (offsets MBS losses).

---

## Investment implications

**Bull case:**
- Largest mREIT, most diversified
- MSR portfolio provides rate hedge
- 8 consecutive quarters positive returns
- Attractive yield in falling rate environment

**Bear case:**
- Complex, hard to analyze
- Leverage amplifies losses
- Rate volatility hurts
- Dividend cut history (industry-wide)
