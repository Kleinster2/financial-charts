**Vibra** (Vibra Energia) is Brazil's largest fuel distributor — the former Petrobras Distribuidora / BR Distribuidora, rebranded Vibra in 2022 after [[Petrobras]] fully divested its stake in 2021. It supplies roughly 8,000 BR-branded service stations and the Lubrax lubricants line, and is pushing beyond fuels into power trading and renewables (Comerc, Evolua).

The core is a high-volume, thin-margin distribution business geared to Brazilian fuel demand; the diversification into electricity and renewables is the attempt to add a higher-margin, less cyclical leg. Now an independent, no-controlling-shareholder company since the Petrobras exit.
