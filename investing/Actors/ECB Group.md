**ECB Group** — Brazil's largest biodiesel producer (13.25% market share, 2021). Parent of BSBIOS (rebranded to Be8). Two FAME biodiesel plants (Passo Fundo and Marialva) with combined capacity of 936 million liters/year. Acquired [[Petrobras]] Biocombustivel's 50% stake in BSBIOS in February 2021 for ~$47M (BSBIOS valued at BRL 1.235B / ~$231M) through subsidiary RP Holdings. Founded by Erasmo Carlos Battistella. Board chair: Francisco Turra (former Agriculture Minister). Strategic plan to become Latin America's largest advanced biofuels producer. Revenue: R$5.2B (2020). Private.

Building Omega Green biorefinery in Paraguay ($800M) — 20,000 bpd HVO/SAF/green naphtha, signaling pivot from FAME to advanced biofuels. Completion 2026.
