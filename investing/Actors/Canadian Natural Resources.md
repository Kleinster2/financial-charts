# Canadian Natural Resources

Canada's largest oil and natural gas producer by volume. Operates the Horizon oil sands mine and upgrader in Alberta, plus extensive conventional and thermal heavy oil operations. Produces byproduct sulfur from bitumen upgrading at Horizon.
