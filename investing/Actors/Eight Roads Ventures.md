**Eight Roads Ventures** — global VC firm, the proprietary investment arm of [[Fidelity International]] (formerly Fidelity Investments' direct investing arm). ~$11B AUM. Offices in London, Mumbai, Shanghai, Tokyo. Invests across tech, healthcare, consumer. One of the earliest institutional VCs in [[China]] and [[India]].
