**Aldemir Bendine** — Brazilian banker and former executive. Ex-president of [[Banco do Brasil]] (2009-2015) and [[Petrobras]]. Arrested in 2017 during [[Lava Jato]] investigation for alleged R$3 million bribe from [[Odebrecht]]. Currently president of [[Banco Digimais]] (appointed late 2025), seen as political bridge between evangelical controller [[Edir Macedo]] and PT-aligned Central Bank under [[Gabriel Galípolo]].
