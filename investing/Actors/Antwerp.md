# Antwerp

[[Belgium]]'s diamond trading capital and one of the historical centers of the global diamond trade. Trade volume dropped from a peak of ~$41B in 2022 to ~$19B by 2025 as the [[Diamond market collapse]] eroded demand. Alongside Surat and Dubai, one of three major diamond trading hubs globally.
