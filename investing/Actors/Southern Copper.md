## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Lowest cost major producer
- 60+ year reserve life
- Multiple expansion projects
- Strong margins at any copper price
- Vertical integration (smelters)
- LatAm growth exposure

**Bear:**
- Premium valuation (expensive)
- Peru political risk
- [[Mexico]] regulatory uncertainty
- Grupo [[Mexico]] control (governance)
- Community opposition to expansion
- [[ESG]] concerns (water, tailings)
