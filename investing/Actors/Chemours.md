# Chemours

American chemicals company spun off from DuPont in July 2015. Produces fluoroproducts (Teflon [[PTFE]]/FEP/PFA, Opteon HFO refrigerants), titanium dioxide (Ti-Pure), and chemical solutions. Listed on NYSE (CC). Co-holds key patents with [[Honeywell]] for HFO-1234yf, the dominant next-generation automotive refrigerant. Major consumer of [[Hydrofluoric Acid]] as feedstock for fluoropolymer and refrigerant production.
