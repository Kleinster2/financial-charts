# SEA

SEA (NYSE Arca: SEA) is the US Global Sea to Sky Cargo ETF — a basket of global cargo-transport companies spanning marine shipping (tankers, dry bulk, container) and air freight. A broad cargo/shipping proxy. Launched 2022.

## Use case

A shipping-ETF replicability benchmark for the [[Tankers#Cluster validation|tanker cluster validation]]. Like [[BOAT]], SEA mixes tankers with other shipping/air-cargo segments, so the pure-tanker basket may out-cohere it — the test of whether tankers are a distinct factor. See [[Tankers]].
