Amundi -- Europe's largest asset manager by AUM (EUR 2.2T+), listed on [[Euronext]]. ~69% owned by [[Crédit Agricole]]. Created in 2010 from merger of Crédit Agricole and Société Générale asset management arms. Acquired Lyxor (2022) to become Europe's \#2 ETF provider behind [[BlackRock]].
