**Rivos** — RISC-V AI chip startup acquired by [[Meta]] (September 2025). Taped out a CUDA-compatible RISC-V processor.

---

## Synopsis

Santa Clara-based startup that designed a CUDA-compatible processor on the RISC-V instruction set architecture — a notable technical achievement given [[CUDA moat]] is [[NVIDIA]]'s primary competitive barrier. Was seeking funding at a $2B+ valuation before [[Meta]] acquired the company (announced September 30, 2025). The acquisition strengthens Meta's in-house semiconductor design capability and reduces dependence on [[NVIDIA]] for inference workloads. Rivos fits Meta's broader strategy of custom silicon development alongside its existing [[MTIA]] chip program. The CUDA-compatibility angle is strategically significant: if you can run CUDA code on non-NVIDIA hardware, you undercut the ecosystem lock-in that keeps hyperscalers buying [[NVIDIA]] GPUs.
