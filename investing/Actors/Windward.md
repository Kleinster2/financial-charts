## 2026 Hormuz crisis

Windward data (Mar 19): 1,000+ ships in the [[Persian Gulf]] affected by GPS signal jamming during the [[Iran conflict economic disruption|Hormuz closure]]. The jamming serves dual purpose — disrupts commercial shipping navigation and impedes incoming drones/missiles from finding targets. Windward's analysis of AIS signal patterns has become a key source for tracking the operational status of the strait.
