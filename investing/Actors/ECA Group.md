# ECA Group

French underwater robotics company. Founded 1936 by Marcel Gianoli and Paul Rozycki as Etudes et Constructions Aeronautiques. Pioneered the PAP (Poisson Auto-Propulsé) mine disposal drone in 1970 (~450 units sold to ~20 navies). Acquired by [[Jean-Pierre Gorgé]]'s Finuchem in 1992. Merged with [[iXblue]] in 2022 to form [[Exail Technologies]].
