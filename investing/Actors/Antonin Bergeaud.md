# Antonin Bergeaud

French economist working on productivity, innovation, and technology diffusion. His vault relevance is as a co-author of the "ABG" critique of [[Paul Krugman]]'s [[US-Europe productivity paradox]] argument.

## Relevant analysis

One of the three economists ([[Philippe Aghion]], Bergeaud, [[Luis Garicano]]) Krugman responds to in his May 30 2026 post arguing the US-EU productivity-growth gap is methodology-dependent. See [[US-Europe productivity paradox]].
