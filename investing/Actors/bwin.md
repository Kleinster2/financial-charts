Online sports betting and gaming brand, now part of [[Entain]] (formerly GVC Holdings). Originally Austrian, merged with PartyPoker parent in 2011, acquired by GVC in 2016. George Daskalakis (co-founder of [[Betano]]) was bwin country manager from 2002.
