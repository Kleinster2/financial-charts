**Straits Times Index** — [[Singapore]]'s benchmark stock market index, comprising the 30 largest companies by market cap on the SGX. Covers ~85% of the local equity market by capitalization. Dominated by the three domestic banks ([[DBS]], [[OCBC]], [[UOB]]), which account for roughly 50% of index weight. Crossed 5,000 for the first time on Feb 12, 2026. Southeast Asia's best-performing market index in 2024 (+15%).
