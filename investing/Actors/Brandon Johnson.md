#actor #politician #usa

**Brandon Johnson** — Mayor of Chicago (since May 2023). Former Cook County commissioner and CTU organizer. Approval rating historically low: 6.6% (Feb 2025), recovering to 31% (Oct 2025). Presides over $35.9B unfunded pension liability (~25% funded ratio) and $1.15B FY2026 budget shortfall closed via ~$500M in new taxes/fees. S&P revised city debt outlook to negative (Jan 2026) after city split pension payment due to cash crunch.
