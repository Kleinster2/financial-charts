**Rebekah Neumann** — Wife of [[Adam Neumann]], former chief brand and impact officer at [[WeWork]]. First cousin of actress Gwyneth Paltrow. Founded WeGrow, a private school in Chelsea (2018–2020). The S-1 included a clause giving her influence over CEO succession — a governance red flag that contributed to the IPO's collapse.
