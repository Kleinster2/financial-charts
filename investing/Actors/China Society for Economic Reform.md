# China Society for Economic Reform

Economic-reform think tank based in [[China]]. Platform for former [[NDRC]] officials and senior economists — including vice-president [[Peng Shaozong]] — to publish policy analysis that sits close to the official line but has more latitude for candor than direct NDRC statements. Surfaced into Western media during the [[2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis]] after Peng's March 2026 "abandon wishful thinking" paper on [[Iran]]-war supply-chain risk.
