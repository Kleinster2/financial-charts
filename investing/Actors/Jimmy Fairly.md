# Jimmy Fairly

**French affordable luxury eyewear** — vertically integrated, DTC-focused. Founded 2010 by Sacha Bostoni and Antonin Chartier. ~€75-150M revenue (2025), 160+ stores across Europe. [[HLD]] majority owner since 2021. "Buy one, give one" social mission.

## Business model

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Model | Vertically integrated, DTC |
| Category | Eyeglasses and sunglasses |
| Price point | €99-€199 (vs €300+ traditional opticians) |

## Ownership

[[HLD]] acquired majority stake in July 2021. Founders retain significant ownership. Jean-Bernard Lafonta (HLD founder) serves as Chairman of Supervisory Board.

Investment supports:
- French market consolidation
- European expansion (UK, Belgium, Germany, Spain)
- Global ambitions
