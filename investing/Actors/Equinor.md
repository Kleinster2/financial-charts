## Low-carbon positioning

**Advantages:**
- Electrified offshore platforms
- CCS at Sleipner (since 1996)
- Low flaring/methane
- Carbon intensity targets

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Lowest carbon oil production
- Offshore wind scale
- Johan Sverdrup cash cow
- Strong balance sheet

**Bear:**
- Norwegian tax increases
- Offshore wind returns uncertain
- Mature North Sea base
- Government control
