## Arctic policy (2026)

Submitted response to EU consultation calling for the bloc to drop its 2021 effective moratorium on Arctic oil and gas drilling. Part of a broader industry push (10+ companies) framing Arctic hydrocarbons as essential to European energy security. See [[Arctic#EU Arctic moratorium review (2026)]].

## Lithium — Standard Lithium JV

[[Equinor]] partnered with [[Standard Lithium]] on a $1.5B [[Direct lithium extraction|DLE]] project in Lafayette County, Arkansas, over the Smackover Formation. FID planned autumn 2025. Allison Kennedy Thurmond (VP North America Lithium) said Equinor's oil and gas skills — drilling, pumping, processing fluids — transfer directly: "We don't have to go off and hire thousands of people with new skills."
