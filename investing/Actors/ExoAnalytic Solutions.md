# ExoAnalytic Solutions

Space surveillance firm operating a network of **400 telescopes** worldwide to track spacecraft in high orbits. Founded 2008 by Doug Hendrix to adapt missile defense sensor technology for space domain awareness. Being acquired by [[Anduril]] (announced Mar 11, 2026, terms undisclosed).

## What they do

Telescopes track spacecraft in geosynchronous and high-Earth orbits. Software converts observations into situational awareness tools for US national security agencies — monitoring adversary spacecraft, coordinating American assets on orbit. Customers include [[US Space Force]] and national security agencies.

Dual-use capability: Machine vision algorithms for spotting satellites also applicable to interceptors tracking incoming threats — directly relevant to [[Golden Dome]] missile defense.
