#actor #company #utility #power #sanitation #brazil #public

**Equatorial** (Equatorial Energia) is one of Brazil's largest private multi-utility groups — electricity distribution and transmission, and, since 2024, water and sanitation. It trades on B3 as `EQTL3`.

Distribution is the core: roughly 14.8 million consumers across a mix of mature and turnaround concessions, with a regulatory asset base (RAB) near R$34.9B. In June 2024 Equatorial entered sanitation by taking a ~15% reference stake in [[Sabesp]] for R$6.9B (about $1.2B) during the São Paulo water utility's privatization. The transmission segment was reclassified as discontinued operations from 2025 as the group reshapes its portfolio. 2024 was a record year — adjusted EBITDA of R$10.9B (+11.3%) and adjusted net income of R$2.5B (+19.4%).

For portfolio purposes the source reference was credit: Equatorial appeared as an "Equatorial debenture" in a Brazilian fixed-income sleeve — a high-grade infrastructure issuer rather than an equity position. See [[Brazilian fixed income]].
