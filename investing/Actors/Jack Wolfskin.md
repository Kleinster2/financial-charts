# Jack Wolfskin

**German outdoor apparel and equipment brand.** Acquired by [[Anta Sports]] in 2019. Specializes in hiking, camping, and outdoor gear. Struggling post-acquisition — European outdoor market competitive and Anta's China-centric expertise hasn't translated. Part of Anta's multi-brand portfolio strategy alongside [[Fila]] and [[Descente]].
