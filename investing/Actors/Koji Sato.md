# Koji Sato

Outgoing CEO and president of [[Toyota]], stepping down in April 2026 to become vice chairman. Koji Sato took the CEO role in April 2023, succeeding [[Akio Toyoda]] (the founder's grandson, who remained chairman). His 3-year tenure was defined by an aggressive pivot toward battery EVs after years of Toyota's public skepticism about full electrification. Announced the next-gen EV platform with solid-state batteries targeting 2027-2028 production, and restructured Toyota's EV division to compete with [[BYD]] and [[Tesla]]. His successor [[Kenta Kon]] — a finance background executive — signals Toyota's board prioritizing capital discipline over the technology-first approach Sato championed.
