# XME

XME (NYSE Arca: XME) is the SPDR S&P Metals & Mining ETF — an equal-weighted basket of US metals and mining companies spanning steel, aluminum, diversified mining, coal, gold, copper, and other metals producers. A broad US mining proxy. Launched 2006.

## Use case

The broad-mining control for the [[Rare earth equity beta#Cluster validation|rare-earth cluster validation]] — the test of whether the rare-earth names are distinct from general mining beta or just track the broader metals-and-mining complex. In the 1Y test through 2026-06-18, XME clusters with the US rare-earth names. See [[Rare earth equity beta]].
