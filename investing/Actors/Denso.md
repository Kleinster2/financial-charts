**Denso** — Japanese automotive components manufacturer and [[Toyota]] group's primary Tier 1 supplier. One of the world's largest auto parts companies by revenue. Investing in autonomous driving via [[Turing Inc]] partnership and joint AV development. Listed on Tokyo Stock Exchange (6902) and US ADR (DNZOY).
