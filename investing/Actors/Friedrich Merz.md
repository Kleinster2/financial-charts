#actor #person #politician #germany

**Friedrich Merz** — German Chancellor since 2025 (CDU). Former BlackRock Germany chairman. Reformed the [[Schuldenbremse reform|Schuldenbremse]] to enable unlimited defense borrowing. Wants [[Germany]] as Europe's leading military power. Aligned with [[Italy]]'s Meloni on defense and manufacturing.
