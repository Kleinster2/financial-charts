# Tudor Pickering Holt

Tudor, Pickering, Holt & Co. is a Houston-based energy-sector investment banking and asset-management firm, founded in 2007 by Bobby Tudor (ex-Goldman Sachs energy), Dan Pickering (ex-Simmons & Company), and Maynard Holt. The firm covers oil and gas upstream and midstream M&A, energy-transition advisory, restructuring, and institutional asset management in energy-related strategies.

Acquired by [[Perella Weinberg Partners]] in 2016 as the first major acquisition after PWP's initial growth phase, giving PWP the Houston energy bench it lacked. Has operated under the TPH brand within the PWP platform since.
