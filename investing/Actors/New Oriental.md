# New Oriental

New Oriental Education & Technology Group (NYSE: EDU; HKEX: 9901) is one of [[China]]'s largest private education companies, founded 1993 by Yu Minhong (Michael Yu). Before the 2021 [[Common Prosperity|"double-reduction" (双减) crackdown]] it was the dominant K-12 after-school tutoring brand; since the ban it has pivoted to overseas study and test prep, non-academic tutoring, adult education, and — distinctively — livestream e-commerce through its East Buy (东方甄选) unit, the agricultural-products platform that made presenter Dong Yuhui a national phenomenon.

Member of the [[Chinese ed-tech]] cohort (the [[#Quick stats|three US-listed tutoring ADRs]] tested for cluster structure); the most diversified survivor of the three, and the one whose e-commerce pivot most decoupled it from the others.
