# Braza Group

Brazilian FX bank and crypto infrastructure company. Issuer of [[BBRL]], a real-pegged stablecoin on the [[XRP Ledger|XRPL]]. Ranks 6th in Brazil's interbank market. Balance sheet >$500M (2024). CEO Marcelo Sacomori. Also issues USDB (USD-pegged stablecoin).
