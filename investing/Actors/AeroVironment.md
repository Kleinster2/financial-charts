## Ukraine relevance

**Switchblade in Ukraine:**
- US sent 700+ Switchblade 300s to Ukraine
- Mixed results — learning curve, Russian jamming
- Validated loitering munition concept

**Demand driver:** Ukraine war boosted interest in small, cheap, expendable drones.
