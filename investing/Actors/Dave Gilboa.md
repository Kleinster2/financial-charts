## Why Dave Gilboa matters

Gilboa is one of the operators who helped build Warby Parker into a scaled consumer retail brand. His participation in Blank Street's financing is useful as a signal that the company was attracting founder-operator capital, not just venture firms.

---

## Blank Street link

Blank Street's Oct. 13, 2021 Series A announcement named Dave Gilboa as one of the strategic investors joining the round led by [[General Catalyst]] and [[Tiger Global]].
