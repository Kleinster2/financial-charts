## What it tracks

FIXA11 targets the same prefixado exposure as [[IRFM11]] but implements it through DI (interbank rate) futures under an S&P/B3 index, rather than by holding LTN/NTN-F directly. The economic exposure — a bet on the path of nominal Brazilian rates — is the same; the wrapper and the index methodology differ.

## Use case

A prefixado-duration sleeve from a different issuer (BB Asset) and a futures-based construction. Useful as an alternative or complement to the It Now [[IRFM11]] for the same fixed-rate view.

## Correlation

0.39 daily-return correlation to [[EWZ]] over two years — the same Brazil rate-cycle overlap with equities as the rest of the duration family. See [[Brazilian fixed-income ETFs]].
