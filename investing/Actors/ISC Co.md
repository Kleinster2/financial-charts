# ISC Co

Korean semiconductor test socket manufacturer listed on KOSDAQ. Produces test sockets, burn-in sockets, and related consumables for IC testing across logic, memory, and automotive applications. Part of the growing Asian test interface supply chain benefiting from AI chip complexity, where higher pin counts, tighter pitches, and increased power delivery requirements are driving socket redesigns and replacement cycles.

ISC competes with [[WinWay Technology]] (Taiwan), [[Enplas]] (Japan), and fellow Korean player [[LEENO]]. The test socket market is fragmented and consumable-driven -- sockets wear out after a set number of insertions, creating steady replacement demand tied to test volumes rather than one-time equipment sales.
