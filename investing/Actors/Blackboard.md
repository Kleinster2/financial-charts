#actor #software #edtech #lms #private

**Blackboard** — legacy US learning management system, founded 1997, dominant in US higher education through the 2000s before being overtaken by [[Canvas LMS]] in the late 2010s. Sequential PE workout: taken private by Providence Equity Partners in 2011 for $1.64B, acquired by [[Veritas Capital]] / [[Anthology]] in October 2021 for an enterprise value reportedly above $3B. Bundled into [[Anthology Chapter 11 2025-2026|Anthology's Chapter 11]] filing September 29, 2025. Emerged February 27, 2026 as a standalone, debt-free Blackboard Inc. under [[Oaktree Capital]] and Nexus Capital ownership, having shed all non-LMS businesses. Roughly 12% of North American higher-ed enrollment by Spring 2025 [[Edutechnica]] data — third place behind [[Canvas LMS]] (~50%) and [[D2L Brightspace]] (~20%).
