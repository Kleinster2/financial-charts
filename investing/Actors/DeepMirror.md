**DeepMirror** — Guangzhou/Hong Kong-based spatial intelligence company founded in 2019. Builds multi-layer digital twin platform integrating 3D recognition, SLAM, and edge computing. Parent of [[Looper Robotics]] (Physical AI brand). Co-founded by Harry Hu (CEO) and [[Qifeng Chen]] (HKUST professor).
