# Xeneta

Oslo-based freight analytics platform providing real-time container and air cargo rate benchmarking to shippers, carriers, and forwarders. Widely cited by financial media during the 2026 [[Strait of Hormuz]] crisis for its data on container rate spikes, port congestion, and schedule disruption. Key analyst voices: [[Peter Sand]] (chief analyst) and [[Destine Ozuygur]] (senior analyst).
