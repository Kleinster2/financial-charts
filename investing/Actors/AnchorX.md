# AnchorX

Asia-based fintech developing AxCNH, a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the offshore yuan ([[Chinese yuan|CNH]]). Received preliminary approval from [[Kazakhstan]]'s Astana Financial Services Authority (AFSA) — the first regulatory clearance of a CNH-pegged stablecoin anywhere. AxCNH is built on [[Conflux Network]] and integrated with TokenPocket wallet. The go-to-market thesis is cross-border trade and settlement for [[Belt and Road]] corridors, particularly Central Asia.
