#actor #nuclear #usa #utility

**Vogtle** — Nuclear power plant in Burke County, Georgia. Operated by [[Southern Company]] subsidiary Georgia Power. Units 3 & 4 (AP1000 reactors) completed 2023 — only new US nuclear units in decades. Came in $17B over budget, 7+ years late. Key reference point for [[Fermi]]'s AP1000 plans.
