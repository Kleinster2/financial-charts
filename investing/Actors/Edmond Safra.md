**Edmond Safra** — Lebanese-Brazilian banker (Aug 6, 1932 - Dec 3, 1999), founder of [[Republic New York Corporation]] (1966) and [[Trade Development Bank]] (Geneva). Sold Republic and Safra Republic Holdings to [[HSBC]] for $10.3B in 1999. Died Dec 3, 1999 from smoke inhalation in a fire at his Monaco penthouse — set by nurse Ted Maher attempting a staged rescue. Brother of [[Joseph Safra]]. The Edmond J. Safra Foundation continues his philanthropic legacy.
