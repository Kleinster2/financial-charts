**UOB** — Third-largest bank in [[Singapore]] by market cap (~S$58B, 2025). Founded in 1935 by Wee Kheng Chiang. Strong retail and SME banking franchise across [[ASEAN]]. Acquired Citigroup's consumer banking operations in four Southeast Asian markets (2022). Listed on SGX (U11.SI).
