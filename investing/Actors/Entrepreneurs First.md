#actor #accelerator #talent #uk

**Entrepreneurs First** — London-based talent investor and start-up incubator. Co-founded 2011 by [[Matt Clifford]] and Alice Bentinck. Backs individuals before they have an idea or co-founder, matching them and providing pre-seed capital and infrastructure. Operates across London, San Francisco, New York, Singapore, Paris, and Bangalore.
