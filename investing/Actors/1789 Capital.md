**1789 Capital** — politically conservative [[venture capital]] firm founded by [[Omeed Malik]] in 2022, positioning itself as a counterweight to what it calls "woke capital" in Silicon Valley. The firm invests in companies aligned with its ideological framework, including media, fintech, defense, and energy. Named for the year of the ratification of the US Constitution and the French Revolution.

## Synopsis

1789 Capital's vault relevance stems from its position at the intersection of politics and capital allocation during the [[Donald Trump|Trump]] era. The firm gained attention for declining a nine-figure allocation to [[Anthropic]] in February 2026, citing ideological concerns about AI safety alignment — a decision that crystallized the emerging "parallel economy" thesis in VC. [[Omeed Malik]], previously at Neuberger Berman and Bank of America, has built the firm around the premise that conservative-aligned capital is underserved and that ESG-driven exclusions create mispriced opportunities.

The firm's AUM and returns are not publicly disclosed, but its deal flow benefits from Trump-world connections: [[Donald Trump]]'s son has been involved as a partner. Whether the political alignment thesis generates alpha or merely captures a niche LP base remains an open question.
