**Capital Group** --- Los Angeles-based asset manager (est. 1931), parent of the American Funds family. Launched its first ETFs in 2022 and became the fastest-growing active ETF issuer in the industry, reaching ~$53B in active ETF AUM by end-2025. Named 2025 ETF Issuer of the Year by ETF.com. One of six firms capturing roughly half of all active ETF inflows in 2025.
