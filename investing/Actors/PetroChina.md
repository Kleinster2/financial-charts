**PetroChina** — [[China]]'s largest oil and gas producer, the listed arm of state group CNPC. One of the high-dividend state-owned majors at the center of the [[中特估]] SOE value-up re-rating, alongside [[CNOOC]] and [[Sinopec]]. Listed in Hong Kong (0857.HK) and Shanghai (601857.SS); its NYSE ADR (PTR) was delisted in 2022. Used in this vault as a China SOE-energy control group for the [[China Unicom|China-telecom cluster]] — the energy SOEs cluster separately from the telecoms, confirming a distinct telecom factor.
