## CP2 expansion (Mar 2026)

Announced $8.6B investment to expand CP2 export facility in Louisiana at a Tokyo conference (Mar 14, 2026). CEO [[Michael Sable]] on Europe: *"In my opinion, there is not an energy crisis in Europe because there's available, very inexpensive gas that will deliver inexpensive electricity."* Said he believes the US will increase its global share of the [[LNG]] market as a result of [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|Middle East turmoil]].

CP2 would be Venture Global's third facility after Calcasieu Pass and Plaquemines. The expansion bet aligns with the post-Hormuz supply gap — with [[QatarEnergy]] under force majeure, US exporters are the swing suppliers for [[Europe]] and [[Asia]].
