## What It Tracks

The spot price of gold, less expenses. Each share is a fractional claim on bullion held in vaults. Lower expense ratio than [[GLD]] (0.25% vs 0.40%), which is why long-term holders prefer it; GLD keeps the tightest spreads and deepest options for traders.

## Use Case

The portfolio's monetary hedge — real-rates proxy, inflation and crisis insurance, and a dollar-debasement trade. Gold drove diversified portfolios in 2024-25 alongside central-bank buying. A core non-correlated sleeve that pairs naturally with managed futures ([[DBMF]]) against an equity book.
