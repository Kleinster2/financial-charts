# Simkhai

American fashion brand blending East Coast edge with West Coast ease. Founded by Jonathan Simkhai.

## Business

- **Founded:** 2010 by Jonathan Simkhai (Parsons, FIT trained)
- **Headquarters:** Los Angeles (Hollywood atelier)
- **Recognition:** CFDA/Vogue Fashion Fund winner (2015), CFDA Incubator alumnus
- **Private company** — no outside investment

## Product

- Women's ready-to-wear (primary)
- Menswear
- Accessories (handbags, footwear)
- Focus on sculptural construction, hand-detailing

**Aesthetic:** Elevated everyday — architectural silhouettes with intimate craft details.

## Retail

- DTC via simkhai.com
- NYFW runway shows
- Wholesale: Net-a-Porter, Saks, Neiman Marcus
- Pop-up atelier in NYC during Fashion Week

## Celebrity

Strong celebrity following — Taraji P. Henson, Selma Blair, Olivia Palermo at SS26 show.
