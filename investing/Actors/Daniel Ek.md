#actor #person #sweden #tech #investor #founder

**Daniel Ek** — [[Spotify]] co-founder and executive chairman. Net worth ~$9B. Founded [[Prima Materia]] (2021) to deploy $1B into European "moonshots." Key investments: [[Helsing]] (AI defense, chairs board), [[Northvolt]] (batteries, bankrupt), [[Neko Health]] (AI diagnostics, co-founder). Stepped down as Spotify CEO Sep 2025.
