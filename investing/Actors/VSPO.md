VSPO (rebranded Hero Esports) — Chinese esports company founded 2016 by Dino Ying. Hosts competitions across China, Southeast Asia, and South Korea for titles including Honor of Kings, PUBG, and CrossFire. [[Savvy Games Group]] invested $265M (Series C, Feb 2023), becoming the largest institutional shareholder.
