# Geneva

Swiss lakeside city and the world's single largest hub for physical commodity trading. HQ for [[Vitol]], [[Trafigura]], [[Gunvor]], [[Mercuria Energy Group]], and most top-tier independent energy traders, plus [[Glencore]]'s legacy trading roots in nearby Baar. The Geneva cluster emerged in the 1960s–70s from Swiss banking secrecy, favorable corporate tax, and proximity to Mediterranean oil flows.
