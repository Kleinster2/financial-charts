**Sunny Optical** (Sunny Optical Technology, 2382.HK) is a [[China]]-based maker of optical components and modules — smartphone camera lenses and modules, vehicle lenses, and AR/VR optics — and one of the largest global lens suppliers. It was a cornerstone investor in the [[Lingyi iTech]] Hong Kong IPO (June 2026).
