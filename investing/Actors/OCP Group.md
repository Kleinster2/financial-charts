#stub

OCP Group is the world's largest producer of phosphate rock, phosphoric acid, and phosphate-based fertilizers (DAP, MAP, TSP) by volume. Headquartered in Casablanca, [[Morocco]]. State-owned (Moroccan government holds majority).

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| HQ | Casablanca, [[Morocco]] |
| Ownership | Moroccan state majority |
| Key facility | Jorf Lasfar (world's largest integrated phosphate fertilizer complex, 2,000 hectares) |
| Sulfuric acid capacity | ~5.8 Mt (Maroc Phosphore III-IV alone) |
| Fertilizer output | ~15 Mt/year plant nutrition solutions |
| Phosphate reserves | Controls ~70% of global reserves |
