## Apr 2026 — millionaire-bracket surtax announcement

Mamdani announced a new high-bracket surtax (NYC millionaire/billionaire surcharge) as part of the FY2026 funding push — covered on Bloomberg Real Yield Apr 24 2026 in the context of the broader US state wealth-tax-vs-surtax landscape. [[Citadel]] ([[Kenneth Griffin]]) publicly responded by saying the firm is considering halting its NYC investment plans. The Citadel announcement video reportedly featured a $38M apartment in the background — a framing detail Bloomberg flagged as the visual subtext for the pushback.

This is consistent with existing precedent on tax-driven domicile shifts: Citadel already moved its HQ from Chicago to Miami citing tax and policy concerns. Florida's case-law against any state income tax makes it a long-standing exit destination for high-AGI New Yorkers. Whether the Mamdani surtax actually passes and survives state-level review is the next question; even the *threat* of the surtax appears to be repricing high-end NYC investment commitments.

Source: Michael Bologna ([[Bloomberg]] Industry Group senior tax correspondent), Bloomberg Real Yield Apr 24 2026.
