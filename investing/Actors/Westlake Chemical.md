# Westlake Chemical

**Westlake Chemical** (now Westlake Corporation) is a major US chlor-alkali and vinyls producer with ~3.7 Mt NaOH capacity (8,140M lbs/year). Vertically integrated from chlor-alkali through PVC and PVC pipe/fittings. One of three US producers with both diaphragm and membrane cell grades. Acquired Axiall in 2016 for $3.8B.
