# Crescent Energy

US oil and gas E&P company focused on Texas (Eagle Ford) and Rocky Mountain region (Uinta, Wyoming). NYSE: CRGY. ~$2.7B market cap. Q3 FY25 revenue $867M. Chairman John Goff ([[Goff Capital]]). Founded 2011, HQ in Houston. Forward dividend yield ~5.6%. Acquired [[Vital Energy]] to expand Permian position. Also active in carbon capture (CCUS) in Wyoming. Enterprise value ~$6.1B.
