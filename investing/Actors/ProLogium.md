## Technology

### Lithium Ceramic Battery (LCB)

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| Oxide ceramic electrolyte | Stable, non-flammable |
| Bipolar stacking | Higher voltage per cell |
| No liquid electrolyte | Inherently safe |
| Wide temp range | -30°C to 80°C |

### Superfluidized process

**Breakthrough:** Enables faster manufacturing of ceramic electrolyte at scale.
