## Composition

Energy-heavy: ~55% energy (WTI, Brent, heating oil, natural gas, gasoline), ~25% metals (gold, silver, aluminum, zinc), ~20% agriculture (corn, wheat, soybeans, sugar).

## Use Case

Broad [[Commodities]] proxy. DBC vs [[GSG]] in the dashboard compares two broad commodity approaches. DBC is the larger, more liquid option.
