## Significance

Recursive Superintelligence is one of several pre-product, pre-revenue alumni-founded labs being priced at unicorn-or-higher levels (compare [[Ineffable Intelligence]] $5.1B post-money seed, [[AMI Labs]] $4.5B post-money seed, [[Thinking Machines Lab]] $12B). The pattern is that frontier-lab founder credibility is being underwritten at valuations that historically required commercial proof points.
