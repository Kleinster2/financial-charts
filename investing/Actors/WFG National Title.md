## Insights

**FNF pedigree** — Founded by Patrick Stone, who spent 20+ years at [[Fidelity National Financial]] rising to President and COO. Brings insider knowledge of industry leader's playbook.

**Agent-centric approach** — The "three C's" (Character, Capacity, Capital) guide underwriting and agent relationships. Focus on understanding agents' businesses rather than just balance sheets.

**Independent growth beneficiary** — Part of the independent underwriter wave that grew from 15% to 27% combined market share (2019-2024).
