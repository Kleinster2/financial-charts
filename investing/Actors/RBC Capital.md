**RBC Capital** is the capital markets and investment banking division of [[Royal Bank of Canada]], Canada's largest bank by market cap (~C$230B). RBC Capital Markets ranks among the top 10 global investment banks by revenue and is a leading equity research franchise, frequently cited in this vault for analyst calls on [[Semiconductors]], [[AI]], energy, and financials. The firm is also a top-tier equity underwriter in North America and a major player in Canadian fixed income and FX markets.
