# Ji Yichao

Co-founder and chief scientist of [[Manus AI]] (parent: Butterfly Effect). Built the [[AI agents|AI agent]] technology that drew comparisons to [[DeepSeek]] in early 2025 and led to a $2B [[Meta]] acquisition in December 2025 — the showcase deal for the [[Singapore washing]] offshore-restructuring playbook. Summoned to Beijing by [[NDRC]] on March 22, 2026 alongside CEO [[Xiao Hong]] and informed he could not leave mainland China during the regulatory review (cited as 3-12 months). The travel restriction on the founders provided the operational binding constraint that prevented the [[Meta]] integration even before [[NDRC]] ordered the deal unwound on April 27, 2026.
