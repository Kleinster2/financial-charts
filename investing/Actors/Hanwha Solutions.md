# Hanwha Solutions

**Hanwha Solutions** is the chemicals and energy arm of Hanwha Group, one of South Korea's largest chlor-alkali producers. Also a major solar energy company (Qcells brand). Target chlor-alkali capacity of ~1.1 Mt NaOH by end 2024. Exploring sodium-ion battery applications using [[Caustic Soda]] as precursor.
