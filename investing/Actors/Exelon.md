## Regulatory model

**Pure regulated utility:**
- Predictable earnings
- Rate base growth
- Formula rates in Illinois
- Multi-year rate plans

Lower risk, lower growth than peers with generation.

---

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Largest customer base
- Rate base growth
- Regulatory stability
- Data center demand
- Grid modernization spend

**Bear:**
- Lower growth (no generation)
- Illinois political risk
- Inflation impact on costs
- No renewable upside
