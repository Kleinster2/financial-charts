**Delian Alliance Industries** is a [[Greece]]-based maritime defense startup building autonomous defense systems for naval warfare, described as an "[[Iron Dome]] for autonomous warfare" focused on the maritime domain. Founded by Dimitrios Kottas, formerly of [[Apple]]'s Special Projects Group, the company is part of the broader European defense and rearmament wave.
