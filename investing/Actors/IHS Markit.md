## History

**2016: IHS + Markit merger**
- IHS Inc. (energy/auto data) + Markit Ltd. (financial data)
- Created diversified data company

**2020: S&P Global deal announced**
S&P agreed to acquire IHS Markit for $44B in all-stock transaction.

**2022: Merger closed**
- IHS Markit shareholders received 0.2838 SPGI shares per share
- Doug Peterson (S&P CEO) led combined company

**Required divestitures:**
- S&P sold: CUSIP Global Services, LCD (leveraged loan data)
- IHS sold: OPIS (oil prices), coal/metals/mining, PetroChem Wire

**2023: Engineering business sold**
S&P sold former IHS engineering operations to KKR, rebranded as Accuris.

## Legacy in S&P Global

IHS Markit assets now form:
- **S&P Global Mobility** — automotive data segment
- **Part of Commodity Insights** — merged with Platts
- **Part of Market Intelligence** — financial data
