## Epstein connection

Jeffrey Epstein invested $500K in the 2014 seed round via Kyara Investments III (co-owned with Joi Ito). Stake sold months later.

Adam Back statement: "Blockstream has no direct nor indirect financial connection with Jeffrey Epstein, or his estate."
