**Andrew Bailey** — Governor of the [[Bank of England]] since March 2020. Previously CEO of the Financial Conduct Authority (2016-2020) and a career Bank of England official (1985-2016). Managing the BoE through the [[Operation Epic Fury|Iran conflict]] oil shock — publicly pushed back on market pricing of rate hikes, saying in early April 2026 that markets had "gone too far," though futures curves continue to price two hikes from the 4.50% base rate.
