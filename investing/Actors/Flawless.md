Flawless is a London-based AI company specializing in visual dubbing and performance editing for film and television. Its core product, TrueSync, uses AI to re-animate actors' lip movements to match dialogue in any target language, enabling seamless dubbing without the uncanny valley of traditional lip-sync mismatches. The technology meets TPN (Trusted Partner Network) security standards required by major Hollywood studios, which is critical for handling pre-release content.

Founded by Scott Mann (filmmaker) and Nick Lynes, Flawless is positioned at the intersection of AI and the global content localization market, which is valued at $20B+ and growing as streamers like [[Netflix]], [[Disney]], and [[Amazon]] push for simultaneous global releases in dozens of languages. Traditional dubbing is slow (weeks per language), expensive, and produces variable quality — TrueSync promises to compress this to hours while preserving the original actor's performance. The company has partnerships with major studios, though specific clients remain undisclosed due to NDA. Competitors include Deepdub, Papercup, and emerging generative video tools, but Flawless's TPN certification and studio relationships provide a first-mover advantage in the highest-value segment of the market.
