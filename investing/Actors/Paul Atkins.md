## Crypto involvement

**Pre-SEC:**
- Co-chair, Token Alliance (Chamber of Digital Commerce)
- Advisory board, Securitize (crypto securities firm)
- Helped develop digital asset industry best practices

**As Chairman:**
- Created SEC Crypto Task Force (hired [[Michael Selig]] as Chief Counsel)
- Ended "regulation by enforcement" approach
- Working on "commonsense crypto asset taxonomy"

---

## Regulatory philosophy

**Free-market, innovation-friendly:**
- Known for advocating reduced regulatory burdens
- Believes clear rules better than enforcement actions
- Skeptical of aggressive SEC expansion

**Contrast with Gensler:**
| Gensler (2021-2025) | Atkins (2025-) |
|---------------------|----------------|
| 100+ crypto enforcement actions | Rulemaking first |
| "Most tokens are securities" | Taxonomy to clarify |
| Hostile to industry | Collaborative |
