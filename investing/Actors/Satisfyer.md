## Business model

**Volume play.** While [[LELO]] charges $150+ for premium vibrators, Satisfyer offers comparable functionality at $20-50. Sold in drugstores (CVS, Walgreens equivalents), Amazon, mass retail — not just specialty stores.

**Distribution moat:** Present in 100,000+ retail locations globally. This scale enables manufacturing economies and creates barrier to entry.

## Competitive threat to Lovense

Satisfyer offers app-connected devices at lower prices than [[Lovense]], but **lacks the cam platform integration** that drives Lovense's flywheel. A model choosing between the two picks Lovense because it works with Chaturbate/OnlyFans — Satisfyer doesn't have those partnerships.

## Risks

- **Race to bottom** — commoditization pressure on margins
- **No platform ecosystem** — unlike Lovense, no recurring engagement mechanism
- **Brand perception** — "cheap" positioning limits premium expansion
- **Concentration** — dependent on retail partners
