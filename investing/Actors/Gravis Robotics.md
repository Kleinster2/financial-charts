Gravis Robotics -- ETH Zurich spinout building retrofit kits for excavators and loaders. Uses adaptive "feels the soil" control. Human-alongside model (not fully autonomous), delivering ~30% productivity gains.

## Approach

Unlike full-autonomy competitors ([[Bedrock Robotics]], [[Built Robotics]], [[SafeAI]]), Gravis keeps the human operator in the loop. The system augments rather than replaces -- adaptive control that responds to soil conditions in real time.
