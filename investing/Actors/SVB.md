#actor #bank #usa #defunct

Technology and venture-focused bank. Founded 1983 in Santa Clara. Collapsed March 10, 2023 — the second-largest US bank failure in history — after a deposit run triggered by unrealized losses on long-duration bond holdings. Seized by FDIC; deposits transferred to First Citizens BancShares.
