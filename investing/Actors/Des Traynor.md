# Des Traynor

Co-founder and Chief Strategy Officer of [[Intercom]], the customer messaging platform. Previously co-founded Contrast, a Dublin-based software design consultancy, alongside [[Eoghan McCabe]].

## Synopsis

Des Traynor is one of four [[Intercom]] co-founders (with [[Eoghan McCabe]], Ciaran Lee, and David Barrett) who built the company from a Dublin consultancy side project into a $1.3B+ valued customer communications platform. His role as CSO positions him as the product-strategy voice of the company, and he has been a prolific writer and speaker on customer engagement, product-led growth, and the shift from support tickets to conversational interfaces. His blog posts and conference talks at SaaStr, Web Summit, and Business of Software helped establish Intercom's thought-leadership brand in the SaaS ecosystem.

Traynor's strategic influence matters in the current moment because [[Intercom]] is in the middle of an AI pivot under McCabe's returned leadership, rebranding from "business messenger" to "AI-first customer service platform" with their Fin AI agent. The transition from human-mediated to AI-mediated customer interactions is one of the clearest near-term use cases for LLMs in enterprise software, and Intercom's early bet on this (Fin launched 2023, powered by [[OpenAI]]) positions the company as a case study in whether incumbent SaaS players can ride the AI wave or get disrupted by it.
