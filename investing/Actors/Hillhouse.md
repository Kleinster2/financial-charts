**Hillhouse** — Major Asia-focused investment firm founded by [[Zhang Lei]] in 2005. Originally a hedge fund seeded by the Yale Endowment ([[David Swensen]]). ~$100B+ AUM across public equities, PE, and venture. Early backer of [[JD.com]], [[Tencent]], [[PDD Holdings]]. HQ in Singapore and Hong Kong.
