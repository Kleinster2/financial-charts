# Mercedes-Benz

**German luxury automaker** — world's largest luxury car brand by revenue. Spun off from Daimler AG in 2021 (trucks became Daimler Truck). Partner in [[ACC]] battery JV with [[Stellantis]] and [[TotalEnergies]].

## EV strategy

Mercedes originally targeted 50% EV sales by 2025, later walked back to "market dependent." Partnered in [[ACC]] for European battery supply — Germany and Italy gigafactories now shelved (Feb 2026). Shifting toward hybrid/PHEV bridge strategy alongside pure BEV.
