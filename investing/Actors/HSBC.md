**HSBC** — UK's largest bank by assets. $3.2T total assets (Sep 2025). $70B TTM revenue. HQ London (moving to Newgate St 2027). Asia-focused strategy under CEO Georges Elhedery (2024-). Restructuring into 4 divisions, exiting non-core European/US IB, acquiring full ownership of Hang Seng Bank ($14B, Oct 2025). Targeting mid-teens RoTE through 2027.
