## Index Composition

**S&P [[Banks]] Select Industry Index** covers:
- Diversified [[Banks]] (large caps like [[JPMorgan]], [[Bank of America]])
- Regional [[Banks]]
- Asset Management & Custody [[Banks]]
- Diversified Financial Services
- Commercial & Residential Mortgage Finance

**Modified equal-weight** — Unlike cap-weighted ETFs, provides more balanced exposure across large/mid/small caps. No single stock dominates.

## KBE vs KRE

| Feature | KBE | KRE |
|---------|-----|-----|
| Scope | All US banks | Regional banks only |
| Large bank exposure | Yes (JPM, BAC, etc.) | No |
| Holdings | ~80 | ~140 |
| AUM | $1.4B | $3B |
| Use case | Broad bank sector | Regional bank plays |

**When to use KBE:** Broad US banking exposure, want large bank diversification.

**When to use KRE:** Pure regional bank bet, rate sensitivity, M&A speculation.
