# Aeluma

Small-cap startup developing technology to grow III-V semiconductors (InP/GaAs) directly on silicon wafers, addressing the hybrid laser integration challenge in silicon photonics. Traditional silicon photonics requires separate laser chips to be bonded or coupled to silicon optical circuits — a complex and expensive process.

## Investment case

If successful, Aeluma's approach could eliminate the need for complex laser bonding or coupling processes in silicon photonics by growing the laser directly on the silicon substrate. This would address one of the key cost and complexity barriers in silicon photonics manufacturing.
