Tadawul (Saudi Exchange) — the main stock exchange in [[Saudi Arabia Tech|Saudi Arabia]]. Benchmark index is TASI (Tadawul All Share Index). Operated by Saudi Tadawul Group (listed on its own exchange). Key venue for [[Saudi Vision 2030]] capital markets development — growing IPO pipeline as PIF monetizes portfolio.
