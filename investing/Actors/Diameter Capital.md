## Apr 2026 — buying forced sales

[[Jonathan Lewinsohn]] (managing partner, co-founder) framed the unfolding private-credit redemption stress as Lehman-style bank-run dynamics: *"There is a reckoning threatening... If you look at what has gone on in the last three or four months as people have tried to get money out of private credit funds — private credit doesn't have a liability mismatch. We have the opportunity to buy loans being sold."* Diameter is positioned as a *liquid-side* buyer absorbing forced sales out of gated BDCs and stressed insurer balance sheets — the same opportunity set distressed-debt specialists ([[Marblegate Asset Management]], [[Strategic Value Partners]], [[Davidson Kempner]]) are also pursuing in early 2026.

*Source: Bloomberg, week of Apr 20, 2026; replayed on Bloomberg Real Yield Apr 24*
