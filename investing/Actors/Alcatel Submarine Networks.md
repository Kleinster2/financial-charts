# Alcatel Submarine Networks

French submarine cable systems firm, generally referred to as ASN — wholly-owned subsidiary of [[Nokia]] since the 2016 Alcatel-Lucent acquisition. ASN is one of the "big three" subsea cable manufacturers and installers globally (alongside [[NEC]] and [[SubCom]]) and is widely considered the largest by global market share through most of the 2020s. The firm operates the world's largest subsea cable manufacturing facility at Calais, France, and a fleet of cable-laying ships. Nokia announced in late 2024 that it was reviewing strategic options for ASN, including a potential sale or carve-out — a process still unresolved as of mid-2026.
