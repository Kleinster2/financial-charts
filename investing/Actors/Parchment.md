#actor #software #edtech #subsidiary

**Parchment** — academic credential management platform and network, acquired by [[Instructure]] February 1, 2024 for $835 million gross / $795 million net. Parchment provides the digital infrastructure for institutions to issue, verify, and exchange transcripts, diplomas, certificates, and other learner credentials. At time of acquisition Parchment had 13,000+ customer institutions and had exchanged more than 165 million credentials over two decades. Now operates as the credentialing layer of the [[Instructure]] platform, connecting [[Canvas LMS]] coursework on the inside of the institution to credentials that flow to employers and other institutions on the outside.
