## What It Tracks

Active global investment-grade corporate credit. Against a passive US IG benchmark ([[LQD]]) it adds global issuers and active duration/sector positioning, and sits inside the broader [[Investment grade bonds]] complex. It is a credit fund, not a Treasury fund — it carries spread risk, so it co-moves with risk assets in a crisis rather than hedging them.
