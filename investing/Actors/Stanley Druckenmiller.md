## The pound trade (1992)

Druckenmiller identified the trade; Soros sized it up.

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Position | $10B short GBP |
| Profit | ~$1B in one day |
| Context | UK forced out of ERM |
| Druckenmiller's role | Spotted opportunity, built position |
| Soros's role | "Go for the jugular" — increased size |

> "George's contribution was pushing me to take a gigantic position."
