#actor #crypto #fintech #usa

**Anchorage Digital Bank** — First federally chartered digital asset bank in the US (OCC charter, Jan 2021). Selected as issuer for [[Tether]]'s USAT stablecoin (launched Jan 2026) — a federally regulated alternative to USDT designed for [[GENIUS Act]] compliance. [[Cantor Fitzgerald]] serves as reserve custodian for USAT.
