# Warrior Met Coal

A US pure-play producer of premium low-volatility hard coking coal, mining Alabama's Blue Creek seams almost entirely for seaborne export to steelmakers in Europe, South America, and Asia. Among the most concentrated met-coal exposures in the listed universe (premium HCC pricing, export-only), with the multi-year Blue Creek mine expansion as its growth driver. A member of the [[Coal miners]] cohort's met-coal core with [[Alpha Metallurgical Resources]], [[Ramaco]], [[Peabody Energy]], and [[Core Natural Resources]].
