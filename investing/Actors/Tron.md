#actor #crypto #blockchain

**Tron** — Layer-1 blockchain founded by [[Justin Sun]] (2017). Carries the largest share of [[Tether|USDT]] transfer volume due to low transaction fees (~$0.01 vs Ethereum's $1-10+). Dominant chain for sub-$1,000 stablecoin transfers in emerging markets. TRX is the native token. Tron Inc. — the corporate entity holding 365M+ TRX in treasury — trades on Nasdaq as TRON following a $100M reverse merger with [[SRM Entertainment]] arranged by [[Dominari Securities]] in mid-2025 (debut July 24, 2025). The deal was the first major deployment of the [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]] mechanic later operationalized at scale by [[American Ventures]].

## SRM Entertainment reverse merger (June – July 2025)

The Tron Inc. corporate vehicle was created by injecting Tron-affiliated assets and a 365M+ TRX treasury into the listed shell of [[SRM Entertainment]] (former toy supplier with Disney / Universal Studios / SeaWorld licensing). [[Dominari Securities]] arranged the transaction — the first major deployment of the [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]] mechanic that [[American Ventures]] later operationalized at scale across [[XTEND]], [[Powerus]], [[Cove Kaz Capital]], and others. SRM stock rose ~460% on the merger announcement; market cap reached ~$140M. SRM was rebranded as Tron Inc. and moved from ticker SRM to TRON.

[[Justin Sun]]'s formal post-merger role is advisor (not officer / not director).
