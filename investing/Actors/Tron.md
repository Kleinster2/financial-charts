#actor #crypto #blockchain

**Tron** — Layer-1 blockchain founded by Justin Sun (2017). Carries the largest share of [[Tether|USDT]] transfer volume due to low transaction fees (~$0.01 vs Ethereum's $1-10+). Dominant chain for sub-$1,000 stablecoin transfers in emerging markets. TRX is the native token.
