**Hilco Global** — Chicago-based private financial services and asset-valuation platform focused on distressed and special-situation assets. [[ORIX]] USA acquired a majority stake in September 2025.

Hilco matters to ORIX because distressed investing is only as good as the collateral marks. The platform gives [[ORIX]] USA practical valuation, liquidation, and advisory muscle in situations where conventional lenders often rely on generic appraisals or sponsor guarantees.

## Evolution

- 1987-2025: Founded by [[Jeffrey Hecktman]] as an asset-valuation and disposition platform, then expanded into real estate, retail liquidation, advisory, and special situations. The September 2025 ORIX majority-stake transaction shifted Hilco from founder-led independent platform to a strategic component of ORIX USA's private-credit buildout.
