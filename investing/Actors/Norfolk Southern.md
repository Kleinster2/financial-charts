# Norfolk Southern

US Class I freight rail operator — eastern half of the US. In 2025, agreed to be acquired by [[Union Pacific]] in an $87.7bn transaction (the second-largest US M&A deal of the year per LSEG). Combined entity would be the first transcontinental US rail operator, a transformative consolidation across the eastern and western Class I networks. [[Wachtell Lipton]] advised on the deal.
