#actor #investor #usa

**Cox Capital Partners** — Philadelphia-based investment management firm specializing in secondary liquidity solutions for non-traded funds. Founded April 2020. Provides direct cash liquidity to shareholders of non-traded BDCs, non-traded REITs, interval funds, and other private/feeder funds.
