# Jonas Gahr Støre

Norwegian politician, Prime Minister of [[Norway]] (2021–2025) as leader of the [[Labour Party (Norway)]]. His government raised the [[Wealth tax]] from 0.85% to 1.1% in 2022, triggering significant [[Capital flight]]. Won re-election in September 2025.
