**Fortress Investment Group** — alternative asset manager founded in 1998. First PE firm to IPO (2007, NYSE: FIG). Acquired by [[SoftBank]] for $3.3B in 2017. Managed [[Rithm Capital]] (then New Residential) externally until 2022 internalization. ~$48B AUM.
