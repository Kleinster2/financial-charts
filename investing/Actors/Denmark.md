# Denmark

Denmark is a Scandinavian country with an outsized economic footprint relative to its ~5.9M population. GDP of ~$410B (2024) is driven by pharmaceuticals, shipping, wind energy, and agriculture. Home to [[Novo Nordisk]] (Europe's most valuable company by market cap) and [[A.P. Moller-Maersk]] (world's second-largest container shipping line). The Danish krone is pegged to the euro via ERM II, giving the country monetary stability without eurozone membership.

## Investment relevance

Denmark's investment profile is dominated by [[Novo Nordisk]], whose [[GLP-1 receptor agonists]] franchise (Ozempic, Wegovy) made it Europe's largest company by market cap in 2024. The [[TotalEnergies]] Tyra gas field redevelopment in the Danish North Sea is a significant European energy security project. Denmark's commitment to 2GW+ offshore wind and its position as a logistics hub (Maersk, DSV) make it a node in both energy transition and global trade themes. Greenland, a Danish autonomous territory, became geopolitically significant after [[Donald Trump]] renewed interest in acquiring it (2025).
