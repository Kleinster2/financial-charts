**Immunomedics** was an oncology-focused biotech acquired by [[Gilead Sciences]] for $21B in 2020, the largest deal in [[Gilead Sciences]]'s history. The acquisition brought Trodelvy (sacituzumab govitecan), an antibody-drug conjugate (ADC) for triple-negative breast cancer, into [[Gilead Sciences]]'s pipeline. Trodelvy became a key growth driver for [[Gilead Sciences]]'s oncology franchise and has since expanded into additional tumor types.
