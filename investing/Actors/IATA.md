#actor #aviation #industry-body

**IATA** — trade association for the world's airlines. 340 member airlines representing ~83% of global air traffic. Sets industry standards, publishes economic forecasts, and monitors [[Jet fuel]] pricing.

---

## Why IATA matters

IATA's Jet Fuel Price Monitor is the benchmark reference for global aviation fuel costs. Their economic forecasts ($88/bbl jet fuel assumption for 2026, $250B+ global airline fuel bill) drive airline budgeting and hedging decisions. Also sets the framework for [[Sustainable Aviation Fuel]] adoption targets and CORSIA (Carbon Offsetting and Reduction Scheme for International Aviation).
