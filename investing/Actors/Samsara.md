## Product

The Connected Operations Platform connects IoT sensors across fleets, equipment, and facilities:
- Vehicle telematics (GPS, dash cams, driver safety)
- Equipment monitoring (utilization, maintenance alerts)
- Site visibility (environmental sensors, access control)
- AI-powered analytics, workflow automation, API integrations
- Rated #1 Fleet Management on G2 through 2025 and into 2026
