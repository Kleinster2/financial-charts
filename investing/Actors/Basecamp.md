37signals (d/b/a Basecamp) — American software company. Founded 1999 by Jason Fried, DHH (David Heinemeier Hansson), and others. Products: Basecamp (project management), HEY (email), Once (self-hosted software). Bootstrapped, profitable since founding. ~80 employees. Vocal advocates of building small, profitable software companies without venture capital.
