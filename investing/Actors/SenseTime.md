## Entity List impact

Added to US Entity List in 2019:

| Restriction | Effect |
|-------------|--------|
| US chips | Cannot purchase [[NVIDIA]] directly |
| US investors | Some divested |
| US customers | None |
| Workarounds | Domestic chips, third-party procurement |

**Stock impact:** IPO was delayed, priced lower. Ongoing overhang.
