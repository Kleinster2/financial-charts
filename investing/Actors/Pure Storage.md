## AI infrastructure thesis

**Why Pure matters for AI:**

1. **Storage bottleneck** — AI training requires massive data throughput; Pure's FlashBlade optimized for this
2. **NVIDIA ecosystem** — Certified at highest levels for DGX, HGX deployments
3. **Enterprise adoption** — Fortune 500 AI deployments need proven infrastructure
4. **Subscription model** — Evergreen reduces capex friction for AI buildouts
