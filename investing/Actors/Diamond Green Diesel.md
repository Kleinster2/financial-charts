# Diamond Green Diesel

**Largest renewable diesel producer in North America** — 50/50 joint venture between [[Valero]] and [[Darling Ingredients]]. ~1.2 billion gallons/year (~78,000 b/d) of renewable diesel capacity across Norco, LA and Port Arthur, TX facilities. Converting ~50% of Port Arthur capacity to [[Sustainable Aviation Fuel]] (expected 2025).
