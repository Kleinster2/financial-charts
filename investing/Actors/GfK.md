# GfK

German market research company acquired by [[NIQ]] (formerly NielsenIQ) in 2023. Founded in 1934, historically Germany's largest market research firm. Focused on consumer and retail analytics, point-of-sale data, and consumer panels across Europe and Asia. The acquisition combined NIQ's Americas strength with GfK's European dominance.
