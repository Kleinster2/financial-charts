# Comscore

Public cross-platform measurement company (NASDAQ: SCOR). Provides digital audience measurement, TV measurement, and advertising analytics. Longest-running U.S. competitor to [[Nielsen]]. Received full JIC certification as a transactable currency for the 2025-26 TV season. Uses large-scale set-top box data for TV measurement.
