# Kjell Inge Røkke

Norwegian billionaire industrialist, chairman and largest shareholder of [[Aker ASA]]. Was [[Norway]]'s richest person before relocating to Lugano, Switzerland in 2022 following the government's [[Wealth tax]] increase. Wrote an open letter to Aker's board describing his "difficult choice" to leave. Net worth ~$4.5B. Background as a fisherman turned industrial tycoon.
