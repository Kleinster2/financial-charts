#actor #asset-manager #hedge-fund #credit #brazil #private

**JGP** is one of Brazil's oldest independent asset managers, co-founded in 1998 by André Jakurski — a co-founder of Banco Pactual and a "godfather" of Brazil's hedge-fund industry. JGP runs multi-strategy, equity, credit, real-estate, and pension funds plus a wealth-management arm, and ranks among the country's top independent houses.

Jakurski (Harvard MBA 1973) built JGP out of the Banco Pactual diaspora into one of Brazil's first true hedge funds; by 2013 it managed roughly $3.8B and has since grown into a top-five independent manager with a sizeable credit franchise. The credit business is the relevant thread for cross-border portfolio work: JGP credit funds and the debentures they hold show up inside Brazilian fixed-income allocations, where their exposure to local corporate-distress / judicial-recovery (RJ) risk is a recurring flag.
