**iRobot** — Creator of Roomba. [[Consumer]] robotics pioneer. Amazon acquisition collapsed in 2024; iRobot filed Chapter 11 in Dec 2025 and was delisted as part of a sale to Picea Robotics/Santrum Hong Kong.
