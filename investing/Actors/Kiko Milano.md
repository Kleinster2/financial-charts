# Kiko Milano

**Italian color cosmetics brand** — prestige quality at accessible prices. Founded 1997 by Percassi family in Bergamo. [[L Catterton]] acquired majority stake April 2024. ~€950M revenue (2025 est.), 1,100+ stores in 66 countries. Approaching $1B milestone. US expansion focus for 2026.

## Investment case

*Note: Kiko Milano is private.*

**Bull:**
- Near $1B revenue, strong momentum
- L Catterton expertise in beauty scaling
- US market underpenetrated
- Italian heritage differentiator
- Percassi family alignment (kept stake)

**Bear:**
- Crowded cosmetics market
- NYX, e.l.f. well-established in US
- Retail footprint = fixed costs
- Fashion cycles affect color cosmetics
