# DSV

**DSV** is a Danish freight-forwarding and logistics group. In the 2026 chokepoint crisis it is relevant as a Middle East logistics operator moving selected cargo overland through [[Saudi Arabia]] and [[Turkey]] when sea routes are constrained.

## May 2026 chokepoint workaround

The FT article cites DSV as moving cargo through [[Saudi Arabia]] and [[Turkey]] during the [[Strait of Hormuz]] crisis. The read-through is not that road freight can replace container or tanker capacity, but that logistics firms with overland networks can preserve high-priority cargo flows when sea lanes become unreliable.
