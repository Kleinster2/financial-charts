## At X-energy

Sell led [[X-energy]] through its Apr 24 2026 IPO debut, the first sizable advanced-reactor company to come public via traditional IPO route (vs SPAC routes used by [[Oklo]] and [[NuScale]]). The company priced at $23 — above the $16-$19 marketed range — and closed Day-1 at $29.20 (+27%) for ~$11.9B implied equity value.

In the Apr 24 Bloomberg Tech interview, Sell framed three pillars: replication ("the key to mastery") for cost-down on the Xe-100 design; a customer triple ([[Dow]] industrial → [[Amazon]] data center → [[Energy Northwest]] utility) covering three end-markets; and regulatory progress under the new [[NRC]] leadership ("a key part of launching this new era of nuclear").
