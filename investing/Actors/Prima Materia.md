**Prima Materia** — Investment firm founded 2021 by [[Daniel Ek]] ([[Spotify]]) and Shakil Khan (ex-Spotify, CoinDesk founder). €1B committed to European "moonshots." Not traditional VCs — describe themselves as "builders and active owners." Highly selective: only ~3 direct investments. Key holdings: [[Helsing]] (€12B AI defense, Ek chairs), [[Neko Health]] ($1.8B, Ek co-founded), Epiterna (longevity).

## Investment philosophy

Prima Materia explicitly rejects the traditional VC model:

| Traditional VC | Prima Materia |
|----------------|---------------|
| Portfolio approach | Concentrated (~3 companies) |
| Board seats | Active ownership, operational involvement |
| 5-10 year horizons | "100-year project" |
| Financial returns focus | Fundamental/societal challenges |

They describe themselves as financing "moonshots" with ambitious scientists and engineers.
