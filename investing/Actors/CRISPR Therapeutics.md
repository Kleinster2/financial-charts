## Apr 24 -11.6% drop — sentiment, not earnings

CRSP fell 11.6% to $48.78 on Apr 24 with no new company disclosure scheduled — Q1 2026 earnings are not due until mid-May. The move resolved into three overlapping concerns:

| Concern | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Casgevy commercial ramp | 2025 full-year Casgevy revenue $116M (Q4 alone $54M) versus the steeper trajectory implied by analyst models. Patient activation tripled YoY (147 collections, 64 infusions in 2025) but the cycle from collection to revenue recognition is long, and the absolute revenue base remains small. |
| Cash burn | Q4 2025 net loss $130.6M (vs $37.3M prior-year quarter); R&D $83.5M. Trailing nine-month loss ~$450M. With Vertex milestone deferrals ending and commercialization spend rising, quarterly burn could exceed $150M in 2026. |
| Insider sales | CEO Samarth Kulkarni sold over 10,000 shares ahead of the move. Plus the broader read on insider behavior given the rumor backdrop. |
| Vertex takeover speculation | Renewed market chatter about [[Vertex]] acquiring CRSP — the partnership economics (40% CRSP / 60% Vertex profit split) and CRSP's standalone cash burn make the takeover thesis recurrent. |

The underlying narrative is that the Casgevy commercial story is real but slower than the gene-therapy hype cycle priced. The capital structure has been reinforced (March 2026 $536M convertible raise at ~$76.56 conversion) but the income statement still implies an extended cash-burn runway.

Q1 2026 earnings expected May 11, 2026. The Apr 24 move sets a low bar for the print to surprise positively if Casgevy revenue and patient-activation trajectory beat the marked-down expectations.

## Casgevy (exa-cel)

First FDA-approved CRISPR therapy (Dec 2023)

| Indication | Status |
|------------|--------|
| Sickle cell disease (SCD) | FDA approved |
| Beta thalassemia (TDT) | FDA approved |

| Bahrain | Approved |
| Italy | Reimbursement agreement |

### How it works

1. Patient's stem cells extracted
2. CRISPR edits BCL11A gene
3. Edited cells reinfused
4. Produces fetal hemoglobin (HbF)
5. Functional cure for SCD/TDT
