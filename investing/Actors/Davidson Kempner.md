**Davidson Kempner** — Credit hedge fund ($38B AUM) known for profiting from corporate blow-ups. Founded 1983 by Marvin Davidson, joined by Thomas Kempner in 1984. Managing partner/CIO: Tony Yoseloff (joined 1999). Made ~$3B from [[Lehman Brothers]] collapse. In March 2026, published a report diagnosing "growing risks in the private capital industry" — argues excessive leverage, weak cash flows, and loose debt contracts make a ripe default environment. Positioning to profit from forced private credit asset dumps.
