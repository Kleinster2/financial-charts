# Money Report

**Money Report** — Brazilian financial media brand, affiliated with Times Brasil | CNBC. Producer of the annual "Brazilian Week" series of NYC events. The 7th edition (May 11-14, 2026) was the Money Report-branded layer of [[Brazil Week NYC 2026]], curating private dinners, sponsored panels (at [[Amazon]] HQ, [[BlackRock]], [[BTG Pactual]]), and a closing lunch at Fasano Park Avenue.

## Why it matters

Money Report's Brazilian Week functions as the curated private-dinner and private-panel layer that sits alongside the institutional events ([[BrazilCham]] gala, [[LIDE]] Forum, [[Financial Times|FT]] Brazil Summit). For Brazilian executives traveling to NYC for the week, the MR-branded private lunches and dinners are where deal conversations are had off the record — the public stage is at the Harvard Club and AMNH; the private one is at Restaurant Daniel and Fasano Park Avenue.
