**UK Atomic Energy Authority** (UKAEA) — the [[UK]]'s national nuclear fusion research laboratory, headquartered in Culham, Oxfordshire. Responsible for fusion energy research and technology development since 1954. Parent organization of [[UK Fusion Energy]] (formerly UKIFS), the government's commercial fusion development arm. Operates the Joint European Torus (JET), which held the world record for fusion energy output (59MJ, Feb 2022) until its decommissioning.
