# Ian Cumming

Co-founder of [[Leucadia National]] (now [[Jefferies]]) with [[Joe Steinberg]]. [[Harvard Business School]] 1970. Previously at [[Carl Marks & Company]]. Led Leucadia for 34 years, compounding returns at ~20% annually and growing the conglomerate to a net worth of $6.8B by retirement in 2013. Also owned Snowbird Ski Resort and co-founded Powdr Corporation. Died February 2, 2018 at age 77 at his home in Jackson, Wyoming.
