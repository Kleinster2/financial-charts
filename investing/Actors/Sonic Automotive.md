# Sonic Automotive

Sonic Automotive (NYSE: SAH) is a franchised auto retailer that also runs EchoPark, a standalone used-vehicle retail format. The new+used franchise business plus the EchoPark used-only build-out make it the most used-car-weighted of the [[Franchised auto dealers]] cohort. Not to be confused with [[Panasonic Automotive]].
