**Miguel McKelvey** — American architect, co-founder of [[WeWork]] with [[Adam Neumann]]. Met Neumann at a Tribeca rooftop party around 2005, tipped him off about affordable commercial space in Dumbo. Co-founded GreenDesk (2008) and then WeWork (2010). Departed WeWork in 2020.
