# Extra Space Storage

Extra Space Storage (NYSE: EXR) is the second-largest US self-storage REIT after its 2023 merger with Life Storage, owning, operating, and third-party-managing storage facilities. A pure self-storage single-asset REIT. A member of the [[Self-storage REITs]] cohort.
