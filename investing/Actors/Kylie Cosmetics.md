**Kylie Cosmetics** is a beauty brand founded by [[Kylie Jenner]] in 2015, initially launched as Kylie Lip Kits and built almost entirely on social media marketing and the Kardashian-Jenner audience. [[Coty]] acquired 51% for $600M in January 2020 at an implied $1.2B valuation — a deal that became a cautionary tale of creator-brand decay under corporate ownership. Revenue reportedly declined post-acquisition as the hype cycle cooled, manufacturing shifted to Coty's supply chain, and the brand struggled to evolve beyond its lip-kit origin. Forbes later revised its earlier "youngest self-made billionaire" coverage, acknowledging inflated revenue figures. The brand remains a case study in the limits of the influencer-to-brand playbook: explosive launch driven by personal audience, rapid valuation, but difficulty sustaining growth once the founder's attention and cultural relevance shift.
