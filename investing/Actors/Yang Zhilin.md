## Research contributions

### [[Transformer]]-XL (2019)

Extended [[Transformer]] architecture for longer sequences:
- Segment-level recurrence mechanism
- Relative positional encodings
- Enabled longer context understanding

**Impact:** Foundation for long-context models that followed.

### XLNet (2019)

Generalized autoregressive pretraining:
- Outperformed BERT on 20 tasks at release
- Permutation language modeling
- Bidirectional context without masking

**Citations:** Highly influential in NLP research.
