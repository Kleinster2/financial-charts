#actor #person #media #canada

[[Feras Antoon]] — former CEO of [[Aylo]] (MindGeek). Lebanese-Canadian, based in Montreal. With [[David Tassillo]], purchased MindGeek from [[Fabian Thylmann]] in 2013 for €73M. Resigned June 2022 under pressure from management and board. Sold stake to [[Ethical Capital Partners]] as part of $400M deal in March 2023. Named as co-conspirator in organized crime allegations related to [[Amaya Inc.]] insider trading investigation (Quebec Market Authority).
