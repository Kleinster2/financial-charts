# Franco-Nevada

Franco-Nevada (NYSE/TSX: FNV) is the largest precious-metals royalty and streaming company — a capital-light business that buys royalties and metal streams on mines operated by others, rather than mining itself. Its portfolio spans gold (the majority), silver, PGMs, and a smaller energy royalty book, diversified across dozens of assets. The bellwether of the [[Precious metals royalties]] cohort.
