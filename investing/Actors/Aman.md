# Aman

Ultra-luxury hotel and resort company founded 1988 by Indonesian hotelier Adrian Zecha. First property: Amanpuri, Phuket. 36 properties in 20 countries, 12 in pipeline. Owned by Vladislav Doronin (became sole owner 2015 via Pontwelly Holding Company, acquired from DLF for $358M in 2014). Valued at ~$3B (2022). Properties typically <55 rooms with up to 7:1 staff-to-guest ratio. Expanding into lifestyle: spin-off brand [[Janu]] (11 properties in pipeline), clothing line, fragrance, skincare, supplements. Upcoming: Aman Beverly Hills (2027), Aman Miami Beach (2027), Amanvari Mexico (2026), Aman at Sea yacht (2027). Est. $100–500M revenue. ~$1.3B total funding. HQ in Switzerland.
