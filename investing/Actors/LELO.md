## Founding

Founded 2003 by industrial designers **Eric Kalén** and **Carl Magnuson**, plus engineer **Filip Sedic**. Started with 10 employees in Stockholm; now 600+ across offices in Melbourne, San Jose, and Shanghai.

**Design philosophy:** Brought Scandinavian industrial design aesthetic to sex toys. Products look like consumer electronics, not adult novelties. This enabled retail distribution in mainstream stores.

## Ownership

**Partly owned by Trustar Capital Partners** (Chinese PE firm). Private company — no disclosed valuation.

**IPO exploration (2021):** Considered London listing at £1B+ ($1.4B) valuation. Did not proceed — likely due to market conditions and adult industry stigma with public markets.
