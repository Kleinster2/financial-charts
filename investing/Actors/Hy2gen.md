**Hy2gen** — German private developer of renewable-hydrogen and hydrogen-derived-fuels projects for hard-to-abate industrial sectors.

Hy2gen is relevant to the vault because it represents the project-developer layer of the hydrogen stack: the company is trying to turn hydrogen demand from a thematic story into financed, built, industrial assets. That makes it a useful readthrough name for suppliers like [[Plug Power]].

## Why it matters

Hy2gen matters because it sits at the project-developer layer of the hydrogen stack rather than the equipment-vendor layer. For [[Plug Power]], that makes it a useful counterparty: if Hy2gen keeps advancing real projects, Plug can show demand for both electrolyzer systems and hydrogen supply logistics.

## Relationship with [[Plug Power]]

- Apr 2, 2026: Plug said it won the FEED contract to supply a 275 MW GenEco PEM electrolyzer system for Hy2gen Canada's Courant project in [[Canada]].
- Plug said the Courant facility is intended to make low-carbon ammonia and then renewable ammonium nitrate for mining demand.
- Plug said the award builds on previous collaborations in [[Europe]] and ongoing hydrogen-supply arrangements.
- In October 2025, Plug said it was sourcing hydrogen for [[H2CAST]] from Hy2Gen ATLANTIS in Werlte, [[Germany]], where Plug held exclusivity on the plant's green-hydrogen output.
