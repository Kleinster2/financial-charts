# CF Industries

The world's largest ammonia producer with ~10.4 Mt/year capacity. Headquartered in Deerfield, Illinois. Founded 1946. Donaldsonville, Louisiana plant is the single largest ammonia production facility globally. Developing blue ammonia via the Blue Point Complex JV with [[JERA]] and [[Mitsui]] (Louisiana, target production 2029).
