# BGC Group

Interdealer brokerage and financial technology company. Originally spun out of [[Cantor Fitzgerald]] in 2004 as BGC Partners L.P. (named for founder B. Gerald Cantor), merged with eSpeed (Cantor's electronic trading platform) in 2008 to become publicly traded. Acquired [[Newmark]] Knight Frank in 2011 (spun off in 2018). [[Cantor Fitzgerald]] retains controlling interest. [[Howard Lutnick]] served as chairman until becoming Commerce Secretary (2025).
