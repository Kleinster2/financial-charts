# Heikki Malinen

Heikki Malinen is CEO of [[Neste]], the world's largest producer of [[Sustainable Aviation Fuel]] and renewable diesel. He took the role in May 2023 after serving as CEO of Outokumpu (stainless steel). Malinen is leading Neste through its ~$10B transformation from a traditional Finnish oil refiner to a global renewable fuels leader, including the $1.9B Singapore refinery expansion (completed 2023) that doubled renewable product capacity to 5.5 Mt/year.

Before Outokumpu and Neste, Malinen held executive roles at [[Fortum]] and served on the board of the Finnish state investment company Solidium. His appointment was seen as a signal that [[Neste]]'s board wanted operational discipline and capital allocation rigor for the next phase — scaling production while navigating feedstock cost volatility and the uncertain pace of SAF mandates in the [[EU]] and US.
