**Legg Mason** — Former asset manager, acquired by [[Franklin Templeton]] for $4.5B in 2020. Founded 1899 in Baltimore. Home of [[Bill Miller]]'s legendary Value Trust (15-year S&P streak). [[Michael Mauboussin]] was chief investment strategist. Key affiliate Western Asset ($460B fixed income) was the prize.

## Why Legg Mason matters

**Home of legendary investors.** [[Bill Miller]] built his 15-year streak here. [[Michael Mauboussin]] developed his research approach. The firm's independent affiliate model let star managers operate autonomously.

**Western Asset was the prize.** The $460B fixed income shop accounted for 57% of Legg Mason's AUM and was Franklin Templeton's main target.

## Investment implications

**No longer exists as independent entity.** Affiliates now part of Franklin Templeton.

**Legacy:**
- Proved independent affiliate model could work
- Incubated legendary investors (Miller, Mauboussin)
- Western Asset continues as major fixed income manager

**Indirect exposure:**
- Franklin Resources (BEN) owns former Legg Mason affiliates
- Western Asset funds available through Franklin Templeton
