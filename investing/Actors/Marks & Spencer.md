**Marks & Spencer** is a British retailer operating across food and clothing, listed on the LSE as MKS. Revenue exceeds GBP 13B. The company has a long history of failed international expansion: it exited France (closed all stores), shut Hong Kong and China operations, and withdrew from multiple other markets. Now primarily focused on the UK and Ireland, with selective international presence through franchise partnerships.
