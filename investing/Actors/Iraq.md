# Iraq

OPEC member and 5th largest oil producer globally (~4.5M bbl/day). Rumaila is the world's largest oilfield, shut during the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|Hormuz crisis]] due to storage constraints. Heavy fuel oil consumer for power generation. All Iraqi crude exits via Basra/Gulf terminals through the [[Strait of Hormuz]], making exports fully dependent on strait security.
