Lynas Rare Earths is the leading non-Chinese rare earth producer, anchored by the Mt Weld mine in [[Australia]] and processing operations in [[Malaysia]]. It is the main investable proof point for [[Japan]]'s post-2010 rare earth diversification strategy, but its May 2026 heavy-rare-earth output still shows how small the non-China alternative base remains.
