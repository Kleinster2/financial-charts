## Why Abbott matters

**CGM market leader.** Abbott's FreeStyle Libre franchise achieved its third consecutive year of billion-dollar growth. The system dominates consumer CGM with broad adoption across diabetic and non-diabetic users tracking metabolic health.

**Diagnostic breadth.** From rapid COVID tests (BinaxNOW) to core laboratory systems to point-of-care diagnostics, Abbott touches most of the diagnostic value chain.

**Exact Sciences acquisition.** In November 2025, Abbott announced acquisition of Exact Sciences at $105/share (~$23B), positioning it to lead in cancer diagnostics. **Deal closed March 2026** — Q1 print now reflects partial-quarter contribution; FY26 adj EPS guide embeds $0.20 dilution.

**Stock reaction:** ABT **-6.0% to $95.47 Apr 16**, a **-3.3σ** move (60-day beta-adj). Headline EPS met — Q2 guide is what the market punished.

**Structural reads:**
- Exact Sciences closed Mar 2026, earlier than the Q2-2026 close originally messaged — Q1 print is the first post-close quarter, with the $0.20 dilution now explicit in FY guide.
- Nutrition weakness (-6% reported, Similac/Ensure) is the volume-pricing tradeoff continuing — long-tail problem (GLP-1 pressure on infant formula less severe than feared; pricing actions eating near-term volumes).
- Device segment (Libre CGM, EP with Volt PFA, Navitor TAVR) remains the growth engine — double-digit. Cancer diagnostics now additive via Exact Sciences + Oncomine.
- Q2 guide miss is the tell: midpoint ~$1.28 vs $1.34 consensus is a ~5% cut. Absent the Exact Sciences dilution ($0.20), the guide would be closer to $1.48 — so the FY EPS print is structurally stronger; Q2 is where the deal integration shows up.

## Investment considerations

**Bull case:**
- Libre CGM growth runway (non-diabetic expansion)
- Exact Sciences positions for cancer diagnostics leadership
- Diversification buffers segment weakness
- Strong organic growth + accretive M&A

**Bear case:**
- Nutrition segment struggling (Similac recall aftermath)
- COVID diagnostic revenue still declining
- GLP-1 drugs could reduce diabetes prevalence long-term
- Exact Sciences integration risk
