## How GDIT fits in GD

[[General Dynamics]] has four segments:

| Segment | Revenue | Products |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Marine Systems | ~$13B | Submarines (Virginia, Columbia) |
| Aerospace | ~$10B | Gulfstream jets |
| Combat Systems | ~$8B | Tanks, vehicles |
| **Technologies** | ~$13B | **GDIT** + Mission Systems |

GDIT is the IT services piece of the Technologies segment.
