## Insights

- **Unmatched scale** — 140K employees, 100+ countries. No peer comes close. Scale enables cross-selling, data advantages, and global client coverage.
- **Diversified revenue** — 70% from Workplace Solutions (recurring/resilient), 27% from Advisory (transactional). Less cyclical than pure brokerage.
- **Vertical integration** — Brokerage + property management + development + investment management. Captures multiple fee streams per asset.
