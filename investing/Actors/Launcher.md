#actor #space #usa #acquired

**Launcher** — Small-lift rocket startup founded 2017 by [[Max Haot]] (former Livestream founder). Acquired by [[Vast Space]] in February 2023 — Haot joined Vast as president (later CEO), and Launcher's Engine-2 propulsion technology was integrated into Vast's space station program. Pre-acquisition, Launcher was developing Launcher Light (small-lift orbital rocket) and Orbiter (in-space transfer vehicle).

## Why Launcher matters

The 2023 Launcher → Vast acquisition is the pivot point for both companies: Vast gained propulsion engineering capability and an experienced commercial-space CEO ([[Max Haot]]); Launcher's roadmap was effectively redirected from small-lift launch to commercial space station development. The Engine-2 tech that powers Vast's [[Haven-1]] reaction control system traces directly to Launcher's pre-acquisition R&D.
