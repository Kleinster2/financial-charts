# Scott Barshay

M&A partner at [[Paul, Weiss]]. The 2016 lateral move that brought him from [[Cravath, Swaine & Moore]] to Paul, Weiss is the conventional date stamp on the [[Big Law transformation]] — the first time a top corporate-M&A originator at a true lockstep elite firm was moved with a market-rate package. The shift, reported as multiples of Cravath's lockstep band, kicked off a decade of similar moves and the structural shift toward investment-bank-style compensation in Big Law.
