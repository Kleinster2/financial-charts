# Ecko Unltd.

**Ecko Unltd.** is an American urban fashion brand founded by [[Marc Ecko]] in 1993. Peaked at $1B+ global revenue (2009) as the largest streetwear brand in the world. Iconix Brand Group acquired 51% for $109M in 2009; the brand subsequently lost cultural relevance through over-distribution into discount channels.
