# Starwood Property Trust

Starwood Property Trust (NYSE: STWD) is the largest US commercial mortgage REIT — originating and holding commercial real-estate (CRE) loans and securities — externally managed by Barry Sternlicht's [[Starwood Capital]]. A levered CRE-credit vehicle, not a property owner. A member of the [[Mortgage REITs]] cohort (commercial/credit wing).
