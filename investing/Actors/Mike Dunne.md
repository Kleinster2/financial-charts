**Mike Dunne** — laser physicist. CTO and co-founder of [[Inertia Enterprises]]. Professor at Stanford / SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory. Directed the Linac Coherent Light Source. Previously led [[LLNL]]'s power plant design program based on [[National Ignition Facility]] technology. Ran the UK's Central Laser Facility, overseeing the world's most powerful lasers at the time.
