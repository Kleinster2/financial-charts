# Li Ning

**Chinese sportswear company** founded by Olympic gymnast Li Ning. Hong Kong-listed (2331.HK). Direct competitor to [[Anta Sports]] in China domestic market. Known for "China Li Ning" nationalist branding and fashion-forward designs. Benefited from 2021 Xinjiang cotton boycott of Western brands. More fashion-oriented positioning vs Anta's multi-brand portfolio approach.
