# Sunrun

Sunrun (Nasdaq: RUN) is the largest US residential solar installer, operating primarily a lease / power-purchase-agreement (PPA) model in which it owns the rooftop systems and sells the power, increasingly bundled with battery storage. As a financing-heavy residential model, it is the most interest-rate-sensitive name in the [[Solar]] cohort — high rates raise its cost of capital and depress residential demand. A high-volatility, rate-driven residential-solar name.
