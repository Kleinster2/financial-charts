## Business model

**Transaction fees + data:**
- Per-contract fees
- Clearing fees
- Market data licensing
- Colocation services
- 85%+ operating margins

Volume × volatility = revenue.

---

## Volatility correlation

| Market condition | CME impact |
|------------------|------------|
| High volatility | Revenue surge |
| Rate uncertainty | Treasury volume up |
| Commodity swings | Energy/metals volume up |
| Low volatility | Volume decline |

CME benefits from uncertainty.

---

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Monopoly on key benchmarks
- Volatility = tailwind
- High margins (85%+)
- [[Network effects]]
- Rate uncertainty persists

**Bear:**
- Volume-dependent
- Low volatility risk
- [[Crypto]] competition
- Regulatory scrutiny
