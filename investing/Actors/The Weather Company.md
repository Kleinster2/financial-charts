# The Weather Company

Weather data and technology platform. Owned by [[IBM]] (acquired 2016). Previously led by David Kenny (now Executive Chairman of [[Nielsen]]). Operates weather.com and provides B2B weather data, forecasting APIs, and advertising solutions. Sold by IBM to private equity firm Francisco Partners in 2023 for ~$1B.
