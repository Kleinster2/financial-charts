# O'Reilly Automotive

O'Reilly Automotive (Nasdaq: ORLY) is a premium US auto-parts retailer serving both DIY consumers and DIFM (do-it-for-me) professional shops through a dense store and distribution network, with consistent mid-single-digit comps through cycles. It executed a 15-for-1 stock split in mid-2025. A member of the [[Auto parts retail]] cohort and half of its robust core.
