## Memory cost response

**Stockpiling strategy:**
- Racing to stockpile memory inventory
- Like [[Lenovo]], building buffer
- Working capital tied up in inventory
- Goal: Insulate from price spikes

**Pricing approach:**
- Holding prices steady through holiday quarter
- Reconsider market situation in new year
