**EU** — European Union. 27 member states. World's largest single market. Political and economic union with shared currency (eurozone), institutions, and policy.

## Key policies

### Single market

| Freedom | Coverage |
|---------|----------|
| Goods | Tariff-free, harmonized standards |
| Services | Partial (financial passporting) |
| Capital | Free movement |
| People | Free movement (Schengen) |

### Trade policy

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Common external tariff | Customs union |
| FTA negotiator | Commission negotiates for all 27 |
| Major FTAs | [[EU-Japan EPA]], [[CETA]], [[EU-Mercosur agreement]] |

See [[Trade Agreements]] and [[Trade]] for bilateral tensions.

### Competition policy

| Tool | Examples |
|------|----------|
| Antitrust | Google fines ($8B+) |
| Merger control | Blocked Siemens-Alstom |
| State aid | Apple Ireland €13B |

### Regulation

| Area | Key rules |
|------|-----------|
| Data | GDPR |
| AI | EU AI Act |
| Digital markets | DMA, DSA |
| Climate | Green Deal, Fit for 55 |
| Finance | MiFID, Basel implementation |
