## Google Vizag AI hub partner (Apr 2026)

[[AdaniConneX]] is one of the two named local infrastructure partners for [[Google Cloud]]'s $15B Visakhapatnam AI hub, alongside [[Bharti Airtel]]'s [[Nxtra]]. The project is framed as a gigawatt-scale AI ecosystem with three data-center campuses, local construction/operations, fiber and cable-landing infrastructure, and a long-term clean-energy strategy.

For Adani, this is the concrete version of the February 2026 AI data-center commitment. The group is not getting model economics; it is converting land, power, construction, and data-center operations into hyperscaler-grade capacity. That can be a strong infrastructure business even if the [[India AI]] sovereignty question remains open.

*Sources: Google Cloud press release, Apr 28 2026; [[Rohit Lamba]] / [[Raghuram Rajan]], May 7 2026.*

## US legal-resolution (FT May 14, 2026)

The [[SEC]] settled its case with Gautam Adani and his nephew Sagar Adani on Thursday May 14. [[DoJ]] and [[OFAC]] are preparing follow-on resolutions that would close all open US enforcement actions against Adani and the group. Adani's legal team disclosed to SEC and DoJ officials that the billionaire had pledged $10B of US investment after Trump's 2024 election win — the person familiar said the investment was not part of any law-enforcement deal, but the timing is unmissable.

| Item | Resolution |
|---|---|
| SEC settlement (filed Thursday May 14) | Gautam Adani $6M + Sagar Adani $12M; no admission of wrongdoing; court approval pending |
| DoJ criminal case (bribery allegations) | Preparing to dismiss per FT sources; case could not have gone to trial without defendant US travel |
| OFAC investigation (alleged Iranian fuel imports into India) | Adani Group company in talks to settle |
| Pledged US investment (announced post-Trump 2024) | $10B |
| Counsel | Robert Giuffra (co-chair, Sullivan & Cromwell) — also represented Trump; hired earlier in 2026 |

The settlement removes the cross-border legal overhang that had constrained Adani's offshore movements and group financing. Deven Choksey (DRChoksey FinServ, Mumbai): "the real significance is reputational and strategic... with legal clouds cleared, the group can refinance existing high-cost debt at tighter spreads and longer tenures."

Adani Group share reaction Friday: Adani Ports +2%, Adani Enterprises +2.9%, Adani Green (the company implicated in the SEC / DoJ cases) +1.7%. Two strategic actions in the same week as the SEC filing: [[Adani Ports]] / [[Oceaneering International]] subsea engineering partnership in Europe; [[Uber]] CEO Dara Khosrowshahi visit to Ahmedabad announcing Uber's first India data centre to be built with Adani Group ("test and deploy our tech").

Regulatory backdrop: The Trump administration last year halted then restarted enforcement of the Foreign Corrupt Practices Act. Adani himself was not charged under [[FCPA]], but the pause signalled lower anti-bribery enforcement priority. India's capital markets regulator cleared the group of Hindenburg allegations in 2025.

*Source: [SEC settles Adani case as US authorities move to end actions against Asia's richest man](https://www.ft.com/content/4d9bfc38-9f14-42a5-8962-6f6ce83f3eb0), FT, May 14 2026, by Kaye Wiggins (NY) and Chris Kay + Krishn Kaushik (Mumbai).*
