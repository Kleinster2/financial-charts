## Investment case

**Bull:**
- 713M MAUs, growing
- Profitability achieved
- Podcasts/audiobooks diversification
- Price increases sticking
- Market leader globally

**Bear:**
- Label negotiating power
- Apple/Amazon competition
- Margin pressure from content costs
- Podcast ROI questioned
- High P/E (~73x)
