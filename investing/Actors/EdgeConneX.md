## Project Walleye (Apr 2026)

Operating a [[Meta]]-backed data center campus in New Albany, Ohio. Seeking $3B in construction loans with a first-of-its-kind financing structure: lenders fund both construction and power generation assets in a single transaction. The campus will operate in "island mode" -- its own microgrid running on natural gas, independent from the main grid, with potential grid connection later. Debt pitched at [[SOFR]] + 250bp, expected to receive private investment-grade rating thanks to Meta lease. See [[BYOP]], [[AI infrastructure financing]].

*Source: FT, Michelle Chan, Antoine Gara, Eric Platt, Rafe Rosner-Uddin (Apr 3, 2026)*
