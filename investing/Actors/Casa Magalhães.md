Casa Magalhães — a Brazilian retail- and commercial-automation company founded in 1966 in Fortaleza, Ceará, serving 18,000+ clients with point-of-sale and retail-management systems. Acquired by [[Grupo Boticário]] in 2021, then sold to family office [[Cleam Capital]] in March 2025 — one of the deals that assembled Cleam's logistics/retail-tech portfolio.
