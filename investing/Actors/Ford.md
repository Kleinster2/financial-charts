## F-150 Lightning retirement

**Strategy pivot:**
- Ending pure-EV F-150 Lightning production
- Shifting to extended-range EVs (EREV)
- Battery + combustion generator hybrid approach
- New Universal EV Platform launching 2027

Despite strong Q3 sales, Ford abandoning full-EV truck strategy.

---

## Future: Affordable EVs

**2027 target:**
- Midsize EV pickup (Ranger/Maverick sized)
- Starting ~$30,000
- Based on new Universal EV Platform
- CEO Farley: affordable EVs "right around the corner"
