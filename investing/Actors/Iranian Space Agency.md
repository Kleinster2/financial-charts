# Iranian Space Agency

[[Iran]]'s civilian space agency, distinct from the [[IRGC]] Aerospace Force which runs [[Iran]]'s military satellite, missile, and drone programmes. [[Israel]]'s military said it struck the Iranian Space Agency's main research centre in mid-March 2026, asserting the centre was being used to develop military satellites and intelligence collection and to direct "fire toward targets across the Middle East." The strike illustrates the increasing blur between Iranian civilian and military space activity; separately, the IRGC Aerospace Force had by then acquired survivable foreign-ground-station ISR via [[Emposat]] and [[TEE-01B]] that was not affected by the domestic strike (see [[IRGC Chinese satellite targeting March 2026]]).
