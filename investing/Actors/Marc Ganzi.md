## Asset rotation

As CEO, Ganzi rotated **$73B of assets**:
- Sold $40B legacy real estate (hotels, offices, healthcare, retail)
- Built $33B digital holdings (data centers, towers, fiber)

Transformed Colony from distressed real estate into pure-play digital infrastructure.
