# Hony Capital

Hony Capital is a [[China]] private equity firm founded in 2003, affiliated with [[Legend Holdings]] (the parent of [[Lenovo]]). With ~$15B+ in cumulative capital raised, it is one of China's largest homegrown PE firms. Hony invests across technology, healthcare, consumer, and industrial sectors, with a mix of growth equity and buyout strategies.

The firm was established by John Zhao (Zhao John) as Legend Holdings' PE arm, initially focused on SOE restructurings and management buyouts during China's state-sector reform era. It evolved into a broader platform, backing deals like the $3.5B buyout of Pizza Hut China (later divested) and investments in healthcare (China Biologic Products) and financial services. Hony's relationship with Legend Holdings gives it a strategic edge in deal sourcing within China's tech and manufacturing ecosystem, though the firm has operated increasingly independently as it raised third-party capital from global LPs.
