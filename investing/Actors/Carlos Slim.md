Carlos Slim — Mexican billionaire, ~$99B net worth (2025). Controls [[América Móvil]] (Latin America's largest telecom), Grupo Carso (industrial conglomerate), and significant real estate holdings. Richest person in Latin America. América Móvil's Claro brand sponsors [[Cadillac]] F1 via Sergio Perez.
