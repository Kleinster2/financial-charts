# Cleary Gottlieb

Global law firm founded 1946 in New York. ~1,200 lawyers across 16 offices worldwide. Top-tier in sovereign debt restructuring (advised [[Argentina]], [[Greece]], others), cross-border M&A, antitrust, and capital markets. Known for representing debtor-side in major EM restructurings.
