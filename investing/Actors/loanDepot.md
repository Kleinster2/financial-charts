## Insights

- **11-quarter loss streak** — First profitable quarter in Q3 2024, then back to losses. Structural challenges from rate environment.
- **Cybersecurity disaster** — Jan 2024 ransomware attack exposed 16.6M customer records. $12-17M direct cost, class action lawsuits ongoing.
- **Founder return** — Anthony Hsieh returned as CEO after Frank Martell's planned departure. Betting on digital transformation.
