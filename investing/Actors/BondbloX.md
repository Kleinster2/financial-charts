#actor #fintech #fixedincome #singapore #private

**BondbloX** — Singapore-based fintech platform democratizing bond market access. Fractional bond trading ($1,000 minimum) via blockchain-based exchange. Formerly BondEvalue.

## Product

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|

| Pricing | Live from FINRA TRACE, transparent bid-ask |
