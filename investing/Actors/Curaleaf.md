# Curaleaf

A US cannabis multistate operator with one of the largest retail and cultivation footprints in the country, spanning consumer brands (Select, Grassroots) and an expanding international medical business in Europe. Among the biggest US MSOs by revenue alongside [[Trulieve]] and [[Green Thumb Industries]], though historically more leveraged and less consistently cash-generative than [[Green Thumb Industries|GTBIF]]. Federally illegal under [[Cannabis rescheduling|Schedule I]], taxed under [[280E]], OTC/CSE-listed. A member of the [[Cannabis]] cohort's US MSO core (sub-intra 0.84), it collapses into [[MSOS]].
