Otter.ai — established AI meeting notes platform with bot-based capture and live collaborative transcription. Founded 2016, based in Mountain View.

## Why Otter matters

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Category | AI meeting notes (bot + local) |
| Differentiator | Live collaborative transcription; team Channels |
| Last disclosed round | $50M Series B (Feb 2021) |
| Headcount | 278 employees (Apr 2026) |
| Status | Private |

Per [[YipitData]] (Apr 2026), Otter has roughly 20% more customers than [[Granola]] but spend per customer is plateauing. The longer-tenured enterprise install base is the moat; the absence of a fresh round since Feb 2021 leaves valuation stale relative to the AI [[SaaS]] cohort.

Open question after Apr 15 2026: [[Fathom AI]] shipped a hybrid bot / bot-free capture mode with speaker diarization, explicitly framed as a [[Granola]] response. Otter has not yet matched the architecture retrofit. Whether it does in Q2–Q3 2026 is the next read on whether the bot-vs-bot-free axis is closing across the bot-based cohort, or whether Otter's enterprise install-base discipline holds the moat without the architectural change.
