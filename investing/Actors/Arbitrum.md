#actor #crypto #blockchain #l2

**Arbitrum** — Ethereum layer-2 rollup network developed by Offchain Labs. Uses optimistic rollup technology to batch transactions off-chain and settle them on [[Ethereum]] mainnet, producing lower fees and higher throughput than L1 while inheriting Ethereum's security. Home to a meaningful slice of the decentralized AI landscape — [[Morpheus]] inference network and [[Aethir]] GPU-compute network both chose Arbitrum for enterprise-compatible tooling.

## Relevance

For the [[Decentralized AI landscape]], Arbitrum has attracted compute- and inference-layer projects that want Ethereum-grade settlement without L1 gas costs. [[Morpheus]] and [[Aethir]] both settle on Arbitrum. This differentiates Arbitrum from [[Solana]] (home to data-layer projects like [[Grass]] and agent frameworks like [[ElizaOS]]) and [[Base]] (home to agent-launchpad protocols like [[Virtuals Protocol]]).
