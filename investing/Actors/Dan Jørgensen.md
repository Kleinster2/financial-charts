# Dan Jørgensen

[[EU]] Energy Commissioner (from Dec 2024). Danish politician ([[Social Democrats (Denmark)|Social Democrats]]). Lowered EU gas storage target from 90% to 80% and pushed deadline from Nov 1 to Dec 1 during the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|Iran conflict]] energy crisis. Publicly framed the situation as a "long-lasting crisis" (Apr 2026).
