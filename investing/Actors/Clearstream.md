## Core services

**Settlement & Custody:**
- International securities (Eurobonds — EUR 14T market)
- German domestic securities
- 300,000+ domestic and internationally traded bonds

**Collateral Management (Triparty):**
- 550+ participants
- Real-time allocation, optimization, substitution
- Central bank pledge facilities (BCL, Bundesbank, Fed, MAS)
- **ECMS integration** launched June 2025 — only CSD with full tri-party collateral management integration

**Global Securities Financing (GSF):**
- Securities lending and repo
- EUR 718B outstanding (Nov 2024)

**Fund Services (Vestima):**
- World's largest fund processing platform
- Order routing, DVP settlement, custody

---

## Competitive position

**Duopoly with [[Euroclear]]:**
- Both dominate Eurobond settlement
- Connected via "the Bridge" — electronic link for inter-ICSD settlement
- **Joint digitization initiative** (2025): Q1 2026 readiness for dematerialized Eurobond issuance

**[[Deutsche Borse]] integration:**
- Synergies with Eurex (clearing), 360T (FX)
- Part of Securities Services segment
- Parent revenue: EUR 5.8B (2024), +15% YoY

## Recent developments

**D7 Digital Platform:**
- Next-generation digital post-trade platform
- First dematerialized issuances processed
- ECB trials (May-Nov 2024): tokenized commercial paper, intraday repo
- DLT platform launching Q4 2025 with Eurobonds
- E-shares planned for H2 2026

**Crypto Custody (April 2025):**
- Launched via Crypto Finance (Deutsche Borse entity)
- MiCAR license obtained January 2025
- Bitcoin and Ether custody for institutional clients

**T+1:** EU/UK move to T+1 on October 11, 2027. Joint research with [[DTCC]], [[Euroclear]].

## Iran sanctions history

- 2014: $152M settlement with OFAC
- Held $2.8B in securities for Central Bank of Iran via US omnibus account
- Criminal investigation by SDNY
- ~$4.9B in Iranian assets frozen (2008), ongoing legal disputes
