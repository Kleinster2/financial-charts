**Tokamak Energy** — UK-based fusion energy company developing compact spherical tokamak reactors. Founded 2009, spun out of [[Culham Centre for Fusion Energy]]. CEO Warrick Matthews. Raised >$250M. Targets grid-connected fusion power by early 2030s. Uses high-temperature superconducting (HTS) magnets for compact reactor design.
