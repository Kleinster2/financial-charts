Rob Lucas is CEO of [[CVC Capital Partners]], one of Europe's largest private equity firms. Took the CEO role as CVC prepared for and executed its April 2024 IPO on the Amsterdam Euronext, which valued the firm at approximately €15B. Previously served as CVC's managing partner and head of European operations. Under his leadership, CVC has expanded into credit, secondaries, and infrastructure, managing over €185B in AUM across private equity, credit, and other strategies. The IPO made CVC the latest in a wave of PE firms going public (following [[KKR]], [[Apollo]], [[Ares Management]], and [[Blackstone]]), driven by the secular shift toward permanent capital vehicles.
