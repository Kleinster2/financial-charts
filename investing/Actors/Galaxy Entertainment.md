# Galaxy Entertainment

Hong Kong-listed casino and resort operator, one of the six concessionaires licensed to operate in [[Macau]]. Runs Galaxy Macau (the largest integrated resort on Cotai Strip) and StarWorld Hotel on the Macau Peninsula. Controlled by the Lui family through Galaxy Entertainment Group Ltd.

Galaxy is the most mass-market-oriented of the Macau operators, with the highest proportion of revenue from non-VIP gaming. The company has been investing heavily in Phase 4 of Galaxy Macau, expanding convention, entertainment, and non-gaming amenities to align with the Chinese government's push to diversify Macau beyond gambling. Held as a ~2.9% weight in [[EWH]] (iShares MSCI Hong Kong ETF).
