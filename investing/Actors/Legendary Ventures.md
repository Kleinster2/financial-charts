# Legendary Ventures

**Legendary Ventures** is a US-based investment firm that led [[SpaceX]]'s Series N round in August 2020, a $2.567B raise at $270/share.

## Synopsis

Legendary Ventures is notable for leading one of SpaceX's pivotal fundraises — the August 2020 Series N at $270/share, which valued the company at roughly $46B. This round came at a critical inflection point: [[SpaceX]] had just demonstrated Crew Dragon's first crewed mission to the ISS (May 2020, the DM-2 mission) and [[Starlink]] was beginning its beta rollout. The round effectively priced in both the launch business maturation and the Starlink optionality that would later drive the company's valuation past $200B.

Leading a SpaceX primary round requires meaningful institutional heft — these are not participation stakes but lead allocations with significant capital commitments. The $2.567B round size reflected the growing appetite for SpaceX exposure among institutional investors at a time when the private space market was shifting from speculative to infrastructure-grade. Legendary's positioning as lead placed it at the center of SpaceX's capital formation during the Starlink scaling phase, before the secondary market frenzy that would later push implied valuations far higher.
