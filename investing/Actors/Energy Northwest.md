**Energy Northwest** — Pacific Northwest joint-action public-power agency. Operates Columbia Generating Station (only commercial nuclear reactor in the Pacific Northwest) and is one of the early utility customers for [[X-energy]]'s Xe-100 small modular reactor.

## X-energy partnership

Energy Northwest is one of [[X-energy]]'s anchor utility customers, alongside [[Dow]] (industrial) and [[Amazon]] (data-center). Cited by CEO [[Clay Sell]] in the Apr 24 2026 Bloomberg Tech IPO interview as part of the customer triple validating Xe-100 across three end-markets. Specific deployment timeline and unit count have not been publicly disclosed.
