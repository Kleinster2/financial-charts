# Reese Witherspoon

Actress and media entrepreneur (Hello Sunshine, sold to Candle Media for ~$900M in 2021). Vocal AI advocate — calls AI a "mandatory utility for creators." Positioned at the intersection of Hollywood and AI adoption, framing AI as essential creative infrastructure rather than a threat to creative workers.
