# Reese Witherspoon

Actress and media entrepreneur. Founded Hello Sunshine (sold to Candle Media for ~$900M in 2021). Calls AI a "mandatory utility for creators" — one of the strongest pro-AI statements from a major Hollywood figure. Her production company background makes her a natural integrator of AI content tools.
