# Shanghai Stock Exchange

China's largest stock exchange by market cap. Main board focus — large-cap SOEs, financials, industrials. Founded 1990.

---

## Key facts

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Founded | 1990 |
| Listed companies | ~2,200 |
| Market cap | ~$7T |
| Focus | Large-cap, SOEs, financials |
| Index | SSE Composite, SSE 50 |

---

## Role in A-share market

Primary venue for:
- State-owned enterprise listings
- Large-cap financials (banks, insurers)
- Blue chips

Works in coordination with [[Shenzhen Stock Exchange]] and [[Beijing Stock Exchange]] on regulatory policy. See [[China A-share exchanges]] for coordinated actions.
