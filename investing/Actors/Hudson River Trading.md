## Mid-frequency expansion: Prism unit

HRT launched a separate unit called Prism to house mid-frequency trading signals with holding periods longer than traditional HFT (hours to days rather than seconds). Prism reportedly produced more than $2bn in profits in 2024, making it one of the most significant profit centers at the firm.

The expansion reflects the broader [[HFT-hedge fund convergence]] trend: pure low-latency trading is butting up against the limits of physics and intense competition, so firms with excess capital are deploying it into strategies with longer holding periods and higher capacity.

HRT has also tapped debt markets to boost firepower beyond retained profits, alongside [[Citadel Securities]] and [[Jane Street]].

*Source: FT Alphaville (Sep 29 2025)*
