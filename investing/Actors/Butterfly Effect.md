# Butterfly Effect

Original mainland China parent company of [[Manus AI]], the [[AI agents|AI agent]] platform founded by [[Xiao Hong]] and [[Ji Yichao]]. Backed by [[HongShan]] (lead, ~$85M valuation) and [[Benchmark]]. Underwent offshore [[Singapore washing|restructuring]] in July 2025 — relocating headquarters and core operating entity to [[Singapore]] — ahead of the December 2025 $2B [[Meta]] acquisition. The restructuring is the specific corporate action that the [[NDRC]] April 27, 2026 unwind order treats as an unauthorized cross-border technology transfer requiring an export license that was never sought.
