**Tim Sweeney** is the founder and CEO of [[Epic Games]], the private gaming and real-time-3D software company behind [[Fortnite]] and [[Unreal Engine]].

## HOF relevance

Sweeney matters to the HOF cluster because Epic is still founder-led. The investment exposure is not just to Fortnite or Unreal Engine; it is also to Sweeney's long-running platform fight against distribution tolls and his willingness to use Epic's private balance sheet for strategic litigation and ecosystem building.
