# GHCL

[[India]]'s largest [[Soda Ash|soda ash]] producer (~30% domestic market share) with 1.2 Mt/year capacity at Sutrapada, Gujarat. Received environmental clearance (Dec 2024) for a greenfield 1.1 Mt expansion in Kutch, Gujarat (Rs 6,500 crore / ~$780M investment over six years). Synthetic production via Solvay process.
