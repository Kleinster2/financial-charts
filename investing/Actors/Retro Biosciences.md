#actor #biotech #longevity #healthcare #private

**Retro Biosciences** is a private regenerative-medicine and longevity company backed by [[Sam Altman]], working on cellular reprogramming, small molecules, and gene therapy with the stated goal of adding ten healthy years to human lifespan. Part of the [[Longevity]] cohort and the most clinically advanced of the [[Sam Altman]]-adjacent reprogramming names.

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Status | Private |
| Backer | [[Sam Altman]] ($180M seed) |
| Valuation | ~$1.8B (round closed May 2026; earlier press floated a $5B target) |
| Approach | iPSC-derived therapies, small molecules, gene therapy; partial reprogramming |
| Lead clinical program | Alzheimer's pill (enhances clearance of protein aggregates); first-in-human trial underway, no dose-limiting toxicities; data expected ~August 2026 |
| AI collaboration | OpenAI GPT-4b — designed novel Yamanaka-factor variants with >50x reprogramming-marker expression vs wild type (Aug 2025) |
| Goal | +10 healthy years of lifespan |

Retro is notable for being further into the clinic than better-funded peers like [[Altos Labs]], and for the explicit AI-for-biology link to [[OpenAI]] (the GPT-4b protein-design collaboration). The August 2026 Alzheimer's readout is the field's nearest binary catalyst.
