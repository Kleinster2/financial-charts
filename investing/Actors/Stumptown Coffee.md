# Stumptown Coffee

Portland-based specialty coffee roaster founded 1999. Acquired by Peet's Coffee ([[JDE Peet's]]) in 2015, making it part of the [[JAB Holding]] coffee portfolio. Known for direct-trade sourcing and cold brew innovation.
