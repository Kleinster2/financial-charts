# Beats

Audio brand and former independent company, Beats Electronics, that [[Apple]] acquired in 2014 — its largest acquisition ever. Founded in 2006 by rap producer Dr. Dre and music-industry executive Jimmy Iovine, Beats built the dominant premium-headphone brand (Beats by Dr. Dre) and a subscription service, Beats Music.

[[Apple]] announced the ~$3bn purchase (about $2.6bn cash plus roughly $400mn in stock) on May 28 2014 and closed it that August; Dre and Iovine joined Apple as executives. Beats Music became the technical and licensing foundation for [[Apple Music]], launched in 2015, while the Beats hardware brand was kept as a distinct premium headphone sub-label. The deal remains Apple's largest acquisition; the ~$2bn [[Apple acquires Q.ai|Q.ai deal]] ranks second. See [[Apple]] for the parent's M&A and Services history.
