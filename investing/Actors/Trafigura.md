# Trafigura

Swiss commodity trading house, one of the world's largest. Major trader of crude oil, refined products, and metals (zinc, copper). Operates TFG Marine, a bunkering JV with [[Frontline]] and [[Golden Ocean Group]], ranked #9 globally (9.7M MT). Private, founded 1993.
