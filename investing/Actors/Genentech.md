#actor #biotech #pharma #healthcare #acquired

**Genentech** is the pioneering biotechnology company, founded in 1976 by Herbert Boyer and venture capitalist Robert Swanson, that commercialized recombinant DNA technology and effectively created the biotech industry. It has been a wholly-owned member of the [[Roche]] Group since 2009 and no longer trades independently.
