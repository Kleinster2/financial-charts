## Business lines

Hartree operates across energy and commodity markets:

**Trading:**
- Oil and refined products
- Natural gas and LNG
- Power and electricity
- Base metals

**ESG & renewables ventures:**
- **Vertree** — carbon markets
- **AMPYR** — renewable energy
- **AMPYR Solar Europe** — solar development
- **Hartree Solutions** — sustainable energy consulting

The firm has positioned itself at the forefront of energy transition investments.

## [[Project Vault]] role

Selected as one of three **procurement managers** for the $12B strategic critical minerals stockpile alongside [[Traxys North America]] and [[Mercuria Energy Group]].

Responsibilities include:
- Sourcing raw materials globally
- Managing physical commodity flows
- Building strategic inventory
