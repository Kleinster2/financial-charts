#actor #restaurateur #hospitality #nyc

**John McDonald** — New York City restaurateur and entrepreneur, founder and CEO of [[Mercer Street Hospitality]]. Columbia University grad who opened MercBar in SoHo in 1993 instead of going to Wall Street. Built a portfolio of 10+ restaurant/bar concepts across NYC, Chicago, and Miami. Earned a Michelin star for Lever House Restaurant (2005). Also founded CITY Magazine (three National Magazine Awards), co-founded [[Tasting Table]] (2008), and co-founded EBOOST (performance supplements, 2007).
