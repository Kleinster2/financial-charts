# Haruhiko Kuroda

31st Governor of the [[BOJ]] (Mar 2013 - Apr 2023), appointed by PM [[Shinzo Abe]] as the architect of [[Abenomics]]' monetary pillar. Launched Quantitative and Qualitative Monetary Easing (QQE) in April 2013, introduced negative interest rates (-0.1%) in January 2016, and [[yield curve control]] in September 2016. Under his decade-long tenure, the BOJ's balance sheet expanded from ~30% of GDP to over 130%, and JGB holdings rose from 11.5% to over 53% of the outstanding market. Previously served as president of the Asian Development Bank (2005-2013) and as a Ministry of Finance official.
