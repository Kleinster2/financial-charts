# SIL

SIL (NYSE Arca: SIL) is the Global X Silver Miners ETF — a basket of global silver-mining companies, with top weights in the large primary silver miners ([[Pan American Silver|PAAS]], [[Wheaton Precious Metals|WPM]], and the others). The benchmark for the [[Silver equity beta]] cohort. Launched 2010.

## Use case

The silver-miner-ETF benchmark for the [[Silver equity beta#Cluster validation|silver cluster validation]]. SIL holds the exact cohort names, so the silver miners cannot separate from it — the ~zero +0.008 intra-advantage vs SIL/SLV is the ETF-replicable signature. And gold miners ([[GDX]]) sit inside the same cluster (silver miners are −0.041 vs GDX), so SIL, SLV, and GDX are one precious-metals-mining complex. See [[Silver equity beta]].
