### Apr 2026: intent-following vehicle agents

At the Apr 2026 Beijing Auto Show, [[Reuters]] highlighted Xpeng as an early example of the [[Automotive AI]] shift from map-following ADAS toward intent-following vehicle agents. Xpeng said its updated AI model lets drivers give goal-style commands — for example, "park near the entrance to the shopping center" — instead of choosing a precise spot on a map. The vehicle can use cameras to navigate even without mapping or coordinates.

That is a meaningful change in framing. Xpeng's autonomy story is not just about higher L2/L3 automation; it is about turning the car into an agent that interprets user intent and acts in the physical world. That links Xpeng to both [[Autonomous vehicles]] and [[Embodied AI]].
