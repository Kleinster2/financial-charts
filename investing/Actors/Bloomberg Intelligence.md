## Stablecoin forecast (May 2026)

[[Bloomberg Intelligence]] projects that total stablecoin transfer volume will reach over $56T by 2030 — cited on Bloomberg TV's May 2026 piece on cross-border stablecoin payments. The forecast is roughly a 150x scale-up from the [[Artemis Analytics]] / [[McKinsey]] / [[Matt Higginson]]-filtered real payments baseline of ~$390B in 2025 (see [[Stablecoins]]). The trajectory assumes stablecoin rail integration into mainstream payment flows (creator economy, B2B treasury, retail remittance) plus sustained cost compression versus correspondent banking.

The figure is forward-looking, sensitive to GENIUS Act activation pacing, regulatory clarity in Asia (where ~60% of stablecoin payments originate per Artemis), and the speed at which fintech operators like [[Remitly]] and [[Coins.ph]] shift transaction-engine routing toward stablecoin sandwiches. See [[Stablecoins]] for measurement framework and [[GENIUS Act]] for the US regulatory inflection.
