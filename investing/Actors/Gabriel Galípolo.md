**Gabriel Galípolo** — President of [[Banco Central do Brasil]] since January 2025. Lula's appointee who turned hawkish — hiked [[Selic]] from 10.75% to 15.00% (+450bp), vindicating the 2021 CB independence law. Former executive secretary of the Finance Ministry and CEO of Banco Fator.
