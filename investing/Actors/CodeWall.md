# CodeWall

**CodeWall** — one-person cybersecurity firm founded by Paul Price. Focuses on probing companies that have published ethical hacker guidelines. Used its own AI agent to breach [[McKinsey]]'s Lilli AI platform in February 2026, gaining full read/write access to the production database within 2 hours. The AI agent autonomously selected McKinsey as a target — what CodeWall frames as the future of AI-era threat landscapes.
