# China Academy of Space Technology

State-run Chinese institution for satellite design and space-systems engineering. Operates under the [[China Aerospace Science and Technology Corporation]] (CASC) umbrella. Acts as a talent pipeline for both state and commercial Chinese space programmes — [[Emposat]] founder [[Richard Zhao]] spent 15 years at CAST before starting the commercial ground-services firm that now serves the [[IRGC]] through [[TEE-01B]]. CAST's alumni network is one mechanism by which the [[Military-civil fusion|civil-military fusion]] doctrine propagates into nominally commercial entities like [[Earth Eye Co]] and [[Emposat]] (see [[Chinese dual-use commercial satellite exports]]).
