# Griffon Aerospace

**Griffon Aerospace** — Madison, Alabama-based drone manufacturer producing the MQM-172 Arrowhead, a Shahed-derivative one-way attack drone competing with [[SpektreWorks]]' [[LUCAS]]. Larger, longer-range than LUCAS (1,500 nm range, 100 lb payload vs. LUCAS at 444 nm / 40 lb). Also produces target drones for military training. Marine Corps testing the Arrowhead. Won FTUAS design phase contract (2023) that [[AeroVironment]] lost. Part of the broader Drone Dominance Program ecosystem.
