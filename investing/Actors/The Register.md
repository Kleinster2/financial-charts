### Rendered-output prompt injection (May 2026)

The May 29 2026 Permiso / [[ChatGPT]] prompt-injection story is useful because it moves the prompt-injection problem from hidden instructions inside retrieved content to rendered assistant output. The reported exploit chain used attacker-controlled web content to make assistant output look like a normal summary plus a fake security alert, including a QR-code pivot in one variant.
