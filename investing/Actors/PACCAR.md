# PACCAR

**Third-largest heavy truck manufacturer globally** — parent company of Kenworth and Peterbilt truck brands. Ticker PCAR. Develops proprietary MX-13 diesel engine (45%+ uptake in its trucks, reducing dependence on [[Cummins]]). Historically [[Cummins]]' single largest customer for heavy-duty engines. Also manufactures DAF trucks in Europe.
