#actor #person #executive #edtech

**Josh Coates** — early CEO of [[Instructure]] (2010-2018) and chairman of the board through 2020. Previously founder of Mozy (online backup service, sold to EMC for $76M in 2007). Volunteer adjunct professor at BYU in 2008, where he taught the software-business class in which [[Brian Whitmer]] and [[Devlin Daley]] built the Canvas prototype. Provided the initial angel investment that launched the company.
