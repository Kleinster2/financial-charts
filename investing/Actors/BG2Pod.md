BG2Pod is a bi-weekly tech, markets, and investing podcast hosted by [[Bill Gurley]] ([[Benchmark]]) and Brad Gerstner ([[Altimeter Capital]]), combining two of Silicon Valley's most connected investors into arguably the highest-influence-per-listener media property in tech.

## Synopsis

Launched in ~2024, BG2Pod occupies a unique niche in tech media: rather than chasing audience scale (its ~73K YouTube subscribers are modest by podcast standards), it operates as a signal channel for the tech investment elite. The guest roster — [[Jensen Huang]], [[Satya Nadella]], [[Sam Altman]], [[Dara Khosrowshahi]], [[Brian Chesky]] — reads like a who's who of the most consequential technology CEOs, and the hosts' status as active investors (Gurley at [[Benchmark]], Gerstner at [[Altimeter Capital]]) means conversations carry implicit endorsement weight that pure journalist-hosted shows cannot match. When Gurley or Gerstner express a view on AI infrastructure spending, [[NVIDIA]]'s competitive position, or regulatory risk, the audience includes LPs, co-investors, and other GPs who allocate capital based on these signals.

The podcast's investment relevance is as a primary source for executive sentiment and strategic framing. CEO interviews on BG2Pod tend to be more candid than CNBC appearances or earnings calls because the hosts are peers, not journalists — the dynamic is closer to a board meeting than an interview. Gerstner's background adds a public-markets lens (Altimeter runs both venture and public equity strategies), while Gurley brings the pure venture perspective honed over decades at Benchmark (early investor in [[Uber]], [[Zillow]], [[GrubHub]]). The show launched episodes covering AI chip economics, [[Cerebras]] IPO dynamics, and [[Anthropic]]'s competitive positioning that moved market narratives. For the vault, BG2Pod episodes are primary source material — treat as executive interview transcripts rather than media coverage.
