## What it tracks

A MarketVector index of ultra-short (7–60 day) Brazilian Treasury bills — essentially a cash / overnight-rate proxy, like a money-market fund. The instrument a Carteira Aberta guest named when describing his shift to B3 fixed-income ETFs as a cash sleeve.

## Use case

A cash-management alternative to [[LFTS11]] (Tesouro Selic) — both are the floating/ultra-short, near-zero-duration end of the B3 fixed-income ETF family, used for liquidity rather than duration. With essentially no price history yet, [[LFTS11]] is the usable proxy for this exposure.
