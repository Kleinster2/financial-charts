## Why Ascenty matters

LatAm's largest connectivity and DC services provider:

- **Scale**: 38 units across Brazil, Mexico, Chile, Colombia
- **Integrated**: Own 4,000+ km fiber network connecting DCs and submarine cables
- **Digital Realty ecosystem**: Access to ServiceFabric platform, 500+ global DCs
- **Frost & Sullivan**: 2024 Latin American Company of the Year
