Bob Chapek — former CEO of [[Disney]] (Feb 2020–Nov 2022). Previously ran Disney Parks (Experiences division). Lasted less than three years before board replaced him with returning [[Bob Iger]]. Tenure marked by COVID, Florida political disputes, and shareholder discontent.
