# CARZ

CARZ (Nasdaq: CARZ) is the First Trust S-Network Future Vehicles & Technology ETF — a global basket spanning automakers, auto suppliers, EV makers, and autonomous/mobility-technology names. The closest listed proxy for "the auto industry," though it is diversified enough across OEMs, suppliers, and tech that its daily returns run close to broad discretionary/market beta.
