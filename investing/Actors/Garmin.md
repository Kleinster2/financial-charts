## Investment case

Bull:
- Diversified across 5 segments (not just wearables)
- Premium brand with pricing power
- Vertical integration (own manufacturing)
- Aviation/marine = high barriers to entry
- Fitness segment accelerating

Bear:
- Auto OEM segment declining, producing operating losses
- [[Apple]] Watch competition in fitness
- Consumer discretionary exposure in downturn
- High valuation after +16% earnings pop
