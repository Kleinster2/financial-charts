# Soulbrain

South Korean electronic materials company specializing in semiconductor chemicals, including electronic-grade [[Hydrofluoric Acid]], etchants, CMP slurries, and CVD/ALD precursors. Listed on KOSDAQ (357780.KQ). Became [[South Korea]]'s domestic HF champion after [[Japan]]'s July 2019 export restrictions on semiconductor materials — doubled HF production capacity and now supplies approximately two-thirds of Korean chipmakers' hydrofluoric acid needs.
