# Strategas Securities

Independent investment research and strategy firm covering macro, policy, sector rotation, and ETFs. Founded by Jason DeSena Trennert. Part of [[Baird]] since 2018. Known for its ETF strategy desk led by [[Todd Sohn]] and for policy research tying Washington moves to market impact.
