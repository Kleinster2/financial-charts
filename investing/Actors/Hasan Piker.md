Turkish-American political commentator and Twitch streamer (b. 1991). Nephew of Cenk Uygur (The Young Turks). Largest political streamer on Twitch — 24K avg concurrent viewers, 365M+ total hours watched, 313K peak concurrent. 2024 election night coverage drew **7.5M total viewers**. Launched Himbo Fitness (activewear). Net worth ~$4M.

Political streaming viewership up 56% YoY (Tubefilter, May 2025). Hasan directly competes with MSNBC in the progressive demo, from a bedroom, at zero production cost.
