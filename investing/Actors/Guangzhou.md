# Guangzhou

Capital of Guangdong province, [[China]], with a metro population of ~18.7M. One of China's four "Tier 1" cities alongside [[Beijing]], [[Shanghai]], and [[Shenzhen]]. Guangzhou is the historical heart of Chinese trade — the Canton Fair (China Import and Export Fair), held biannually since 1957, remains the country's largest trade exhibition. The city's GDP of ~$430B (2024) is driven by manufacturing, auto production, trade, and an expanding biotech sector.

Guangzhou is a major hub for China's EV industry, hosting [[BYD]] and GAC Group operations. The city's Nansha district has become a focus for autonomous vehicle testing and smart city infrastructure. Guangzhou also serves as the southern anchor of the Greater Bay Area economic zone, which links it with [[Shenzhen]], [[Hong Kong]], and [[Macau]] in a combined metro economy rivaling Tokyo or New York.
