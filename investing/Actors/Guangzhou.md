**Guangzhou** — capital of Guangdong province, [[China]]. Population ~18.7M. One of China's top-tier cities alongside [[Beijing]], [[Shanghai]], and [[Shenzhen]]. Major manufacturing, trade, and tech hub. Home to the Canton Fair, China's largest trade exhibition.
