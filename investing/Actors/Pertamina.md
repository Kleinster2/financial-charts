**Pertamina** — [[Indonesia]]'s state-owned oil and gas company, the mandated buyer and blender for the national biodiesel program. Operates refineries at Plaju (South Sumatra, 119,240 kl/month) and Kasim (West Papua, 15,898 kl/month) prepared for B40 [[Biodiesel (FAME)|biodiesel]] production.
