SGMC Capital is a Singapore-based investment firm focused on global macro and multi-strategy investing. [[Mohit Mirpuri]] is a partner at the firm. SGMC has been vocal on geopolitical hedging strategies, particularly around the 2026 Iran conflict and its implications for energy markets and portfolio construction. The firm's Singapore base reflects the city-state's growing role as an Asian wealth management and hedge fund hub, with $4.7T+ in assets under management flowing through the jurisdiction.
