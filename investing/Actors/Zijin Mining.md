#actor #mining #gold #copper #china #public

**Zijin Mining** — China's largest gold producer and one of the world's top copper miners. Aggressive global acquirer with operations across 9+ countries. Partly state-owned.

![[zijin-price-chart.png]]
*10x since 2020 (~¥4 → ¥40). Accelerated 2024-25 on record gold prices and M&A activity.*

![[zijin-fundamentals.png]]
*Revenue 4x from ¥75B to ¥327B quarterly (2015-2025). Net income exploded from ¥2B to ¥45B quarterly — 30% CAGR.*

See [[Zijin-Allied Gold acquisition]] for full details.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- China's mining champion with global scale
- Record gold prices boosting margins
- Kamoa-Kakula is world-class copper asset
- Aggressive M&A building reserve base
- Lithium exposure via Neo Lithium

**Bear:**
- US sanctions limiting market access
- State ownership = geopolitical risk
- Environmental track record
- Premium valuations on HK/Shanghai
