**DRC** — Central African country. World's largest cobalt producer and a rapidly growing copper producer. Key mining jurisdiction for [[Ivanhoe Mines]] (Kamoa-Kakula), [[Glencore]] (Katanga/KCC, Mutanda), and [[Barrick Gold]] (Kibali).
