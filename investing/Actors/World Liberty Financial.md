**World Liberty Financial** is a DeFi protocol and crypto venture associated with the [[Donald Trump]] family. Co-founded by [[Zach Witkoff]] and Trump associates. Launched in 2024 as a decentralized finance platform with governance token sales, positioning the Trump brand within the digital asset ecosystem. [[Justin Sun]] is one of the largest external investors in WLFI and is in a publicly-acknowledged dispute with the WLF camp as of May 2026 — a tension whose investing-vault relevance is that Sun is simultaneously the operating principal on [[Tron]] (whose Nasdaq listing was arranged by [[Dominari Securities]], in which the Trump brothers each own ~6.28%). Two arms of the Trump-family balance sheet are transacting on opposite sides of a single counterparty's fight.
