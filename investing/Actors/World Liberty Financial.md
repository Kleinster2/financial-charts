**World Liberty Financial** is a DeFi protocol and crypto venture associated with the [[Donald Trump]] family. Co-founded by [[Zach Witkoff]] and Trump associates. Launched in 2024 as a decentralized finance platform with governance token sales, positioning the Trump brand within the digital asset ecosystem.
