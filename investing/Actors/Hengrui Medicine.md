Hengrui Medicine (Jiangsu Hengrui Pharmaceuticals Co., Ltd.) is [[China]]'s largest domestic pharmaceutical company by revenue and a flagship of the country's transition from generic-heavy manufacturing to innovative drug R&D. Listed in [[Shanghai Stock Exchange|Shanghai]] under 600276.SS. [[Bernstein]] highlighted Hengrui as a top pick in [[China biotech R&D leadership]] as of Apr 2026 — the cleanest example of a legacy generics player successfully phasing out commodity revenue and replenishing the topline with innovative drugs.

## Why Hengrui matters

Hengrui's structural story is the opposite of newer biotech entrants like [[Innovent Biologics]]. Innovent is "biotech transitioning to commercial pharma." Hengrui is "commercial pharma transitioning to innovative pharma." The former takes lab science to market; the latter takes a generic-heavy income statement and rebuilds it around higher-margin innovative molecules. Both pathways feed [[China biotech R&D leadership]], but their risk profiles are different: Hengrui has the cushion of a large existing revenue base while it executes the transition.

## Strategic transition

| Phase | Position |
|-------|----------|
| Pre-2018 | Domestic generics + branded-domestic anchor; minimal innovative pipeline |
| 2019-2023 | Aggressive R&D ramp; first innovative molecules approved domestically |
| 2024-2026 | Outlicensing global rights for multiple programs; portfolio rotation away from generics-pricing pressure |

The transition matters because Hengrui's prior revenue base was hit hard by China's [[Volume-Based Procurement]] (VBP) program, which compresses generic prices through centralized government tendering. Hengrui's solution — pivot the portfolio to innovative drugs whose pricing is set outside the VBP regime — is the playbook every other legacy Chinese pharma will need to execute.
