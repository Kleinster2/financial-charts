# IIT Bombay

Premier engineering and technology university in [[India]], located in Mumbai. One of the original five Indian Institutes of Technology established in the 1950s (founded 1958 with UNESCO assistance and Soviet cooperation). Consistently ranked among the top engineering schools in Asia, with particular strength in computer science, electrical engineering, and materials science.

IIT Bombay produces a disproportionate share of the tech and finance talent in [[India]]'s economy and in Silicon Valley. Its alumni network includes founders, CTOs, and senior executives across [[Google]], [[Microsoft]], [[NVIDIA]], and major hedge funds. The institute's computer science program is among the most selective in the world, admitting roughly 1 in 200 applicants through the JEE Advanced exam.
