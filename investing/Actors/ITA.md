# ITA

ITA (NYSE Arca: ITA) is the iShares U.S. Aerospace & Defense ETF — a basket of US aerospace and defense stocks spanning the defense primes, commercial airframers/engine makers, and aerospace components/aftermarket suppliers. The standard A&D sector benchmark.

## Use case

The replicability benchmark for aerospace/defense cohorts. In the [[Aerospace aftermarket#Cluster validation|aerospace-aftermarket cluster validation]], the components/aftermarket names ([[Heico|HEI]]/[[Howmet Aerospace|HWM]]/[[Curtiss-Wright|CW]]) cluster INSIDE ITA with a NEGATIVE intra-advantage (−0.063) — they track ITA more than each other, so they are ITA/industrials beta, not a distinct factor (though distinct from the defense primes, +0.184). [[TransDigm|TDG]] sits apart as an idiosyncratic singleton. The [[Defense primes]] cohort is the separable A&D pole; the aftermarket names resolve to ITA.
