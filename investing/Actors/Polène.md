# Polène

**French accessible luxury handbag brand** — minimalist design, DTC model. Founded 2016 by Mothay siblings. €142M revenue (2023, +125% YoY), 80% international. [[L Catterton]] took minority stake in 2024. Rapid flagship expansion: Paris, London, Beijing, Milan. Family retains majority control.

## Success factors

- **Social media:** Viral on Instagram, TikTok

## Investment case

*Note: Polène is private.*

**Bull:**
- Triple-digit growth sustained
- 80% international — global demand
- L Catterton/LVMH expertise
- Family control = long-term vision
- Accessible luxury sweet spot
- €26M net profit on €142M revenue (18% margin)

**Bear:**
- Can growth continue at scale?
- Physical retail expansion = capital intensive
- Luxury slowdown could hit aspirational buyers first
- Competition from established brands moving down-market
