## Relevance

AIXBT is the clearest case study of the 2024-2025 AI-agent token bubble and its deflation. A single-function agent (crypto market commentary) reached hundreds of millions in FDV on the thesis that tokenized agents would commoditize alpha generation. Most of that value evaporated as the agent's signals failed to deliver sustained alpha. Useful case study for the [[Decentralized AI landscape]] in explaining why agent-layer tokens are speculation-heavy and emission-dependent rather than revenue-backed.
