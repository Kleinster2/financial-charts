# Fireblocks

Digital asset custody and tokenization infrastructure company. Provides secure transfer, storage, and issuance of digital assets for institutions. Used by [[Mercado Bitcoin]], [[Bitso]], and [[Foxbit]] as custody/tokenization layer for [[BRL1]] stablecoin.
