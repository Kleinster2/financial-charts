# Pan American Silver

Pan American Silver (NYSE/TSX: PAAS) is the largest primary silver miner by output, operating silver and gold mines across the Americas (Mexico, Peru, Bolivia, Canada, Argentina), expanded via the 2023 Yamana asset acquisition. The bellwether of the [[Silver equity beta]] cohort.
