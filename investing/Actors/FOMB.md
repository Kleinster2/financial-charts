**FOMB** (Financial Oversight and Management Board) — Federal body created by [[PROMESA]] (2016) to oversee [[Puerto Rico]]'s fiscal recovery and debt restructuring. Has resolved 4 of 5 major bankruptcies; [[PREPA]] remains outstanding.
