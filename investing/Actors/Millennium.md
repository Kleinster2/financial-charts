## Pod structure

Pioneer of "pod shop" model:

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Pods | 300+ independent teams |
| Risk limits | Strict drawdown triggers |
| Capital allocation | Dynamic based on performance |
| Pass-through | Expenses charged to LPs |

Each pod operates semi-autonomously with own P&L.
