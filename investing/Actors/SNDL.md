# SNDL

A Canadian cannabis and liquor company, formerly Sundial Growers, that used its meme-stock-era equity raises to pivot from a struggling pure-play cultivator into the largest private-sector liquor and cannabis retailer in Canada (via the Alcanna/Nova Cannabis and Wine and Beyond banners), backed by a large net-cash position. The retail and liquor mix makes it structurally different from the pure LPs, yet it still co-moves with the [[Cannabis]] cohort on [[Cannabis rescheduling|US-reform]] sentiment. Federally legal in Canada and NASDAQ-listed. A member of the Canadian LP bloc; collapses into [[MSOS]]/[[MJ]].
