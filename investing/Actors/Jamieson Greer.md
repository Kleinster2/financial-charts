# Jamieson Greer

**Jamieson Greer** — US Trade Representative (cabinet rank, Senate-confirmed April 2025), the official running the post-[[SCOTUS IEEPA ruling|SCOTUS]] [[Section 301]] pivot that replaced struck-down IEEPA tariffs. A Hogan Lovells international trade partner and King & Spalding alum who served as chief of staff to USTR Robert Lighthizer during the Trump 1.0 [[China]] [[Section 301]] investigation — i.e. the operational through-line from the 2018 China tariffs to the 2026 multi-economy Section 301 expansion and the novel use of trade law to challenge allies' domestic drug-pricing regimes ([[US Section 301 investigation of Germany drug pricing (2026)|Germany probe]], 2026).
