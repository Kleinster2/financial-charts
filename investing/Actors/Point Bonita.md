# Point Bonita

Investment managed through [[Jefferies]]' asset management subsidiary. Subject of a $30M pre-tax markdown in Jefferies' Q4 2025 results. In March 2026, [[Western Alliance Bancorp]] sued Jefferies for $126M related to the Point Bonita investment, triggering a 13.5% single-day stock decline in JEF on March 6, 2026.
