Chris Van Hollen — US Senator (D-Maryland) since 2017. Serves on the Senate Appropriations, Banking, and Foreign Relations Committees. Has been one of the most active senators on Trump administration ethics and conflict-of-interest oversight, co-signing the February 26, 2026 letter to Commerce Secretary [[Howard Lutnick]] alleging conflict of interest in the [[USA Rare Earth]] deal. Prior to the Senate, served in the House of Representatives (2003–2017) including as Ranking Member of the House Budget Committee.

## Key 2026 actions

- February 26, 2026 — Co-signed letter (with [[Elizabeth Warren]] and [[Ron Wyden]]) alleging that the [[Commerce Department]] "created demand" for [[Cantor Fitzgerald]]'s services by conditioning the USAR LOI on matched private capital. See [[Lutnick family conflicts of interest]].
