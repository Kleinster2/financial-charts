**Heraeus Precious Metals** — German precious metals refiner and technology company. One of world's largest bullion refiners. Part of Heraeus Group (family-owned since 1660).

## Business

### Precious metals services

| Service | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Refining | Gold, silver, platinum group metals |
| Trading | Physical bullion, coins, bars |
| Recycling | Industrial and consumer precious metals |
| Products | Investment bars, industrial materials |

### Market position

- One of five LBMA-approved gold refiners in Germany
- Refines ~400 tonnes of gold annually
- Supplies mints, jewelers, industrial users
