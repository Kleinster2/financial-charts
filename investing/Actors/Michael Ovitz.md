#actor #individual #media #usa

**Michael Ovitz** — Co-founder of Creative Artists Agency (CAA). Former president of [[Walt Disney]]. Board member of Scour Inc. ([[Travis Kalanick]]'s first company, P2P file sharing, bankrupted 2000).

---

## Role in Pershing Square UMG bid

Hosted the pivotal dinner at his Beverly Hills home in late March 2026 where [[Bill Ackman]] first sounded out [[Universal Music Group|UMG]] CEO [[Lucian Grainge]] about the takeover proposal. The meeting was kept high level to avoid triggering disclosure requirements (FT, Apr 9 2026).

After the dinner, Ovitz told Grainge he would act as a "bridge" between UMG's management and Ackman. Under the proposal, Ovitz would become chairman of the new NYSE-listed entity.
