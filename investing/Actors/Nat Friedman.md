# Nat Friedman

#actor #individual #investor #ai #executive

## Investment thesis

**AI-first:** Bet early and heavily on generative AI wave. NFDG positioned as the AI-native fund — not a generalist fund that happens to do AI.

**Founder access:** GitHub/YC networks provide deal flow that generalist funds can't match.
