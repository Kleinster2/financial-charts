# XRP Ledger

Decentralized Layer 1 blockchain created by [[Ripple]]. Known for scalability, low fees (~$0.0002/tx), and near-instant settlement (~3-5 seconds). Used by [[Braza Group]] for [[BBRL]] stablecoin — chose XRPL for institutional-grade throughput.
