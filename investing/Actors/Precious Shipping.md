# Precious Shipping

**Precious Shipping** (SET: PSL) is a Thai dry bulk shipping company and one of Southeast Asia's largest dry bulk operators. CEO Khalid Hashim is a prominent industry voice on freight markets, geopolitical disruption, and shipping insurance risk.

## Synopsis

Precious Shipping operates a fleet of supramax and ultramax dry bulk carriers, transporting commodities like grain, coal, steel, and minerals across global trade routes. The company is listed on the Stock Exchange of Thailand and has been publicly traded since 1993. While small by global standards compared to [[Star Bulk Carriers]] or [[Golden Ocean]], PSL is significant as one of the few publicly traded pure-play dry bulk operators in Southeast Asia, giving it disproportionate visibility as a regional shipping bellwether.

CEO Khalid Hashim's public commentary makes PSL a recurring vault reference. Hashim is one of the shipping industry's most quoted executives, regularly appearing in trade press on topics ranging from freight rate dynamics to geopolitical risk pricing. His public skepticism of Trump's March 2026 Gulf shipping insurance guarantee — "Nothing is sure and we need immediate clarity" — reflects the broader industry view that government promises don't translate into underwritable risk without specific policy mechanics. PSL's exposure to [[Strait of Hormuz]] transit routes makes Hashim's perspective on [[Iran conflict insurance impact|Iran conflict insurance dynamics]] operationally informed rather than theoretical.
