# Precious Shipping

Thai dry bulk shipping company (SET: PSL). One of Southeast Asia's largest dry bulk operators. CEO Khalid Hashim is a prominent industry voice, frequently quoted on freight markets and geopolitical disruption. Publicly skeptical of Trump's March 2026 Gulf shipping insurance guarantee — "Nothing is sure and we need immediate clarity."
