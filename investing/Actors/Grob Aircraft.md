**Grob Aircraft** — Southern German aircraft manufacturer acquired by [[Helsing]] as the manufacturing base for the CA-1 Europa autonomous combat aircraft. The acquisition closed in 2025 and gives Helsing a real airframe-production capability to pair with its Centaur autonomy stack.

## Why Grob matters

The acquisition converted [[Helsing]] from a software-first defense AI company into a software-plus-hardware vendor. For [[Bundeswehr]] procurement, hardware production in Germany matters — much of the political support for the Helsing/[[Stark]] contracts is grounded in domestic industrial-base arguments.
