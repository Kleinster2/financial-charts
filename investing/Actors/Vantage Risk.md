## Acquisition by Howard Hughes Holdings

[[Bill Ackman]]'s [[Howard Hughes Holdings]] acquired Vantage Risk for $2.1B, financed with cash and up to $1B in HHH stock from [[Pershing Square]]. Pershing Square will manage Vantage's investment portfolio — making this an [[Insurance float]]-funded investment vehicle in the [[Berkshire Hathaway]] mold.

Vantage's P&C specialty lines (political violence, cyber, litigation) carry shorter-duration float than the life/annuity books favored by [[Apollo]]/[[Athene]] and [[KKR]]/[[Global Atlantic]], but align with Berkshire's original model (GEICO, National Indemnity).

See [[Howard Hughes Vantage Risk acquisition]] for full deal details.
