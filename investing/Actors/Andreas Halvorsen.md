## AI stance

Halvorsen has taken a publicly cautious view on AI as an investment theme. From a Viking investor letter:

*"We have observed that the closer you get to needing to make a judgment call, the less useful AI is."*

*"At current valuations, we believe the range of outcomes remains too wide to make a big directional bet."*

*"Will AI prove to be a powerful deflationary force that justifies record valuations? Perhaps. But the data so far shows that the cost is high, and the benefits are unpredictable, and appear small to date."*

Viking uses AI operationally (VikingGPT 2.0 chatbot) but avoids large directional bets on AI stocks. Contrasts with Tiger cub peers like [[Coatue]] ([[Philippe Laffont]]) who are heavily positioned.

*Source: Bloomberg, Viking investor letter*
