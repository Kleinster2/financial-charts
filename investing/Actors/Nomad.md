# Nomad

Nomad is a Brazilian fintech (founded 2020) that gives Brazilian residents a US-dollar financial life: an FDIC-insured US bank account, an international debit card, USD transfers, and offshore investing in US stocks, ETFs, and fixed income through its FINRA-registered broker-dealer Nomad Investment Services, Inc. (NIS). It is one of the main retail channels — alongside [[Avenue Securities]] and [[Banco Inter]]'s Global Account — through which a Brazilian buys US securities offshore, including the underlying shares behind US-listed names and their [[Brazilian Depositary Receipts|BDRs]].

## Evolution

- 2020 — Founded; the first platform to offer Brazilian residents a US bank account, anchored on USD accounts, cards, and remittance.
- May 2022 — Series B of $32M (Tiger Global, Spark Capital, Stripes, Monashees, Globo Ventures) to scale the US-account base.
- 2023–25 — Layered offshore investing on top via FINRA-registered Nomad Investment Services; a $61M growth round valued the company near R$1.8B.
- 2025 — 3.5M+ clients and ~R$7B in assets under custody abroad; ~$120M annualized revenue and first-time cash generation; launched a Wealth tier ($25k minimum) and opened a Miami office (H2).

The 2025 inflection — roughly $120M annualized revenue and first-time cash generation — shifted the story from growth-at-all-costs toward a self-funding offshore-finance platform, with the new Wealth tier moving it up-market from transactional accounts.

## Model

Nomad pioneered offering Brazilian residents a US bank account, then layered offshore investing and a wealth tier on top:

- US dollar account plus an international debit card, FDIC-insured to $250,000.
- Offshore brokerage (US stocks, ETFs, fixed income) via FINRA-registered NIS.
- USD transfers, remittance, and international purchases.
- Wealth (2025): personalized service, risk management, continuous monitoring, $25k minimum.

The recurring economics sit in the FX spread on every BRL→USD funding flow plus brokerage and, increasingly, wealth fees — the same FX-spread engine that drives [[Avenue Securities]]. As an offshore (USD, abroad) channel, Nomad is the alternative to the onshore [[Brazilian Depositary Receipts|BDR]] route, which keeps everything in reais on [[B3]].
