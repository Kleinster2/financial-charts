# European People's Party

**European People's Party** — center-right European party family and the largest group in [[EU]] politics. It is a key channel for market-relevant [[EU]] positions on trade defense, industrial policy, fiscal policy, and regulatory strategy.

## June 2026 China trade stance

Ahead of the June 18 2026 [[EU]] leaders' summit, the EPP said it would not accept [[China|Chinese]] market intervention and called for the [[European Commission]] to upgrade trade-defense tools. The statement was signed by von der Leyen, [[Friedrich Merz]], and 10 other leaders, but the summit still chose dialogue before immediate confrontation. See [[EU-China trade]].
