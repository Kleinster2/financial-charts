**Rumo** — Brazilian rail logistics company, ~30.3% owned by [[Cosan]] (20.3% direct + 10% via total return swaps). Holds concessions for agricultural export rail corridors in Mato Grosso do Sul, Parana, Santa Catarina, and Rio Grande do Sul, with access to the ports of Paranagua, Sao Francisco do Sul, and Rio Grande. Ticker: RAIL3 ([[B3]]).
