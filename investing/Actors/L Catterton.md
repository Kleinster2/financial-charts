# L Catterton

**Consumer-focused private equity giant** — largest dedicated consumer PE firm globally. $37B AUM. Formed 2016 from merger of Catterton Partners + [[LVMH]]/Groupe Arnault operations. 60% owned by partners, 40% by LVMH/Arnault family. Known investments: [[Birkenstock]], Peloton, Equinox, Restoration Hardware. 383 portfolio companies, 27 IPOs, 14 unicorns.

## LVMH relationship

Merger in 2016 combined:
- Catterton's North American and Latin American PE operations
- LVMH and Groupe Arnault's European and Asian PE + real estate operations

**Strategic value:** [[LVMH]] expertise and network for brand-building, distribution, international expansion. Not just capital — operational support from the world's largest luxury conglomerate.

## Investment thesis

Targets consumer brands with:
- Strong brand equity
- Growth potential in international markets
- Benefit from LVMH expertise (luxury positioning, distribution, operations)
- Fragmented categories ripe for consolidation

Sweet spot: premium/luxury consumer, wellness, beauty, fashion, hospitality.
