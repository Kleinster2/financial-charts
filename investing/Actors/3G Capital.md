**3G Capital** -- Brazilian-American PE firm founded by Jorge Paulo Lemann, Marcel Telles, and Carlos Alberto Sicupira. Known for aggressive cost-cutting and leveraged consumer roll-ups. Key holdings: [[AB InBev]] (world's largest brewer), [[Kraft Heinz]] (with [[Berkshire Hathaway]]). Closest strategic analog to [[JAB Holding]].
