**C6 Bank** — Brazilian digital bank. Founded 2018. **40% owned by [[JPMorgan Chase]]** (2021, ~$11B valuation). Competes with [[Nubank]], [[Inter]], [[BTG Pactual]] in Brazil's crowded fintech market. Offers full-service digital banking including international accounts (C6 Conta Global).
