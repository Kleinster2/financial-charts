## Meta infrastructure role

Meta's Jan. 2026 appointment announcement framed Powell McCormick as a global-finance and relationship executive for the next growth phase, with explicit coordination across compute and infrastructure teams. FT's Jun. 5 report puts her beside [[Susan Li]] in Meta's exploration of a possible tens-of-billions stock sale after [[Alphabet equity raise June 2026|Alphabet's AI-infrastructure equity raise]] cleared strong demand.

The strategic read is that Meta is treating AI infrastructure as both a financing problem and a political / relationship-management problem: power, land, data-center permitting, sovereign partnerships, and capital markets now sit inside the operating plan.

*Sources: existing [[Meta]] leadership / infrastructure sections for the current role; [FT](https://www.ft.com/content/e6df645d-1709-4a77-b15d-aa43a0209efd), Jun. 5 2026.*
