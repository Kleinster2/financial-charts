**Tokyo smart city** — World's largest metro (37M) with advanced transit, earthquake early warning (80 sec advance), and Olympics 2020 digital legacy. Society 5.0 national framework. 5G/6G push for 2025-2030.

## Key systems

### Transit

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Daily subway riders | 8.7M |
| Rail operators | 13+ (coordinated) |
| On-time rate | 99%+ |
| Contactless | Suica/Pasmo (since 2001) |

**Technology:** Real-time tracking, demand prediction, crowd management.

### Earthquake Early Warning

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Advance notice | Up to **80 seconds** |
| Coverage | Nationwide network |
| Integration | Transit auto-stop, elevators |
| Accuracy | Magnitude ±0.5 |

**Impact:** Trains automatically brake, elevators stop at nearest floor, factory equipment powers down.

### Flood/Storm Surge

| System | Function |
|--------|----------|
| Metropolitan Area Outer Underground Discharge Channel | World's largest flood diversion |
| AI prediction | Storm surge modeling |
| Real-time monitoring | River/drainage sensors |
