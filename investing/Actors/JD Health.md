# JD Health

The healthcare arm of [[JD.com]] — China's largest online pharmacy by revenue, plus telemedicine, chronic-disease management, and supply-chain services for drugs and medical products. Spun out and listed on the HKEX (6618) in December 2020 in what was then one of Hong Kong's largest healthcare IPOs; [[JD.com]] retains majority control.

A top-10 holding of [[KWEB]]. It pairs JD's logistics and supply-chain backbone with online-pharmacy regulation tailwinds, but trades on the [[China platform regulation|China healthcare-platform regulatory cycle]] (online-prescription rules, drug pricing) as much as on the broad internet factor.
