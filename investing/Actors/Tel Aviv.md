# Tel Aviv

[[Israel]]'s commercial and technology capital, home to the [[Tel Aviv Stock Exchange]] and the densest startup ecosystem outside Silicon Valley. The city and its surrounding Gush Dan metropolitan area (population ~4 million) account for the majority of Israel's GDP, tech employment, and venture capital activity.

Tel Aviv is Israel's most expensive real estate market, with residential prices that rank among the highest globally relative to local incomes. The city's tech sector -- centered on cybersecurity, AI, semiconductors, and defense technology -- has produced companies including [[Check Point]], [[Mobileye]], and [[Wiz]]. Real estate development is a major sector, with [[Hagag Group]] among the prominent luxury developers concentrated in Tel Aviv.
