**Sachin Katti** — Head of Industrial Compute at [[OpenAI]] (since November 2025), formerly CTO and AI Officer at [[Intel]]. Now leads all OpenAI infrastructure teams, reorganized into three groups: technical design, commercial partnerships, and facility operations.

## Context

Katti took charge of OpenAI's infrastructure organization after a strategic pivot away from building and owning data centers toward renting from hyperscalers. Infrastructure group leaders previously reported to [[Greg Brockman]]; Katti's appointment centralized the function under a single leader with hardware industry experience. His arrival preceded the departure of Keith Heyde (Director of Physical Infrastructure, advocate of in-house ownership) and the exit of three core [[Project Stargate|Stargate]] infrastructure leaders — Peter Hoeschele, Shamez Hemani, and Anuj Saharan — who left together for the same unnamed startup (Apr 10, 2026).

See [[OpenAI Infrastructure Spend]] and [[OpenAI talent exodus]] for full context.
