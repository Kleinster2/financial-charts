# Josh Feltman

Corporate restructuring partner — formerly head of [[Wachtell Lipton]]'s corporate restructuring practice, now at [[Kirkland & Ellis]] under a reported $80M / 3-year package. The single largest publicly disclosed Wachtell-to-rival defection in the FT's account of the [[Big Law transformation]] cycle, and the central data point cited in that frame: a guaranteed multi-year cash package that converts a structurally underpaid lockstep originator into immediate market-rate compensation.
