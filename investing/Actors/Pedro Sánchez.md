## June 2026 China trade stance

At the June 18 2026 [[EU]] summit, Sánchez publicly described [[China]] as a potential ally while Spain opposed immediate new trade-defense measures. That made Spain the visible member-state brake on a harder EU-China line. See [[EU-China trade]].
