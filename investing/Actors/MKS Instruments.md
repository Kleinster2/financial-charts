## Core thesis

MKS sits one layer below the largest front-end wafer-fab-equipment vendors like [[Applied Materials]] and [[Lam Research]]. It sells the vacuum, photonics, motion-control, power, gas-delivery, and surface-treatment subsystems that ride semiconductor capex, advanced packaging, and specialty industrial demand together.

That makes it a semicap exposure, but not a pure-play process-tool vendor. The stock usually trades with the semi complex, while fundamentals also reflect electronics packaging, specialty industrial end markets, and the drag or relief from the balance sheet.
