**Unit 8200** — IDF signals intelligence unit. Israel's NSA equivalent. 5,000 soldiers. Produced founders of Check Point, CyberArk, [[Wiz]], Waze, Viber, NSO Group. ~50% of Israeli $100M+ exits led by alumni.

## Culture

Operates more like a startup than military unit:
- Flat hierarchy
- Encourages initiative
- Technical meritocracy
- Network-building emphasized

"The IDF is an incubator for ideas... people acquire the skillset, knowledge, tools, and network of potential co-founders."
