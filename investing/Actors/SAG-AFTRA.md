# SAG-AFTRA

**SAG-AFTRA** — Screen Actors Guild–American Federation of Television and Radio Artists. Union representing ~160,000 actors, broadcasters, and media professionals. Led 2023 strike over AI protections. Condemned [[ByteDance]]'s [[Seedance]] 2.0 (Feb 2026) for "blatant infringement" of member likenesses.
