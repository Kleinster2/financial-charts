Ike Perlmutter — former chairman of [[Marvel Entertainment]]. Sold Marvel to [[Disney]] ($4B, 2009) and remained a Disney shareholder. One of the "forceful characters" [[Bob Iger]] negotiated with across his acquisition spree. Allied with [[Nelson Peltz]] ahead of the 2023-24 proxy fight, speaking with Peltz and [[Bob Chapek]] about a board seat (FT, Jun 2026).
