Kokomodo and [[Celleste Bio]] are both Israeli cell-cultured cocoa start-ups, each anchored to a different global chocolate processor. Kokomodo has a broader pipeline (cacao + coffee); Celleste has first-mover status on an actual finished-bar production milestone at a major chocolate manufacturer's industrial site. [[Cargill]] sits on both sides of the technology split via the Kokomodo (cell-based) and [[Voyage Foods]] (ingredient substitution) partnerships.
