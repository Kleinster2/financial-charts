# ExxonMobil

ExxonMobil (NYSE: XOM) is the largest US integrated oil & gas major — upstream production, downstream refining, and chemicals — and the bellwether of the [[Oil and gas equity beta]] cohort. Integration (refining/chemicals partly hedging crude) does not decouple its stock from the crude-price factor: it trades as oil-equity beta alongside the pure E&P names.
