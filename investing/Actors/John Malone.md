## Tax efficiency philosophy

| Tactic | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Tracking stocks** | Separate assets without tax event |
| **Spin-offs** | Tax-free separation |
| **Debt financing** | Interest deduction |
| **No dividends** | Avoid dividend tax |
| **Long holding periods** | Defer capital gains |

**Result:** Compounded wealth for decades while minimizing taxes.
