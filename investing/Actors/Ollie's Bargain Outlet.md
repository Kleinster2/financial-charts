# Ollie's Bargain Outlet

Ollie's Bargain Outlet (Nasdaq: OLLI) is an extreme-value / closeout retailer — "Good Stuff Cheap" — selling overstock and closeout merchandise across general categories from smaller-format stores. It sits in the value/off-price family but on a closeout model distinct from the apparel/home off-price majors, which makes it the loose outlier of the [[Off-price retail]] cohort.
