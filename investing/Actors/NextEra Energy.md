## Two businesses

### Florida Power & Light (FPL)
- Regulated utility
- ~12M customers
- Florida = growth market
- Rate base expansion
- Some data center demand

### NextEra Energy Resources
- Unregulated renewable developer
- Wind, solar, storage
- Sells power via PPAs
- Growth engine
- Data center PPAs growing

---

## Data center exposure

**Renewable PPAs for hyperscalers:**
- Hyperscalers want clean energy (ESG)
- NextEra sells long-term renewable PPAs
- Wind/solar farms dedicated to DCs
- Growing revenue stream

**Less direct than Dominion:**
- Not primary utility for DC Alley
- But sells renewable PPAs nationally
- Indirect AI exposure

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Largest, lowest-cost renewable developer
- Regulated utility = stability
- Hyperscaler PPA demand
- Clean energy secular trend
- Premium valuation justified

**Bear:**
- Less direct DC exposure than Dominion/Vistra
- Florida not DC hub
- Interest rate sensitivity
- Premium valuation = limited upside
- Renewable competition increasing
