# Doximity

Doximity (NYSE: DOCS) is the professional network for US physicians — the "LinkedIn for doctors" — monetized through pharma and health-system advertising, physician hiring/recruiting, and telehealth tooling. It is the profitable, high-margin outlier of the listed digital-health names: a B2B platform business rather than a cash-pay or enterprise-care provider, which is why it trades apart from the rest.
