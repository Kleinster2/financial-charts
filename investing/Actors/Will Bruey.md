## HOF relevance

Bruey is the operating counterpart to [[Delian Asparouhov]]'s [[Founders Fund]] / venture network role. In HOF's second-level map, he is the person responsible for converting Varda's orbital-manufacturing thesis into repeatable capsule missions, pharma processing, and defense/customer flight-test work.
