## June 2026 China dependency roadmap

At the June 18 2026 [[EU]] summit, Orpo asked the [[European Commission]] to prepare a roadmap of vulnerable areas and options for reducing dependencies on [[China]]. That makes Finland part of the "de-risk first" wing of the EU-China trade debate. See [[EU-China trade]] and [[Rare earth leverage]].
