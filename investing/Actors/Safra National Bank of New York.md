# Safra National Bank of New York

Privately held US bank serving high-net-worth individuals, local businesses, and international corporations, with ~$30B in client assets. Part of the [[J. Safra Group]] banking dynasty, one of the wealthiest banking families in the world. Overseen by [[Jacob Safra]], who manages the family's international banking operations.

Branches in New York, Miami, Aventura, and Palm Beach, with offices throughout Latin America -- reflecting the Safra family's deep roots in Brazilian and Lebanese banking networks. The bank focuses on private banking, commercial lending, and trade finance. Acquired Delta North Bankcorp in 2023, expanding its US footprint. SNBNY operates as a sibling entity to [[J. Safra Sarasin]] (Swiss private bank) and [[Banco Safra]] (Brazil's fifth-largest private bank).
