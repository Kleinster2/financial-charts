**Safra National Bank of New York** — privately held U.S. bank serving high-net-worth individuals, local businesses, and international corporations. ~$30B in client assets. Branches in New York, Miami, Aventura, and Palm Beach, with offices throughout Latin America. Part of the [[J. Safra Group]], overseen by [[Jacob Safra]]. Acquired Delta North Bankcorp in 2023.
