# Diptyque

**French luxury fragrance and candle house** — cult scented candles, niche perfumes. Founded 1961 on Boulevard Saint-Germain by three designers. Acquired by Manzanita Capital (Fisher family office) in 2005. 124 boutiques worldwide. Original Paris boutique still operates at founding location.
