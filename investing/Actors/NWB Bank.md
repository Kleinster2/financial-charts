**NWB Bank** (Nederlandse Waterschapsbank) -- Dutch promotional bank for water authorities and the broader public sector. Founded 1954. HQ [[The Hague]]. Owned 81% by Dutch water boards, 17% by Dutch State, 2% by provinces. Total assets EUR79B (2024). Rated Aaa by [[Moody's]]. 5th largest bank in [[Netherlands]] by assets. ECB-supervised Significant Institution. Sister institution to [[BNG Bank]]; together they dominate Dutch public-sector lending.
