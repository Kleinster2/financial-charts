## Key positions

Told FT (Dec 2025) that India's "sweet spot" of strong growth and low inflation would allow rates to remain low for a "long period" — following 1.25pp of rate cuts. The Iran conflict (Feb 28, 2026) reversed this outlook: energy import costs surging, rupee at record lows, RBI burning >$20B in FX reserves in under three weeks.
