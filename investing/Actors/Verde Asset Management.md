#actor #asset-manager #hedge-fund #macro #brazil #private

**Verde Asset Management** is the Brazilian multi-strategy manager built around Luis Stuhlberger and the Fundo Verde, one of Brazil's oldest and most storied macro hedge funds. It manages roughly R$16B across Brazilian and global multi-strategy funds and pension plans.

Stuhlberger began at Hedging-Griffo in 1981, took over the brokerage in 1985, structured its fund-management arm in 1992, and launched the Fundo Verde in 1997. The macro multimarket fund compounded more than 18,000% over its first 24 years, with 2008 (−6.4%) its first down year and the only one until a 2021 drawdown. Hedging-Griffo was absorbed into [[Credit Suisse]] (Credit Suisse Hedging-Griffo / CSHG); Stuhlberger and his team spun the strategy out as the independent partnership Verde Asset Management in 2015. In 2025 [[Vinci Partners]] — now branded Vinci Compass after its merger with Compass Group — announced the acquisition of a 50.1% controlling stake in Verde.

Verde is the reference case for two recurring portfolio lessons: the best funds are often closed to new money (Fundo Verde stayed shut for long stretches), and the most storied vehicles carry key-person risk — the track record is inseparable from Stuhlberger himself.
