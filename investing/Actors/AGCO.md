# AGCO

AGCO (NYSE: AGCO) is a global agricultural-machinery maker — Fendt, Massey Ferguson, Valtra tractors plus the Fuse precision-ag platform and GSI grain/protein equipment. A pure-play farm-equipment major (more ag-concentrated than the diversified [[Deere]]), and a member of the [[Agricultural equipment]] cohort.
