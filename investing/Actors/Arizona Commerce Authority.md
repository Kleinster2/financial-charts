# Arizona Commerce Authority

Arizona Commerce Authority is Arizona's statewide public-private economic-development organization, led by President and CEO Sandra Watson, and currently one of the key institutional explanations for why Arizona has become the cleanest US semiconductor-reshoring case. Its relevance to the vault is not as an investable company, but as a state-capacity actor: it coordinates business attraction, workforce programs, international delegations, supplier development and local execution around [[TSMC Arizona expansion]], [[Intel]], [[LG Energy Solution]] and the broader [[CHIPS Act]] buildout.

## Role in semiconductor reshoring

ACA is the coordinating layer between federal industrial policy and local capacity. Arizona Commerce Authority says the state has won more than 60 semiconductor expansions since 2020, representing more than $210B in capital investment. The agency's semiconductor page also links the state's pitch to workforce accelerators, supplier mapping, Intel apprenticeship programs, TSMC expansion, and microelectronics education networks.

That makes ACA more than a promotional office. In the [[ChinaTalk]] May 2026 Arizona abundance frame, the institution acts like a state-level quarterback: helping align companies, local governments, community colleges, utilities, workforce systems, and political messaging so federal subsidies and private capex become actual fabs, battery plants and supplier capacity.

## Investing relevance

ACA is a useful case for measuring which US geographies can actually absorb federal industrial-policy funding. If the Arizona model works, the lesson is not simply "low tax state wins." It is that permitting competence, workforce institutions, utility coordination, supplier maps, community-college capacity and a single state-level quarterback can become investable infrastructure.

The watch item is replicability. Arizona has legacy semiconductor depth, aerospace/defense culture, population growth, and a water-management politics that other states may not share. If those conditions are rare, CHIPS Act benefits concentrate geographically even when the subsidy program is national.
