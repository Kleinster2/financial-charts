| Term | Value |
|------|-------|
| Amount | $1.15B |
| Type | Term loan |
| Context | Neiman acquisition financing |
| Status | Impaired in [[Saks bankruptcy]] (Jan 2026) |

Apollo's credit arm is exposed to retail distress through this position. Bonds trading at 6-46 cents on dollar.
