**CMOC Group** (HKEX: 3993) — Chinese mining company and the world's second-largest [[Niobium]] producer (~11% global share). Acquired Anglo American's Brazilian niobium/phosphates business (Boa Vista mine) in 2016. Also a major cobalt, copper, and molybdenum producer.
