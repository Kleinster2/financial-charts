**Shantanu Narayen** — [[Adobe]] CEO from 2007 to 2026 (departing once successor named, transitioning to Chairman). Longest-serving CEO of a major software company. Led Adobe's transformation from perpetual-license to subscription model (Creative Cloud, 2013) and the $4.75B [[Marketo]] and $1.68B [[Magento]] acquisitions. Announced departure March 12, 2026 amid skepticism about Adobe's AI positioning despite [[Firefly]] investments. Joined Adobe in 1998. Born in Hyderabad, India; BS from Osmania University, MS from Bowling Green, MBA from [[UC Berkeley]].
