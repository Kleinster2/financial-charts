# LifeMD

LifeMD (Nasdaq: LFMD) is a direct-to-consumer subscription telehealth provider — the small-cap analogue of [[Hims & Hers]], spanning GLP-1 weight-management, primary care, and men's/women's health, while divesting its non-core WorkSimpli software arm. It carries a securities-fraud class action. As the closest business-model peer to Hims, it is the cohort's tightest tie to HIMS (0.40), but the pair is too loose to form a cluster.
