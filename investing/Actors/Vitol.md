# Vitol

World's largest independent energy trader, headquartered in [[Geneva]], [[Switzerland]]. Employee-owned, founded 1966 in [[Rotterdam]]. Trades ~8M bbl/day of crude and products. #7 global bunker supplier (~13M MT physical bunker supply in 2024).
