**Pure Data Centres** is a [[UK]]-based data centre operator backed by [[Oaktree Capital]]. Launched Europe's first off-grid data centre microgrid in Dublin in March 2026 — a 110MW facility running on biomethane with hydrogenated vegetable oil (HVO) backup, bypassing [[Ireland]]'s strained electricity grid entirely.
