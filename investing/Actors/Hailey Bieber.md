**Hailey Bieber** (nee Baldwin) is a model and entrepreneur who founded [[Rhode]], a skincare brand launched in June 2022 that became one of the fastest celebrity-to-brand success stories in beauty history. [[e.l.f. Beauty]] acquired Rhode in May 2025 for $1B — achieving a billion-dollar exit just three years after launch.

The Rhode trajectory is a reference case for the [[Influencer-to-brand playbook]]: Bieber leveraged her social media following (~50M+ Instagram) to launch a DTC skincare line positioned as affordable "clean" beauty (core products priced $25-35), built viral momentum through TikTok-native marketing (the "glazed donut" skin trend she popularized), and scaled rapidly through both DTC and retail distribution (Sephora). The $1B exit validates the model where a celebrity founder with authentic brand affinity can build enterprise value that exceeds what traditional beauty brands achieve in the same timeframe.

Bieber is the daughter of actor Stephen Baldwin and wife of Justin Bieber. Prior to Rhode, she modeled for major fashion brands and appeared in campaigns for [[Versace]], Saint Laurent, and Jimmy Choo. The Rhode acquisition made her one of the youngest self-made billionaire-adjacent entrepreneurs in beauty.
