# Lithia Motors

Lithia Motors (NYSE: LAD) is the largest US franchised auto-dealer consolidator by revenue, selling new and used vehicles with finance & insurance (F&I), parts, and service, plus the Driveway online platform and a captive-finance arm. The roll-up bellwether of the [[Franchised auto dealers]] cohort.
