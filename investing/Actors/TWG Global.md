TWG Global — Mark Walter's multinational conglomerate. Co-chaired by Walter and Thomas Tull (Legendary Entertainment founder). Controls [[Guggenheim Partners]] ($345B+ AUM), Group 1001/[[Gainbridge]] ($76.4B AUM), and a sports empire spanning [[Cadillac]] F1, [[Los Angeles Dodgers]], [[Los Angeles Lakers]], and [[Chelsea FC]].
