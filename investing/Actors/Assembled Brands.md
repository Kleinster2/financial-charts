# Assembled Brands

Holding company and growth capital provider for fashion/lifestyle brands. Founded by Adam Pritzker.

## Business

- **Founded:** 2013 by Adam Pritzker
- **Headquarters:** New York
- **Model:** Brand building + financing solutions for consumer companies
- **Funding:** $100M from Oaktree Capital (Series C)

## Financing Business

Provides working capital tailored to consumer brands:
- Inventory-only financing for e-commerce
- AR + inventory-backed credit lines
- Growth capital for scaling brands

**Sectors:** Fashion/apparel, beauty/wellness, food/beverage, home.

Alternative to traditional VC — doesn't require equity dilution for inventory financing.

## Adam Pritzker

- Member of Pritzker family (Hyatt Hotels)
- Son of John Pritzker, grandson of Jay Pritzker
- Previously co-founded General Assembly (sold to Adecco for $400M, 2018)

## Thesis

VC financing mechanism is broken for consumer brands — inventory-heavy businesses need different capital structures than software. Assembled Brands provides infrastructure for emerging brands to scale.
