#actor #company #healthcare #hospitals #insurance #brazil #public

**Rede D'Or** (Rede D'Or São Luiz) is Brazil's largest private hospital network — more than 70 hospitals plus oncology and a large diagnostics footprint — and, since the 2022 acquisition of [[SulAmérica]], a vertically integrated hospitals-plus-health-insurance group. It IPO'd in December 2020 and trades on B3 as `RDOR3`.

The SulAmérica merger was the strategic pivot: owning both the care provider and the insurer aligns incentives across the chain and gives Rede D'Or scale in a consolidating private-health market. The model is capital-intensive (hospital build-out and M&A) but defensive in demand.
