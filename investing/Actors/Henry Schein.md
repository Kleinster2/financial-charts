# Henry Schein

Henry Schein (Nasdaq: HSIC) is a distributor of dental and medical-surgical products, equipment, and technology/value-added services to office-based dental and physician practices — a different end-market from the prescription-drug wholesalers. Used as the adjacent healthcare-distribution control in the [[Drug distributors]] cluster validation.
