## LSEG business

London Stock Exchange Group is more than an exchange:

| Segment | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Capital Markets | LSE, Turquoise, Borsa Italiana |
| Post Trade | LCH clearing |
| Data & Analytics | Refinitiv (acquired 2021) |
| FTSE Russell | Index provider |

Refinitiv acquisition ($27B) transformed LSEG into data company.

---

## Global role

- Primary European listing venue
- Gateway for emerging market companies to access Western capital
- Strong in resources, financials, consumer goods
- Dual listings with [[NYSE]], [[HKEX]]
