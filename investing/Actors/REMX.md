# REMX

REMX (NYSE Arca: REMX) is the VanEck Rare Earth and Strategic Metals ETF — a basket of global companies engaged in producing, refining, and recycling rare-earth and strategic metals. Holdings span the Western rare-earth producers ([[MP Materials|MP]], [[Lynas Rare Earths|Lynas]]), Chinese rare-earth majors (China Northern Rare Earth, JL MAG, etc.), and adjacent strategic-metal and lithium names — so it is a broad strategic-metals proxy rather than a pure rare-earth one. Launched 2010.
