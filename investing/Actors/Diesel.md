# Diesel

**Italian denim and fashion brand** — founded by Renzo Rosso (1978), flagship of [[OTB]] group. Revenue ~€1B (largest OTB brand). Glenn Martens revitalized brand since 2020, shifting customer base to 52% Gen Z.

## Glenn Martens era (2020–)

**The turnaround:** Rosso hired Belgian designer Glenn Martens to revitalize a stale brand.

**Results:**
- 13% revenue growth (2023)
- +3.2% growth (2024) despite market downturn
- Customer base shifted: **52% Gen Z**
- Viral moments: distressed denim, oversized silhouettes

**Approach:** Kept Diesel's rebellious DNA but made it contemporary. Denim innovation, sustainability focus, inclusive casting.

**Dual role:** Martens also leads [[Maison Margiela]] as of 2025 (succeeded John Galliano).

---

## Brand positioning

**Heritage:** Premium denim, Italian craftsmanship, rebellious attitude

**[[Target]]:** Gen Z, streetwear-adjacent, €200-800 price point

**Competition:** True Religion (faded), G-Star, premium denim segment

**Strength:** Denim credibility + fashion relevance under Martens
