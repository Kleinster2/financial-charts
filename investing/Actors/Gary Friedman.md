# Gary Friedman

Chairman and CEO of [[RH]] since 2001 (CEO title since 2014). Former [[Williams-Sonoma]] executive. Joined RH when the company was near bankruptcy; repositioned the brand from mid-market "Restoration Hardware" to luxury "RH" — galleries, hospitality, membership model. ~2.98M shares (~12% voting power). The singular visionary behind RH's transformation; key-man risk is a persistent investor concern.
