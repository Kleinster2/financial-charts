Mao Zedong — Founding Chairman of the People's Republic of [[China]] (1949-1976). Revolutionary leader whose political campaigns (Great Leap Forward, Cultural Revolution) killed tens of millions and shaped the CCP's institutional DNA. [[Xi Jinping]]'s power consolidation is the most significant concentration of authority since Mao.
