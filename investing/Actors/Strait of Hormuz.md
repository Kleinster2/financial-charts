# Strait of Hormuz

Strategic waterway between [[Iran]] and Oman connecting the Persian Gulf to the Gulf of Oman. ~20% of global oil supply transits daily (~20M bbl/day). Narrowest point: 21 miles. Controlled by [[Iran]]'s Revolutionary Guard on the northern shore.
