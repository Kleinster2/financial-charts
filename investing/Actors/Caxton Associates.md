### March 2026 — Iran conflict losses

Caxton's macro fund fell 15% to March 20 — among the worst losses of any major hedge fund during the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|Iran conflict]]. The [[Steepener trade]] and other rate-sensitive positions were crushed as markets violently repriced from expected rate cuts to rate hikes after oil surged above $110/bbl and [[Iran]] closed the [[Strait of Hormuz]]. Macro funds bore the brunt: [[Brevan Howard]] master fund -6.6%, [[ExodusPoint]] -4.5%. Multi-manager pod shops fared better ([[Citadel]] -1.9%, [[Millennium]] -1.2%).

*Source: FT, Apr 8 2026*
