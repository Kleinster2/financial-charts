On Bloomberg Tech (Apr 24 2026), Acree explicitly anchored upside on three pillars: (1) CPU demand strength from AI inferencing and agentic workloads; (2) [[Intel Foundry Services|18A]] yield improvement and 6-7x production-expectation increase; and (3) [[Intel Foundry Services|14A]] external-customer attraction with [[NVIDIA]] explicitly named as an example. He also cited US strategic-asset framing (US government's ~10% stake in Intel) as a tacit catalyst, and called [[TSMC]] capacity constraints in [[Taiwan]] "palpable" with [[Samsung]] as a distant second focused more on memory than logic.
