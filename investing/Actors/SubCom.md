# SubCom

US-headquartered submarine cable systems firm, one of the "big three" globally (alongside [[NEC]] and [[Alcatel Submarine Networks]]). Originated as the submarine-cable division of AT&T (separated from Bell Labs lineage), spun off through Tyco Electronics → TE Connectivity → became standalone SubCom in 2018 when [[Cerberus Capital Management]] acquired it. Focused on full-system subsea cable design, manufacturing, installation, and maintenance. Significant US government and defense exposure (NSA-related projects historically); central to the US-led subsea-cable strategic-infrastructure thread.
