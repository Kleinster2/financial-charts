**ERCOT** (Electric Reliability Council of Texas) — grid operator managing ~90% of [[Texas]] electricity load, serving ~26M customers. Independent ISO, not subject to [[FERC]] jurisdiction due to lack of interstate interconnections. Infamous for [[Winter Storm Uri]] grid collapse (Feb 2021).
