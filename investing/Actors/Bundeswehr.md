**Bundeswehr** — Federal defense forces of [[Germany]]. Modernizing aggressively under the [[Zeitenwende]] / [[Schuldenbremse reform]] era after decades of underinvestment. Procurement under the Merz government has pushed toward diversified supplier bases and domestic champions (e.g., the Feb–Apr 2026 three-supplier kamikaze-drone program with [[Helsing]], [[Stark]], and [[Rheinmetall]]).
