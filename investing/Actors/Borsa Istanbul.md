# Borsa Istanbul

**Borsa Istanbul** is [[Turkey]]'s sole stock exchange, headquartered in Istanbul. The benchmark index is the BIST 100 (ticker: XU100), which tracks the 100 most liquid stocks and is heavily weighted toward financials and industrials. The exchange also operates equity, debt, derivatives, and precious metals markets.

BIST has been one of the standout emerging market stories in recent years. After Turkey's unorthodox monetary policy (ultra-low rates under [[Erdogan]]'s pressure) drove massive domestic retail participation — Turks piled into equities as an inflation hedge when lira deposits yielded negative real returns — the exchange saw a structural increase in domestic liquidity. The pivot to orthodox monetary policy in mid-2023 under new central bank leadership initially sparked concerns about equity outflows, but the BIST 100 has continued to climb as foreign investors returned. January 2026 saw ~$2B in net foreign inflows, and the index was up ~25% YTD by mid-February 2026.

Key listed companies include [[Aselsan]] (defense electronics), Koç Holding (conglomerate), and major banks (Garanti, Akbank, İş Bank). The defense sector has become an increasingly important component as Turkish arms exports grow.
