**Alexandr Wang** — CEO and co-founder of [[Scale AI]]. Appointed Chief AI Officer at [[Meta]] (June 2025) after Meta paid $14.3B for a 49% stake in Scale AI, leading the new [[Meta Superintelligence Labs]] and precipitating [[Yann LeCun]]'s departure. Shipped first model — [[Muse Spark]] — on Apr 8 2026, nine months into the role.
