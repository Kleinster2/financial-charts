## Insights

**Largest independent title underwriter** — fifth overall behind Big Four ([[Fidelity National Financial]], [[First American Financial]], [[Old Republic International]], [[Stewart Information Services]]).

**Agent-only model** — Unlike Big Four who operate both direct and agency channels, Westcor works exclusively through independent agents. Never competes with its own distribution. This earns agent loyalty.

**Independent growth story** — Independent underwriters grew from 15% combined share (2019) to 26.9% (2024), with Westcor leading the pack.
