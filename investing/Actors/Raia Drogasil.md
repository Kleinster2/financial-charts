#actor #company #retail #pharmacy #healthcare #brazil #public

**Raia Drogasil** (RD) is Brazil's largest drugstore chain, formed by the 2011 merger of Droga Raia and Drogasil. It runs more than 3,000 stores under the Raia and Drogasil banners plus a growing health-services and digital platform (RD Saúde), and trades on B3 as `RADL3`.

The model is defensive consumer-staple retail — pharmacy demand is steady through the cycle — with the structural tailwind of Brazil's aging population and the formalization of a still-fragmented pharmacy market. Margins are thin retail margins; the growth lever is store density plus higher-margin health services.
