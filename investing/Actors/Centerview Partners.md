## Venezuela restructuring role (2026)

[[Reuters]] [identified Centerview](https://www.reuters.com/world/americas/lazard-bids-replace-centerview-venezuela-financial-adviser-source-says-2026-06-14/) as Venezuela's financial adviser in reporting on June 14, 2026 that [[Lazard]] had bid to replace it. The reported adviser contest is not evidence that Venezuela has agreed a restructuring transaction: debt terms, creditor treatment, and even the final adviser appointment remain unannounced. It does show that the process has progressed from a sanctions-constrained possibility toward organized preparation after [[OFAC]]'s May 5 [General License 58](https://ofac.treasury.gov/recent-actions/20260505).
