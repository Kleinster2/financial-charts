## Why IMCO matters

Ontario's answer to Canada's pension giants (CPPIB, OTPP, CDPQ):

- **Scale**: C$86B+ AUM, ~100 potential clients with C$140B addressable
- **Long-term**: Diversified across public/private markets for sustained returns
- **Infrastructure focus**: Active in digital infrastructure (Scala investment)

## Origins

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| 2015 | Investment Management Corporation Act passed |
| Jul 2016 | IMCO formally created |
| Jul 2017 | Operations began with OPB and WSIB |
| 2024 | Added FSRA, Tarion, OC Transpo, OCWA (C$2.6B) |

Created to provide pension funds lower costs through economies of scale — modeled on Canada's successful pension managers.
