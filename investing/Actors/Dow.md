# Dow

**Dow** is one of the world's largest chemical companies, a major producer of plastics, industrial intermediates, and specialty chemicals. Spun off from DowDuPont in 2019. Significant chlor-alkali capacity in Europe (Stade, Schkopau plants in Germany), with ISCC PLUS-certified low-carbon caustic soda and chlorine.
