#actor #crypto #exchange

**Binance** — World's largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume. Founded by Changpeng Zhao (CZ) in 2017. Settled with US DOJ/CFTC/FinCEN in Nov 2023 for $4.3B (AML/sanctions violations); CZ served four months in prison. Delisted [[Tether|USDT]] spot pairs for EEA users under [[MiCA]] (Jul 2025). Operates BNB Chain (formerly Binance Smart Chain).
