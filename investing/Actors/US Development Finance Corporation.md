# US Development Finance Corporation

US government development finance institution created in 2019 (BUILD Act), successor to OPIC. Invests in and provides financing/insurance for private-sector projects in developing countries. Max $60B portfolio. In March 2026, Trump ordered DFC to provide political risk insurance for Gulf shipping — a novel mandate far outside its traditional development finance scope.
