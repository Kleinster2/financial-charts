# ULA

United Launch Alliance — joint venture of [[Boeing]] and [[Lockheed Martin]] providing rocket launch services. Primary vehicles: Atlas V (legacy) and Vulcan Centaur (next-gen).

---

## Why it matters

ULA is a key launch provider for [[Amazon]] Leo, having flown 4 of the first 7 Kuiper missions on Atlas V. Also critical U.S. national security launch provider.
