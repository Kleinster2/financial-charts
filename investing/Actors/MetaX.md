**Bull:**

- Not sanctioned (TSMC access)
- Revenue growing 400%+ YoY
- China GPU demand captive

**Bear:**
- Still deeply unprofitable
- Valuation astronomical
- Competition from peers
- NVIDIA still 54% China share
- Break-even years away
