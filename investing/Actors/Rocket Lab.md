**Rocket Lab** — Small satellite launch and space systems company. Operates the Electron rocket (dedicated small-sat launch) and is developing the medium-lift Neutron vehicle. Founded by Peter Beck in New Zealand, now HQ in Long Beach, CA. Second-most-frequent US orbital launch provider after [[SpaceX]].
