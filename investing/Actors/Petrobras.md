## Pre-salt reserves

**World-class deepwater:**
- Sub-salt formations off Brazil coast
- 50-100B barrels recoverable
- Low lifting costs (~$6/bbl)
- Technology leader in deepwater

## Government control

**State-owned dynamics:**
- Brazilian government = majority shareholder
- Fuel price controls (political)
- Dividend policy uncertainty
- Management turnover with elections

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Pre-salt = low-cost, long-life assets
- Strong free cash flow
- High dividend yield
- Deepwater expertise

**Bear:**
- Government interference
- Dividend policy changes
- BRL currency risk
- Political uncertainty
