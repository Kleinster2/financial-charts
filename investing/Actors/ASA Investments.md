**ASA Investments** — Brazilian financial firm founded by Alberto Safra, son of [[Joseph Safra]]. Created after Alberto recruited the CEO and senior executives from [[Banco Safra]], which his father viewed as betrayal — leading to Alberto's reduced inheritance allocation. The launch of ASA was the catalyst for the [[Safra family]] inheritance dispute (2020-2024). Under the July 2024 settlement, Alberto divests from [[J. Safra Group]] and pursues business independently through ASA.
