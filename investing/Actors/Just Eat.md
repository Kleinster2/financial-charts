# Just Eat

Just Eat began as a UK food-delivery marketplace (founded 2001, Copenhagen origins, listed LSE 2014). In 2020 it combined with Takeaway.com in a £10.5B contested deal (rival interloper bid by [[Prosus]] failed to prevent the combination). The combined Just Eat Takeaway.com listed in Amsterdam (TKWY.AS).

In February 2025, [[Prosus]] succeeded in acquiring Just Eat Takeaway.com in a second round of M&A — completing the deal that it had failed to win five years earlier. Just Eat is now a [[Prosus]] subsidiary and is delisted.

[[Gleacher Shacklock]] advised on the 2019 Just Eat / Takeaway.com combination, one of the most-contested UK delisting deals of the period.
