## Investment case

**Bull:**
- [[Platform economics]] (recurring, high-margin)
- 90M+ account base
- CTV ad market growing
- OS licensing upside
- FAST leadership (The Roku Channel)

**Bear:**
- [[Amazon]]/[[Google]] competition
- Hardware commoditization
- US market saturating
- International expansion slow
- Valuation premium
