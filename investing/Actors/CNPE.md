**CNPE (Conselho Nacional de Politica Energetica)** — Brazil's National Energy Policy Council, the body that sets and adjusts mandatory biofuel blending percentages. Chaired by the Minister of Mines and Energy. Key authority over the [[Brazil Biodiesel Program|PNPB biodiesel mandate]] (B15 as of Aug 2025, heading to B20 by 2030) and ethanol blending (27% anhydrous in gasoline). Retains discretion to delay or accelerate mandate increases, making it the critical policy variable for biofuel investors.
