# NTT World Engineering Marine

Japanese submarine cable repair and maintenance specialist, subsidiary of [[NTT]] Corporation (specifically of NTT Communications / NTT Group). Distinct from the major cable installers ([[NEC]], [[SubCom]], [[Alcatel Submarine Networks]], [[HMN Tech]]) in that it focuses primarily on cable repair, maintenance, and survey work rather than new cable construction. Operates a fleet of cable repair ships and is one of the operators contracted under regional maintenance agreements (such as the Yokohama Zone agreement covering Northeast Asian waters). The firm is the prototypical case Ward-Jackson uses in the [[Economic latency]] framework for "small and ageing repair fleet" capability that lacks surge capacity.
