**Screaming Eagle Acquisition Corp** (formerly Nasdaq: SCRM) was the blank-check vehicle used to take [[Lionsgate]]'s studio business public in a de-SPAC merger in May 2024 — the first stage of separating the studio from [[Starz]] — sponsored by serial media-SPAC operators Harry Sloan, Jeff Sagansky, and Eli Baker.

## The de-SPAC

Screaming Eagle raised ~$750M in its January 2022 IPO. By the time the [[Lionsgate]] deal closed, heavy shareholder redemptions had drained most of the trust — only $75.7M remained — so the bulk of the ~$350M delivered to [[Lions Gate Entertainment]] came from a $274.3M upsized PIPE. The proceeds went to debt paydown. Post-merger, the parent retained 87.2% of Lionsgate Studios; former SPAC shareholders, founders, and PIPE investors held ~12.8%.

The sponsors are among the most prolific media-SPAC operators: their prior "Eagle" vehicles took DraftKings (Diamond Eagle) and Skillz (Flying Eagle) public. The structure created a separately traded studio entity with a public market price while [[Starz]] was still attached — setting the table for the full separation completed in May 2025.
