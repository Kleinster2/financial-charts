#actor #taiwan #foundry #semiconductors

**PSMC** (Powerchip Semiconductor Manufacturing Corp) — Taiwan's third-largest pure-play foundry after [[TSMC]] and [[UMC]], headquartered in Hsinchu. A specialty/legacy-node foundry whose strategic relevance in 2026 comes from two deals: it is the technology partner for [[Tata Electronics]]' ~$14B Dholera fab (India's first advanced-logic plant, under the [[India Semiconductor Mission]]) and it sold its Miaoli fab to [[Micron]] for $1.8B for advanced DRAM/HBM expansion.

PSMC matters to the vault as the foundry enabling India's domestic wafer-capacity bet and as a Taiwan foundry asset being partially absorbed by the memory super-cycle. It is the named technology partner that makes the [[Tata Electronics]]/[[India execution gap|India fab ambition]] a buildable project rather than only a policy statement.
