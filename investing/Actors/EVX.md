# EVX

EVX (NYSE Arca: EVX) is the VanEck Environmental Services ETF — a small, modified-cap-weighted basket of US environmental-services stocks (waste collection/disposal, recycling, water/remediation, environmental consulting). Because it spreads across many smaller environmental names beyond the waste majors, its beta behaves more like broad industrials than the listed waste oligopoly.

## Use case

The replicability benchmark for the [[Solid waste#Cluster validation|waste-management cluster validation]]. The key finding is that EVX does NOT capture the waste oligopoly: the WM/RSG/WCN/GFL basket carries a +0.424 intra-advantage versus EVX, and EVX clusters with [[XLI]]/SPY rather than with the waste majors — so the distinct waste factor must be owned as the basket, not via this diluted ETF.
