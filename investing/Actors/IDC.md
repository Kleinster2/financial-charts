**IDC (International Data Corporation)** — IT market research firm. Standard source for PC, server, smartphone shipment data.

---

## Why IDC matters for this vault

Industry-standard source for hardware shipment data:

| Coverage | Use case |
|----------|----------|
| **PC shipments** | Dell, HP, Lenovo market share |
| **Server shipments** | Data center demand proxy |
| **Smartphone shipments** | Apple, Samsung, Xiaomi share |
| **Cloud infrastructure** | IaaS/PaaS spending |

---

## Key data products

| Tracker | Coverage |
|---------|----------|
| Quarterly PC Tracker | Unit shipments, market share |
| Quarterly Server Tracker | Server unit/revenue share |
| Quarterly Mobile Tracker | Smartphone shipments |
| Cloud Tracker | Infrastructure spending |

---

## How to use in vault

IDC data useful for:
- PC demand trends ([[Dell]], [[HP Inc.]], [[Lenovo]])
- Server demand as AI infrastructure proxy
- Smartphone market share shifts
- Validating OEM revenue trends

**Note**: Shipment data = units, not revenue. ASP matters.
