# Sisecam

**Sisecam** is a Turkish glass and chemicals conglomerate and the world's second-largest [[Soda Ash|soda ash]] producer, with ~5 Mt/year capacity across [[Turkey]], the US, Bosnia-Herzegovina, and Bulgaria. Plans to double to 10 Mt by 2028 via US natural soda expansion. Acquired Ciner Resources' US Wyoming operations (previously traded as SIRE on NYSE, delisted Dec 2024). 26% of shares listed on BIST (SISE.IS); 74% held by Is Bank.
