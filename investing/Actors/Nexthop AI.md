# Nexthop AI

AI data center networking startup building switches and infrastructure specifically designed for AI workloads. Raised **$500M Series B** at $4.2B valuation (Mar 10, 2026) — oversubscribed round led by [[Lightspeed Venture Partners]], with [[Andreessen Horowitz]], [[Altimeter Capital|Altimeter]], and all existing investors.

## What they do

Designs networking gear for AI data centers — switches optimized for the massive data movement required by AI training and inference clusters. Launched three new switches alongside the funding announcement. Competes in the AI networking layer alongside [[Arista Networks]], [[Cisco]], and [[Broadcom]] (networking ASICs).

Market context: Hyperscalers ([[Alphabet]], [[Amazon]], [[Meta]], [[Microsoft]]) expected to spend ~$650B on AI data center infrastructure in 2026. Networking is the bottleneck as clusters scale beyond tens of thousands of GPUs.
