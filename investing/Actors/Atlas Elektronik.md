# Atlas Elektronik

German naval defense and electronics company. Joint venture between [[ThyssenKrupp Marine Systems]] (TKMS) and [[Airbus]]. Products include the SeaFox expendable mine disposal system and ARCIMS unmanned surface vehicle. Competitor to [[Exail Technologies]] in mine countermeasures. Part of [[Saab]]-led consortium bidding for Australia SEA1905.
