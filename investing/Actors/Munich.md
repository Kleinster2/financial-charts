# Munich

Capital of Bavaria and [[Germany]]'s third-largest city (~1.6M population, ~6M metro). Munich is the insurance capital of continental Europe and a major center for industrial manufacturing, automotive, and technology. Headquarters to [[Allianz]] (world's largest insurer), [[Munich Re]] (world's largest reinsurer), [[Siemens]] (industrial conglomerate), and [[BMW]] (luxury automaker). The city's concentration of insurance and reinsurance capital — [[Allianz]], [[Munich Re]], and dozens of specialty underwriters — makes it a global pricing center for catastrophe risk. Munich is also Germany's wealthiest city by per-capita income, with a cost of living well above Berlin or Frankfurt. The Technical University of Munich (TUM) feeds talent into the city's growing AI and robotics sector, and the city hosts the annual Munich Security Conference, the world's leading defense policy forum.
