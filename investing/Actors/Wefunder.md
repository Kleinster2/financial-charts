Wefunder — equity-crowdfunding platform that allows retail investors to back private startups, typically at the same terms as institutional VC investors. Operates under [[Reg CF]] and [[Reg A+]] frameworks in the US.

---

## Why Wefunder matters

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Category | Equity crowdfunding |
| Notable raise on platform | [[Fathom AI]] Series A community allocation — $2M+ from 1,300+ users in two weeks (Sep 2024) |
| Status | Private |

In the [[Fathom AI]] case Wefunder hosted the 10% community-investor tranche of a Telescope Partners-led $17M Series A — retail investors received the same economic terms as the institutional lead. The structure is rare in standard VC rounds and serves both as a retention signal (paying users putting capital in) and as a distribution / evangelism asset.
