**DISA** (Defense Information Systems Agency) — US Department of Defense combat support agency providing IT and communications infrastructure to the military. Manages the [[Joint Warfighting Cloud Capability]] contract ($9B ceiling) and classified networks (SIPRNet, JWICS). Headquartered at Fort Meade, Maryland.
