## Investment thesis

**Bull:**
- Housing underbuilt long-term
- Pro customer stickiness
- Supply chain moat
- Dividend aristocrat (15+ years)
- M&A optionality (SRS)
- Aging housing stock

**Bear:**
- Housing cycle exposure
- Interest rate sensitivity
- Lowe's competition
- Amazon threat (basics)
- Labor costs
