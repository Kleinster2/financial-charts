## Iran conflict context (March 2026)

In a client note (Mar 12), Rapidan said while US Treasury selling front-month crude futures would be "unprecedented," they *"cannot completely rule it out"* given the panic situation. The note reflected growing market speculation after Brent crude swung from ~$120 to below $100 on March 10.

*Source: FT (Mar 12, 2026)*

[[Bob McNally]] in Al Jazeera analyst roundup (Mar 19) on $200 oil question: "Nobody knows" the demand destruction level, "but it may well be above previous nominal highs at $147."
