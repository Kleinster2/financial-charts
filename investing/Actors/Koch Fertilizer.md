# Koch Fertilizer

Private US fertilizer producer owned by [[Koch Industries]]. ~3 Mt/year ammonia capacity. Acquired Iowa Fertilizer Company (850 kt capacity) for $3.6B in 2023. Part of Koch Ag & Energy Solutions division. Operations across the US, Canada, and international.
