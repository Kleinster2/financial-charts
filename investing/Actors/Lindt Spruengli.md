#actor #company #consumer #confectionery #switzerland #public

Lindt & Sprüngli — Swiss premium chocolate manufacturer and one of the largest global chocolate companies by revenue. Listed on SIX Swiss Exchange as LISN. Known for premium positioning (Lindor truffles, Excellence bars) and vertical integration from bean to bar. Backs [[Food Brewer]], a Swiss cell-based cocoa start-up, as a supply-diversification hedge against West [[Africa]] cocoa risk.

## Context

As a premium-positioned chocolate maker, [[Lindt Spruengli]] is more exposed than value-segment peers to cocoa butter price volatility — its recipes use higher cocoa butter fractions and allow less input substitution. The 2024-25 spike (cocoa butter topping €40,000/tonne; see [[Cocoa]]) put a disproportionate margin squeeze on the premium tier, which is the category-level motivation behind the [[Food Brewer]] partnership.
