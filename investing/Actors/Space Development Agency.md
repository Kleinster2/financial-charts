**Space Development Agency** -- US Space Force organization building the Proliferated Warfighter Space Architecture (PWSA), a proliferated low-Earth-orbit satellite network for military communications, missile warning, missile tracking, and targeting support.

## Why it matters

SDA is the demand node behind many US defense-space supplier relationships. Its proliferated architecture pulls in satellite buses, optical communications, RF payloads, missile-warning sensors, launch services, and ground-network software on faster cycles than legacy national-security space procurement.

The supplier base matters for [[Pax Silica]] because PWSA-type programs turn sub-tier satellite inputs into national-security procurement questions. A small satellite bus or payload supplier can become strategically relevant if it sits inside the Transport or Tracking Layer production chain.
