[[Jiangxi Copper]] is [[China]]'s largest copper smelter and a top-five global copper producer. Operates the Guixi smelter. A major involuntary producer of [[Sulfuric Acid]] as a smelting byproduct, targeting 6.53 Mt acid output in 2025. Dual-listed in Shanghai and Hong Kong.
