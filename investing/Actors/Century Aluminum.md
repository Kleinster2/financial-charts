# Century Aluminum

**Century Aluminum (CENX)** — a US primary aluminum producer (smelting), headquartered in Chicago; NASDAQ-listed. Operates smelters at Mt Holly (SC), Sebree (KY), Hawesville (KY, idled), and Grundartangi (Iceland), and is developing a new US smelter backed by a DOE grant. Because aluminum smelting is extraordinarily power-intensive, CENX is highly leveraged to both the aluminum price and electricity costs, giving it high volatility (~67%). [[Glencore]] is a major shareholder and offtake counterparty. With [[Alcoa]] it forms the aluminum pair — the ROBUST, cleanly separable sub-cohort of [[Steel and aluminum equity beta]], distinct from the steel names.
