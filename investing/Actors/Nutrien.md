#stub

[[Nutrien]] is the world's largest provider of crop inputs and services, formed from the 2018 merger of PotashCorp and Agrium. Major producer of potash, nitrogen, and phosphate fertilizers. Significant sulfuric acid consumer in phosphate operations.
