## 2008 crisis history

**Near-collapse:**
- CDS exposure via AIG Financial Products
- $182B government bailout
- Systemic risk designation
- Decade+ of restructuring

**Recovery:**
- Repaid bailout with profit to government
- Shed non-core assets
- Refocused on core insurance

---

## Corebridge separation

**Life and Retirement spinoff:**
- Corebridge Financial (CRBG)
- AIG retaining ~50% stake
- Simplification play
- Focus on P&C going forward

## Investment case
