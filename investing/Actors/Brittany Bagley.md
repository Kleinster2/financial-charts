# Brittany Bagley

CFO and COO of [[Axon|Axon Enterprise]], one of the few executives in public markets holding both the financial and operational reins of a high-growth company simultaneously.

## Synopsis

Brittany Bagley joined [[Axon]] as CFO in 2020 and added the COO title in 2022, consolidating financial strategy and operational execution under one executive. Her background is unusually diverse for a tech-defense CFO: she was previously CFO at [[Sonos]] (2019-2020), where she navigated the smart speaker company's IPO aftermath and DTC pivot, and before that spent over a decade as a Managing Director at [[KKR]], the private equity giant. The KKR tenure gave her deep capital allocation and M&A experience that translates directly to Axon's acquisition-driven growth strategy.

At [[Axon]], Bagley oversees the financial architecture of a company that has transitioned from a single-product TASER manufacturer into a SaaS-enabled public safety platform (Axon Cloud, body cameras, drone-as-first-responder). The dual CFO/COO role reflects CEO [[Rick Smith]]'s trust in Bagley's execution capability and Axon's relatively lean organizational structure for a ~$45B market cap company. Axon has been one of the strongest-performing mid-cap defense/tech stocks, with revenue growing 25%+ annually and expanding margins as the recurring software revenue base scales. Bagley's PE background at [[KKR]] is visible in Axon's disciplined approach to capital deployment -- the company has avoided the empire-building acquisitions that derail many high-growth companies.
