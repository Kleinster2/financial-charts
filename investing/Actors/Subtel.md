**Subtel** — [[Chile]]'s Subsecretariat of Telecommunications, the regulatory body overseeing telecom infrastructure. Under the Ministry of Transport and Telecommunications. Two Subtel officials (Undersecretary Claudio Araya, cabinet head Guillermo Petersen) were targeted by [[US Chile cable visa sanctions]] (Feb 2026) over the [[Chile-China Express]] cable.
