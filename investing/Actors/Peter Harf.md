**Peter Harf** -- German businessman who built [[JAB Holding]] into a $50B+ investment firm over 40+ years for the [[Reimann]] family. Joined Benckiser in 1981 (ex-[[BCG]]). Created [[Reckitt Benckiser]] via 1999 merger. Restructured family assets into JAB in 2012. Retired Apr 2025 at age 78. Remains fully invested and chairs [[Alfred Landecker Foundation]].
