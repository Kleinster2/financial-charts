# Moove

[[Cosan]]'s lubricants subsidiary — one of the largest lubricant producers in [[Brazil]] and a growing international player. Produces and distributes Mobil-branded lubricants under license from [[ExxonMobil]] in Brazil, and operates in Europe and the US. IPO'd on NYSE in Oct 2024 (ticker: MOOV) at $24.50/share.
