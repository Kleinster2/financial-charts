**Moove** — [[Cosan]]'s lubricants subsidiary (~70%). Sole manufacturer of Mobil-branded products in [[Brazil]] under [[ExxonMobil]] license. Also operates in Europe and US (acquired PetroChoice Lubrication). [[CVC Capital Partners]] Fund VII holds 30% (since Dec 2018). Attempted NYSE IPO (Jul 2024) but withdrew in Oct 2024 after bids came in at $12.50-13, well below the $14.50-17.50 target range. IPO had targeted ~$1.9B valuation.
