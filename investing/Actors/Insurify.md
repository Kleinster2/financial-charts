# Insurify

**Insurify** — AI-powered online insurance shopping platform. Privately held. Launched the first insurance app in [[OpenAI]]'s ChatGPT app directory (February 2026), triggering a selloff in insurance broker stocks.

## Why Insurify matters

Insurify embedded AI-powered insurance comparison inside ChatGPT, reaching 300M+ users. This is a **distribution play** — the technology existed, but putting it inside a general-purpose AI platform made it a catalyst for the [[Insurify insurance broker selloff February 2026]].
