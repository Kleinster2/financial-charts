## Venezuela position

**Unique positioning:**
- Only major foreign bank with significant current presence in Venezuela
- Maintained operations through Maduro era while US/other banks exited
- If political transition succeeds, positioned for growth

**Opportunity:** Energy/infrastructure projects as Venezuela rebuilds.

**Risk:** Political instability, sanctions complexity, currency controls.
