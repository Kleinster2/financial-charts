Constellation Software — Canadian vertical market software conglomerate. Founded 1995 by Mark Leonard. IPO 2006 on TSX (CSU.TO). Acquires and holds small/mid-size vertical market software businesses — 500+ acquisitions to date across 75+ verticals. Decentralized operating model: acquired companies keep autonomy. Bootstrapped acquisition model funded by operating cash flow post-IPO (no debt, no secondary equity raises). Market cap ~C$90B+.
