#actor #company #public #shell-company

Dogehash Technologies — Nasdaq-listed meme-coin-sector company that served as the listed shell for the [[Thumzup Media]] reverse merger arranged through the [[Dominari Holdings]] / [[American Ventures]] pipeline. Post-merger the combined entity rebranded into a data-center company. One of three named American Ventures-affiliated public-company shells in the second half of 2025, alongside [[SRM Entertainment]] (Tron deal) and [[Aureus Greenway Holdings]] (Powerus deal).

## Vault relevance

Dogehash is the meme-coin-sector entry in the Trump-family-aligned reverse-merger lineup. The combined Thumzup/Dogehash entity's post-merger pivot to data-center positioning illustrates the optionality the [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]] structure preserves: once a private operating company has inherited a listing, it can subsequently rebrand into whatever sector best matches the prevailing capital-markets narrative.
