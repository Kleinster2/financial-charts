# Professional Bull Riders

Professional Bull Riders (PBR) is the premier bull riding organization, founded in 1992, promoting more than 200 events globally each year. Formerly owned by [[Endeavor]], it was sold to [[TKO Group Holdings]] in February 2025 as part of the ~$3.25B all-equity sports-assets transaction, joining [[UFC]] and [[WWE]] under the TKO umbrella.
