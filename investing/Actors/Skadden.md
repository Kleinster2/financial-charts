# Skadden

Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom — major NY-based US law firm with global footprint. Historically a top-tier M&A practice and one of the firms most associated with the rise of corporate dealmaking through the 1980s. Now a peer of [[Wachtell Lipton]], [[Kirkland & Ellis]], [[Latham & Watkins]], [[Cravath, Swaine & Moore]], [[Paul, Weiss]], and [[Sullivan & Cromwell]] in the elite NY M&A bar — though smaller-revenue and smaller per-partner economics than the Kirkland-Latham scale leaders.
