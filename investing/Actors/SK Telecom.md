Merged AI chip subsidiary Sapeon into [[Rebellions]] (Dec 2024). ## AI export-ban trigger (June 2026)

SK Telecom is an [[Anthropic]] investor and was one of roughly 150 [[Project Glasswing]] partners with restricted [[Claude Mythos|Mythos]] access. US officials flagged the carrier over suspected [[China]] ties — historical and thin (parent [[SK Group]] held a China Unicom stake until 2009; the carrier reported only ~$1.9M China revenue and ~7 staff there in 2024) — and pressed Anthropic to revoke its access, days before the June 12 blanket directive that barred all foreign nationals and took [[Claude Fable 5|Fable 5]] and [[Claude Mythos|Mythos 5]] offline worldwide. SK Telecom denies any China ties; no underlying evidence is public, so the allegation is contested. Full event detail in [[Anthropic#Jun 12 — Commerce export ban; Fable 5 / Mythos 5 taken offline|Anthropic]] and [[Export controls]].

*Sources: [Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2026/06/15/how-anthropic-lost-white-houses-trust-then-its-flagship-product/), June 15, 2026; WIRED; Tom's Hardware; Korea JoongAng Daily — June 15–18, 2026.*
