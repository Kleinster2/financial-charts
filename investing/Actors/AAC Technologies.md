**AAC Technologies** (2018.HK) is a [[China]]-based supplier of acoustics, haptics, optics and precision-mechanical components for smartphones and consumer electronics, and a long-standing [[Apple]] supply-chain name. It competes with [[Lingyi iTech]] and [[Luxshare Precision]] in the Chinese functional-component complex.
