# Kazakhstan

Central Asian petrostate and major commodity exporter. World's largest uranium producer (via ISL mining), significant oil/gas producer (Tengiz, Kashagan, Karachaganak), and one of the world's top sulfur exporters via Tengizchevroil operations.
