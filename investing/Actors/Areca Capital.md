Areca Capital is a [[Malaysia|Malaysian]] boutique asset management firm based in Kuala Lumpur, specializing in conventional and Shariah-compliant funds. The firm manages equity, balanced, and fixed income funds for retail and institutional clients in the Malaysian market. [[Danny Wong]] serves as CEO and is the firm's primary public voice on market strategy.

Areca is a mid-tier player in Malaysia's competitive fund management industry, which is shaped by Islamic finance requirements (a significant portion of Malaysian assets must be Shariah-compliant) and dominated by larger players like [[CIMB-Principal]], [[Public Mutual]], and [[Permodalan Nasional Berhad]]. The firm's analysts are regularly quoted in Malaysian financial media on equity strategy, sector allocation, and geopolitical risk positioning.
