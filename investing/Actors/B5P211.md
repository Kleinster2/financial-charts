## What it tracks

The IMA-B 5 sub-index holds only the shorter NTN-Bs (up to five years), so B5P211 delivers inflation protection with much less duration risk than [[IMAB11]] or [[IB5M11]] — closer to "real-rate carry" than to a duration bet.

## Use case

The conservative IPCA sleeve: positive real yield with limited mark-to-market swing. A common building block for investors who want inflation linkage on the short end without taking a strong view on long real rates.

## Correlation

Lower duration than IB5M11 but still meaningfully equity-overlapping — 0.41 daily-return correlation to [[EWZ]] over two years. Even short IPCA duration shares the Brazil rate-cycle factor; only the floating-rate cash ETF escapes it. See [[Brazilian fixed-income ETFs]].
