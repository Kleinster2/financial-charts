# Hin Leong Trading

Former [[Singapore]]-based oil trader. Founder Lim Oon Kuin ("OK Lim") hid $800M in futures losses. When revealed: liabilities $4.05B vs assets $714M, with only $141M in actual oil inventory against $1.28B declared. Filed bankruptcy April 2020. OK Lim sentenced to 17.5 years (Nov 2024). Collapse reshuffled Singapore's bunkering market — [[MPA]] awarded licenses to Minerva ([[Mercuria]]) and TFG Marine ([[Trafigura]]) after Hin Leong's exit.
