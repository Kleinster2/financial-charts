# BOAT

BOAT (NYSE Arca: BOAT) is the SonicShares Global Shipping ETF — a basket of global marine-shipping companies spanning crude and product tankers, dry bulk, containerships, and LNG/gas carriers. Because it spreads across all shipping segments, it is a broad-shipping proxy rather than a pure-tanker one. Launched 2021.

## Use case

A shipping-ETF replicability benchmark for the [[Tankers#Cluster validation|tanker cluster validation]]. Because BOAT mixes tankers with dry bulk, containers, and LNG (different rate cycles), the pure crude/product-tanker basket may be tighter than BOAT — the test of whether tankers are a distinct factor or just broad-shipping beta.
