# Mercado Bitcoin

Brazil's largest crypto exchange. Part of the consortium (with [[Bitso]] and [[Foxbit]], using [[Fireblocks]] custody) that launched [[BRL1]], a real-pegged stablecoin. Handles billions in trading volume. Owned by 2TM Group.
