#actor #government #data

**EIA** — Statistical and analytical agency within the [[Department of Energy]]. Primary source for US energy data. Publishes the Short-Term Energy Outlook (STEO), Weekly Petroleum Status Report, and Annual Energy Outlook (AEO).
