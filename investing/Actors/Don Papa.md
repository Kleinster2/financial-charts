# Don Papa

**Filipino premium rum brand** — produced on Negros Island, Philippines. Acquired by [[Diageo]] in 2023 for an undisclosed sum. Named after Dionisio Magbuelas ("Papa Isio"), a revolutionary hero from Negros. Positioned as a super-premium aged rum with a distinctive illustrated bottle. Hosted an exclusive launch event at [[Aubi & Ramsa]]'s Wynwood location (Mar 2021).
