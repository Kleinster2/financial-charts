**WTO** (World Trade Organization) — international organization governing rules of trade between nations, established 1995 as successor to GATT. 164 members. Headquartered in Geneva. Dispute settlement system has been effectively paralyzed since the US blocked Appellate Body appointments (2019-present), reducing it from a rules-based enforcer to a negotiating forum.
