**Swissquote** — Swiss online bank and trading platform, listed on SIX as SQN.SW. One of Europe's leading digital-first financial services companies, offering multi-asset trading, banking, and crypto services under Swiss regulatory protection.

---

## Synopsis

Swissquote has built a profitable niche as the Swiss alternative to US-dominated online brokerages. Its differentiator is not price — Swiss regulation makes it more expensive than [[Interactive Brokers]] or [[Robinhood]] — but the regulatory wrapper: Swiss banking secrecy (reduced but not eliminated), FINMA oversight, and deposit protection attract a wealth-preservation clientele that values jurisdiction over cost. The platform serves over 500,000 accounts with CHF 60B+ in client assets, offering equities, FX, options, crypto, and even mortgage products. Revenue growth has been driven by crypto trading (volatile but high-margin) and geographic expansion into Asia.

The AI disintermediation threat is the key risk to monitor. Swissquote's value proposition includes research, portfolio analytics, and financial advice — all services where AI agents could reduce the perceived need for a human-curated platform. If [[Claude]] or equivalent systems can provide personalized portfolio analysis and trade execution, the premium that Swissquote charges over bare-metal brokerages becomes harder to justify. That said, the Swiss regulatory moat is real: wealth management clients choosing Swissquote are buying a jurisdiction, not just a platform. The company is profitable with strong margins (net income margin typically 25%+), making it one of the few European fintechs that does not depend on eventual profitability to justify its valuation.
