### SanDisk era (1988-2016)

Co-founded SanDisk with Eli Harari and Jack Yuan in 1988. Rose through engineering ranks:
- Director of memory design
- VP Product Development
- SVP Engineering
- COO & EVP
- **CEO (2011-2016)**

Led SanDisk through the $19B acquisition by [[Western Digital]] in 2016.

### Micron era (2017-present)

Recruited to lead Micron in May 2017 after Mark Durcan's retirement. Under his leadership:
- Navigated 2023 memory downturn
- Positioned Micron for AI/[[HBM]] boom
- Announced **$200B US investment** (Jan 2026)
- Stock +239% in 2025

## Significance

**Why he matters:**

1. **Flash memory pioneer** — Co-invented many foundational technologies enabling high-capacity flash. 70+ patents.

2. **Built two memory giants** — Led SanDisk from startup to $19B exit, now steering Micron through AI transformation.

3. **Industry statesman** — Chairman of Semiconductor Industry Association (2019), National Academy of Engineering member (2022).

4. **US memory champion** — Leading domestic memory manufacturing revival amid [[CHIPS Act]] and geopolitical tensions.
