**Rumble** — Video-sharing platform positioned as a free-speech alternative to YouTube. Publicly traded (NASDAQ: RUM). Received a $775M strategic investment from [[Tether]] — Tether's largest single venture deployment. Focused on building out cloud infrastructure (Rumble Cloud) as an alternative to [[Amazon|AWS]] and big tech platforms.
