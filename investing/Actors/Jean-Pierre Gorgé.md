# Jean-Pierre Gorgé

French industrialist and founder of the defense-technology dynasty that became [[Exail Technologies]]. A graduate of École Polytechnique and Sciences Po, he served as a procurement official at the DGA (Direction générale de l'Armement) from 1969-1975 before moving through the Ministry of Industry and SCAM/Alstom. Founded Finuchem in 1988 as a chemicals business, then pivoted to defense technology by acquiring [[ECA Group]] in 1992 — a move that planted the seed for France's leading autonomous underwater vehicle manufacturer. Renamed the holding company Groupe Gorgé in 2009 and gradually handed operational control to his son [[Raphaël Gorgé]], who completed the transformation into a pure-play maritime defense autonomy company with the 2022 [[iXblue]] acquisition and rebranding to [[Exail Technologies]].
