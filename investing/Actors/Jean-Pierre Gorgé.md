# Jean-Pierre Gorgé

French industrialist. Founded Finuchem (1988), acquired [[ECA Group]] (1992), built what became [[Exail Technologies]]. Polytechnique and Sciences Po graduate. Former DGA (Direction générale de l'Armement) procurement official (1969-1975). Father of [[Raphaël Gorgé]].
