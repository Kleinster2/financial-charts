## Business

Integrated energy logistics: shipping, port operations, marine services. Fleet includes tankers, gas carriers, offshore support vessels.

**Growth pillars:**
- Fleet expansion (+20% target by 2026)
- Geographic expansion (Asia, Africa)
- Sustainability ($1B investment target by 2030)

Revenue grew 36% YoY in Q3 2025.

## Investment case

One of the few listed entry points into [[ADNOC]]'s value chain alongside ADNOC Distribution and ADNOC Gas. Benefits from Abu Dhabi's production expansion and global [[LNG]] trade growth.
