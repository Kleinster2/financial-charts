**Anodot** — Israeli / US analytics-software vendor providing AI-driven business-monitoring and anomaly-detection services to enterprise customers. Notable as the third-party supply-chain pivot in the April 2026 [[ShinyHunters]] breach of [[Take-Two|Rockstar Games]] (~80M records claimed): the attacker chain ran Anodot → [[Snowflake]] → Rockstar customer data. The pattern is structurally identical to the 2024 [[EPAM Systems]] / Snowflake supply-chain compromise that enabled the broader 165-organization Snowflake credential-theft campaign — analytics integrators and BI platforms become a credentialed pivot point into customer data warehouses.
