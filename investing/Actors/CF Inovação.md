# CF Inovação

Brazilian fintech company founded in 2023 by [[Tony Volpon]] (ex-[[Banco Central do Brasil|BCB]] Deputy Governor) and José Carneiro. Initially focused on real estate tokenization, pivoted to stablecoins. Issuer of [[BRD]], a yield-bearing [[Brazilian real]]-pegged stablecoin backed by government bonds.
