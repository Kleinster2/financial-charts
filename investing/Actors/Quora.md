## Products

**Q&A Platform:**
- User-generated questions and answers
- Expert verification system
- Monetized through advertising

**Poe (AI chatbot platform):**
- Launched 2022
- Aggregates multiple LLMs ([[ChatGPT]], [[Claude]], others)
- Subscription model
- Competes with [[ChatGPT]], [[Perplexity]]

---

## AI pivot

Quora pivoted to AI in two ways:

1. **Data licensing** — Quora content used to train LLMs (controversial, like [[Reddit]])
2. **Poe** — Building AI interface product

Poe is D'Angelo's bet that the value is in the interface layer, not the model layer.
