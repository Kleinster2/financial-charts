## Strategic significance

**Only US producer of heavy rare earths:**

| Element | US capacity | Phoenix Tailings |
|---------|-------------|------------------|
| [[Terbium]] (Tb) | None at scale | **Producing** |
| [[Dysprosium]] (Dy) | None at scale | **Producing** |

**Context:** US has no other domestic capacity for heavy REEs. [[MP Materials]] does light REEs only.

---

## Technology

**Recycling approach:**
- Processes mining byproducts (not primary ore)
- Extracts rare earths from waste streams
- Cleaner than traditional mining
- Lower capex than greenfield mines

---

## Customers

Supplying defense sector with Tb and Dy metals. Specific customers not disclosed.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Only US heavy REE producer
- [[Defense]] demand secured
- Zero [[China]] supply chain
- Recycling = lower environmental footprint
- Government support tailwind

**Bear:**
- Small scale (200 tons/year)
- Private (no liquid investment)
- Scaling risk
- Cost competitiveness unproven
