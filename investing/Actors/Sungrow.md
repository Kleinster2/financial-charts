# Sungrow

Sungrow Power Supply is a [[China]]-based power-electronics manufacturer, listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange (300274.SZ). The world's largest manufacturer of solar PV inverters by volume (competing with [[Huawei]] in the same segment), a major battery-storage supplier, and since 2020 the parent of Sungrow Hydrogen — one of the four largest Chinese alkaline [[Electrolyzers|electrolyzer]] manufacturers. Together with [[Longi Green Energy]], Sungrow is the cleanest example of Chinese clean-tech verticalization from renewables generation hardware into hydrogen manufacturing.

## Why Sungrow matters

Sungrow's relevance to the [[Second China shock]] frame sits on three axes:

1. **PV inverters** — Sungrow and [[Huawei]] together hold well over half the global inverter market. The last decade of this sector tells the story of Western inverter manufacturers (SMA Solar, Enphase at residential tier, SolarEdge) either losing utility-scale share or being forced into niche residential positioning.

2. Battery storage systems — utility-scale BESS is the newest major clean-tech segment, and Chinese manufacturers (Sungrow, BYD, [[CATL]]-adjacent) are already dominant in integrated system supply. US manufacturers ([[Fluence]], Powin) retain niches but face margin compression.

3. [[Electrolyzers|Electrolyzers]] — Sungrow Hydrogen scaled to multi-GW alkaline manufacturing capacity within four years of market entry, tracking the same pattern as [[Longi Green Energy]]'s hydrogen pivot.

The parent's power-electronics expertise is directly relevant to electrolyzer manufacturing — the rectifier and power-conditioning systems between grid-connected AC supply and the electrolyzer's DC requirement are a non-trivial engineering challenge, and power electronics is Sungrow's core competence.

## The cross-sector verticalization pattern

Sungrow's product suite spans the full on-site renewable-plus-hydrogen project stack:

| Component | Sungrow product |
|---|---|
| Solar modules | Not Sungrow (partners with Longi, JinkoSolar, etc.) |
| PV inverters | Sungrow (#1-2 global) |
| Power conditioning / rectifiers | Sungrow (native expertise) |
| Battery storage | Sungrow PowerTitan/PowerStack |
| Alkaline electrolyzers | Sungrow Hydrogen |
| PEM electrolyzers | Developing |
| H2 balance-of-plant integration | Sungrow offering |

This integrated-stack approach lets Sungrow bid on complete renewable-plus-electrolyzer projects as a single EPC-style equipment supplier. Western competitors cannot match the bundle because no single Western company owns the full product range at competitive cost.

The EU Hydrogen Bank auction results from 2023-2024, where Chinese-equipment-dominant bids cleared at prices Western pure-plays could not match, are partly explained by exactly this Sungrow-style bundled offering.
