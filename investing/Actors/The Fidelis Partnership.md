**The Fidelis Partnership** — London-based specialty insurer led by CEO Richard Brindle. Operates at [[Lloyd's of London]]. Split from Fidelis Insurance Holdings (now [[Fidelis Insurance Group]], publicly listed as FIHL) in 2023, retaining the underwriting platform while FIHL kept the balance sheet. Partners with [[Blackstone]] on a [[Lloyd's of London|Lloyd's]] syndicate — Blackstone committing up to $170M over three years (Dec 2025). Brindle on the arrangement: "We do the actual underwriting and they hold the capital — they hold us to account."
