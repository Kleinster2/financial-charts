#actor #crypto #exchange

**Bitfinex** — Cryptocurrency exchange operated by iFinex Inc. (registered in Hong Kong). Shares common ownership with [[Tether]] — both controlled by [[Giancarlo Devasini]] and [[Paolo Ardoino]]. Historically one of the largest crypto exchanges by volume; subject to NYAG and CFTC enforcement alongside Tether for reserve commingling.
