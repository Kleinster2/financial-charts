**Hilco Real Estate** — real-estate advisory arm of [[Hilco Global]], focused on distressed, bankruptcy, and special-situation property transactions.

The investing relevance is not standalone scale; it is collateral intelligence. Hilco Real Estate gives [[ORIX]] and [[Hilco Global]] a property-specific workout channel when commercial real estate stress creates loan sales, bankruptcy processes, or asset dispositions.
