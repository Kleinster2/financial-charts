**Lee Hsien Loong** — Third Prime Minister of [[Singapore]] (2004-2024). Son of founding PM [[Lee Kuan Yew]]. Oversaw Singapore's financialization era: wealth management AUM grew from under $500B to over $4T during his tenure. Opened integrated resorts (2010), launched the VCC fund structure (2020), and built the family office tax incentive regime. Stepped down May 2024, succeeded by [[Lawrence Wong]]. Now serves as Senior Minister.
