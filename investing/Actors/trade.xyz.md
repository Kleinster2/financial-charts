# trade.xyz

trade.xyz is a crypto startup that builds perpetual contracts on [[Hyperliquid]]'s HIP-3 infrastructure. It emerged as the most prolific builder on the platform, developing some of the most heavily traded commodity and equity [[Perpetuals|perpetuals]] — and in March 2026 became the first entity to offer an officially licensed [[S&P 500]] derivative on a decentralized platform.

## Products

trade.xyz builds perpetual contracts tracking traditional assets on Hyperliquid:
- Commodity perpetuals: oil, gold, silver — became key weekend pricing windows during the Iran conflict
- Equity index perpetuals: [[S&P 500]] (first officially licensed, Mar 2026)
- Oil tracking contract: ~$500M daily trading volume (Mar 2026)

The S&P 500 perpetual uses a tiered data approach:
- US trading hours → official S&P index data
- Extended hours → institutional data provider quotes
- All markets closed → internal pricing model designed by trade.xyz
