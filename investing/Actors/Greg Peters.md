**Greg Peters** — Co-CEO of [[Netflix]] since January 2023. He joined Netflix in 2008 and previously served as Chief Operating Officer, Chief Product Officer, and International Development Officer. Peters is the operating executive most closely tied to product, pricing, advertising, and international scaling.
