Ross Nordeen is a co-founder of [[xAI]], [[Elon Musk]]'s AI lab. As of March 2026, he is one of only two remaining original co-founders (out of 11) alongside [[Manuel Kroiss]] — the other nine have departed, including notable names like Greg Yang (to [[Microsoft]]), Jimmy Ba, and Toby Pohlen. Before xAI, Nordeen worked at [[DeepMind]] on research infrastructure and large-scale model training. The attrition rate among xAI's founding team — losing 9 of 11 co-founders within roughly two years — is unusually high even by AI lab standards and raises questions about the internal dynamics under Musk's management style. xAI's Grok model family and the 100,000-GPU Colossus cluster in Memphis represent the technical output of the team Nordeen helped build.
