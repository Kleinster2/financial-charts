# Centrifuge

Centrifuge is a DeFi startup focused on tokenized real-world assets. In September 2025, [[S&P Global]] licensed the [[S&P 500]] to Centrifuge for a tokenized index fund on the [[Coinbase]]-developed [[Base]] blockchain — part of S&P's broader push into digital-asset infrastructure.
