# Fundstrat

Independent research firm co-founded by [[Tom Lee]] and Tom Block in 2014. Known for equity strategy, digital assets research, and the proprietary "Granny Shots" stock-picking methodology.

---

## Synopsis

Fundstrat carved its niche by combining traditional equity strategy (sector allocation, earnings models, macro calls) with early, aggressive adoption of digital asset research at a time when most Wall Street research shops dismissed crypto entirely. [[Tom Lee]] became one of the highest-profile Bitcoin bulls on the Street, calling major moves in both directions and building a media presence (CNBC regular, conference circuit) that gives Fundstrat outsized brand recognition relative to its size. The firm sells research to institutional and retail investors, with products spanning equity strategy, crypto/digital assets, technical analysis, and a data science offering.

The "Granny Shots" portfolio — a quantitative stock-selection model that screens for companies scoring well on multiple fundamental factors simultaneously — has become Fundstrat's signature product, consistently generating attention (and controversy) for its performance claims. The firm's research output tends to be high-conviction and directional, which means it moves sentiment among the retail-institutional crossover crowd that follows [[Tom Lee]]'s calls. For the vault, Fundstrat matters as a signal source: when Lee makes a public call (his 2024 Bitcoin $150K target, his persistent equity bullishness), it reflects a view that a meaningful slice of the market acts on, making it worth tracking whether or not you agree with the thesis.
