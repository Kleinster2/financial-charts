# Gymshark

UK-based digital-native athletic apparel brand, founded 2012 by Ben Francis. Private — [[General Atlantic]] took a 21% stake in 2020 at a ~$1.3B valuation. Built through social media and influencer marketing. Primarily targets gym/fitness consumers in the [[Athleisure]] space.
