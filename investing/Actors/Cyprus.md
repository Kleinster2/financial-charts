# Cyprus

Eastern Mediterranean island nation; EU member since 2004. Secondary commodity-trading domicile for firms headquartered in [[Geneva]] — notably [[Gunvor]], which holds a secondary office in Nicosia. Long-standing corporate-tax preference and Russian-facing financial infrastructure made Cyprus a common shell-company jurisdiction in the 2000s–2010s; EU-level AML tightening has narrowed that role since 2018.
