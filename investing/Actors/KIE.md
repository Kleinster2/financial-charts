# KIE

KIE (NYSE Arca: KIE) is the State Street SPDR S&P Insurance ETF — an equal-weighted basket of US insurance stocks spanning P&C insurers and reinsurers, life insurers, multi-line, and brokers. The equal weighting gives broad coverage of the insurance complex rather than concentration in a few mega-caps.

The replicability benchmark for insurance cohorts.
