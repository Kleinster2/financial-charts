Acquired — Deep-dive tech/business podcast hosted by Ben Gilbert and David Rosenthal. Episodes run 3–4 hours covering a single company's full history. Launched 2015, went full-time 2023. 1M+ listeners per episode, #1 technology show on [[Apple]] Podcasts and [[Spotify]]. Sponsors pay $500–700K per 6-month deal. Also runs Acquired Capital, a $30M investment fund.
