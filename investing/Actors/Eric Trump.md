## The Dominari relationship

Eric Trump and [[Donald Trump Jr.]] befriended [[Kyle Wool]], CEO of [[Dominari Securities]], in 2024. Both joined Dominari's advisory board in late 2024, around the same time. The brothers' combined ~12.56% stake plus advisory-board roles plus anchor-LP positions in American Ventures' SPVs make them the central marketing wedge for the [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]]. The FT reported that Eric Trump and Don Jr "are anchor investors in most of [American Ventures'] deals" and that "Republican types affiliated with them" round out each SPV with ~$500K checks.
