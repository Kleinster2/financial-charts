**Saudi Arabia** — World's largest oil exporter and de facto leader of [[OPEC]]. Undergoing economic transformation via [[Saudi Vision 2030]], funded by oil revenues and the [[Saudi PIF]]. Pivoting domestic energy mix from crude burn to gas ([[Jafurah]]) + renewables. Population ~36 million. GDP ~$1.1 trillion.

## Energy transition

Domestic power mix target: 50% renewables + 50% gas (eliminating crude burn). Key projects:
- [[Jafurah]] — flagship unconventional gas ($100B+)
- [[Ghawar]] — conventional oil mainstay (~3.8M bpd)
- NEOM renewable megaprojects
