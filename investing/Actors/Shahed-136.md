# Shahed-136

Iranian-made one-way attack drone (loitering munition) produced by Iran Aircraft Manufacturing Industrial Company (HESA). Designated Geran-2 (Герань-2) by [[Russia]], which uses a license-produced variant extensively in the [[Ukraine war]].
