# Mansur Gavriel

New York-based accessories brand known for minimalist leather goods. Made in Italy.

## Business

- **Founded:** 2012 by Floriana Gavriel and Rachel Mansur
- **Headquarters:** New York (designed), Italy (manufactured)
- **Signature:** Bucket bag — minimal hardware, clean lines
- **Private company**

## Product

| Category | Signature styles |
|----------|-----------------|
| Bags | Bucket Bag, Mini Bucket, Cloud Clutch, Gaia Shoulder |
| Shoes | Expanding category |

**Design philosophy:** Minimal, functional, no unnecessary hardware. Art and design-inspired.

## Retail

- DTC via mansurgavriel.com
- Wholesale: Saks, Nordstrom, Farfetch, Net-a-Porter

## Positioning

Fits [[Modern luxury]] and [[Designer jewelry]] adjacent:
- Accessible luxury price points
- Cult following from understated design
- Made in Italy quality
- DTC-first with selective wholesale

Early pioneer of the "It bag" without logos — predated quiet luxury trend.
