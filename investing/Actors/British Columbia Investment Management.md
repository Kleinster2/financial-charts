British Columbia Investment Management Corp (BCI) is one of [[Canada]]'s largest institutional investors, managing ~C$250B in pension, insurance, and endowment assets for public sector clients in British Columbia. Headquartered in Victoria, BC, BCI serves over 30 clients including the BC Public Service Pension Plan, Municipal Pension Plan, and WorkSafeBC, making it the third-largest pension fund manager in Canada behind [[CPP Investments]] and [[CDPQ]].

BCI's investment strategy emphasizes diversification across public equities, fixed income, real estate, infrastructure, and private equity, with a growing allocation to alternatives and private markets that mirrors the broader Canadian pension model (the "Maple Model" of direct investing). In February 2026, BCI acquired a $1.4B loan portfolio from [[Blue Owl Capital]]'s OBDC II fund amid a wave of private credit redemption pressure, demonstrating the liquidity advantage large pension funds hold when distressed sellers need exit routes.
