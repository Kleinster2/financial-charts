# ABA

American Bankers Association. Trade association representing banks of all sizes across the [[United States]]. Alongside [[ICBA]], advocates for reforming the [[Farm Credit System]] to reduce competitive advantages from FCS tax exemptions. Reports that the US banking industry held $205B in farm loans (~38% of total farm lending) at end of 2024.
