# Manuel Kroiss

Co-founder of [[xAI]], known by the handle "Makro." One of only two remaining original co-founders (out of the 11 who launched the company in July 2023) as of March 2026, alongside [[Ross Nordeen]]. The attrition rate — 9 of 11 founders departing within ~2.5 years — is extraordinary even by AI lab standards, reflecting the intensity of [[Elon Musk]]'s management style and the competitive pull from other labs. Kroiss's background is in machine learning research. Before xAI, he worked at [[DeepMind]]. His continued presence suggests he holds a meaningful equity stake and technical role within xAI's core model development team, which built [[Grok]].
