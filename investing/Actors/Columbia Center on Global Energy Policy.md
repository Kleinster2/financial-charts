# Columbia Center on Global Energy Policy

Research center at [[Columbia University]]'s School of International and Public Affairs (SIPA), focused on energy policy, markets, and geopolitics. CGEP produces some of the most policy-relevant energy research in the US, bridging academia and government on oil, gas, renewables, and energy security across major producing and consuming nations.

Founded in 2013 by Jason Bordoff (former Obama White House energy adviser). The center's work covers the energy transition, commodity markets, China's energy strategy, and US energy policy. Its scholars are frequently cited in Congressional testimony and appear at major policy forums.
