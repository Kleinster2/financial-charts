**Glashütte** — Small town in Saxony, Germany, historic center of German watchmaking since Ferdinand Adolph Lange established the industry there in 1845. Home to [[A. Lange & Söhne]], Glashütte Original ([[Swatch Group]]), NOMOS Glashütte, and [[Wempe]] Chronometerwerke. German law protects the "Glashütte" designation (movements must be at least 50% manufactured there).
