# Luis Videgaray

Former Mexican Secretary of Foreign Affairs and Secretary of Finance, now co-founder and managing partner of [[Affinity Partners]] alongside [[Jared Kushner]]. Videgaray served under President Enrique Pena Nieto — first as Secretary of Finance (2012-2016), where he managed Mexico's fiscal policy during the oil price crash, then as Foreign Minister (2017-2018), where he became the key Mexican interlocutor with the incoming Trump administration. He developed a close personal relationship with Kushner during the NAFTA renegotiation ([[USMCA]]) talks, which became the foundation for their post-government partnership. At [[Affinity Partners]], Videgaray focuses on Latin American deal sourcing and investor relations, leveraging his network across [[Mexico]], the Gulf states, and Washington.
