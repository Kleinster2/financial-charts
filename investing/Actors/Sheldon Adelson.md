**Sheldon Adelson** (d. January 11, 2021, age 87, non-Hodgkin lymphoma) — founder of [[Las Vegas Sands]]. Republican mega-donor ($500M+ to campaigns). Built the Venetian Las Vegas, pioneered the Cotai Strip concept in [[Macau]], created [[Marina Bay Sands]]. Net worth ~$33B at peak.
