#actor #vc #private #uae

Alpha Wave Global — Abu Dhabi-based growth-equity and venture investment platform, formerly known as Falcon Edge Capital. Multi-asset, multi-stage, with concentrated bets across AI infrastructure, fintech, and emerging-market technology. Disclosed as the fifth-largest holder of [[Cerebras]] Class B common at the May 13, 2026 IPO pricing, with a 6.5% stake — a position the May 14 Day-1 close marked at roughly $6.2B fully diluted.

Not to be confused with [[Alphawave Semi]], the British semiconductor-IP company; the names overlap but the entities are unrelated.

## Cerebras position (May 2026 IPO)

Alpha Wave Global's 6.5% stake in [[Cerebras]] Class B implied ~$3.67B in disclosed equity value at the $185 IPO price (6.5% × $56.4B fully diluted), rising to ~$6.16B at the May 14 Day-1 close of $311.07. The firm's presence on the disclosed top-5 holders list is the only Gulf-region position visible on the cap table — [[UAE G42]] and [[MBZUAI]], despite their 86% combined share of 2025 revenue, are notably absent from the equity register. That is the clean separation of customer concentration from equity-side governance documented in [[Cerebras IPO revival April 2026]].
