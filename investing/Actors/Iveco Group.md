**Iveco Group** — Italian commercial vehicle manufacturer spun off from [[CNH Industrial]] in January 2022. Produces trucks (IVECO brand), buses, specialty/defense vehicles, and powertrains. Being acquired by [[Tata Motors]] (a [[Tata Group]] subsidiary) for ~EUR 3.8B in an all-cash deal announced July 2025, expected to close Q2 2026. Defense business (Iveco Defence Vehicles, Astra brand) sold separately to [[Leonardo]] for EUR 1.7B.

The Daily light commercial vehicle line holds ~40% of Europe's electric light CV market, compared to [[Ford]]'s 23% — a key zero-emission asset in the deal.

## Evolution

- 1975 — IVECO formed as merger of Fiat's truck operations (OM, Lancia Veicoli Speciali) with several European truck makers
- Fiat era — operated under Fiat Group umbrella for decades, built European truck/bus franchise
- Jan 2022 — spun off from [[CNH Industrial]] as independent listed company on Euronext Milan
- Jul 2025 — [[Tata Motors]] announces EUR 3.8B all-cash acquisition; defense unit sold to [[Leonardo]] for EUR 1.7B
- Oct 2025 — Italy grants conditional golden power approval (Turin HQ, employment protections)
- Q2 2026 — expected close, creating EUR 22B combined commercial vehicle group
