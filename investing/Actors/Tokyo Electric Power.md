# Tokyo Electric Power

[[Japan]]'s largest electric utility. Operator of the Fukushima Daiichi nuclear plant (2011 disaster). Co-owner (50%) of [[JERA]], Japan's largest power generation company (JV with [[Chubu Electric Power]]). JERA is a key player in ammonia co-firing for coal power decarbonization.
