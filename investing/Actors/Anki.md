Anki — Consumer robotics company known for Cozmo and Vector robots. Co-founded by Boris Sofman, Mark Palatucci, and Hanns Tappeiner (all CMU Robotics PhDs). Raised ~$200M total before shutting down in April 2019 due to failed acquisition deal.
