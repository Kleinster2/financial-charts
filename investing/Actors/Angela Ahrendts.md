**Angela Ahrendts** was SVP of Retail at [[Apple]] from 2014 to 2019. Previously CEO of [[Burberry]]. An external hire brought in to reimagine Apple retail, she departed after five years -- a cautionary example of outside executives struggling to embed at Apple.
