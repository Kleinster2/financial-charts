#actor #reference #fixed-income

Complete inventory of [[Amazon]] outstanding bonds. All bonds are senior unsecured. Amazon filed a new shelf registration (S-3ASR) in Feb 2026, signaling additional issuance likely.

Other credit facilities: $15B unsecured revolving credit (Nov 2028, undrawn); $5B short-term revolving (Oct 2026, undrawn); $30B commercial paper program ($0 outstanding, expanded from $20B in Apr 2025).
