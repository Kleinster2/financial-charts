# Hendrick's

**Scottish gin brand** — owned by [[William Grant & Sons]] (privately held, family-owned). Distilled at the Girvan distillery in Ayrshire, Scotland. Distinctive for its cucumber-and-rose botanical profile and apothecary-style bottle. One of the top premium gin brands globally. Used as a spirit partner by [[Aubi & Ramsa]] (Choco Hazelnut Martini flavor).
