# Kinetic Partners

**Kinetic Partners** is an investment firm that participates in large-scale private technology rounds, most notably as a participant in [[SpaceX]]'s June 2022 equity raise.

## Synopsis

Kinetic Partners operates in the institutional allocation space for high-demand private tech rounds, where access matters more than sourcing. The firm participated in [[SpaceX]]'s June 2022 equity round ($1.68B at ~$127B valuation), a deal led by [[Mirae Asset]]. At that valuation, SpaceX was already the most valuable private company globally, and the round attracted a mix of sovereign wealth, family offices, and specialized tech investors.

The firm is relevant to the vault primarily through its SpaceX exposure. In the private markets, SpaceX rounds function as a liquidity barometer — the investor lists reveal which institutions are gaining access to the most capital-constrained deal flow. Kinetic's inclusion alongside [[Mirae Asset]], [[Space.VC]], and other participants signals the firm's positioning in the institutional private-tech allocation network.
