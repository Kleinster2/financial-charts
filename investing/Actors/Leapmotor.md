## Apr 2026 automotive AI context

[[Reuters]] named Leapmotor among the Chinese EV companies designing their own chips to reduce reliance on [[NVIDIA]]. That places the company inside the broader [[Automotive AI]] transition from battery-and-assembly competition toward vehicle operating systems, smart-driving models, and captive AI silicon.

Leapmotor was also cited as one of the domestic competitors squeezing [[BYD]]'s China sales momentum as the EV price war intensifies.
