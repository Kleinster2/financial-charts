#actor #company #utility #water #sanitation #brazil #public

**Sabesp** (Companhia de Saneamento Básico do Estado de São Paulo) is Brazil's largest water and sanitation utility, serving most of São Paulo state. It trades on B3 as `SBSP3` and on the NYSE as the ADR `SBS`.

In July 2024 the São Paulo state government privatized Sabesp, cutting its holding to a minority in a landmark follow-on offering; [[Equatorial]] came in as the strategic anchor with a ~15% reference stake (R$6.9B). The investment case is the post-privatization efficiency story — pulling the universalization-of-sanitation capex plan forward to 2029 while cutting the losses and cost base a state operator carried.
