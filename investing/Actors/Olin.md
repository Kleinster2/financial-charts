## Lake City Army Ammunition Plant

Olin operates for US Army:
- Largest small-caliber facility in US
- 1.6B rounds/year capacity
- Long-term contract
- Expansion underway

## Investment thesis

**Bull:**
- Ammunition demand surge (rearmament)
- Lake City monopoly position
- Winchester brand strength
- Integrated chemicals/defense

**Bear:**
- Chemicals cyclicality dominates
- Epoxy weakness
- Commodity exposure
- Small defense revenue share
- ESG concerns (ammunition)
