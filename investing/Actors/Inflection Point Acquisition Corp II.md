Inflection Point Acquisition Corp II — Special purpose acquisition company (SPAC) that sponsored the March 2025 business combination taking [[USA Rare Earth]] public on NASDAQ under ticker USAR. Affiliates of the SPAC sponsor anchored USAR's subsequent $1.5B [[PIPE financing]] in January 2026, reinforcing their role as ongoing backers of the USAR capital structure through the company's 2026 industrial-policy build-out.
