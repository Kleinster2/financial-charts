# TA Associates

American growth-oriented private equity firm founded 1968 by Peter Brooke. One of the earliest modern PE firms. $65B total capital raised. Latest flagship fund TA XV closed at $16.5B hard cap (June 2023, oversubscribed). Invests $70M–$500M per transaction in companies valued $100M–$3B. Five sectors: technology, healthcare, consumer, financial services, business services. ~219 employees, 50 partners, 431 active portfolio companies. HQ in Boston (200 Clarendon St, 56th floor). CEO Ajit Nedungadi (since 2021). Ranked #19 in PEI 300 (2024). Named Mid-Market Firm of the Year in Americas (2025).
