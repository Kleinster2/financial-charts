# Robert F. Kennedy Jr.

**Robert F. Kennedy Jr.** — US Secretary of Health and Human Services (HHS) under [[Trump II]] (confirmed February 2025), running the "Make America Healthy Again" (MAHA) agenda. Environmental lawyer (Natural Resources Defense Council, Waterkeeper Alliance founder) turned vaccine-skeptic activist (founder, Children's Health Defense) — a politically atypical HHS secretary whose tenure has paired deregulatory signals on the [[FDA]] with the administration's pharma-pricing and burden-sharing trade posture.
