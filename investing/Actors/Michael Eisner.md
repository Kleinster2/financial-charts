Michael Eisner — long-serving CEO of [[Disney]] (1984-2005). His late tenure was rocked by board ructions, investor unrest, and a hostile takeover attempt by Comcast; the Disney-Pixar relationship with [[Steve Jobs]] disintegrated on his watch. [[Bob Iger]] was his COO before succeeding him in 2005. [[Steven Spielberg]] recalled Disney under Eisner as a backwater for anything but G-rated fare.
