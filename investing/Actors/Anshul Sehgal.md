## Why he matters

Sehgal is one of the clearest personifications of Goldman's rates culture. Before taking over the broader FICC role, he ran US interest-rate products and had earlier served in rates and volatility trading roles. The April 2026 FT follow-up on Goldman's weak quarter made his business especially important because it identified the rates desk as the main source of underperformance inside FICC.
