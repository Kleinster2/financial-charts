## Bitcoin strategy

**Jack Dorsey is a Bitcoin maximalist:**
- Cash App Bitcoin buying/selling
- Bitcoin on balance sheet
- TBD building Bitcoin infrastructure
- Spiral funding Bitcoin open-source
- Mining hardware development

**Thesis:** Bitcoin becomes internet's native currency, Block is the on-ramp.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Two strong businesses (Square + Cash App)
- Bitcoin infrastructure play
- SMB payments moat
- Cash App growth
- Dorsey's vision (long-term)

**Bear:**
- Bitcoin volatility affects earnings
- SMB competition ([[Toast]], Clover)
- Cash App competition (Venmo, Zelle)
- Dorsey distraction (Twitter/X history)
- Valuation premium
