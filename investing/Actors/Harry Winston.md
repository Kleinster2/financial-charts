# Harry Winston

Iconic American high jewelry and watch house, now owned by [[Swatch Group]] (acquired 2013 for ~$1B). Known as the original "King of Diamonds." Competes directly with [[Graff]] in ultra-high-end diamond jewelry. ~$1B estimated revenue.
