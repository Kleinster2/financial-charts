# Kim Kardashian

Kim Kardashian is a media personality and entrepreneur with ~365M Instagram followers, making her one of the most commercially valuable personal brands in the world. She co-founded [[SKIMS]] (shapewear and apparel) in 2019, which reached a $4B+ valuation by 2023 — the most successful celebrity-to-brand venture in history by valuation. She also founded KKW Beauty (later relaunched as SKKN by Kim, a skincare line).

Kardashian's business trajectory is the positive case study in the [[Influencer-to-brand playbook]], contrasting sharply with [[Jessica Alba]]'s [[Honest Company]] ($1.4B IPO → $200M market cap). The difference: [[SKIMS]] built genuine product differentiation (inclusive sizing, technical fabrics) and stayed private long enough to scale without public-market scrutiny. Kardashian also leveraged her platform for a private equity partnership — [[Carlyle]] invested at a $4B valuation — rather than rushing to IPO. Beyond business, she has pursued criminal justice reform advocacy, studying for the California bar exam and lobbying for presidential commutations, which broadened her public profile beyond entertainment.
