# Porsche

German luxury sports car and SUV manufacturer. Unlike [[Ferrari]]'s scarcity model, Porsche pursues volume (~300,000+ units/year). Majority owned by [[Volkswagen]] (75% of ordinary shares). IPO'd Sept 2022 at ~€75B valuation. Revenue ~€40B (2024). Cayenne and Macan SUVs drive volume; 911 is the brand icon.
