# Bruno Corano

Based: New York City (Times Square studio) / São Paulo
Born: São Paulo, SP
Education: University of Pennsylvania (economics undergrad), Harvard Business School (specialization), plus coursework at Columbia and MIT
Career: ~30 years in markets and business; ~17 years racing motorcycles in parallel

Brazilian economist, entrepreneur, and investor based in NYC. Runs [[Corano Capital]] (vault-stated $2B AUM from 2023 Exame profile; not freshly re-verified). Hosts the Wall Street Cast videocast (originated Jul 2023 on YouTube/podcast; premiered on [[BM&C News]] 2025-08-28, Thursdays 8 PM) and serves as executive producer + 2026-season presenter of the revived Manhattan Connection on [[BM&C News]]. Also a former professional motorcycle racer — three-time SuperBikes champion (2009, 2010, 2012). An unusual profile that bridges Brazilian finance, media, motorsport, and the NYC Brazilian business community.

## Background

### Motorsport career (championship era 2009-2012; full pilot career ~17 years)
- Three-time SuperBikes champion (2009, 2010, 2012) — confirmed by SuperBike Brasil's official site as "tricampeão da modalidade máxima do motociclismo nacional"; vault previously stated four-time, corrected 2026-05-14
- Official [[Kawasaki]] Brazil pilot during championship era (Elf Monster Energy Kawasaki team)
- Founder/operator of multiple motorsport businesses: BMW Rider Experience, [[SuperBike Brasil]] (founded 2010, administered by Corano since founding), MotoSchool, APM, Ohlins Brasil, Junior Cup Brasil, MotoTest
- Chief instructor of the MotoSchool Piloting Center
- President of the Association of Motorcycle Racing Pilots
- Created the Junior Cup — a youth development program for motorcycle racing
- Still actively involved in motorsport businesses

### Finance career
- From a family of entrepreneurs (grandfather was a lawyer, businessman, and Secretary of Finance for São Paulo state)
- Built career between Brazil and US over ~30 years
- Founded Corano Capital in New York
- Penn and Harvard credentials
- Focus on connecting Brazilian capital with US investment opportunities

### Media career
- Built a studio near Times Square for content production
- Launched Wall Street Cast (Jul 2023) — weekly videocast with Brazilian guests in NYC
- Produced and financed the Manhattan Connection revival (Nov 2023)
- Regular commentator on Brazilian financial media
