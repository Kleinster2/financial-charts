# Bruno Corano

Based: New York City (Times Square studio) / São Paulo
Born: São Paulo, SP
Education: University of Pennsylvania (undergrad), Harvard Business School (specialization)
Career: ~30 years in markets and business

Brazilian economist, entrepreneur, and investor based in NYC. Runs [[Corano Capital]] ($2B AUM), hosts the Wall Street Cast videocast and the revived Manhattan Connection on [[BM&C News]]. Also a former professional motorcycle racer — four-time SuperBikes champion. An unusual profile that bridges Brazilian finance, media, motorsport, and the NYC Brazilian business community.

## Background

### Motorsport career (2009-2017)
- Four-time SuperBikes champion (Brazil's premier motorcycle racing series)
- Founder/operator of multiple motorsport businesses: BMW Rider Experience, SuperBike Brasil, MotoSchool, APM, Ohlins Brasil, Junior Cup Brasil, MotoTest
- Created the Junior Cup — a youth development program for motorcycle racing
- Still involved in motorsport businesses

### Finance career
- From a family of entrepreneurs (grandfather was a lawyer, businessman, and Secretary of Finance for São Paulo state)
- Built career between Brazil and US over ~30 years
- Founded Corano Capital in New York
- Penn and Harvard credentials
- Focus on connecting Brazilian capital with US investment opportunities

### Media career
- Built a studio near Times Square for content production
- Launched Wall Street Cast (Jul 2023) — weekly videocast with Brazilian guests in NYC
- Produced and financed the Manhattan Connection revival (Nov 2023)
- Regular commentator on Brazilian financial media
