# Bruno Corano

Based: New York City (Times Square studio) / São Paulo
Born: São Paulo, SP
Education: University of Pennsylvania (economics undergrad), Harvard Business School (specialization), plus coursework at Columbia and MIT
Career: ~30 years in markets and business; ~17 years racing motorcycles in parallel

Brazilian economist, entrepreneur, and investor based in NYC. Runs [[Corano Capital]] (~$2.2B AUM as of September 2025, refreshed 2026-05-18; described in Brazilian press as the largest Brazilian private wealth manager in the United States, with ~90% of clients from Brazil and ~10% from the US). Hosts the Wall Street Cast videocast (originated Jul 2023 on YouTube/podcast; premiered on [[BM&C News]] 2025-08-28, Thursdays 8 PM) and serves as executive producer + 2026-season presenter of the revived Manhattan Connection on [[BM&C News]]. Also a former professional motorcycle racer — three-time SuperBikes champion (2009, 2010, 2012). An unusual profile that bridges Brazilian finance, media, motorsport, and the NYC Brazilian business community.

## Corano Capital — structure, partner & US regulatory footprint (verified 2026-06-24)

Structure (per Corano's 2025-09-17 Capital Aberto "Além do Capital" interview): two arms under the Corano brand, both rooted in a Brazilian corporate entity (CNPJ) Corano set up in 2004 when he inherited the investment book of mentor [[José Aurélio de Camargo]] ("Zé Aurélio," d. 2004), including a legacy private-equity fund.

| Arm | Base | Clients | AUM |
|---|---|---|---|
| Corano Capital (NY) | New York | Brazilian + foreign HNW | bulk of ~US$2.2B |
| Corano (São Paulo) | São Paulo | local Brazilian, mass-affluent | ~R$480M (~US$90M) |

The São Paulo arm is a family office built by [[Daniel Faria]] and merged in ~2023–2024 to reach a "perfil menos exigente" (mass-affluent) tier — the vehicle for the down-market strategy below. Partner [[Danilo Santiago]] runs one of the firm's in-house equity strategies. [[Interactive Brokers]] is the named "principal parceiro" — the custody and execution layer giving global asset access.

Down-market strategy (same interview, headline "levar carteiras dos super ricos para clientes com menos capital replicarem"): broaden from UHNW family clients to mass-affluent Brazilians who want USD/global exposure, using Corano's social-media and podcast presence as the inbound funnel and a "replicate the model portfolio" product. The Wall Street Cast / Manhattan Connection / Carteira Aberta media layer is the top of that funnel.

## Background

### Motorsport career (championship era 2009-2012; full pilot career ~17 years)
- Three-time SuperBikes champion (2009, 2010, 2012) — confirmed by SuperBike Brasil's official site as "tricampeão da modalidade máxima do motociclismo nacional"; vault previously stated four-time, corrected 2026-05-14
- Official [[Kawasaki]] Brazil pilot during championship era (Elf Monster Energy Kawasaki team)
- Founder/operator of multiple motorsport businesses: BMW Rider Experience, [[SuperBike Brasil]] (founded 2010, administered by Corano since founding), MotoSchool, APM, Ohlins Brasil, Junior Cup Brasil, MotoTest
- Chief instructor of the MotoSchool Piloting Center
- President of the Association of Motorcycle Racing Pilots
- Created the Junior Cup — a youth development program for motorcycle racing
- Still actively involved in motorsport businesses

### Finance career
- From a family of entrepreneurs (grandfather was a lawyer, businessman, and Secretary of Finance for São Paulo state)
- Built career between Brazil and US over ~30 years
- Founded Corano Capital in New York
- Penn and Harvard credentials
- Focus on connecting Brazilian capital with US investment opportunities

### Media career
- Built a studio near Times Square for content production
- Launched Wall Street Cast (Jul 2023) — weekly videocast with Brazilian guests in NYC
- Produced and financed the Manhattan Connection revival (Nov 2023)
- Regular commentator on Brazilian financial media
