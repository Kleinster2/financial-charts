## Constitutional shift

Historic changes under Kishida/current government:

| Policy | Change |
|--------|--------|
| Defense budget | 1% → **2% GDP** target |
| Counterstrike | Now permitted (missiles) |
| Arms exports | Three principles relaxed |
| AUKUS | Pillar 2 technology sharing |

## Investment thesis

**Bull:**
- Structural budget doubling
- Constitutional constraints lifting
- China threat = sustained demand
- Quality industrial base

**Bear:**
- Defense = small % of conglomerate revenue
- Pacifist culture resistance
- Slow procurement process
- Limited pure-play options
