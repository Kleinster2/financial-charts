# Gordon Bajnai

Gordon Bajnai is CEO of [[Campbell Lutyens]] and, on close of the [[Lazard]] acquisition announced 2026-04-30, becomes co-CEO of Lazard CL alongside [[Holcombe Green]]. Bajnai previously served as Prime Minister of Hungary (April 2009 - May 2010), bridging the Ferenc Gyurcsány government and the return of Viktor Orbán's Fidesz, and led Hungary's IMF-EU bailout negotiations during the global financial crisis.
