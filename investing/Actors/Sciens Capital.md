## Regulatory

**SEC settlement (May 2023):**
- Charged with compliance failures on fund valuation procedures
- Violated Section 206(4) of Investment Advisers Act
- Penalty: $275,000
- Required to retain independent compliance consultant

Not a fraud case — procedural compliance issue on NAV calculations for illiquid assets.
