# Conference Board

A nonprofit business membership and research organization founded in 1916. Publishes the Consumer Confidence Index (monthly, ~3,000 households surveyed), the Leading Economic Index (LEI), and other widely followed macro indicators. Consumer Confidence emphasizes labor market conditions more than the [[Consumer sentiment|University of Michigan]] survey.
