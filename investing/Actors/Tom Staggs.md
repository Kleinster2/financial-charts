Tom Staggs — former senior [[Disney]] executive (COO; previously parks chief). Once regarded as a leading successor candidate to [[Bob Iger]] before departing. Listed by the FT (Jun 2026) among the senior executives whose departures were "casualties" of Iger's rolling tenure at the top. Later co-founded Candle Media with [[Kevin Mayer]].
