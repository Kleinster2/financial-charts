# Quanta Computer

World's #2 AI server assembly contractor (~17% global market share). Taiwan-based ODM serving all four major CSPs ([[Microsoft]], [[Amazon]], [[Google]], [[Meta]]). FY2025 revenue NT$2.124T (+50.5% YoY) — record, driven by AI server ramp. Plans to at least double AI server production capacity by end of 2026.
