Charlie Kirk — rightwing US political activist and commentator. The target of a [[Jimmy Kimmel]] joke whose aftermath drew FCC pressure on Disney: Disney took Kimmel off the air and asked him to apologize, hours after an FCC threat (FT, Jun 2026). [[Bob Iger]] said the move was about taste, not politics.
