**Marina Bay Sands** — flagship Singapore integrated resort owned and operated by [[Las Vegas Sands]]. Arguably the world's most profitable casino property -- FY2025 revenue $5.59B, EBITDA $2.92B. ~72% Singapore casino market share. $8B expansion (IR2) under construction, targeting 2031.
