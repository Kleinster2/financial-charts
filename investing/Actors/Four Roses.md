# Four Roses

**Kentucky bourbon distillery** — owned by [[Kirin Holdings]] (Japan) since 2002. Located in Lawrenceburg, KY. Known for using 10 distinct bourbon recipes (2 mash bills x 5 yeast strains), producing a range from the standard Yellow Label to the premium Single Barrel and Small Batch Select. Used as a spirit partner by [[Aubi & Ramsa]] (Kentucky Creme Brulee flavor).
