# Steel Dynamics

**Steel Dynamics (STLD)** — a top-three US steelmaker on the electric-arc-furnace (EAF) mini-mill model, headquartered in Fort Wayne, IN; NASDAQ-listed. Three segments: steel mills (flat-roll, long products), metals recycling (OmniSource), and steel fabrication, with the new Sinton, TX flat-roll mill the recent growth driver. Notably also building primary aluminum capacity (a Columbus, MS rolling mill) — a steelmaker stepping into the cohort's other metal. With [[Nucor]] it forms the tight EAF mini-mill pair (0.845) at the core of the [[Steel and aluminum equity beta]] cohort.
