# Farmer Mac

Federal Agricultural Mortgage Corporation. GSE that provides a secondary market for agricultural real estate and rural housing mortgage loans. Ticker: AGM on NYSE. Regulated by [[Farm Credit Administration]]. Holds ~3% of total US agricultural debt.
