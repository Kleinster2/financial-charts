**Piaget** — Swiss luxury watchmaker and jeweler, founded 1874 in La Côte-aux-Fées. Owned by [[Richemont]]. Known for ultra-thin movements (pioneered since 1957) and high jewelry watches. Operates both a movement manufacture and a jewelry/dial atelier in Geneva.
