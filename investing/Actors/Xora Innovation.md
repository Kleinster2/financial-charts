Xora Innovation is a [[venture capital]] firm focused on deep tech and robotics investments, notable as a backer of [[Bedrock Robotics]] and other frontier hardware startups.

## Synopsis

Xora Innovation operates in the deep tech VC segment — investing in companies building physical-world technology (robotics, advanced materials, industrial automation) rather than pure software plays. The firm backs early-stage companies where the technical risk is high but the defensibility (via patents, hardware IP, and manufacturing know-how) is correspondingly strong. [[Bedrock Robotics]] is among its notable portfolio companies, placing Xora in the growing cohort of VCs betting on the [[humanoid robotics]] and industrial automation wave.

The deep tech VC segment has attracted increased capital since 2023 as investors recognized that AI's physical-world applications (robotics, autonomous systems, energy) require patient capital and hardware expertise that generalist software VCs lack. Firms like Xora compete with larger deep tech investors such as [[Lux Capital]], Playground Global, and DCVC for deals in robotics and automation. The differentiation for smaller firms is typically domain expertise and willingness to invest at the earliest stages — pre-revenue, pre-prototype — where the due diligence is fundamentally about evaluating the science rather than the traction metrics that software VCs rely on.
