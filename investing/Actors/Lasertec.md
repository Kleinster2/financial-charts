# Lasertec

Lasertec (Tokyo: 6920.T) is the Japanese semiconductor-equipment company with a near-monopoly on EUV photomask and mask-blank inspection systems — the actinic (EUV-wavelength) mask inspection tools (ACTIS) that [[TSMC]], [[Samsung]] and [[Intel]] need to qualify defect-free EUV masks. A pure semiconductor-equipment / metrology name (not a materials maker), and one of the most volatile, highest-multiple EUV-cycle beneficiaries on the Tokyo exchange. Grouped with [[Tokyo Electron]] as Japanese semi equipment — a distinct factor from the Japan materials names.
