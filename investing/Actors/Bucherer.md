**Bucherer** — Swiss luxury watch and jewelry retailer, founded 1888. Acquired by [[Rolex]] in August 2023, making Rolex the world's largest vertically integrated watch retailer. The acquisition reshaped the competitive landscape for independent multi-brand retailers like [[Wempe]] and [[Watches of Switzerland]].
