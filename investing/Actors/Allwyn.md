#actor #private #lottery #gambling

**Allwyn** — World's largest lottery operator by revenue, controlled by Czech billionaire Karel Komarek's KKCG. Holds lottery licenses in UK (Camelot), Czech Republic, Austria, [[Greece]] (via [[OPAP]]), and Illinois. 36.75% minority stake in [[Betano]]. Merging with [[OPAP]] in EUR 16B deal to create world's 2nd-largest listed gambling company.
