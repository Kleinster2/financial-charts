**Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL)** — US [[DOE]] national laboratory in Livermore, California. Home to the [[National Ignition Facility]], where fusion ignition was first achieved (Dec 2022). Major programs in nuclear weapons stewardship, fusion energy, supercomputing, and national security.
