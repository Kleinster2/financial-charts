**Council of Institutional Investors** — nonprofit association of asset owners, asset managers, and related organizations. Primary advocacy: "one share, one vote" corporate governance standard. Lobbies against [[Dual-class shares]] and for mandatory sunset provisions.

---

## Key positions

- Dual-class shares should include mandatory time-based sunset provisions (7 years or fewer post-IPO)
- One share, one vote as core governance principle
- Opposes non-voting shares (e.g. [[Snap]] Class A)
- Pressed S&P to exclude dual-class from indices (S&P banned 2017, reversed April 2023)
