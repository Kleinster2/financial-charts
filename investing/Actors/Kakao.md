**Kakao** — Korean internet conglomerate. KakaoTalk messaging monopoly, Kakao Brain AI, fintech, mobility, entertainment.

---

## Why Kakao matters

Kakao is Korea's dominant consumer internet platform:

| Role | Details |
|------|---------|
| Messaging | KakaoTalk ~95% Korea penetration |
| AI research | Kakao Brain LLM development |
| [[Fintech]] | KakaoBank, KakaoPay |
| Mobility | Kakao Mobility (ride-hail) |

---

## KakaoTalk

**Korea's messaging monopoly:**

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| MAU | 50M+ (Korea ~97% reach) |
| Position | Near-total messaging dominance |
| Features | Chat, payments, commerce, news |
| Moat | [[Network effects]], ecosystem |

Unlike US (fragmented messaging), Korea consolidated on KakaoTalk.

## Kakao Brain (AI research)

**Korean LLM development:**

| Model | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| KoGPT | Korean language model |
| Karlo | Text-to-image |
| Various | Korean NLP research |

**Challenge:** Competing with US labs on smaller budget. Focus on Korean language/culture.

**Restructuring:** Kakao Brain folded into Kakao Corp (2023) amid cost cutting.
