## Investment thesis

BEV targets technologies that can reduce >500M tons CO2/year:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Clean power | Nuclear, geothermal, fusion |
| Storage | Long-duration batteries |
| Transport | EVs, sustainable aviation |
| Industry | Green steel, cement |
| Agriculture | Alternative proteins |

Only backs "hard tech" — not software or incremental improvements.

## Investment angle

**Bull:**
- Best-connected climate investor
- Patient capital structure
- Portfolio diversification
- Hyperscaler relationships
- Policy influence

**Bear:**
- Private (can't invest directly)
- Long timelines
- Many bets will fail
- Public portfolio companies mixed results

**Exposure**: Public portfolio companies (QS, LILM) or downstream beneficiaries.
