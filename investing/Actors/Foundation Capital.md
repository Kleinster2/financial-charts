## Cerebras position (May 2026 IPO)

Foundation Capital's 8.3% stake in [[Cerebras]] Class B at the $185 IPO price implied ~$4.68B in disclosed equity value (8.3% × $56.4B). At the May 14 Day-1 close of $311.07, the implied mark moved to ~$7.87B fully diluted. The firm sat ahead of [[Eclipse Ventures]] (7.3%) and [[Alpha Wave Global]] (6.5%) on the disclosed top-5 holders list, behind [[Fidelity]] (11.3%) and [[Benchmark]] (9.5%).
