JFB Construction Holdings Inc. (Nasdaq: JFB) — New York-based construction company serving as the listed shell for the [[XTEND]] reverse merger announced February 17, 2026. The combined entity is expected to trade as XTND on Nasdaq following an expected mid-2026 close at a $1.5bn deal valuation. JFB stock jumped ~14% on the merger announcement.
