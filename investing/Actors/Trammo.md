# Trammo

The world's largest independent ammonia trader. Headquartered in New York. Handles physical trading, shipping, and logistics for [[Ammonia]], sulfur, and other chemicals. Key intermediary in global ammonia trade flows between producers in the Middle East, Trinidad, and Russia and consumers globally.
