Fundrise Innovation Fund — publicly traded venture-capital fund (NYSE: VCX), sponsored and managed by [[Fundrise]]. Listed on [[NYSE]] under VCX on Mar 19, 2026, giving retail investors access to private AI/tech companies — one of the first and largest public venture funds on a major U.S. exchange. The fund is the tradeable security here; [[Fundrise]] is the platform company that runs it.

![[vcx-price-chart.png]]

First-day action was wild — NAV ~$19/share, opened ~$31, briefly spiked to $125, closed at $76. By early April trading around $112, a massive premium to NAV reflecting demand for private AI exposure in a public wrapper.
