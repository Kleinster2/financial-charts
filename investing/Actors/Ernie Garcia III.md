## The turnaround

**2022 crisis:**
- Stock down 98% from peak
- $9B+ debt from ADESA acquisition
- Layoffs, cost cuts
- Creditor negotiations

**2023-24 recovery:**
- $1B+ expenses removed
- GPU (gross profit per unit) focus
- ADESA integration working
- Stock recovered dramatically

## Governance questions

[[Gotham City Research]] (Jan 2026) raised concerns about father-son structure:
- Father's entities ([[DriveTime]], Bridgecrest) transact with Carvana
- Loans flow between related parties
- Same auditor (Grant Thornton) across all entities

Garcia III has defended related-party transactions as arm's-length and disclosed.
