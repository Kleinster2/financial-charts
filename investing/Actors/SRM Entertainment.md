#actor #company #public #crypto

SRM Entertainment — Nasdaq-listed merchandise / toy supplier (licensing deals with Disney, Universal Studios, SeaWorld) that became the listed shell for the [[Tron]] reverse merger arranged by [[Dominari Securities]] in mid-2025. After the deal closed in July 2025 the company rebranded as Tron Inc. and adopted the ticker TRON; it is now the largest publicly-traded holder of TRX tokens with a treasury of 365M+ TRX. The first major deployment of the [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]] mechanic.

## Vault relevance

SRM is the historical first deployment of the structural mechanic later operationalized at scale by [[American Ventures]]. The deal predates American Ventures' principal SPV-factory phase — Dominari arranged the SRM/Tron transaction directly in mid-2025, and subsequent pipeline deals (XTEND/JFB, Powerus/AGH, Cove Kaz/SKBL, Thumzup/Dogehash) route through American Ventures' series-numbered SPVs.

The deal's scale (+460% stock pop, $140M post-deal market cap) and the treasury-strategy framing (large publicly-traded crypto-token holder) became templates for subsequent crypto-treasury reverse mergers (see [[Crypto-to-AI pivot]] and adjacent treasury-vehicle structures).
