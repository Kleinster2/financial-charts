**Ana Botín** — Executive Chair of [[Santander]], [[Europe]]'s largest bank by market cap. Daughter of Emilio Botín, succeeded her father in 2014. Oversaw a 2025-2026 M&A spree: sold [[Bank Polska]], acquired [[Webster Financial]] (US) and [[TSB]] (UK). 2025 compensation €15M. Bitter rivalry with [[Andrea Orcel]] whom she tried to hire as CEO then rescinded the appointment — €43.4M Spanish court compensation award, Supreme Court ruling expected early 2027.
