#actor #crypto #bitcoin #cypherpunk #uk

**Adam Back** — British cryptographer and cypherpunk. Invented Hashcash (1997), the proof-of-work system used in Bitcoin mining. CEO of [[Blockstream]]. One of few people cited in Satoshi Nakamoto's Bitcoin whitepaper.

## Blockstream

Co-founded 2014. Built to $3.2B valuation. Products include Liquid Network, hardware wallets, mining infrastructure.

---

## Bitcoin Standard Treasury (2025)

New venture merging with Cantor Equity Partners I (SPAC). Brandon Lutnick-chaired. Potential $3B+ Bitcoin treasury deal (~30,000 BTC).

---

## Epstein connection

Epstein invested $500K in Blockstream's 2014 seed via Kyara Investments III. Back stated the stake was sold months later and Blockstream has "no direct nor indirect financial connection" to Epstein or his estate.
