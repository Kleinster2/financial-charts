# National Storage Affiliates

National Storage Affiliates (NYSE: NSA) is a mid-cap US self-storage REIT with a differentiated participating-regional-operator (PRO) structure, concentrated in secondary and tertiary markets. The smallest of the public self-storage names. A member of the [[Self-storage REITs]] cohort.
