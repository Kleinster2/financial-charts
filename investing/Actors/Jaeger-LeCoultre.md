**Jaeger-LeCoultre** — Swiss luxury watchmaker, founded 1833 in Le Sentier (Vallée de Joux). Owned by [[Richemont]]. Known as the "watchmaker's watchmaker" for supplying movements to other houses. Created the Reverso (1931), one of the most iconic watch designs. Over 1,400 calibres developed.
