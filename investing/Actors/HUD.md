#actor #government #usa

**HUD** — the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development, the federal agency for housing policy, FHA mortgage insurance, and community development. Its investing relevance runs through housing-supply policy: the [[21st Century ROAD to Housing Act]] directs HUD to issue guidance to localities on zoning and land-use reform to reduce barriers to building.
