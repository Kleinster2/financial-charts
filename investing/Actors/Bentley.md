**Bentley** — British luxury car manufacturer, subsidiary of [[Volkswagen]] Group since 1998. HQ Crewe, England. Produces Continental GT, Flying Spur, Bentayga. ~15,000 vehicles/year. UAE is key market — [[Al Habtoor Group]] operates world's largest Bentley showroom.
