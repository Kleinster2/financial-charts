**Playa Vista** is a ~1,000-acre master-planned community in West LA on the former Howard Hughes Aircraft Company land near Marina del Rey, developed by Playa Capital Co. LLC (a [[Morgan Stanley]]/[[Goldman Sachs]]/[[Oaktree Capital]] venture) under CEO [[Steve Soboroff]].

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Location | West LA |
| Size | ~1,000 acres |
| Phase 1 | 3,100+ residential units, 2M sq. ft. office |
| Phase 2 | 2,600 units (approved Apr 2010) |
| Open space | 70% preserved |
| Wetlands | 415 acres to Ballona Wetlands |
| Sold | To Brookfield, 2012 |
