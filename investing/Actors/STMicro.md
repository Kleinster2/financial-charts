**STMicroelectronics** — European semiconductor company (French-Italian). Strong in automotive, industrial, and power.

---

## Why it matters

- Major automotive chip supplier
- Power semiconductor leader (SiC ramp with Tesla)
- MEMS sensors (accelerometers, gyroscopes)
- European fab presence
