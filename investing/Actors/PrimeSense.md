# PrimeSense

Israeli 3D sensing startup acquired by [[Apple]] in 2013 for ~$360M. Technology became **Face ID**.

---

## Technology

Developed structured-light 3D sensing — projects infrared dots and reads the pattern to map depth. Originally licensed to [[Microsoft]] for Kinect.

---

## Apple acquisition

| Aspect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Price | ~$360M |
| Year | 2013 |
| Outcome | Face ID (iPhone X, 2017) |

Face ID shipped in hundreds of millions of iPhones. One of Apple's most successful acquisitions by impact.

---

## Leadership

CEO [[Aviad Maizels]] later founded [[Q.ai]], also acquired by Apple (2026, ~$2B). Two successful Apple exits.
