**A. Lange & Söhne** — German luxury watchmaker based in [[Glashütte]], Saxony. Owned by [[Richemont]]. Founded 1845 by Ferdinand Adolph Lange, expropriated under East German regime (1948), refounded 1990 by Walter Lange after reunification. Produces ~5,500 watches per year. Known for Lange 1, Zeitwerk, and Datograph.
