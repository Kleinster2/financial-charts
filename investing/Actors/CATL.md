## Technology roadmap

**2026 launches:**
- **Sodium-ion batteries** — large-scale availability by end of 2026

**LFP dominance:**
- Lower cost vs NMC
- No cobalt/nickel dependency
- Tesla Model 3/Y standard range use CATL LFP

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Unassailable market leadership
- Technology moat (LFP, sodium-ion)
- Tesla/VW/BMW locked in
- Energy storage growth (grid-scale)
- Vertical integration

**Bear:**
- China exposure (tariffs, geopolitics)
- Pricing pressure from BYD
- Western localization costs
- Overcapacity risk in China
