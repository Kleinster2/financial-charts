# Divvy

**SMB expense management platform** — corporate cards + budgeting software. Founded 2016 by Blake Murray and Alex Bean (Utah). **Acquired by [[Bill.com]] for $2.5B** (Jun 2021). Now part of BILL platform.

## Bill.com acquisition (Jun 2021)

| Term | Value |
|------|-------|
| Acquirer | [[Bill.com]] (NYSE: BILL) |
| Price | **$2.5B** |
| Structure | $625M cash + $1.875B stock |
| Announced | May 6, 2021 |
| Closed | June 1, 2021 |

**Strategic rationale:** Bill.com gained expense management and corporate cards to complement its AP/AR automation. Combined offering lets SMBs manage all B2B spending in one place.
