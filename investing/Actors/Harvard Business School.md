Harvard Business School — graduate business school of [[Harvard]] University, founded 1908 in Boston. Largest endowment of any business school (~$5.3B). Pioneered the case method of teaching. Publishes case studies on companies and investors including [[Totem Point]] ("Growth Investing at Totem Point," Case 119-091, 2019).

## Revenue model

Unlike most university schools, HBS funds most of its operations from earned income rather than endowment distributions:

| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| MBA tuition | ~$75K/year |
| Harvard Business Publishing | Case studies, HBR magazine |
| Executive Education | Short programs for working professionals |
| HBS Online | Digital courses |
| Endowment distribution | ~21% of revenue (lowest share among Harvard schools) |
