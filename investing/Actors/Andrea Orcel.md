**Andrea Orcel** — Italian banker, CEO of [[UniCredit]] since April 2021. Former investment banker at [[UBS]], architect of major European financial M&A including the [[ABN Amro]] three-way break-up (2007). Appointed but then rescinded as [[Santander]] CEO by [[Ana Botín]] — a Spanish court awarded him €43.4M compensation, with a Supreme Court ruling expected early 2027. Under his leadership [[UniCredit]] shares roughly quadrupled, ROTE hit 19.2%, and 2025 compensation reached €16M. Three M&A attempts as CEO ([[Monte dei Paschi]], [[Banco BPM]], [[Commerzbank]]) — none completed yet.
