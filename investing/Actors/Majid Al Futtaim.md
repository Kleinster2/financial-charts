**Majid Al Futtaim** — Private UAE conglomerate (~$9.2B revenue, 2024). Operates Mall of the Emirates, 27 malls, 13 hotels, Carrefour franchise across MENA. Total assets AED 68.8B. Founder family net worth ~$8.9B. One of the largest privately held companies in the Middle East.
