## Mexico operations

Nissan has been one of Mexico's largest automakers for decades:

- COMPAS plant ([[Aguascalientes]]): Joint venture with [[Mercedes-Benz]], closing May 2026. [[BYD]], [[Geely]], [[VinFast]] bidding to acquire. See [[COMPAS Mexico plant sale 2026]]
- Second plant (near Mexico City): Also closing as part of global restructuring
- Infiniti QX50 and QX55 models being cancelled (produced at COMPAS)
