**Daniela Rus** — [[MIT]] professor and director of CSAIL; leads research on [[Liquid neural networks]] — dynamic-connection networks that act more like biological brains, need fewer neurons and less compute, and show better focus on road obstacles (vs roadside features) in self-driving research, especially at dawn/dusk transitions (FT, Nov 20 2024). Smaller compute footprint makes the approach attractive for onboard robot hardware. She co-founded [[Liquid AI]] (2023), the MIT spinout commercialising the architecture as Liquid Foundation Models.
