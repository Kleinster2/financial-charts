#actor #realestate #germany

Munich-based real estate boutique. Helped [[BVK]] expand into US commercial real estate starting 2018. Served as intermediary between the German pension fund and [[Michael Shvo]].

## Structure

- Parent: Deutsche Finance Group (Munich)
- US subsidiary: Deutsche Finance America (Denver), run by Jason Lucas
- Key executive: Sven Neubauer (executive partner)

## Business model

Pitched deals to German institutional investors. Also offered retail funds with fees up to 17% in first year. Role described as "structuring, coordinating and regulatory functions" — claims no "discretionary decision-making authority" over investment selection.

Jason Lucas would draft due diligence memos for Neubauer, who briefed BVK's Rainer Komenda.

## BVK relationship

Coordinated ~€1.6B of US real estate investments for BVK with Shvo:
- 685 Fifth Avenue, 711 Fifth Avenue, SoHo (NYC)
- Transamerica Pyramid (SF)
- Big Red/The Red (Chicago)
- Beverly Hills and Miami Beach developments

General partners (Deutsche Finance, Shvo, Bilgili) received:
- Yearly asset management fees
- Leasing and property management fees
- Carried interest (potentially every 3 years based on appraised values)
- Equity stakes

## Compliance issues

- Hosted internship for BVK employee Komenda's relative
- Close relationship between Neubauer and Komenda included high-end dinners at Koi, Mandarin Oriental (Munich)
- BVK now seeking to exit the relationship
- Lucas stepped away from day-to-day DFA management in 2025; parent brought in outside firm to reposition US portfolio
