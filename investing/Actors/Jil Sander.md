# Jil Sander

**German minimalist luxury house** — founded 1968, owned by [[OTB]] since 2021. Known for pure, architectural design. Lucie and Luke Meier led 2017-2025; Simone Bellotti appointed as new CD.

## Leadership changes (2025)

- **CEO:** Serge Brunschwig (ex-Fendi chief) appointed, also OTB Chief Strategy Officer
- **CD:** Simone Bellotti replacing the Meiers

Signals OTB investing in Jil Sander's growth potential.

---

## Brand positioning

**Aesthetic:** Minimalism, purity, precision tailoring. "Queen of Less."

**Heritage:** Invented modern minimalist luxury fashion. Influenced The Row, Lemaire, etc.

**Challenge:** Balancing founder's austere vision with commercial viability.
