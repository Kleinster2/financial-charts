# Norsk Hydro

**Norsk Hydro** is a Norwegian aluminum and renewable energy company. Operates Alunorte in Brazil, the world's largest alumina refinery, making it one of the world's largest consumers of [[Caustic Soda]] for the Bayer process. Integrated from bauxite through aluminum smelting and extrusion.
