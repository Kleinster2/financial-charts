# Joe Kiani

**Joe Kiani** is an Iranian-American electrical engineer who founded [[Masimo]] in 1989 with $40,000 from a second mortgage on his condominium. Co-developed Signal Extraction Technology (SET) pulse oximetry with Mohamed Diab. Led Masimo from garage startup through 2007 IPO to ~$1B+ in revenue. Acquired [[Sound United]] consumer audio for $1.03B in 2022 (later sold for $350M), triggering [[Politan Management]] activism. Ousted from CEO role and board September 19, 2024 after Politan won second proxy fight. Retains ~5% stake in Masimo (~$490M at $180 deal price).
