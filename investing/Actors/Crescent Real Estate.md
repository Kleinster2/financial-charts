# Crescent Real Estate

Fort Worth-based real estate investment, management, and development company. Co-founded by John Goff ([[Goff Capital]]) and Richard Rainwater. IPO'd on NYSE in May 1994 at ~$500M — grew to $6.5B under Goff's leadership as CEO. Sold to [[Morgan Stanley]] in Aug 2007 (15.4% compounded annual return, $2.5B+ in dividends over 13 years as a public company). Reacquired by Goff in Nov 2009 (partnership with [[Barclays]]); Goff became sole principal owner Dec 2017 after purchasing Barclays' interest. Now $10B+ in AUM, development, and investment capacity. Major project: Crescent Fort Worth (200-room hotel, 168K sqft office, 175 residences, [[Canyon Ranch]] urban wellness center).
