# MJC

Micronics Japan Co. (MJC) — Japanese probe card manufacturer that dominates the memory probe card segment. Probe cards are the interface between test equipment and semiconductor wafers during production testing, and MJC's strength in memory-specific designs makes it a critical supplier to [[Samsung]] and [[Micron]]. The memory probe card market is a duopoly between MJC (Samsung/Micron) and [[FormFactor]] (which supplies [[SK Hynix]]), with the split roughly following customer loyalty rather than pure technology differentiation. Listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange. The HBM boom has increased probe card demand — each HBM stack requires more test touchpoints than conventional DRAM, expanding MJC's addressable market per wafer.
