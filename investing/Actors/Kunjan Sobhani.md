## Notable commentary

On Bloomberg Tech (Apr 24 2026) following [[Intel]]'s Q1 2026 print, Sobhani anchored three reads:

- Gross-margin beat over revenue beat — the Q1 gross-margin beat was larger than the revenue beat, a quality signal for the foundry ramp suggesting cost-side improvement rather than just demand-side strength.
- 18A production expectations — cited a six-to-seven-times increase in [[Intel Foundry Services|18A]] production expectations, with yield improvement encouraging.
- 14A external-customer scale — described external-foundry revenue opportunity as moving from "hundreds of millions" to "billions of dollars" with the 14A node, framing 14A as the actual external-foundry monetization vehicle. On the [[TERAFAB]] / [[Tesla]] question, he said 14A "Terafab confirmed" based on industry conversations — but noted product timing and revenue magnitude remain dependent on execution.
