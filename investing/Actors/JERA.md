# JERA

[[Japan]]'s largest power generation company. 50/50 JV between [[Tokyo Electric Power]] (TEPCO) and [[Chubu Electric Power]]. Leading ammonia co-firing pilot at Hekinan coal plant (20% ammonia, targeting 50-100%). JV partner with [[CF Industries]] and [[Mitsui]] on the Blue Point Complex blue ammonia project in Louisiana (~1.1 Mt/year, production target 2029).
