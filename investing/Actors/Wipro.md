# Wipro

Wipro (NYSE: WIT) is one of India's big-three IT-services majors (with [[TCS]] and [[Infosys]]), offering outsourced application development, BPO, consulting, and managed services on a global-delivery/labor-arbitrage model. Long the laggard of the Indian three — slower growth, more restructuring, recurring leadership churn — which is why it is the loose outlier of the [[IT services]] cohort rather than part of its tight core. A member (the outlier) of the [[IT services]] cohort.
