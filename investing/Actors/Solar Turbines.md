## Caterpillar relationship

**Wholly-owned subsidiary:**
- Acquired by Caterpillar in 1981
- Operates as distinct brand
- Benefits from CAT distribution/service
- Energy & Transportation segment

**Investment exposure:**
- Solar Turbines not separately traded
- Invest via Caterpillar (CAT) stock
- Gas turbines = small % of CAT revenue
- But growing with AI/DC demand

## Why Meta chose hybrid fleet

Meta's Socrates South uses Solar Turbines + Siemens Energy + Caterpillar engines:

**Diversification benefits:**
- No single-vendor dependency
- Multiple technology types
- Supply chain resilience
- Competitive pricing

**Contrast:** OpenAI UAE Stargate uses single-vendor (Ansaldo Energia).
