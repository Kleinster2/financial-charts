## May 2026 Hormuz relevance

*Source: [IEEJ official site](https://eneken.ieej.or.jp/en/), accessed May 25, 2026; [[Reuters]] via [MarketScreener](https://www.marketscreener.com/news/former-marubeni-ceo-warns-japan-could-face-chemical-product-shortages-from-late-june-ce7f5addd98bfe27), May 25, 2026.*

IEEJ's May 25, 2026 webinar, "The Impact of the Ongoing War in the Middle East on Global Energy Security: A Follow-up Analysis Special Edition," hosted the Kokubu warning that Japan could face chemical-products shortages from around the end of June if Middle East [[Naphtha]] supply is not replaced.

IEEJ's own May 2026 site framing also emphasizes Japan's exposure to Middle East crude and the [[Strait of Hormuz]]. That makes it a useful institutional source for the Japan-specific energy-security layer of the [[2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis]], distinct from broader oil-market analyst coverage.
