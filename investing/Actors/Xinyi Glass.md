**Xinyi Glass** is a Hong Kong-listed Chinese glass manufacturer (HKG: 0868), one of the world's largest producers of float glass, automotive glass, and architectural glass. Founded in 1988 in [[Shenzhen]], the company operates manufacturing facilities across [[China]], [[Malaysia]], and other Southeast Asian markets. Xinyi also has a separately listed solar glass subsidiary, Xinyi Solar Holdings (HKG: 0968), which is one of the top two global suppliers of photovoltaic glass alongside [[Flat Glass Group]].

The investment case rests on Xinyi's vertical integration — it produces its own silica sand, soda ash inputs, and runs its own float glass lines, giving it a cost advantage over less integrated peers. The automotive glass segment benefits from rising vehicle production in China and growing exports, while solar glass rides the global PV installation wave. Revenue split is roughly 40% float glass, 30% automotive, and the remainder from construction and other segments. The company has consistently delivered ROE above 15%.

Xinyi competes with [[Fuyao Glass Industry Group]] (the global leader in automotive glass OEM), [[AGC Inc.]], [[Saint-Gobain]], and [[NSG Group]] in various segments.
