## Why CPP matters

One of the world's largest pension funds with aggressive infrastructure allocation:

- **Scale**: On track to hit $1T USD by 2037
- **Alternatives focus**: 48% in real estate, infra, private equity
- **Data center push**: Major investor in DC platforms globally
- **Power infrastructure**: Targeting AI/DC power bottleneck
