# Packaging Corp

The third-largest US containerboard and corrugated-packaging producer (after [[International Paper]] and Smurfit WestRock) — a near-pure-play on corrugated boxes for e-commerce, food, and industrial shipping. Its economics turn on containerboard pricing, old-corrugated-container (OCC) recovered-fiber input costs, mill utilization, and box-shipment volumes. A member of the [[Packaging]] cohort with [[International Paper]] (the containerboard sibling), [[Ball|Ball Corporation]] (metal cans), and [[Amcor]] (flexible).
