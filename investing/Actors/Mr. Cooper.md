## Insights

- **Largest servicer acquired by largest originator** — Creates vertically integrated mortgage giant. Rocket now touches search ([[Redfin]]), origination, title, closing, and servicing.
- **Recapture thesis** — Rocket's technology can convert Mr. Cooper's 10M servicing customers into refinance/purchase leads when rates drop.
- **Jay Bray to Rocket** — 25-year Mr. Cooper CEO becomes President & CEO of Rocket Mortgage, joins Rocket board.

## Strategic rationale

**For Rocket:**
- Adds largest servicing book in US
- Recapture opportunity: convert servicees to new originations
- Combined with [[Redfin]] acquisition (July 2025) creates end-to-end platform
- $500M+ invested in data and AI

**For Mr. Cooper shareholders:**
- 35% premium
- Participation in combined company upside
- Scale benefits

---

## Post-acquisition

Mr. Cooper brand will be retired. All servicing functions rebranded under Rocket name. Combined company services 1 in 6 US mortgages.
