# TAL Education

TAL Education Group (NYSE: TAL) is a [[China]] education company founded 2003 by Zhang Bangxin, historically built on the Xueersi (学而思) K-12 tutoring brand. The 2021 [[Common Prosperity|"double-reduction" (双减) crackdown]] gutted its core academic-tutoring business; it has since pivoted to small-class enrichment, non-academic programs, content, and learning hardware (the Xueersi learning machines), positioning the device line as an AI-enabled education product.

Member of the [[Chinese ed-tech]] cohort (the three US-listed tutoring ADRs tested for cluster structure).
