DAMO Academy (达摩院) is [[Alibaba]]'s global research arm, founded 2017. Develops core technology across AI, semiconductors, quantum computing. Responsible for [[Qwen]] models, the T-Head semiconductor division ([[XuanTie C950]], [[Zhenwu 810E]]), and [[Alibaba]] Cloud infrastructure R&D. Headquartered in Hangzhou with labs in Beijing, Singapore, Seattle, and Bellevue.
