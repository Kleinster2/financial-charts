# Pictet

Swiss private bank and asset manager founded in 1805 in Geneva, one of the oldest and most established names in European wealth management. Operates as a partnership with eight managing partners -- a structure that emphasizes long-term thinking and avoids the quarterly earnings pressure of listed peers. Manages ~CHF 700B+ in assets under management and custody.

Pictet operates three main businesses: wealth management (private banking for UHNW clients), asset management (institutional funds, thematic strategies), and alternative investments. The asset management arm is known for thematic investing -- water, clean energy, digital, biotech, robotics -- giving it a distinctive positioning among European asset managers. Raymond Sagayam is Managing Partner and Co-CEO of Pictet Asset Management.
