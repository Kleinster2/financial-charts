## Thesis on private credit

Milgram argued at the 2023 Milken Conference that credit underwriting had turned sloppy and loans were broadly overvalued — leaving private credit exposed if the economy turned. Three years later, with the [[Private credit retail liquidity crisis 2026]] unfolding, the thesis is being validated.

Milgram (Apr 2026): *"You will hear more and more people coming to our view because they are realising what we knew three years ago: this is not about a few bad loans, rather it is the systematic mischaracterisation of credit standards and asset quality."*

*Source: FT Due Diligence (Apr 2, 2026)*
