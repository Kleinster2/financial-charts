We-Vibe is a brand within [[Lovehoney Group]], not a standalone company.

## Brand history

- **Founded:** 2008 by Bruce and Melody Murison (Ottawa, Canada)
- **Original company:** Standard Innovation Corporation
- **Product:** First app-controlled couples vibrator
- **Acquired:** 2018 merger with Womanizer Group → WOW Tech Group
- **Current owner:** [[Lovehoney Group]] (via 2021 merger)

## Privacy scandal (2016)

Settled class action for $3.75M after We-Vibe app was found collecting usage data without consent. Led to industry-wide privacy improvements.

## Key products

- We-Vibe Sync
- We-Vibe Chorus
- We-Vibe Moxie (wearable)
