# Realty Income

Realty Income (NYSE: O) is the largest US net-lease REIT — "The Monthly Dividend Company" — owning ~15,000+ single-tenant properties (retail, industrial, gaming, data centers) on long-term triple-net leases. An S&P 500 dividend aristocrat. The bellwether of the [[Net-lease REITs]] cohort.
