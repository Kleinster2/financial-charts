# David Vélez

David Vélez (Vélez Osorno) is the Colombian-born founder, CEO, and controlling shareholder of [[Nubank]], Latin America's largest digital bank. A Stanford MBA and former [[Sequoia Capital]] partner who led the firm's Latin America push, he co-founded Nubank in São Paulo in 2013 (with Cristina Junqueira and Edward Wible) after running headlong into Brazil's banking oligopoly. Through Nubank's dual-class structure his 20-votes-per-share Class B gives him roughly 74% of the vote on about 20% of the economics, a stake that has made him one of Latin America's wealthiest entrepreneurs.
