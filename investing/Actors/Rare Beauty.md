Rare Beauty is a cosmetics brand founded by [[Selena Gomez]] in 2020 that has become the most commercially successful celebrity beauty line, valued at an estimated $2.7B (Fortune, 2025). Revenue reached $540M in 2024, roughly doubling from 2023, driven by cult products (the Soft Pinch Liquid Blush became a viral sensation with 2B+ TikTok views) and strong Sephora sell-through. The brand explored a $2B+ sale in 2024 — reportedly drawing interest from [[LVMH]], [[Estee Lauder]], and private equity — but Gomez publicly stated she has no plans to sell. Rare Beauty donates 1% of sales to the Rare Impact Fund for mental health services, a differentiator in a space where cause marketing often feels performative. The brand's success contrasts sharply with [[Prime Hydration]]'s collapse, suggesting that beauty (high repeat purchase, emotional loyalty) is structurally better suited to celebrity/influencer branding than beverages.
