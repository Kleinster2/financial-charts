# Jim McKelvey

**Jim McKelvey** — co-founder of [[Block]] (formerly Square) alongside [[Jack Dorsey]]. Glass artist and entrepreneur whose inability to accept a credit card payment for his glasswork in 2009 inspired Square's founding. Author of "The Innovation Stack." Also co-founded Invisibly (data marketplace) and LaunchCode (nonprofit tech education).
