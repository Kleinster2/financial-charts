ICIS — Independent Commodity Intelligence Services. London-based energy and commodity market data and analytics provider, subsidiary of [[RELX]] (LSE: REL). Publishes pricing benchmarks, supply/demand data, and market intelligence for natural gas, LNG, petrochemicals, fertilizers, and power markets.
