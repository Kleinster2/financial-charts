# KE Holdings

China's largest housing-transactions and services platform, operating the Lianjia (链家) brokerage chain and the Beike (贝壳) online platform that connects agents, listings, and buyers across new and existing homes. Dual-listed (NYSE: BEKE, Aug 2020 IPO; HKEX: 2423, 2022). A top-10 holding of [[KWEB]] and a [[Chinese internet]]-adjacent platform, though it trades primarily on the [[China platform regulation|China property cycle]] rather than the internet factor.

Founded by Zuo Hui (左晖, died May 2021; succeeded by Peng Yongdong); historically backed by [[Tencent]], SoftBank, and Hillhouse. Hit hard by the 2021–2024 China property downturn and the platform crackdown, it has since leaned into home renovation, rentals, and an asset-light agent-services model.
