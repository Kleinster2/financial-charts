# Joe Biden

46th President of the [[United States]] (2021–2025). Democrat, former Senator from Delaware (1973–2009) and 47th Vice President under [[Barack Obama]] (2009–2017). Defeated [[Donald Trump]] in the 2020 election; lost the 2024 rematch. Born November 20, 1942, in Scranton, Pennsylvania.

Presidency defined for investing-vault purposes by: the [[Inflation Reduction Act]] (climate/clean-energy industrial policy), the [[CHIPS and Science Act]] (semiconductor reshoring), expanded export controls on China, and the post-COVID inflation cycle that peaked at 9.1% in June 2022. Appointed [[Jerome Powell]] to a second term as [[Federal Reserve]] chair and [[Janet Yellen]] as Treasury Secretary.
