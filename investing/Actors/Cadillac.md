## Ownership and operations

[[TWG Global]] acquired full ownership of Andretti Global in November 2024 after disputes between FOM and Michael Andretti. British entity: TWG Cadillac Formula 1 Team Ltd. Dan Towriss (CEO of [[Gainbridge]]/Group 1001) named CEO of TWG Motorsports. GM and TWG established GM Performance Power Units LLC for works engines starting 2029.

TWG's broader motorsport portfolio: Andretti IndyCar, Spire Motorsports ([[NASCAR]]), Wayne Taylor Racing (IMSA), Walkinshaw TWG Racing.
