# Darling Ingredients

**World's largest renderer and recycler of organic byproducts** — converts animal fats, used cooking oil, and food waste into usable products. Ticker DAR. Critical role as feedstock supplier for renewable diesel via its 50% stake in [[Diamond Green Diesel]] JV with [[Valero]].
