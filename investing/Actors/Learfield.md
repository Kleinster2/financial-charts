**Learfield** — Dallas-based college sports media, sponsorship, ticketing, licensing, and NIL technology platform. Monetization engine for collegiate sports IP. ~$1.2B annual revenue. [[TPG]] announced a ~$2B controlling-stake acquisition on Apr 14, 2026 via its TPG Sports and TPG Capital platforms, with [[Charlesbank Capital Partners]] retaining a minority stake and [[Fortress Investment Group]] and [[Arctos]] exiting.

## Business

Learfield connects college athletic programs with commercial partners across:
- Multimedia rights (broadcast, radio, digital)
- Sponsorship sales
- Ticketing technology
- Licensed merchandise
- Official team websites / digital content
- Name, image, and likeness (NIL) facilitation
- AI-enabled data platforming

School partners include Alabama, Michigan, Ohio State, Oklahoma, Oregon, Tennessee, Texas, USC.
