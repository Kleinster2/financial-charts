# Kinross Gold

**Kinross Gold (KGC)** — Canadian senior gold producer headquartered in Toronto, listed on the NYSE (KGC) and TSX (K). Roughly 2 million ounces of annual gold production across a geographically diversified asset base: Tasiast (Mauritania, its single largest mine), Paracatu (Brazil), Round Mountain and Bald Mountain (Nevada), La Coipa (Chile), and the Great Bear development project (Ontario). A member of the senior gold-miner tier alongside [[Newmont]], [[Barrick Gold]], and [[Agnico Eagle]], with higher jurisdiction risk (Mauritania, West Africa) than the North-America-weighted peers.
