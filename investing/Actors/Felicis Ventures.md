# Felicis Ventures

#actor #vc #usa #private #early-stage

Known for founder-friendly terms — contractual promise to never vote against founders.

## Investment philosophy

**Founder-friendly terms:** Felicis includes a contractual clause promising never to vote against founders — aligns incentives and differentiates from traditional VCs.

**Thesis-driven:** Focus on infrastructure, security, and "future of health" rather than chasing hot sectors.
