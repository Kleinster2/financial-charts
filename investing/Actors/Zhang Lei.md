**Zhang Lei** — Founder and Chairman of [[Hillhouse]]. Born in Zhumadian, Henan province. Yale MBA, where he pitched [[JD.com]] to [[David Swensen]] as first major investment. Built Hillhouse into one of Asia's largest investment firms (~$100B+ AUM). Author of *The Value* (2020, Chinese bestseller on long-term investing).
