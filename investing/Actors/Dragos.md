# Dragos

Operational-technology (OT) and industrial control system (ICS) cybersecurity firm. On Jun 18 2026, [[Accenture]] agreed to acquire a majority stake in Dragos as part of a ~$4.2bn cyber-acquisition package (alongside runZero and NetRise) aimed at defending industrial and operational systems against AI-enabled attackers. See [[Cybersecurity consolidation]].
