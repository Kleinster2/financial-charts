# TD Cowen

US investment bank focused on growth sectors -- technology, healthcare, and sustainability -- now operating as a division of [[TD Bank]] after its 2023 acquisition.

## Synopsis

Cowen Inc. was a mid-cap investment bank with a distinctive niche: deep sector expertise in healthcare/biotech, technology, and sustainability, combined with a research franchise that punched above its weight. The firm traced its roots to the 1918 founding of Cowen & Company but was reconstituted multiple times. Under CEO Jeffrey Solomon, Cowen built a differentiated advisory and capital markets business that attracted [[TD Bank]]'s attention as the Canadian bank sought to expand its US wholesale banking footprint.

[[TD Bank]] acquired Cowen in March 2023 for ~$1.3B ($39/share), a ~60% premium to the pre-announcement price, and rebranded the combined entity TD Cowen. The deal gave TD a ready-made US investment banking platform with established sector coverage and equity research capabilities. TD Cowen analysts regularly appear in vault notes across healthcare, technology, and defense coverage. The integration risk was cultural -- Cowen's entrepreneurial, sector-specialist culture versus TD's large-bank compliance framework -- but the research output has remained consistent post-merger. For the vault, TD Cowen matters primarily as a research source with strong coverage of growth and innovation sectors.
