**Banco Safra** — Brazilian financial services company headquartered in São Paulo, 6th-7th largest private bank in Brazil. Founded 1955 by the [[Safra family]] after their migration from Lebanon. Services include investment banking, private banking, asset management, and retail banking (neobank AgZero). Run by [[David Safra]]. Part of the broader [[J. Safra Group]] alongside [[J. Safra Sarasin]] and [[Safra National Bank of New York]].
