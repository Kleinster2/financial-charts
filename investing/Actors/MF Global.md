# MF Global

Broker-dealer that collapsed in October 2011 after overleveraged bets on European sovereign debt. Led by former [[Goldman Sachs]] co-chairman and [[New Jersey]] Governor Jon Corzine. Filed for bankruptcy on October 31, 2011 — the eighth-largest bankruptcy in US history at the time. Customer funds (~$1.6B) went missing. The collapse triggered a contagion scare that hit [[Jefferies]] stock (-20%) on fears of similar exposure, catalyzing Jefferies' merger with [[Leucadia National]].
