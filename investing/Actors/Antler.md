**Antler** — Global early-stage venture firm and accelerator, structurally between [[Entrepreneur First]] (talent-first / pre-team) and [[Y Combinator]] (post-team). Active across 30 cities. Closed a $510M global fund in January 2026, with half earmarked for US founders, bringing total capital under management above $1B. 1,493 portfolio companies, two unicorns (Lovable — vibe-coding platform; Airalo — eSIM tech). Launched Antler ONE flagship program in Berlin in March 2026 with €500K initial check and up to €30M follow-on. See [[Startup accelerators]] for landscape context.

## Strategic position

Antler's distinguishing structural feature is the global cohort-and-matching model: enroll candidates pre-team in a residency, match cofounders, then deploy capital. The January 2026 US-allocation pivot signals that the firm is moving aggressively into [[Y Combinator]]'s home territory at the moment YC is scaling to ~1,000 companies/year. The Antler ONE Berlin launch puts a flag in Continental Europe with multi-stage capital ambition (€500K → €30M) that no Continental-European peer matches.
