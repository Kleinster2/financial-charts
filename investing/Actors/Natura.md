**Natura** (Natura &Co) is the Brazilian cosmetics group built on relationship / direct selling, now focused on the Natura brand plus the integrated Avon Latin America business after shedding its global acquisitions. It trades on B3 as `NATU3`.

The arc is a retreat from the debt-funded global roll-up: Natura &Co sold Aesop to [[L'Oréal]] for an enterprise value of US$2.58B (2023), exited The Body Shop, and is exploring a separation or sale of Avon International. A 2025 corporate simplification incorporated the Natura &Co Holding back into Natura Cosméticos, and the listed share reverted from `NTCO3` to `NATU3` effective July 2025 (one-for-one). What remains is a cleaner Natura + Avon Latam beauty platform.
