# BA&SH

**French contemporary womenswear** — effortless Parisian chic, boho-meets-polish. Founded 2003 by childhood best friends Barbara Boccara and Sharon Krief (BA + SH). €300M revenue (2024), 330 stores across three continents. Went through debt restructuring in late 2024 — founders took back operational control in 2025. HLD remains minority shareholder.

## Recent developments (2025-2026)

**Restructuring complete (Mar 2025):** Exited fast-track protection. €15M shareholder injection. "New Beginnings" strategic plan focuses on back-to-basics, international growth, online expansion.

**NYC flagship (Jan 2026):** 50th North American store. Soho location. Collaboration with designer Maryam Nassir Zadeh for launch.

**Leadership:** New head of retail hired. Streamlining store fleet while boosting e-commerce.

## Investment case

*Note: BA&SH is private.*

**Bull:**
- Founders back in control — alignment restored
- Full-price sales +21% signals brand health
- [[China]] upside (50 stores, "huge potential")
- Contemporary segment resilient vs luxury
- Debt restructured, balance sheet cleaned

**Bear:**
- Just exited near-bankruptcy
- North America underperforming
- Crowded contemporary market (Sandro, Maje, etc.)
- No luxury moat — exposed to fast-fashion competition
- HLD ownership structure unclear
