Allianz Global Investors is the asset management subsidiary of [[Allianz]], managing approximately EUR 560B in third-party assets alongside sister firm [[PIMCO]] under the Allianz Asset Management umbrella. Headquartered in [[Munich]], AllianzGI operates across equities, fixed income, multi-asset, and alternatives, with a growing emphasis on private markets and sustainability-linked strategies.

The US arm (AGI US) pleaded guilty to criminal securities fraud in May 2022 over the [[Structured Alpha]] scandal — a series of options-based funds managed by [[Gregoire Tournant]] that suffered catastrophic losses during the March 2020 COVID crash. Tournant was indicted on fraud charges for allegedly concealing risk from investors. [[Allianz]] paid $6B in combined penalties and restitution, and AGI US was banned from advising US registered investment funds for ten years. The scandal was one of the largest asset management fraud cases in US history and forced Allianz to restructure its US operations, transferring most AGI US assets to [[Voya Financial]].
