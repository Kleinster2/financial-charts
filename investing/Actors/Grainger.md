# Grainger

W.W. Grainger (NYSE: GWW) is the largest US distributor of maintenance, repair, and operations (MRO) products — industrial supplies sold to businesses and institutions through a high-touch and a low-cost (Zoro/MonotaRO) model. The anchor of the [[Industrial distributors]] cohort.
