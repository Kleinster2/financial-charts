## Rate sensitivity

| Rate environment | Impact |
|------------------|--------|
| **Falling rates** | Refi boom, volume surge |
| **Rising rates** | Purchase-only, volume drops |
| **Stable rates** | Servicing income steady |

**Servicing hedge:** $2.1T portfolio generates fee income regardless of origination volume.

## Investment implications

**Bull case:**
- Dominant scale (1 in 6 mortgages)
- Full homeownership ecosystem
- Recapture drives volume when rates fall
- Technology/efficiency advantages

**Bear case:**
- Rate sensitivity on origination
- Integration risk (Mr. Cooper, Redfin)
- [[UWM]] competitive pressure
- Regulatory scrutiny
