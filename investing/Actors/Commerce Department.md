Commerce Department — cabinet-level executive department of the US federal government responsible for promoting economic growth, administering trade policy, and running several regulatory bureaus central to industrial policy and technology regulation. Under Secretary [[Howard Lutnick]] (confirmed February 2025), the department has become the primary vehicle for the second Trump administration's industrial policy, including CHIPS Act funding execution, critical-minerals equity stakes, export controls via the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS), and trade remedy investigations.
