Zoho Corporation — Indian-American SaaS company. Founded 1996 as AdventNet by Sridhar Vembu and Tony Thomas. Offers 50+ business applications (CRM, email, accounting, HR). Fully bootstrapped — zero outside investment. ~$1B+ revenue, 15,000+ employees. Headquartered in Chennai with US operations in Austin.
