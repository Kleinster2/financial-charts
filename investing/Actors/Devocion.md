# Devocion

Brooklyn-based Colombian specialty coffee roaster. Differentiator is speed from harvest to cup — sources exclusively from Colombia and roasts within 10 days of harvest (vs. industry standard of months). Operates cafes in Brooklyn and Manhattan.
