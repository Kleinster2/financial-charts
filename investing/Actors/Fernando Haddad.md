**Fernando Haddad** — Brazil's Finance Minister (since Jan 2023) under President [[Lula]]. Former mayor of São Paulo (2013-2017) and PT presidential candidate (2018). Overseeing fiscal policy during a period of elevated [[Selic]] rates and corporate stress. Attended [[Lula]]'s Brasília meeting on the [[Raizen]] crisis (~Feb 2026).
