# AAII

The American Association of Individual Investors, a nonprofit membership organization founded in 1978 to educate individual investors. Best known for the weekly AAII Sentiment Survey (since 1987), the longest-running retail investor sentiment dataset — members report whether they are bullish, bearish, or neutral on stocks over the next six months.

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Founded | 1978 |
| Headquarters | Chicago, IL |
| Membership | ~2 million (individual investors) |
| Key product | Weekly Sentiment Survey (since 1987) |
| Survey method | Online poll of members (bullish/bearish/neutral) |
