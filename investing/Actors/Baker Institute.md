# Baker Institute

The James A. Baker III Institute for Public Policy is a think tank housed at Rice University in Houston, Texas. Founded in 1993 and named for former US Secretary of State James A. Baker III, the institute spans foreign and domestic policy with a Center for Energy Studies (CES) launched in October 2012 that the University of Pennsylvania's Think Tanks and Civil Societies Program ranked the world's number-one energy and resource think tank for three consecutive years (2018-2020). For investing-vault purposes, the institute matters primarily as a recurring source of energy-market data and Gulf-state production analysis — most prominently the $50-70 billion annual revenue estimate for the UAE's unconstrained production capacity that anchors the [[2026 UAE OPEC exit]] note's "what was at stake" framing.
