# Pinheiro Neto

Brazil's preeminent corporate law firm, founded 1942. Full-service with ~840 members including 118 partners. Offices in Sao Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, Brasilia, plus representative offices in Palo Alto and Tokyo. Chambers Brazil Law Firm of the Year. Serves ~2,000 local and international groups.
