# Sakana AI

Tokyo-based AI research lab focused on nature-inspired, efficient AI models optimized for Japanese language and culture.

## Overview

Founded 2023 by former [[Google DeepMind]] researchers. Philosophy centers on evolutionary/collective intelligence approaches rather than brute-force compute scaling — the core technique, M2N2 (Model Merging of Natural Niches), discovers non-obvious parameter combinations across existing models using evolutionary algorithms without gradient-based training, drastically cutting compute requirements. Published in Nature Machine Intelligence. Japan's most valuable private startup as of Nov 2025.

Headquarters: 1-17-1 Toranomon, Minato-ku, Tokyo. ~138 employees (Feb 2026).

## Strategic notes

- Japan's first AI unicorn and most valuable private startup
- [[IQT|In-Q-Tel]] investment signals US intelligence community interest in nature-inspired AI approaches
- Heavy backing from Japanese financial institutions (MUFG, SMBC, Mizuho) and corporates (NEC, Fujitsu, Mitsubishi Electric)
- Three declared strategic pillars: financial sector, defense, manufacturing/physical AI
- Differentiated approach: nature-inspired efficiency vs compute-heavy Western labs
- Japan-optimized models (EvoLLM-JP, EvoVLM-JP) targeting domestic enterprise market
- MIC disinformation detection contract shows government trust
- Expanding from research into enterprise products (Sakana Marlin)
