# Yara

Norway-based global fertilizer and ammonia producer. #2 globally in ammonia with ~8 Mt/year capacity. Sets the Tampa CFR ammonia benchmark through monthly negotiations with [[Mosaic]]. Pioneering green ammonia (Porsgrunn, Norway — 500 kt/year target; Sluiskil, Netherlands JV with [[Orsted]]). Global production network spanning Europe, Americas, Africa, Asia.
