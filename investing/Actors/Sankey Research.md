Sankey Research is an independent oil and gas research firm founded in 2020 by [[Paul Sankey]], a veteran sell-side energy analyst. Subscription-based products include the weekly Sunday Sankey newsletter, the Happy Hour conference call, an annual virtual refining conference, and thematic research. Distributed via [[Analyst Hub]].
