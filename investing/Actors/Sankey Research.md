Sankey Research is an independent oil and gas research firm founded in July 2020 by [[Paul Sankey]], launched at the depths of COVID and the negative oil price aftermath. Sankey had been the sole analyst to predict negative WTI prices in April 2020 and "bet on the cycle" by going independent. Distributed via [[Analyst Hub]], which provides infrastructure (compliance, publishing tools, CRM, market data access) for independent sell-side analysts.

Subscription-based, serving 200+ institutional investor clients (buy-side cross-section) and corporate clients. Operates independently alongside Sankey's March 2026 Senior Advisor role at [[Oliver Wyman]].

X/Twitter handle: @crudegusher.

## Notable calls

- April 2020: predicted negative oil prices (sole analyst to do so)
- 2024: "What Happens When Oil Hits $50/bbl?" -- widely read scenario note
- Feb 2026: bearish crude ($40/bbl target once geopolitical premium unwinds), bullish oil equities as "severely undervalued." Every 100M barrels returning to market takes ~$4 off crude.
- March 2026: characterized [[2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis|Hormuz crisis]] as worst since [[1973 oil crisis|1973]], called it a "structural change in the risk of oil" and a "crisis of shipping with no obvious solution."
