**New Murabba** is a [[Saudi PIF]] giga-project in [[Riyadh|Riyadh]] -- a mixed-use downtown development anchored by the Mukaab, a 400m cube structure designed as the project's centerpiece. The development covers ~19 km² and aims to become Riyadh's new downtown, with residential, commercial, retail, and entertainment components under the [[Saudi Vision 2030]] umbrella.
