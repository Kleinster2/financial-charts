## Arctic / Polar Silk Road

COSCO is China's primary operator on the [[Northern Sea Route]]:

| Year | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 2021 | Multiple Arctic voyages; saved thousands of tons of fuel vs Suez |
| Ongoing | Builds experience for expanded Arctic operations |

Route offers Shanghai–Rotterdam in ~25 days vs 35-45 via Suez. Seasonal (Jul–Oct), requires Russian icebreaker escorts.
