# Imperial Brands

**Imperial Brands (IMBBY)** — UK-based global tobacco company, the #4 Western nicotine major behind [[Philip Morris]], [[British American Tobacco]], and [[Altria]]. Headquartered in Bristol; primary listing in London (IMB.L) with a US OTC ADR (IMBBY). Combustibles-heavy portfolio — JPS, West, Davidoff, Gauloises, Winston (US via ITG Brands) — with a smaller and slower next-generation business (blu vaping) than its larger peers. Run as a high-dividend, buyback-driven capital-return story rather than a smoke-free growth story.
