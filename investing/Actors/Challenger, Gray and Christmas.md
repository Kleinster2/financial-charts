# Challenger, Gray and Christmas

[[Challenger, Gray and Christmas]] is a US outplacement and executive-coaching firm best known in the vault for the Challenger Job Cut Report, a recurring dataset on announced layoffs, hiring plans, and cited layoff reasons including [[Artificial intelligence|AI]].

## Why it matters

The monthly Challenger Job Cut Report is one of the few high-frequency private datasets that explicitly categorizes announced layoffs by cited reason. That makes it useful for tracking whether [[AI labor displacement]] is moving from named-company anecdotes into a broader employer-attribution pattern.

*Sources: [Challenger, Gray & Christmas about page](https://www.challengergray.com/about-us/); [Challenger report archive](https://www.challengergray.com/blog/category/job-cuts-report/).*
