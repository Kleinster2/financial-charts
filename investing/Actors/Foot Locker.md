# Foot Locker

**Largest athletic footwear retailer** — Foot Locker, Champs Sports, Kids Foot Locker, WSS. $7.99B revenue (FY24), 2,410 stores in 26 countries. CEO Mary Dillon (former Ulta) executing "Lace Up Plan" turnaround. **Nike dependency reduced from 70%+ to 60%** — diversifying into On, Hoka, New Balance, Adidas. FY24 returned to profitability ($18M net income vs -$330M prior year). Stock still down ~50% from 2021 peak.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Turnaround working (profitability restored, +300bps gross margin)
- Brand diversification succeeding (40% non-Nike)
- On, Hoka, New Balance gaining share = Foot Locker benefits
- Stock -50% from peak, cheap (~12x forward PE)
- Mary Dillon's track record (transformed Ulta)
- HQ move saves costs

**Bear:**
- Revenue still declining (-2%)
- Nike dependency still 60% — can't escape
- Nike turnaround = uncertain partner dynamics
- Athletic retail commoditizing (DTC shift)
- [[Consumer]] spending uncertain
- International exposure (macro risk)
