# Air India

**Air India** — India's flag carrier. Acquired by [[Tata Group]] in January 2022 after decades of government ownership. Undergoing fleet modernization (470 aircraft ordered from [[Airbus]] and [[Boeing]]).

## Iran conflict impact (March 2026)

Mounting extra services as existing flights sell out — benefiting from Gulf carrier disruption on India-Europe and India-Far East routes. Indian routes particularly affected by Pakistan airspace closure. See [[Iran conflict airline disruption]].
