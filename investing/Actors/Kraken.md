#actor #crypto #exchange

**Kraken** — US-based cryptocurrency exchange founded in 2011. One of the oldest regulated crypto exchanges. Settled with SEC in 2023 over staking services ($30M). Delisted [[Tether|USDT]] for EEA users under [[MiCA]] (Jul 2025).
