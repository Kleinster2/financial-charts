#actor #crypto #exchange

**Kraken** is a US-based cryptocurrency exchange founded in 2011. One of the oldest regulated crypto exchanges. Settled with [[SEC]] in 2023 over staking services ($30M). Delisted [[Tether|USDT]] for [[EU]] users under [[MiCA]] (Jul 2025).
