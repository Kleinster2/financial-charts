# Mota-Engil

**Portugal's largest construction company** — €5.9B revenue (2024), operations in 21 countries. Euronext Lisbon: EGL. 40% Mota family, 32% [[CCCC]] (China). Strong in Africa and LatAm. Won [[Santos-Guarujá Tunnel]] PPP (R$6.8B, Sep 2025).

## Why Mota-Engil matters

**Portugal's infrastructure champion with global reach:**

- **Scale**: Largest Portuguese construction company, €15.7B backlog
- **Africa boom**: Nigeria, Angola driving 59% regional growth — "strongest profitability"
- **LatAm expansion**: 40% of group revenue by 2026 (up from 33%)
- **CCCC partnership**: 32% Chinese ownership enables mega-project access
- **Diversified**: Construction, concessions, environment, energy

## Investment relevance

**Why Mota-Engil matters:**

1. **Africa infrastructure proxy** — Riding commodity/LNG boom in Angola, Mozambique, Nigeria.

2. **LatAm expansion** — 40% of revenue by 2026. Santos-Guarujá + Tren Maya position them as go-to contractor.

3. **CCCC synergies** — Chinese backing enables mega-project financing and technical capability.

4. **Concessions income** — Toll roads (Ascendi) provide stable cash flows alongside lumpy construction.

5. **Valuation** — Portugal's top-performing stock (2025), but still under-followed outside Iberia.
