# Tilray

A Canadian-rooted cannabis and consumer company formed by the 2021 Tilray–Aphria merger, now the most diversified name in the [[Cannabis]] cohort: Canadian and international medical cannabis plus a sizeable US craft-beer and beverage portfolio (acquired from Anheuser-Busch and others), which makes it a partial hedge against pure cannabis beta. Federally legal in Canada and NASDAQ-listed. A member of the Canadian LP bloc; despite the beverage diversification it still co-moves with the cannabis complex on [[Cannabis rescheduling|US-reform]] headlines and collapses into [[MSOS]]/[[MJ]].
