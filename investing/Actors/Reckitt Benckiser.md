**Reckitt Benckiser** -- British consumer goods company (now branded Reckitt). Formed from 1999 merger of Benckiser (from [[Reimann]] family / [[JAB Holding]] predecessor) and Reckitt & Colman. Brands: Lysol, Dettol, Durex, Nurofen, Enfamil, Finish, Vanish. ~$42B market cap. [[Bart Becht]] was CEO through the merger; [[Peter Harf]] engineered the deal.
