# Columbia Threadneedle Investments

Columbia Threadneedle Investments is the global asset-management arm of [[Ameriprise Financial]], formed from the 2015 combination of Columbia Management (US) and Threadneedle Investments (UK). It manages money across equities, fixed income, and multi-asset strategies for institutional and retail clients.

Tracked in the vault mainly as the former employer of [[Toby Nangle]], who ran its asset-allocation team (about $160bn AuM) as Global Head of Asset Allocation until 2022.
