## LFP batteries

Lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries are gaining share vs. NMC chemistries:

**Advantages:**
- Non-toxic cathode material (vs. cobalt/manganese oxide)
- Constant voltage delivery
- Higher charge cycle capability
- Lower fire risk

**Applications:** Grid storage, EVs, backup power systems
