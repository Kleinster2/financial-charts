Aletheia Capital is a Singapore-based independent equity research firm specializing in Asian markets. Founded in 2019, it provides institutional investors with differentiated, bottom-up research across Southeast Asia, India, China, and broader EM. The firm positions itself as an alternative to bulge-bracket research, offering contrarian and deep-dive coverage that larger banks have deprioritized under MiFID II cost pressures.

[[Nirgunan Tiruchelvam]] is the firm's head of investment research and its most visible analyst, regularly quoted on Asian equity strategy and geopolitical risk in portfolios.
