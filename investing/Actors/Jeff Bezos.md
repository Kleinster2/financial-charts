### $100B manufacturing fund

Bezos is in early talks (Mar 2026) to raise $100B for a "manufacturing transformation vehicle" — acquiring companies in chipmaking, defense, and aerospace, then applying Prometheus AI for automation. Fundraising in Middle East sovereign wealth, Singapore, and major asset managers. [[JPMorgan Chase]] in preliminary talks via its Security and Resiliency Initiative ($10B fund, led by [[Todd Combs]], ex-[[Berkshire Hathaway]]). Would rival [[SoftBank]]'s Vision Fund in scale.
