**Watches of Switzerland** — UK-listed luxury watch retailer (LSE: WOSG). Largest multi-brand luxury watch retailer in the UK, with significant US presence. Hit hard by the [[Rolex]]-[[Bucherer]] acquisition (shares dropped 29% on the news). Also operates Mappin & Webb and Goldsmiths brands.
