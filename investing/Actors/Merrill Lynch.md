# Merrill Lynch

Historic Wall Street brokerage and investment bank, acquired by [[Bank of America]] during the 2008 financial crisis for ~$50B. Now operates as Bank of America's wealth management and brokerage arm. Created the [[MOVE Index]] (1994, Harley Bassman) before selling it to [[ICE]] in 2019.
