#actor #cybersecurity #ransomware

**World Leaks** — ransomware/data-extortion group that publicly posts stolen corporate files to pressure victims. Known intrusions include a [[Nike]] breach (claimed Jan 2026) and the [[Tata Electronics]] theft (Jun 2026), the latter exposing >200,000 files including pre-release [[Apple]] iPhone 18 Pro supplier and component data, plus [[Tesla]], [[TSMC]], and [[Qualcomm]] documents.

The group's public posture is leak-driven extortion (publish to coerce payment) rather than pure encryption. [[Reuters]] has not independently verified the authenticity of all claimed leaks.
