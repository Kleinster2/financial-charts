#actor #person #crypto #china

Justin Sun — Chinese-born crypto entrepreneur, founder of [[Tron]] (2017), and the public face of one of the largest blockchain treasury vehicles in U.S. equity markets. Took Tron public via a $100M reverse merger with [[SRM Entertainment]] in mid-2025 (closed July; trades on Nasdaq as TRON), arranged by [[Dominari Securities]]. Concurrently a major investor in [[World Liberty Financial]] (the [[Donald Trump|Trump]] family's DeFi venture) — a position now publicly disputed with the WLF camp as of May 2026.

## Risk context

- Sun has a history of [[SEC]] scrutiny: in March 2023 the SEC charged Sun with unregistered securities offerings and market manipulation (case ongoing in May 2026).
- Sun's WLF investment is the subject of a public dispute with the WLF principals; the FT (May 8, 2026) characterized this as "high-profile."
- Sun was named in a September 2025 letter from Rep. [[Sean Casten]] to the SEC requesting scrutiny of Sun's U.S. activities.
