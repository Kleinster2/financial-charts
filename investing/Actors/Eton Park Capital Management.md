#actor #investor #hedgefund #usa #historical

Eton Park Capital Management — Eric Mindich's New York multi-strategy hedge fund, founded 2004 and closed 2017. Mindich (a former [[Goldman Sachs]] partner and youngest-ever Goldman partner) launched Eton Park with $3.5bn — at the time the largest hedge fund launch in history. The firm grew to ~$14bn before performance and the rotation away from multi-strategy formats led Mindich to return capital and close. The closure produced an unusually concentrated alumni network: multiple Eton Park partners spun out to found their own firms, with [[Anand Desai]] / [[Darsana Capital Partners]] (Q1 2014, pre-closure) among the most prominent successors.
