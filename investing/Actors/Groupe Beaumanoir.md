# Groupe Beaumanoir

**French fashion retail conglomerate** — family-owned, multi-brand portfolio. Founded 1981 by Roland Beaumanoir in Saint-Malo. €2.1B revenue (FY2024), 2,700 points of sale. Owns Cache Cache, Morgan, Bonobo, Bréal, La Halle. Acquired [[Sarenza]] (2022), Jennyfer (2025). Consolidator of distressed French fashion brands.
