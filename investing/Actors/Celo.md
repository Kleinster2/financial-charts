# Celo

Mobile-first blockchain protocol focused on financial inclusion. Native stablecoin ecosystem includes [[cREAL]] (real-pegged) and cUSD (dollar-pegged). Migrated to an Ethereum L2 in 2024. CELO is the native governance/utility token.
