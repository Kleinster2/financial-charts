# Axcelis

Axcelis (Nasdaq: ACLS) is a semiconductor capital-equipment maker specializing in ion implantation — the Purion product line — used in chip fabrication. It is a smaller, more cyclical wafer-fab-equipment (WFE) name outside the [[WFE|WFE quartet]] of oligopolists, with outsized exposure to mature-node power devices (silicon carbide for autos/industrial) and memory, which makes it more volatile than the [[Applied Materials|AMAT]]/[[Lam Research|LRCX]]/[[KLA Corporation|KLAC]]/[[ASML]] process-tool leaders.
