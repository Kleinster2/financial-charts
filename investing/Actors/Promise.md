Promise is a generative AI film and television studio building MUSE, a proprietary production workflow that integrates AI tools across pre-production, production, and post-production. Founded by George Strompolos (former CEO of Fullscreen, the YouTube multi-channel network acquired by [[AT&T]] / Otter Media), Jamie Byrne (ex-YouTube head of creator partnerships), and Dave Clark (GenAI filmmaker). Backed by Peter Chernin's North Road Company, [[a16z]], and the [[Google]] AI Futures Fund. Acquired Curious Refuge, the leading GenAI film education platform, to build a talent pipeline. The bet is that AI-native studios can produce content at a fraction of traditional costs while maintaining theatrical quality — a thesis that will be tested as the first MUSE-produced projects reach distribution.
