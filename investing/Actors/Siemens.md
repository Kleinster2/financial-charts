## Q1 FY2026 earnings (Feb 12, 2026)

**Strong start — outlook raised.**

| Metric | Q1 FY2026 | YoY |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| Orders | €21.4B | +10% |
| Revenue | €19.1B | +8% |
| Industrial Business profit | €2.9B | +15% |
| Industrial Business margin | 15.6% | +150bps |
| Net income (cont. ops) | €2.23B | +24% |
| EPS pre PPA | €2.80 | |
| Order backlog | €120B | Record |
| Book-to-bill | 1.12 | |

*Headline net income €2.2B vs €3.9B YoY misleading — prior year included €2.1B Innomotics sale gain.*

**Segment detail:**

| Segment | Orders | Revenue | Margin | Notes |
|---------|--------|---------|--------|-------|
| **Smart Infrastructure** | €7.2B (+22%) | +10% | 19.0% (+210bps) | Data center orders record €1.8B, electrification +38% |
| **Digital Industries** | €4.8B (+13%) | +10% | 17.8% | Software ARR €5.3B (+10%), [[Altair]]/Dotmatics integration -70bps |
| **Mobility** | €2.9B | +9% | 9.0% (+60bps) | Backlog €51B incl €15B services |

**Regional:** Americas +11%, EMEA +8%, Asia/Australia +5% (India +15%).

**Raised FY2026 guidance:**
- EPS pre PPA: €10.70-€11.10 (from €10.40-€11.00)
- Targeting upper half of 6-8% comparable revenue growth
- Smart Infrastructure: 6-9% growth, 18-19% margin
- Digital Industries: 5-10% growth, 15-19% margin

**Shareholder returns:** €5.35 dividend, ~€4.4B buybacks, 18M share retirement (Mar 2026).

**Strategic:** Expanded [[NVIDIA]] and [[Microsoft]] collaborations — building an Industrial AI Operating System. Completed sale of Airport Logistics business (US). [[Siemens Healthineers]] spin-off preparations on track.
