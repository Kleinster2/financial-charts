**IMAX** (NYSE: IMAX) is the premium-format licensor of theatrical exhibition — it licenses its large-format projection/sound systems and remasters films for them, taking a royalty-like cut of premium ticket revenue rather than operating theaters at scale. The asset-light counterpart to the exhibitors [[AMC Entertainment]] and [[Cinemark]].
