Zoe Lofgren — US Representative (D-California's 18th/19th congressional district) since 1995. Ranking Member of the House Science, Space, and Technology Committee, with longstanding jurisdiction over federal research funding, NIST, NASA, and technology industrial policy. Sent a March 19, 2026 letter to Commerce Secretary [[Howard Lutnick]] focused on the 10% federal equity stake embedded in the [[USA Rare Earth]] letter of intent — its valuation mechanics, governance rights, and precedent for future industrial-policy deals.

## Key 2026 actions

- March 19, 2026 — House Science Committee letter to Secretary Lutnick requesting documentation of the USA Rare Earth equity-stake terms and governance rights. See [[Lutnick family conflicts of interest]].
