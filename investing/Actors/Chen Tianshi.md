Chen Tianshi and his brother Chen Yunji were both child prodigies admitted to [[USTC]]'s "Gifted Class" as teenagers. They co-founded Cambricon in 2016 as a spinoff from [[Chinese Academy of Sciences|CAS]]'s Institute of Computing Technology. The brothers designed the neural processing unit architecture that powered early [[Huawei]] Kirin chips before Cambricon pivoted to standalone AI accelerators. Added to the US [[Entity List]] in December 2022.

Revenue exploded 600%+ in 2025. Wealth surged 800% since the start of 2024. State benefit: procurement mandates prioritize [[Cambricon Technologies|Cambricon]]'s neural processing units for government and military applications.
