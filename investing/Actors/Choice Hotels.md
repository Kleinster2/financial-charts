# Choice Hotels

Choice Hotels (NYSE: CHH) is a US asset-light hotel franchisor concentrated in the economy and midscale segments (Comfort, Quality Inn, Sleep Inn, Cambria) — heavily domestic and franchise-fee-driven. A member of the [[Hotels and lodging]] cohort, where it pairs with [[Wyndham]] as the domestic economy franchisors, apart from the global upscale big-3.
