**Lorenz Meier** — Founder and CEO of [[Auterion]]. Co-created [[PX4]], the dominant open-source drone autopilot, at ETH Zurich before founding Auterion in 2018. Led the company to ~€100M revenue and profitability in 2025. Negotiated the November 2025 [[Rheinmetall]] shareholding as a minority stake rather than a takeover — preserving Auterion's ability to sell its OS to multiple hardware vendors.
