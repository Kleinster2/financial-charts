# REM

REM (NYSE Arca: REM) is the iShares Mortgage Real Estate ETF — the benchmark basket of US mortgage REITs, spanning both agency mREITs ([[Annaly Capital|NLY]], [[AGNC]]) and commercial/credit mREITs ([[Starwood Property Trust|STWD]], [[Blackstone Mortgage|BXMT]]). A levered mortgage-credit/rate proxy, distinct from equity-REIT ETFs. Launched 2007.

## Use case

The mortgage-REIT-ETF benchmark for the [[Mortgage REITs#Cluster validation|mortgage-REIT cluster validation]]. REM holds the exact cohort names, so the mREITs cannot separate from it — the NEGATIVE −0.169 intra-advantage vs REM (the names correlate with REM more than with each other) is the ETF-replicable signature.
