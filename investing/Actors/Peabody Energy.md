# Peabody Energy

The largest US coal producer — diversified across thermal (Powder River Basin + other domestic) and seaborne metallurgical/thermal coal (Australia). Its mix straddles both ends of the coal market: domestic power-generation thermal (with an AI-data-center-electricity-demand tailwind keeping plants online) and seaborne coking/thermal exposed to Asian steel and power demand. A member of the [[Coal miners]] cohort with [[Alpha Metallurgical Resources]], [[Warrior Met Coal]], [[Ramaco]], [[Core Natural Resources]], and [[Alliance Resource Partners]].
