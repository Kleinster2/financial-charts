## Role and significance

The Chief AI Architect role is new — created in June 2025 explicitly for Kavukcuoglu. Mandate: accelerate the integration of [[Google DeepMind]] model output into Google's product surface. The dual-title structure (Chief AI Architect at Google + CTO at DeepMind) was deliberate; he needed continued credibility with the research org while gaining authority over product decisions across [[Google Search]], [[Workspace]], [[Android]], and [[Google Cloud]].

The appointment is part of [[Google]]'s broader response to [[OpenAI]]'s lead in productisation and the [[DeepMind diaspora]] — alumni who left to found competing labs after the post-[[ChatGPT]] product-focus shift. Promoting Kavukcuoglu to a company-wide role signals to remaining DeepMind researchers that internal scope can rival what's available externally.
