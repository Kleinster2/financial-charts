**NRG Energy** — Houston-based independent power producer and retail energy company (NYSE: NRG): competitive generation across ERCOT and the East plus one of the largest US retail electricity books (Reliant). Member of the [[AI-power IPPs]] cohort. Its AI-demand program: a Project Development Agreement with [[GE Vernova]] and Kiewit's TIC unit for up to 5.4 GW of new combined-cycle gas generation in ERCOT and PJM — 1.2 GW of 7HA turbine slots reserved, 1.2 GW in service by 2029, 1.2 GW by 2030, ~3 GW more through 2032 — with letters of intent from two data-center developers for NRG-owned sites (initial phase ~400 MW) and a data-center agreement expected to be announced in 2026.
