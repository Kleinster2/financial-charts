**Joseph Safra** — Lebanese-Brazilian banker (Sep 1, 1938 - Dec 10, 2020), chairman of the [[Safra family]] banking empire. World's richest banker at death (~$25.5B, Forbes 2020). Born in Aleppo, moved to Brazil 1952 with father [[Jacob Safra (banker)|Jacob Safra]]. Co-founded [[Banco Safra]] (1955), later built empire spanning [[J. Safra Sarasin]] (Switzerland), [[Safra National Bank of New York]], 200+ real estate properties. Suffered from Parkinson's disease. Succession triggered family feud between sons Alberto, [[Jacob Safra]], and [[David Safra]], resolved July 2024.
