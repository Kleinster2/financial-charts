# KYEC

King Yuan Electronics Co. (KYEC) is a Taiwanese OSAT (outsourced semiconductor assembly and test) company specializing in semiconductor final testing. KYEC holds a monopoly on [[NVIDIA]] GB-series package testing and has exposure to [[Amazon]]'s custom ASICs, making it the standout pure-play on AI test demand in the OSAT space. The company's testing expertise in advanced packaging — particularly CoWoS and chiplet-based designs — positions it at a critical bottleneck in the AI chip supply chain.

Founded in 1987 and headquartered in Hsinchu, [[Taiwan]], KYEC competes with larger OSATs like [[ASE]] and [[Amkor]] but differentiates through its testing specialization (most OSATs focus on assembly, treating testing as secondary). As AI chips grow more complex — with higher transistor counts, multi-die architectures, and tighter power/thermal specifications — testing becomes a larger share of total OSAT value and a bigger bottleneck. KYEC's NVIDIA relationship is the key asset: as Blackwell and successor GPU generations ramp, KYEC's testing capacity directly gates NVIDIA's shipment volumes.
