# Nathan Lambert

AI researcher and post-training lead at the [[Allen Institute for AI]] (Ai2). Founder of the [[ATOM Project]] (American Truly Open Models), an initiative to establish US leadership in open-weight AI models. Author of the definitive book on Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback and writer of the Interconnects newsletter, one of the most widely read technical AI substacks.

Previously a research scientist at [[Hugging Face]], and before that worked at [[Google|DeepMind]] and Facebook AI Research. His team at Ai2 coined the term "RLVR" (Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards) in the Tulu 3 work before [[DeepSeek]] popularized the approach. On ATOM's motivation: "July 2025, we had four or five DeepSeek-caliber Chinese open-weight models and zero from the US. That was the moment when I was like, I guess I have to spend energy on this because nobody else is gonna do it."
