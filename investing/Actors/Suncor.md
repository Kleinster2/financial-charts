# Suncor

Canadian integrated energy company and the largest oil sands producer. Operates Base Plant and Syncrude (100% owner since 2024 acquisition) near Fort McMurray, Alberta. Produces >800 kt of marketable sulfur annually as a byproduct of bitumen upgrading.
