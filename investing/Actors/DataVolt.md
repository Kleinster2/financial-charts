# DataVolt

> [!info] One-line read
> Large-scale data-center developer anchoring Saudi Arabia's AI-infrastructure push — partnered with [[HUMAIN]] ([[Saudi PIF]]) on a 6.4 GW pipeline and with [[NEOM]] on the $5B Oxagon net-zero AI campus. The build-out arm of the Kingdom's sovereign-AI ambition.

DataVolt is a data-center infrastructure developer active across Saudi Arabia and the UAE, focused on large-scale, renewable-powered AI campuses. It is the construction-and-operations partner behind several of the Kingdom's flagship AI projects, working alongside [[Saudi PIF]]'s AI company [[HUMAIN]] and the [[NEOM]] giga-project.
