**KEPCO** — South Korea's largest electric utility. State-controlled. Generates, transmits, and distributes electricity. International operations include power plant construction (nuclear, gas, renewables). Building 320 MW cogeneration plant for [[Saudi Aramco]]'s [[Jafurah]] development on a build-own-operate-transfer (BOOT) basis.
