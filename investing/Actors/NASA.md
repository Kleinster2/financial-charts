NASA — US federal agency responsible for civilian space program, aeronautics research, and space science. Key funder of [[SpaceX]] through COTS ($278M, 2006), CRS ($1.6B, 2008), and Commercial Crew programs. Artemis lunar program relies on SpaceX Starship for lunar lander.
