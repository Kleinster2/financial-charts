#actor #regulator #sports

**World Anti-Doping Agency** (WADA) — international independent agency established in 1999 to coordinate the global anti-doping framework in elite sports. Headquartered in Montreal. Operates and maintains the [[World Anti-Doping Code]], a binding framework adopted by the [[International Olympic Committee|IOC]], national olympic committees, international sport federations, and most major professional leagues. WADA's enforcement reach is structurally limited to athletes and federations that have voluntarily adopted the Code, but this constitutes nearly the entire elite-sports world. WADA's rule violations are appealed through the [[Court of Arbitration for Sport]]. Current president is [[Witold Bańka]] (since 2020). Public position on the [[Enhanced Games]]: condemned as "irresponsible and immoral" with explicit warnings that participating athletes risk multi-year competitive ineligibility.
