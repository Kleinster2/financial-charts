#actor #company #retail #food-retail #brazil #public

**Assaí** (legal name Sendas Distribuidora) is Brazil's leading cash-and-carry (atacarejo) retailer, spun off from Grupo Pão de Açúcar (GPA) in 2021 as France's Casino exited Brazil. It runs roughly 290 wholesale-format stores and trades on B3 as `ASAI3` and on the NYSE as the ADR `ASAI`.

The atacarejo format — bulk, low-price, serving both small businesses and price-sensitive households — has been the structural winner in Brazilian food retail, taking share from hypermarkets. Assaí's growth has been an aggressive store-conversion and build-out program (including converting acquired Extra hypermarket sites), which is capital-intensive and leaves a thin net margin.
