Mailchimp — American email marketing and marketing automation platform. Founded 2001 by Ben Chestnut and Dan Kurzius in Atlanta. Bootstrapped for 20 years with zero outside investment, reaching $800M+ revenue before being acquired by [[Intuit]] for $12B in 2021 — one of the largest bootstrapped exits in tech history.
