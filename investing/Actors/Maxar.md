**Maxar Technologies** — Space infrastructure and Earth observation. Gateway PPE contractor. Acquired by [[Advent International]] for $6.4B (2023).

---

## Why Maxar matters

| Business | Scale | Relevance |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| **Earth Intelligence** | WorldView satellite fleet | Geospatial imagery for defense/commercial |
| **Space Infrastructure** | Satellite manufacturing | Robotic arms (ISS, Gateway), PPE module |
| **[[Artemis]]** | Gateway PPE | $375M NASA contract |

One of few vertically integrated space companies — builds satellites AND operates imagery constellation.
