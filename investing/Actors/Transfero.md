# Transfero

Brazilian crypto company. Issuer of **BRZ**, the largest [[Brazilian real]]-pegged stablecoin (~$13.6M circulation). BRZ does not pass through yield to holders. Competes with [[BRD]] ([[CF Inovação]]) and BRLV (Crown).
