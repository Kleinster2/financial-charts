# Jorge Leon

Senior Vice President / Head of Oil Market Analysis at [[Rystad Energy]]. Former senior OPEC Secretariat economist (2014-2018). Frequent on-record commentator on OPEC+ dynamics, Saudi production policy, and oil-market structure for Bloomberg, Reuters, FT, and the Wall Street Journal.

## Key framings

On the [[2026 UAE OPEC exit]] (April 28, 2026): losing the UAE removes "significant leverage from OPEC"; [[Saudi Arabia]] must now "do more of the heavy lifting on price stability" as the bloc loses a key "shock absorber." The shock-absorber framing identifies the UAE's historical role as the member willing to absorb above-quota cuts to support Saudi-led production discipline — an operational point distinct from the volume-share metric.
