Trump Media & Technology Group (Nasdaq: DJT) — public parent of [[Truth Social]], the [[Donald Trump|Trump]]-family-controlled social-media platform. Took public via reverse merger with the Digital World Acquisition Corp [[SPAC]], underwritten by [[D Boral Capital]] (formerly EF Hutton) — a high-visibility precedent for politically-aligned [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]] activity. Per Forbes (March 2026), the position is valued at roughly $2bn, down ~$1.3bn YoY as the stock has more than halved while the company continues to operate at a loss.
