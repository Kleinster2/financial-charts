#actor #individual #crypto

**Giancarlo Devasini** — Chairman and co-founder of [[Tether]], estimated ~40-47% equity stake. Former plastic surgeon turned early Bitcoin adopter. Controls the largest individual ownership share of both Tether and [[Bitfinex]] through their parent entity iFinex Inc. One of the most influential and least public figures in crypto.
