#actor #chef #restaurants #nyc

**Riad Nasr** — Montreal-born chef, co-founder of [[Frenchette Group]]. Trained with Michel Bras in [[France]], then moved to [[New York City]] for [[Daniel Boulud]]'s Daniel in 1993 where he met [[Lee Hanson]]. Co-executive chef at [[Keith McNally]]'s Balthazar (1997–2013), Minetta Tavern, and Schiller's. Launched Frenchette in Tribeca in 2018. 2025 James Beard Outstanding Restaurateur (with Hanson).
