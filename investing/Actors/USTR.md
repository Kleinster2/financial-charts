USTR — US executive branch agency responsible for developing and coordinating trade policy, leading trade negotiations, and enforcing trade agreements. Administers [[Section 301]], [[Section 232 semiconductor tariff|Section 232]], and other trade remedy authorities. Led by the US Trade Representative (cabinet-level, Senate-confirmed).
