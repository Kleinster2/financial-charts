# PanAgora

PanAgora Asset Management is a Boston-based quantitative investment manager, best known in the risk-parity context as the firm where Edward Qian coined the term "risk parity" in his 2005 paper. PanAgora runs systematic multi-asset and equity strategies, and its Risk Parity Multi Asset franchise is a direct implementation of Qian's risk-contribution framework.
