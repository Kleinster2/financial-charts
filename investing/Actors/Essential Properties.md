# Essential Properties

Essential Properties Realty Trust (NYSE: EPRT) is a net-lease REIT focused on single-tenant, service-oriented and experiential middle-market businesses (car washes, quick-service restaurants, medical/dental, early childhood education) on long-term triple-net leases. A member of the [[Net-lease REITs]] cohort.
