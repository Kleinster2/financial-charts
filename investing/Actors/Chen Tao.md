[[Victory Giant]] is a [[China]] PCB (printed circuit board) manufacturer serving AI server and data center markets. Founded in 2003. Supplies [[NVIDIA]] — produces high-density circuit boards for GPU clusters. The AI buildout has driven massive demand for high-layer-count PCBs used in GPU servers. Despite being a billionaire, Chen Tao was photographed standing in the back row at an [[NVIDIA]] gala with [[Jensen Huang]].
