## Why Piemonte Capital matters

First mover in Brazil DC private equity:

- **DC-only mandate**: First Brazilian AM focused exclusively on data centers
- **Local advantage**: Deep Brazil/LatAm knowledge vs global competitors
- **Renewable thesis**: Brazil's clean grid = ESG-compliant DCs for hyperscalers
- **Elea track record**: Parent's success with [[Elea Data Centers]] proves model

---

## Investment thesis

**Brazil's sustainable DC advantage:**

| Factor | Implication |
|--------|-------------|
| 83% renewable grid | Only major market that can power DCs entirely on clean energy |
| Hyperscaler ESG mandates | Big Tech needs green DCs for Scope 2/3 commitments |
| AI demand surge | LatAm digital economy + AI deployment accelerating |
| Underbuilt market | Brazil DC capacity lags demand vs US/Europe |

**Target**: DC assets across Brazil and LatAm, leveraging renewable energy positioning.
