# IYT

IYT (NYSE Arca: IYT) is the iShares US Transportation ETF — a basket of US transportation stocks spanning railroads, trucking/LTL, air freight & parcel, and airlines. It is the standard transports benchmark (it tracks a transportation-average index).

## Use case

The replicability benchmark for freight cohorts. In the [[Trucking and LTL#Cluster validation|trucking/LTL cluster validation]], IYT is the nearest proxy — it absorbs the trucker cluster at the 0.50 cut because IYT is itself truck/parcel-heavy — but the truckers separate from IYT across thresholds 0.30–0.45 (+0.203 intra-advantage), so trucking is a distinct factor that IYT merely approximates. The [[Railroads]] cohort sits APART from IYT throughout (+0.203 too), so rail is distinct with no close ETF proxy. IYT is the transports blend; rail and trucking are two distinct freight sub-factors inside it.
