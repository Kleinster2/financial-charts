**Reed Hastings** — Co-founder of [[Netflix]] and the executive most responsible for building the company from a DVD-by-mail service into the global streaming leader. He served as CEO from 1998 to 2023, then remained executive chairman / chairman. In the Q1 2026 shareholder letter, Netflix said Hastings would not stand for re-election to the board at the June 2026 annual meeting.
