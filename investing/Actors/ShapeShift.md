#actor #company #crypto

**ShapeShift** — Non-custodial crypto trading platform founded 2014 by [[Erik Voorhees]]. Converted to a DAO in 2021, distributing FOX tokens to users. Pioneered the "exchange without accounts" model — users retain custody of keys and swap via aggregated DEX liquidity.

## Relevance

Primary vault relevance is as the prior company of [[Venice AI]] founder Voorhees — ShapeShift's thesis (disintermediate centralized crypto exchanges) is the template Voorhees carried into Venice (disintermediate centralized AI platforms).
