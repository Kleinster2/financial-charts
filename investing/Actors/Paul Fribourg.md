#actor #person #executive #agribusiness

Paul Fribourg — Chairman and CEO of [[Continental Grain Company]] since 1997, sixth-generation principal of the Fribourg family that has controlled the food-and-agriculture group since its 1813 founding in [[Arlon]], Belgium. He drove the pivot from commodity grain trading (sold to [[Cargill]] in 1999) to long-term investing and operating across food, agriculture and consumer businesses, including the [[Arlon Group]] private-equity arm.
