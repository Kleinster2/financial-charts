# Edwards Lifesciences

Edwards Lifesciences (NYSE: EW) is the structural-heart pure-play — the leader in transcatheter aortic valve replacement (TAVR) and surgical heart valves, plus a transcatheter mitral/tricuspid franchise. Unlike the diversified device generalists, Edwards trades on a narrow set of franchise-specific drivers (TAVR volume growth, trial read-outs, competitive entry), which is why it sits at the periphery of the [[Medtech]] cohort.
