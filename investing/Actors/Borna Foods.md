# Borna Foods

London-based wholesaler specialised in pistachios. ~95% of company turnover is pistachio products. Supplies food manufacturers in nut butters, ice cream, baklava, and confectionery. Sources from California, Spain, Turkey, and Iran. Used as a primary on-the-ground voice in Apr 2026 [[FT]] reporting on [[Pistachio market|pistachio price]] disruption from the [[Operation Epic Fury|2026 Iran conflict]].

## Apr 2026 voice

Heydaripour, on the Iranian pistachio disruption (FT, Apr 26 2026):

- "It's like gambling — we don't know at what price to sell."
- "It's difficult to talk to the suppliers in Iran as their internet has been shut down. They can't reply to emails."
- "We are trying to replace Iranian pistachio with other origins but there is a problem… Iranian pistachios contain a high level of oil compared to other origins. That makes a lot of difference in the taste."
- One UK baklava producer he supplies "can't replace Iranian with American because when he puts it into the oven, it's going to be burnt dry."
- "We're getting close to summer, and in the summer we traditionally sell a lot because of ice cream."
- "Iranians always find a way."
