**Aflac** — Columbus, Georgia-based supplemental insurance carrier; NYSE-listed (ticker AFL); one of the largest providers of supplemental insurance in the United States and the largest provider of cancer / medical insurance in Japan (where the Aflac Japan segment historically generates ~70% of operating earnings). Aflac was one of the publicly-named victims of the 2025 US insurance-sector wave of [[Scattered Spider]] intrusions alongside Erie Insurance and Philadelphia Insurance — the sector-rotation that followed the April-May 2025 UK retail wave (Marks & Spencer, Co-op, Harrods). The insurance-sector targeting reflected Scattered Spider's adaptive sector-rotation pattern toward industries that had not yet hardened against the help-desk-vishing playbook. Aflac disclosed limited operational impact relative to peer engagements; full incident-disclosure detail remained constrained by US insurance-regulatory and litigation considerations.
