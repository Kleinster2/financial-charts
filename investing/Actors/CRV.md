CRV (Charles River Ventures) — early-stage venture capital firm founded 1970, one of the oldest VCs in the US. Based in Menlo Park and San Francisco. Focus: seed and Series A in enterprise software, consumer, and infrastructure. Co-led [[Tailscale]] Series B (May 2022, $100M) alongside [[Insight Partners]]. Other notable investments: Twitter (seed), Zendesk, DoorDash, Airtable, Mercury.
