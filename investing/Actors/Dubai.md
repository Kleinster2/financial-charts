**Dubai** — Emirate and city-state within the [[UAE]], population ~3.7M. Ruler: Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum. GDP ~$117B. Global hub for tourism (19.6M visitors 2025), real estate, trade, and finance. Home to Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, DIFC, Jebel Ali port. Zero income/property/capital gains tax. Golden Visa program. Key developers: [[Emaar]], [[DAMAC]], [[Al Habtoor Group]], [[Majid Al Futtaim]].
