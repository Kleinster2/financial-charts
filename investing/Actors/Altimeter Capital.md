#actor #investor #hedgefund #usa #private

**Altimeter Capital** is a technology-focused crossover investment firm founded by [[Brad Gerstner]] in 2008 — public equities plus late-stage private venture, with positions held through IPO into public markets. Headquartered in Menlo Park, CA. Reported AUM varies by source and date: ~$10B (Institutional Investor, 2025); ~$20B per earlier vault entry.

## Principals

- [[Brad Gerstner]] — founder and CIO. Lawyer-turned-investor; known for the SPAC boom, [[Snowflake]], public advocacy and TV presence; co-hosts [[BG2Pod]]; runs an annual Altimeter conference.
- [[Clark Tang]] — partner (youngest ever named, 2024); leads AI, software-infrastructure, and semiconductor investments across public and private books. Pitched [[NVIDIA]] internally in Sep 2022; Altimeter bought Oct 2022, before the generative-AI repricing. Publishes compute-economics frameworks on X — see [[Revenue per GW]] (Mar 2026).
