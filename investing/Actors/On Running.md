#actor #fashion #athletic #switzerland #public

On Running — Swiss performance running brand co-founded by [[Roger Federer]]. CHF 2.32B revenue (2024, +29%), guiding CHF 2.98B in 2025 (+34% constant currency). The fastest-growing major athletic brand globally. Known for CloudTec cushioning technology. IPO'd September 2021 at $24/share. Premium positioning ($150-$300 shoes) in a category dominated by [[Nike]]. Market cap ~$15B at ~$43/share (Feb 2026).

## Investment case

Bull:
- +29% growth at $2.3B scale, guiding +34% cc for 2025
- 62.5% gross margin (higher than [[Nike]], [[Adidas]], and Hoka)
- [[Asia]] Pacific tripling — massive runway remaining
- Apparel growing 3x shoes — product diversification inflecting
- DTC mix expanding, driving margin improvement
- [[Roger Federer]] partnership provides enduring brand credibility
- [[Nike]] weakness creates structural share gain opportunity
- Balance sheet pristine (CHF 1B cash, no net debt)

Bear:
- ~35x forward PE — priced for sustained 25%+ growth
- Growth decelerating (47% in 2023, 29% in 2024)
- 93% shoes, 57% Americas — still concentrated
- Running shoe cycles are fickle (Hoka also surged then cooled)
- [[Nike]] turnaround under [[Elliott Hill]] could compress share
- [[Swiss franc]] headwinds for non-CHF revenue
- No proven cultural/lifestyle crossover yet (running-only brand)
- No CFO currently in seat until Frank Sluis joins May 2026
