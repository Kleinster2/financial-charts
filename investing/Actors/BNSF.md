**BNSF Railway** — Largest US freight railroad. Owned by Berkshire Hathaway.

---

## Why BNSF matters

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Owner | [[Berkshire Hathaway]] (100%) |
| Acquired | 2010 for $44B |
| Track miles | 32,500 |
| Revenue | ~$24B/year |

---

## Network

**Western US dominance:**
- Chicago to West Coast
- Pacific Northwest to Texas
- Agricultural heartland
- Intermodal hubs

Cannot be replicated — railroad rights-of-way irreplaceable.

## Investment case

**Not directly investable** — fully owned by Berkshire.

**Via Berkshire:**
- Steady cash generator
- Infrastructure moat
- E-commerce tailwind (intermodal)
- Coal headwind
