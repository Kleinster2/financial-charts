Founded Unitree in 2016 while still a master's student at [[Shanghai University]]. Built the company into [[China]]'s most visible robotics firm — quadruped robots at 1/10th the price of [[Boston Dynamics]]. Unitree's G1 humanoid ($16K) and Go2 robot dog ($1,600) set the consumer price floor for the industry.
