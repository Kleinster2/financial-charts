**EQT Corporation** (NYSE: EQT) is the largest US natural gas producer, focused on the Appalachian Basin (Marcellus and Utica shales). Not to be confused with [[EQT]] (Swedish PE firm, OMX Stockholm).

## AES acquisition (Mar 2026)

Part of a GIP/[[BlackRock]]-led consortium (with CalPERS, QIA) that agreed to acquire [[AES]] for $33.4B ($15/share cash), announced March 2, 2026. The deal takes AES private and expands EQT Corp into electricity and renewables — a strategic pivot from pure-play nat gas.

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Deal value | $33.4B (enterprise) / $10.7B (equity) |
| Structure | Consortium: GIP ([[BlackRock]]), EQT Corp, CalPERS, QIA |
| Price | $15/share cash |
| Target | [[AES]] (utility, renewables) |

Market concern: financing/dilution risk from the deal's scale relative to EQT Corp's equity base.
