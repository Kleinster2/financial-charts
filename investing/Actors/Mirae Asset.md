# Mirae Asset

**Mirae Asset** is a South Korean financial services group and one of Asia's largest asset managers, with over $500B in AUM across mutual funds, ETFs, alternatives, and venture investments.

## Synopsis

Mirae Asset has grown from a domestic Korean mutual fund house (founded 1997 by Park Hyeon-joo) into a global platform spanning wealth management, securities brokerage, life insurance, and alternative investments. The group's US presence includes Global X ETFs (acquired 2018), which manages thematic and income-focused ETFs — a product line that has benefited from the thematic investing boom around AI, robotics, and clean energy. Mirae also runs a significant venture and growth equity operation that led [[SpaceX]]'s June 2022 equity round ($1.68B at ~$127B valuation), putting it among the handful of Asian institutions with access to top-tier US private tech allocations.

For the investing vault, Mirae Asset represents the growing influence of Korean institutional capital in global markets. South Korean pension funds (especially the [[National Pension Service]]) and financial groups like Mirae have become significant allocators to US alternatives, private credit, and infrastructure — partly driven by the structural challenge of Korea's aging demographics and low domestic returns. Mirae's willingness to lead a SpaceX round (not just participate) signals the kind of relationship-based access that positions Korean capital as a counterweight to the [[Middle East]] sovereign wealth funds in private market deal flow.
