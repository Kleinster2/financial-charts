#actor #person #finance

Kyle Wool — Chief Executive Officer of [[Dominari Securities]] (the broker-dealer subsidiary of [[Dominari Holdings]], Nasdaq: DOMH). Architect of the [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]] that has used Dominari's underwriting book and the [[American Ventures]] SPV factory to bring [[Tron]], [[XTEND]], [[Powerus]], [[Cove Kaz Capital]], and [[Thumzup Media]] onto Nasdaq via reverse mergers in 2025–2026. Befriended [[Donald Trump Jr.]] and [[Eric Trump]] in 2024, leading to their joint advisory-board appointments at Dominari in late 2024 and their combined ~12.56% ownership stake.

## Vault relevance

Wool is the operational principal of the [[Sponsored reverse merger pipeline]]. His public footprint relative to other Dominari executives is significant — he has spoken on the record to the FT and is the named recipient of the [[House Select Committee on China]]'s March 8, 2026 letter. The committee letter requested information from Wool personally on Dominari's role facilitating Chinese small-cap IPOs implicated in suspected ramp-and-dump manipulation; 11 of 29 Chinese-issuer IPOs underwritten by Dominari since 2020 fit the committee's manipulative profile.

The friendship with the Trump brothers in 2024 is the relationship that produced the late-2024 advisory-board appointments and the combined ~12.56% Trump-family ownership stake. Without Wool's relationship, the Dominari/American Ventures pipeline would not have its central marketing wedge.
