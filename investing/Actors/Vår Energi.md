## Arctic drilling advocacy (2026)

Submitted response to [[EU]] consultation on Arctic policy, arguing Norway is a predictable energy supplier at a time of strategic importance. CEO Nick Walker noted a third of EU gas originates from Norway, with about a third of that produced in the Norwegian Sea and the Barents Sea — [[Arctic]] and sub-Arctic waters. Called for the Arctic to be more precisely defined, arguing the Barents Sea is ice-free year-round. See [[Arctic#EU Arctic moratorium review (2026)]].
