## What makes Morningstar, Morningstar

**The star ratings moat:**
- Retail investors trust Morningstar stars
- Fund companies market "5-star" ratings
- Advisors use Morningstar research
- Creates demand for data/analytics

**Different market than Bloomberg:**
- Bloomberg/Refinitiv = institutional traders
- Morningstar = retail, advisors, wealth managers
