#actor #ai #infrastructure #inference #private #usa

Inferact is the commercial company built by the creators of vLLM, the open-source LLM inference engine (known for its PagedAttention memory technique) that runs on over 400,000 GPUs worldwide. Incubated in Ion Stoica's UC Berkeley lab — the same lineage as [[RadixArk]]'s SGLang — it is the highest-valued name in the [[Open-source inference engines]] cluster, the supply-side force behind cheap self-hosted model serving.
