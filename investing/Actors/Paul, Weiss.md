# Paul, Weiss

Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison — major NY corporate law firm; the 2016 hire of M&A partner [[Scott Barshay]] from [[Cravath, Swaine & Moore]] is conventionally cited as the moment the [[Big Law transformation]] cycle began. Paul Weiss's compensation package for Barshay was reportedly multiples of what Cravath's lockstep system could pay, and the move proved that even at the most resistant lockstep firms a top originator could be moved with money.
