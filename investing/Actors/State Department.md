## Pax Silica and trusted AI diplomacy

The [[State Department]] is the coordinating institution for [[Pax Silica]], the Trump administration's non-binding framework for trusted semiconductor, AI infrastructure, critical-mineral, energy, advanced-manufacturing, and logistics supply chains. The June 2026 Washington summit broadened that work through the Joint Statement on AI Opportunity, which [[Italy]] signed through Ambassador Francesco Varricchio at a summit convened by [[Jacob Helberg]].

For investors, the department's role matters because technology diplomacy is migrating from abstract alignment into supplier qualification and procurement access. A partner country's status inside Pax Silica or the AI Opportunity track can become a signal about where US-aligned data-center, semiconductor, critical-mineral, and advanced-manufacturing cooperation is easiest to route.
