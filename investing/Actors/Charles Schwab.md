## TD Ameritrade acquisition (2020)

**$26B deal created a giant:**
- Combined client assets
- Thinkorswim platform (options traders)
- Eliminated major competitor
- Integration completed 2023-2024

|---------|-------------|

---

## Response to Robinhood

**Commission cuts (2019):**
- Schwab went to $0 commissions
- Forced industry to follow
- Killed discount brokerage model
- Now competes on service, scale

**Schwab's advantage:**
- Full-service (banking, advice)
- Trust/reputation
- RIA custody platform
- Scale economics

---

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Massive scale ($9T AUM)
- Net interest income machine
- RIA custody moat
- Demographic tailwind (aging, wealth transfer)
- Integration synergies

**Bear:**
- Interest rate sensitivity
- SVB-style deposit flight risk (2023)
- [[Fintech]] competition
- Slow to innovate
- [[Crypto]]/tokenization laggard
