# My First Million

**My First Million** is a business ideas podcast hosted by Sam Parr and Shaan Puri, part of the [[HubSpot]] Podcast Network, drawing millions of weekly listeners across audio and YouTube.

## Synopsis

My First Million carved out a distinctive niche in the business podcast landscape by focusing on actionable business ideas and opportunity analysis rather than interviews or news recaps. The show's format — two hosts riffing on business concepts with real revenue data, market sizing, and operational detail — has built a loyal audience of entrepreneurs and small-business operators. With ~500K YouTube subscribers and strong audio downloads, MFM sits in the upper tier of business podcasts by engagement, though well below the scale of generalist shows like [[Joe Rogan]].

The economic story is the [[HubSpot]] acquisition pipeline. HubSpot acquired Sam Parr's media company The Hustle for a reported ~$27M (mid-eight figures), gaining MFM as part of the content network. This fits HubSpot's broader strategy of using owned media to drive top-of-funnel leads for its CRM and marketing software — podcasts as customer acquisition cost, not standalone P&L. Parr and Puri retained creative control, which has kept the show's tone independent. For the vault, MFM is relevant as both a signal of how SaaS companies are vertically integrating media and as an occasional source of business analysis on companies and sectors we track.
