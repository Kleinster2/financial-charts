**Shane Legg** — New Zealand-born British AI researcher. Co-founded [[DeepMind]] in 2010 with [[Demis Hassabis]] and [[Mustafa Suleyman]]. Remains Chief AGI Scientist at [[Google DeepMind]] — the most research-pure of the three co-founders and the only one without a public investor portfolio.

## Significance

Legg is the most research-anchored DeepMind co-founder. Where [[Hassabis]] became the public face and angel-investor hub, and [[Suleyman]] left to found [[Inflection AI]] and now leads [[Microsoft AI]], Legg has remained at DeepMind through the 2014 Google acquisition, the 2023 Google Brain merger, and the post-2024 diaspora. The continuity matters for the lab's research culture — he is one of the institutional anchors against the productisation pull.

He coined "Artificial General Intelligence" (AGI) in his 2007 PhD work with Marcus Hutter. The term is now standard.
