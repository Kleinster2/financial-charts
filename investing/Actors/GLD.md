**SPDR Gold Shares** — the largest physically-backed gold ETF. Each share represents ~1/10 oz of gold held in London vaults.

## Use Case

Primary [[Gold]] proxy in the vault. GLD vs [[SLV]] vs [[PPLT]] vs [[PALL]] in the dashboard shows precious metals relative performance. Gold typically leads in risk-off environments and inflation hedging.
