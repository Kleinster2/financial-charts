# Matthieu Boulanger

Partner and head of Europe at [[HPS Investment Partners]] — the private credit firm acquired by [[BlackRock]] in July 2024. Public face of HPS's European platform; quoted on the May 18, 2026 [[Citi-HPS direct lending EMEA partnership May 2026|Citi-HPS €15B EMEA direct lending partnership]] announcement, where he framed the deal as a way for HPS to "leverage Citi's extensive network and origination pipeline in EMEA."
