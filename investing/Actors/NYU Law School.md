# NYU Law School

Law school of [[New York University]]. Alma mater of all four founders of [[Wachtell Lipton]] (1965): [[Marty Lipton]], Herbert Wachtell, Leonard Rosen, and George Katz. Per the FT's 2026 account, the four were Jewish graduates who felt unwelcome at "white shoe" New York firms and built Wachtell as an alternative — the institution-of-origin that shaped one of the most consequential 20th-century US corporate-law firms.
