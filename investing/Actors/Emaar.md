**Emaar** — Dubai's largest real estate developer. Public (DFM: EMAAR). Revenue ~$13.5B (2025), market cap ~$40B. Founded 1997 by Mohammed Alabbar. Built Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, Dubai Marina, Downtown Dubai. International projects across MENA, India, Turkey. The benchmark for Dubai real estate development.
