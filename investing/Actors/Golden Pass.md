# Golden Pass

LNG export terminal under construction in Sabine Pass, Texas. Joint venture between [[QatarEnergy]] (70%) and [[Exxon]] (30%). Next major US LNG project slated to come online (mid-2026).

## Strategic significance (Mar 2026)

With [[Ras Laffan]] offline under force majeure and physically damaged by the March 18 missile strike, Golden Pass becomes strategically critical for [[QatarEnergy]] — its first major production asset outside Qatar. Bloomberg (Mar 19): the disruption at Ras Laffan may force Qatar to expand overseas investments to hedge against future disruptions.

Golden Pass also matters for the US LNG export ramp: alongside [[Venture Global]]'s Plaquemines and [[Cheniere]]'s expansions, it represents the next wave of US export capacity entering a market where ~20% of global LNG supply ([[Qatar]]) is offline.
