# Arkema

French specialty chemicals company. Major producer of [[PVDF]] (Kynar brand), fluorogases, high-performance polymers, and adhesives. Listed on Euronext Paris (AKE.PA). Kynar PVDF is a leading binder material for lithium-ion battery electrodes, making Arkema a key beneficiary of EV battery demand growth. Also holds competing patents for some HFO refrigerant formulations, alongside [[Honeywell]] and [[Chemours]].
