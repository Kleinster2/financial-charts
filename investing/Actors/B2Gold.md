# B2Gold

**B2Gold (BTG)** — Canadian intermediate gold producer headquartered in Vancouver, listed on the NYSE (BTG) and TSX (BTO). Around 1 million ounces of annual production from a geographically dispersed, higher-jurisdiction-risk base: Fekola (Mali, its largest mine), Masbate (Philippines), and Otjikoto (Namibia), with the Goose mine at Back River (Nunavut, Canada) ramping as its newest and first Canadian operation. The lowest-priced and most jurisdiction-exposed of the [[Gold equity beta]] cohort — West Africa concentration (Mali) is its defining risk.
