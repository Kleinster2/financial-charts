## Insights

**\#1 by transaction count** — Meridian built dominance by serving small/mid-size building owners who needed financing. Volume-driven model with hundreds of brokers nationwide.

**Freddie Mac scandal (2023)** — Brokers allegedly falsified client financials to obtain larger loans. Freddie Mac barred Meridian from placing deals through seller-servicers. Existential threat to agency lending business.

**Brooks hire signals reset** — Bringing in former Acting Comptroller of the Currency to rebuild risk infrastructure and lender relationships. Regulatory credibility play.

## The scandal

| Date | Event |

| Jun 2024 | New Jersey office layoffs |
| Jul 2024 | President [[Yoni Goodman]] departs |

**Allegation:** Brokers falsified client financials to obtain larger loans from Freddie Mac.

**Impact:** Lost Freddie Mac access, executive exodus, layoffs, broker departures.
