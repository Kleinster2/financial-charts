**Crown Castle** — US cell tower REIT, one of the "Big Three" tower companies alongside [[American Tower]] and [[SBA Communications]]. Owns ~40,000 towers and ~85,000 route miles of fiber concentrated in the top US markets. Unlike American Tower's global footprint, Crown Castle is US-only.
