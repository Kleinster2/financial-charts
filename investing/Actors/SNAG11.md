## What it owns

A Fiagro (Fundo de Investimento nas Cadeias Produtivas Agroindustriais) holding agribusiness receivables and rural-property credit — the agro version of a [[CRA]]-backed paper fund, with the same tax-exempt monthly distributions to individuals.
