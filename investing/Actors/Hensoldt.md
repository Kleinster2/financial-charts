# Hensoldt

Hensoldt (Xetra: HAG.DE) is the German defense sensor-house — radar (TRML / TRS family), optronics, avionics, and electronic warfare — spun out of [[Airbus]] Defence & Space in 2017 (under [[KKR]]) and IPO'd in Frankfurt in September 2020. The German state holds a strategic stake (~25%) via KfW. It is a core [[European rearmament]] beneficiary: post-Ukraine air-defense demand drives sensor content (IRIS-T and Skyranger fire-control radar, counter-UAS), amplified by the 2024–2026 NATO spending surge.
