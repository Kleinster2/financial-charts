# DoubleVerify

DoubleVerify (NYSE: DV) is a listed digital-ad verification and measurement company — fraud detection, viewability, and brand-safety/suitability analytics that sit across the programmatic supply chain rather than buying or selling inventory. A measurement-layer [[Ad-tech]] name, structurally distinct from the demand-side ([[Trade Desk]]) and supply-side ([[PubMatic]]/[[Magnite]]) platforms.
