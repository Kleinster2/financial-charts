# Paramount Skydance

Combined entity formed by the David Ellison-led merger of Skydance Media with [[Paramount Global]] (closed 2024). Currently the buyer in the pending $109.9bn acquisition of [[Warner Bros Discovery]] — the largest US M&A transaction of 2025 per LSEG. [[Wachtell Lipton]] advises Paramount Skydance on the deal.
