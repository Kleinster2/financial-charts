# Viking Therapeutics

Viking Therapeutics (Nasdaq: VKTX) is a US clinical-stage biopharma and the leading listed pipeline challenger to the [[Eli Lilly]] / [[Novo Nordisk]] obesity duopoly. Its lead asset is VK2735, a dual GLP-1/GIP receptor agonist developed in both subcutaneous and oral formulations for obesity ([[GLP-1 receptor agonists]]). As a single-asset clinical-stage name, it trades as a high-beta, catalyst-driven biotech rather than on commercialized revenue — the speculative leg of the obesity complex.
