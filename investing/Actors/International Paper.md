# International Paper

**International Paper** is one of the world's largest producers of fiber-based packaging and pulp. Major consumer of [[Caustic Soda]] in the kraft pulping process. Completed acquisition of DS Smith in 2025, creating a combined packaging leader. Operations in North America, Europe, and Latin America.
