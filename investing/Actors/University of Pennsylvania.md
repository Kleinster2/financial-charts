University of Pennsylvania — Ivy League research university in Philadelphia. Founded 1740 by Benjamin Franklin. Home to the Wharton School, the world's first collegiate business school (1881) and consistently ranked among the top MBA programs globally. Deep pipeline into finance — Wall Street, hedge funds, private equity.
