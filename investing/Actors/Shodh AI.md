# Shodh AI

**Shodh AI** is an Indian AI startup applying LLM techniques to research domain datasets, shortlisted under the IndiaAI Mission.

## Synopsis

Shodh AI operates within [[India]]'s nascent but rapidly growing domestic AI ecosystem, focused on applying large language model techniques to domain-specific research datasets. The company was shortlisted under the IndiaAI Mission — the Modi government's initiative to build sovereign AI capabilities through compute infrastructure, foundational model development, and application-layer startups. Being on the IndiaAI shortlist provides both validation and potential access to subsidized compute through the government's planned 10,000+ GPU procurement program.

The Indian AI startup landscape is distinct from the US/Chinese ecosystems in important ways: domestic compute is scarce (India has a fraction of the installed GPU base of the US or China), talent is abundant but often exported to US labs, and the application opportunity is enormous given India's 1.4B population and under-digitized sectors (healthcare, agriculture, government services, legal). Shodh AI's research-domain focus positions it to serve institutional and government clients who need LLM capabilities applied to Indian-language, India-specific datasets — a niche that global models serve poorly. For the vault, Shodh AI is a data point on the broader question of whether India can build a domestic AI application layer despite its compute disadvantage.
