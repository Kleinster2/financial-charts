## What It Tracks

An actively managed global balanced book — the go-anywhere allocation franchise of the Dennis Stattman lineage. Measured against a global 60/40 reference, but in practice it carries a more international and often more defensive mix than a US 60/40, so it tends to lag a US-tilted balanced index in US-equity-led runs and to cushion in drawdowns.
