# Yum! Brands

Yum! Brands (NYSE: YUM) is a global quick-service restaurant franchisor — KFC, Taco Bell, Pizza Hut, and Habit Burger — running an asset-light franchise-royalty model. A member of the [[Restaurants]] cohort, where it pairs tightest with [[McDonald's]] (the other large global QSR franchisor).
