# Equinox

**Luxury fitness club operator** — premium gyms, hotels, media. ~$1B revenue (pre-COVID), 100+ locations. Majority owned by Related Companies (Stephen Ross). [[L Catterton]] minority investor. Raised $1.8B in 2024 to refinance and expand. Blink Fitness (budget brand) filed bankruptcy 2024.

## Recent challenges

**Blink Fitness bankruptcy (Aug 2024):**
- Budget gym chain owned by Equinox
- Filed Chapter 11
- Blamed COVID impact, rising costs
- Shows difficulty of budget segment

**NY settlement (2025):**
- $600K settlement with New York State
- Allegations: made cancellations difficult
- Subscription practices under scrutiny

## Investment case

*Note: Equinox is private.*

**Bull:**
- Luxury segment resilient
- $1.8B refinancing provides runway
- Hotels expansion (high-margin)
- Premium pricing power
- Post-COVID gym recovery

**Bear:**
- Blink bankruptcy shows execution challenges
- High fixed costs (real estate)
- Subscription scrutiny (regulatory)
- Never went public despite rumors
- COVID showed vulnerability of physical fitness
