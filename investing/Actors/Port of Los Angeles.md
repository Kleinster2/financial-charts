# Port of Los Angeles

**Port of Los Angeles** — largest container port in the Americas, located in San Pedro Bay, [[Los Angeles]]. Handles ~10M TEU annually.

## Lithium-ion fire incidents

- 2024: Battery fire shut down 70% of port for two days
- Nov 2025: One Henry Hudson container ship fire — ~200 firefighters, planes, boats deployed. Under investigation as of Mar 2026. Seroka confirmed lithium-ion batteries were "part of the combustion"
