## Waymo partnership

Manufacturing partner for [[Waymo]]'s next-generation robotaxi fleet:
- Arizona facility producing 2,000+ vehicles
- Announced 2024, ramping 2025–2026
- Helps Waymo scale beyond Jaguar I-PACE limitations
