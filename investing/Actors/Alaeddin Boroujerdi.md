# Alaeddin Boroujerdi

Iranian politician and member of the [[Iran|Islamic Republic]]'s parliament ([[Majlis]]). Sits on the National Security and Foreign Policy Committee. Publicly claimed in March 2026 that [[Iran]] was charging $2M transit fees on vessels passing through the [[Strait of Hormuz]], framing it as a "new concept of sovereignty" after 47 years. Iran's Foreign Ministry subsequently distanced itself from the remarks, but [[Bloomberg]] confirmed ad hoc fees were being charged.
