# Dun & Bradstreet

Commercial data and analytics company providing business decisioning data to companies worldwide. Taken private in 2019 by a consortium led by CC Capital, Cannae Holdings, and Thomas H. Lee Partners for ~$5.4B. Re-IPOed in 2020 (NYSE: DNB). Taken private again in 2022 by Clearlake Capital for ~$6.4B. Historically owned [[Nielsen]] (1984-1996).
