## Strategic direction

Under Barra, GM has pivoted aggressively toward EVs:

**Key initiatives:**
- Goal to offer **only EVs by 2035**
- Investments in autonomous driving (Cruise)
- Ultium battery platform development
- Factory conversions for EV production

**2018 restructuring:**
- Closed 5 North American plants
- 14,000 layoffs
- Drew criticism from [[Donald Trump]], who threatened to remove subsidies
