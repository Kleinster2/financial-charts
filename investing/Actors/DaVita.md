**DaVita** (NYSE: DVA) — Largest dialysis provider in the United States. Operates kidney care clinics and provides dialysis services to patients with chronic kidney disease and end-stage renal disease.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Essential healthcare service (non-discretionary)
- Duopoly pricing power
- Aging population = growing ESRD prevalence
- Cash generative, shareholder returns

**Bear:**
- Medicare reimbursement pressure
- Competition from home dialysis
- Regulatory risk
- Labor costs
