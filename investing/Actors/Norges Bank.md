# Norges Bank

Central bank of [[Norway]], established 1816. Sets monetary policy for the Norwegian krone (NOK) and houses [[Government Pension Fund Global|Norges Bank Investment Management (NBIM)]], the subsidiary that manages the $2T+ [[Government Pension Fund Global|GPFG]] on behalf of the Norwegian Ministry of Finance. The bank's dual role — domestic monetary authority plus operational manager of the world's largest sovereign wealth fund — is unusual among G10 central banks.
