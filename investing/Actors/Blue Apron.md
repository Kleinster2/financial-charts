# Blue Apron

Meal-kit delivery company. IPO'd 2017 (NASDAQ: APRN). Struggled post-IPO with customer acquisition costs and competition from [[Amazon]] Fresh, [[HelloFresh]], and grocery delivery. [[Linda Findley]] served as CEO 2019-2024, leading a turnaround that culminated in sale to Wonder Group in September 2023.
