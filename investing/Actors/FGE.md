# FGE

Energy consultancy covering oil, gas/LNG, and NGLs markets. Founded 1984 by Dr. Fereidun Fesharaki. Research spans 130+ countries, 280+ energy-industry clients (oil companies, financial traders, power/utilities). Merged with NexantECA (chemicals advisory) with backing from TA Associates (private equity).

FGE is one of the most-cited energy consultancies on Middle East oil/gas supply disruptions and OPEC dynamics. ## Mar 2, 2026 — Iran conflict analysis (CNN)

Dr. Nasseri provided the most detailed public supply-risk framework during the [[2026 Iran conflict market impact|Iran conflict]]. Three risk layers:

1. Iranian production (2.5-2.6M bbl/day) at risk if regime change succeeds
2. [[Strait of Hormuz]] closure — 20M+ bbl/day of oil + 5M bbl of product (>20% of global supply). Only 1-2 Iranian/Chinese vessels observed transiting eastbound
3. Regional infrastructure attacks escalating — Ras Tanura, Qatar [[LNG]], Dukhan, Jebel Ali

Key calls: [[OPEC|OPEC+]] 206K bbl/day unwind is "insignificant" given scale. [[LNG]] and gas "not as easily replaceable" as oil if disruption prolongs. ~4M tons/day of LPG and naphtha to Asian petrochemical sectors at risk. Market expecting IEA emergency stock release from OECD countries.
