# WisdomTree

Mid-tier ETF issuer ($144.5B AUM at year-end 2025) that carved out niches in currency-hedged equity, fundamentally weighted indexing, and more recently defense-themed and crypto products. The defense ETF franchise, led by [[WDEF]], became a breakout growth story in 2025-2026 as European rearmament accelerated demand for pure-play defense exposure. The firm also acquired [[Ceres Partners]] in October 2025 ($275M + $225M earnout), entering private markets via US farmland (~$1.8B, 176,000 acres across 12 states).

## Product franchise

WisdomTree competes not on scale (13th-largest US ETF issuer, far behind the [[BlackRock]]/[[Vanguard]]/[[State Street]] oligopoly) but on higher-margin niches where it can be first or differentiated.

### Core product lines

| Category | Flagships | Edge |
|----------|-----------|------|
| Currency-hedged equity | DXJ ([[Japan]]), HEDJ ([[Europe]]) | Original franchise; capture local equity returns without FX drag |
| Fundamentally weighted | DLN, DGS | "Modern Alpha" brand; weight by dividends/earnings, not market cap |
| Currency ETFs | BZF, CYB, FXM, USDU | Pure FX exposure |
| Defense ETFs | [[WDEF]], [[WDAF]], [[WDGF]] | Suite covering Europe, Asia, Global; WDEF fastest European equity ETF to $3B |
| Crypto/digital | European-listed crypto ETPs (BLOC, WALT) | Applied for US spot Bitcoin ETF (rejected pre-2024, before [[IBIT]] wave) |
| Tokenized funds | 15 funds across 7 blockchains | WisdomTree Prime (retail) and Connect (institutional) platforms; tokenized AUM ~$800M (+25x in 2025) |
| Private markets | US farmland via [[Ceres Partners]] | $1.8B AUM, 176K acres; adjacent to solar, AI data infrastructure, water |

### Defense ETF franchise

The breakout story. [[WDEF]] (Europe Defence UCITS) launched March 2025 and became the fastest equity ETF in European history to reach $3B AUM (achieved in ~3.5 months). By March 2026 it held ~$5.3B. WisdomTree expanded the suite with [[WDAF]] (Asia) and [[WDGF]] (Global), both US-listed September 2025 and UCITS-listed March 2026. A 3x leveraged Europe defence ETF was also launched.

The defense franchise drove WisdomTree up 7 positions in the 2025 ETF Issuer Power Rankings (ETF Stream), with $3.9B in inflows to high-margin thematic products.

## Competitive positioning

WisdomTree sits in the gap between the Big Three passive giants and the niche alternative factories (see [[ETF Issuers]] for the full landscape). It cannot compete on fees against [[Vanguard]] (0.08% avg) or on distribution against [[BlackRock]], so it targets structural niches:

- vs. [[Invesco]]: Invesco has deeper commodity and factor suites; WisdomTree has the stronger currency-hedged franchise and a more aggressive tokenization play
- vs. VanEck: similar thematic/niche positioning. Both surged on the 2025-2026 defense ETF wave, but WisdomTree's WDEF was first to market in European defence, giving it a massive AUM lead
- vs. Global X: both compete in thematic ETFs; WisdomTree has broader product diversity

## Tokenization strategy

WisdomTree Prime (retail app) and WisdomTree Connect (B2B/institutional) enable tokenized fund access across 7 blockchains: [[Ethereum]], Arbitrum, Avalanche, Base, Optimism, Plume, Stellar (expanded to [[Solana]] January 2026). 15 tokenized funds including EPXC (Equity Premium Income Digital Fund). Tokenized AUM grew 25x in 2025 to ~$800M. BNY banking integration provides fiat-to-blockchain rails.
