**Vicky Safra** is a Brazilian billionaire, matriarch of the [[Safra family]] banking dynasty, and Brazil's richest person with a net worth of ~$26.9B (Forbes, Feb 2026). Born Vicky Sarfati in Greece to a Sephardic Jewish family, she married banker [[Joseph Safra]] and became the steward of the Safra financial empire after his death in December 2020. She controls the [[J. Safra Group]] alongside her sons: [[Jacob Safra]] (international operations, including [[J. Safra Sarasin]] in Switzerland and the Chiquita banana business) and [[David Safra]] ([[Banco Safra]], Brazil's sixth-largest bank). The Safra fortune spans private banking in Switzerland, commercial banking in Brazil, real estate in New York and London, and agribusiness — a diversification strategy that makes the family less exposed to any single country's economic cycle but harder to value as a consolidated entity.
