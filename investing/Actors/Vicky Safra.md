**Vicky Safra** — Brazilian billionaire, matriarch of the [[Safra family]] banking dynasty. Net worth ~$26.9B (Forbes Feb 2026), Brazil's richest person. Widow of [[Joseph Safra]] (died Dec 2020). Controls the [[J. Safra Group]] alongside sons [[Jacob Safra]] (international operations) and [[David Safra]] ([[Banco Safra]], Brazil).
