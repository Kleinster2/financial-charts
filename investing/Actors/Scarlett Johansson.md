Scarlett Johansson — actor; starred in Marvel's Black Widow. Sued [[Disney]] in 2021 after Black Widow was released on Disney+ and in cinemas simultaneously, arguing the hybrid release breached her pay deal (FT, Jun 2026). The lawsuit was one of the controversies that compounded pressure on [[Bob Chapek]]'s tenure.
