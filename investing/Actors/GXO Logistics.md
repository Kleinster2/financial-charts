## Robotics program

GXO operates an "Incubation Program" to evaluate robotics partners through standardized processes with specific criteria and checkpoints. Active [[Robots as a Service|RaaS]] partnerships:

| Partner | Robot | Status |
|---------|-------|--------|
| [[Agility Robotics]] | Digit (bipedal) | Testing |
| [[Apptronik]] | Apollo (bipedal) | Testing |
| [[Reflex Robotics]] | Wheeled humanoid | Piloting (Fortune 100 retailer) |

GXO is the most active logistics company in humanoid robot evaluation — positioning to deploy whichever platform proves viable at scale.
