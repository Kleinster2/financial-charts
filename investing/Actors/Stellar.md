#actor #crypto #nonprofit

**Stellar** — Cryptocurrency platform and nonprofit Stellar Development Foundation. Founded 2014 by Jed McCaleb (former [[Ripple]] CTO, also founder of [[Vast Space]]) and Joyce Kim. Native token: XLM (Lumens). Designed for cross-border payments and tokenized asset issuance. Apache-licensed open-source protocol.
