## Why MBZUAI matters

MBZUAI is not important here because it is a university brand. It matters because the April 2026 Cerebras filing showed that a large share of what looked like hyperscale AI hardware revenue was still concentrated in an Abu Dhabi research and sovereign-AI cluster rather than a broad commercial customer base.

That makes MBZUAI a useful node for reading both sides of the story:
- sovereign and research capital can pull frontier infrastructure into existence faster than ordinary enterprise demand
- customer concentration can look strategic in private markets and risky in public markets at the same time
