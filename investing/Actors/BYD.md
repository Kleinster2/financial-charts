#actor #automotive #china #ev

**BYD** — Chinese EV and battery giant. Vertically integrated from cells to vehicles. Largest EV maker globally by volume.

---

## Why it matters

- Surpassed Tesla in EV sales (2024)
- Own semiconductor division (BYD Semiconductor) — power chips, MCUs
- Vertical integration model: batteries, chips, vehicles
- Expanding globally despite [[Export controls]] concerns

---

## Semiconductor angle

BYD Semiconductor:
- Power semiconductors (IGBTs, SiC)
- Automotive MCUs
- Reduces reliance on [[Infineon]], [[STMicro]], [[NXP]]
- Potential IPO candidate
