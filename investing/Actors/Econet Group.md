**Econet Group** — Pan-African telecom and technology conglomerate founded by [[Strive Masiyiwa]]. Core asset is Econet Wireless (Zimbabwe, Lesotho, Burundi). Also operates Liquid Intelligent Technologies (fiber/cloud across 40+ African countries), Cassava Technologies, and Vaya Technologies (fintech/ride-hailing). HQ in Johannesburg.
