**Safra family** — Lebanese-Syrian Jewish banking dynasty originating from Aleppo's Sephardic merchant networks, now controlling one of the world's largest privately held financial empires (~$350B total AUM across entities). Patriarch [[Jacob Safra (banker)|Jacob Safra]] (1891-1963) founded Banque Jacob E. Safra in Beirut (1920), migrated to Brazil (1952). Sons [[Edmond Safra]] and [[Joseph Safra]] built the global empire. Joseph died Dec 2020 worth ~$25.5B (world's richest banker). Control now with widow [[Vicky Safra]] (~$26.9B, Brazil's richest person) and sons [[Jacob Safra]] (international) and [[David Safra]] (Brazil).
