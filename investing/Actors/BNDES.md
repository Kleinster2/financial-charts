## Why BNDES matters

Brazil's primary infrastructure financing engine:

- **Scale**: One of world's largest development banks — bigger than [[World Bank]]
- **Infrastructure focus**: 55%+ of lending goes to infrastructure
- **Concessions leader**: Largest structurer of infrastructure concessions among peer DFIs
- **Green finance**: Major issuer of [[green bonds]], partner with AIIB/NDB
