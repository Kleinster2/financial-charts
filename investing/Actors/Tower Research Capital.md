## Mid-frequency expansion

Tower has significantly expanded into mid-frequency trading strategies (1-5 day holding periods), which now account for approximately 25-30% of revenues, up from under 10% just 2-3 years earlier. This shift reflects the broader [[HFT-hedge fund convergence]] trend.

Tower has also talked to investors about creating more traditional fund-like investment vehicles, which would mark a further step from pure prop trading toward hedge fund-like structures. Industry insiders predict that HFT-powered long-short equity funds will eventually emerge from firms like Tower.

*Source: FT Alphaville (Sep 29 2025)*
