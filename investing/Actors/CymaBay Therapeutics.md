**CymaBay Therapeutics** was a liver disease-focused biotech acquired by [[Gilead Sciences]] for $4.3B in 2024. The acquisition brought seladelpar, a PPAR-delta agonist for primary biliary cholangitis (PBC), into [[Gilead Sciences]]'s hepatology pipeline. The deal was part of CEO [[Daniel O'Day]]'s strategy to expand [[Gilead Sciences]] beyond its core HIV franchise.
