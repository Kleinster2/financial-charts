# KPN

Koninklijke KPN N.V. (Royal KPN) is the Netherlands' largest telecommunications operator, headquartered in The Hague. Listed on Euronext Amsterdam (ticker: KPN). Operates fiber broadband, mobile, fixed-line, and enterprise networking across the Netherlands and select European markets. Long history as the former Dutch state PTT (post and telecom) operator, privatized in the 1990s.

[[Twilio]] (NYSE: TWLO) named KPN as a Q1 2026 customer win in its April 30, 2026 earnings release, alongside AI-agent companies [[Sierra]] and [[Bland.ai]]. KPN's category is different from those two — KPN is a network-side telecom buying [[Twilio]] APIs for messaging / authentication / call-routing rather than an AI-native company building agents on top of Twilio. The strategic read: even traditional European telcos that historically operate in-house messaging and Verify-style 2FA infrastructure are now buying it from CPaaS providers, which extends the Twilio thesis from net-new AI-agent traffic to legacy-telco displacement of in-house infrastructure.

The win is a meaningful European geographic expansion for Twilio's enterprise pipeline.
