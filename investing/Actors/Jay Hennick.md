# Jay Hennick

Founder and Chairman of [[FirstService]] and Chairman/CEO of [[Colliers]]. Started in 1972 as a teenager founding Superior Pools (swimming pool management) in Toronto. Built old FirstService into a diversified property services conglomerate, then split it in 2015 into [[Colliers]] (commercial real estate) and new [[FirstService]] (residential + brands). Eliminated dual-class voting structure in 2019.
