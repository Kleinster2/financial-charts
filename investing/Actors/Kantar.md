# Kantar

Global data, insights, and consulting company. #2 global market research firm with ~30,000 employees. Owned by [[Bain Capital]] (60%) and [[WPP]] (40%). Spun out of WPP in 2019. Stronger outside the U.S. -- competes with [[Nielsen]] globally in media measurement and with [[NIQ]] in consumer research. Known for brand diagnostics, cross-media effectiveness, and TGI consumer panels.
