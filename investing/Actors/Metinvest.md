[[Metinvest]] — largest [[Ukraine]]ian steelmaker and mining group, controlled by [[Rinat Akhmetov]]'s SCM Holdings. Lost Mariupol plants (Azovstal, Ilyich) to Russian occupation in 2022 (~$3.5B in damages, half of steelmaking capacity). Produced ~2.1Mt of steel in 2024. Planning $293M investment in 2025 including two DRI green steel modules.
