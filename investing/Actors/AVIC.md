# AVIC

Aviation Industry Corporation of China — one of [[China]]'s largest state-owned defense and aerospace conglomerates. Ranked #6 globally among defense companies. Parent entity of AVIC Chengdu UAS (688297.SS), maker of the Wing Loong drone family.
