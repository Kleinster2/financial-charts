#actor #media #italy

**Media for Europe** — Italian media conglomerate (formerly Mediaset), controlled by the Berlusconi family via Fininvest. Relevant as an M&A precedent: acquired 76% of [[ProSiebenSat.1]] in 2025 after launching a low-ball offer at a 29% stake, then accumulating shares in the open market — the playbook [[UniCredit]] is now replicating with [[Commerzbank]].
