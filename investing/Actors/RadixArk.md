#actor #ai #infrastructure #inference #private #usa

RadixArk is the commercial company behind SGLang, the open-source LLM inference engine incubated in Ion Stoica's UC Berkeley lab that processes trillions of tokens a day for [[Google]], [[Microsoft]], xAI, and [[NVIDIA]]. It launched in 2025 to commercialize SGLang (known for its RadixAttention prefix-cache technique) and is part of the [[Open-source inference engines]] cluster — the supply-side erosion of lab-API economics. Founders Ying Sheng (built inference systems for xAI's Grok) and Banghua Zhu (ex-[[NVIDIA]]).
