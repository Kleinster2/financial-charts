## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Engineering services more defensible
- Software products business grows faster
- Digital transformation demand
- Strong margins (~20% EBIT)

**Bear:**
- AI automation threat to services
- Visa policy risk (US)
- Wage inflation
- Slower growth than peers historically
