# ICL Group

Israeli specialty minerals and chemicals company, operating phosphate mines in the Negev Desert and potash extraction from the Dead Sea. Produces phosphoric acid (white and green), specialty phosphates, bromine compounds, and potash fertilizers. Dual-listed on NYSE (ICL) and TASE.
