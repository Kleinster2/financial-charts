Qifeng Chen is a computer science professor at [[HKUST]] (Hong Kong University of Science and Technology), holding dual appointments in the CSE and ECE departments. Co-founder of [[DeepMirror]], a spatial intelligence startup that later became [[Looper Robotics]]. His research spans computer vision, image processing, spatial intelligence, and the intersection of perception and robotics. Stanford PhD (2017, advised by Vladlen Koltun), HKUST BSc (2012). His academic work on neural rendering and 3D scene understanding contributed to the foundation of spatial AI that now underpins autonomous navigation and robotic manipulation systems.
