## What It Tracks

The [[S&P 500]] — 500 largest US public companies by market cap. Cap-weighted, so mega-caps dominate. Top 10 holdings are ~35% of the fund.

SPY is more expensive but has the tightest spreads and deepest options market. Institutional standard.

## Use Case

The default US equity benchmark. Everything gets compared to SPY. In this vault:
- **Individual stocks** — compare vs SPY for alpha
- **Sector ETFs** — [[XLK]], [[XLF]], etc. vs SPY for rotation
- **International** — [[EEM]], [[EFA]] vs SPY for relative strength
