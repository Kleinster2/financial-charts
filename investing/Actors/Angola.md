# Angola

Southern African petrostate and now Africa's largest diamond producer (overtook [[Botswana]] in 2025 with sales up ~70% YoY). OPEC member. Economy dominated by oil and mining.

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Population | ~36M |
| GDP | ~$85B |
| Key exports | Oil (~90% of exports), diamonds |
| Currency | Kwanza (AOA) |
| Diamond rank | Africa's #1 (2025), overtook [[Botswana]] |
| OPEC member | Yes |

---

## Diamond surge

Angola's diamond sales jumped ~70% in 2025, overtaking [[Botswana]] as Africa's largest producer. Selling at prevailing market prices — well below [[De Beers]]' official rates — Angola undercut attempts by De Beers and [[Alrosa]] to stabilize supply through coordinated production cuts.

*Source: Bloomberg, March 18 2026*
