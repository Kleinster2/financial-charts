AIX Ventures is an AI-focused venture capital firm investing in early-stage (pre-seed and seed) startups where AI is core to the product, founded and run by prominent AI practitioners rather than generalist VCs. It is most relevant to the vault as [[Richard Socher]]'s investing vehicle (alongside his operating company [[You.com]]) and as an early backer of names the vault tracks.
