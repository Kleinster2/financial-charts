**Fact Coalition** — Washington-based tax transparency advocacy group. Full name: Financial Accountability and Corporate Transparency Coalition. Analyzed FASB ASU 2023-09 disclosures from 40 major companies and calculated $11bn+ in collective 2025 tax savings from booking profits in low-tax jurisdictions, with ~$5.5bn attributable to 10 pharma/biotech companies. See [[Pharma jurisdictional tax arbitrage]].
