[[Veolia]] is a French multinational utility company specializing in water management, waste management, and energy services. Sold its North American sulfuric acid regeneration business (SAR) to American Industrial Partners for $620M in July 2024 (rebranded as Nexpera). Had acquired the SAR business from Chemours for $325M in 2016.
