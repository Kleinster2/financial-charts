|--------|-------|

Valuation cut from the $12B last private round reflects cooled fintech sentiment. Would be [[India]]'s 2nd-largest fintech IPO behind [[Paytm]]'s ~$20B listing in 2021 ([[Paytm]] now trades at ~$7.1B market cap — a cautionary comp).

[[Walmart]] trimming stake by ~12%. [[Tiger Global]] and [[Microsoft]] exiting entirely. No primary capital raised — this is purely a liquidity event for existing shareholders.

Part of [[India]] digital champion IPO wave (see [[Asia IPO pipeline 2026]]).

## Investment case

Bull:
- \#1 in massive [[India]] payments market
- [[Walmart]] backing
- UPI adoption still growing
- Expanding financial services
- Path to profitability clearer

Bear:
- UPI is zero-fee (monetization challenge)
- [[Google]] Pay competition
- Regulatory risk ([[India]] fintech)
- Valuation cut to $9B-$10.5B from $12B last round — market repricing fintech
- [[Paytm]] IPO at $20B now trades at $7.1B — cautionary comp
- Needs adjacent revenue streams
