## Ownership

Wholly owned by the Aponte-Diamant family:
- Founded by **Gianluigi Aponte** and **Rafaela Aponte-Diamant**
- Currently led by **Diego Aponte**
- Never publishes financial results

## Market Position

Largest container line globally, ahead of [[Maersk]] (~16%) and [[CMA CGM]] (~12%).

Operates independently — not part of any shipping alliance since 2M alliance with Maersk ended in 2025.

## Terminal Investments

MSC's terminal arm (TiL - Terminal Investment Limited) is acquiring [[Hutchison Ports]] assets:
- $22.8B deal with BlackRock consortium (announced Mar 2025)
- 43 ports, 199 berths across 23 countries
- Excludes China/Hong Kong assets
- Would make MSC/TiL the largest terminal operator globally

## Investment Angle
