# IonQ

IonQ (NYSE: IONQ) is the largest and most-liquid listed pure-play quantum-computing company, building trapped-ion quantum computers and selling access via cloud platforms ([[Amazon]] Braket, [[Microsoft]] Azure, [[Google]] Cloud). A clinical-stage-equivalent business with minimal commercialized revenue — it trades as the anchor of the listed [[Quantum computing]] momentum cohort, on quantum-sector sentiment rather than fundamentals.
