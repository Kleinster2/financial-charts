## Energy transition

Mercuria is actively pivoting toward renewables:
- Committed $2B+ to energy transition investments
- Target: 50% of portfolio in renewables within 5 years (per 2021 statement)
- Banks increasingly requiring green targets for loans

---

## Ownership

Founders and top executives hold significant stakes. Polish businessmen Gregory Jankilevitsch and Wiaczeslaw Smolokowski are also shareholders (via earlier J&S Group structure).

## [[Project Vault]] role

Selected as one of three **procurement managers** for the $12B strategic critical minerals stockpile alongside [[Hartree Partners]] and [[Traxys North America]].

Mercuria's global trading infrastructure and metals expansion make it well-suited for strategic sourcing.
