**Carta** — Cap table management and private markets infrastructure. Knows equity ownership of 40,000+ companies. Building private stock exchange.

---

## Why Carta matters

Carta is the operating system for private company equity:

| Product | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Cap table | Equity management for startups |
| 409A valuations | IRS-required valuations |
| Fund admin | VC fund management |
| CartaX | Private stock exchange |
| Data | Private market intelligence |

---

## The data moat

**Carta sees everything:**
- 40,000+ companies on platform
- $3T+ in equity managed
- Funding rounds, valuations, dilution
- Employee equity grants
- Secondary transactions

**This data is incredibly valuable** for understanding private markets.

---

## CartaX (Private exchange)

**Building a stock exchange for private companies:**
- Secondary trading for employees
- Tender offers for companies
- Liquidity without IPO
- Competes with [[Forge Global]]

**Regulatory:** Licensed as ATS (Alternative Trading System).
