# Royal Gold

Royal Gold (Nasdaq: RGLD) is a precious-metals royalty and streaming company holding interests in gold (and some silver/copper) production across a portfolio of operating mines, with no mining operating or capital costs of its own. One of the big-three gold royalty names. A member of the [[Precious metals royalties]] cohort.
