**Jamal Khashoggi** — [[Saudi Arabia|Saudi]] journalist and [[Washington Post]] columnist. Murdered October 2, 2018 at the Saudi consulate in Istanbul, [[Turkey]]. His killing triggered a mass boycott of the 2018 [[Future Investment Initiative]] summit and a global backlash against [[Saudi Arabia]] and [[Mohammed bin Salman]].
