## Why TIM Brasil matters

Major force in Brazil's telecom market:

- **Scale**: 72.6M customers, presence in all Brazilian states since 2002
- **Coverage leader**: First to 100% 4G municipal coverage (including rural)
- **5G pioneer**: First tests in 2019, Nokia partnership (Aug 2024)
- **Oi acquisition**: Bought mobile assets with Vivo and Claro (R$7B total, 2022)
