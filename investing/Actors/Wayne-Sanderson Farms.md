Wayne-Sanderson Farms — the third-largest poultry producer in the United States, formed in 2022 when a joint venture of [[Cargill]] and [[Continental Grain Company]] acquired [[Sanderson Farms]] and merged it with Wayne Farms. 2025 sales rose ~18% (+$1.3B) to roughly $8.7 billion, with a growing tilt toward further-processed, value-added chicken.
