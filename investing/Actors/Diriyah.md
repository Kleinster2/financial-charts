**Diriyah** is a UNESCO World Heritage site and mega-development project northwest of [[Riyadh|Riyadh]], managed by the Diriyah Gate Development Authority (DGDA). Wholly owned by [[Saudi PIF]]. The site is the birthplace of the first Saudi state (1744) and is being developed into a cultural, hospitality, and retail destination with a $63B+ budget. One of the flagship giga-projects under [[Saudi Vision 2030]].
