The **Netherlands** -- Western European constitutional monarchy. HQ Amsterdam (capital), The Hague (seat of government). Population ~17.9M. GDP ~EUR1T. AAA-rated sovereign (Moody's Aaa, S&P AAA, Fitch AAA). Major European economy with strengths in trade, logistics, agriculture, energy, and financial services. Home to global multinationals including [[ASML]], [[Shell]], [[ING Group]], [[Philips]]. Public debt ~46% of GDP (well below eurozone average of ~89%). Key financial institutions include [[BNG Bank]] and [[NWB Bank]] (promotional banks for public sector).
