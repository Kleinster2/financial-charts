---

## Why EllaLink matters

Direct transatlantic link:

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Route | [[Brazil]] (Fortaleza) → Portugal (Sines) |
| Length | 6,200 km |
| Capacity | 100+ Tbps |
| Operational | 2021 |
| Expansion | French Guiana 2025 |

First cable to bypass US routing between South America and [[Europe]].

---

## Data sovereignty

**Why bypass matters:**
- No US transit = no US jurisdiction
- GDPR compliance easier
- Latency reduction (~50%)
- European data stays European

Post-Snowden infrastructure.

---

## Route

**Landing points:**
- Fortaleza, [[Brazil]] (Praia do Futuro)
- Sines, Portugal
- French Guiana (2025 extension)
