#actor #retail #grocery

**Carrefour** is a French multinational grocery retailer, the second-largest in the world by revenue. Euronext: CA. Revenue exceeds EUR 80B across hypermarkets, supermarkets, and convenience stores. HQ in Massy, France. The company has exited many international markets over the years but retains strong positions in France, [[Brazil]] (via Atacadao, a major cash-and-carry operation), and parts of Europe and MENA.
