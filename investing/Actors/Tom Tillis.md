## Fed confirmation holdout (2026)

After DOJ served [[Jerome Powell|Powell]] with grand jury subpoenas in January 2026, Tillis publicly stated he would oppose all Fed nominees "until this legal matter is fully resolved." Position remained firm as of the April 14, 2026 [[Scott Bessent]] interview — with [[Kevin Warsh]]'s Senate Banking Committee hearing scheduled for April 21, 2026, the Tillis holdout puts confirmation before the May 15 Powell chair-term expiration in doubt. Bessent's answer on Tillis: "I think Senator Tillis believes that Kevin Warsh is an excellent candidate" — but no vote commitment.
