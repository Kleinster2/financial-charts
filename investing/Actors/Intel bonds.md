#actor #reference #fixed-income

Complete inventory of [[Intel]] outstanding bonds. Mix of senior unsecured notes and Oregon/Arizona municipal bonds. Intel's credit story is the **inverse of the AI-era winners** — relentless downgrade trajectory as foundry losses mount.

Other credit facilities: $10B commercial paper program ($0 outstanding); $7B 5-year revolving credit (Feb 2029, undrawn); $5B 364-day credit facility (Jan 2026, undrawn). Total available liquidity ~$49.4B (incl. $37.4B cash/investments).

Seven downgrades across three agencies in under three years. Driven by foundry losses ($7B operating loss in 2023), [[AMD]] taking CPU share, missing the AI accelerator market, Pat Gelsinger's departure (Dec 2024), negative FCF, and dividend suspension (Q1 2025).
