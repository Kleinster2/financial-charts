# Peter Orszag

Peter Orszag is CEO and Chairman of [[Lazard]] (since October 2023), succeeding Ken Jacobs. Prior career: Director of the [[Office of Management and Budget]] (2009-2010) and Director of the [[Congressional Budget Office]] (2007-2008) under the [[Barack Obama]] administration; Citigroup vice chairman (2010-2016) before joining Lazard. Drives the post-2023 strategic pivot: rebalancing Lazard's mix away from cyclical M&A advisory toward private capital advisory ([[Campbell Lutyens]] acquisition / Lazard CL) and active asset management.
