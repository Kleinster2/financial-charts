# CB Insights

**CB Insights** is a New York-based market intelligence platform that tracks private company data, venture capital activity, emerging technology trends, and industry analysis. Founded in 2008 by Anand Sanwal, the company built its reputation on data-driven research reports, startup rankings, and its annual AI 100 and Fintech 250 lists — which became meaningful signals for VC deal flow and startup visibility.

The platform aggregates data from SEC filings, patent applications, earnings transcripts, news, and its own proprietary datasets to provide analytics on private markets that are otherwise opaque. Clients include corporate strategy teams, venture capital firms, and investment banks that use CB Insights for competitive intelligence, market mapping, and due diligence.

CB Insights competes with [[PitchBook]] (owned by [[Morningstar]]), [[Crunchbase]], and [[Sacra]] in the private market data space. PitchBook is the larger, more comprehensive platform; CB Insights differentiates on editorial-quality research and trend analysis rather than raw data coverage. The company has remained private and does not disclose revenue, though it has raised over $50M in venture funding.
