# Ramaco

A small-cap US metallurgical-coal producer (Central Appalachia, for seaborne and domestic steel) that has become a market story for a second reason: its Brook Mine in Wyoming, where it is developing a rare-earth and critical-minerals deposit hosted in coal — a coal-to-critical-minerals optionality the market prices speculatively. The combination makes it the highest-volatility member of the [[Coal miners]] cohort (met-coal cyclicality plus a rare-earth re-rating call). A cohort member with [[Alpha Metallurgical Resources]], [[Warrior Met Coal]], [[Peabody Energy]], and [[Core Natural Resources]].
