**Value Partners** (Value Partners Group, 806.HK) is a Hong Kong-based asset manager focused on Greater China and Asian equities and fixed income, and one of the region's larger independent fund houses. It was a cornerstone investor in the [[Lingyi iTech]] Hong Kong IPO (June 2026).
