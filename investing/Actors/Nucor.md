# Nucor

**Nucor (NUE)** — the largest US steelmaker and the bellwether of the electric-arc-furnace (EAF) mini-mill model, melting scrap rather than running blast furnaces. Headquartered in Charlotte, NC; NYSE-listed. The most product-diversified US steel producer (sheet, bar, plate, beams, joists, decking, fasteners), known for a counter-cyclical balance sheet and steady through-cycle profitability — reflected in the lowest volatility of the steel cohort (~29%). With [[Steel Dynamics]] it forms the tight EAF mini-mill pair (0.845) at the core of the [[Steel and aluminum equity beta]] cohort, distinct from the integrated/iron-ore model of [[Cleveland-Cliffs]].
