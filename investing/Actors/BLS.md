#actor #government #stub

**BLS** (Bureau of Labor Statistics) — principal US federal agency for labor economics and statistics. Publishes nonfarm payrolls (monthly employment report), CPI, PPI, Job Openings (JOLTS), productivity data, and MSA-level employment statistics. Part of the [[Department of Labor]].
