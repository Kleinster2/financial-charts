**Reema Bandar Al Saud** — [[Saudi Arabia]]'s Ambassador to the [[United States]] since 2019, the first woman to serve as a Saudi ambassador. Full title: HRH Princess Reema bint Bandar bin Sultan Al Saud. A key diplomatic bridge between [[Saudi Arabia]] and the [[United States]] during the [[Saudi Vision 2030]] era, overseeing the bilateral relationship across defense, investment, and energy ties.
