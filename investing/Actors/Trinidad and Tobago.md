# Trinidad and Tobago

Caribbean island nation and the world's largest ammonia exporter by volume. Gas-fed petrochemical industry centered on Point Lisas Industrial Estate. Exports ~4-5 Mt/year of [[Ammonia]] to the US East Coast, Latin America, and Europe. Also a major methanol exporter. Declining natural gas reserves pose long-term supply risk.
