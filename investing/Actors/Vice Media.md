Vice Media — Digital media company founded in 1994 in Montreal. Once valued at $5.7B, filed Chapter 11 bankruptcy in May 2023. Assets acquired by consortium led by Fortress Investment Group for ~$350M. Motherboard tech vertical spawned [[404 Media]].
