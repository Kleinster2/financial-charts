# Sarenza

**French online shoe retailer** — e-commerce pioneer since 2005. €200-500M revenue range, 500+ brands, 60,000+ SKUs. Acquired by Groupe Beaumanoir in 2022 after [[HLD]] ownership period. Pure-play digital, 26 European countries.

## Strategic fit with Beaumanoir

[[Groupe Beaumanoir]] (€2.1B revenue) owns fashion retail brands including Cache Cache, Bréal, Bonobo, Morgan. Sarenza acquisition adds:
- Digital capabilities
- Footwear expertise
- Pan-European logistics
- E-commerce know-how

Synergies in sourcing, logistics, digital infrastructure.
