# Kyrsten Sinema

Role: Former US Senator
State: Arizona
Party: Independent (left Democratic Party Dec 2022)
In office: 2019 — Jan 2025 (did not seek re-election)

Centrist senator known for blocking party-line Democratic legislation (Build Back Better, filibuster reform). Spoke at the [[Hill and Valley Forum]] in May 2024. Her independent stance and willingness to cross party lines aligned with the forum's bipartisan positioning.
