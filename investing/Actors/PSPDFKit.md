#actor #company #software #private

PSPDFKit (rebranded to Nutrient) — Austrian document SDK company founded by [[Peter Steinberger]] in 2011. Bootstrapped for 13 years before raising $116M from [[Insight Partners]] in 2024. Powers PDF viewing, annotation, and editing across ~1 billion user devices.

## Overview

Started as an iOS PDF framework, expanded into a cross-platform document SDK (PDF, Office, digital signatures) used by enterprises globally. Steinberger bootstrapped the company for over a decade before taking outside capital. After the Insight Partners raise, Steinberger stepped back to work on [[OpenClaw]], then joined [[OpenAI]] in Feb 2026.
