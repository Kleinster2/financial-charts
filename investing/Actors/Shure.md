Shure — American audio equipment company. Founded 1925 in Chicago. Major player in microphones, wireless systems, in-ear monitors, and headphones. Private, employee-owned. Competes with [[Sennheiser]], [[Rode Microphones]], [[Audio-Technica]], and [[Yamaha]] in pro audio.
