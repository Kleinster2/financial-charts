# Do-Fluoride

Chinese fluorine chemicals and battery materials company, listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange (002407.SZ). Operates an integrated value chain from fluorspar to [[Hydrofluoric Acid]] (anhydrous and electronic-grade) to lithium hexafluorophosphate (LiPF6) for lithium-ion battery electrolytes. Electronic-grade HF quality has reached UP-SSS level (G5) — the highest purity grade for leading-edge semiconductor processes. Founded 2003, IPO 2013 (~CNY 1.5B raised). Headquartered in Changsha, Hunan.
