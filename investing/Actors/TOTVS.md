# TOTVS

**Brazil's largest software company** — ERP, CRM, HR, financial software. ~50% Brazil ERP market share. **[[B3]]: TOTS3** (first software company in Ibovespa). 70,000+ corporate clients. **Acquired [[Linx]] for R$3.05B** (Jul 2025) after losing 2020 bidding war to [[StoneCo]].

## Investment relevance

**Why TOTVS matters:**

1. **Brazil software monopoly** — ~50% ERP market share, dominant position.

2. **Linx acquisition** — Finally got target 5 years later at half price; M&A patience example.

3. **Cloud transition** — 34% of growth from cloud; SaaS model improving margins.

4. **Techfin optionality** — JV with Itaú for embedded finance.

5. **First software in Ibovespa** — Brazil's tech blue chip.
