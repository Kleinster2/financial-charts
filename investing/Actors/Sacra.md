# Sacra

#actor #startup #private #usa #data #research

**Sacra** — Private markets research platform. Revenue/valuation estimates for pre-IPO companies. API + MCP server for programmatic access. Founded 2020 by [[Walter Chen]]. ~$1M raised. Competes with [[Tegus]], [[Visible Alpha]], [[PitchBook]].

## Competitive position

| vs | Sacra advantage | Sacra weakness |
|----|-----------------|----------------|
| [[PitchBook]] | Cheaper, API-first | Less comprehensive |
| [[Tegus]] | Revenue estimates | Smaller expert network |
| [[CB Insights]] | More focused | Less brand recognition |

**Positioning:** Research for investors, not just data. Revenue models differentiate from pure databases.

---

## Why it matters

Private company valuations increasingly important:
- Pre-IPO secondaries growing
- Employee equity decisions
- VC/PE due diligence
- Public market comps

Sacra fills gap between expensive terminals ([[PitchBook]], [[Bloomberg LP]]) and free but shallow sources.
