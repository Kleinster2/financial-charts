# Orsted

Danish renewable energy company. World's largest offshore wind developer. JV with [[Yara]] on green ammonia production at Sluiskil, Netherlands (100 MW electrolyzer, ~75 kt/year green ammonia, operational target 2025). Formerly DONG Energy (Danish Oil and Natural Gas); renamed 2017 after completing pivot from fossil fuels to renewables.

---

## US offshore wind: Trump suspension and recovery (Feb 2026)

Ørsted's two major US offshore wind projects were among five developments whose leases were suspended by the [[Donald Trump]] administration in late Dec 2025 on national security grounds.

Courts lifted both suspensions:
- Revolution Wind (JV with [[Skyborn Renewables]]): suspension lifted Jan 12, 2026. First electricity expected "within weeks" of Feb 6 reporting date, commercial operations H2 2026
- Sunrise Wind (wholly owned): suspension lifted Feb 2, 2026. Commercial operations target H2 2027

DKr600M (~€80M) impairment booked for interruption costs (Q4 2025 results).

CEO Rasmus Errboe (took over Feb 2025): "We are fully back to work and construction on both projects is moving forward according to plan."

### Strategic pivot

Errboe strengthened finances by selling stakes in US and Taiwan offshore projects and divesting European onshore business. Future focus: Europe and select Asia-Pacific markets (including Taiwan). Encouraged by European government commitments to ~15 GW/year offshore wind between 2031-2040.

After getting burned in US (2023 interest rate spike + cost overruns), Ørsted pulling back from US market for future projects.
