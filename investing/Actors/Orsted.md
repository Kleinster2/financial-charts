# Orsted

Danish renewable energy company. World's largest offshore wind developer. JV with [[Yara]] on green ammonia production at Sluiskil, Netherlands (100 MW electrolyzer, ~75 kt/year green ammonia, operational target 2025). Formerly DONG Energy (Danish Oil and Natural Gas); renamed 2017 after completing pivot from fossil fuels to renewables.
