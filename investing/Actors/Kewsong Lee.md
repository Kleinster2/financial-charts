# Kewsong Lee

**Kewsong Lee** — Former CEO of [[Carlyle]] (2018-2022). Departed abruptly Aug 2022 after conflict with founders over control and strategy. Founded BellTower Partners (2023). Vice Chair of United Soccer League (2025).

## Carlyle tenure

Joined Carlyle 2013 from [[Warburg Pincus]]. Became co-CEO 2018 with [[Glenn Youngkin]]. Sole CEO after Youngkin left (2020).

**Accomplishments:** Diversified Carlyle away from PE roots — credit assets rose from 17% to 38% of total AUM (2018-2022).

**Departure:** Fell out with founders ([[David Rubenstein]], [[William Conway Jr.]], [[Daniel D'Aniello]]) over control. Stock down 31% YTD when he left — worst among PE peers.
