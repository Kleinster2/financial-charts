Clifford Chance — Magic Circle law firm headquartered in London. One of the five largest law firms in the world by revenue. Cut 10% of its back office at end of 2025, partly in anticipation of AI taking over some tasks — among the first major law firms to explicitly link headcount reductions to AI.
