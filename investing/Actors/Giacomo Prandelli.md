## Key thesis: AI compute is structurally tied to US gas (May 1, 2026)

Argument: hyperscaler reliability requirements (99.99% uptime, 12-36 month deployment cycles) plus AI capex scale ($1.1-1.2T 2025-2027 vs $200B 2024 baseline) make gas the only viable scaling solution. Solar (23% capacity factor) and wind (34%) cannot reliably power 100+ MW campuses; battery storage doesn't span multi-day weather; nuclear takes 7-10 years. Gas plants deploy quickly + pair naturally with renewables.

Valuation gap: tech sector 34% of S&P 500 trades at high-20s-to-mid-30s P/E for AI demand; energy sector 3.35% trades at mid-teens P/E for the supply infrastructure making compute possible.

Named beneficiaries (turbine + power + LNG layer): [[GE Vernova]], [[Siemens Energy]], [[Mitsubishi]], [[Vistra]], [[Constellation Energy]], plus hyperscaler customers [[Amazon|AWS]] and [[Meta]] as long-term contract counterparties.

See [[Power constraints#"$1T AI trade runs on gas" — the structural valuation gap (May 1, 2026)|Power constraints]] for the full ingest.

## Key thesis: US is the global strategic reserve (May 4, 2026)

Argument: with Saudi 3+ mb/d spare capacity stranded by Hormuz, [[2026 UAE OPEC exit|UAE OPEC+ exit]], and [[Qatar]]/[[Kuwait]] force-majeure-on-exports, US storage tanks and production capacity are the only meaningful oil reserve the global market can actually access.

Data points:
- US crude exports 6.4 mb/d (record); combined crude + refined 12.9 mb/d (highest in EIA history)
- US near-net-crude-exporter status on weekly basis (first since WWII)
- One-week US system drawdown ~26 M barrels (commercial 6.2M + SPR 7.1M)
- China liquidating 1.3B-barrel 2025 stockpile (March imports -2.5% YoY)
- Storage-buffer depletion timeline: September 2026

Pairs with [[Helima Croft]] "fine print on reopening" + [[Adam Tooze]] "big oil not coming to Trump's rescue" capital-discipline read: there is no faster supply rescue available, which concentrates the strategic-reserve role on the US Gulf Coast export apparatus.

See [[US Energy Independence Six Countries#May 4, 2026 — "America Is Now the World's Strategic Reserve" (Giacomo Prandelli)|US Energy Independence Six Countries]] for the full ingest.
