# Cape of Good Hope

**Cape of Good Hope** is the southern tip of Africa used as an alternative shipping route when the Suez/Red Sea corridor is disrupted. Rerouting adds 10-14 days for Middle East-to-Europe voyages. Not a bottleneck but a cost and time penalty. Saw surge traffic during the 2024 Houthi disruption and the 2026 [[Strait of Hormuz]] closure.
