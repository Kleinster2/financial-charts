# Toteme

**Swedish minimalist fashion house** — modern uniform aesthetic. Founded 2014 in Stockholm by Elin Kling (influencer/editor) and Karl Lindman. €129M revenue (2023), profitable since 2015. Altor took minority stake 2021. Founders retain majority, committed long-term.

## Altor investment (2021)

Nordic PE firm Altor took minority stake. €8.3B total fund commitments. Focus: medium-sized Nordic companies, growth initiatives.

Patient capital approach — no forced exit timeline aligned with founders' long-term vision.
