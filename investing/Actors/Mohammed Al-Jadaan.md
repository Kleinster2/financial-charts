**Mohammed Al-Jadaan** — Minister of Finance of [[Saudi Arabia]] since 2017. Architect of Saudi fiscal austerity measures and a central figure in [[Saudi Vision 2030]] fiscal planning. Previously served as Chairman of the Capital Markets Authority (CMA), the Saudi securities regulator.
