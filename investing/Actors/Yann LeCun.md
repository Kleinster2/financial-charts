## Key contributions

**Convolutional Neural Networks (1989):**
- LeNet architecture for handwritten digit recognition
- Revolutionized computer vision
- Foundation for modern image AI

**Backpropagation:**
- Early form proposed during PhD (1987)
- Core algorithm for training neural networks

**Other:**
- DjVu image compression
- Lush programming language

## Views on AI

Vocal critic of AGI hype. Advocates for "world models" — AI that understands physics and causality rather than just pattern matching. Skeptical of LLM-only approaches to human-level AI.
