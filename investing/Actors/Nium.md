## Why it matters for the investing vault

For [[Coinbase]]: Nium provides the global payment infrastructure Coinbase needs to monetize USDC for cross-border B2B without building its own correspondent-banking network. Coinbase's stablecoin revenue ($305M Q1 2026, +11% YoY) is structurally tied to circulation; Nium expands the addressable circulation footprint.

For [[Stripe]]: a competing horizontal cross-border payments rail. Nium does not run a wallet stack like Privy.

For [[Circle]]: any USDC distributed via Nium accrues to Circle's reserve revenue base, of which Coinbase shares.
