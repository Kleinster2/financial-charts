# National Samsung Electronics Union

National Samsung Electronics Union -- largest labor union at [[Samsung]], representing workers in the South Korean electronics and semiconductor operations. Its 2026 wage dispute became a direct supply-chain risk because Samsung is the world's largest [[Memory|memory]]-chip maker.
