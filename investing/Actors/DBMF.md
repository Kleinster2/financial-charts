## What It Tracks

DBMF runs a regression-based "Dynamic Beta" model that estimates the aggregate positioning of the largest managed-futures funds across equities, rates, currencies, and commodities, then holds those futures directly. The aim is the diversifying return stream of a CTA without the fees, lockups, or single-manager selection risk.

## Use Case

A crisis hedge / portfolio "freio." Managed futures are one of the few liquid strategies that can be positively convex in equity selloffs — trend-followers flip short into sustained declines — so DBMF complements a long-equity book rather than tracking it. It earned its keep in 2022 when stocks and bonds fell together. Liquid and penalty-free, unlike the structured products that crowd retail portfolios.
