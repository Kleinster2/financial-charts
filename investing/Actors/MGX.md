## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Massive sovereign capital
- AI-focused mandate
- Global deal access (Stargate, TikTok)
- UAE government backing

**Bear:**
- Geopolitical complexity (US-UAE-China)
- Execution on $100B target
- Competition from Saudi PIF
- G42/China concerns (US scrutiny)
