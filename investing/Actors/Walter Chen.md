Walter Chen is CEO and co-founder of [[Sacra]], a private company research platform that provides in-depth financial analysis and market maps for private tech companies — effectively a PitchBook alternative built on proprietary research rather than database aggregation. A three-time founder: iDoneThis (productivity tool, acquired 2015), Animalz (content marketing agency serving SaaS companies), and Sacra. Angel investor at Golden Wok. Cornell (math/CS), Michigan Law.

Sacra's model is notable in the private markets research space: instead of scraping Crunchbase and SEC filings like most competitors, it produces analyst-written deep dives on private companies with revenue estimates, competitive positioning, and business model breakdowns. The platform has become a frequently cited source for private company valuations in tech media and venture capital.
