## Pipeline

Lead asset is a T-cell engager antibody treatment in early-stage trials targeting four autoimmune diseases. [[RBC Capital Markets]] analyst Evan Seigerman described it as a "pipeline-in-a-product" opportunity with potential expansion into multiple myeloma.

[[Gilead]] in advanced discussions with [[Galapagos]] to co-develop some of Ouro's medicines.
