**Sandton Capital Partners** is a [[New York City|NYC]]-based distressed credit investor, co-founded by Rael Nurick. Earliest financial backer of [[Nscale]], transitioning from ~$110M in distressed loans (15-17.5% interest, steep OID) to lead equity investor (Series A) and top-5 shareholder in a $14.6B company. Nurick sits on Nscale's board.
