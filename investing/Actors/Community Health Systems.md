# Community Health Systems

Community Health Systems (NYSE: CYH) is a US acute-care hospital operator concentrated in non-urban and mid-size markets. It is the most heavily leveraged and distressed of the listed operators — a multi-year divestiture and deleveraging story whose equity (≈$3) trades on balance-sheet survival as much as on operating fundamentals. That idiosyncratic risk makes it the outlier of the [[Hospital operators]] cohort.
