# Tony Blair Institute

UK-headquartered think tank founded in 2016 by former [[UK]] prime minister Tony Blair, formally the Tony Blair Institute for Global Change (TBI). Operates across geopolitics, technology policy, economic strategy, and government modernisation; partners with governments globally on policy design. The Economic Security unit produced the May 15, 2026 [[Economic latency]] framework via [[Guy Ward-Jackson]].

## Why it matters

TBI sits in the small set of policy shops that publish operationally-detailed industrial-policy proposals — concrete fund sizes, eligibility tests, contracting mechanisms — rather than the more common high-level framings. The [[Economic latency]] framework's three-tier hierarchy and contingent-contract architecture is an example of this style. Treat TBI's economic-statecraft output as policy-ready proposals, not academic framings.
