**IWC** is a Swiss luxury watchmaker (International Watch Company), founded in 1868 in Schaffhausen by American watchmaker Florentine Ariosto Jones. Owned by [[Richemont]] since 2000 as part of its Specialist Watchmakers division. One of the few Swiss manufactures based in German-speaking Switzerland rather than the French-speaking Jura region, IWC is known for its engineering-forward approach, in-house movements, and strong associations with aviation (Pilot's Watch, Big Pilot) and nautical heritage (Portugieser, Aquatimer). Annual production is estimated at 70,000-80,000 pieces, placing it in the upper tier of Richemont's watch brands but below [[Cartier]] in revenue contribution.
