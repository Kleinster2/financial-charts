**IWC** — Swiss luxury watchmaker (International Watch Company), founded 1868 in Schaffhausen. Owned by [[Richemont]]. Known for Portugieser, Pilot's Watch, and Big Pilot collections. One of the few Swiss manufactures based in German-speaking Switzerland rather than French-speaking regions.
