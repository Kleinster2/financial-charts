#actor #company #public #data-center

Thumzup Media — formerly a U.S. marketing / social-media company that merged with publicly-listed [[Dogehash Technologies]] (a Dominari/American Ventures-affiliated meme-coin shell) and subsequently rebranded as a data-center company. The rebrand makes Thumzup the only American Ventures pipeline deal to date that has changed sectors materially post-merger — from marketing to data-center infrastructure, riding the AI-compute capex cycle.
