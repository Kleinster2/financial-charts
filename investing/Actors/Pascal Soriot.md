# Pascal Soriot

**Pascal Soriot** — Chief Executive of [[AstraZeneca]] since October 2012; the longest-serving CEO in the FTSE 100 pharma cohort and the architect of AstraZeneca's pipeline turnaround (oncology franchise — Tagrisso, Enhertu via the Daiichi Sankyo partnership; the COVID-19 vaccine; Alexion rare-disease acquisition). A French-trained veterinarian and former Genentech/Roche executive who took over AstraZeneca at its post-patent-cliff nadir and rebuilt it into the UK's largest listed drugmaker. A recurring industry voice on US-Europe drug-pricing tension and the [[Pharma R&D burden-sharing|burden-sharing]] fight.
