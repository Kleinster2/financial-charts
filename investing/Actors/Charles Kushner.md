**Charles Kushner** is the father of [[Jared Kushner]] and [[Josh Kushner]], founder of [[Kushner Companies]]. Convicted in 2005 of illegal campaign contributions, tax evasion, and witness tampering (sentenced to 2 years federal prison). Currently serving as U.S. Ambassador to France under the second [[Donald Trump]] administration.
