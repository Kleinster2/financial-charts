#actor #retail #discount

**Action** is a Dutch non-food discount variety retailer founded in 1993 in Enkhuizen. 3,302 stores across 15 European countries, EUR 16.0B revenue (2025), 84,246 employees. Majority-owned (65.3%) by [[3i Group]]. Expanding to the US in late 2027/early 2028.
