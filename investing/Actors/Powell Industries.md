# Powell Industries

Powell Industries (Nasdaq: POWL) is a US small-cap maker of custom-engineered electrical distribution and control equipment — switchgear, bus duct, and integrated power-control systems — for utility, industrial, oil-and-gas/LNG, and increasingly data-center markets. It is the smallest and most concentrated of the listed [[Datacenter power and cooling]] equipment names, and re-rated sharply through 2023–2025 on the combined LNG-buildout and AI-datacenter power demand. Its partly LNG/oil-gas-driven order book makes it the loosest member of the power/cooling cohort.
