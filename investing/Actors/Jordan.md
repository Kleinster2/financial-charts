#actor #country #mena

**Jordan** — Middle Eastern kingdom bordering [[Israel]], [[Saudi Arabia]], Iraq, and Syria. Resource-light economy with one structurally important export: Dead Sea mineral extraction. [[Arab Potash Company]] is a major global potash producer, and Jordan Phosphate Mines Company is a significant phosphate exporter. Jordan and Israel jointly exploit Dead Sea brines under a 1994 peace treaty framework.
