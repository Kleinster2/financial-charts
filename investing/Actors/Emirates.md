# Emirates

**Emirates** — flag carrier of [[UAE|Dubai]], largest international airline by revenue passenger-kilometers. Hub at Dubai International Airport (DXB) — world's busiest international airport. Wholly owned by [[Investment Corporation of Dubai]].

## Iran conflict impact (March 2026)

Operating reduced flight schedule to significantly fewer destinations since [[Operation Epic Fury|2026 Iran conflict]]. Adding 1+ hour to most routes avoiding Gulf, Iranian, and Iraqi airspace. Dubai airport hit by drones on at least 4 occasions since resuming traffic — aircraft placed in holding patterns. See [[Iran conflict airline disruption]].
