**FIFA** is the global governing body of association football (soccer), headquartered in Zurich with 211 member associations. President [[Gianni Infantino]] has led since 2016. Vault relevance centers on [[Saudi Arabia]] hosting the [[2034 FIFA World Cup]] -- estimated $25-30B in stadium costs across 15 venues, with [[Saudi PIF]] involvement in infrastructure delivery.
