**The Hour Glass** — Singapore-based luxury watch retailer, publicly listed on SGX (AGS.SI). Founded 1979 by Henry Tay. Operates ~50 boutiques across Asia-Pacific (Singapore, Malaysia, Australia, Japan, Thailand, Vietnam). One of the largest independent multi-brand luxury watch retailers in the Asia-Pacific region.
