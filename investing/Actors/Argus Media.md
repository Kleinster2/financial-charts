Argus Media is a London-based independent price reporting agency (PRA) providing benchmark pricing, market intelligence, and analytics for global commodity and energy markets. Founded in 1970, the firm publishes price assessments used as settlement references in physical and derivatives contracts across crude oil, refined products, natural gas, LPG, biofuels, metals, agriculture, and emissions markets.

Argus operates alongside [[Platts]] ([[S&P Global]]) and [[OPIS]] as one of the three dominant PRAs in energy commodities. Its price assessments — particularly in US crude (Argus Sour Crude Index), European refined products, and biofuels — serve as contractual benchmarks for billions of dollars in physical trade. The firm has expanded aggressively into [[Sustainable Aviation Fuel|SAF]] and biofuel price reporting as those markets mature, tracking Chinese SAF exports to Europe and feedstock pricing globally. Argus employs over 1,000 staff across 25+ offices and remains privately held, owned by management and [[General Atlantic]].
