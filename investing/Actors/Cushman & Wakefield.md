# Cushman & Wakefield

Global commercial real estate services firm, one of the three largest alongside [[CBRE]] and [[JLL]]. NYSE: CWK. Provides leasing, capital markets, property management, valuation, and advisory services across office, industrial, retail, and multifamily sectors worldwide.

Founded in 1917 in New York. Currently headquartered in Chicago. The firm went public in 2018 after a decade under private equity ownership ([[DTZ]] merger in 2015 backed by TPG, PAG Asia, and the Ontario Teachers' Pension Plan). John Santora, who spent 47 years at Cushman & Wakefield, became [[WeWork]] CEO in June 2024 to lead the restructured company post-bankruptcy.
