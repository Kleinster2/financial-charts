## Investment case

**Bull:**
- Jewelry = resilient luxury category
- Cartier brand strength
- Celebrity tailwinds
- Americas/Japan strong
- Store of value narrative

**Bear:**
- High valuation
- Margin pressure (costs, FX)
- China recovery uncertain
- Watch market weak (ex-Richemont)
- Profit-taking after run
