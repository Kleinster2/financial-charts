#actor #company #services

**Sitel** — global customer-experience / business-process-outsourcing services firm; in 2022, a Sitel subcontractor's compromised laptop was the supply-chain entry vector through which [[LAPSUS$]] obtained initial access to [[Okta]]'s customer-support tenant. Sitel was an Okta third-party support vendor providing customer-service tier operations; the compromised engineer's machine carried credentials that could view approximately 366 Okta customer environments. The January 2022 incident detection lagged its January 25 public confirmation by four days; LAPSUS$ posted screenshots in March 2022 to demonstrate access, prompting the public crisis. The Sitel / Okta supply-chain breach became a textbook case for third-party-vendor risk in the identity-provider category and shaped subsequent SOC2 Type 2 audit scope across the IdP sector. Sitel rebranded as Foundever in March 2023 following its merger with Sykes Enterprises.
