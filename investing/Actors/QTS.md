## Why QTS matters

Scale — QTS is the largest private data center platform, with capacity that has tripled since the Blackstone acquisition. The $25B+ pipeline dwarfs public REIT competitors.

Hyperscale focus — primary customers are hyperscalers ([[AWS]], Azure, [[Google Cloud|GCP]]) and large enterprises needing wholesale capacity for AI workloads.

Blackstone's AI infrastructure bet — QTS is central to Blackstone's thesis that AI will drive unprecedented data center demand. The platform receives priority capital allocation.
