# Joe Gebbia

Co-founder of [[Airbnb]] (2008, with [[Brian Chesky]] and Nathan Blecharczyk). Designer by training (RISD). Left Airbnb operations in 2022 but remains on the board.

## DOGE / National Design Studio

Appointed by [[Donald Trump]] as the first-ever US Chief Design Officer, leading the **National Design Studio** within [[DOGE]]. Mission: redesign government services to be "as seamless as the Apple App Store." Executive order requires agency heads to consult with Gebbia for "initial results by July 4, 2026."

## Samara

Co-founded **Samara**, an accessory dwelling unit (ADU) startup building prefab backyard homes. Starting at $299K for 430 sq ft studios in the Bay Area. Applies Airbnb design thinking to housing supply.
