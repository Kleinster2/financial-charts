## Rebrand to AMC Global Media (Apr 2026)

On April 8, 2026 the company amended its articles of incorporation (Nevada) to become AMC Global Media Inc., keeping the AMCX ticker — a marker of its pivot to streaming as the primary revenue engine. Share classes (A/B) unaffected.

## Apr 2026 — debt maturity wall pushed out

Exchange offer announced February 23, 2026: any-and-all swap of the 10.25% senior secured notes due 2029 into new 10.50% senior secured notes due 2032. Outcome: $830.6M exchanged at early settlement plus $30.7M late-tendered — 98.43% of the outstanding principal; the residual redeemed April 6, 2026 at 105.125% plus accrued interest. Pushes the maturity wall out three years at a 25bp coupon cost — removing the near-term refinancing overhang. [[Seeking Alpha]] upgraded to Buy in the same window, citing stabilizing revenue and the improving streaming transition.

The stock re-rated through the event window: $6.52 (Mar 27) → $7.17 (Apr 2, +2.4% on the day) → $7.69 (Apr 14), and reached $10.08 by June 11.
