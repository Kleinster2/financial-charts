# John Kartsonas

Founder and managing partner of [[Breakwave Advisors]], the issuer of [[BWET]] (tanker shipping ETF) and [[BDRY]] (dry bulk shipping ETF). Public-facing voice for the futures-based shipping ETF franchise; quoted in Bloomberg (April 2026) on the 2026 tanker rate rally and its transition from war-driven to fundamentals-driven.

## Views

Post-Hormuz thesis transition (April 2026): "The story right now is war and disruption, but this could transition to a fundamental story" — concentrated ownership ([[Sinokor Group]]), sanctions shrinking the compliant fleet, and aging global vessel base support tanker rates independent of the conflict.

On BWET's 3.5% expense ratio: headline fee drops with AUM scale; fund's use of futures leaves most assets in cash earning interest, offsetting costs. Mechanical reminder: "If rates decline, the fund will also decline."
