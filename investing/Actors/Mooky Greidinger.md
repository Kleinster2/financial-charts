# Mooky Greidinger

**Moshe "Mooky" Joseph Greidinger** — Israeli businessman, former CEO of [[Cineworld]] / [[Regal Cinemas]]. Third-generation cinema family — grandparents established movie theaters in Haifa in the 1930s.

## Career

- Grew up working in father's cinemas in Israel during school years
- Joined family business 1976, expanded internationally
- Built Cineworld into world's second-largest cinema chain
- Led through Chapter 11 bankruptcy (Sep 2022) and emergence (Aug 2023)
- Received suspended jail sentence in Israel (2022) over monopolies breach
