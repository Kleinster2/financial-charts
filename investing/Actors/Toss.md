**Toss** — South Korea's leading financial super app. 24M MAU (~half of Korea's population). Operated by Viva Republica. Planning US IPO Q2 2026 at $10-15B valuation — would be largest Korean US IPO since [[Coupang]] ($4.6B, 2021). Founded by ex-dentist Lee Seung-gun after 8 failed startups. First operating profit 2023.
