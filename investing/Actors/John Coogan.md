**John Coogan** is an entrepreneur, YouTube creator, and co-host/co-founder of [[TBPN]] (The B1M Podcast Network). Previously co-founded [[Soylent]] as CTO (the meal-replacement startup that grew to ~$100M revenue), co-founded Lucy Goods (a [[Y Combinator]] batch company), and served as entrepreneur-in-residence at [[Founders Fund]]. His YouTube channel (~430K subscribers) covers tech, business, and venture capital topics with a data-driven, long-form explainer format. Alongside co-host [[Jordi Hays]], TBPN has become a notable voice in tech media, particularly on startup economics and venture dynamics.
