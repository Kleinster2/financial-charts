## Core argument

Russia has built a comprehensive sovereign ecosystem around unmanned systems and AI — top-down strategy with measurable targets, civilian industry as primary innovation source, state champion-picking that scales the few systems that work, and absence of Western-style ethical or legal constraints on autonomous weapons. Russia is "way ahead of NATO" at the operational-deployment level, even where the underlying technology is not always superior.
