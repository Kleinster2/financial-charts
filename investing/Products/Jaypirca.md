## Jun 2026 European CLL opinion

On Jun 26, 2026, WSJ's close recap said Lilly rose 7.1% after the company reported a positive European regulatory opinion recommending Jaypirca for chronic lymphocytic leukemia. In local `prices_long`, LLY closed at $1,208.12, up 7.13% on the day.

That matters because Lilly's core bear case is not only GLP-1 competition; it is concentration risk at a very high market cap.
