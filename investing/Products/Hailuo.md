## Positioning per FT (May 17, 2026)

Per the FT (Eleanor Olcott in Beijing), Hailuo is one of the three Chinese video models [[Director AI]] founder Ben Chiang switches between depending on task and cost — alongside [[Kling]] (his primary tool) and [[Seedance]] 2.0:

> *"Director AI mainly uses Kling, developed by Kuaishou, while also switching between ByteDance's Seedance 2.0 and Hailuo from start-up MiniMax, depending on the task and cost."*

This is the "top quality tier" framing for Hailuo even though the FT's Arena leaderboard chart does not show Hailuo in the top 4 of any category (text-to-video, image-to-video, or video edit). The gap between "benchmarked best" and "professionally used" is the gap between Arena scores and production tool selection — pricing, API access, and task-specific quality matter as much as headline rankings for working creators.

[[MiniMax]] is also one of the Chinese AI labs accused by [[Anthropic]] (Feb 23 2026) of large-scale distillation of [[Claude]] — relevant context for how MiniMax built capability faster than its training-compute footprint would suggest.

*Source: [FT](https://www.ft.com/content/9804b1de-653b-40b2-bffb-17c76ebebe34), May 17 2026.*
