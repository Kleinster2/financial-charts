**Tianyi Cloud** (天翼云) — [[China Telecom]]'s public-cloud platform and the #2 public-cloud IaaS provider in [[China]] behind [[Alibaba Cloud]]. FY2025 revenue exceeded RMB120bn, far larger than the cloud businesses of [[China Mobile]] (Mobile Cloud, ~RMB56bn H1) or [[China Unicom]]. The vehicle that makes China Telecom the cloud/AI leader of the three state telecoms, built on the [[东数西算]] national computing buildout.
