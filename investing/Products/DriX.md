# DriX

Autonomous surface drone series made by [[Exail Technologies]] for hydrography, surveillance, and defense. DriX H-8: fleet of 5 sold to European navy for surveillance. DriX H-9: new long-range model with up to 20-day autonomy (2x the H-8). First H-9 sale to a global hydrographic authority; second to [[France|French Navy]] SHOM. DriX sales +75% in 2025.
