**Khorramshahr** -- [[Iran]]'s longest-range confirmed ballistic missile, a liquid-fueled medium-range ballistic missile derived from [[North Korea]]'s BM-25/Musudan (itself based on the Soviet R-27 submarine-launched missile). At full 1,800 kg payload, range is approximately 2,000 km; with a lightened reentry vehicle (~750 kg), range extends to an estimated 3,000 km per [[United Kingdom|UK]]/[[France]]/[[Germany]] assessments. Managed by the [[IRGC]] Aerospace Force. The missile that defines [[Iran]]'s strategic reach ceiling -- and the [[range-payload tradeoff]] that prevents it from delivering a nuclear warhead beyond regional distances.
