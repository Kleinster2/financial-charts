# Gemini Omni

Gemini Omni — [[Google]]'s video-first generative media model in the [[Gemini]] family. Announced at Google I/O 2026, Omni Flash creates and edits video from text, image, audio, and video inputs and is distributed through Gemini, [[Google Flow]], [[YouTube]] Shorts, and YouTube Create.

---

## The story

Gemini Omni is Google's move to make Gemini a creation tool, not only a reasoning or search layer. The important product shift is conversational editing: the user can ask for changes to an existing video rather than starting from a prompt-only generation flow. That matters because most useful creator work is iterative.

The distribution path is as important as the model. Gemini Omni appears in the Gemini app for subscribers, in [[Google Flow]] for creative workflows, and in [[YouTube]] products for short-form remixing. Google is therefore using Omni to connect three loops: consumer AI subscriptions, creator tooling, and YouTube supply growth.

The competitive read is that generative video is expensive and quality-sensitive. Google has the model lab, cloud runtime, and distribution, but the product will be judged on controllability, rights/provenance, and whether creators actually publish Omni-assisted work rather than treating it as a demo surface.
