#product #etf #credit #fixed-income

Man Active Income ETF — actively managed credit ETF by [[Man Group]]. Invests across corporate, government, and securitized debt using a bottom-up, unconstrained approach. No fixed benchmark — allocates by margin of safety across sectors and geographies through the cycle. Launched Sep 16, 2025 alongside sister fund [[Man Active High Yield ETF|MHY]]. Man Group's first standalone ETFs.

## Strategy

- Bottom-up credit selection across multiple sectors: corporate, government, securitized
- No fixed geographical or sectoral allocation — rotates through cycle
- Margin of safety guides allocation rather than benchmark replication
- At least 80% of net assets in investment-grade and high-yield debt instruments
- ~30 concentrated positions (high-conviction)
- Gives retail access to [[Man Group]]'s $42.7B global credit platform — previously institutional-only strategies
