# YU7 GT

Performance variant of the [[YU7]] SUV; [[Xiaomi]]'s first vehicle developed with European engineers and the model the company is using to enter Europe. Announced at the April 2026 Beijing Auto Show by [[Lei Jun]], who said the YU7 GT "can match the standards of a top-tier German car." Launch targeted for late May 2026.

## Why it matters

The YU7 GT is the entry vehicle for Xiaomi's European push. Xiaomi opened an EV R&D center in [[Munich]] in 2025 staffed by more than 75 engineers; the YU7 GT is the first product output. Lei Jun's "top-tier German car" framing positions Xiaomi explicitly against [[Porsche]], [[BMW]], and [[Mercedes-Benz]] in the premium segment, rather than competing with European volume carmakers on price.

Analyst Matthias Schmidt has argued Xiaomi can succeed in Europe but more likely "at the expense of volume carmakers rather than German premiums" given how long the rite of passage to a premium-brand badge typically takes.
