# PixVerse

AI video generation platform from AISphere (founder: Wang Changhu, formerly Microsoft Research Asia and [[ByteDance]]). Launched globally in January 2024 and scaled to one of the largest consumer-facing video generation products in the world. AISphere's R1 model offers real-time interactive world modeling with ~2-second latency, drawing interest from the gaming industry.
