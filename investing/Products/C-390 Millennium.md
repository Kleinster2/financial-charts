## UAE order (May 2026) — first Middle Eastern customer

On May 4, 2026, the UAE Ministry of Defence announced the purchase of 10 C-390 aircraft. Contract value not publicly disclosed. The deal follows UAE President [[Mohamed bin Zayed]]'s February 2026 meeting with [[Lula]] on trade, technology, and defense cooperation and is consistent with the UAE's BRICS-aligned posture since accession in January 2024. The order is the first by any Middle Eastern state and opens the addressable market for Gulf partners (Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait) that historically defaulted to C-130J or A400M.

Parallel to the platform purchase, UAE state-owned [[EDGE Group]] holds equity in two Brazilian defense firms — 50% of [[SIATT]] (since Sept 2023) and 51% of [[Condor Non-Lethal Technologies]] (since May 2024) — concentrating the UAE-Brazil defense industrial relationship in São José dos Campos.
