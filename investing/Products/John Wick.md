#product #media #franchise

**John Wick** is [[Lionsgate]]'s flagship action franchise — created by Derek Kolstad, directed by Chad Stahelski, and starring Keanu Reeves — that crossed $1 billion in worldwide box office in 2023 and is among the studio's most valuable owned IP.

## Box office

| Film | Year | Worldwide gross |
|------|------|-----------------|
| John Wick | 2014 | ~$86M |
| John Wick: Chapter 2 | 2017 | $174.3M |
| John Wick: Chapter 3 – Parabellum | 2019 | $328.3M |
| John Wick: Chapter 4 | 2023 | $447M+ |
| From the World of John Wick: Ballerina | 2025 | ~$131M |

The mainline films show a textbook escalation — each chapter roughly doubling the prior through Chapter 3, peaking with Chapter 4 — which is what pushed the franchise past $1B and made it a core [[Lionsgate]] asset. The 2025 spinoff *Ballerina* (Ana de Armas) broke the trend: at ~$131M worldwide against a large budget it was reported as a ~$94M loss, opening behind *Lilo & Stitch*, and dragged [[Lionsgate]]'s first-half FY2026 results.

*Ballerina*'s miss raised questions about how far the universe can extend beyond Keanu Reeves; the mainline sequel and Donnie Yen spinoff are the test of whether the franchise is a durable annuity or a single-star vehicle. See [[Lionsgate]] → Franchises and slate.
