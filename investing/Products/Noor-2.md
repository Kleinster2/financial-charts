# Noor-2

Iranian military imaging satellite operated by the [[IRGC]] Aerospace Force. Estimated resolution 12-15 metres, insufficient to identify aircraft or monitor vehicle-level activity at foreign military bases. Succeeded by [[Noor-3]] (~5 m claimed); both were substantially exceeded by the Chinese-built [[TEE-01B]] acquired by the IRGC in late 2024 (see [[IRGC Chinese satellite targeting March 2026]]).
