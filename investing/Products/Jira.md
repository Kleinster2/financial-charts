**Jira** is [[Atlassian]]'s project and issue tracking platform, serving as the de facto enterprise standard for software development workflow management. Originally launched in 2002 as a bug tracker, it evolved into a full agile project management suite spanning software development (Jira Software), IT service management (Jira Service Management, formerly Opsgenie), and business project tracking (Jira Work Management). The platform anchors Atlassian's cloud ecosystem alongside [[Confluence]], Bitbucket, and Trello, with deep integrations into CI/CD pipelines, version control, and developer tooling. Jira's moat is workflow lock-in: once an engineering organization builds its processes around Jira's ticket taxonomy, sprint boards, and automation rules, switching costs become prohibitive.
