# Google Pics

Google Pics — [[Google]]'s AI image creation and editing surface announced in the Workspace-heavy I/O 2026 update. It sits near [[Google Workspace]] in the product map, giving Gemini users a first-party place to create, edit, and adapt images for work and communication.

---

## The story

Google Pics is the image-side counterpart to [[Gemini Omni]] and [[Google Flow]]. Omni and Flow target video and creator workflows; Pics targets everyday image work inside the productivity suite. The strategic point is to keep visual creation inside Google's account and document graph instead of sending users to standalone image tools.

This is a smaller product than [[Search AI Mode]] or [[Gemini Spark]], but it matters as part of the suite logic. If Workspace users can dictate, draft, triage, and edit images without leaving Google apps, Gemini becomes workflow infrastructure rather than a chatbot destination.
