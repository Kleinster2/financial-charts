# Ask YouTube

Ask YouTube — [[YouTube]]'s conversational search and discovery feature powered by [[Gemini]]. Announced in the Google I/O 2026 bundle, it turns YouTube's video library into a question-answering and exploration surface rather than a keyword search box only.

---

## The story

Ask YouTube matters because YouTube is not just entertainment inventory; it is a massive how-to, education, review, music, and creator archive. Conversational search lets Google expose that archive through Gemini while keeping the user inside YouTube's monetized environment.

The feature is also a defensive move. If users ask ChatGPT or Perplexity for answers that would historically have led to YouTube videos, YouTube risks losing discovery intent before the video impression happens. Ask YouTube keeps that intent native and gives Gemini a media-rich corpus that standalone assistants cannot replicate as cleanly.
