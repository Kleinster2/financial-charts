## Overhaul (2026)

Major upgrade powered by [[Gemini]] under multiyear [[Apple-Google AI deal]]:
- Chatbot-style conversational interaction
- On-screen content analysis
- Personal data integration (apps, calendar, files)
- Universal search improvements

**Timeline (updated Feb 12):** ~~iOS 26.4 (March)~~ delayed — many capabilities now iOS 26.5 (**May/June 2026**). Personal context features likely **iOS 27 (September 2026)**. Full chatbot revamp expected with fall iPhone launch.

Runs through Apple devices and [[Private Cloud Compute]] — no user data shared with [[Google]].
