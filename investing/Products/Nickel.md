#stub

Nickel is a base metal critical to stainless steel production (~70% of demand) and increasingly to lithium-ion battery cathodes (NMC, NCA chemistries). Global production ~3.6 Mt (2024). Indonesia dominates supply via NPI (nickel pig iron) and HPAL (high-pressure acid leaching) expansion.
