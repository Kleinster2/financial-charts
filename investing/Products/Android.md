# Android

Android — [[Google]]'s mobile operating system and one of the largest distribution surfaces for [[Gemini]]. At Google I/O 2026, Android's AI role expanded through Gemini-powered intelligent eyewear, Android XR, and [[Android Halo]], the status layer for live AI agents.

---

## Why it matters

Android is the bridge between Gemini as a software assistant and Gemini as ambient computing. Search, Workspace, and YouTube are app surfaces; Android is the system surface. That gives Google a place to show when an agent is active, route permissions, and carry AI from phones into eyewear and other XR devices.

The I/O 2026 direction suggests Google is preparing for agents that persist beyond a chat window. [[Android Halo]] is the key trust mechanism: if an agent is working in the background, the user needs to see it, interrupt it, and understand what it is doing. Without that status layer, background agency becomes a UX and safety problem.
