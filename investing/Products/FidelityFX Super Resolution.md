# FidelityFX Super Resolution

#product #graphics #gaming #upscaling

FidelityFX Super Resolution is [[AMD]]'s game upscaling technology. In the [[Steam Machine]] context, Valve uses FSR as part of the 4K/60 performance target rather than promising native 4K rendering across the Steam library.
