# K-STER C

Expendable underwater mine disposal ROV made by [[Exail Technologies]]. Guided to seafloor mines and IEDs, neutralizes with shaped charge. Developed by [[ECA Group]] in 2008. Backlog >1,000 units. Major orders: ~€60M from [[Belgium]]/[[Netherlands]] (rMCM program, 2024), ~€40M via [[NATO]]/NSPA framework (Jan 2026, "several hundred" units). Production at Ostend, Belgium facility.
