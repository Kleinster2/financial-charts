**F-35** (Lightning II) — [[Lockheed Martin]]'s 5th-generation stealth multirole fighter. World's most expensive weapons program (~$1.7T lifecycle). Three variants for US and allied forces. The benchmark that European programs like [[FCAS]] and [[Global Combat Air Programme]] aim to reduce dependence on.
