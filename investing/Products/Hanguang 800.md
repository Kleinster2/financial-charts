The Hanguang 800 (含光800) is [[Alibaba]]'s earlier-generation AI inference chip, developed by T-Head ([[DAMO Academy]]). Announced September 2019 at Alibaba Cloud's Apsara Conference. Neural processing unit (NPU) optimized for inference workloads. Deployed on [[Alibaba]] Cloud for image search, video analysis, and recommendation systems. Predecessor to the [[Zhenwu 810E]].
