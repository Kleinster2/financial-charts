# Steam Controller

#product #gaming #hardware #controller

Steam Controller is [[Valve]]'s couch-input layer for [[Steam platform|Steam]], [[Steam Deck]], [[Steam Machine]], and [[Steam Frame]]. The second-generation model launched May 4, 2026 at $99 in the United States.

## Why it matters

The controller is the least glamorous but most necessary part of the Steam living-room strategy. A console-like PC fails if users need a keyboard and mouse on the couch. Steam Controller extends the [[Steam Deck]] input model to a separate gamepad: trackpads for mouse-oriented games, gyro for aiming, grip buttons, and Steam Input profiles for the long tail of PC games.
