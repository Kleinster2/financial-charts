**SM-3** -- the [[United States]] Navy's primary exo-atmospheric ballistic missile interceptor, part of the [[Aegis]] combat system. Manufactured by [[RTX]] (Raytheon Missiles & Defense) with [[Aerojet Rocketdyne]] providing the kinetic warhead's divert thruster. Uses hit-to-kill technology -- no explosive warhead, it destroys incoming missiles through kinetic impact at closing speeds exceeding 10 km/s. Deployed on Aegis-equipped destroyers and cruisers, and at Aegis Ashore sites in [[Romania]] and [[Poland]]. An SM-3 successfully intercepted an Iranian intermediate-range ballistic missile targeting [[Diego Garcia]] on March 21, 2026 -- the system's first confirmed combat intercept of an IRBM-class target.
