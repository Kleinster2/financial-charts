**Chase Pay Over Time** -- [[JPMorgan Chase]]'s credit card installment product. Converts eligible purchases of $100+ into fixed monthly payments over 3--24 months. Formerly "My Chase Plan."

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Parent | [[JPMorgan Chase]] |
| Channel | Chase credit cards (consumer) |
| Min purchase | $100 |
| Term | 3--24 months |
| Interest | 0% (fixed monthly fee instead) |
| Monthly fee | Up to 1.72% of purchase amount |
| Max active plans | 10 per card |
| Rewards | Earned on original purchase amount |

Available on most consumer Chase credit cards. Business cards not eligible. Also available at [[Amazon]] checkout for eligible cardholders.

Sibling product to [[Chase Pay In 4]] (debit-card BNPL for smaller purchases).
