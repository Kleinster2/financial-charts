# Seascan ROV

Retrievable high-performance remotely operated vehicle made by [[Exail Technologies]] for mine identification. Deployed from [[Inspector USV]] platforms. Unlike the expendable [[K-STER C]], Seascan is designed for retrieval and reuse.
