#product #ai #llm

GPT-5.5 is an [[OpenAI]] frontier model in the [[GPT]] family — the system developed under the codename [[OpenAI Spud|Spud]] and released April 23, 2026 as OpenAI's most capable model and the direct counter to [[Anthropic]]'s [[Claude Mythos|Mythos]] / [[Claude Fable 5|Fable 5]]. [[Greg Brockman]] described it as the result of nearly two years of research and the new base system for [[ChatGPT]]. It is referenced by [[OpenAI]] CFO [[Sarah Friar]] (June 2026) as having launched at roughly 2x the prior model's price while still netting customers ~20-30% lower cost per token on efficiency gains — a worked example of price decoupling from cost (see [[Inference economics]]).
