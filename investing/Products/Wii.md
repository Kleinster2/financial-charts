[[Nintendo]]'s motion-controlled game console, launched 2006. Expanded gaming's addressable market to non-gamers through intuitive motion controls rather than hardware power — the template for [[Nintendo]]'s subsequent accessibility-over-specs strategy that continued with the [[Nintendo Switch]].
