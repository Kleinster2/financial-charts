# PTFE

Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), best known by the [[Chemours]] trade name Teflon. The dominant fluoropolymer by volume (~65% of the ~$8-10B global fluoropolymer market). Produced from [[Hydrofluoric Acid]] via the chain: HF → HCFC-22 → TFE monomer → PTFE. Applications span non-stick coatings, chemical processing seals and gaskets, electrical insulation, aerospace, and semiconductor equipment linings. Approximately 1.8 tonnes of HF consumed per tonne of PTFE produced.
