# UMIS

Unmanned Mine Countermeasure Integrated System made by [[Exail Technologies]]. Full MCM toolbox combining aerial, surface ([[Inspector USV]]), and underwater ([[A18-M]], [[K-STER C]]) unmanned vehicles with towed sonars and sweeps, unified through UmiSoft C2 software. Selected by [[Belgium]]/[[Netherlands]] (rMCM program) and [[Indonesia]]. Won ~€400M contract from undisclosed navy (Q1 2025) — largest in company history.
