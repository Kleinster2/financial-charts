#product #consumer #cosmetics #fragrance #brazil #direct-sales

[[Eudora]] — cosmetics, makeup and fragrance brand launched by [[Grupo Boticário]] in February 2011, built for the direct-sales (relationship-selling) channel and e-commerce, with a portfolio of 600+ products. It is the group's entry into the direct-sales model that [[Natura]] and Avon historically dominated in Brazil, and has been cited as the fastest-growing brand in the Boticário portfolio.
