# Steam platform

#product #gaming #platform #marketplace

Steam is [[Valve]]'s PC game storefront, library, distribution, community, and payments platform. It is the economic base that makes [[SteamOS]] hardware strategically valuable.

---

## Why it matters

Steam is the distribution layer Valve is defending with hardware. [[Steam Deck]] made the Steam library portable; [[Steam Machine]] is the attempt to make it native to the living room; [[Steam Frame]] extends the same library into VR and non-VR headset use. The hardware does not need to become a console monopoly to matter. It only needs to keep the Steam account, library, and store at the center of PC gaming as users move across form factors.
