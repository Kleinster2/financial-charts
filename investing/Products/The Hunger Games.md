**The Hunger Games** is [[Lionsgate]]'s tentpole young-adult franchise, adapted from Suzanne Collins's novels, with five films grossing $3.35 billion worldwide and a sixth — *Sunrise on the Reaping* — due November 20, 2026.

| The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes | 2023 | $361M |

The original quadrilogy (2012–2015) was the engine — $2.99B across four films and the asset that, more than any other, built [[Lionsgate]] into a major studio. The 2023 prequel *Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes* opened to $98M but finished at $361M, the franchise's lowest, showing the IP can still open but at a reduced ceiling once detached from the original cast.

---

## Sunrise on the Reaping

The sixth film, *Sunrise on the Reaping* (dir. Francis Lawrence, who directed all the sequels), is a prequel centered on a young Haymitch Abernathy, slated for November 20, 2026. It is one of [[Lionsgate]]'s biggest theatrical swings and a key FY2027 Motion Picture catalyst — the franchise's ability to re-scale toward original-quadrilogy levels is a direct input to the studio's profitability and re-rating case.
