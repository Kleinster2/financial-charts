#product #hardware #wearables

Pixel Watch — [[Google]]'s smartwatch line, launched 2022. The premium screen-on tier of the Google wearable lineup; sits above [[Fitbit Air]] (screenless, $99) and [[Fitbit Charge 6]] (screen, $159) at $349+. Pairs with the [[Google Health]] app and supports [[Google Health Premium]].

The Pixel Watch 4 (current generation) carries the highest-spec biometric sensors in the Google wearable stack. Algorithms developed for Pixel Watch 4 cascade down to the cheaper Fitbit hardware lines — [[Fitbit Air]] runs Pixel Watch 4-derived algorithms on Charge 6-class sensors.
