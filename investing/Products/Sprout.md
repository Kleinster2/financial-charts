**Sprout** — A 3'6", 59-pound bipedal humanoid robot built by Fauna Robotics for social spaces. Acquired by [[Amazon]] on March 24, 2026. Developer platform priced at $50,000, designed for homes, schools, and R&D partners. Safety-first design for environments with children and pets.

---

## Strategic context

The contrast with [[Astro]] is the story:

| | [[Astro]] | Sprout |
|---|---|---|
| Form | Wheeled | Bipedal humanoid |
| Approach | Built in-house (800+ people) | Acqui-hire (startup) |
| Design philosophy | Alexa-first, surveillance | Social-first, safety |
| Price | $1,000-$1,450 | $50,000 (dev platform) |
| Status | Invite-only 4+ years, business version killed | Acquired, developer ecosystem |

Amazon is pivoting from trying to build a home robot from scratch to acquiring a humanoid developer platform and its founding team.
