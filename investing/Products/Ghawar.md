**Ghawar** — World's largest conventional oil field. Located in eastern [[Saudi Arabia]], operated by [[Saudi Aramco]]. Discovered 1948. ~280 km long. Produces ~3.8 million bpd (~30% of Saudi output). Source rock is the Jurassic Tuwaiq Mountain formation — the same formation targeted by the adjacent [[Jafurah]] unconventional gas play.
