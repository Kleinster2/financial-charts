# A18-M

Mid-size autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV) made by [[Exail Technologies]] for mine countermeasure operations. Up to 24-hour endurance, covers 20-40 km2 per mission. Extended variant being developed with [[Thales]] SAMDIS 600 sonar for France's SLAMF program (8 + 8 option units, deliveries from H1 2028).
