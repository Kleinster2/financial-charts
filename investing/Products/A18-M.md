# A18-M

Mid-size autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV) manufactured by [[Exail Technologies]] for mine countermeasure (MCM) operations. The A18-M is designed for autonomous seabed survey and mine detection in littoral and deep-water environments, with up to 24-hour endurance and coverage of 20-40 km2 per mission. The vehicle integrates [[Exail Technologies|Exail]]'s own fiber-optic gyroscope inertial navigation (inherited from the [[iXblue]] acquisition) with sonar payloads for high-resolution seabed imaging. An extended variant is being developed with [[Thales]]'s SAMDIS 600 synthetic aperture sonar for France's SLAMF (Système de Lutte Anti-Mines Futur) program — an 8-unit order with 8 options, deliveries from H1 2028. The SLAMF contract is significant because it validates the A18-M as the French Navy's primary mine-hunting platform, with export potential to NATO allies facing similar mine threats in the Baltic, North Sea, and Persian Gulf.
