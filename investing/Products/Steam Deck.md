# Steam Deck

#product #gaming #hardware #handheld #steamos

Steam Deck is [[Valve]]'s handheld [[SteamOS]] gaming PC and the proof point that made the 2026 [[Steam Machine]] plausible.

---

## Why it matters

Steam Deck turned Valve's old living-room theory into a working product. The same stack now carries into [[Steam Machine]]: verified games, Steam Input, suspend/resume, Proton compatibility, and a Steam-first UI. Valve's GDC 2026 guidance that all Deck Verified games are Machine Verified makes Steam Deck the compatibility base for the living-room hardware push.
