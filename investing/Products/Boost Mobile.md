**Boost Mobile** — Prepaid wireless brand owned by [[EchoStar]]. 7.51M subscribers. Originally a Sprint brand, acquired by Dish Network in 2020 as a condition of the [[T-Mobile]]/Sprint merger (FCC-mandated divestiture). Now operates as a hybrid MNO on [[AT&T]] network infrastructure after EchoStar shut down its standalone [[5G]] Open RAN network in Nov 2025. Also integrating [[Starlink]] Direct-to-Cell for satellite connectivity.
