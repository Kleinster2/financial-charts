# YU7

[[Xiaomi]]'s second EV, an SUV launched in 2025. Priced at around $35,000, positioned as a direct rival to the Tesla Model Y, with design cues that the FT compared to the [[Ferrari]] Purosangue. Received 200,000 pre-orders in three minutes at launch — extending the order velocity precedent set by the [[SU7]].
