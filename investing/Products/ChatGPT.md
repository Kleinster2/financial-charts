# ChatGPT

[[OpenAI]]'s consumer AI interface. 700M weekly active users. Powered by [[GPT]] and [[o]] model families.

---

## Scale

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Weekly active users | **700M** (early 2025) |
| Paying subscribers | ~20M |
| Downloads H1 2025 | 350M |
| 2024 revenue | $2.7B (75% of OpenAI) |
| Launch | November 2022 |

Fastest product to 100M users (2 months). On track to be most downloaded app of 2025.

---

## [[Consumer]] pricing

| Plan | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Free | $0 | GPT-4o access, limited |
| **Go** | $8/mo | GPT-5.2 "Instant" (speed-optimized) |
| **Plus** | $20/mo | GPT-5, higher limits |
| **Pro** | $200/mo | Unlimited GPT-5, o1 reasoning, priority |

Go tier ($8/mo) launched 2026 — cheaper entry point with "Instant" model optimized for speed.

---

## Features

- **Voice mode** — Natural conversation (GPT-4o onward)
- **Vision** — Image understanding
- **DALL-E** — Image generation built-in
- **Code interpreter** — Runs Python, analyzes data
- **Browsing** — Real-time web access
- **Custom GPTs** — User-created specialized bots
- **Memory** — Remembers preferences across sessions

## Business model

ChatGPT is 75% of OpenAI's revenue. [[Consumer]]-heavy (unlike [[Anthropic]]'s 80% enterprise).

**[[Consumer]] device (announced Jan 2026):** OpenAI planning first hardware product for H2 2026. Confirmed at Davos by Chris Lehane. Form factor unknown.
