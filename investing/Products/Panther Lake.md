# Panther Lake

[[Intel]]'s [[Intel 18A|18A]]-based client processor — branded Core Ultra Series 3 — and the first consumer product to ship on the [[Intel 18A]] node. Unveiled at CES in January 2026 and shipping from January 27 2026, built at Fab 52 in Chandler, Arizona. Panther Lake is Intel's first AI PC platform on 18A, pairing the RibbonFET + PowerVia node with on-package GPU and NPU blocks for on-device AI.

Its strategic weight is as a yield proof point: high-volume Panther Lake shipments are the first real-world stress test of whether [[Intel 18A]] can deliver competitive yields at scale (running ~60-75% in 2026, with 200+ OEM system designs signalling adoption). For the [[Intel]] turnaround thesis, Panther Lake ramping cleanly is the evidence that 18A is production-ready — the precondition for winning external foundry customers. See [[Intel 18A]] for the node and [[Apple-Intel chip-making agreement May 2026]] for the external-customer angle.
