**Orion** — [[Lockheed Martin]] crew capsule for NASA's [[Artemis]] lunar program. Designed for deep-space crew transport — beyond Earth orbit, including lunar transit. Q1 2026 architecture restructure under [[Jared Isaacman]]: Orion now docks with [[SpaceX]] [[Starship]] in Earth orbit and transfers to Starship for lunar surface transit, replacing the previous SLS-Orion-Gateway architecture.

## Why Orion matters

Orion was the surviving piece of [[Boeing]] [[SLS]] / Orion / [[Lunar Gateway]] architecture after the Q1 2026 restructuring. SLS lost its core role and Gateway was paused, but Orion remained — repurposed to dock with Starship in Earth orbit rather than as the lunar transit vehicle. The reuse decision validates Lockheed's >15-year Orion development investment and sustains Orion as a NASA-funded program through Artemis V+.
