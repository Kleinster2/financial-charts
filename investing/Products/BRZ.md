# BRZ

[[Transfero]]'s stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the [[Brazilian real]]. Oldest real-pegged stablecoin, with the most integrated liquidity ecosystem — trading pairs include BRZ-USDC ($15.1M), BRZ-USDT ($14.7M), BRZ-BUSD (~$9.1M). ~$13.6M circulation. Historically dominant on Polygon. No yield pass-through.
