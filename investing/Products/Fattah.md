**Fattah** -- [[Iran]]'s claimed hypersonic missile series, managed by the [[IRGC]] Aerospace Force. The Fattah-1 (2023, two-stage solid-fuel, ~1,400 km range, claimed Mach 13-15 terminal speed) was first used operationally in June 2025 during Operation True Promise III. The Fattah-2 variant adds a hypersonic glide vehicle for maneuverable reentry, with first operational launch on March 1, 2026. Designed to defeat missile defense systems through speed and maneuverability rather than extended range.
