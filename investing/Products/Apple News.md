# Apple News

[[Apple]]'s news-aggregation app, launched in 2015 with iOS 9, bundling articles from participating publishers into a single feed. In March 2019 Apple added News+, a ~$13/month subscription folding in magazines and newspapers, with Apple taking a revenue share from the publishers it carries.

Like [[Apple Maps]], News functions as an advertising surface within Apple's expanding Services business — one of the channels (App Store search ads, News, the Stocks app) behind an estimated ~$8bn of iOS ad revenue in 2026 per the [[Financial Times]]. The Services ad build-out is central to the reinvestment debate in [[Apple#Innovation gap and the reinvestment question (FT, May 2026)]].
