# Noor-3

Iranian military imaging satellite operated by the [[IRGC]] Aerospace Force. [[Iran]] claims ~5-metre resolution, a significant improvement on the earlier [[Noor-2]] (12-15 m) but still roughly an order of magnitude less precise than the Chinese-built [[TEE-01B]] the IRGC subsequently acquired from [[Earth Eye Co]]. Neither Noor-2 nor Noor-3 produce imagery sharp enough to identify individual aircraft or track vehicle-level activity at US military bases, which is why the TEE-01B acquisition was a material upgrade (see [[IRGC Chinese satellite targeting March 2026]]).
