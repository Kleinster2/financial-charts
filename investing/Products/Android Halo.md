# Android Halo

Android Halo — [[Google]]'s [[Android]] status layer for live AI agents. Announced at Google I/O 2026 alongside Gemini-powered eyewear, Halo is meant to show when an agent is active, what it is doing, and where user attention or approval is needed.

## Why it matters

Agentic AI creates a visibility problem: if software can keep working after the user stops looking at a screen, the user needs a persistent way to see what is active and interrupt it. Android Halo is Google's early answer on [[Android]]. It is less a standalone app than a trust surface for background agents.
