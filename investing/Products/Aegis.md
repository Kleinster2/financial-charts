**Aegis** -- the [[United States]] Navy's integrated naval weapons system, developed by [[Lockheed Martin]] (originally RCA/GE). The AN/SPY-1 phased array radar and Aegis combat system form the backbone of US naval air and missile defense. Deployed on 100+ [[Arleigh Burke]]-class destroyers and [[Ticonderoga]]-class cruisers. The Aegis Ballistic Missile Defense (BMD) variant integrates the [[SM-3]] interceptor for exo-atmospheric ballistic missile defense. Land-based Aegis Ashore installations operate in [[Romania]] (Deveselu, operational 2016) and [[Poland]] (Redzikowo, operational 2024). An Aegis-equipped destroyer successfully directed the [[SM-3]] intercept of an Iranian IRBM targeting [[Diego Garcia]] on March 21, 2026.
