# BRLA

Real-pegged stablecoin issued by BRLA Digital and Avenia. Deployed on Polygon. Ranks #1 among real-pegged stablecoins in unique remittances (widest retail coverage). Most-traded USD-denominated DEX pair since March 2025.

Positioned as a compliant fiat bridge to crypto — remittances and cross-border payments focus.
