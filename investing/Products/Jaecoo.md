# Jaecoo

Off-road / SUV sub-brand of [[Chery]], paired with sibling brand [[Omoda]] in Chery's European retail rollout. Targets a more outdoor / overlanding consumer position than Omoda's mainstream-crossover positioning, but typically retailed alongside Omoda in shared dealerships.

Per the FT (May 2026, Gideon Rachman), West London Tesla dealerships have been turning over to Omoda + Jaecoo retail formats — a consumer-level signal of the [[China soft power|Chinese-brand displacement]] of [[Tesla]] in EU markets where [[Elon Musk]]'s political alignment now reduces brand appeal.
