# Omoda

Crossover-SUV / EV sub-brand of [[Chery]]. Together with sibling brand [[Jaecoo]], the spearhead of Chery's European retail rollout. Per the FT (May 2026, Gideon Rachman), the West London Tesla dealership the columnist had used previously closed and reopened as an Omoda + Jaecoo showroom — a small but vivid example of [[China soft power|Chinese-brand displacement]] of [[Tesla]] in EU consumer markets where [[Elon Musk]]'s political alignment with [[Trump II]] now functions as a brand discount.
