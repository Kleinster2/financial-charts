**Sling TV** — Live streaming TV service owned by [[EchoStar]] (formerly [[Dish Network]]). Launched in 2015 as the first major skinny-bundle alternative to traditional cable. ~2M subscribers. Competes with YouTube TV, Hulu + Live TV, and [[DirecTV]] Stream. Was part of the failed 2024 [[DirecTV]] acquisition attempt.
