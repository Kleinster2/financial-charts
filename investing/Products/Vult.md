[[Vult]] — Brazilian color-cosmetics (makeup) brand, in the market since 2004 and acquired by [[Grupo Boticário]] in March 2018. Vult is the group's mass-market makeup play, distributed largely through multi-brand retail (pharmacies, perfumarias and other third-party points of sale) rather than Boticário's own franchise network — broadening the group's reach into affordable color cosmetics.
