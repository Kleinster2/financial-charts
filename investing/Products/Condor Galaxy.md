#product #ai-infrastructure

Condor Galaxy — strategic AI supercomputer network built by [[Cerebras]] and operated by [[UAE G42]]. Multi-site, multi-system cluster of Cerebras CS-3 systems, deployed across the US and the UAE as part of the long-running G42 sovereign-AI build-out. Largest single customer relationship in the [[Cerebras]] revenue base prior to the May 2026 [[OpenAI]] expansion — and one of the structural anchors of the 86% 2025 revenue concentration the [[Cerebras IPO revival April 2026]] S-1 disclosed.

## Significance

Condor Galaxy is the canonical reference deployment for [[Cerebras]]'s wafer-scale architecture — proof that the CS-3 / WSE-3 product can run at hyperscale-cluster sizes under a sovereign-AI buyer. The relationship predates the [[OpenAI]] commitment and was the original validation customer that made the wafer-scale story underwritable.

Per the [[Cerebras IPO revival April 2026]] S-1, [[UAE G42]] was 24% of 2025 revenue and [[MBZUAI]] was 62%. Condor Galaxy is the named program through which both flow. The May 14 Day-1 trading print on CBRS — opening +89%, closing +68% — was effectively the public market pricing in the forward [[OpenAI]] expansion, not the realized Condor Galaxy economics that delivered 2025 revenue.
