**PRSCX** — [[T. Rowe Price]]'s flagship science & technology mutual fund. $12.5B AUM. Heavily concentrated in [[NVIDIA]], [[Broadcom]], [[Apple]], [[Microsoft]]. Has crushed [[QQQ]] since inception.

## Why PRSCX matters

Actively managed tech fund that has dramatically outperformed its passive benchmark. ~+4,900% since 1999 vs ~+500% for QQQ — roughly 10x the return. The fund's edge widened sharply from 2020 onward, driven by early and heavy positioning in AI infrastructure names.
