# PVDF

Polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF) is a high-performance fluoropolymer produced from [[Hydrofluoric Acid]] via the chain: HF → VDF monomer → PVDF. The dominant binder for lithium-ion battery cathodes and anodes (~2-5% of electrode composition), making it a critical material for the energy transition. Also used as separator coating in batteries, chemical piping, and architectural coatings. Key trade names: Kynar ([[Arkema]]) and Solef ([[Syensqo]]).
