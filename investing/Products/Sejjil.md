**Sejjil** -- [[Iran]]'s first two-stage solid-fuel medium-range ballistic missile. Range of approximately 2,000 km with a 700 kg payload. Represented a generational leap when first tested in 2008 -- the first Iranian missile that could strike [[Israel]] on short notice without hours of liquid-fuel preparation. Managed by the [[IRGC]] Aerospace Force. Not tested since 2012; deployment status uncertain per [[CSIS]].
