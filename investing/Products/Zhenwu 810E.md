The Zhenwu 810E is [[Alibaba]]'s self-developed AI accelerator chip, unveiled January 30, 2026 by T-Head ([[DAMO Academy]]). GPU-class performance for AI training and inference — 96GB HBM2e memory, 700 GB/s inter-chip bandwidth via proprietary ICN interconnect. Comparable to [[NVIDIA]] H20, in some respects superior to A800. Deployed in 10,000-card clusters on [[Alibaba]] Cloud serving 400+ customers including State Grid, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and [[XPeng]]. Fully in-house architecture and software stack — no CUDA dependency. Optimized for [[Qwen]] training and inference.
