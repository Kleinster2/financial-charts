# SU7

[[Xiaomi]]'s first electric vehicle, launched in 2024. A sports sedan branded "Speed Ultra 7." Generated 50,000 orders in the first 30 minutes after orders opened, became one of [[China]]'s best-selling cars, and reached profitability in roughly 18 months — faster than [[Tesla]]. The platform on which Xiaomi's EV business was scaled before the second model ([[YU7]], 2025) and the European-engineered [[YU7 GT]] (2026).

## Notable moments

- 2025 fatal crash with semi-autonomous driving engaged killed three people; prompted Chinese regulators to tighten oversight of unproven self-driving deployments.
- [[Ford]] CEO [[Jim Farley]] reportedly bought an SU7 personally and called it an "existential threat."
- Karl-Thomas Neumann (ex-[[VW]] [[China]] CEO): SU7 Ultra is a "crying loud warning sign" for Western carmakers.
