#product #semiconductors #space

TERAFAB — $20-25B joint chip fabrication facility announced by [[Elon Musk]] on March 21, 2026, at Giga Texas in Austin. Joint venture between [[Tesla]], [[SpaceX]], and [[xAI]]. Targets 1 terawatt of AI compute annually (~50x current global AI output of ~20 GW). 100 million sq ft planned. Two chip families: D3 space-hardened processors for orbital AI satellites and AI5/AI6 edge-inference processors for [[Tesla]] vehicles. 80% of output earmarked for space-based systems. Represents ~70% of [[TSMC]]'s entire global production at full capacity. Chips destined for lunar satellite manufacturing feed the [[Lunar Mass Driver]] pipeline.
