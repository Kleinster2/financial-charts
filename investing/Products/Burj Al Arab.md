**Burj Al Arab** — Dubai's iconic sail-shaped luxury hotel, opened 1999. Managed by [[Jumeirah]] Group (Dubai Holding). 321m tall, 202 duplex suites. Built by [[Al Habtoor Group]] Engineering. Widely called the world's only "7-star" hotel. Symbol of Dubai's luxury positioning.
