# BRLV

Real-pegged stablecoin issued by [[Crown]]. ~$19M on-chain circulation — largest among real-pegged stablecoins by outstanding tokens. Launched ~mid-2024.

Crown raised $13.5M Series A led by [[Paradigm]] (Dec 2025) at $90M valuation, with $67M in total commitments — the most VC-backed real-pegged stablecoin project.
