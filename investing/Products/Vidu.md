# Vidu

AI video generation model from ShengShu Technology. Launched before [[OpenAI]]'s [[Sora]] became widely available, making it one of the earliest production-grade Chinese video generation systems. Founded March 2023 by Zhu Jun (Tsinghua University professor, chief scientist).
