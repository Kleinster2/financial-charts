# Google Flow

Google Flow — [[Google]]'s creative AI workflow surface for building and editing media with [[Gemini Omni]]. It is distinct from [[Flow]], the real-estate startup; in the I/O 2026 stack, Flow is the higher-control creator environment connecting Gemini video generation to publishing workflows.

---

## The story

Google Flow is the product wrapper around the creative side of Gemini. Where the Gemini app exposes Omni as a consumer feature, Flow is positioned as a workflow surface: prompt, edit, iterate, assemble, and prepare media for downstream use.

The strategic role is to give Google a creator tool that can feed [[YouTube]] and justify paid AI tiers. Generative video has to become repeatable before it becomes economically important. Flow is the attempt to make that repeatability happen inside Google's own toolchain instead of leaving the workflow to third-party editors.
