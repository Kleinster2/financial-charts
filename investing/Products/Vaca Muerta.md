**Vaca Muerta** — Argentina's giant unconventional shale formation in Neuquen Basin. ~308 Tcf recoverable gas resources. Producing and setting records as of 2025-2026. Compared to [[Permian Basin]] geology. Key operators include [[YPF]], [[Chevron]], and [[Shell]]. Alongside [[Jafurah]], one of the two major non-US unconventional plays reaching commercial scale.
