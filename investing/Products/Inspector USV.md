# Inspector USV

Autonomous unmanned surface vehicle manufactured by [[Exail Technologies]] for mine countermeasure (MCM) operations. The Inspector serves as the mothership platform within the [[UMIS]] integrated system, deploying and recovering underwater vehicles including the [[A18-M]] AUV and [[K-STER C]] expendable mine neutralizer, along with towed sonars for seabed mapping.

The vessel operates autonomously or via remote control, navigating minefields without putting crew at risk. Its design reflects the broader naval shift from manned minesweepers to unmanned MCM systems -- a transition driven by both cost (a single minesweeper costs far more than a fleet of USVs) and survivability (mines are designed to kill ships). Selected by [[Belgium]], the [[Netherlands]] (rMCM program), and [[Indonesia]] as part of [[UMIS]] contracts.
