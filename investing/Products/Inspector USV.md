# Inspector USV

Autonomous unmanned surface vehicle made by [[Exail Technologies]] for mine countermeasure operations. Carries AUVs, ROVs ([[K-STER C]], [[Seascan ROV]]), and towed sonars. Part of the [[UMIS]] integrated MCM system.
