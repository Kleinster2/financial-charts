#product #hardware #wearables

Fitbit Charge 6 — [[Fitbit]]'s mid-tier fitness tracker, launched September 2023. Sits between [[Fitbit Air]] (screenless, $99) and [[Pixel Watch]] ($349+) at $159. Carries a screen plus an on-demand ECG app — the latter is the substantive feature missing from [[Fitbit Air]], which uses passive PPG-only background AFib detection instead.

The Charge 6 sensor stack is the basis for the [[Fitbit Air]] sensor pipeline; Air subtracts the screen and the ECG app while inheriting the optical heart rate, SpO2, skin temperature, and accelerometer/gyro hardware (with [[Pixel Watch]] 4-derived algorithms on top).
