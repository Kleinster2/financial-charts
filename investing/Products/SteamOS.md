# SteamOS

#product #gaming #operating-system

SteamOS is [[Valve]]'s gaming-first, [[Linux]]-based operating system for Steam hardware. It powers [[Steam Deck]], [[Steam Machine]], and [[Steam Frame]].

## Why it matters

SteamOS is Valve's hedge against PC gaming being controlled entirely by [[Windows]] and console storefronts. The operating system turns Steam from a storefront into an environment: suspend/resume, Steam Input, verified compatibility, cloud saves, desktop access, and a console-like UI. [[Steam Machine]] is the living-room test of whether that environment can work without a handheld screen.
