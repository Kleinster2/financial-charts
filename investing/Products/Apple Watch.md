**Apple Watch** — [[Apple]]'s smartwatch, launched 2015. Dominates global smartwatch market with ~50% share. Key growth driver for Apple's Services and Wearables segment.

---

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Launch | April 2015 |
| Latest | Series 10, Ultra 2 (2024) |
| Market share | ~50% of smartwatches |
| Installed base | 100M+ |

---

## Why it matters

- Health monitoring (ECG, blood oxygen, fall detection)
- Apple Services attach rate (Fitness+, Apple Care)
- Wearables segment now ~$40B/year revenue
