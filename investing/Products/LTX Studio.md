**LTX Studio** is a browser-based end-to-end AI filmmaking platform built by [[Lightricks]], the Israeli creative-tools company. It converts text prompts and scripts into storyboards, animated sequences, and pitch decks, with persistent character profiles to maintain visual consistency across scenes. The platform represents Lightricks' pivot from mobile photo/video editing (Facetune, Videoleap) into generative AI content creation. LTX Studio leverages both proprietary and open-source models — Lightricks released LTXV-13B (May 2025) and LTX-2 (Oct 2025) as open-source video generation models, positioning itself in both the application layer and the model layer of the AI video stack. Integration with [[Google]]'s Veo 3 for audio-visual generation further extends capabilities.
