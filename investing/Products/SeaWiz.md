**SeaWiz** — Close-In Weapon System (CIWS) deployed on naval vessels. Fully autonomous anti-air defense — shoots down incoming missiles and aerial threats without human intervention. Manufactured by [[RTX]] (Raytheon division).

Cited by [[Trae Stephens]] ([[Anduril]], Apr 6, 2026) as precedent for autonomous weapons: "Fully autonomous weapons are not new. SeaWiz has been deployed on naval vessels that shoots down aerial threats fully autonomously. You don't have time to make decisions about incoming missiles." Accountability is baked in — the captain of the vessel is responsible for whatever actions the weapon system takes.
