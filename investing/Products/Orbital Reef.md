**Orbital Reef** — [[Blue Origin]] + [[Sierra Space]] commercial space station. [[Commercial LEO Destinations]] Phase 1 awardee (~$130M, Dec 2021). Target architecture is a modular, multi-tenant orbital business park with manufacturing, research, and crewed mission capabilities. Status as of Q1 2026: Phase 2 procurement strategy under review per [[Jared Isaacman]]'s alternative framework — Orbital Reef is the Blue/Sierra competitive entrant against [[Vast Space]] [[Haven-2]], [[Voyager]] [[Star Lab]], and [[Axiom Space]].

## Why Orbital Reef matters

For [[Blue Origin]]'s strategic positioning, Orbital Reef is the LEO complement to [[Project Sunrise]] (orbital DC) and Blue Moon ([[Artemis]] V lunar lander). The integrated Blue stack — New Glenn launch, Orbital Reef destination, Project Sunrise compute, Blue Moon lunar — is Bezos's answer to the SpaceX vertical-integration playbook.
