[[Beleza na Web]] — multi-brand online beauty retailer, a pioneer of beauty e-commerce in Brazil, acquired by [[Grupo Boticário]] in August 2019. It is the group's standalone digital beauty platform — described as the largest of its kind in Latin America — selling Boticário-owned and third-party brands and giving the group a direct multi-brand e-commerce channel alongside its franchise and direct-sales models.
