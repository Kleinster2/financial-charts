# Daily Brief

Daily Brief — [[Google]]'s proactive morning summary surface inside the [[Gemini]] app. Announced at Google I/O 2026, it synthesizes personal context such as inbox, calendar, and Gemini history into priorities and suggested next steps.

---

## The story

Daily Brief is the low-friction side of Google's personal-agent strategy. [[Gemini Spark]] asks the user to trust an agent to keep working. Daily Brief asks the easier first question: can Gemini summarize what matters today, surface next actions, and become part of the morning routine?

That distinction matters because habit formation usually precedes delegation. A user who accepts Daily Brief as a reliable organizer is more likely to trust Spark with execution later. Google is using the Gemini app as the front door for both: a daily synthesis surface and a persistent action agent.

The product also shows why Google's default data surfaces matter. Calendar, Gmail, Docs, Drive, Search, Maps, and Android context are exactly the materials a daily assistant needs. Standalone chat apps can ask the user to upload context; Google can try to make the context ambient.

## Product boundary

Daily Brief summarizes and prioritizes. [[Gemini Spark]] acts. Search agents monitor external information. [[Google Workspace]] voice and AI Inbox capture and triage work context. Daily Brief is the synthesis layer that can point the user toward those next actions without yet becoming the executor.
