# SleepIQ

Biometric sleep-tracking platform embedded in [[Sleep Number]] 360 Smart Beds. Sensors track heart rate, breathing rate, movement, and bed pressure to generate a personalized SleepIQ score. Over 32 billion hours of biometric sleep data collected. Generates recurring revenue through digital subscriptions and data-driven personalization features.
