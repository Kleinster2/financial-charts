# SleepIQ

Biometric sleep-tracking platform embedded in [[Sleep Number]] 360 Smart Beds. Sensors integrated into the mattress track heart rate, breathing rate, movement, and bed pressure to generate a personalized SleepIQ score (0-100 scale). The platform has collected over 32 billion hours of biometric sleep data, making it one of the largest longitudinal sleep datasets outside of clinical research. SleepIQ generates recurring revenue for Sleep Number through digital subscriptions and data-driven personalization features — the bed adjusts firmness and position automatically based on the sleeper's biometric patterns. This data flywheel is core to Sleep Number's differentiation against commodity mattress retailers like [[Mattress Firm]] and DTC competitors like Casper: the smart bed creates switching costs and a subscription revenue stream that a traditional mattress cannot replicate.
