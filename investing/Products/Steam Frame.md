# Steam Frame

#product #gaming #vr #hardware #steamos

Steam Frame is [[Valve]]'s wireless VR and non-VR headset in the 2026 Steam hardware family, built to stream from PCs including [[Steam Machine]] while also supporting standalone [[SteamOS]] play.

---

## Why it matters

Steam Frame broadens Valve's hardware thesis beyond "console for Steam." It makes [[Steam platform|Steam]] a headset library, not just a PC library, and it makes [[Steam Machine]] a potential streaming host. The device also extends [[Proton (Valve)|Proton]] and [[SteamOS]] into ARM/FEX compatibility work, which could matter for future non-x86 Steam devices.
