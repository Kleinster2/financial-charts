## EU DMA gatekeeper risk (Jun 2026)

On Jun 25 2026, the [[European Commission]] reached a preliminary position that [[Microsoft]] should be designated as a [[Digital Markets Act]] gatekeeper for Azure. The Commission described Azure as the [[EU]]'s second-largest cloud computing service and emphasized lock-in, switching costs, ecosystem breadth, and AI procurement as reasons the service may be an important gateway for business users.

The designation is not final. If confirmed, Microsoft would have six months to bring Azure into DMA compliance. See [[EU cloud DMA preliminary findings 2026]].
