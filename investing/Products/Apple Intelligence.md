# Apple Intelligence

Apple's on-device and [[Private Cloud Compute]]-backed AI feature set, announced at WWDC in June 2024 as Apple's answer to the generative-AI wave. Spans writing tools, image features, notification summaries, and the agentic [[Siri]] overhaul. Made by [[Apple]].

The personalized, agentic [[Siri]] features advertised at launch slipped badly — delayed from 2024 into 2025–2026 (the internal "Endgame" project missed its spring 2025 deadline), triggering a $250mn false-advertising settlement and, ultimately, the decision to license [[Google]]'s [[Gemini]] as Siri's backbone (see [[Apple-Google AI deal]] and [[Apple#Innovation gap and the reinvestment question (FT, May 2026)]]).
