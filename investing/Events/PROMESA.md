**PROMESA** (Puerto Rico Oversight, Management, and Economic Stability Act) — Federal law enacted June 2016 enabling [[Puerto Rico]] to restructure its public debts through Title III bankruptcy proceedings and creating the [[FOMB]] to oversee fiscal recovery.
