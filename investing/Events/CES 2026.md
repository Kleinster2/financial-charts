# CES 2026

**Consumer Electronics Show 2026** — Las Vegas, January 6-9, 2026. Major tech announcements across AI, chips, robotics, and consumer electronics.

---

## Key announcements

### NVIDIA

**Jensen Huang keynote highlights:**

| Announcement | Details |
|--------------|---------|
| **Vera Rubin NVL72** | 5x inference vs Blackwell, 10x lower cost/token, H2 2026 |
| **Alpamayo** | Open reasoning models for autonomous vehicles, 10B parameter |
| **Cosmos AI** | Physics simulation foundation model |
| **Robotics platform** | Full-stack ecosystem for physical AI |
| **Siemens partnership** | Industrial AI integration |
| **DLSS 4.5** | Gaming update (separate briefing) |

**Key quote:** "Computing has been fundamentally reshaped as a result of accelerated computing, as a result of artificial intelligence."

**Mercedes partnership:** 2025 Mercedes CLA first vehicle with full NVIDIA AV stack including Alpamayo.

See [[NVIDIA]] for company context.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|

### Robotics

**CES 2026 = "year of the humanoid"**

| Company | Robot | Announcement |
|---------|-------|--------------|
| [[Boston Dynamics]] | Atlas | **Production-ready**, first deployments to Hyundai & Google DeepMind |
| [[1X Technologies]] | NEO | **Pre-orders live** ($20K), EQT 10K robot deal |
| [[Unitree]] | G1, H2, R1 | Full lineup, martial arts demos, mass-market pricing |
| [[Hyundai]] | AI Robotics Strategy | Atlas, Spot, MobED integration |
| [[LG Electronics]] | CLOiD | Home robot, "Zero Labor Home" |
| NVIDIA | Platform | Full-stack robotics ecosystem |
| LEM Surgical | Dynamis | FDA-cleared surgical humanoid (NVIDIA booth) |

**Boston Dynamics Atlas specs:**
- 56 degrees of freedom
- 7.5-foot reach
- 110 lb lifting capacity
- 4-hour battery, hot-swappable
- First deployments: Hyundai Metaplant Georgia (2026)

**China dominance:** 50% of humanoid exhibitors were Chinese (Unitree, Agibot, Galbot, EngineAI, Noetix). Morgan Stanley: China filed 7,705 humanoid patents vs 1,561 US in past 5 years.
