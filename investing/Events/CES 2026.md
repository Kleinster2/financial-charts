### Solid-State [[Batteries]]

**Breakthrough year:** Multiple companies announced production-ready solid-state batteries.

| Company | Announcement | Significance |
|---------|--------------|--------------|
| **[[Donut Lab]]** | First production solid-state shipping Q1 2026 | 400 Wh/kg, 5-min charge, Verge Motorcycles |
| **[[ProLogium]]** | Superfluidized all-inorganic ceramic battery | No thermal runaway in ARC testing |
| **ESOX Group** | [[Defense]] drone/UGV integration | Military applications |

**Donut Lab specs:**
- Energy density: **400 Wh/kg** (2x [[Tesla]])
- Charge time: **5 minutes** (full)
- Cycle life: **100,000+** cycles
- First customer: Verge Motorcycles (600km range)

**ProLogium applications:** EVs, e-bikes, humanoid robots, AI datacenters.
