#event #space

**Polaris Dawn** — September 10-15, 2024. First commercial spacewalk. Highest Earth orbit since Apollo (~1,400 km). Privately funded by [[Jared Isaacman]] (commander), launched on [[SpaceX]] [[Crew Dragon]].

## Why Polaris Dawn matters

The first successful commercial spacewalk validated SpaceX's EVA suit development and demonstrated that commercial orbital missions can include capabilities (high-orbit operations, EVAs) that previously required national space agencies. The mission also validated [[Jared Isaacman]]'s expanded commercial astronaut credentials ahead of his [[NASA]] administrator appointment in 2025.

The Polaris Program (planned 3-mission series) was paused after Isaacman's NASA appointment. Mission 2 (Polaris Dawn II) and Mission 3 (Starship-based) are unscheduled.
