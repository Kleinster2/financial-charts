#event #space #disaster

**Apollo 1** — January 27, 1967. Cabin fire during a launch rehearsal at Kennedy Space Center killed all 3 crew (Gus Grissom, Ed White, Roger Chaffee). [[Type A mishap]]. Caused by an electrical short in the pure-oxygen atmosphere. Apollo program reset by ~21 months; major redesign of the command module followed.

## Why Apollo 1 matters

The first [[Type A mishap]] precedent (alongside [[Challenger]] and [[Columbia]]) that frames NASA's most severe accident classification. Cited by [[Jared Isaacman]] in his Feb 2026 reclassification of [[Boeing Starliner]] CFT as a Type A mishap — placing Starliner in the same regulatory tier as Apollo 1.
