#event #space

**Inspiration4** — September 15-18, 2021. First all-civilian orbital spaceflight. Privately funded by [[Jared Isaacman]] (commander), launched on [[SpaceX]] [[Crew Dragon]] from Kennedy Space Center.

---

## Why Inspiration4 matters

First commercial orbital mission with no professional astronauts. Validated the [[SpaceX]] [[Crew Dragon]] platform for non-NASA commercial human spaceflight. Established [[Jared Isaacman]]'s commercial astronaut credentials, the foundation for [[Polaris Dawn]] (Sep 2024) and ultimately his appointment as [[NASA]] administrator.
