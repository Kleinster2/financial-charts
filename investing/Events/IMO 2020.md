# IMO 2020

**International Maritime Organization regulation capping sulfur content in marine fuel at 0.5%** — effective January 1, 2020 (down from 3.5%). Forced the global shipping fleet to switch from high-sulfur fuel oil (HSFO) to very low-sulfur fuel oil (VLSFO), marine gasoil (MGO), or install scrubbers. Initially feared to cause a diesel supply crisis by competing for middle distillate molecules, but the feared spillover largely didn't materialize — VLSFO blends absorbed most of the transition.
