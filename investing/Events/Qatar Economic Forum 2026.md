## Why it matters

The speaker list reveals the forum's real function: connecting [[GCC]] sovereign capital with global alternative asset managers and tech/energy CEOs. [[KKR]], [[Carlyle]], [[General Atlantic]], [[Citadel]], and [[Pictet]] all present — the largest concentration of alt-asset firepower at any single Middle East event. [[Tokamak Energy]] (fusion) and [[M42]] (Abu Dhabi AI/health) signal the region's bet on frontier technology. [[Hillhouse]]'s Zhang Lei and [[WuXi Biologics]]' Chen represent the [[China]]/Asia corridor. [[Steven Mnuchin]]'s [[Liberty Strategic Capital]] bridges US policy and Gulf capital.

The theme itself — "The Global Rebalance" — is the [[GCC]]'s self-conception: no longer just energy suppliers but capital allocators reshaping global markets.
