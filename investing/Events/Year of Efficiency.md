#event #usa

**Year of Efficiency** — [[Mark Zuckerberg]]'s 2023 restructuring of [[Meta]] following the [[Meta 2022 collapse]]. Transformed company from growth-at-all-costs to disciplined operator.

---

## Actions

| Date | Action |
|------|--------|
| Nov 2022 | 11,000 layoffs (13% of workforce) |
| Mar 2023 | 10,000 additional layoffs |
| May 2023 | Flattening management layers |
| 2023 | Reality Labs spending capped |
| 2023 | AI/Reels investment prioritized |

---

## Results

- Total headcount reduction: ~25% (87K → 67K)
- Operating margin: 20% → 40%+
- Stock: $88 (Nov 2022) → $500+ (2024)
- Narrative shift: metaverse skepticism → AI leader
