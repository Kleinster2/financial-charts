The 2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis is an ongoing disruption to global oil shipping through the strait, triggered by the Iran-US/Israel conflict. Iran has effectively controlled or impeded transit through the strait, threatening ~20% of global oil supply. Supertanker rates hit all-time highs as insurers dropped war risk coverage. Described by [[Paul Sankey]] as the worst supply disruption "possibly since 1973."
