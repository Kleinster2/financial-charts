**Winter Storm Elliott** — December 2022 winter storm that triggered gas freeze-offs and emergency load-shedding procedures across [[PJM]] and neighboring grid regions. Demonstrated [[Natural gas]] deliverability vulnerability in the Mid-Atlantic and Midwest, reinforcing concerns raised by [[Winter Storm Uri]] eighteen months earlier.
