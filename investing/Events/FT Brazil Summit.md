# FT Brazil Summit

**FT Brazil Summit** — annual New York conference co-produced by the [[Financial Times]] and [[BrazilCham]], held alongside the World Bank and IMF spring meetings. 25th edition in 2026 (held May 13, Convene One Liberty / One Liberty Plaza), as part of [[Brazil Week NYC 2026]]. Brings together policymakers, executives of major Brazilian and US corporations, financial-market leaders, and global investors for keynote addresses and expert panels on Brazilian fiscal strategy, trade, investment, and macroeconomic positioning.

## Why it matters

The 25-year tenure makes the FT Brazil Summit the longest-running Brazil-focused investor convening in New York. The 2026 edition lands in the same week as the [[BrazilCham]] Person of the Year Gala and the 15th [[LIDE]] Brazil Investment Forum, making the three-event triangle (LIDE / FT / BrazilCham) the highest-density single-week briefing window for Brazil-curious US institutional capital. The summit historically features a keynote by a senior Brazilian official (Finance Minister, BCB Governor, or equivalent) — the absence or thinness of senior-government representation is itself a market signal.
