On Friday May 8, 2026, the publicly traded pure-play space stocks moved as a single basket — seven vault-actor names rallied between +10.8% and +34.2% on the same session, an average move of ~20.4%, with every name closing more than 2.5σ above expectation. The catalyst was [[Rocket Lab]]'s record Q1 2026 earnings print released the prior evening, but the dispersion was sector-wide rather than idiosyncratic — every space-launch + space-services + earth-observation pure-play participated, including names with no commercial overlap with Rocket Lab. This is the cleanest single-day basket move the small-cap space cohort has produced since the post-[[SpaceX]] [[SpaceX IPO 2026|IPO]] announcement runs.

## Idiosyncratic vs basket diagnostic

The structural test for whether this rally is "RKLB Q1 = read-across to peers" (idiosyncratic with positive correlation spillover) versus "sector regime change" (broad re-rating of the small-cap space cohort multiple) is the dispersion *within* the basket. The math:

| Test | Result | Read |
|------|--------|------|
| Average move | +20.4% | Sector-level |
| Range (max - min) | 23.4pp (RKLB +34.2% vs PL +10.8%) | Wide |
| Standard deviation | ~8pp | High |
| RKLB-share of move (move-weighted average) | RKLB carries ~24% of basket gain weight | Concentrated but not dominant |

The wide dispersion (+10.8% to +34.2%) argues *both* effects are operative:

- Sector re-rating component — every name participating with statistically significant moves indicates the market is re-pricing the entire cohort's multiple, not just RKLB. The catalyst chain — RKLB Q1 + Golden Dome + SpaceX IPO + Iran defense premium — applies asymmetrically but to all names.
- Read-across component — the move ordering (RKLB > BKSY > RDW > LUNR > ASTS > SPIR > PL) approximately tracks commercial overlap with Rocket Lab. The names with launch services or constellation hardware exposure (BKSY, RDW, LUNR) moved more than the pure-data names (PL).

Conclusion: this is a *catalyst-led re-rating* of the basket, not a single-stock idiosyncratic ripple. The Friday move is best understood as the market raising the multiple on the cohort because the RKLB print + Golden Dome selection + Motiv acquisition collectively validate that the small-cap space basket is monetizing the [[Satellite primer|space-as-infrastructure]] thesis at scale. Whether the re-rating sticks depends on subsequent prints — [[AST SpaceMobile]] earnings the following Monday is the first downstream test.
