# Operation Earnest Will

US Navy escort operation for Kuwaiti oil tankers through the Persian Gulf (July 1987 – September 1988) during the Iran-Iraq War. Largest US naval convoy operation since WWII. Tankers re-flagged under US flag to justify military protection. Precedent invoked by Trump in March 2026 for proposed Hormuz escort mission during [[Operation Epic Fury|2026 Iran conflict]].
