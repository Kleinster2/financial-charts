HMTX Industries v United States — Federal Circuit ruling (Sep 25, 2025) unanimously upholding [[Section 301]] Lists 3 and 4A tariffs on Chinese imports. Rejected APA challenges, major questions doctrine arguments, and constitutional delegation claims. Found Section 307(a)(1)(C) independently authorizes [[USTR]] to modify and escalate tariffs.

## Significance

Cemented [[Section 301]]'s legal foundation months before the [[SCOTUS IEEPA ruling]] struck down IEEPA tariffs. The Federal Circuit's finding that USTR has independent statutory authority (not just delegated presidential power) made Section 301 the legally durable tariff vehicle.
