**Winter Storm Uri** — February 2021 winter storm that caused catastrophic grid failures across [[Texas]] ([[ERCOT]]). 200+ deaths. [[Samsung Semiconductor]], [[NXP]], and [[Infineon]] semiconductor fabs shut down, scrapping hundreds of millions of dollars in wafers. Demonstrated the fragility of energy-intensive defense-relevant manufacturing in gas-dependent grid regions.
