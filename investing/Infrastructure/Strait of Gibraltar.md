# Strait of Gibraltar

The Strait of Gibraltar connects the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea between [[Spain]] and [[Morocco]]. It is the western gate for Mediterranean shipping and a long-reroute chokepoint in the Verschuur-Lumma-Hall framework.

---

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Connects | Atlantic Ocean and Mediterranean Sea |
| Bordering states | [[Spain]], [[Morocco]] |
| Alternative if blocked | Long rerouting around Africa or through alternate overland/port chains |

---

## May 2026 chokepoint dependency read

The [[Financial Times]] visual story treated Gibraltar as one of the chokepoints where a blockage can require vessels to travel more than 8,000 miles. In the Ukraine example, the FT / Verschuur-Lumma-Hall table attributed about 18% of [[Ukraine]]'s seaborne trade to the strait.
