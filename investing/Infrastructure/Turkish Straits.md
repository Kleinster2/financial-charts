# Turkish Straits

**Turkish Straits** — the Bosporus and Dardanelles — connect the Black Sea to the Mediterranean via the Sea of Marmara. Transit of ~2.5-3M bbl/day of crude, primarily Russian and Kazakh exports. The BTC pipeline (1M bpd) serves as a partial bypass.
