# Korea Strait

The Korea Strait connects the East China Sea and the Sea of Japan between [[South Korea]] and [[Japan]]. In the investing vault it is a Northeast Asia routing chokepoint tied to Korean/Japanese trade exposure rather than a primary oil-shock channel.

## May 2026 chokepoint dependency read

The FT / Verschuur-Lumma-Hall table assigns about 6% of [[Ukraine]]'s seaborne trade exposure to the Korea Strait. The small share is still useful because it shows how dependency maps include second- and third-order routes far from the country being measured.
