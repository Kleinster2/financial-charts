# Jiuquan Satellite Launch Center

Primary [[China|Chinese]] satellite launch facility, located in the Gobi Desert in northwestern China. Launch site for [[TEE-01B]] (June 2024), the Chinese-built imaging satellite subsequently transferred to [[Iran]]'s [[IRGC]] Aerospace Force via the [[Earth Eye Co]] "in-orbit delivery" contract (see [[IRGC Chinese satellite targeting March 2026]]). Operates manned and unmanned missions including Shenzhou crewed flights, commercial satellite launches, and test payloads.
