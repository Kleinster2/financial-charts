# Lombok Strait

The Lombok Strait is an Indonesian passage east of Bali connecting the Indian Ocean to the Java Sea and wider Pacific routes. It is a deeper but longer alternative to the [[Strait of Malacca]] for some large vessels and for ships seeking to avoid scrutiny in Malacca.

## May 2026 shadow-fleet route signal

The FT visual story, using [[UANI]], [[MarineTraffic]], and Copernicus, showed the Iranian supertanker Huge reappearing in the Lombok Strait on May 3, 2026 after sailing dark near [[Sri Lanka]] and before appearing in the South China Sea on May 13. The route bypassed [[Strait of Malacca]], which the article linked to higher US and partner scrutiny of illicit or sanctioned oil flows.
