# Oresund Strait

The Oresund Strait runs between [[Denmark]] and [[Sweden]] and is one of the passages connecting the Baltic Sea to the North Sea. In the investing vault it is part of the broader [[Danish Straits]] system.

## May 2026 chokepoint dependency read

The FT visual story cites the Oresund as a no-reroute chokepoint in the Verschuur-Lumma-Hall framework and assigns it about 27% of [[Ukraine]]'s seaborne trade exposure in the article's country table. The implication is that Baltic/North Sea access can be a material country-level vulnerability even when the headline crisis is in the Gulf.
