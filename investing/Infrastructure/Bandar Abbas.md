# Bandar Abbas

[[Iran]]'s primary commercial port, located on the southern coast of the country at the entrance to the [[Strait of Hormuz]]. Handles the majority of Iran's seaborne imports and exports — including pistachios, refined products, and general cargo — and is the main pressure point for any trade-disruption analysis of Iran during periods of [[Strait of Hormuz]] tension. The Shahid Rajaee container terminal is the largest sub-facility.

## Why Bandar Abbas matters for this vault

When the [[Strait of Hormuz]] is contested or [[Iran]]'s coast is under blockade, the structural pinch point for Iranian non-oil trade is Bandar Abbas. It is the default first impact point for sanctions, war, and naval blockade scenarios. The 2026 conflict has rerouted Iranian agricultural exports (notably [[Pistachio market|pistachios]]) onto slower and more expensive land routes — through [[Turkey]] to [[Mersin]] and the [[Suez Canal]] for India-bound cargo, or by rail to [[China]].
