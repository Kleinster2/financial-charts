# Dover Strait

The Dover Strait is the narrow passage between the [[United Kingdom]] and [[France]], connecting the English Channel to the North Sea. In the investing vault it matters as a high-density European trade chokepoint rather than an energy chokepoint.

## May 2026 chokepoint dependency read

The [[Financial Times]] / Verschuur-Lumma-Hall country dependency table cited the Dover Strait as handling about 20% of [[Ukraine]]'s seaborne trade. The market point is that even short European sea lanes can matter in country-level dependency maps when trade is already forced through a limited set of ports and passages.
