# Cushing

The delivery point for [[WTI]] futures contracts and the largest commercial crude oil storage hub in the [[United States]]. Located in Cushing, Oklahoma, with ~76M barrels of storage capacity. Connected to [[Gulf Coast refiners]] and Midcontinent refineries by pipeline.
