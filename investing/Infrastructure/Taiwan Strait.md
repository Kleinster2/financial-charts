# Taiwan Strait

The Taiwan Strait separates [[Taiwan]] from mainland [[China]]. Navigational detours can be short compared with Suez or Panama, but the economic risk is larger because any serious disruption would likely be embedded in a broader US-China or regional security crisis.

## May 2026 chokepoint dependency read

The FT article cites business executives' worst-case scenario as a [[China]]-[[Taiwan]] conflict. The vessel detour around the strait may be manageable, but the semiconductor, manufacturing, sanctions, insurance, and alliance effects would be far larger than the extra sailing distance. Verschuur-Lumma-Hall identify geopolitical tensions at the Taiwan Strait as one of the highest-risk chokepoint-hazard combinations.
