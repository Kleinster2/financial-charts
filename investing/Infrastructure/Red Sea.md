# Red Sea

The Red Sea is the sea corridor linking the Indian Ocean via [[Bab el-Mandeb]] to the [[Suez Canal]] and the Mediterranean. In the investing vault it matters as the shipping route whose security determines whether Europe-Asia traffic uses Suez or diverts around the [[Cape of Good Hope]].
