# Khor Fakkan

Deepwater container port on the east coast of the UAE (Sharjah emirate), outside the [[Strait of Hormuz]] on the Gulf of Oman. Strategic positioning: gives shippers a UAE option that bypasses the Hormuz chokepoint. Operated by Gulftainer. Named in post-ceasefire 2026 shipping commentary as facing persistent schedule disruption despite being a Hormuz bypass option, because of rerouted cargo volumes overwhelming capacity ([[Destine Ozuygur]], [[Xeneta]]).
