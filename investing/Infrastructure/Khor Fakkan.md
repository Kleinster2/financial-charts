# Khor Fakkan

Khor Fakkan is a deepwater container port on the east coast of the [[UAE]], outside the [[Strait of Hormuz]] on the Gulf of Oman. It is strategically useful as a Hormuz-bypass port, but April 2026 commentary still cited it as exposed to rerouting congestion and schedule disruption.
