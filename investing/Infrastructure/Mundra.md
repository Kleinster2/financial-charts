# Mundra

Deepwater port on the [[India]]n coast of the Gulf of Kutch, Gujarat. India's largest private commercial port, operated by Adani Ports (APSEZ). Major container and bulk gateway handling a significant share of India's western-coast maritime trade. Named in post-ceasefire 2026 shipping commentary as one of three Indian Ocean / Gulf hubs facing persistent schedule disruption from the [[Strait of Hormuz]] crisis rerouting ([[Destine Ozuygur]], [[Xeneta]]).
