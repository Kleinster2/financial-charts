# Mundra

Mundra is a deepwater port on India's Gulf of Kutch coast and a major western-coast gateway for container and bulk trade. It was cited in April 2026 shipping commentary as one of the hubs where post-ceasefire schedule disruption could persist after the [[Strait of Hormuz]] shock.
