Salalah — major port in southern [[Oman]] on the Arabian Sea, outside the [[Strait of Hormuz]]. A potential terminus for southbound pipeline bypass routes from the Gulf. Forced to shut temporarily in early April 2026 after drone attacks during the [[Operation Epic Fury|Iran conflict]], demonstrating that Omani ports are not immune from Iranian security threats despite being geographically outside the strait.
