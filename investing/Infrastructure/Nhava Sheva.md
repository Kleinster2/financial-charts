# Nhava Sheva

Nhava Sheva, formally Jawaharlal Nehru Port, is one of India's largest container gateways on the west coast near Mumbai. It was named in April 2026 shipping commentary as a port where schedule disruption could linger after the Hormuz ceasefire headline.
