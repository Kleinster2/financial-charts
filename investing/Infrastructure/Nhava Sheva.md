# Nhava Sheva

Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust (JNPT), [[India]]'s largest container port, located across the harbor from Mumbai. Government-operated. Handles the majority of India's containerized foreign trade on the west coast. Named in post-ceasefire 2026 shipping commentary as one of three Indian Ocean / Gulf hubs facing persistent schedule disruption from [[Strait of Hormuz]] rerouting ([[Destine Ozuygur]], [[Xeneta]]).
