# Short interest tracker

#tracker #markets #signals

Monthly short interest snapshots for public companies in the vault. See [[Short interest]] for interpretation framework.

---

## How to use

- Update monthly (around 15th when FINRA data refreshes)
- Add notes on inflection points
- Compare SI trend vs stock price trend
- Flag: SI rising after big run = emerging skepticism
