#concept #regulation #usa

**NEPA** — the National Environmental Policy Act, the 1970 U.S. law requiring federal agencies to assess the environmental impact of major actions before proceeding. NEPA review is a recurring bottleneck for construction, infrastructure, and energy projects, and permitting timelines are a structural constraint on housing and infrastructure supply. The [[21st Century ROAD to Housing Act]] streamlines NEPA review to speed home construction.
