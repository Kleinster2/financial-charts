**Value vs Growth** — Fundamental investing style divide. Cheap stocks vs. fast growers. Cyclical rotation.

---

## Definitions

| Style | Focus | Metrics |
|-------|-------|---------|
| **Value** | Cheap, undervalued | P/E, P/B, dividend yield |
| **Growth** | Fast-growing | Revenue growth, PEG |
| **GARP** | Growth at reasonable price | Hybrid |
| **Quality** | High returns, stable | ROE, margins |

---

## Value characteristics

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Low P/E | Cheap earnings |
| Low P/B | Below book value |
| High dividend | Income focus |
| Cyclical | Economically sensitive |
| Contrarian | Out of favor |

---

## Growth characteristics

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| High revenue growth | >15-20% |
| High P/E | Expensive on earnings |
| Low/no dividend | Reinvesting |
| Secular | Long-term trends |
| Momentum | Market favorites |

---

## Rate sensitivity

| Environment | Favored style |
|-------------|---------------|
| Falling rates | Growth |
| Rising rates | Value |
| Low rates | Growth |
| High rates | Value |

Growth stocks have longer duration (cash flows further out).
