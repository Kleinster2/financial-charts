## Investment implications

**US advantage plays:**
- [[NVIDIA]], [[AMD]] — AI chips
- Apple — premium phones (but China exposure)
- Tesla — EVs (but China competition)

**China advantage plays:**
- BYD — EVs, batteries
- DJI — drones (private)
- CATL — batteries
- Huawei — phones, telecom (limited access)

**Watch:**
- Robotics — China price disruption
- AI — DeepSeek efficiency gains
- EVs — tariff walls vs cost advantage
