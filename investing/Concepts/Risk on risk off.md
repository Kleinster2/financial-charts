# Risk On / Risk Off

Market regime framework. "Risk on" = investors seek return, buy equities/credit/EM. "Risk off" = investors seek safety, buy treasuries/gold/cash.

---

## Regime indicators

| Indicator | Risk On | Risk Off |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| IG spreads | Tightening (<100 bps) | Widening (>150 bps) |
| VIX | <20 | >30 |
| HY spreads | Compressing | Blowing out |
| USD | Weakening | Strengthening |
| [[Gold]] | Flat/down | Rising |
| EM flows | Inflows | Outflows |
| Treasury yields | Rising (growth) | Falling (safety) |

## What triggers risk-off

| Catalyst | Mechanism |
|----------|-----------|
| Fed hawkish surprise | Rates up, multiples down |
| Credit event | Spreads blow out |
| Geopolitical shock | Flight to safety |
| Growth scare | Earnings revisions down |
| Liquidity crunch | Forced selling |
