**EU ETS** (European Union Emissions Trading System) — World's largest carbon trading system. Covers ~40% of EU greenhouse gas emissions. Carbon price range: $50-150/tonne CO₂ (2024-2025). Sets the benchmark price for [[CBAM]] carbon border certificates.

---

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Launched | 2005 |
| Coverage | ~40% of EU GHG emissions |
| Carbon price (2024-2025) | €50-150/tonne |
| Mechanism | Cap-and-trade with declining cap |
