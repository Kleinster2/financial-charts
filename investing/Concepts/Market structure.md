## Payment for order flow (PFOF)

**How it works:**

1. Retail broker (Robinhood) routes order to market maker
2. Market maker pays broker for the flow
3. Market maker profits from spread
4. Retail gets "free" trading

**Controversy:** Conflict of interest vs price improvement debate.

**Players:** [[Citadel Securities]], Virtu dominate PFOF.
