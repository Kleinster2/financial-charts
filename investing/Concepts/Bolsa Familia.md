**Bolsa Familia** — [[Brazil]]'s flagship conditional cash transfer program serving ~19.2 million families. Became politically explosive in the betting context: 27% of payments (R$ 3.7B) were transferred to betting sites in January 2025, leading to a government ban on ~900,000 recipients from betting platforms.
