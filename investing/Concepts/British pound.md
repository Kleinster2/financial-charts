## Gilt crisis legacy (Sep 2022)

Truss mini-budget triggered GBP crash to 1.035 (near parity).

**Lessons:**
- UK vulnerable to fiscal credibility shocks
- "Gilt vigilantes" constrain fiscal space
- BoE had to intervene in gilt market

Current government (Starmer) fiscally cautious as a result.
