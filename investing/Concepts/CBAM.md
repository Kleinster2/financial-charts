**CBAM** (Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism) — The [[EU]]'s carbon border tax on imported goods. Phases in 2026-2034. Imposes a carbon cost on imports based on emissions intensity of production, equalizing carbon costs between EU domestic producers (subject to [[EU ETS]]) and foreign exporters. Structurally advantages low-emission producers like [[Brazil]] over high-emission exporters like [[China]] and [[India]].
