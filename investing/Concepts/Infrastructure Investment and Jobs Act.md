**Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act** — 2021 U.S. infrastructure law that funds transport, grid, broadband, and public-works investment, making it a durable policy reference point across infrastructure and utility notes.

---

## Why it matters

For the vault, IIJA is a financing framework for real-asset buildout. It matters wherever federal money changes the pace of utility upgrades, grid hardening, transport capex, or broadband deployment.
