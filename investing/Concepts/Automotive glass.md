#concept #manufacturing #automotive

Specialized glass used in vehicles -- windshields (laminated), side windows, and rear windows (tempered). The global automotive glass market is concentrated among a handful of manufacturers, with [[Fuyao Glass Industry Group]] holding ~30% global share.

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Global market size | ~$25B (2024 est.) |
| Key technology | Laminated (windshield), tempered (side/rear) |
| Growth drivers | ADAS sensors, HUDs, larger glass surfaces in EVs |
| Consolidation | Top 5 manufacturers hold ~75%+ share |

## Major producers

- [[Fuyao Glass Industry Group]] — #1 globally (~30% share)
- [[AGC Inc.]] — #2, Japanese multinational
- [[Saint-Gobain]] — French industrial conglomerate
- [[NSG Group]] — Japanese, owns Pilkington
- [[Xinyi Glass]] — Chinese, growing share
- [[Vitro]] — Mexican, strong in Americas
