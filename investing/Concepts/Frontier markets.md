**Frontier markets** — Smaller, less liquid emerging markets. Higher risk/reward. Vietnam, Nigeria, Bangladesh, Kenya.

---

## Frontier vs EM

| Factor | Frontier | Emerging |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Market cap | Smaller | Larger |
| Liquidity | Lower | Higher |
| Foreign access | Limited | Better |
| Volatility | Higher | High |
| Correlation | Lower to DM | Moderate |
