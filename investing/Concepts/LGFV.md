#concept #china #debt #fiscal #stub

**LGFV (local government financing vehicle)** — off-balance-sheet entities [[China|Chinese]] municipalities use to borrow and fund infrastructure, circumventing direct local-government borrowing limits. Collectively a major source of China's "hidden" local debt and a recurring systemic-risk concern. Relevant to [[Data as an asset]]: some heavily indebted LGFVs have used recognized data assets as collateral for fresh loans under China's 2024 data-accounting rule — linking data recognition to leverage, with the [[US-China Economic and Security Review Commission]] noting the underlying asset value is "difficult to determine."
