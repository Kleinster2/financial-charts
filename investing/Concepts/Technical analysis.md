#concept #investing #trading #charts

**Technical analysis** — Study of price and volume patterns. Timing tool. Support, resistance, trends, indicators.

---

## Core principles

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Price discounts all | Information in price |
| Prices trend | Momentum persists |
| History repeats | Patterns recur |

---

## Trend identification

| Trend type | Definition |
|------------|------------|
| **Uptrend** | Higher highs, higher lows |
| **Downtrend** | Lower highs, lower lows |
| **Sideways** | Range-bound |

---

## Support and resistance

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Support** | Price floor, buying interest |
| **Resistance** | Price ceiling, selling pressure |
| **Breakout** | Price moves through level |
| **Breakdown** | Price falls through support |

---

## Key chart patterns

| Pattern | Signal |
|---------|--------|
| Head & shoulders | Reversal (bearish) |
| Inverse H&S | Reversal (bullish) |
| Double top/bottom | Reversal |
| Cup and handle | Continuation (bullish) |
| Triangle | Continuation |
| Flag/pennant | Continuation |

---

## Moving averages

| MA | Use |
|-----|-----|
| 20-day | Short-term trend |
| 50-day | Medium-term trend |
| 200-day | Long-term trend |
| Golden cross | 50 crosses above 200 (bullish) |
| Death cross | 50 crosses below 200 (bearish) |

---

## Momentum indicators

| Indicator | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| **RSI** | Overbought (>70) / oversold (<30) |
| **MACD** | Trend direction, momentum |
| **Stochastics** | Overbought/oversold |
| **ADX** | Trend strength |

---

## Volume analysis

| Signal | Interpretation |
|--------|----------------|
| High volume breakout | Confirmation |
| Low volume breakout | Suspicious |
| Volume climax | Potential reversal |
| Declining volume | Trend weakening |

---

## Fibonacci levels

| Level | Use |
|-------|-----|
| 23.6% | Shallow retracement |
| 38.2% | Common retracement |
| 50% | Half retracement |
| 61.8% | Deep retracement |

---

## Limitations

| Issue | Reality |
|-------|---------|
| Self-fulfilling | Popular levels matter because watched |
| Lagging indicators | React to past prices |
| Pattern subjectivity | Different interpretations |
| Fundamental ignore | Misses value changes |

---

## Integration with fundamentals

| Use case | Approach |
|----------|----------|
| Entry timing | Buy fundamentally good at support |
| Exit timing | Sell overvalued at resistance |
| Risk management | Stop losses at key levels |
| Position sizing | Reduce at overbought |
