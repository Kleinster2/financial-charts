#concept #alphabet #ai #gemini

Google Health Coach — Gemini-powered AI health coach inside [[Google Health Premium]], launching globally May 19, 2026. Combines fitness data, sleep, nutrition, environment, cycle tracking, and (with consent) US medical records into adaptive plans and proactive recommendations. Customized via onboarding around goals, equipment, injuries, lifestyle.

The Coach is the Gemini surface that monetizes the [[Fitbit Air]] / [[Pixel Watch]] / [[Fitbit Charge 6]] hardware funnel — see [[Google Health Premium]] for the subscription wrapper, [[Fitbit Air launch May 2026]] for the launch context.

---

## Capabilities

- Adaptive fitness plans (cardio, strength, recovery)
- Deeper sleep insights (stages, debt, recovery scoring)
- Proactive health recommendations
- Multimodal logging (text, photo, voice for nutrition / symptoms / environment)
- US medical records ingestion (with explicit user consent)
