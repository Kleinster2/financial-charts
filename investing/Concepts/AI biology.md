#concept #ai #biotech

AI biology — use of machine learning, generative models, and high-throughput experimentation to design, optimize, or discover biological systems and therapeutics.

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Core use case | Drug and protein design |
| Key workflow | Model generation + wet-lab validation |
| Example actor | [[Generate Biomedicines]] |
| Investing relevance | Extends AI infrastructure value into downstream scientific platforms; visible in [[NVIDIA]]'s [[13F]] book through [[Generate Biomedicines]] |

## Why it matters

AI biology is one of the downstream markets that can justify persistent demand for accelerated compute without relying only on consumer AI or enterprise agents. The core question is whether generated biological candidates move from faster search to clinically useful therapeutics.
