Full note lives in the history vault: [1973 Oil Crisis](obsidian://open?vault=history&file=06%20-%20Contemporary%2F1973%20Oil%20Crisis)

[[OAPEC]] embargo over the Yom Kippur War (Oct 1973 - Mar 1974). Crude quadrupled from ~$2.90 to ~$11.65/bbl, removing ~4.4 mb/d. Created the [[IEA]], the [[Strategic Petroleum Reserve]], and the [[Petrodollar]] system. Standard benchmark for severe supply shocks — the [[2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis]] removed ~4.5x more volume.
