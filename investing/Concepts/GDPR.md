# GDPR

**GDPR** — the EU General Data Protection Regulation, the bloc's core privacy and personal-data protection regime. In the investing vault it matters as a compliance, fines, data-governance, and platform-regulation risk surface for companies operating in or serving the [[EU]].

## Market relevance

GDPR turns data governance into a direct financial and regulatory exposure. It is relevant for cloud platforms, ad-tech, [[SaaS]], consumer apps, cybersecurity incidents, and any issuer whose business model depends on EU personal-data processing.
