# Streaming wars

The competitive battle between streaming services for subscribers, content, and profitability.

| Service | Subs | Owner | Status |
|---------|------|-------|--------|
| [[Netflix]] | 280M+ | Netflix | Profitable leader |

---

## Phase evolution

| Phase | Period | Focus |
|-------|--------|-------|
| **Land grab** | 2019-2022 | Subscriber growth at all costs |
| **Reality check** | 2022-2023 | Losses unsustainable |
| **Profitability** | 2024-present | Margins > growth |
| **Consolidation** | 2025+ | Mergers, bundles |
