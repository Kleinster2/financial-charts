#concept #trade #china #asean

**China-ASEAN FTA** — Free trade agreement between China and ASEAN 10. Predates RCEP. World's largest FTA by population when signed (2010). Now subsumed into RCEP.

---

## Overview

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Parties | China + ASEAN 10 |
| Population | 2B+ |
| Combined GDP | ~$20T |
| Signed | 2002 (goods), 2007 (services) |
| Full implementation | 2010 |
| Upgraded | 2015, 2019 |
| Status | Active, RCEP overlays |

## Key terms

### Tariffs

| Phase | Coverage |
|-------|----------|
| 2005 | Early Harvest (agriculture) |
| 2010 | Zero tariffs (ASEAN-6 + China) |
| 2015 | Zero tariffs (CLMV countries) |
| 90%+ goods | Duty-free |

**CLMV:** Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, Vietnam — longer transition.

| Sector | Access |
|--------|--------|

## Relationship to RCEP
