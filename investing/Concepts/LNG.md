#concept #energy #commodity

**LNG** — Liquefied natural gas. Natural gas cooled to -260°F for shipping. Enables global gas trade. Key fuel for AI data center power.

---

## Why LNG matters for AI thesis

| Connection | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Data center power | Gas turbines for baseload/peaker plants |
| Faster than nuclear | Gas plants built in 2-3 years vs 10+ for nuclear |
| Cleaner than coal | ~50% less CO2, bridge fuel |
| [[Crusoe Energy]] model | Stranded gas → compute |
| Hyperscaler backup | Gas for grid reliability |

---

## How LNG works

```
Production → Liquefaction → Shipping → Regasification → Distribution
   (well)      (-260°F)     (tankers)    (terminal)      (pipeline)
```

**Why liquefy?** Gas volume shrinks 600x, enabling ocean transport.

## Investment case

**Bull:**
- AI/data center power demand
- Europe needs Russia replacement
- Asia demand growing
- US cost advantage
- Energy security priority
- Bridge fuel narrative

**Bear:**
- Overbuild risk (too much capacity)
- Renewable competition long-term
- Permitting challenges (US pause 2024)
- Price volatility
- ESG pressure on fossil fuels
