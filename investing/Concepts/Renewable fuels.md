# Renewable fuels

**Drop-in and blended fuel alternatives produced from biological or renewable feedstocks** — includes renewable diesel (HVO), biodiesel (FAME), [[Sustainable Aviation Fuel]] (SAF), ethanol, and renewable natural gas (RNG). Governed in the US by the Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS), which mandates blending volumes via Renewable Identification Numbers (RINs).
