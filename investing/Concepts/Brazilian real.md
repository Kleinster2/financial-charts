## Election-year volatility window (2026)

Every Brazilian presidential election since 2002 has produced 8-15% realized currency volatility in the final 60-90 days before the vote. With the [[2026 Brazil presidential election]] first round on October 4 and runoff October 25, 2026, the vol window opens roughly August. Hedging USD-denominated returns becomes more expensive through Q3 as implied vol rises. Initial market reaction to [Flávio Bolsonaro](obsidian://open?vault=Brazil%20News%20%26%20Analysis&file=People%2FFl%C3%A1vio%20Bolsonaro)'s December 2025 candidacy announcement was a ~3% BRL sell-off on the endorsement alone.
