#concept #defense #drones

Drone Dominance — second-Trump-term Pentagon procurement initiative aimed at fielding 200,000+ U.S.-made drones by 2027 with $1.1bn in committed funding. Combined with [[Pete Hegseth|Secretary Hegseth]]'s July 2025 reclassification of small drones as "consumable" supplies (allowing field-commander direct procurement), the December 2025 [[FCC]] foreign-drone ban (which removed [[DJI]] from federal purchase eligibility), and the June 2025 *"Unleashing American Drone Dominance"* executive order (expanded BVLOS, prioritized U.S.-made systems), the program forms the federal-policy backdrop for the second-term U.S. drone-startup procurement boom — including the [[Trump family investment portfolio|Trump family]] drone quartet ([[Powerus]], [[XTEND]], [[Unusual Machines]], [[Aureus Greenway Holdings]]).
