#concept #alphabet #app #health

Google Health — [[Google]]'s consumer health app, replacing the Fitbit app on May 19, 2026. Hosts the free tier (basic data, history) and the paid [[Google Health Premium]] tier with [[Google Health Coach]]. Pairs with [[Fitbit Air]], [[Fitbit Charge 6]], [[Pixel Watch]], and other supported wearables.

---

## Role

The app is the consumer-facing surface for the [[Google]] health platform: free tier handles data display, sync, and history; the [[Google Health Premium]] tier ($9.99/mo or $99/yr; bundled with [[Google AI Pro]] / [[Google AI Ultra]]) unlocks [[Google Health Coach]] and adaptive coaching.

Replacing the Fitbit app is a platform consolidation move — the Fitbit brand survives on hardware ([[Fitbit Air]], [[Fitbit Charge 6]]) but the software identity migrates to Google.
