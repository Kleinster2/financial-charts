#concept #accounting

**Remaining performance obligations** (RPO) — total contracted but unrecognized revenue, combining deferred revenue and backlog. Key forward indicator for enterprise software and cloud companies — measures committed future revenue not yet delivered.

## Why it matters

RPO represents binding commitments, not pipeline or bookings. For cloud/infrastructure companies, large RPO signals visibility into future revenue but carries execution risk — contracts can be renegotiated, customers can slow consumption, and concentration in a few large contracts amplifies counterparty risk.
