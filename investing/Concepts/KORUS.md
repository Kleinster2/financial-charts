**KORUS** — US-Korea Free Trade Agreement. Major bilateral FTA covering semiconductors, autos, agriculture. Renegotiated under Trump 2018.

---

## Overview

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Parties | US, South Korea |
| Population | 380M |
| Combined GDP | ~$28T |
| Signed | Jun 2007 |
| In force | Mar 2012 |
| Renegotiated | 2018 (Trump) |

### Industrial goods

|--------|-----------|
| [[Semiconductors]] | Duty-free |

### Autos (contentious)

| Flow | Original | 2018 revision |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Korea → US | 2.5% → 0% | Extended phase-out |
| US → Korea | 8% → 0% | Kept |
| Safety standards | Korea aligned | Expanded US access |
| US truck tariff | 25% until 2021 | Extended to 2041 |

**Trump renegotiation:** Extended 25% US truck tariff 20 more years.

## Investment relevance

### Semiconductor trade

| Flow | Value | Key products |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Korea → US | $25B+ | Memory (Samsung, SK), foundry |
| US → Korea | $10B+ | Equipment, EDA, materials |

KORUS = duty-free semiconductor trade framework.

## Trump renegotiation (2018)

| Change | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Truck tariff | Extended 25% to 2041 |
| Steel | Korea quota (exempt from 232) |
| Auto safety | US standards accepted |
| Trade balance | Symbolic changes |

**Context:** Trump threatened withdrawal; Korea accepted minor changes.
