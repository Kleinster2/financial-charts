#concept #commodities #industrial-metals

**Base metals** — Non-ferrous industrial metals: aluminum, zinc, nickel, lead, tin. Distinct from precious metals (gold, silver). Primary vehicle: DBB (Invesco DB Base Metals Fund — aluminum, zinc, copper weighted).

## Key drivers

| Driver | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **China demand** | ~50% of global consumption |
| **Green transition** | Aluminum, copper, nickel demand |
| **Dollar strength** | Inverse relationship |
| **Energy costs** | Aluminum production sensitive |
| **Supply constraints** | Tight for Al, Cu, Sn; loose for Ni, Zn |
