## Why CHF is a safe haven

| Factor | Mechanism |
|--------|-----------|
| **Political neutrality** | No geopolitical risk |
| **Current account surplus** | Net creditor nation |
| **Banking system** | Deep, stable, discreet |
| **Low inflation** | Historically stable prices |
| **SNB credibility** | Strong institution |

---

## SNB policy

SNB historically fights CHF strength (hurts exports):
- Negative rates (until 2022)
- FX intervention (accumulating reserves)
- Verbal jawboning

**Recent shift:** Allowing more CHF strength as inflation controlled.
