**Latency arbitrage** — Profiting from the speed differential between trading venues. The fastest participants see price changes at one exchange and race to pick off stale quotes at others before they update. The practice was exposed in [[Flash Boys]] (2014) and is the central issue in [[IEX]]'s market structure innovations.

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Time advantage | Microseconds to milliseconds |
| Key critic | [[IEX]] (Brad Katsuyama) |
| Defense | Liquidity provision, tighter spreads |
| Options amplifier | Hundreds of strikes per stock magnify stale-quote exposure |
