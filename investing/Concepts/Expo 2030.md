**Expo 2030** is the world exposition awarded to [[Riyadh|Riyadh]], [[Saudi Arabia]], scheduled to open in 2030. Estimated cost ~$7.8B. The project is protected from [[Saudi PIF]] spending cuts, reflecting its priority status within [[Saudi Vision 2030]]. Expected to drive infrastructure development and international visibility alongside other Saudi giga-projects.
