#concept #macro #crisis

**GFC** — Global Financial Crisis (2007-2009). Triggered by US subprime mortgage collapse, became global banking crisis. Reshaped financial regulation and industry structure.

---

## Timeline

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| 2007 | Subprime defaults rise |
| Mar 2008 | Bear Stearns fails |
| Sep 2008 | Lehman Brothers bankruptcy |
| Oct 2008 | TARP bailouts |
| 2009 | Recovery begins |

---

## Regulatory response

| Reform | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| [[Basel III]] | Higher bank capital requirements |
| Dodd-Frank | US financial regulation overhaul |
| Volcker Rule | Limits proprietary trading |

---

## Industry shifts

Banks retreated from:
- [[Middle-market]] lending
- Proprietary trading
- Complex structured products

[[Private credit]] and [[Alternative asset manager]]s filled gaps.
