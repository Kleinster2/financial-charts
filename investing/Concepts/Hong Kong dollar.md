# Hong Kong dollar

The currency of [[Hong Kong]], pegged to the US dollar since 1983 under a linked exchange rate system. The peg is maintained by the Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA) within a band of 7.75–7.85 HKD per USD.

Three commercial banks are authorized to issue HKD banknotes: [[HSBC]], [[Standard Chartered]], and [[BOC Hong Kong]].
