## What this tracks

**Inclusion criteria:**
- Top 3-month price performance among held/followed names
- Clear fundamental catalyst (not just momentum)
- Tied to active thesis

**Exclusion criteria:**
- Momentum fades (3M return drops significantly)
- Thesis breaks
- Position closed

**Not tracked here:**
- Index components (see [[Mega-cap tech acronyms]])
- Short positions
- Speculative names without thesis
