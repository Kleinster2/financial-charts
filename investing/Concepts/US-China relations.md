## Investment framework

**Tensions rise →**
- [[Defense]] stocks rally
- China-exposed tech sells off ([[Apple]], NVIDIA)
- Critical mineral plays gain
- Friendshoring beneficiaries ([[Mexico]], Vietnam, [[India]])

**Tensions ease →**
- China tech rallies
- US multinationals recover
- EM broadly benefits
