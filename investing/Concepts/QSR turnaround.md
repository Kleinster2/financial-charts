# QSR turnaround

Operating reset pattern in quick-service restaurants: simplify the menu, improve throughput, fix labor/store execution, rebuild brand trust, and only then push unit growth or pricing.

## Why it matters

The market often reprices QSR turnarounds before full earnings recovery because traffic inflection is the first observable proof that the operating playbook is working. [[Starbucks]] under [[Brian Niccol]] is the current vault case study; [[Chipotle]] is the comparison investors use because Niccol led that earlier reset.
