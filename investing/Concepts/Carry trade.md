**Carry trade** — Borrow low-yield currency, invest in high-yield. Classic: borrow JPY, buy AUD/EM. Risk-off unwinds violently.

---

## How carry trade works

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Borrow in low-yield currency (JPY, CHF) |
| 2 | Convert to high-yield currency (AUD, EM) |
| 3 | Invest in high-yield assets |
| 4 | Earn yield differential |
| 5 | Repay loan, keep spread |

---

## Classic pairs

| Funding | Target | Spread |
|---------|--------|--------|
| JPY (0%) | AUD (4%) | +4% |
| JPY (0%) | MXN (10%) | +10% |
| CHF (1%) | ZAR (8%) | +7% |
| EUR (3%) | BRL (12%) | +9% |

---

## Risks

| Risk | Impact |
|------|--------|
| FX moves | Can wipe out carry gains |
| Rate changes | Spread compression |
| Risk-off | Rapid unwind |
| Liquidity | Crowded exits |

---

## Unwind dynamics

| Trigger | Mechanism |
|---------|-----------|
| Risk-off event | Carry traders exit |
| Funding currency rallies | JPY strengthens |
| Target currency falls | Double loss |
| Margin calls | Forced selling |

**August 2024 example**: BOJ rate hike triggered massive JPY carry unwind.

---

## When carry works

| Condition | Favorable |
|-----------|-----------|
| Low volatility | Stable FX |
| Stable rates | Predictable spread |
| Risk-on sentiment | Position building |
| Yield differential | Wide enough |
