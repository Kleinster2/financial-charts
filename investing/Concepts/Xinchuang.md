#concept #china #technology-policy

**Xinchuang** (信创 / IT Application Innovation) — China's state-mandated initiative to replace foreign software and hardware in government, SOEs, and critical infrastructure with domestic alternatives by 2027.

---

## Core mandate

Document 79 requires replacement of foreign IT across:
- Central and local government agencies
- State-owned enterprises (SOEs)
- Critical sectors (finance, telecom, energy, transportation)

Target: complete domestic substitution by 2027.

## Scope

Covers operating systems, office software, databases, ERP, middleware, security, and hardware (CPUs, servers). Primary targets for replacement: [[Microsoft]] (Windows, Office 365), [[SAP]], [[Oracle]], [[VMware]].
