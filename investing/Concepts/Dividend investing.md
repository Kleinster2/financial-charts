**Dividend investing** — Focus on dividend-paying stocks. Income generation, total return, compounding via reinvestment.

---

## Dividend metrics

| Metric | Formula | Good |
|--------|---------|------|
| **Yield** | Annual div / Price | 2-5% |
| **Payout ratio** | Div / EPS | 30-60% |
| **FCF payout** | Div / FCF | <70% |
| **Growth rate** | YoY increase | >5% |

---

## Dividend safety

| Factor | Healthy | Concerning |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Payout ratio | <60% | >80% |
| FCF coverage | >1.5x | <1x |
| Debt/EBITDA | <3x | >5x |
| Track record | Consistent | Cuts history |

## Dividend growth vs high yield

| Strategy | Characteristics |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Dividend growth** | Lower yield, faster growth |
| **High yield** | Higher current income, slower growth |
| **Hybrid** | Moderate yield + growth |

## Dividend traps

| Signal | Red flag |
|--------|----------|
| Yield >8% | Likely unsustainable |
| Price declining | Market sees cut coming |
| Payout >100% | Borrowing to pay |
| Earnings falling | Coverage eroding |

**High yield can signal distress, not opportunity.**
