**Free cash flow** — Cash available after maintaining/expanding the business. Best measure of shareholder value creation.

---

## FCF calculation

| Method | Formula |
|--------|---------|
| **Simple** | Operating cash flow - Capex |
| **Detailed** | EBIT(1-t) + D&A - Capex - ΔNWC |
| **Levered** | FCF - Interest expense |
| **Unlevered** | EBIT(1-t) + D&A - Capex - ΔNWC |

---

## FCF vs. earnings

| Dimension | FCF | Earnings |
|-----------|-----|----------|
| Manipulation | Harder | Easier |
| Non-cash | Excluded | Included |
| Capex timing | Recognized | Spread |
| Working capital | Captured | Hidden |
| Cash reality | Yes | No |

**"Earnings are an opinion, cash is a fact."**

---

## FCF quality indicators

| Signal | Interpretation |
|--------|----------------|
| FCF > Net income | High quality earnings |
| FCF < Net income | Aggressive accounting |
| Negative FCF, positive NI | Capex heavy or working capital issue |
| Consistent FCF | Predictable business |

---

## FCF conversion

| Metric | Formula | Good |
|--------|---------|------|
| FCF/Net income | FCF ÷ NI | >80% |
| FCF/Revenue | FCF ÷ Rev | >10% |
| FCF/EBITDA | FCF ÷ EBITDA | >50% |

---

## Uses of FCF

| Use | Priority |
|-----|----------|
| Debt service | Required |
| Reinvestment | Growth |
| Dividends | Return |
| Buybacks | Return |
| M&A | Optionality |

---

## FCF yield

| Yield | Interpretation |
|-------|----------------|
| >10% | Potentially cheap |
| 5-10% | Fair value range |
| 3-5% | Expensive, needs growth |
| <3% | Very expensive |

**FCF Yield = FCF / Market Cap**

---

## Sector differences

| Sector | FCF profile |
|--------|-------------|
| Tech/SaaS | High FCF, low capex |
| Industrials | Cyclical FCF |
| Utilities | Regulated, stable |
| REITs | Use FFO instead |
| Financials | Use earnings |

---

## Red flags

| Signal | Concern |
|--------|---------|
| Persistent negative FCF | Cash burn |
| FCF << earnings | Quality issue |
| Rising receivables | Collection problems |
| Capex cutting | Mortgaging future |
| Stock comp ignored | Dilution hidden |
