#concept #trade #asia #pacific

**CPTPP** — Comprehensive and Progressive Trans-Pacific Partnership. High-standards Pacific trade bloc. US withdrew 2017. China, UK seeking accession.

---

## Overview

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Parties | 11 countries |
| Population | 500M |
| Combined GDP | ~$13T |
| Signed | Mar 2018 |
| In force | Dec 2018 |
| Origin | TPP (US-led) |
| US withdrawal | Jan 2017 |

---

## Member countries

| Region | Members |
|--------|---------|
| **Asia** | Japan, Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore, Brunei |
| **Oceania** | Australia, New Zealand |
| **Americas** | Canada, Mexico, Chile, Peru |

**Notable absences:** US (withdrew), China (applied 2021), Taiwan (applied 2021), UK (acceded 2023).

---

## Key terms

### Tariff elimination

| Category | Coverage | Timeline |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Industrial goods | 99%+ | 7-20 years |
| Agriculture | 95%+ | Varies |
| Sensitive items | Japan rice, Canada dairy | Limited |

Higher ambition than RCEP (90%).

### Suspended provisions (post-US)

| Provision | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| IP extensions | Suspended |
| Biologics data protection | Suspended (12→5 years) |
| ISDS for investment agreements | Suspended |
| Some labor/environment | Suspended |

**22 provisions suspended** after US withdrawal — these were US priorities.

### Rules of origin

| Product | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Autos | 45% → 55% (phased) |
| Textiles | Yarn-forward |
| General | Substantial transformation |

Regional cumulation across all 11 members.

### Services and investment

| Provision | Level |
|-----------|-------|
| Negative list | Yes (high ambition) |
| Financial services | Deep liberalization |
| Telecom | Competition requirements |
| E-commerce | Strong protections |
| Mode 4 | Moderate access |

Most ambitious services chapter of major FTAs.

### Digital trade

| Provision | Treatment |
|-----------|-----------|
| Data localization | Prohibited |
| Cross-border flows | Protected |
| Source code | Cannot require disclosure |
| Customs duties | Banned |

Strong chapter — template for USMCA digital provisions.

### Labor and environment (suspended)

| Provision | Original | Now |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| ILO standards | Binding | Suspended |
| Enforcement | Dispute settlement | Weakened |
| Environment | Paris-aligned | Suspended |

US provisions suspended — may revive if US rejoins.

### State-owned enterprises

| Provision | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Commercial basis | SOEs must operate commercially |
| Subsidies | Disciplines on support |
| Transparency | Disclosure requirements |

**Anti-China:** SOE disciplines seen as targeting Chinese model.

## Accession applications

| Applicant | Applied | Status | Prospects |
|-----------|---------|--------|-----------|
| **UK** | Feb 2021 | Acceded Jul 2023 | ✅ First new member |
| **China** | Sep 2021 | Pending | Unlikely (SOE rules, standards) |
| **Taiwan** | Sep 2021 | Pending | Blocked by China politics |
| **Ecuador** | Dec 2021 | Pending | Possible |
| **Costa Rica** | Aug 2022 | Pending | Possible |
| **Uruguay** | Dec 2022 | Pending | Possible |
| **Ukraine** | Jun 2023 | Pending | Symbolic |

### UK accession

| Significance | Detail |
|--------------|--------|
| First expansion | Post-Brexit trade strategy |
| GDP addition | +$3T |
| Signal | Bloc remains attractive |

### China application challenges

| Barrier | Issue |
|---------|-------|
| SOE disciplines | Would require reform |
| Labor standards | ILO compliance |
| Data rules | Conflicts with sovereignty |
| Political | Japan, Australia opposition |
| Taiwan | Would block Taiwan accession |

China unlikely to meet standards without significant reform.

## Comparison to TPP (original)

| Dimension | TPP | CPTPP |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| Members | 12 (incl. US) | 11 |
| GDP | $28T | $13T |
| US priorities | Included | 22 suspended |
| IP | TRIPS-plus | Scaled back |
| ISDS | Full | Limited |
| Ratification | Never | In force |

CPTPP = TPP minus US provisions.
