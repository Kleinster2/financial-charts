#concept #finance

Venture capital — asset class focused on funding early-stage, high-growth companies in exchange for equity. Concentrated in technology, biotech, and fintech. Key players include [[Andreessen Horowitz]], [[Sequoia Capital]], [[Founders Fund]], [[Khosla Ventures]], and [[GV|Google Ventures]].
