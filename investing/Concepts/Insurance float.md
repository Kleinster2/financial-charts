#concept #insurance #finance #stub

**Insurance float** — premiums collected by insurers before claims are paid. Provides investable capital at near-zero (sometimes negative) cost. [[Berkshire Hathaway]]'s float of $160B+ is the structural advantage underlying [[Warren Buffett]]'s leveraged returns (~1.6x).
