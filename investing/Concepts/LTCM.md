# LTCM

**LTCM (Long-Term Capital Management)** — Hedge fund founded by John Meriwether (1994) that collapsed in 1998 after leveraged convergence trades unwound during the Russian financial crisis. Peak gross leverage exceeded 25:1 on ~$125B in assets against ~$4.7B equity. Fed-coordinated $3.6B bailout by 14 banks to prevent systemic contagion. The canonical example of low net leverage masking catastrophic gross leverage risk.
