#concept #hardware #health

**Health wearables** — Electronic devices worn on the body for health/fitness tracking. This note covers traditional wearables (fitness bands, smartwatches). For AI-powered wearables (smart glasses, AI pins, ambient devices), see [[Screenless AI]].

---

## Market evolution

| Era | Dominant form | Key players |
|-----|---------------|-------------|
| 2011-2014 | Fitness bands | [[Jawbone]], [[Fitbit]], Nike |
| 2015-2020 | Smartwatches | [[Apple Watch]], Samsung, Garmin |
| 2020+ | Health monitors | Apple, Oura, Whoop, CGMs |

---

## Investment themes

- Health data as platform (Apple Health, Google Fit)
- Medical-grade monitoring (FDA-cleared ECG, glucose)
- Insurance/employer wellness programs
- Smart rings emerging (Oura, Samsung Galaxy Ring)
