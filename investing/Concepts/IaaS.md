#concept #cloud

**IaaS** (Infrastructure as a Service) — cloud computing model where providers deliver virtualized compute, storage, and networking on demand. The layer where AI infrastructure spending concentrates — GPU clusters, training compute, inference capacity.

## Quick stats

| Provider | IaaS position | AI infra focus |
|----------|--------------|----------------|
| [[Amazon|AWS]] | #1 | Trainium/Inferentia custom chips + [[NVIDIA]] |
| [[Microsoft|Azure]] | #2 | [[OpenAI]] partnership, Maia custom chips + [[NVIDIA]] |
| [[Google|GCP]] | #3 | TPUs + [[NVIDIA]] |
| [[Oracle Cloud Infrastructure|OCI]] | #4 | [[NVIDIA]]-only, fastest growing (+84%) |
