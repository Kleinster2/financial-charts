#concept

**App Tracking Transparency (ATT)** — [[Apple]] iOS privacy feature (iOS 14.5, April 2021) requiring apps to request user permission before tracking across other apps/websites.

---

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Launch | April 2021 (iOS 14.5) |
| Opt-out rate | ~75% of users |
| [[Meta]] revenue impact | ~$10B annually |

---

## Impact

Broke cross-app tracking that powered precision ad targeting. [[Meta]], [[Snap]], and mobile gaming companies hit hardest. Accelerated shift toward first-party data and contextual advertising.
