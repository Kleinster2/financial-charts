**Platform economics** — Businesses connecting producers and consumers. Value from orchestration, not production.

---

## Platform vs. pipeline

| Dimension | Platform | Pipeline |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Value creation | Orchestration | Production |
| Assets | Network, data | Inventory |
| Marginal cost | Near zero | Unit cost |
| Scaling | Network effects | Linear |
| Example | Airbnb | Marriott |

## Platform power dynamics

| Stage | Behavior |
|-------|----------|
| **Growth** | Subsidize one side |
| **Maturity** | Raise take rates |
| **Dominance** | Vertical integration |

---

## Winner-take-most dynamics

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Single-homing | Strengthens winner |
| Multi-homing | Weakens moat |
| Differentiation | Enables coexistence |
| Regulation | Fragments market |
