# Bletchley Declaration

Bletchley Declaration -- November 2023 frontier-AI safety declaration agreed at the UK-hosted AI Safety Summit. It matters for the investing vault because it is one of the few AI-governance reference points that included both the [[United States]] and [[China]] at a moment of strategic rivalry.

## Why it matters

The declaration does not solve AI arms-race dynamics, export controls, or model-weight proliferation. Its value is narrower: it proves that rival AI powers can acknowledge shared frontier-model risks in a public multilateral setting. That is why later proposals for U.S.-China AI guardrails keep circling back to Bletchley as precedent.

*Source: GOV.UK, "AI Safety Summit 2023: The Bletchley Declaration," published Nov 1 2023, updated Feb 13 2025: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/ai-safety-summit-2023-the-bletchley-declaration*
