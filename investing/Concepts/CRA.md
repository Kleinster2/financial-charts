# CRA

A Certificado de Recebíveis do Agronegócio is the agribusiness analogue of the [[CRI]] — a securitization of agribusiness receivables (supplier financing, commodity sales, agro-input credit) packaged and sold to investors.

Chart data unavailable — this is an instrument category, not a single tracked price series.

## Mechanics

- Income-tax-exempt for individuals, the same tax incentive that makes [[CRI]] attractive — Brazil uses the exemption to channel retail savings into housing and agribusiness.
- Backed by a pool of agro receivables; the big protein and ag names are common originators (a [[Marfrig]] CRA appeared in the Carteira Aberta portfolios).

## Risks / watch-outs

- Illiquid — secondary sales take a mark-to-market hit.
- Credit risk runs to the agribusiness counterparties and the commodity cycle behind them.
