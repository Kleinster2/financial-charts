# Hang Seng Index

The benchmark stock market index for [[Hong Kong]], comprising the 80 largest companies listed on the [[HKEX]]. Published by [[Hang Seng Bank]]'s Hang Seng Indexes Company.

Gained 28% in 2025 (best year since 2017), briefly trading above 28,000 in early 2026. Valuations remain cheap — P/E ~11.5x vs [[S&P 500]] ~22x forward.
