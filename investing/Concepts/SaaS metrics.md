**SaaS metrics** — Key performance indicators for software-as-a-service businesses. Recurring revenue, retention, efficiency.

---

## Revenue metrics

| Metric | Definition | Good |
|--------|------------|------|
| **ARR** | Annual recurring revenue | Growth rate |
| **MRR** | Monthly recurring revenue | Trend |
| **ACV** | Annual contract value | >$100K enterprise |
| **TCV** | Total contract value | Multi-year deals |
| **Bookings** | New contracts signed | Leading indicator |

---

## Growth metrics

| Metric | Definition | Good |
|--------|------------|------|
| **YoY growth** | Year-over-year ARR | >30% |
| **NRR** | Net revenue retention | >120% |
| **GRR** | Gross revenue retention | >90% |
| **Logo churn** | Customer loss rate | <10% |

---

## Net revenue retention (NRR)

| Component | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| Expansion | Upsells, cross-sells |
| Contraction | Downgrades |
| Churn | Cancellations |

**NRR = (Starting ARR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / Starting ARR**

| NRR | Interpretation |
|-----|----------------|
| >130% | Elite (Snowflake tier) |
| 120-130% | Excellent |
| 110-120% | Good |
| 100-110% | Adequate |
| <100% | Leaky bucket |

---

## Efficiency metrics

| Metric | Definition | Good |
|--------|------------|------|
| **Rule of 40** | Growth + FCF margin | >40% |
| **Magic number** | Revenue efficiency | >1.0 |
| **CAC payback** | Months to recover CAC | <18 |
| **LTV/CAC** | Unit economics | >3x |

---

## Unit economics

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **CAC** | Customer acquisition cost |
| **LTV** | Lifetime value |
| **Gross margin** | Revenue - COGS |
| **Contribution margin** | Post-S&M margin |

---

## Rule of 40

**Growth rate + FCF margin ≥ 40%**

| Company type | Balance |
|--------------|---------|
| Hypergrowth | 60% growth, -20% margin |
| Balanced | 30% growth, 10% margin |
| Mature | 15% growth, 25% margin |

| Metric | Purpose |
|--------|---------|

## Red flags

| Signal | Concern |
|--------|---------|
| NRR declining | Product-market fit erosion |
| CAC rising | Competition, saturation |
| Churn spiking | Customer satisfaction |
| Deferred revenue falling | Demand weakness |
