#concept #crypto

XRP — Native token of the [[Ripple]] payment network. Designed for cross-border payments and institutional settlement. Won partial victory in SEC lawsuit (July 2023) — court ruled secondary market sales to retail were not securities, but institutional sales were. Settlement reached in 2025.
