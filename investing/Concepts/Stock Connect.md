# Stock Connect

[[Stock Connect]] is the cross-border trading scheme linking the [[Shanghai]] and [[Shenzhen]] exchanges with [[Hong Kong]], letting mainland investors buy eligible HK-listed shares (southbound) and international investors buy eligible mainland A-shares (northbound) through their home exchange. It is the main channel through which mainland capital reaches Hong Kong-listed Chinese equities.

---

## Why it matters

For A+H dual-listed companies (e.g., [[Lingyi iTech]], [[CATL]], [[Luxshare Precision]]), southbound flow is a key driver of the H-share price and of whether the persistent A-H discount widens or narrows. Strong southbound demand tends to compress the discount; weak demand lets it persist. Eligibility for southbound trading is therefore a material catalyst for newly HK-listed mainland names.
