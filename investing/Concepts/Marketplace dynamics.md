**Marketplace dynamics** — Two-sided platforms connecting buyers and sellers. Liquidity, chicken-and-egg, take rates.

---

## Marketplace types

| Type | Supply | Demand | Example |
|------|--------|--------|---------|
| **E-commerce** | Merchants | Shoppers | Amazon 3P |
| **Services** | Providers | Customers | Uber |
| **Accommodations** | Hosts | Guests | Airbnb |
| **Labor** | Workers | Employers | Upwork |
| **Classified** | Sellers | Buyers | eBay |

---

## Key metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **GMV** | Gross merchandise value |
| **Take rate** | Revenue / GMV |
| **Liquidity** | Transaction success rate |
| **Time to match** | Speed of transaction |
| **Repeat rate** | Buyer/seller retention |

---

## Chicken-and-egg problem

| Stage | Strategy |
|-------|----------|
| **Launch** | Seed one side (usually supply) |
| **Growth** | Subsidize harder side |
| **Scale** | Balance investment |
| **Maturity** | Monetize both sides |

## Liquidity dynamics

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Supply density | Better matching |
| Demand concentration | Pricing power |
| Multi-homing | Reduces stickiness |
| Disintermediation | Leakage risk |

---

## Competitive moats

| Moat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Supply lock-in | Exclusive sellers |
| Demand aggregation | Traffic source |
| Trust/reviews | Information advantage |
| Local density | Geographic network effects |

---

## Disintermediation risk

| Signal | Response |
|--------|----------|
| Direct bookings | Add value, reduce friction |
| Contact exchange | Delay until transaction |
| Recurring relationships | Managed marketplace |

---

## Investment signals

| Signal | Interpretation |
|--------|----------------|
| Take rate rising | Platform power |
| GMV growing faster than revenue | Investing in growth |
| Supply churn high | Economics pressure |
| Multi-homing increasing | Moat erosion |
