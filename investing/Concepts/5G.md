**5G** — Fifth-generation wireless. 10-100x faster than 4G. Enables IoT, autonomous vehicles, edge computing.

---

## 5G specifications

| Spec | Capability |
|------|------------|
| Speed | Up to 10 Gbps |
| Latency | <10ms (vs. 50ms 4G) |
| Density | 1M devices/km² |
| Bands | Low, mid, mmWave |

---

## Spectrum bands

| Band | Speed | Range | Use |
|------|-------|-------|-----|
| Low (<1GHz) | Moderate | Wide | Rural coverage |
| Mid (1-6GHz) | Good | Medium | Urban coverage |
| mmWave (>24GHz) | Fastest | Short | Dense urban |
