**Lei das Bets** — Law 14,790/2023, [[Brazil]]'s regulatory framework for fixed-odds sports betting, signed December 2023, market launched January 1, 2025. Created SPA (Secretaria de Premios e Apostas) as regulator. 12% GGR tax (proposed increases to 18% blocked by Congress). 79 licensed operators, mandatory .bet.br domain.
