**Emerging markets** — Developing economies with growth potential. Higher risk/return. [[China]], India, Brazil, EM [[Asia]].

---

## EM definition

| Criteria | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| GDP per capita | Lower than developed |
| Market access | Improving liquidity |
| Growth potential | Higher than DM |
| Risk profile | Political, currency, liquidity |

## EM vs DM characteristics

| Factor | EM | DM |
|--------|-----|-----|
| Growth | Higher | Lower |
| Volatility | Higher | Lower |
| Valuation | Cheaper | Premium |
| Currency risk | Higher | Lower |
| Governance | Variable | Stronger |
