#concept #finance #credit

**Middle-market** — Companies with $10M-$1B EBITDA, too large for small business loans, too small for investment-grade bond markets. Primary borrowers from [[direct lender]]s.

---

## Characteristics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| EBITDA | $10M - $1B |
| Revenue | $50M - $5B |
| Employees | 100 - 10,000 |

Often PE-backed, family-owned, or founder-led.

---

## Financing gap

Banks retreated post-[[GFC]] due to:
- [[Basel III]] capital requirements
- Regulatory scrutiny
- Preference for larger, liquid deals

[[Private credit]] filled the gap.
