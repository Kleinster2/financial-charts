#concept #china #equities #soe #stub

**中特估** (zhōngtègū, "China special valuation system" / 中国特色估值体系) — a policy-backed re-rating theme, prominent since 2022–2023, holding that [[China|Chinese]] state-owned enterprises trade at unjustifiably low valuations and should be re-rated upward. Championed by the CSRC and supported by "national team" buying, it favors high-dividend, cash-generative SOEs — banks, energy majors, and the telecoms. The driver behind the 2021–2026 re-rating of [[China Unicom]], [[China Mobile]], and [[China Telecom]], whose tightening cluster co-movement tracks the theme.
