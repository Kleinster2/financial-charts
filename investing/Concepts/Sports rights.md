# Sports rights

The battle for live sports broadcasting rights — the last "must-have" linear content driving media valuations and strategy.

---

## Why sports matter

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Live viewing** | No time-shifting (unlike scripted) |
| **Ad rates** | 2-3x other programming |
| **Bundling power** | Forces cable/streaming retention |
| **Cultural relevance** | Shared experience |
| **Demographics** | Male 18-49 (valuable) |
