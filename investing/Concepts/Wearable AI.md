**Wearable AI** — AI-powered devices worn on the body: smart glasses, pendants, earbuds. Emerging category competing for "always-on AI assistant" use case.

---

## Why it matters

Wearables are the next AI interface battleground:
- Always available (unlike phones)
- Context-aware (audio, visual)
- Lower friction than pulling out a device

## Meta strategy

1. Start with audio/camera glasses (Ray-Ban Meta)
2. Graduate users to full AR (Orion)
3. Acquired Limitless (wearables startup)
4. Competing with 70+ Chinese entrants

---

## Amazon Bee

Acquired Sep 2024. $50 always-listening wearable:
- Records/transcribes daily activities
- Week-long battery life
- No display or camera
- Privacy: audio processed real-time, deleted after

Part of broader Alexa+ strategy.
