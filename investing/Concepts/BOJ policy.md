**BOJ policy** — Bank of Japan monetary policy. Yield curve control ending. Rate normalization after decades of ZIRP.

---

## Historical context

| Era | Policy |
|-----|--------|
| 1990s-2000s | Zero interest rate policy (ZIRP) |
| 2013 | Abenomics, massive QE |
| 2016 | Negative rates, YCC introduced |
| 2022-23 | YCC bands widened |
| 2024 | YCC ended, rates positive |

---

## Yield curve control (YCC)

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Target | 10-year JGB yield ~0% |
| Mechanism | BOJ buys unlimited bonds |
| Band | Initially ±0.1%, widened to ±1% |
| End | March 2024 |

BOJ owned 50%+ of JGB market.

---

## Rate normalization path

| Date | Action |
|------|--------|
| Mar 2024 | End negative rates, YCC |
| 2024-25 | Gradual rate hikes |
| Target | Neutral rate ~1-2% |

First rate hikes since 2007.
