**Business cycle** — Economic expansion and contraction phases. Drives sector rotation, valuations, earnings.

---

## Cycle phases

| Phase | Characteristics |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Early recovery** | GDP bottoming, rates low, unemployment high |
| **Expansion** | GDP growing, hiring increasing, confidence rising |
| **Late cycle** | GDP slowing, inflation rising, rates peaking |
| **Recession** | GDP contracting, layoffs, fear |

---

## Phase indicators

| Phase | Leading signals |
|-------|-----------------|
| Early recovery | Yield curve steepening, PMI bottoming |
| Expansion | Credit growth, capex rising |
| Late cycle | Yield curve flat/inverted, wage inflation |
| Recession | PMI < 50, jobless claims rising |

---

## Sector rotation

| Phase | Outperformers |
|-------|---------------|
| Early recovery | Financials, consumer discretionary |
| Expansion | Tech, industrials |
| Late cycle | Energy, materials |
| Recession | Utilities, healthcare, staples |

---

## Asset class performance

| Phase | Favored assets |
|-------|----------------|
| Early recovery | Stocks, high yield bonds |
| Expansion | Stocks, commodities |
| Late cycle | Commodities, cash |
| Recession | Treasuries, gold |

---

## Key indicators

| Indicator | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **GDP growth** | Output measure |
| **PMI** | Manufacturing/services health |
| **Unemployment** | Labor market |
| **Yield curve** | Rate expectations |
| **LEI** | Composite leading indicator |
| **Consumer confidence** | Sentiment |

---

## Cycle length (historical)

| Metric | US average |
|--------|------------|
| Expansion length | ~6 years |
| Recession length | ~11 months |
| Full cycle | ~7 years |

Post-2008 expansion was unusually long (11 years).

---

## Investment implications

| Action | Timing |
|--------|--------|
| Add cyclicals | Early recovery |
| Full equity | Expansion |
| Reduce risk | Late cycle |
| Add defensives | Pre-recession |
| Buy dip | Mid-recession |
