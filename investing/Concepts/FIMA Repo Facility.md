# FIMA Repo Facility

Standing [[Federal Reserve]] facility allowing foreign central banks and international monetary authorities to temporarily exchange their [[Treasuries]] held in custody at the [[Federal Reserve|NY Fed]] for US dollars. Launched as a temporary facility in March 2020 during the [[March 2020 liquidity crisis]], made permanent in July 2021.

Purpose: give foreign CBs an alternative to selling Treasuries on the open market during stress events. Without it, forced Treasury sales by foreign CBs can widen the [[Basis trade]] spread and trigger self-reinforcing selloffs. The facility's existence is meant to break this transmission channel.
