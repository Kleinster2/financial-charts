#stub

Rare earth elements (REE) are a group of 17 metallic elements critical to permanent magnets (NdFeB for EVs and wind turbines), electronics, defense systems, and catalysts. [[China]] controls ~60% of mining and ~90% of processing. Extraction via sulfuric acid roasting requires 3.4-4.0 tonnes H2SO4 per tonne of rare earth oxide (REO). Global recycling rate remains below 5%.
