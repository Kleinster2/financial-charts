#concept #investing #sustainable #governance

**ESG** — Environmental, Social, Governance factors. Sustainable investing framework. $30T+ AUM. Political controversy.

---

## ESG components

| Factor | Examples |
|--------|----------|
| **Environmental** | Carbon emissions, climate risk, waste |
| **Social** | Labor practices, diversity, community |
| **Governance** | Board structure, executive pay, ethics |

---

## ESG investing approaches

| Approach | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Exclusion** | Avoid "sin stocks" (tobacco, weapons) |
| **Integration** | Consider ESG in analysis |
| **Best-in-class** | Top ESG within sectors |
| **Thematic** | Clean energy, water |
| **Impact** | Measurable positive outcomes |
| **Engagement** | Active ownership, voting |
