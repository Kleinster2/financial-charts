# Department Store Decline

**Multi-decade structural decline** of traditional department stores due to e-commerce, changing consumer behavior, and real estate cost pressure.

---

## The dynamics

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| E-commerce | Direct brand sales bypass department stores |
| Fast fashion | Zara, H&M capture mid-market |
| Off-price | TJX, Ross take value shoppers |
| Real estate | Large footprints become cost burdens |
| Brand control | Luxury brands prefer owned retail |

---

## Timeline

| Era | Trend |
|-----|-------|
| 1990s | Peak department store era |
| 2000s | E-commerce pressure begins |
| 2010s | Anchor store closures accelerate |
| 2020 | COVID accelerates closures |
| 2024-26 | [[Saks bankruptcy]], consolidation failures |
