# Robotics

Physical AI — robots that interact with the real world. CES 2026 declared "year of the humanoid."

---

## Why robotics matters

| Factor | Significance |
|--------|--------------|
| Labor shortage | Aging demographics, wage inflation |
| AI advancement | LLMs + vision + manipulation converging |
| Cost curves | Hardware costs declining |
| Validation | First production deployments (2026) |
