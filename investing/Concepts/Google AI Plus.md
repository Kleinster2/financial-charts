#concept #subscription #alphabet #ai

Google AI Plus — [[Google]]'s entry-tier consumer AI subscription, $7.99/month in the US (launched January 27, 2026). Below [[Google AI Pro]] ($19.99/mo) and [[Google AI Ultra]] ($249.99/mo). Provides [[Gemini]] access at lower usage limits.

Does not include [[Google Health Premium]] — that bundle starts at AI Pro.
