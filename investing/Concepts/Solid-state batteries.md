# Solid-State Batteries

Next-generation battery technology replacing liquid electrolyte with solid. Finally reaching production in 2026.

---

## Why solid-state matters

| Benefit | vs. Li-ion | Impact |
|---------|------------|--------|
| **Energy density** | 2x higher (400+ Wh/kg) | Double range or half weight |
| **Charging speed** | 5-10 min full charge | Eliminates range anxiety |
| **Safety** | No thermal runaway | No fires, simpler cooling |
| **Cycle life** | 100,000+ cycles | 10x longer lifespan |
| **Temperature** | Wider operating range | Works in extreme cold/heat |

## Technology approaches

| Electrolyte type | Companies | Pros | Cons |
|------------------|-----------|------|------|
| **Oxide ceramic** | [[ProLogium]], QuantumScape | Stability | Brittle |
| **Sulfide** | Toyota, Samsung SDI | Conductivity | Air-sensitive |
| **Polymer** | Solid Power | Flexible | Lower performance |
