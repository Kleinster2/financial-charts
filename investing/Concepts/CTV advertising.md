# CTV advertising

Connected TV (CTV) advertising — the fastest-growing segment of digital advertising. Programmatic buying of streaming TV inventory.

---

## Market size

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| US CTV ad spend (2024) | ~$30B |
| Growth rate | 15-20% annually |
| US CTV ad spend (2027E) | ~$45B |
| % of total TV ad spend | ~35% (and rising) |

---

## What is CTV

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **CTV** | Connected TV — any TV connected to internet |
| **OTT** | Over-the-top — streaming content delivery |
| **AVOD** | Ad-supported video on demand |
| **FAST** | Free ad-supported streaming TV |

## Why CTV is growing

| Driver | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Cord-cutting** | Linear TV audience shrinking |
| **Streaming ad tiers** | Netflix, Disney+ added ads |
| **Measurement** | Better targeting than linear |
| **Premium content** | Brand-safe environments |
| **Reach** | 85%+ of US households |

---

## Programmatic vs direct

| Method | Description | Trend |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| **Direct** | Publisher sells to advertiser | Declining share |
| **Programmatic** | Automated via DSP/SSP | Growing share |

**Shift:** CTV moving from direct sales to programmatic (like display did).

---

## Key metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **CPM** | Cost per thousand impressions |
| **Completion rate** | % of ads watched fully |
| **Reach** | Unique households/viewers |
| **Frequency** | How often same viewer sees ad |

**CTV CPMs:** $20-40+ (premium vs display $2-5).
