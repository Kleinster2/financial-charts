# Laffer curve

Economic theory positing that tax revenue is a non-linear function of tax rates — beyond some optimal rate, higher taxes reduce revenue by discouraging economic activity or incentivizing avoidance/emigration. Named after economist Arthur Laffer. Central to debates around [[Wealth tax]] policy and [[Capital flight]].
