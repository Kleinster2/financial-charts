# Smart cities

#concept #infrastructure #iot #ai #urbanization

**Smart cities** — Urban areas using IoT sensors, AI, and data analytics to optimize infrastructure, services, and quality of life. $586B market (2026) growing to $1.4T+ by 2030. Key themes: connectivity, surveillance, sustainability, and massive capex.

## Technology stack

### Layer 1: Sensors & IoT

| Application | Function |
|-------------|----------|
| Traffic sensors | Congestion monitoring, signal optimization |
| Environmental | Air quality, noise, weather |
| Waste management | Fill-level detection, route optimization |
| Water | Leak detection, quality monitoring |
| Energy | Smart meters, grid balancing |
| Parking | Occupancy detection |

**Scale:** 122M+ IoT connections in urban lighting networks alone.

### Layer 2: Connectivity

| Technology | Role |
|------------|------|
| 5G | High-bandwidth, low-latency backbone |
| LoRaWAN | Low-power wide-area for sensors |
| Fiber | Backhaul infrastructure |
| Edge computing | Local processing, reduced latency |

**Adoption:** 57% of cities implementing 5G connectivity (2025).

### Layer 3: AI & Analytics

| Application | Function |
|-------------|----------|
| Traffic prediction | Route optimization, congestion forecasting |
| Computer vision | Surveillance, incident detection |
| Predictive maintenance | Infrastructure monitoring |
| Energy optimization | Demand forecasting, grid balancing |

**Adoption:** 54% of cities investing in AI integration.

### Layer 4: Platforms

| Function | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| City operating systems | Unified dashboards, cross-domain integration |
| Digital twins | Virtual city models for simulation |
| Citizen apps | Service delivery, engagement |

## Surveillance angle

### The tradeoff

| Benefit | Risk |
|---------|------|
| Public safety | Privacy erosion |
| Traffic optimization | Mass surveillance |
| Crime reduction | Social control |
| Efficiency | Data security |

### China model vs Western model

| Aspect | China | Western |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Surveillance | Extensive, integrated | Limited, regulated |
| Data ownership | State | Mixed (privacy laws) |
| Social credit | Yes | No |
| Adoption speed | Fast | Slower (democratic process) |
