# Seven sons of national defence

Seven [[China|Chinese]] universities designated as national-defence institutions with deep collaborative ties to the [[People's Liberation Army]]: Harbin Institute of Technology, Beihang University, Beijing Institute of Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Northwestern Polytechnical University, and Harbin Engineering University. They are primary sources of Chinese aerospace and defence engineering talent. Their alumni populate both state institutions like the [[China Academy of Space Technology]] and commercial [[Military-civil fusion|MCF]] firms like [[Earth Eye Co]] — one of the mechanisms by which Chinese commercial-space companies end up with de facto military capability. All seven are on the US Entity List and face US visa restrictions for students in sensitive disciplines.
