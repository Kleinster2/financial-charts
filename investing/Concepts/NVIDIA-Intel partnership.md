# NVIDIA-Intel partnership

**January 2026** — NVIDIA and Intel announce strategic partnership + $5B equity investment. Potential lifeline for Intel turnaround.

---

## The deal

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Equity investment** | $5B in Intel common stock |
| **Share price** | $23.28/share |
| **Shares acquired** | ~217.4 million |
| **Status** | FTC approved |
| **Announced** | September 2025 (closed Jan 2026) |

---

## Strategic partnership

**Joint development across multiple generations:**
- Custom data center products
- PC products
- Focus on connecting NVIDIA + Intel architectures via **NVLink**
- Hyperscale, enterprise, and consumer markets

**Key tech:** NVLink integration — NVIDIA's high-speed interconnect on Intel silicon.

---

## Why it matters

### For Intel

| Benefit | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Capital injection** | $5B cash at a time of financial stress |
| **Validation** | Jensen backing Intel's turnaround |
| **Foundry signal** | Could mean NVIDIA considering Intel Foundry |
| **Stock price support** | Major buyer at $23.28 |
| **Technology partnership** | NVLink expertise |

### For NVIDIA

| Benefit | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Supply diversification** | Reduce TSMC dependency |
| **US manufacturing** | CHIPS Act alignment |
| **Intel packaging (EMIB)** | Alternative to TSMC CoWoS |
| **Geopolitical hedge** | Non-Taiwan supply chain |
| **Strategic influence** | Ownership stake in competitor |

---

## Speculation: What's NVIDIA buying?

**Possible scenarios:**
1. **Foundry capacity** — Intel 18A for future NVIDIA products
2. **Packaging** — EMIB as CoWoS alternative
3. **x86 integration** — NVIDIA AI + Intel CPUs
4. **Defensive** — Prevent Intel collapse / acquisition by rival
5. **All of the above** — Multi-pronged partnership
