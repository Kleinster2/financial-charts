#concept #supplychains #geopolitics

China plus one — Supply chain diversification strategy where companies maintain [[China]] operations but add at least one alternative manufacturing base. Primary beneficiaries: [[India]], [[Vietnam]], [[Mexico]], [[Indonesia]].

## Quick stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Type | Supply chain strategy |
