**Metropolitan Statistical Area** — an [[OMB]]-defined geographic unit built around a core urban area of 50,000+ population, plus surrounding counties where 25%+ of workers commute to the core. The standard unit for federal economic reporting ([[BLS]] employment, Census demographics, HUD housing data). ~384 MSAs in the US. Multiple adjacent MSAs with 15%+ commuting interchange form a [[Combined Statistical Area]].
