# S&P 100

A subset of the [[S&P 500]] comprising 100 of the largest U.S. companies with listed options. Ticker: OEX. The original [[VIX]] (1993) was based on S&P 100 options before switching to S&P 500 in the 2003 methodology overhaul (the original S&P 100-based calculation continues as VXO).
