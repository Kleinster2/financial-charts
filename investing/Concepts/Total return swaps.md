# Total return swaps

**Total return swaps** — Derivative contract where one party pays the total return (price appreciation + dividends/coupons) of a reference asset to the counterparty, in exchange for a fixed or floating rate. Allows synthetic long or short exposure without owning the underlying asset. Partially visible to [[prime brokerage]] desks (the structuring desk sees them but they're messier to aggregate than direct short positions).
