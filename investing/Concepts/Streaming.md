#concept #media #streaming

**Streaming** — Direct-to-consumer video delivery over the internet, bypassing traditional cable/satellite distribution. The dominant secular trend in media since ~2015, accelerated by COVID. Key players: [[Netflix]], [[Disney]] (Disney+), [[Amazon]] (Prime Video), [[Warner Bros Discovery]] (Max), [[Comcast]] (Peacock), [[Apple]] (Apple TV+), [[Paramount Global]] (Paramount+).
