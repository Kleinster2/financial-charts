## Key concepts

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **MFN** | Most-favored nation (WTO baseline, all equal) |
| **Preferential** | Better than MFN (FTA benefit) |
| **Rules of origin** | Content required for preference |
| **Cumulation** | Count partner inputs as own |
| **Negative list** | Everything open unless listed |
| **Positive list** | Only listed sectors open |
| **TRQ** | Tariff-rate quota (low rate up to limit) |
| **Mixed agreement** | EU + national competence (all parliaments) |
| **Provisional** | Trade parts apply, investment waits |
