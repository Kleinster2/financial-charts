#concept #infrastructure #connectivity

**Submarine Cables** — 99% of intercontinental data travels via undersea cables. Hyperscalers now dominate.

---

## Why submarine cables matter

The internet's physical backbone:

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Global cables | 600+ (532 operational) |
| Data share | 99% of intercontinental |
| Hyperscaler owned | 59 cables (up from 20 in 2017) |
| Market growth | 13.5% CAGR (single-owner) |

---

## The hyperscaler takeover

**From buying to owning:**

| Year | Hyperscaler-owned cables |
|------|--------------------------|
| 2017 | 20 |
| 2024 | 59 |
| Trend | Vertical integration |

Google, Meta, Microsoft, Amazon now own infrastructure.
