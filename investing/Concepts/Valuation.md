#concept #investing #fundamentals #valuation

**Valuation** — the set of methods for estimating what a business or security is worth: discounted cash flow (intrinsic value from projected [[Free cash flow]]), relative/multiples (pricing against peers), and asset-based approaches. The hub for the vault's valuation concepts.
