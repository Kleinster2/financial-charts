#concept #energy #oil #shale

**Bakken** is a US shale [[Oil|oil]] play centered in [[North Dakota]] and Montana. It was one of the first large-scale proof points for horizontal drilling and hydraulic fracturing in oil-weighted shale, with [[Continental Resources]] and [[Harold Hamm]] central to its development.
