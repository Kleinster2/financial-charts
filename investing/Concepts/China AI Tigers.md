# [[China]] AI Tigers

[[China]]'s AI ecosystem is often described using animal metaphors. The most investment-relevant grouping is the Six Tigers: $1B+ AI model startups challenging both US labs and domestic tech giants.

---

## Six Tigers (AI model startups)

The $1B+ valuation club of independent AI model labs:

| Company | Chinese | Focus | Valuation | Key model |
|---------|---------|-------|-----------|-----------|
| [[Zhipu]] | 智谱AI | Enterprise AI | $3B+ | [[GLM]]-4 |
| [[Moonshot AI]] | 月之暗面 | [[Consumer AI]] | $4.8B | [[Kimi]] K2.5 |
| [[MiniMax]] | 小冰/MiniMax | Multimodal / long context | HK-listed 0100.HK; proposed STAR RMB shares | [[MiniMax M3]] |
| Baichuan Intelligence | 百川智能 | Open weights | $2B+ | Baichuan |
| [[StepFun]] | 阶跃星辰 | Video generation | $2B+ | Step |
| [[01.AI]] | 零一万物 | Bilingual AI | $1B+ | [[Yi]] |

Common traits:
- Founded 2023 or later
- Raised $500M+ each
- Compete with [[GPT]]-4 on Chinese benchmarks
- Mix of consumer and enterprise focus

## Investment implications

The cohort is no longer cleanly private. [[MiniMax]] and [[Zhipu]] moved first through [[Hong Kong]] listings, and MiniMax is now testing whether the same model-lab story can also tap mainland RMB-share capital through the [[STAR Market]].

Indirect exposure:
- [[Alibaba]] — investor in [[Moonshot AI]], competes via [[Qwen]]
- [[Tencent]] — investor / ecosystem exposure to [[MiniMax]]
- [[MiniMax securities note]] — direct listed exposure via 0100.HK
- [[Microsoft]] — Azure offers some [[China]] models

Thesis implications:
- [[China]] AI ecosystem deeper than just [[Baidu]] and [[ByteDance]]
- Competition drives rapid iteration
- Valuations suggest AI premium persists
- Watch for HK/[[China]] IPOs
- Public-market validation is becoming part of the product narrative: model releases, token-volume claims, and exchange access now reprice together

## Synthesis

The Six Tigers lens is most useful when it separates capability competition from investability. The model story is broad: [[Moonshot AI]] owns long-context consumer momentum, [[MiniMax]] is pushing multimodal / long-context public-market validation, [[Zhipu AI]] leans enterprise, and [[StepFun]] / Baichuan / [[01.AI]] fill out the open-weight and video ecosystem. The capital-market story is narrower: [[MiniMax]] is now the cleanest listed proxy, and its proposed [[STAR Market]] path turns the cohort from a private-VC category into a public-market watchlist.
