#stub

Industrial commodities are bulk chemicals and materials produced at scale for use as inputs across manufacturing, agriculture, mining, and energy sectors. Includes [[Sulfuric Acid]] (most-produced chemical by volume), caustic soda, chlorine, ammonia, ethylene, and propylene. Demand tracks industrial production and is a macro indicator.
