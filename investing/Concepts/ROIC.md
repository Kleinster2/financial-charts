**ROIC** — Return on invested capital. Measures how efficiently a company turns capital into profits. Quality signal.

---

## ROIC calculation

| Formula | Components |
|---------|------------|
| **ROIC** | NOPAT / Invested Capital |
| **NOPAT** | EBIT × (1 - Tax Rate) |
| **Invested Capital** | Equity + Debt - Cash |

Alternative: Total Assets - Non-interest-bearing Liabilities

---

## ROIC interpretation

| ROIC | Interpretation |
|------|----------------|
| >20% | Excellent, wide moat |
| 15-20% | Good, competitive advantage |
| 10-15% | Average |
| <10% | Below cost of capital likely |
| <WACC | Value destruction |

---

## ROIC vs. other metrics

| Metric | Limitation |
|--------|------------|
| ROE | Ignores debt |
| ROA | Includes non-operating assets |
| ROCE | Similar to ROIC |
| ROIC | Capital structure neutral |

ROIC is capital structure agnostic.

---

## ROIC decomposition

| Component | Driver |
|-----------|--------|
| **Margins** | NOPAT / Revenue |
| **Turns** | Revenue / Invested Capital |

**ROIC = Margin × Capital Turns**

---

## Business model implications

| Model | ROIC driver |
|-------|-------------|
| Luxury | High margins |
| Retail | High turns |
| SaaS | Low capital, high margins |
| Commodity | Low both |

---

## ROIC decay

| Signal | Concern |
|--------|---------|
| ROIC declining | Moat erosion |
| Competition | Margin pressure |
| Reinvestment | Diminishing opportunities |
| Maturity | Growth slowing |

---

## Investment implications

| Scenario | Interpretation |
|----------|----------------|
| High ROIC + reinvestment | Compounding machine |
| High ROIC + no reinvestment | Cash cow, return capital |
| Low ROIC + capex | Value destruction |
| Rising ROIC | Improving business |
