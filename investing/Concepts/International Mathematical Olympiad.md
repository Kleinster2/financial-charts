#concept #benchmark #math

**International Mathematical Olympiad** — the premier international mathematics competition for pre-university students, held annually since 1959. Used by [[OpenAI]] as a primary benchmark for reasoning model progress: [[Jakub Pachocki]] tracked IMO problem-solving as a northstar for the o1 through o3 reasoning model roadmap. The advantage of IMO problems as a benchmark: definitive verifiability (clear whether solved) with arbitrary difficulty.
