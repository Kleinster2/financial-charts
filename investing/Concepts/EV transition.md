---

## Transition drivers

| Driver | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Regulation | ICE bans, emissions targets |
| Battery costs | Down 90% since 2010 |
| Model availability | 500+ EV models globally |
| TCO parity | EVs cheaper to operate |
| [[Consumer]] preference | Growing acceptance |

---

## Transition barriers

| Barrier | Status |
|---------|--------|
| Charging infrastructure | Improving but gaps |
| Range anxiety | Less concern with newer models |
| Upfront cost | Premium narrowing |
| Grid capacity | Investment needed |
| Battery materials | Supply chain constraints |
