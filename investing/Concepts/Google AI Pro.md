#concept #subscription #alphabet #ai

Google AI Pro — [[Google]]'s mid-tier consumer AI subscription, $19.99/month in the US. Includes [[Gemini]] access, NotebookLM Plus, Google Home Premium, and as of May 2026 includes [[Google Health Premium]] at no extra cost. Sits between [[Google AI Plus]] ($7.99/mo) and [[Google AI Ultra]] ($249.99/mo).

---

## What's included

| Feature | AI Pro |
|---------|--------|

| NotebookLM Plus | Yes |
| Google Home Premium | Yes |
| [[Google Health Premium]] | Yes (bundled, normally $99/yr standalone) |
| Cloud storage | Standard |

---

## Strategic role

AI Pro is the consolidation tier — the price point at which an existing Premium-only subscriber ($9.99/mo) sees a +$10/mo step that includes Premium for free, plus the rest of Google's AI subscription stack. The May 2026 [[Fitbit Air launch May 2026|Fitbit Air launch]] formalizes the device → Premium → AI Pro funnel.
