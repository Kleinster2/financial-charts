# Bitcoin ETF

Exchange-traded product providing spot [[Bitcoin]] price exposure to traditional brokerage-account holders. U.S. spot Bitcoin ETFs launched January 11, 2024 following SEC approval, opening a structural distribution channel for crypto into retirement accounts, RIAs, and institutional allocators previously unable to custody crypto directly.

---

## Approved U.S. spot Bitcoin ETFs (Jan 2024 launch cohort)

Notable issuers: BlackRock (IBIT), Fidelity (FBTC), [[Grayscale]] (GBTC — converted from trust), Ark/21Shares (ARKB), Bitwise (BITB), VanEck (HODL), Invesco/Galaxy (BTCO), Franklin (EZBC), WisdomTree (BTCW), Valkyrie (BRRR).

---

## Why it matters

- First crypto asset with mainstream ETF wrapper in U.S.
- Established regulatory template that spot Ethereum ETPs followed in 2024, and that new 2026 filings (including [[Grayscale]]'s GTAO filing for [[Bittensor]]) are attempting to extend to altcoins.
- Institutional AUM ramped to tens of billions within months of launch.
