#concept #esg #governance

**B Corp** — Certification from B Lab for companies meeting high standards of social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency. Over 8,000 certified companies across 90+ countries.

Certification requires scoring 80+ on the B Impact Assessment (covering governance, workers, community, environment, customers) and amending corporate governing documents to consider all stakeholders.
