#concept #subscription #alphabet #ai

Google AI Ultra — [[Google]]'s premium AI subscription family above [[Google AI Pro]]. Google I/O 2026 changed the ladder: a new $100/month AI Ultra tier launched for heavy [[Gemini]] / [[Google Antigravity]] / creator users, while the previous top Ultra tier was cut from $250/month to $200/month. The $100 tier includes 5x the Pro usage limit in the Gemini app and [[Google Antigravity]], Gemini 3.5 Flash integration, priority Antigravity access, 20TB storage, and [[YouTube]] Premium Individual. The $200 tier keeps the highest limits, including 20x Pro usage and expanded [[Project Genie]] access. Many updates start rolling out from May 19, 2026 through the following weeks.

---

## I/O 2026 subscription reset

The May 2026 subscription change is not just a price cut. Google is turning AI Ultra into the paid control plane for the most compute-intensive Gemini surfaces: [[Gemini Spark]], [[Gemini Omni]], [[Google Flow]], [[Google Antigravity]], [[Project Genie]], and high-usage Gemini app workflows. The subscription bundle therefore mixes three different economics: model/agent compute limits, storage, and non-AI benefits like YouTube Premium.

| Tier | Price | Main I/O 2026 changes |
|------|-------|------------------------|
| [[Google AI Pro]] | $19.99/mo in the U.S. | AI Inbox expansion, YouTube Premium Lite in select countries, access to [[Gemini Omni]] / [[Google Pics]] / [[Google Workspace]] voice features depending on rollout |
| Google AI Ultra | $100/mo | New tier; 5x Pro usage in Gemini app and Antigravity, 20TB storage, YouTube Premium, priority Antigravity |
| Google AI Ultra top tier | $200/mo | Former $250 tier repriced; 20x Pro usage in Gemini app and Antigravity; Project Genie expansion |

[[Gemini Spark]] is the subscription hinge. Google says Spark will roll out first to trusted testers during I/O week, then as a U.S. beta for AI Ultra subscribers the following week. If Spark becomes a repeated-use personal agent, Ultra is less a content bundle and more a compute-retainer product.

*Sources: [Google AI subscription updates from Google I/O 2026](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/google-one/google-ai-subscriptions/), [Pichai I/O 2026 keynote transcript](https://blog.google/intl/en-nz/company-news/sundar-pichai-io-2026/), May 19 2026.*
