#concept #subscription #alphabet #ai

Google AI Ultra — [[Google]]'s top consumer AI subscription, $249.99/month in the US. Includes [[Gemini]] full access (highest usage limits), 30 TB cross-product storage (Photos / Drive / Gmail), YouTube Premium Individual, Google Home Premium Advanced (24/7 video history), and as of May 2026 [[Google Health Premium]] at no extra cost. Sits above [[Google AI Pro]] ($19.99/mo).
