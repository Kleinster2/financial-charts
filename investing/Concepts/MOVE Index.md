# MOVE Index

The bond market's equivalent of the [[VIX]] — measures implied volatility from U.S. Treasury options. Created by Harley Bassman at [[Merrill Lynch]] in 1994, now owned by [[ICE]] (acquired from [[Bank of America]] in 2019). Weighted across four maturities: 2Y (20%), 5Y (20%), 10Y (40%, double-weighted), 30Y (20%). Historical data back to 1988. Often spikes before VIX — the bond market sends earlier warnings. All-time peak: 264.6 on October 10, 2008.
