## WBD rejects Paramount $30 bid, opens 7-day "best and final" window

[[Warner Bros Discovery]] board rejected [[Paramount Global]]'s $30/share hostile bid but gave a **7-day window (until Feb 23)** to submit a "best and final offer" — first formal engagement after rejecting six prior offers. Paramount verbally floated $31/share. WBD secured a waiver from [[Netflix]] to negotiate. Shareholder vote on Netflix deal set for March 20. Activist [[Ancora Holdings]] (~$200M stake) pressuring board to engage; [[Pentwater Capital]] (~50M shares) backs Paramount, CEO eyed for WBD board seat. Key unresolved: $1.5B junior lien fee, debt financing contingencies, certainty of [[Larry Ellison]]'s $40B equity guarantee. Market: PSKY +6%, WBD +2.3%, NFLX -1.4%.
