### China 15th Five-Year Plan (NPC, Mar 5)

- [[China]] 15th Five-Year Plan formally released at NPC. AI is now the "operating system for the economy" — embedded across manufacturing, healthcare, education, logistics. R&D target >3.2% of GDP (record). Core tech: semis, AI, robotics, biotech, quantum, 6G. Non-fossil energy to 25% by 2030. Significantly more protectionist than 14th FYP — explicitly designed to insulate from Western supply chains. Formal end of unified global tech market.

Deploying 20,000 stations by end of 2026 (4,239 done in Jan-Feb). Stations open to non-BYD vehicles. Infrastructure moat play — selling the car AND the gas station.
