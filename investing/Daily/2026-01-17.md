## Headlines

### Fed Chair Powell under criminal investigation

DOJ opened criminal investigation of [[Jerome Powell]] over $2.5B Fed headquarters renovation.

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Trigger | June 2025 Senate testimony on renovation costs |
| Cost overrun | $600M over original budget |
| Reason | Design changes, higher costs, asbestos |
| Lead prosecutor | Jeanine Pirro (Trump appointee) |

**Powell's response (video statement Jan 11):** "The threat of criminal charges is a consequence of the Federal Reserve setting interest rates based on our best assessment of what will serve the public, rather than following the preferences of the President."

**Bessent reaction:** Treasury Secretary "unhappy" with decision — concerned about market impact.

**Political response:** Sen. Thom Tillis (R-NC) says he'll oppose any Fed nominees until legal matter resolved.

**Implications:**
- Powell term as Chair expires May 2026
- Governor term runs to January 2028 — may stay as governor
- Hassett apparently NOT getting Fed Chair role per Trump's Friday comments

### BYD overtakes Tesla as global EV leader

[[BYD]] outsold [[Tesla]] in pure EVs for first time in 2025.

| Metric | BYD | Tesla |
|--------|-----|-------|
| 2025 EV sales | 2.26M | 1.64M |
| YoY change | +28% | **-8.6%** |
| Gap | — | 620K units behind |

**Tesla decline drivers:**
- Q4 2025 drop after $7,500 EV tax credit eliminated
- European sales -28% (Musk political backlash)
- China competition intensifying

**Ford CEO:** "There's no real competition from Tesla, GM or Ford with what we've seen from China. It is completely dominating the EV landscape globally."

**BYD wins despite:** Not selling in US (Tesla's largest market).

### NVIDIA H200 China sales approved with 25% tariff
