### Decentralized AI concept build-out

Created six Concepts/ notes to back-fill the vocabulary used by [[Decentralized AI landscape]]:

- [[Decentralized training]] — the harder, larger-spend sibling to inference networks; coordinates gradient updates across untrusted GPU contributors.
- [[Open-weight models]] — publicly downloadable foundation model weights ([[Meta|Llama]], [[Mistral]], [[DeepSeek]], [[Qwen]], Nous Hermes), distinct from strict open-source.
- [[Tokenomics]] — supply/distribution/demand design choices that determine who captures value as a crypto network grows.
- [[Bonding curve]] — smart-contract pricing mechanism that prices a token as a continuous function of supply, providing instant on-chain liquidity.
- [[Censorship resistance]] — the [[Bitcoin]]-origin design property now backing the crypto-libertarian case for token-incentivized AI infrastructure.
- [[AI agent tokens]] — the "one agent, one token" primitive pioneered by [[Virtuals Protocol]] in late 2024 and cloned across the 2024-2025 cycle.
