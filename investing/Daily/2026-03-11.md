## Markets

### JPMorgan marks down private credit software loan collateral

[[JPMorgan Chase|JPMorgan]]'s trading division marked down software loans used as collateral by private credit funds — first major bank to restrict back-leverage. Pre-emptive, no margin calls. ARES -4.5%, OWL/KKR/CG ~-2.6%. Stress escalates from fund-level (redemptions) to bank-funding layer. See [[JPMorgan private credit collateral markdown]] for full analysis.

*Sources: FT, Reuters, Bloomberg, CNBC (Mar 11, 2026)*
