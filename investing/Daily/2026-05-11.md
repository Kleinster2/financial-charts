### Promoted four session patterns into `docs/cluster-validation.md`

Added "Advanced diagnostics — four follow-on patterns" section to the doc. Each pattern is documented as a reusable procedure with a worked example pointer to [[Space pure-plays]]:

1. Pre/post regime split — re-run validation diagnostics on pre- and post-regime-shift windows separately to quantify structural changes. Worked example: Space pure-plays Nov 2025 regime shift (intra-corr 0.466 → 0.656, PC1 51.9% → 71.2%, PC2 21.2% → 7.9%, pairwise floor lifting from 0.06 to 0.50). Cleanest signature of institutional basket construction is "PC2 collapses while PC1 rises."
2. Event-study factor decomposition — for catalyst days, project each name's return onto PC1 to decompose factor (basket-driven) vs idiosyncratic (name-specific). Worked example: May 8 2026 RKLB +34% = +20% factor + +11.6pp Q1-print idiosyncratic.
3. Multiple objectives produce different optimal subsets — factor-tracking optimization (LUNR+BKSY 0.942 corr) ≠ Sharpe optimization (RKLB+PL 2.03) ≠ cum return optimization (LUNR+PL +254%). Names with low PC1 loading can boost Sharpe in pair-combinations because their idiosyncratic returns diversify the factor names'.
4. Complement test for subset validation — build a basket of names NOT in the proposed subset; expected monotonic ordering subset > full > complement on Sharpe + cum return. Worked example: LUNR+BKSY (1.74) > Full 7 (1.43) > Complement 5 (1.21).

Each pattern has a "When to invoke" table entry; all four are deferred-by-default outside the standard `cluster_analysis.py` output.
