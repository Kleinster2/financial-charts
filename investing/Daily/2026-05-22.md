### Morgan Stanley vs Jeff Currie AI-capex / commodities tension

Expanded [[AI capex arms race]], [[Jeff Currie]], [[Mike Wilson]], and [[Morgan Stanley]] with the May 2026 split between Morgan Stanley's AI-capex-as-risk-asset-engine frame and Currie's AI-capex-as-commodity-demand-shock frame.

The key synthesis: they are not disagreeing about whether AI capex continues. Morgan Stanley is long the first derivative - earnings growth, U.S. equity leadership, supplier revenue, and financing activity. Currie is long the second derivative - scarce energy, copper, turbines, fuel, grid equipment, and other physical inputs without which the buildout cannot happen. Both can be right until funding costs or energy prices force the market to choose.
