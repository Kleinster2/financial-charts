### Fake Claude installer — [[Beagle backdoor]] malware

Created [[Beagle backdoor]] and updated [[Agentic AI security]] / [[Anthropic]]. The May 7 BleepingComputer report describes a fake `claude-pro[.]com` site distributing a 505MB `Claude-Pro-windows-x64.zip` archive. The app appeared to work as a Claude-like installer, but deployed a PlugX-style chain in the background: signed `NOVupdate.exe` side-loading `avk.dll`, decrypting `NOVupdate.exe.dat`, loading DonutLoader, then injecting Beagle into memory. The note-level read: this is not an Anthropic breach, but it is part of Anthropic's security surface. Claude demand is now valuable enough to be malware-distribution infrastructure.
