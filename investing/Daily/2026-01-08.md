### GSE mortgage directive

Trump ordered [[Fannie Mae]] and [[Freddie Mac]] to buy $200B of MBS to compress mortgage spreads. First presidential direct intervention in housing finance via large-scale asset purchases.

- Spreads dropped ~18bps immediately
- [[FHFA]] director Bill Pulte: "We're focused on utilizing Fannie and Freddie in a way that no other president has"
- Raises [[Fed independence]] concerns — asset purchases historically Fed's domain
- Signals administration may view GSEs as policy tools, complicating potential IPO

See [[Fannie Mae]], [[GSE reform]].
