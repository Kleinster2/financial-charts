### FT: Trump sons back $1bn vehicle targeting sectors championed by US president (article, May 8, 2026)

Source: [Financial Times](https://www.ft.com/content/d73183b6-d610-4caa-949d-186cbd59c970), George Steer. The article documents [[American Ventures]] — a Palm Beach SPV factory 90%-owned by [[Dominari Holdings]] (Trump-Tower-headquartered; Trump sons own ~12% combined) — managing $1.04bn across 21 SPVs that channel family-office capital into reverse-merger deals targeting drones, AI, mining, crypto, nuclear. Includes a bubble chart of the 21 SPVs by AUM and sector.

The structural mechanic is distinct from [[SPAC]]s and from [[Shell company AI pivot]]: Dominari underwrites a small-cap shell IPO, American Ventures recapitalizes the shell, and a private operating company merges in — collapsing IPO bookbuild, S-1 review, and PIPE-sponsor-promote scrutiny into a sequence the same broker controls. The deals: [[SRM Entertainment]] / [[Tron]] (closed Jul 2025), [[JFB Construction Holdings]] / [[XTEND]] ($1.5bn, Feb 2026), [[Aureus Greenway Holdings]] / [[Powerus]] (PUSA, Mar 2026), [[Skyline Builders]] / [[Cove Kaz Capital]] (Kazakh tungsten, $1.6bn EXIM/DFC LoIs, Apr 2026), [[Dogehash Technologies]] / [[Thumzup Media]] (data-center pivot). Three of the operating companies received federal-policy events (USAF Powerus Guardian-2 order, EXIM/DFC Cove Kaz letters, Pentagon Unusual Machines / IMOD XTEND contracts) within weeks of merger announcements.
